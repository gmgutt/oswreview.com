WWE Superstars Results 2009

WWE SUPERSTARS RESULTS (2009)

Apr 2009

Air Date: 16th April 2009
Debut episode on WGN
The Undertaker bested Matt Hardy by count-out; Christian toppled Finlay; Cody Rhodes bested Shane McMahon by DQ.

Air Date: 23rd April 2009
The Big Show and Kane defeated CM Punk and WWE Intercontinental Champion Rey Mysterio; Finlay and Hornswoggle dropped Tyson Kidd and Natalya; Edge overcame Kofi Kingson.

Air Date: 30th April 2009
Michelle McCool and Alicia Fox defeated Gail Kim and Maria; Evan Bourne downed Paul Burchill; Ted Dibiase Jr overcame Carlito.

May 2009

Air Date: 7th May 2009
Santino Marella and Kelly Kelly beat Beth Phoenix and Rosa Mendez; Finlay beat Zach Ryder; Chris Jericho pinned John Morrison.

Air Date: 14th May 2009
WWE Intercontinental Champion Rey Mysterio bested Shelton Benjamin; ECW Champion Christian toppled Mark Henry; The Big Show toppled Hornswoggle by DQ.

Air Date: 21st May 2009
ECW Champion Christian battled Tommy Dreamer to a no-contest; CM Punk pinned Chris Jericho; Kofi Kingston defeated William Regal.

Air Date: 28th May 2009
WWE Unified World Tag Champions Primo and Carlito Colon beat Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin; Jack Swagger defeated Finlay; John Cena dropped Ted Dibiase Jr.

Jun 2009


Air Date: 4th June 2009
Goldust and Hornswoggle defeated Jamie Noble and Brian Kendrick; DH Smith downed Tommy Dreamer; Jeff Hardy and R-Truth overturned Chris Jericho and Dolph Ziggler.

Air Date: 11th June 2009
Beth Phoenix and Rosa Mendes beat The Bella Twins; Evan Bourne pinned Zack Ryder; Chris Jericho bested John Morrison.

Air Date: 18th June 2009
Chavo Guerrero Jr beat Santino Marella; Zack Ryder beat Mike Williams; Eve Torres defeated Layla. Maria was guest referee; Christian and WWE Unifed World Tag Champions Carlito and Primo Colon toppled Jack Swagger, DH Smith and Tyson Kidd.

Air Date: 25th June 2009
Dolph Ziggler dropped Jimmy Wang Yang; Jack Swagger bested Evan Bourne; WWE US Champion Kofi Kingston beat MVP and Matt Hardy in a three-way.

Jul 2009


Air Date: 2nd July 2009
Santino Marella beat Brian Kendrick; John Morrison defeated Charlie Haas; Zack Ryder downed Tyler Reks; Edge toppled World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk in a non-title match.

Air Date: 9th July 2009
Zack Ryder beat Tyler Reks; R-Truth defeated Ricky Ortiz; Hornswoggle defeated Chavo Guerrero Jr.

Air Date: 16th July 2009
Cryme Tyme overcame DH Smith and Tyson Kidd; Brie Bella defeated Katie Lea; The Big Show defeated Kofi Kingston.

Air Date: 23rd July 2009
Christian beat William Regal; Evan Bourne pinned Jamie Noble; John Morrison bested Kane by DQ.

Air Date: 30th July 2009
John Morrison defeated Tyson Kidd; William Regal downed Yoshi Tatsu; WWE World Champion Randy Orton bested Primo.

Aug 2009

Air Date: 6th August 2009
Chris Masters overturned Santino Marella; William Regal and Paul Burchill beat Tyler Reks and Yoshi Tatsu; WWE Intercontinental Champion Rey Mysterio dropped Mike Knox by DQ.

Air Date: 13th August 2009
John Morrison defeated DH Smith; Goldust bested Sheamus; Carlito and Rosa Mendes downed WWE US Champion Kofi Kingston and Mickie James.

Air Date: 20th August 2009
Beth Phoenix defeated Kelly Kelly; Shelton Benjamin dropped Tyler Reks; R-Truth overturned Charlie Haas; Mark Henry downed Chris Masters by count-out.

Air Date: 27th August 2009
Jack Swagger defeated Primo; Paul Burchill defeated The Hurricane; Dolph Ziggler bested Finlay and Mike Knox in a three-way.

Sep 2009

Air Date: 3rd September 2009
Evan Bourne defeated Chris Masters; William Regal defeated Tyler Reks; DH Smith, Tyson Kidd and Natalya downed Cryme Tyme and Eve Torres.

Air Date: 10th September 2009
Kane defeated Jimmy Wang Yang; Sheamus pinned Yoshi Tatsu; Nikki Bella beat Katie Lea Burchill; Dolph Ziggler downed Slam Master J; Jack Swagger and The Miz bested WWE US Champion Kofi Kingston and Primo.

Air Date 17th September 2009
Chris Masters downed Santino Marella; ECW Champion Christian defeated Paul Burchill; Dolph Ziggler downed Finlay.

Air Date: 24th September 2009
Primo defeated Chris Masters; Goldust beat William Regal by DQ; WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool and Layla defeated Melina and Maria; Matt Hardy downed Kane by DQ.

Oct 2009

Air Date: 1st October 2009
Shad Gaspard defeated Tyson Kidd; Sheamus beat Tyler Reks; WWE US Champion Kofi Kingston downed The Miz.

Air Date: 8th October 2009
Ted Dibiase Jr defeated Evan Bourne; Shelton Benjamin beat Sheamus by DQ; Cryme Tyme defeated DH Smith and Tyson Kidd.

Air Date: 15th October 2009
Mark Henry defeated Chris Masters; Vladmir Kozlov beat Tommy Dreamer; CM Punk defeated WWE Intercontinental Champion John Morrison by DQ.

Air Date: 22nd October 2009
Kelly Kelly beat Gail Kim; Tommy Dreamer and Goldust defeated Vladimir Kozlov and Ezekiel Jackson; Drew McIntyre and Eric Escobar downed Matt Hardy and R-Truth.

Air Date: 29th October 2009
Jack Swagger beat Primo; Alicia Fox defeated Kelly Kelly; Zack Ryder dropped Tyler Reks; Cryme Tyme and R-Truth overcame DH Smith, Tyson Kidd and Mike Knox.

Nov 2009

Air Date: 5th November 2009
Santino Marella beat Chavo Guerrero Jr; Jack Swagger defeated Primo; Paul Burchill beat Tyler Reks; Chris Jericho defeated Finlay.

Air Date: 12th November 2009
Matt Hardy beat DH Smith; Shelton Benjamin defeated Zack Ryder; MVP downed Ted Dibiase Jr.

Air Date: 19th November 2009
Zack Ryder bested Shelton Benjamin; Eric Escobar toppled Jimmy Wang Yang; Mark Henry pinned Cody Rhodes.

Air Date: 26th November 2009
WWE US Champion The Miz defeated Santino Marella; Vladimir Kozlov defeated Goldust; R-Truth downed Dolph Ziggler.

Dec 2009

Air Date: 3rd December 2009
Ted Dibiase Jr and Cody Rhodes bested Evan Bourne and Primo; The Hurricane beat Zack Ryder by DQ; Dolph Ziggler defeated Matt Hardy.

Air Date: 10th December 2009
Dolph Ziggler beat Finlay; Tyson Kidd and DH Smith bested Slam Master J and Jimmy Wang Yang; Jack Swagger defeated MVP.

Air Date: 17th December 2009
Cryme Tyme bested Dolph Ziggler and Mike Knox; Vance Archer toppled Tommy Dreamer; MVP overturned Jack Swagger.

Air Date: 24th December 2009
Vladimir Kozlov battled Ezekiel Jackson to a double count-out; Chavo Guerrero overcame Primo; Kane dropped Mike Knox.

Air Date: 31st December 2009
WWE Divas Champion Melina downed Alicia Fox; Shelton Benjamin beat Vance Archer by DQ; Cryme Tyme overcame Charlie Haas and Mike Knox; Jack Swagger and Carlito defeated Chris Masters and MVP.
