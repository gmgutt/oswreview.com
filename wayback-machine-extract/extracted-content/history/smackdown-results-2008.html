Smackdown Results 2008

WWE SMACKDOWN RESULTS (2008)

Jan 2008

Air Date: 4th January 2008
Finlay beat WWE US Champion MVP in a non-title match (Beat the Clock); Kenny Dykstra and Victoria defeated Chuck Palumbo and Michelle McCool; Chavo Guerrero Jr downed Sho Funaki (Beat the Clock); Batista battled Curt Hawkins and Zack Ryder to a draw in a handicap match (Beat the Clock);  Jimmy Wang Yang and Shannon Moore dropped Deuce and Domino; The Undertaker battled Mark Henry to a draw (Beat the Clock); Rey Mysterio bested World Heavyweight Champion Edge in a non-title match (Beat the Clock).

Air Date: 11th January 2008
Jamie Noble pinned Chuck Palumbo; Batista defeated WWE US Champion MVP by count-out; The Undertaker bested Mark Henry and Matt Striker in a handicap match; Hornswoggle downed Ranjin Singh in an “arm wrestling contest”; Kane toppled Domino; Rey Mysterio Jr rolled over Chavo Guerrero Jr.

Air Date: 18th January 2008
The Great Khali overcame Finlay; Jimmy Wang Yang and Shannon Moore overturned Deuce and Domino; Batista bested Mark Henry; Michelle McCool toppled Layla; Rey Mysterio Jr and ECW Champion CM Punk rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Edge and Chavo Guerrero Jr by DQ.

Air Date: 25th January 2008
Rey Mysterio Jr defeated Zack Ryder and Curt Hawkins by DQ in a handicap match; Jimmy Wang Yang downed Domino; Michelle McCool, Chuck Palumbo, and Jamie Noble battled Layla and WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz to a no-contest; Finlay battled The Great Khali to a no-contest in a “Belfast Brawl”; The Undertaker bested Big Daddy V; World Heavyweight Champion Edge toppled CM Punk.

Feb 2008

Air Date: 1st February 2008
Ric Flair bested WWE US Champion MVP by DQ; Rey Mysterio Jr and CM Punk toppled World Heavyweight Champion Edge and ECW Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr; Finlay and Hornswoggle overcame Deuce and Domino; Curt Hawkins and Zack Ryder defeated Jimmy Wang Yang and Shannon Moore; The Undertaker and Kane rolled over Mark Henry and Big Daddy V.

Air Date: 8th February 2008
Shelton Benjamin bested Kane by count-out; Chuck Palumbo overcame Jamie Noble; Jesse and Festus overturned Deuce and Domino; Curt Hawkins and Zack Ryder toppled Jimmy Wang Yang and Shannon Moore; The Undertaker, Batista, and Finlay battled The Great Khali, WWE US Champion MVP, and Big Daddy V to a no-contest.

Air Date: 15th February 2008
Batista bested WWE US Champion MVP in a non-title match; Jimmy Wang Yang, Shannon Moore, Jesse and Festus defeated Curt Hawkins, Zack Ryder, Deuce and Domino; Victoria overturned Michelle McCool; The Undertaker pinned Big Daddy V; CM Punk and Kane toppled ECW Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr and Shelton Benjamin; Finlay rolled over The Great Khali.

Air Date: 22nd February 2008
Shelton Benjamin defeated Jimmy Wang Yang; Jesse and Festus downed Deuce and Domino; Big Daddy V dropped Shannon Moore; Batista bested WWE US Champion MVP in a non-title match; Kane pinned The Great Khali; Chuck Palumbo overturned Matt Jackson; Rey Mysterio Jr toppled ECW Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr in a non-title match.

Air Date: 29th February 2008
The Big Show pinned Joey Ryans and Jay Garland in a handicap match; Jimmy Wang Yang and Shannon Moore beat Deuce and Domino; Chuck Palumbo overcame Jamie Noble; Batista and Kane dropped WWE US Champion MVP and The Great Khali; Jesse and Festus downed WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz in a non-title match; Big Daddy V battled Balls Mahoney to a no-contest; The Undertaker rolled over Zack Ryder.

Mar 2008

Air Date: 7th March 2008
CM Punk, Jesse, and Festus defeated ECW Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr, and WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz; WWE US Champion MVP toppled Batista by count-out; Kane bested Chuck Palumbo; The Big Show overcame Jamie Noble by DQ; World Heavyweight Champion Edge, Curt Hawkins and Zack Ryder rolled over The Undertaker in a handicap match.

Air Date: 14th March 2008
The Big Show defeated Jimmy Wang Yang and Shannon Moore in a handicap match; John Morrison pinned The Miz; WWE US Champion MVP downed Batista in a “no holds barred” match; The Great Khali and Chuck Palumbo downed Kane and Jamie Noble; World Heavyweight Champion Edge, ECW Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr, Zack Ryder and Curt Hawkins rolled over Shawn Michaels and Ric Flair in a handicap cage match.

Air Date: 21st March 2008
Batista downed Deuce and Domino in a handicap match; The Big Show defeated Chase Stevens, Andy Douglas, and Luke Hawx in a handicap match; World Heavyweight Champion Edge pinned Sho Funaki; Kane battled Chuck Palumbo to a no-contest; WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz overcame Jesse and Festus; The Undertaker rolled over ECW Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr in a non-title match.

Air Date: 28th March 2008
Mark Henry won an “over-the-top-rope-challenge”; CM Punk pinned John Morrison; Kofi Kingston, Jesse and Festus downed The Miz, Trevor Murdoch, and Lance Cade; Batista bested Snitsky; Michelle McCool and Cherry beat Maryse and Victoria. Eve was guest referee; WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho bested WWE US Champion MVP by DQ.

Apr 2008

Air Date: 4th April 2008
Matt Hardy toppled WWE US Champion MVP in a non-title match; Festus dropped Zack Ryder; Vladimir Kozlov overturned Matt Bentley; WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz overcame Jimmy Wang Yang and Shannon Moore; World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker battled ECW Champion Kane to a no-contest.

Air Date: 11th April 2008
WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz overcame Jimmy Wang Yang and Shannon Moore; Finlay defeated Matt Striker; Victoria pinned Michelle McCool; Vladimir Kozlov overturned Matt Logan; Matt Hardy toppled Chuck Palumbo; World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker rolled over Festus.

Air Date: 18th April 2008
WWE US Champion MVP downed Tommy Dreamer; Hornswoggle pinned Matt Striker; Chavo Guerrero Jr dropped Jamie Noble; The Big Show defeated Mark Henry by DQ; Cherry overcame Victoria; Vladimir Kozlov overturned Leroy Kincaid; Batista battled World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker to a double count-out.

Air Date: 25th April 2008
Shannon Moore downed The Miz; Natalya Neidhart beat Cherry; Edge toppled CM Punk; Vladimir Kozlov overturned Marty Garner; World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker rolled over Batista in a no-DQ match.

May 2008

Air Date: 2nd May 2008
WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz overcame Jimmy Wang Yang and Shannon Moore; Vladimir Kozlov overturned Colin Delaney; Victoria and Natalya Neidhart defeated Michelle McCool and Cherry; Mark Henry pinned Nunzio; WWE US Champion Matt Hardy toppled MVP; Curt Hawkins and Zack Ryder downed Jesse and Festus; Finlay beat Julio Dinero.

Air Date: 9th May 2008
WWE US Champion Matt Hardy toppled CM Punk; MVP battled Finlay to a double pinfall draw; Natalya defeated Kelly Kelly; Batista dropped John Morrison; The Big Show pinned Tommy Dreamer; ECW Champion Kane bested Chavo Guerrero Jr; Vladimir Kozlov overturned Phil Atlas; Edge won a battle royal.

Air Date: 16th May 2008
CM Punk bested Chuck Palumbo; Michelle McCool defeated Layla; The Big Show and Finlay downed MVP and Mark Henry; Vladimir Kozlov overturned Sho Funaki; Cherry pinned Maryse; WWE US Champion Matt Hardy toppled Shelton Benjamin; The Undertaker battled Edge, Curt Hawkins, Zack Ryder, and Vickie Guerrero to a no-contest in a handicap match.

Air Date: 23rd May 2008
Maryse, Deuce and Domino defeated Cherry, Jesse and Festus; Finlay downed Chuck Palumbo; Batista dropped MVP; Vladimir Kozlov overturned Nunzio; WWE US Champion Matt Hardy and Kofi Kingston dropped Shelton Benjamin and Elijah Burke; The Undertaker rolled over Chavo Guerrero Jr.

Air Date: 30th May 2008
WWE US Champion Matt Hardy toppled Elijah Burke; Finlay beat Chuck Palumbo by DQ; CM Punk overturned John Morrison; Batista and The Big Show bested Curt Hawkins and Zack Ryder; MVP bested Jamie Noble; Vladimir Kozlov downed Shannon Moore; Michelle McCool downed Maryse.

Jun 2008

Air Date: 6th June 2008
Finlay defeated Chuck Palumbo in a “Belfast Brawl”; The Great Khali overcame Deuce and Domino in a handicap match; Cousin Sal overcame Santino Marella; Natalya won a “Golden Dreams match” over Cherry, Maryse, Victoria, Layla and Michelle McCool; Vladimir Kozlov downed Jimmy Wang Yang; Batista, Colin Delaney, Funaki and Nunzio toppled World Heavyweight Champion Edge, Curt Hawkins, Zack Ryder and Chavo Guerrero.

Air Date: 13th June 2008
John Morrison bested Finlay; Mark Henry overcame Tommy Dreamer; Cherry beat Maryse; MVP overcame CM Punk by DQ; WWE US Champion Matt Hardy toppled Chuck Palumbo; Jesse and Festus defeated Deuce and Domino; Batista toppled The Great Khali.

Air Date: 20th June 2008
WWE US Champion Matt Hardy dropped Bam Neely; Michele McCool defeated Layla; Vladimir Kozlov downed Jamie Noble; ECW Champion Kane overcame MVP by count-out; The Great Khali bested Charles Robinson; Jesse and Festus toppled Deuce and Domino; Finlay dropped The Miz; Chavo Guerrero Jr rolled over Batista by count-out. Edge was guest referee.

Air Date 27th June 2008
ECW Champion Kane and The Big Show bested Mark Henry and MVP; Finlay and Hornswoggle downed Curt Hawkins and Zach Ryder; Edge defeated WWE US Champion Matt Hardy in a non-title match; Maryse, Victoria and Natalya overcame Michelle McCool, Kelly Kelly and Cherry; Vladimir Kozlov downed Domino; Batista toppled Umaga by DQ.

Jul 2008

Air Date: 4th July 2008
Michelle McCool won a “star-on-a-pole” match over Victoria, Kelly Kelly, Cherry and Maryse; Jesse and Festus defeated Curt Hawkins and Zack Ryder; Jeff Hardy toppled Morrison; Umaga downed Funaki; WWE US Champion Matt Hardy won a four-way over Shelton Benjamin, Chavo Guerrero and Mr Kennedy.

Air Date: 11th July 2008
Shelton Benjamin beat Matt Hardy; Mr Kennedy downed Domino; Finlay and Hornswoggle bested WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz in a non-title match; WWE World Champion Triple H rolled over Chavo Guerrero; Natalya and Maryse overcame Michelle McCool and Cherry; Jesse and Festus overturned Curt Hawkins and Zack Ryder by count-out; Vladimir Kozlov downed Stevie Richards; Edge battled The Big Show to a no-contest.

Air Date: 18th July 2008
Curt Hawkins and Zack Ryder defeated Finlay and Hornswoggle. Edge was guest referee; The Big Show battled MVP, Shelton Benjamin, Vladimir Kozlov and The Great Khali to a no-contest in a handicap match; Natalya downed Cherry; Vickie Guerrero defeated Cherry; Brian Kendrick beat Jimmy Wang Yang; Umaga toppled Mr Kennedy; WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz overcame Jesse and Festus; WWE US Champion Matt Hardy battled Jeff Hardy to a no-contest.

Air Date: 25th July 2008
WWE US Champion Shelton Benjamin beat Jimmy Wang Yang; Festus defeated Curt Hawkins; Vladimir Kozlov downed Stevie Richards; The Great Khali won a battle royal; WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool downed Maryse; Brian Kendrick beat Shannon Moore.

Aug 2008

Air Date: 1st August 2008
MVP bested Jimmy Wang Yang; Mr Kennedy downed Shelton Benjamin; Brian Kendrick beat Stevie Richards; The Great Khali overcame Jeff Hardy; Maria defeated Victoria; The Big Show toppled Domino.

Air Date: 8th August 2008
Maryse and Victoria and Natalya beat WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool, Cherry and Maria; WWE Tag Champions Curt Hawkins and Zach Ryder downed Jimmy Wang Yang and Shannon Moore; Jeff Hardy toppled Shelton Benjamin by DQ; Triple H rolled over The Great Khali by DQ in a “arm-wrestling” match; Vladimir Kozlov downed Jesse; Brian Kendrick beat Super Crazy.

Air Date: 15th August 2008
Jeff Hardy toppled WWE US Champion Shelton Benjamin by DQ; Maryse and Natalya overcame WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool and Maria; Brian Kendrick beat Scotty Goldman (Colt Cabana); WWE World Champion Triple H rolled over Kenny Dykstra; The Big Show bested Ryan Braddock; Vladimir Kozlov downed Festus.

Air Date: 22nd August 2008
Maria downed Natalya by DQ; Brian Kendrick won a battle royal; MVP dropped Festus by count-out; Shelton Benjamin bested Finlay; Jeff Hardy toppled The Great Khali.

Air Date: 29th August 2008
R-Truth beat Kenny Dykstra; Maryse and Natalya defeated WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool and Maria; Jeff Hardy toppled MVP; WWE Tag Champions Curt Hawkins and Zack Ryder battled Jesse and Festus to a no-contest; Brie Bella downed Victoria; WWE World Champion Triple H rolled over Shelton Benjamin.

Sep 2008

Air Date: 5th September 2008
R-Truth defeated Bam Neely; Shelton Benjamin downed MVP; Maryse dropped Maria; Brian Kendrick beat Jeff Hardy; Super Crazy beat Ryan Braddock; Brie Bella overcame Victoria; WWE World Champion Triple H rolled over The Great Khali in a lumberjack match.

Air Date: 12th September 2008
Brie Bella, WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool and Maria defeated Maryse and Natalya and Victoria; R-Truth bested Chavo Guerrero; Vladimir Kozlov downed Scotty Goldman and Funaki in a handicap match; Carlito and Primo beat WWE Tag Champions Curt Hawkins and Zack Ryder in a non-title match; Jeff Hardy won a four-way over MVP, Brian Kendrick and Shelton Benjamin.

Air Date: 19th September 2008
WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool downed Maryse; Ryan Braddock overcame Festus by DQ; R-Truth overturned WWE US Champion Shelton Benjamin in a non-title match; Jeff Hardy toppled The Brian Kendrick; The Great Khali overcame Scotty Goldman; WWE World Champion Triple H rolled over MVP.

Air Date: 26th September 2008
WWE World Champion Triple H and Jeff Hardy toppled MVP and The Brian Kendrick; Maria and Brie Bella beat Victoria and Natalya; Vladimir Kozlov battled The Great Khali to a no-contest; Chavo Guerrero defeated Jimmy Wang Yang; Carlito and Primo toppled WWE Tag Champions Curt Hawkins and Zack Ryder to win the titles.

Oct 2008

Air Date: 3rd October 2008
Batista, Jeff Hardy, Rey Mysterio and Finlay toppled JBL, Kane, MVP and Brian Kendrick; WWE Intercontinental Champion Santino Marella downed WWE US Champion Shelton Benjamin in a non-title match; Mark Henry and The Great Khali dropped Chavo Guerrero in a handicap match; CM Punk and WWE Tag Champions Carlito and Primo overturned Manu and WWE  World Tag Champions Ted Dibiase Jr and Cody Rhodes; WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix bested WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool in a non-title lumberjack match; WWE World Champion Triple H rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Chris Jericho and ECW Champion Matt Hardy in a non-title three-way.

Air Date: 10th October 2008
Brie Bella beat Natalya; R-Truth overturned WWE US Champion Shelton Benjamin in a non-title match; Jeff Hardy toppled MVP; WWE Tag Champions Carlito and Primo Colon, and Jesse and Festus downed Curt Hawkins, Zack Ryder, Kenny Dykstra and Ryan Braddock; The Great Khali overcame Aaron Bolo, Nelson Creed and Scotty Mac in a handicap match; WWE World Champion Triple H battled The Big Show to a no-contest.

Air Date: 17th October 2008
WWE Tag Champions Carlito and Primo Colon beat Curt Hawkins and Zack Ryder; R-Truth overturned MVP; Ezekiel Jackson defeated Super Crazy; The Undertaker overturned The Great Khali by DQ; Maria won a pole match over Brie Bella, Maryse, Natalya, and Victoria; Jesse and Festus downed Ryan Braddock and Kenny Dykstra; Vladimir Kozlov downed Jeff Hardy.

Air Date: 24th October 2008
Maryse and Natalya defeated Michelle McCool and Maria; WWE Tag Champions Carlito and Primo Colon beat Chavo Guerrero and Bam Neely; R-Truth and Kung Fu Naki downed WWE US Champion Shelton Benjamin and MVP; Brian Kendrick beat Super Crazy; WWE World Champion Triple H battled The Undertaker to a no-contest.

Air Date: 31st October 2008
John Morrison and The Miz overcame WWE Tag Champions Carlito and Primo Colon in a non-title match; Jimmy Wang Yang defeated Brian Kendrick by DQ; Rey Mysterio, ECW Champion Matt Hardy and Jeff Hardy toppled Mark Henry, MVP and Kane; WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool, Brie Bella and Maria defeated Victoria, Natalya and Maryse; The Undertaker overturned Chavo Guerrero in a casket match.

Nov 2008

Air Date: 7th November 2008
WWE US Champion Shelton Benjamin bested R-Truth; Ezekiel Jackson beat Jimmy Wang Yang; The Miz and John Morrison toppled Triple H and Jeff Hardy by DQ; Kung Fu Naki overcame Curt Hawkins; The Great Khali overcame MVP; Brie Bella dropped Victoria; Vladimir Kozlov downed The Undertaker by DQ.

Air Date: 14th November 2008
ECW Champion Matt Hardy toppled WWE US Champion Shelton Benjamin; Brian Kendrick beat Carlito; Kung Fu Naki overcame MVP; WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool downed Maria; Jeff Hardy toppled The Undertaker in an “Extreme Rules” match.

Air Date: 21st November 2008
ECW Champion Matt Hardy and R-Truth overturned WWE US Champion Shelton Benjamin and Mark Henry; The Miz and John Morrison overcame Jesse and Festus; Jeff Hardy toppled WWE World Champion Triple H in a non-title match; Brian Kendrick and Ezekiel Jackson downed Primo and Carlito Colon; James Mason dropped MVP; Nikki and Brie Bella rolled over Natalya and Victoria.

Air Date: 28th November 2008
Jeff Hardy toppled Brian Kendrick (Beat the Clock); Maryse, Victoria and Natalya overcame Nikki Bella, Brie Bella and WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool; ECW Champion Matt Hardy battled Vladimir Kozlov to a draw (Beat the Clock); Festus and Jesse overturned John Morrison and The Miz by DQ; Triple H rolled over Shelton Benjamin (Beat the Clock).

Dec 2008

Air Date: 5th December 2008
Edge bested Kung Fu Naki; Maria defeated WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool in a non-title match; Hurricane Helms downed MVP; Gauntlet match: Triple H and Jeff Hardy toppled Zack Ryder and Curt Hawkins, The Miz and John Morrison bested Triple H and Jeff Hardy by count-out; ECW Champion Matt Hardy toppled Chavo Guerrero by DQ; The Undertaker overturned The Big Show in a cage match.

Air Date: 12th December 2008
R-Truth overturned MVP; Brian Kendrick beat Primo Colon; Hurricane Helms defeated WWE US Champion Shelton Benjamin in a non-title match; The Great Khali overcame Curt Hawkins and Zack Ryder in a handicap match; Nikki and Brie Bella downed Natalya and Maryse; Triple H battled Jeff Hardy to a no-contest.

Air Date: 19th December 2008
Hurricane Helms and R-Truth overturned WWE US Champion Shelton Benjamin and MVP; John Morrison dropped Festus; Edge overcame ECW Champion Matt Hardy in a non-title match; Ezekiel Jackson bested Carlito; Maryse toppled Maria; WWE World Champion Jeff Hardy toppled Vladimir Kozlov by DQ.

Air Date: 26th December 2008
Triple H rolled over Chavo Guerrero; WWE US Champion Shelton Benjamin pinned Hurricane Helms; Carlito Colon beat Brian Kendrick; Maryse overturned WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool to win the title; The Great Khali overcame MVP; Vladimir Kozlov downed Jimmy Wang Yang; WWE World Champion Jeff Hardy toppled The Big Show by count-out.
