Smackdown Results 2012

WWE SMACKDOWN RESULTS (2012)

Jan 2012

Air Date:  6th January 2012
WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes defeated Booker T; Hornswoggle beat Heath Slater in an “over-the-top-rope” challenge; Hunico bested Ted Dibiase Jr; Santino Marella downed Drew McIntyre; Epico and Primo toppled WWE World Tag Champions Evan Bourne and Kofi Kingston in a non-title match; Tamina overcame Natalya; World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan rolled over The Big Show by DQ.

Air Date: 13th January 2012
Justin Gabriel beat Heath Slater; WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes bested Ezekiel Jackson; Ted Dibiase Jr overturned Drew McIntyre; Sheamus pinned Jinder Mahal; David Otunga overturned Santino Marella; Brodus Clay squashed Tyson Kidd; Tamina bested Natalya; World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan battled The Big Show to a no-contest in a no-DQ match.

Air Date: 20th January 2012
WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes bested Justin Gabriel; WWE World Tag Champions Primo and Epico defeated Jimmy and Jey Uso; Wade Barrett downed Sheamus in a Tables match; Ted Dibiase Jr overcame Hunico in a flag match; Santino Marella beat Drew McIntyre in a Blindfold match; World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan battled Mark Henry to a no-contest in a lumberjack match.

Air Date: 27th January 2012
WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes defeated Justin Gabriel; Sheamus overturned Drew McIntyre; Primo and Epico bested Yoshi Tatsu and Santino Marella; Hunico beat Ted Dibiase Jr; Brodus Clay defeated Alex Riley; Aksana overcame Natalya; The Big Show toppled Mark Henry by count-out.

Feb 2012

Air Date: 3rd February 2012
Sheamus bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes in a non-title match; WWE World Tag Champions Epico and Primo downed Santino Marella and Jim Duggan; Brodus Clay dropped Heath Slater; WWE Divas Champion Beth Phoenix and Natalya overcame Aksana and Tamina; Randy Orton toppled Wade Barrett.

Air Date: 10th February 2012
The Great Khali beat Jinder Mahal; WWE Divas Champion Beth Phoenix defeated Alicia Fox; The Big Show and Sheamus downed Wade Barrett and Cody Rhodes; Ted Dibiase Jr dropped Hunico; World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan rolled over Randy Orton by DQ.

Air Date: 17th February 2012
The Big Show and The Great Khali defeated Cody Rhodes and Wade Barrett; Hunico pinned Ted Dibiase; WWE World Tag Champions Epico and Primo overturned Jimmy and Jey Uso; World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan defeated Sheamus by DQ; Jinder Mahal downed Ezekiel Jackson; Tamina and Alicia Fox overcame Beth Phoenix and Natalya; Santino Marella won a battle royal.

Air Date: 24th February 2012
Sheamus pinned The Miz; WWE US Champion Jack Swagger and Dolph Ziggler defeated Kofi Kingston and R-Truth; The Great Khali overcame Drew McIntyre; Mark Henry toppled The Big Show by count-out; David Otunga bested Ezekiel Jackson; World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan battled WWE World Champion CM Punk to a draw.

Mar 2012

Air Date: 2nd March 2012
Sheamus overcame Dolph Ziggler; Santino Marella beat Heath Slater; Eve Torres bested Natalya; The Big Show dropped Mark Henry; Justin Gabriel toppled Drew McIntyre; World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan downed Randy Orton by count-out.

Air Date: 9th March 2012
WWE US Champion Santino Marella bested WWE US Champion Jack Swagger in a cage match; Drew McIntyre beat Hornswoggle; Mark Henry dropped Ezekiel Jackson; Teddy Long overcame John Laurinaitis; Kofi Kingston and R-Truth toppled WWE World Tag Champions Primo and Epico in a non-title match; Randy Orton, The Big Show and Sheamus rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan, WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes and The Miz.

Air Date: 16th  March 2012
Kofi Kingston beat David Otunga; AJ overcame Brie Bella; WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes overturned The Great Khali; The Big Show squashed Drew McIntyre; Mark Henry bested Yoshi Tatsu; Chris Jericho rolled over Sheamus by count-out.

Air Date: 23rd March 2012
Mark Henry beat R-Truth; AJ bested Brie Bella; The Big Show toppled Kane by DQ; Brodus Clay overcame Heath Slater; The Great Khali overturned Dolph Ziggler by count-out; WWE World Champion CM Punk and Sheamus rolled over The Miz and World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan.

Air Date: 30th March 2012
Zack Ryder beat Drew McIntyre; Chris Jericho toppled Kofi Kingston.

Apr 2012


Air Date: 6th April 2012
Mark Henry and David Otunga defeated R-Truth in a handicap match; Randy Orton bested Kane in a no-DQ: Ryback downed Barry Stevens; The Big Show dropped Heath Slater; Nikki Bella toppled WWE Diva’s Champion Beth Phoenix in a non-title match; Alberto Del Rio overturned World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus by DQ.

Air Date: 10th April 2012
Mark Henry battled Randy Orton to a no-contest; Ryback downed Benny Camer; Jimmy and Jey Uso downed Heath Slater and Tyson Kidd; The Great Khali, Natalya and Alicia Fox overturned Drew McIntyre and The Bella Twins; Hunico bested Jim Duggan by DQ; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus and Gene Okerlund rolled over Daniel Bryan and Alberto Del Rio.

Air Date: 20th April 2012
Natalya defeated AJ by DQ; Brodus Clay pinned Hunico; Darren Young and Titus O’Neal toppled Jimmy and Jey Uso; Alberto Del Rio toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion The Big Show in a non-title match; Ryback dropped Danny Merman; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus, Randy Orton and WWE Intercontinental Champion The Big Show rolled over Daniel Bryan, Cody Rhodes and Mark Henry.

Air Date: 27th April 2012
Alberto Del Rio toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion The Big Show in a non-title match; WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella beat Alicia Fox; Titus O’Neil and Darren Young downed Ezekiel Jackson and Yoshi Tatsu; Antonio Cesaro dropped Tyson Kidd; The Great Khali pinned Cody Rhodes; Ryback overturned Jacob Kaye; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus rolled over Mark Henry.

May 2012

Air Date: 4th May 2012
World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus bested Daniel Bryan by DQ; WWE World Tag Champions Kofi Kingston and R-Truth dropped Camacho and Hunico; Brodus Clay beat Jack Swagger; Ryback squashed Derrick Bateman; Randy Orton and The Big Show toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes and Kane; WWE Divas Champion Layla defeated Natalya; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus bested Daniel Bryan.

Air Date: 11th May 2012
World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus and Randy Orton battled Chris Jericho and Alberto Del Rio to a double-DQ; AJ beat Kaitlyn; Daniel Bryan defeated The Big Show; Ryback squashed Heath Slater; Antonio Cesaro dropped Alex Riley; Randy Orton toppled Alberto Del Rio by DQ; R-Truth overturned Jack Swagger; Darren Young and Titus O’Neil overcame WWE US Champion Santino Marella and Zack Ryder; Brodus Clay defeated Hunico; Chris Jericho rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus by DQ.

Air Date: 18th May 2012
WWE World Tag Champions R-Truth and Kofi Kingston bested Darren Young and Titus O’Neil; Daniel Bryan toppled Zack Ryder; Kane overcame CM Punk by DQ; WWE US Champion Santino Marella overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes in a non-title match; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus rolled over Randy Orton.

Air Date: 25th May 2012
WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian beat Hunico; Titus O’Neil and Darren Young defeated Jimmy and Jey Uso; Ryback squashed Brian Edwards and Kevin Bendl in a handicap match; WWE US Champion Santino Marella downed Ricardo Rodriguez; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus bested Jack Swagger; Damien Sandow toppled Yoshi Tatsu; Alberto Del Rio rolled over Randy Orton and Kane in a three-way.

Jun 2012

Air Date: 1st June 2012
Sin Cara defeated Heath Slater; Damien Sandow downed Ezekiel Jackson; Ryback squashed Chris Lyons and Ryan Shelton in a handicap match; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus pinned Dolph Ziggler; Zack Ryder and WWE US Champion Santino Marella dropped Darren Young and Titus O’Neil; Cody Rhodes bested Tyson Kidd; WWE World Champion CM Punk battled Kane to a no-contest.

Air Date: 8th June 2012
Albert Del Rio bested The Great Khali; Brodus Clay overcame Derrick Bateman; Sin Cara pinned Drew McIntyre; Ryback squashed Kevin Mahoney and Tony Andriotis; WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian toppled Dolph Ziggler; Antonio Cesaro downed Jimmy Uso; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus rolled over Kane by DQ.

Air Date: 15th June 2012
Daniel Bryan and Dolph Ziggler beat WWE World Champion CM Punk and World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus; Brodus Clay defeated Heath Slater; Beth Phoenix downed Alicia Fox; WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian dropped Jack Swagger; Ryback overcame Ari Cohen and Mike Testa in a handicap match; Damien Sandow toppled Tyson Kidd.

Air Date: 22nd June 2012
Ryback beat Jared Wachtler and Frank Venezia in a handicap match; Alberto Del Rio bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian in a non-title match; Kane dropped Daniel Bryan; Zack Ryder overcame Heath Slater; Darren Young and Titus O’Neil downed Jimmy and Jey Uso; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus toppled Dolph Ziggler.

Air Date: 29th June 2012
WWE Divas Champion Layla beat AJ; Damien Sandow defeated Zack Ryder (MITB Qualifier); Tyson Kidd downed Jack Swagger (MITB Qualifier); WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian and WWE US Champion Santino Marella dropped Cody Rhodes and David Otunga (MITB Qualifier); Ryback squashed Brendan Burke and Dan Barone in a handicap match; Tensai bested Justin Gabriel (MITB Qualifier); World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus rolled over Alberto Del Rio and Dolph Ziggler in a three-way.

Jul 2012

Air Date: 3rd July 2012
The Great Khali and Layla beat Antonio Cesaro and Aksana; Cody Rhodes bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian in a non-title match (MITB Qualifier); Dolph Ziggler downed Alex Riley (MITB Qualifier); WWE US Champion Santino Marella, Jim Duggan and Sgt Slaughter toppled Drew McIntyre, Hunico and Camacho; Ryback squashed Curt Hawkins; Zack Ryder won a battle royal.

Air Date: 13th July 2012
Alberto Del Rio beat Sin Cara; Primo defeated Darren Young; Dolph Ziggler and Cody Rhodes downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian and WWE US Champion Santino Marella; The Big Show dropped The Great Khali; Ryback bested Tyler Reks; Damien Sandow overturned Justin Gabriel; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus rolled over Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 20th July 2012
Darren Young, Titus O’Neil, Hunico and Camacho downed Primo, Epico and WWE World Tag Champions R-Truth and Kofi Kingston; Damien Sandow bested Zack Ryder; Kane toppled Daniel Bryan by DQ; Road Warrior Animal pinned Heath Slater; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus and Rey Mysterio dropped Alberto Del Rio and Dolph Ziggler by DQ.

Air Date: 27th July 2012
WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz downed Christian; Ryback dropped Jinder Mahal by count-out; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus overcame Cody Rhodes; Antonio Cesaro overturned WWE US Champion Santino Marella in a non-title match; Damien Sandow beat Yoshi Tatsu; Alberto Del Rio rolled over Rey Mysterio, Daniel Bryan and Kane in a four-way.

Aug 2012


Air Date: 3rd August 2012
World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus downed Tensai; Antonio Cesaro bested WWE US Champion Santino Marella in a non-title match; Daniel Bryan, WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz and Dolph Ziggler downed Chris Jericho, Christian and Kane; Ryback bested Jinder Mahal by DQ; Darren Young defeated R-Truth; Randy Orton toppled Alberto Del Rio by DQ.

Air Date: 10th August 2012
Sin Cara beat Cody Rhodes; Christian downed Antonio Cesaro; Jinder Mahal won a handicap squash; Darren Young and Titus O’Neil bested Primo and Epico by DQ; Rey Mysterio toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz in a non-title match.

Air Date: 17th August 2012
Rey Mysterio and Sin Cara beat WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz and Cody Rhodes; Curt Hawkins and Tyler Reks won a squash; Eve Torres downed Kaitlyn; Randy Orton bested Daniel Bryan; Antonio Cesaro toppled Zack Ryder; Alberto Del Rio rolled over Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 24th August 2012
Ryback squashed Jinder Mahal; WWE Divas Champion Layla overcame Alicia Fox; Heath Slater toppled Sin Cara; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus bested Dolph Ziggler by DQ; WWE World Tag Champions Kofi Kingston and R-Truth overturned Primo and Epico; Alberto Del Rio rolled over Randy Orton.

Air Date: 31st August 2012
Rey Mysterio pinned Cody Rhodes;  Kaitlyn beat Natalya; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus overcame Damien Sandow by count-out; Darren Young and Titus O’Neil defeated Justin Gabriel and Tyson Kidd; Alberto Del Rio downed Kane; Randy Orton bested Dolph Ziggler.

Sep 2012

Air Date: 7th September 2012
Rey Mysterio and Sin Cara overturned The Miz and Cody Rhodes; Zack Ryder overcame Daniel Bryan by DQ; Randy Orton bested Damien Sandow by count-out; Darren Young and Titus O’Neil downed Primo and Epico; Wade Barrett toppled Yoshi Tatsu; Word Heavyweight Champion Sheamus rolled over David Otunga.

Air Date: 14th September 2012
Sin Cara bested WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz in a non-title match; Kane downed Kofi Kingston; Randy Orton dropped Tensai; Kaitlyn downed Beth Phoenix; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus bested Daniel Bryan.

Air Date: 21st September 2012
Damien Sandow beat Kane; Layla defeated Natalya; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus and Randy Orton bested Alberto Del Rio and Dolph Ziggler; Cody Rhodes toppled Daniel Bryan; Brodus Clay overcame Heath Slater by DQ; Santino Marella downed WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro in a non-title match; WWE World Tag Champions Kane and Daniel Bryan rolled over Damien Sandow and Cody Rhodes by DQ.

Air Date 28th September 2012
WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro defeated Santino Marella; Beth Phoenix downed Natalya; Wade Barrett dropped Zack Ryder; Cody Rhodes and Damien Sandow bested Jimmy and Jey Uso; Ryback pinned Tensai; The Big Show rolled over Randy Orton.

Oct 2012

Air Date: 5th October 2012
Titus O’Neil and Darren Young beat Kofi Kingston and R-Truth (WWE World Tag Title #1 Contender Tournament – First Round); Ryback squashed Primo; Wade Barrett defeated Brandon Groom; WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz overcame World Heavyweight Champion  Sheamus by DQ; WWE World Tag Champions Daniel Bryan and Kane downed Alberto Del Rio and David Otunga; Tensai toppled The Big Show by DQ; WWE World Champion CM Punk rolled over Dolph Ziggler.

Air Date: 12th October 2012
World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus toppled Tensai; Jinder Mahal and Heath Slater dropped Santino Marella and Zack Ryder; Kane toppled Dolph Ziggler by DQ; The Big Show bested Kofi Kingston; Sin Cara pinned Damien Sandow; Alberto Del Rio rolled over Daniel Bryan.

Air Date: 19th October 2012
Randy Orton bested The Miz; Cody Rhodes and Damien Sandow toppled Tyson Kidd and Justin Gabriel; WWE World Tag Champions Kane and Daniel Bryan bested The Big Show and Dolph Ziggler; WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro dropped Ted Dibiase Jr; Heath Slater overturned Brodus Clay; Layla beat Natalya; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus rolled over Wade Barrett in a lumberjack match.

Air Date: 26th October 2012
Kane pinned Cody Rhodes; The Miz beat Yoshi Tatsu; Wade Barrett bested Randy Orton; WWE Divas Champion Eve Torres and Aksana downed Kaitlyn and Layla; Damien Sandow toppled Daniel Bryan.

Nov 2012

Air Date: 2nd November 2012
Darren Young beat Sin Cara; Rey Mysterio downed Titus O’Neil; Wade Barrett bested Randy Orton; The Great Khali toppled David Otunga; R-Truth overcame Justin Gabriel; World Heavyweight Champion The Big Show and The Miz rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston and Sheamus.

Air Date: 6th November 2012
WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston bested The Miz; Titus O’Neil and Darren Young defeated Rey Mysterio and Sin Cara; World Heavyweight Champion The Big Show and Sheamus dropped Wade Barrett and William Regal; Randy Orton bested Alberto Del Rio in a “falls-count-anywhere” encounter.

Air Date: 16th November 2012
Damien Sandow beat WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston in a non-title match; Layla, Kaitlyn and Natalya defeated WWE Divas Champion Eve Torres, Aksana and Alicia Fox; Randy Orton and The Miz toppled Dolph Ziggler and Alberto Del Rio; Kane overturned Wade Barrett by DQ; WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro downed Sin Cara; World Heavyweight Champion The Big Show rolled over The Great Khali.

Air Date: 23rd November 2012
Ryback defeated Darren Young; R-Truth overcame WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro in a non-title match; Alberto Del Rio bested Sin Cara; World Heavyweight Champion The Big Show overcame WWE World Tag Champions Kane and Daniel Bryan in a non-title handicap match; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston toppled Damien Sandow; Dolph Ziggler pinned Randy Orton.

Air Date: 30th November 2012
John Cena dropped Alberto Del Rio; The Great Khali downed David Otunga; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston and WWE World Tag Champions Kane and Daniel Bryan toppled Wade Barrett, Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; Damien Sandow bested Tyson Kidd; Jinder Mahal and Heath Slater defeated Jimmy and Jey Uso; Sheamus rolled over Dolph Ziggler by DQ.

Dec 2012


Air Date: 7th December 2012
World Heavyweight Champion The Big Show defeated Daniel Bryan; Heath Slater, Jinder Mahal and Drew McIntyre downed Jimmy and Jey Uso, and Brodus Clay; Randy Orton toppled Wade Barrett; The Great Khali and Hornswoggle overcame Primo and Epico; Sheamus rolled over Alberto Del Rio.

Air Date: 14th December 2012
World Heavyweight Champion The Big Show pinned R-Truth; Damien Sandow and Cody Rhodes defeated Jimmy and Jey Uso; Kaitlyn downed Aksana; The Great Khali, Hornswoggle, Tyson Kidd and Justin Gabriel dropped Primo, Epico, Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston toppled Alberto Del Rio; WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro bested Sheamus by count-out.

Air Date: 18th December 2012
Damien Sandow defeated Sin Cara; Santino Marella beat Tensai; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston and WWE World Tag Champions Kane and Daniel Bryan overcame Wade Barrett, Titus O’Neill and Darren Young; Ryback dropped WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro in a non-title match; Kaitlyn bested WWE Divas Champion Eve Torres by DQ; Brodus Clay toppled Brad Maddox; Sheamus and John Cena rolled over Dolph Ziggler and World Heavyweight Champion The Big Show by DQ

Air Date: 28th December 2012
Brodus Clay bested Epico; Cody Rhodes, Damien Sandow and Wade Barrett overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston and WWE World Tag Champions Kane and Daniel Bryan; WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro downed Zack Ryder; Dolph Ziggler bested The Miz; Sheamus toppled Brad Maddox; Jimmy and Jey Uso overturned Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; World Heavyweight Champion The Big Show rolled over Alberto Del Rio by DQ.
