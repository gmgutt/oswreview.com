Raw Results 2007

WWE RAW RESULTS (2007)

Jan 2007


Air Date: 1st January 2007
Kevin Federline pinned WWE World Champion John Cena in a non-title, no-DQ encounter; Cryme Tyme and Robbie and Rory McAllister defeated Shelton Benjamin, Charlie Haas, Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch; WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Rob Conway; Melina overcame Maria; Kenny Dykstra and Chris Masters toppled Ric Flair and Carlito; Umaga, Armando Estrada, Jonathan Coachman and Johnny Nitro rolled over WWE World Champion John Cena by DQ in a handicap match.

Air Date: 8th January 2007
WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Kenny Dykstra; Melina and Victoria defeated WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James and Maria; “Donald Trump” pinned “Rosie O’Donnell”; Carlito beat Chris Masters; Cryme Tyme overcame Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch; The Great Khali toppled WWE World Champion John Cena by DQ.

Air Date: 15th January 2007
WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy and Maria defeated Johnny Nitro and Melina; Ric Flair bested Kenny Dykstra; WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James dropped Victoria; JTG pinned Shelton Benjamin; Shawn Michaels toppled WWE World Tag Champions Edge and Randy Orton by DQ in a non-title handicap match.

Air Date: 22nd January 2007
WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Joey Mercury; Super Crazy downed Chris Masters; The Great Khali won a battle royal; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Jonathan Coachman by DQ; WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James and Candice Michelle defeated Melina and Victoria; Kenny Dykstra overturned Carlito; Edge pinned Shawn Michaels in a streetfight.

Air Date: 29th January 2007
Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas defeated Cryme Tyme; Melina beat Maria; Umaga pinned Val Venis; Carlito and Super Crazy downed Kenny Dykstra and Chris Masters; The Great Khali toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy by count-out; WWE World Champion John Cena and Shawn Michaels toppled WWE World Tag Champions Edge and Randy Orton to win the titles.

Feb 2007

Air Date: 5th February 2007
Chris Masters, Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas defeated Carlito and Cryme Tyme; Umaga pinned Balls Mahoney; Super Crazy overcame Johnny Nitro; WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James bested Melina; WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Ric Flair; ECW Champion Bobby Lashley toppled Kenny Dykstra; Shawn Michaels toppled Edge and Randy Orton in a three-way.

Air Date: 15th February 2007
Johnny Nitro and Melina defeated Super Crazy and WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James; The Great Khali toppled Eugene; WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Chris Masters; Ric Flair bested Carlito; World Heavyweight Champion Batista, The Undertaker and WWE World Tag Champions John Cena and Shawn Michaels toppled Edge, Randy Orton, MVP, and Mr Kennedy.

Air Date: 19th February 2007
Umaga overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy to win the title; Ric Flair and Carlito beat Trevor Murdoch and Lance Cade; Edge pinned Rob Van Dam (MITB Qualifier); Melina beat WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James to win the title; The Great Khali toppled Robbie and Rory McAllister in a handicap match; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Randy Orton by DQ.

Air Date: 26th February 2007
Jeff Hardy downed Shelton Benjamin (MITB Qualifier); WWE Women’s Champion Melina pinned Maria; Carlito beat Kenny Dykstra; WWE World Tag Champions John Cena and Shawn Michaels toppled Edge and Randy Orton.

Mar 2007

Air Date: 5th March 2007 
Shawn Michaels toppled Randy Orton; WWE Intercontinental Champion Umaga pinned Jeff Hardy; Chris Masters toppled Jim Duggan in a “Masterlock” Challenge; WWE Women’s Champion Melina pinned Mickie James in a “falls count anywhere” match; Ric Flair battled Carlito to a no-contest (MITB Qualifier); WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Johnny Nitro by DQ.

Air Date: 12th March 2007
Randy Orton defeated Ric Flair and Carlito in a three-way elimination match (MITB Qualifier); WWE World Tag Champions John Cena and Shawn Michaels toppled Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas, Trevor Murdoch and Lance Cade, and Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury in a gauntlet match; Jeff Hardy battled The Great Khali to a no-contest; WWE Women’s Champion Melina pinned Torrie Wilson.

Air Date: 19th March 2007
ECW Champion Bobby Lashley toppled Chris Masters in a “Masterlock Challenge”; Edge won a 10-man battle royal (MITB Qualifier); WWE Intercontinental Champion Umaga pinned Eugene; WWE Women’s Champion Melina overcame Candice Michelle in a “bra and panties” match; Jeff Hardy downed Randy Orton by DQ; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over WWE US Champion Chris Benoit in a non-title match.

Air Date: 26th March 2007
Ashley, Torrie Wilson, and Candice Michelle beat WWE Women’s Champion Melina, Jillian Hall, and Victoria; CM Punk overturned Kenny Dykstra; Vince McMahon pinned ECW Champion Bobby Lashley in a non-title no-DQ match; Randy Orton and Mr Kennedy dropped Matt and Jeff Hardy; The Great Khali toppled Ric Flair by DQ; World Heavyweight Champion Batista and The Undertaker rolled over WWE World Champion John Cena and Shawn Michaels.

Apr 2007

Air Date: 2nd April 2007
WWE World Tag Champions John Cena and Shawn Michaels won a 9-team battle royal; Matt and Jeff Hardy won a 10-team battle royal to win the WWE World Tag Titles; Ric Flair and Carlito beat Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas; The Great Khali toppled Super Crazy; ECW Champion Bobby Lashley toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Umaga and Armando Allejandro Estrada in a handicap match.

Air Date: 9th April 2007
Mickie James and Candice Michelle defeated WWE Women’s Champion Melina and Victoria; Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch beat Ric Flair and Carlito; Shawn Michaels battled Randy Orton to a double-pinfall draw; WWE World Tag Champions Matt and Jeff Hardy downed Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas; ECW Champion Bobby Lashley toppled Shane McMahon by DQ in a “title vs. hair” match.

Air Date: 16th April 2007
Santino Marella beat WWE Intercontinental Champion Umaga in a “no-holds-barred” match to win the title; Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas defeated Ric Flair and Carlito; Johnny Nitro pinned Eugene; Lance Cade bested Jeff Hardy; Chris Masters toppled Super Crazy; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Edge and Randy Orton in a non-title handicap match.

Air Date: 23rd April  2007
Umaga, Vince McMahon and Shane McMahon defeated Robbie Brookside in a no-DQ handicap match; Trevor Murdoch pinned Matt Hardy; WWE Women’s Champion Melina beat Maria; The Great Khali toppled Carlito; Shawn Michaels toppled WWE World Champion John Cena in a non-title match.

Air Date: 30th April 2007
Edge pinned Randy Orton; Jeff Hardy downed Johnny Nitro; Umaga bested Rob Van Dam; Ric Flair and Carlito battled Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas to a no-contest; Mickie James and Candice Michelle defeated WWE Women’s Champion Melina and Victoria.

May 2007


Air Date: 7th May 2007
Mickie James and Candice Michelle defeated WWE Women’s Champion Melina and Victoria; WWE Intercontinental Champion Santino Marella pinned Chris Masters; Umaga beat Robbie and Rory McAllister in a handicap match; The Great Khali toppled Shawn Michaels in a no-DQ match by stoppage; WWE World Tag Champions Matt and Jeff Hardy downed Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas;  Edge pinned Mr Kennedy to win the “Money in the Bank” briefcase; WWE World Champion John Cena battled Randy Orton to a no-contest.

Air Date: 14th May 2007
Jeff Hardy overcame Trevor Murdoch; WWE Intercontinental Champion Santino Marella beat Chris Masters; Candice Michelle pinned Victoria; Bobby Lashley toppled Jonathan Coachman; Carlito beat Val Venis; Shawn Michaels toppled World Heavyweight Champion Edge in a non-title match.

Air Date: 21st May 2007
Ric Flair battled Carlito to a double count-out; Candice Michelle pinned WWE Women’s Champion Melina in a non-title match; Bobby Lashley toppled Chris Masters; WWE World Tag Champions Matt and Jeff Hardy downed Johnny Nitro and Kenny Dykstra; Bobby Lashley toppled Viscera; Bobby Lashley toppled Umaga by DQ; Bobby Lashley toppled Shane McMahon.

Air Date: 28th May 2007
Michelle McCool won a “bikini” battle royal; WWE World Tag Champions Matt and Jeff Hardy, and Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch defeated Shelton Benjamin, Charlie Haas, Johnny Nitro and Kenny Dykstra; Randy Orton bested Rob Van Dam by stoppage; Carlito and Victoria dropped Ric Flair and Torrie Wilson; The Great Khali, Umaga, and Shane McMahon toppled WWE World Champion John Cena and Bobby Lashley in a handicap match.

Jun 2007

Air Date: 4th June 2007
Candice Michelle and Cryme Tyme overcame WWE Women’s Champion Melina, Johnny Nitro and Kenny Dykstra; WWE Intercontinental Champion Santino Marella pinned Chris Masters; Randy Orton bested Ric Flair; Carlito beat Torrie Wilson; Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch defeated WWE World Tag Champions Matt and Jeff Hardy to win the titles; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over The Great Khali and Umaga in a three-way.

Air Date: 11th June 2007
World Heavyweight Champion Edge beat WWE World Champion John Cena by count-out; CM Punk overturned Carlito; Umaga pinned Balls Mahoney; ECW Champion Bobby Lashley toppled Chris Benoit; WWE US Champion MVP beat WWE Intercontinental Champion Santino Marella in a non-title match; The Miz defeated Snitsky by reverse decision-DQ; Candice Michelle overcame Kristal; Batista rolled over Elijah Burke and Jeff Hardy in a three-way; Randy Orton won a 15-man battle royal.

Air Date: 18th June 2007
Brian Kendrick and Paul London defeated Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas; Jeff Hardy downed Daivari; Mick Foley battled Umaga to a no-contest; WWE Women’s Champion Melina and Jillian Hall bested Candice Michelle and Mickie James; Randy Orton and King Booker toppled WWE World Champion John Cena and Bobby Lashley.

Air Date: 25th June 2007
Chris Benoit tribute show.

Jul 2007

Air Date: 2nd July 2007
Randy Orton pinned Jeff Hardy (Beat the Clock); Melina defeated Maria;Umaga beat WWE Intercontinental Champion Santino Marella to win the title; King Booker toppled Val Venis (Beat the Clock); Super Crazy downed Mr Kennedy; Carlito beat The Sandman by DQ; Bobby Lashley toppled Shelton Benjamin (Beat the Clock).

Air Date: 9th July 2007
Bobby Lashley toppled King Booker by DQ; WWE Intercontinental Champion Umaga pinned Santino Marella; WWE World Tag Champions Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch defeated Robbie and Rory McAllister; Snitsky bested Super Crazy; Paul London overturned Shelton Benjamin; WWE Women’s Champion Candice Michelle and Mickie James dropped Beth Phoenix and Jillian Hall; William Regal beat The Sandman by DQ; Bobby Lashley and Jeff Hardy rolled over King Booker and Mr Kennedy.

Air Date: 16th July 2007
Jeff Hardy downed William Regal, Santino Marella, and Shelton Benjamin  in a four-way elimination match; Snitsky pinned Val Venis; Randy Orton bested Cody Rhodes; Melina dropped Mickie James; The Sandman downed Carlito by DQ; Mr Kennedy dropped Super Crazy.

Air Date: 23rd July 2007
Mickie James and Maria defeated Melina and Beth Phoenix; Carlito and William Regal downed The Sandman and Jim Duggan; Randy Orton pinned Cody Rhodes; Jeff Hardy dropped Mr Kennedy by count-out; Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas bested Brian Kendrick and Paul London; WWE World Champion John Cena and WWE Women’s Champion Candice Michelle rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Umaga, and WWE World Tag Champions Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch in a no-DQ handicap match.

Air Date: 30th July 2007
Melina, Jillian Hall, and Beth Phoenix overcame WWE Women’s Champion Candice Michelle, Maria, and Mickie James; WWE Intercontinental Champion Umaga pinned Santino Marella; Cody Rhodes bested Daivari; Mr Kennedy dropped Bobby Lashley; Jerry Lawler toppled King Booker by DQ; Cryme Tyme overcame John Mason and Brandon Gatston; Randy Orton pinned Sgt Slaughter; Carlito beat WWE World Champion John Cena in a non-title match.

Aug 2007

Air Date: 6th August 2007
William Regal won a battle royal; Snitsky pinned Rory McAllister; Jillian Hall beat Mickie James; King Booker toppled Jerry Lawler; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Umaga by DQ.

Air Date: 13th August 2007
Mr Kennedy dropped The Sandman; WWE World Tag Champions Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch defeated Cryme Tyme by DQ; Snitsky pinned Robbie McAllister; Cody Rhodes bested Charlie Haas; WWE World Champion John Cena and WWE Intercontinental Champion Umaga rolled over Randy Orton and Carlito.

Air Date: 20th August 2007
WWE Women’s Champion Candice Michelle and Mickie James dropped Melina and Beth Phoenix; Cody Rhodes pinned Shelton Benjamin; Paul London and Brian Kendrick overcame WWE World Tag Champions Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch in a non-title match; Carlito battled Mr Kennedy to a double-pinfall draw; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Snitsky by DQ.

Air Date: 27th August 2007
Jeff Hardy downed Mr Kennedy by DQ; Cody Rhodes, Paul London and Brian Kendrick overcame Daivari, Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over King Booker by DQ.

Sep 2007

Air Date: 3rd September 2007
Jeff Hardy downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Umaga to win the title; Beth Phoenix pinned Maria; Paul London and Brian Kendrick overcame Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas; Mickie James and Cody Rhodes dropped Jillian Hall and Daivari; Triple H bested Carlito and Umaga in a handicap match by DQ;

Air Date: 10th September 2007
Paul London beat Lance Cade; Triple H pinned Shelton Benjamin; Mickie James dropped Jillian Hall; The Sandman downed Santino Marella by DQ; World Heavyweight Champion The Great Khali bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy in a non-title match.

Air Date: 17th September 2007
WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Shelton Benjamin; Jim Duggan defeated Daivari in a flag match; Triple H bested WWE World Tag Champions Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch in a non-title handicap match; WWE Women’s Champion Candice Michelle and Mickie James dropped Melina and Jillian Hall; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Santino Marella by DQ; Randy Orton toppled Mr Cena by DQ.

Air Date: 24th September 2007
Hardcore Holly dropped Cody Rhodes; Ron Simmons defeated Santino Marella by count-out; Vince McMahon and Carlito beat Triple H in a handicap cage match; Paul London and Brian Kendrick overcame WWE World Tag Champions Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch by DQ; Shelton Benjamin and Beth Phoenix beat WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy and WWE Women’s Champion Candice Michelle; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Jonathan Coachman in a non-title tables match.

Oct 2007

Air Date: 1st October 2007
WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy, Brian Kendrick and Paul London defeated Shelton Benjamin, and WWE World Tag Champions Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch; Hardcore Holly dropped Cody Rhodes; Vince McMahon downed Triple H by DQ. Carlito was guest referee; Mickie James dropped Melina; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Mr Kennedy.

Air Date: 8th October 2007
Candice Michelle, Maria, and Mickie James dropped WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix, Jillian Hall, and Melina; Snitsky downed Val Venis; Triple H bested WWE World Champion Randy Orton and Umaga by DQ in a handicap match; Robbie and Rory McAllister overturned Paul London and Brian Kendrick; WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Mr Kennedy.

Air Date: 15th October 2007
WWE World Champion Randy Orton pinned WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy in a non-title match; Santino Marella bested Val Venis; Mr Kennedy dropped WWE World Champion Randy Orton by DQ; Umaga overcame Andy Simmons in a streetfight; Umaga  defeated Andy Simmons in a “first blood” match; Umaga toppled Andy Simmons in a cage match; Candice Michelle beat Jillian Hall; Rory McAllister overcame Brian Kendrick; Hardcore Holly dropped Cody Rhodes; Shawn Michaels toppled WWE World Champion Randy Orton by DQ.

Air Date: 22nd October 2007
Mickie James, Paul London and Brian Kendrick overcame Melina, and WWE World Tag Champions Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch; Cody Rhodes bested Shelton Benjamin; DH Smith pinned Carlito; Hornswoggle battled Umaga to a no-contest; Ron Simmons defeated Santino Marella by DQ; WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix defeated Candice Michelle in a “best of three falls” encounter; Shawn Michaels and WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed WWE World Champion Randy Orton and Mr Kennedy.

Air Date: 29th October 2007
Kelly Kelly won a battle royal; Hardcore Holly and Cody Rhodes bested Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas; Hornswoggle pinned Jonathan Coachman. Mick Foley was guest referee; Lance Cade downed Paul London; WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy and DH Smith toppled Mr Kennedy and Carlito; WWE World Champion Randy Orton and Umaga rolled over Triple H by DQ in a handicap match.

Nov 2007

Air Date: 5th November 2007
WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix overturned Kelly Kelly; Snitsky downed Joey Munoz; Rey Mysterio and WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Mr Kennedy and Finlay; Cody Rhodes  pinned Trevor Murdoch; Jim Duggan and Super Crazy dropped Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas; Shawn Michaels and Triple H rolled over WWE World Champion Randy Orton and Umaga

Air Date: 12th November 2007
World Heavyweight Champion Batista and The Undertaker rolled over WWE World Tag Champions Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch in a non-title bout; WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix overturned Maria; Rey Mysterio, WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy, and Matt Hardy downed Finlay, and WWE US Champion MVP, and Mr Kennedy; Mickie James, Kelly Kelly, and Michelle McCool beat Melina, Layla, and Jillian Hall; Jerry Lawler pinned Santino Marella; Triple H rolled over Umaga by DQ in a lumberjack match.

Air Date: 19th November 2007
Jerry Lawler beat Santino Marella; WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Umaga by DQ; Cody Rhodes bested Hardcore Holly; Maria and Mickie James dropped Melina and Jillian Hall; Hornswoggle toppled Carlito in a no-DQ match.

Air Date: 26th November 2007
Triple H and WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Umaga and Snitsky; Hardcore Holly, Cody Rhodes, Jim Duggan, and Super Crazy downed WWE World Tag Champions Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch, and Robbie and Rory McAllister; Chris Jericho pinned Santino Marella; Mr Kennedy dropped Brian Kendrick; Mickie James dropped Melina; Ric Flair bested WWE World Champion Randy Orton in a non-title match.

Dec 2007

Air Date: 3rd December 2007
WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix and Melina defeated Maria and Mickie James; Chris Jericho defeated Umaga by DQ; Hornswoggle toppled Carlito and Jonathan Coachman in a no-DQ handicap match; WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Snitsky; WWE World Champion Randy Orton bested Shawn Michaels by count-out.

Air Date: 10th December 2007
WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Carlito in a ladder match; Rob Van Dam downed Santino Marella; World Heavyweight Champion Batista, Triple H, and Ric Flair rolled over WWE World Champion Randy Orton, Edge, and Umaga by DQ; The Great Khali battled Hornswoggle to a no-contest; IRS won a battle royal; Hardcore Holly and Cody Rhodes bested WWE World Tag Champions Trevor Murdoch and Lance Cade to win the titles; Mr Kennedy dropped Marty Jannetty.

Air Date: 17th December 2007
Mickie James, Michelle McCool, Kelly Kelly, and Maria defeated Melina, Jillian Hall, Victoria, and Layla; Triple H rolled over William Regal and Jonathan Coachman in a handicap match; WWE World Tag Champions Hardcore Holly and Cody Rhodes bested Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch; Ric Flair bested Umaga by count-out; Santino Marella and Carlito beat Brian Kendrick and Paul London; Shawn Michaels and WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed WWE World Champion Randy Orton and Mr Kennedy.

Air Date: 24th December 2007
Chris Jericho defeated WWE World Champion Randy Orton by DQ; WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Carlito; Mickie James and Maria battled Kelly Kelly and Layla to a no-contest; Rey Mysterio toppled Mark Henry; Triple H and Shawn Michaels toppled Umaga and Mr Kennedy.

Air Date: 31st December 2007
Mr Kennedy dropped Shawn Michaels; Umaga pinned Jim Duggan; WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix overturned Melina and Mickie James in a three-way; Hornswoggle battled William Regal to a no-contest; WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy battled Santino Marella to a no-contest; Ric Flair bested Triple H by DQ.
