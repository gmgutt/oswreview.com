WWE Cyber Sunday Results

WWE CYBER SUNDAY RESULTS

5th November 2006: “WWE Cyber Sunday 2006”: Cincinatti, OH – 7,000
($400,000 live gate)
Dark match: Super Crazy downed Rob Conway.
PPV Broadcast: Umaga bested Kane; Cryme Tyme overcame Robbie and Rory McAllister, Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch, and Viscera and Charlie Haas in a four-way  “Texas Tornado” match; WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Carlito; Edge and Randy Orton beat Triple H and Shawn Michaels. Eric Bischoff was guest referee; Lita dropped Mickie James in a lumberjill match to win the vacant WWE Women’s Title (WWE Women’s Title Tournament – Final); Ric Flair and Roddy Piper overturned WWE World Tag Champions Kenny and Mikey to win the titles; World Heavyweight Champion King Booker rolled over WWE World Champion John Cena and ECW World Champion The Big Show in a no-DQ, three-way match.

28th October 2007: “WWE Cyber Sunday 2007”: Washington, DC – 10,097
($594,000 live gate)
Dark match: Jesse and Festus overturned Deuce and Domino.
PPV Broadcast: Rey Mysterio toppled Finlay in a stretcher match; ECW Champion CM Punk overturned The Miz; Mr Kennedy dropped WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy in a non-title match; Kane bested WWE US Champion MVP by count-out; Shawn Michaels toppled WWE World Champion Randy Orton by DQ; Triple H pinned Umaga in a streetfight; World Heavyweight Champion Batista rolled over The Undertaker. Steve Austin was guest referee.

26th October 2008: “WWE Cyber Sunday 2008”: Phoenix, AZ – 7,981 (78% full)
($449,465 live gate)
Pre-show: WWE US Champion Shelton Benjamin beat R-Truth.
PPV Broadcast: Rey Mysterio defeated Kane in a “No Holds Barred”  encounter; ECW Champion Matt Hardy pinned Evan Bourne; The Miz and John Morrison downed Cryme Tyme; The Honkytonk Man dropped WWE Intercontinental Champion Santino Marella by DQ; The Undertaker toppled The Big Show in a “last man standing” match; WWE World Champion Triple H bested Jeff Hardy; Batista rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Chris Jericho to win the title. Steve Austin was guest referee.
