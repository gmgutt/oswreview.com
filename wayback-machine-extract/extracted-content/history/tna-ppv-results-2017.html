Impact Wrestling PPV Results 2017

IMPACT WRESTLING PPV RESULTS 2017

6th January 2017: “TNA One Night Only: Live 2017”: Orlando, FL
Shown live on PPV
Dark match: Jade downed Laurel Van Ness.
Impact: (01/12): Abyss and Crazzy Steve battled Bram and Kingston to a double count-out.
Impact: (01/19): Aron Rex and Rockstar Spud defeated Robbie E and Swoggle.
PPV Broadcast: Bobby Lashley toppled Davey Richards; TNA X-Division Champion DJ Z defeated Andrew Everett, Braxton Sutter and Trevor Lee in a four-way elimination match; Bram defeated Robbie E; TNA Impact Grand Champion Moose downed Mike Bennett in a “No Holds Barred” match; Mahabali Shera beat Marshe Rockett; TNA Knockouts Champion Rosemary defeated Sienna; James Storm defeated Jessie Godderz; TNA World Tag Champions Brother Nero and Matt Hardy defeated Eli Drake and Tyrus; TNA World Heavyweight Champion Eddie Edwards defeated Ethan Carter III

10th February 2017: “TNA One Night Only: Joker’s Wild 2017”: Orlando, FL
Taped on 01/07/2017
PPV Broadcast: Davey Richards and Moose downed Matt Hardy and Trevor Lee (Joker’s Wild 2017 – 1st Round); Aron Rex and Rockstar Spud defeated Bad Bones and Jessie Godderz (Joker’s Wild 2017 – 1st Round); Bram and Kingston overcame Mahabali Shera and Robbie E (Joker’s Wild 2017 – 1st Round); Braxton Sutter and Mike Bennett defeated Crazzy Steve and Tyrus (Joker’s Wild 2017 – 1st Round); Brother Nero and DJ Z defeated Eli Drake and Paredyse (Joker’s Wild 2017 – 1st Round); Jade and Sienna beat Allie and Laurel Van Ness (Joker’s Wild 2017 – 1st Round); Caleb Konley and Eddie Edwards overturned Andrew Everett and Marshe Rockett (Joker’s Wild 2017 – 1st Round); Moose won the Joker’s Wild gauntlet battle royal.

17th March 2017: “TNA One Night Only: Rivals 2017”: Orlando, FL
Taped on 01/08/2017
PPV Broadcast: DJ Z defeated Trevor Lee; Braxton Sutter rolled over Rockstar Spud; James Storm defeated Mahabali Shera; Aron Rex defeated Jessie Godderz; Abyss and Crazzy Steve downed Bram and Kingston; Angelina Love downed Madison Rayne; Matt Hardy toppled TNA Impact Grand Champion Drew Galloway; Moose downed Mike Bennett; TNA World Heavyweight Champion Bobby Lashley toppled Jeff Hardy.

14th April 2017: “Impact Wrestling One Night Only: Victory Road – Knockouts Knockdown”: Orlando, FL
Taped on 03/04/2017
Dark matches: Garza Jr beat Mario Bokara; Adam Thornstowe defeated Bram.
PPV Broadcast: Angelina Love downed Kayci Quinn; Leva Bates overturned Allie; Diamanté defeated Amanda Carolina Rodriguez; Impact Wrestling Knockouts Champion Rosemary toppled MJ Jenkins; Alisha Edwards overturned Sienna; Santana Garrett defeated Brandi Rhodes; ODB defeated Rebel; Laurel Van Ness overturned Rachael Ellering; Leva Bates, Alisha Edwards, Santana Garrett and ODB defeated Laurel Van Ness, Impact Wrestling Knockouts Champion Rosemary, Angelina Love and Diamanté.

11th May 2017: “Impact Wrestling One Night Only: Turning Point 2017”: Orlando, FL
Taped on 04/22/2017
Dark matches: Mahabali Shera beat Marshe Rockett by DQ; Dezmond Xavier defeated Idris Abraham; KM downed Joe Coleman.
PPV Broadcast: Mayweather and Wilcox bested Fallah Bahh and Mario Bokara; Laurel Van Ness beat Ava Stone, Mahabali Shera defeated Marshe Rockett; Matt Morgan beat KM; Davey Richards overcame Suicide; Impact Knockouts Champion Rosemary toppled Sienna; Eddie Edwards bested Eli Drake; Impact World Champion Bobby Lashley pinned Impact Grand Champion Moose.

16th June 2017: “Impact Wrestling One Night Only: No Surrender 2017”: Orlando, FL
Taped on 04/23/2017
Dark matches: Mahabali Shera beat QT Marshall; Marshe Rockett defeated Chris Silvio.
PPV Broadcast: Garza Jr and Laredo Kid downed Idris Abraham and Hakim Zane; Mahabali Shera defeated Fallah Boa; Trevor Lee beat Suicide; Braxton Sutter overcame KM; Dezmond Xavier toppled Mario Bokara; TNA Knockouts Champion Rosemary bested Laurel Van Ness; Eli Drake defeated James Storm; James Storm downed Eli Drake;  Impact World Champion Bobby Lashley rolled over Eddie Edwards.
