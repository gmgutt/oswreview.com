Smackdown Results 2014

WWE SMACKDOWN RESULTS (2014)

Jan 2014

Air Date: 3rd January 2014
Jimmy and Jey Uso bested Seth Rolling and Roman Reigns by DQ; R-Truth beat Fandango; WWE World Tag Champions Goldust and Cody Rhodes overturned Erick Rowan and Luke Harper; Nikki Bella defeated Aksana; WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E Langston rolled over Curtis Axel; Jimmy and Jey Uso, and CM Punk toppled Seth Rollins, Roman Reigns and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose.

Air Date: 10th January 2014
Jimmy and Jey Uso battled to a double count-out with Luke Harper and Erick Rowan; Rey Mysterio, Sin Cara and Los Matadores overturned Jack Swagger, Antonio Cesaro, Curtis Axel and Ryback; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E Langston in a non-title match; Xavier Woods overturned Fandango; Seth Rollins, Roman Reigns and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose downed CM Punk, Road Dogg and Billy Gunn.

Air Date: 17th January 2014
Road Dogg and Billy Gunn dropped WWE World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Goldust in a non-title match; Rey Mysterio bested Alberto Del Rio; Naomi downed Tamina Snuka; WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E Langson dropped Fandango; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso bested Jack Swagger and Antonio Cesaro.

Air Date: 24th January 2014
Rey Mysterio and The Big Show dropped Jack Swagger and Antonio Cesaro; The Miz defeated Brodus Clay; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee downed Cameron; Curtis Axel and Ryback bested Los Matadores; Luke Harper and Erick Rowan overturned Darren Young and Titus O’Neill; Kofi Kingston toppled Fandango; Road Dogg, Billy Gunn, Seth Rollins, Roman Reigns and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose.battled WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E Langston, Jimmy Uso, Jey Uso and WWE World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Goldust to a no-contest.

Air Date: 31st January 2014
Antonio Cesaro downed Dolph Ziggler (Elimination Chamber Qualifier); Fandango dropped Xavier Woods; Ryback and Curtis Axel defeated Darren Young and Titus O’Neill; Christian bested Jack Swagger (Elimination Chamber Qualifier); Kofi Kingston toppled Damien Sandow; Cody Rhodes beat Road Dogg; Roman Reigns, Seth Rollins and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose rolled over Daniel Bryan, Sheamus and Rey Mysterio.

 

Feb 2014

Air Date: 7th February 2014
Roman Reigns and Dean Ambrose bested Kofi Kingston and Dolph Ziggler; Sheamus pinned Ryback; Daniel Bryan bested Antonio Cesaro; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee overturned Nikki Bella; Bray Wyatt topped Goldust; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton rolled over Christian.
Air Date: 14th February 2014
Seth Rollins, Roman Reigns and Dean Ambrose beat Sheamus, Christian and Daniel Bryan; Jack Swagger won a four-way over Rey Mysterio, Mark Henry and Kofi Kingston; Jimmy Uso, Jey Uso, Cody Rhodes and Goldust bested WWE World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn, Curtis Axel and Ryback; Darren Young toppled Damien Sandow; The Miz overcame Fandango; Cesaro rolled over WWE World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton in a non-title match.
Air Date: 21st February 2014
Daniel Bryan rolled over Jack Swagger; Daniel Bryan rolled over Cesaro by DQ; Bray Wyatt, Erick Rowan and Luke Harper overcame Rey Mysterio, Cody Rhodes and Goldust; Dolph Ziggler overcame Titus O’Neil; Jimmy Uso dropped Road Dogg; Sheamus toppled Christian.
Air Date: 28th February 2014
Jack Swagger and Cesaro dropped Mark Henry and WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E; Sheamus toppled Alberto Del Rio by DQ; Batista beat Dolph Ziggler; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee defeated Cameron; Daniel Bryan, Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso toppled Kane, Road Dogg and Billy Gunn.

Mar 2014

Air Date: 7th March 2014
Christian toppled Dolph Ziggler; WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso overturned Ryback and Curtis Axel; Natalya and Eva Marie downed WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee and Tamina Snuka; Sheamus overturned Alberto Del Rio; WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E downed Jack Swagger; Daniel Bryan and The Big Show beat Batista and Kane.

Air Date: 14th March 2014
Seth Rollins overturned Damien Sandow; WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E downed Fandango; Goldust and Cody Rhodes overturned Jack Swagger and Cesaro; Bray Wyatt rolled over Kofi Kingston; Nikki Bella defeated Tamina Snuka; Dolph Ziggler bested Alberto Del Rio; The Big Show beat Kane.

Air Date: 21st March 2014
Fernando overcame Fandango; Dolph Ziggler bested Damien Sandow; A four-way between Dean Ambrose and Seth Rollins, Jinder Mahal and Drew McIntyre, Ryback and Curtis Axel, and Jack Swagger and Cesaro ended in a no contest; Alberto Del Rio overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E; The Bella Twins overturned Natalya and Summer Rae; John Cena defeated Luke Harper.

Air Date: 28th March 2014
Seth Rollins and Dean Ambrose downed Jinder Mahal and Drew McIntyre; Seth Rollins and Dean Ambrose downed Ryback and Curtis Axel; Sin Cara defeated Damien Sandow; Fandango overcame Goldust; Sheamus overturned Batista by DQ; The Bella Twins overturned WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee and Tamina Snuka; Jack Swagger bested Jimmy Uso; Mark Henry rolled over The Miz; Bray Wyatt rolled over The Big Show.

Apr 2014

Air Date: 4th April 2014
Dolph Ziggler bested Titus O’Neil; Jack Swagger and Cesaro overcame Los Matadores.

Air Date: 11th April 2014
Cesaro overcame The Big Show by DQ; Ryback and Curtis Axel beat Los Matadores; Rob Van Dam beat Damien Sandow; Wade Barrett rolled over Kofi Kingston; Fandango overcame Santino Marella; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan and WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso battled Kane, Randy Orton and Batista.

Air Date: 18th April 2014
WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E downed Alberto Del Rio by DQ; WWE Divas Champion Paige downed Aksana; El Torito overcame Hornswoggle; Alexander Rusev beat R-Truth; Santino Marella defeated Fandango; Batista defeated Sheamus.

Air Date: 25th April 2014
Cesaro overcame Jack Swagger; Tamina Snuka defeated Natalya; Curtis Axel beat Jey Uso; Luke Harper and Erick Rowan toppled Cody Rhodes and Goldust; Roman Reigns, Seth Rollins and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose downed Ryback, Titus O’Neil,  Damien Sandow, Alberto Del Rio and Wade Barrett in a handicap match.

May 2014

Air Date: 2nd May 2014
Bray Wyatt, Erick Rowan and Luke Harper bested WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso, and Sheamus; Rob Van Dam beat Jack Swagger; Alexander Rusev beat R-Truth by DQ; Dolph Ziggler bested Damien Sandow; WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E downed Titus O’Neil by DQ; WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose won a four-way over Curtis Axel, Ryback and Alberto Del Rio.

Air Date: 9th May 2014
WWE US Champion Sheamus overturned Dean Ambrose; Wade Barrett and Cesaro overcame Rob Van Dam and Big E; Alexander Rusev beat Kofi Kingston; Fandango and Layla defeated Santino Marella and Emma; Roman Reigns overturned Mark Henry; Los Matadores and El Torito overcame Heath Slater and Drew McIntyre and Hornswoggle; Batista defeated Seth Rollins by count-out; Bray Wyatt, Erick Rowan and Luke Harper bested John Cena and WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso.

Air Date: 16th May 2014
WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso overturned Cody Rhodes and Goldust; El Torito overcame Heath Slater; Nikki Bella defeated Natalya. Eva Marie was guest referee; Dolph Ziggler bested Batista by DQ; WWE US Champion Sheamus overturned Titus O’Neil; Santino Marella defeated Damien Sandow; Cesaro overcame R-Truth; John Cena defeated Erick Rowan.

Air Date: 23rd May 2014
Batista defeated Dolph Ziggler in a no-DQ match; Naomi and Cameron toppled Nikki Bella and Eva Marie. Summer Raw was guest referee; Bo Dallas overturned Sin Cara; WWE US Champion Sheamus overturned Alberto Del Rio; WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso overturned Erick Rowan and Luke Harper.

Air Date: 30th May 2014
Cesaro overcame Kofi Kingston; WWE Divas Champion Paige downed Tamina Snuka; Bo Dallas overturned Xavier Woods; El Torito overcame Jinder Mahal; WWE US Champion Sheamus overturned Wade Barrett; Big E downed Titus O’Neil; Adam Rose downed Jack Swagger; Bray Wyatt rolled over Jimmy Uso in a “Last Man Standing” match.

Air Date: 13th June 2014

Air Date: 20th June 2014
Seth Rollins overturned Kofi KingstonDolph Ziggler bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Bad News Barrett in a non-title match; Adam Rose defeated Titus O’Neil; Adam Rose defeated Titus O’Neil; Kane toppled Dean Ambrose; Big E defeated Jack Swagger; Bo Dallas overturned Fandango; John Cena, WWE US Champion Sheamus and Roman Reigns overturned Cesaro, Alberto Del Rio, Randy Orton and Bray Wyatt in a handicap match.

Air Date: 27th June 2014
WWE US Champion Sheamus overturned Bray Wyatt by DQ; Dean Ambrose defeated WWE Intercontinental Champion Bad News Barrett in a non-title match; WWE Divas Champion Paige defeated Cameron; Cesaro and Alberto Del Rio dropped Dolph Ziggler and Rob Van Dam; Rusev beat Sin Cara; Roman Reigns overturned Kane by DQ.
