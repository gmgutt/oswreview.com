Smackdown Results 2001

WWF SMACKDOWN RESULTS (2001)

Jan 2001


Air Date: 4th January 2001
Chris Jericho dropped WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit by DQ; The Dudley Boys overturned Bull Buchanon and The Goodfather; WWF World Champion Kurt Angle toppled The Rock by DQ; Perry Saturn and Terri defeated Matt Hardy and Lita; Val Venis beat Billy Gunn; Rikishi pinned Test; Steve Austin won a three-way over The Undertaker and Kane.

Air Date: 11th January 2001
The Goodfather, Bull Buchanan, and Val Venis defeated Billy Gunn, Matt Hardy, and Jeff Hardy; Test downed K-Kwik; Chris Jericho and The Dudley Boys battled WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit and WWF World Tag Champions Edge, and Christian to a no-contest; Rikishi overcame Kane, The Undertaker, and The Rock in a four-way.

Air Date: 18th January 2001
Chris Jericho battled Perry Saturn to a no-contest in a ladder match; Bubba Ray Dudley overcame Edge; The Acolytes and Jackie overturned Lita, Matt, and Jeff Hardy; Val Venis toppled Test; Steve Blackman dropped Hardcore Holly in a hardcore match; Steve Austin, The Undertaker, and The Rock rolled over WWF World Champion Kurt Angle, Kane, and Rikishi. Triple H was guest referee.

Air Date: 25th January 2001
The Undertaker bested Rikishi by DQ; Jeff Hardy and Bradshaw defeated Matt Hardy and Farooq; The Big Show downed WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho by DQ; Tazz dropped K-Kwik; WWF European Champion Test toppled Christian; Crash and Hardcore Holly beat The Goodfather and Bull Buchanan; WWF World Champion Kurt Angle rolled over Kane.

Feb 2001

Air Date: 1st February 2001
WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys dropped The Hardy Boys in a tables match; WWF Women’s Champion Ivory bested Lita and Jackie in a three-way; The Acolytes overturned Edge and Christian; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho toppled Tazz; The Big Show pinned Billy Gunn; The Undertaker and Kane rolled over Rikishi and Haku in a “First Blood” match; WWF World Champion Kurt Angle defeated The Rock and Triple H in a three-way.

Air Date: 8th February 2001
Chris Benoit and Perry Saturn defeated The Hardy Boys; Rikishi downed Al Snow; Edge and Christian dropped WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys by DQ; Hardcore Holly pinned WWF Hardcore Champion Raven to win the title in a hardcore match; Steven Richards, Bull Buchanan, The Goodfather, and WWF Women’s Champion Ivory overcame The Acolytes, Jacqueline, and The Kat; Triple H bested WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho in a non-title match; The Rock rolled over The Big Show.

Air Date: 15th February 2001
Kane toppled Edge; Matt Hardy overcame Perry Saturn; Steve Austin pinned Chris Benoit; X-Pac downed WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho by DQ; WWF Hardcore Champion Raven battled The Big Show to a no-contest; The Rock battled Triple H to a no-contest.

Air Date: 22nd February 2001
Rikishi pinned Jeff Hardy; The Big Show defeated Crash Holly, Perry Saturn,  Steve Blackman, Hardcore Holly, and K-Kwik in a six-way hardcore match; Chris Benoit and Eddie Guerrero bested WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho and X-Pac; WWF Women’s Champion Ivory battled The Kat to a no-contest; WWF European Champion Test toppled William Regal; WWF World Champion Kurt Angle, Edge, and Christian rolled over The Rock, The Undertaker, and Kane in a no-DQ match.

Mar 2001

Air Date: 1st March 2001
Matt Hardy and Lita defeated Crash and Molly Holly; Chris Benoit downed X-Pac; WWF World Champion The Rock rolled over William Regal by DQ; Kane, The Undertaker, and D-Von Dudley bested Rikishi, Haku, and Christian; Steve Austin battled Kurt Angle to a no-contest.

Air Date: 8th March 2001
WWF World Tag Champions The Hardy Boys beat The Dudley Boys; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho bested Val Venis; Perry Saturn, Eddie Guerrero, and Dean Malenko defeated Chris Benoit in a gauntlet match; Kurt Angle toppled Test; William Regal overcame Al Snow. By virtue of the win, Regal was appointed the new commissioner of The WWF; WWF World Champion The Rock rolled over Rikishi and Haku in a handicap match.

Air Date: 15th March 2001
WWF European Champion Test overcame Eddie Guerrero; The Hardy Boys and Billy Gunn battled Albert, Justin Credible, and X-Pac to a double-DQ; The Dudley Boys overturned WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho in a handicap tables match; Brian Christopher and Steve Blackman bested The Goodfather and Bull Buchanon; Triple H beat Kane; Steve Austin bested Kurt Angle in a no-DQ match.

Air Date: 22nd March 2001
WWF Hardcore Champion Raven battled WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho to a no-contest; X-Pac, Justin Credible, and Albert overcame Brian Christopher, Steve Blackman, and K-Kwik; The Big Show and Triple H  beat Kane in a handicap match; WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys and Spike Dudley beat Edge, Christian, and Rhyno by DQ; WWF World Champion The Rock rolled over Chris Benoit and Kurt Angle in handicap match.

Air Date: 29th March 2001
X-Pac and Justin Credible beat The Acolytes; Chris Benoit and WWF European Champion Test overcame Kurt Angle and Eddie Guerrero; Rhyno dropped Matt Hardy; Trish Stratus battled WWF Women’s Champion Ivory to a no-contest in a “Bra and Panties” match; William Regal and The Big Show toppled WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho and Kane.

Apr 2001

Air Date: 5th April 2001
The Undertaker and WWF Hardcore Champion Kane toppled Steven Richards, Bull Buchanon, and The Goodfather in a handicap match; Rhyno overturned K-Kwik; Kurt Angle and William Regal overcame Chris Benoit in a handicap match; Jeff Hardy beat The Big Show; X-Pac, Justin Credible, and Albert overcame Billy Gunn, Steve Blackman, and Brian Christopher; Triple H rolled over WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho to win the title.

Air Date: 12th April 2001
Albert beat Spike Dudley; WWF Women’s Champion Chyna pinned Ivory; WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian, Kurt Angle, and William Regal overcame Chris Benoit and Chris Jericho in a handicap match; Rhyno battled Hardcore Holly to a no-contest; The Undertaker and WWF Hardcore Champion Kane toppled The Big Show, Sho Funaki, and Taka Michinoku in a handicap match; Test overcame Val Venis; Jeff Hardy beat WWF Intercontinental Champion Triple H to win the title.

Air Date: 19th April 2001
Rhyno overturned WWF Hardcore Champion Kane to win the title; The Acolytes, Sho Funaki, and Taka Michinoku defeated Steven Richards, Val Venis, The Goodfather, and Bull Buchanon; WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero overcame Brian Christopher; The Dudley Boys overturned Chris Jericho and Chris Benoit in a tables match; WWF Women’s Champion Chyna pinned Molly Holly; The Undertaker and Kane toppled WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian in a no-DQ match to win the titles.

Air Date: 26th April 2001
William Regal and Kurt Angle toppled Chris Jericho and Chris Benoit in a “Duchess of Queensbury rules” match;  Matt Hardy beat WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero to win the title; Kane toppled Edge and Christian by DQ in a handicap match; WWF Hardcore Champion Rhyno overturned Test; The Undertaker bested Steven Richards, Bull Buchanon, The Goodfather, and Val Venis in a handicap match; WWF World Champion Steve Austin and WWF Intercontinental Champion Triple H battled Sho Funaki and Taka Michinoku to a no-contest.

May 2001

Air Date: 3rd May 2001
WWF Intercontinental Champion Triple H dropped Jeff Hardy; Raven bested X-Pac; Hardcore Holly downed Kurt Angle; Chris Jericho and Matt Hardy beat Edge, Christian, and Rhyno in a handicap match; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Jerry Lynn toppled Brian Christopher; The Undertaker battled Rikishi to a no-contest.

Air Date: 10th May 2001
Christian beat Chris Jericho; Albert defeated Raven; Lita and The Hardy Boys downed The Goodfather, Bull Buchanon, and Ivory; Rikishi dropped WWF Intercontinental Champion Triple H by DQ; Spike Dudley overcame Crash Holly; Edge toppled Chris Benoit; WWF Hardcore Champion Rhyno overturned Bradshaw.

Air Date: 17th May 2001
Eddie Guerrero and The Hardy Boys beat Perry Saturn, Dean Malenko, and Jerry Lynn; Bradshaw defeated The Big Show; Chris Benoit and Rikishi downed Kurt Angle and William Regal; WWF Women’s Champion Chyna dropped Molly Holly and Ivory in a non-title handicap match; Edge toppled Chris Jericho; Hardcore Holly overcame Spike Dudley; WWF Intercontinental Champion Triple H battled The Undertaker to a no-contest in a “no holds barred” match.

Air Date: 24th May 2001
X-Pac defeated Eddie Guerrero; Kurt Angle toppled Spike Dudley; Dean Malenko beat Raven; Trish Stratus, Steve Blackman, and Brian Christopher dropped Ivory, The Goodfather, and Bull Buchanon; WWF Intercontinental Champion Kane toppled Rhyno; WWF World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and Chris Benoit toppled The Hardy Boys, The Dudley Boys, and Edge and Christian in a “TLC” encounter.

Air Date: 31st May 2001
WWF Intercontinental Champion Kane toppled Edge; Hardcore Holly downed Bubba Ray Dudley; Chris Jericho bested Kurt Angle; WWF Hardcore Champion Rhyno overturned Raven; X-Pac, Albert, and Justin Credible toppled Eddie Guerrero and The Hardy Boys; WWF World Champion Steve Austin rolled over Chris Benoit.

Jun 2001

Air Date: 7th June 2001
Kurt Angle toppled Hardcore Holly (KOTR Qualifier); Jeff Hardy beat WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Jerry Lynn to win the title; WWF Intercontinental Champion Kane toppled X-Pac; The Dudley Boys overturned Dean Malenko and Perry Saturn; Edge overcame Test (KOTR Qualifier); WWF World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and Chris Benoit toppled The Acolytes; WWF World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and Chris Benoit toppled The Big Show and Rhyno by DQ; WWF World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and Chris Benoit toppled WWF World Champion Steve Austin and Vince McMahon.

Air Date: 14th June 2001
WWF World Tag Champions Chris Benoit and Chris Jericho bested Edge and Christian; The Big Show toppled Raven (KOTR Qualifier); WWF European Champion Matt Hardy beat K-Kwik; Kurt Angle toppled The Undertaker by DQ; Test overcame WWF Hardcore Champion Rhyno to win the title; Tajiri beat Crash Holly (KOTR Qualifier); WWF Intercontinental Champion Kane toppled Albert; WWF World Champion Steve Austin bested Spike Dudley.

Air Date: 21st June 2001
The Big Show toppled Spike Dudley; WWF Intercontinental Champion Kane and The Hardy Boys beat Albert, X-Pac, and Justin Credible; Kurt Angle and Rhyno overturned Edge and Christian; WWF Hardcore Champion Test overcame Perry Saturn; Tajiri beat Taka Michinoku; The Dudley Boys overturned WWF World Tag Champions Chris Benoit and Chris Jericho to win the titles.

Air Date: 28th June 2001
Edge and Christian downed WWF Light Heavyweight Champion X-Pac and Billy Gunn; Albert overcame WWF Intercontinental Champion Kane to win the title in a no-DQ match; WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys battled The Hardy Boys to a no-contest in a tables match; Spike Dudley and Molly Holly downed Crash Holly and Jacquelyn; The Big Show beat Perry Saturn; Chris Jericho and Scotty 2 Hotty toppled William Regal and Tajiri.

Jul 2001

Air Date: 5th July 2001
WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys overturned Chris Jericho and Spike Dudley; Jacquelyn toppled Crash Holly; Billy Kidman bested WCW Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms to win the title; Billy Gunn and The Big Show toppled WWF European Champion Matt Hardy and Jeff Hardy; WWF Intercontinental Champion Albert overcame Edge; Scotty 2 Hotty beat Dean Malenko; WCW World Champion Booker T overturned Diamond Dallas Page.

Air Date: 12th July 2001
Tazz defeated Hardcore Holly; Chris Jericho bested Lance Storm; Rhyno overturned Kane; Lita beat Trish Stratus; Jeff Hardy toppled WWF Hardcore Champion Mike Awesome to win the title; The Undertaker and Kurt Angle bested Shane McMahon and Diamond Dallas Page by DQ.

Air Date: 19th July 2001
Diamond Dallas Page toppled Chris Jericho by DQ; Christian overturned Lance Storm; The Undertaker, Kane, and Tajiri beat Tazz and The Dudley Boys; Bradshaw dropped Sean O’Haire; Torrie Wilson defeated Trish Stratus in an “arm wrestling contest”; Rob Van Dam and WCW Cruiserweight Champion Billy Kidman bested WWF Hardcore Champion Jeff Hardy and WWF Light Heavyweight Champion X-Pac.

Air Date: 26th July 2001
The Hardy Boys beat WCW Cruiserweight Champion Billy Kidman and Hurricane Helms; The Dudley Boys overturned Billy Gunn and The Big Show; Diamond Dallas Page toppled Kane by DQ; WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam and WCW US Champion Kanyon bested Chris Jericho and Tajiri; WWF Intercontinental Champion Lance Storm defeated Perry Saturn by DQ; Edge and Christian downed Tazz and Rhyno; Kurt Angle toppled WCW World Champion Booker T to win the title.

Aug 2001

Air Date: 2nd August 2001
WCW World Tag Champions Sean O’Haire and Chuck Palumbo defeated The Hardy Boys; Kane toppled WCW US Champion Kanyon by DQ; The Dudley Boys and Rhyno overturned Chris Jericho, Edge, and Christian; Kurt Angle toppled WWF Intercontinental Champion Lance Storm in a non-title match; WCW World Champion Booker T battled The Undertaker to a no-contest.

Air Date: 9th August 2001
Diamond Dallas Page and WCW US Champion Kanyon bested WWF World Tag Champions The Acolytes to win the titles; Perry Saturn downed Raven; WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Edge; Albert and WCW Cruiserweight Champion X-Pac defeated Spike Dudley and WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Tajiri; The Undertaker and Kane toppled WCW World Tag Champions Sean O’Haire and Chuck Palumbo to win the titles; Chris Jericho bested Hugh Morrus; Kurt Angle and The Hardy Boys battled WWF World Champion Steve Austin and The Dudley Boys to a no-contest in an elimination match.

Air Date: 16th August 2001
The Rock pinned Shawn Stasiak; Albert and WCW Cruiserweight Champion X-Pac defeated William Regal and WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Tajiri; Rob Van Dam and Rhyno overturned Chris Jericho and WWF Hardcore Champion Jeff Hardy; WWF World Tag Champions Diamond Dallas Page and Kanyon bested Edge and Christian; Kurt Angle dropped Tazz; WCW World Champion Booker T battled The Rock to a no-contest in a “lights out” match.

Air Date: 23rd August 2001
WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam battled Jeff Hardy to a no-contest; WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Raven; The Big Show and Billy Gunn bested The Dudley Boys; Kurt Angle toppled WCW US Champion Kanyon in a non-title match; Booker T, Rhyno, and Test overcame WCW World Champion The Rock and The Acolytes; WWF and WCW World Tag Champions The Undertaker and Kane toppled WWF Intercontinental Champion Edge and Christian; Ivory beat Lita; WWF World Champion Steve Austin rolled over Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 30th August 2001
The Big Show and The Acolytes overturned Test and The Dudley Boys; Albert overcame The Undertaker by DQ; WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam and Rhyno overturned WCW World Champion The Rock and Chris Jericho; WCW US Champion Kanyon bested WWF Intercontinental Champion Edge; WWF European Champion The Hurricane and Ivory beat Spike Dudley and Molly Holly; Lance Storm pinned Tajiri; Kurt Angle toppled Booker T.

Sep 2001

Air Date: 4th September 2001
The Hardy Boys beat Justin Credible and Raven; The Undertaker battled Steven Richards to a no-contest; Test overcame Billy Gunn; The Big Show toppled WWF European Champion The Hurricane by DQ; Booker T, Rhyno, and The Dudley Boys overturned WCW World Champion The Rock, Chris Jericho, and The Acolytes; WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over WWF World Champion Steve Austin in a non-title match.

Air Date: 13th September 2001
The Hardy Boys beat The Hurricane and Lance Storm; WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Spike Dudley; Chris Jericho bested Christian; WCW World Champion The Rock pinned Shawn Stasiak; The Acolytes overturned X-Pac and Albert; Booker T overturned The Big Show; Lita beat Ivory; Test and The Dudley Boys overturned Tajiri, William Regal, and Scotty 2 Hotty; Kurt Angle toppled Rhyno.

Air Date: 20th September 2001
The Hurricane and Lance Storm beat The Big Show and Spike Dudley; Kronik overcame Taka Michinoku and Sho Funaki; WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Raven; WWF Intercontinental Champion Edge toppled Kanyon; The Hardy Boys beat The Dudley Boys; WWF World Champion Steve Austin bested Tajiri; WCW World Champion The Rock and The Acolytes overturned Booker T , Test, and Rhyno in an elimination match.

Air Date: 27th September 2001
Booker T and Test beat WCW World Tag Champions The Undertaker and Kane to win the titles; The Hardy Boys beat Kanyon and Rhyno; The Big Show toppled X-Pac; WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Chris Jericho; Ivory beat Molly Holly; WWF Intercontinental Champion Christian battled Bradshaw to a no-contest; WCW World Champion The Rock and WWF World Champion Kurt Angle battled WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys to a no-contest.

Oct 2001

Air Date: 4th October 2001
Edge and The Acolytes overturned WWF Intercontinental Champion Christian, X-Pac, and Albert; The Big Show and Spike Dudley beat The Hurricane and Mike Awesome; WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over WWF World Champion Kurt Angle in a non-title match; Tazz defeated Maven; Booker T overturned Kane; Torrie Wilson bested Stacy Keibler in a swimsuit contest;  WCW World Champion The Rock, Chris Jericho, and Mike Chioda defeated WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys and Nick Patrick.

Air Date: 11th October 2001
The Dudley Boys overcame The Big Show and Spike Dudley; Billy Kidman bested WWF Light Heavyweight and WCW Cruiserweight Champion X-Pac to win the WCW Cruiserweight title; Chris Jericho bested WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam in a non-title match; Kane toppled WWF European Champion The Hurricane by DQ; Tazz defeated Maven; Kurt Angle battled William Regal to a no-contest.

Air Date: 18th October 2001
The Hurricane, Lance Storm, and Molly Holly downed Lita and WCW World Tag Champions The Hardy Boys; Maven bested Tazz; Tajiri beat D-Von Dudley; Booker T and Test overcame The Undertaker and Kane; Raven and Justin Credible battled WCW World Champion The Rock and Chris Jericho to a no-contest; WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam battled Kurt Angle to a no-contest.

Air Date: 25th October 2001
Booker T won a battle royal; Trish Stratus overcame Molly Holly; WCW US Champion Kurt Angle toppled Christian; WCW World Champion Chris Jericho bested Booker T; Al Snow pinned Tazz; The Dudley Boys beat WCW World Tag Champions The Hardy Boys to win the titles; WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over The Rock.

Nov 2001

Air Date: 1st November 2001
Booker T and Test overcame WWF World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and The Rock to win the titles; Stacy Keibler and WCW World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys overturned Lita and The Hardy Boys; WCW US Champion Kurt Angle toppled Kane; WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over WWF Intercontinental Champion Edge; William Regal pinned Tajiri; The Undertaker bested WWF World Champion Steve Austin by DQ.

Air Date: 8th November 2001
WCW World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys beat The Acolytes; WWF Intercontinental Champion Test overcame Matt Hardy; WCW World Champion The Rock defeated Booker T by DQ; The Big Show battled William Regal to a no-contest; Jeff Hardy and Lita beat Lance Storm and Ivory; WWF European Champion Christian downed Tajiri; The Undertaker bested WWF World Champion Steve Austin and WCW US Champion Kurt Angle in a handicap match.

Air Date: 15th November 2001
WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Kane; WWF World Tag Champions The Hardy Boys and WCW US Champion Edge dropped WCW World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys and WWF Intercontinental Champion Test; The Big Show toppled Diamond Dallas Page; Booker T overturned The Undertaker by DQ; William Regal and Ivory beat Tajiri and Torrie Wilson; WCW World Champion The Rock and Chris Jericho bested WWF World Champion Steve Austin and Kurt Angle by DQ.

Air Date: 22nd November 2001
Test overcame Scotty 2 Hotty; The Big Show toppled Tazz; The Undertaker battled Kurt Angle to a no-contest; WWF Women’s Champion Trish Stratus overcame Stacy Keibler; WWF Intercontinental Champion Edge dropped WWF European Champion Christian by DQ; Chris Jericho and WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys overturned WCW World Champion The Rock and WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam in a handicap match.

Air Date: 29th November 2001
WWF European Champion Christian downed Scotty 2 Hotty; WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Bubba Ray Dudley; Kurt Angle toppled WWF Intercontinental Champion Edge in a non-title match; Kane and The Big Show toppled The Hardy Boys; The Undertaker bested Tazz; WWF World Champion Steve Austin bested William Regal in a strap match.

Dec 2001

Air Date: 6th December 2001
Edge, The Big Show, and Kane toppled William Regal and The Dudley Boys; WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Matt Hardy; Test and Christian downed Scotty 2 Hotty and Albert; Crash Holly downed Jacquelyn; The Undertaker bested Bradshaw; WWF World Champion Steve Austin and WCW World Champion The Rock defeated Chris Jericho and Kurt Angle.

Air Date: 13th December 2001
WWF Intercontinental Champion Edge and Rikishi defeated Kurt Angle and William Regal; WWF Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri beat Crash Holly; Jeff Hardy overturned Test by DQ; Kane toppled Lance Storm; Tazz, Scotty 2 Hotty, and Albert overcame WWF European Champion Christian and WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys; Bradshaw pinned Matt Hardy; The Rock and Rob Van Dam rolled over WWF Undisputed World Champion Chris Jericho and WWF Hardcore Champion The Undertaker.

Air Date: 20th December 2001
WWF Intercontinental Champion Edge dropped Kurt Angle by DQ; Lance Storm pinned Rikishi; WWF Hardcore Champion The Undertaker bested Matt Hardy; The Rock pinned Test; WWF World Champion Chris Jericho bested The Big Show; Booker T overturned Steve Austin in a “First Blood” match.

Air Date: 27th December 2001
Tazz and Spike Dudley beat WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys in a non-title match; Christian downed Rikishi by count-out; WWF Women’s Champion Trish Stratus overcame Molly Holly; The Rock and Rob Van Dam rolled over Kurt Angle and Lance Storm; WWF Intercontinental Champion Edge toppled Kane; WWF Hardcore Champion The Undertaker bested Tajiri.
