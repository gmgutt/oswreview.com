09/21/2002 Christopher Daniels & Donovan Morgan Philadelphia, PA 175
Defeated Bryan Danielson and Michael Modest in a tournament final to become the inaugural champions.

Defeated Christopher Daniels and Xavier (a permitted substitute for Donovan Morgan) to win the titles.
