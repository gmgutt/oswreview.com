Raw Results 2000

WWF RAW RESULTS (2000)

Jan 2000

Air Date: 3rd January 2000
The Dudley Boys bested Too Cool by DQ; Kurt Angle toppled Kane by DQ; WWF Co-Intercontinental Champion Chyna overcame Hardcore Holly; Triple H rolled over WWF World Champion The Big Show to win the title; The Big Bossman and Albert dropped Test, The Fabulous Moolah and Mae Young in a handicap match; The Acolytes overturned Pete Gas, Rodney Gas and Joey Abs by DQ in a handicap match; Jeff Hardy bested Al Snow in a cage match; The Rock toppled X-Pac and WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg in a no-DQ handicap match.

Air Date: 10th January 2000
Road Dogg bested Billy Gunn; Matt and Jeff Hardy downed Kurt Angle and Steve Blackman; WWF European Champion Val Venis dropped Edge; Hardcore and Crash Holly downed Chris Jericho and Chyna; WWF World Champion Triple H bested X-Pac; Rikishi and Too Cool bested Al Snow and The Headbangers; D-Lo Brown and The Godfathers toppled The Dudley Boys by count-out; The Big Show and Test dropped The Big Bossman and Albert; WWF World Champion Triple H, X-Pac and WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg rolled over The Rock, Mankind and The Acolytes.

Air Date: 17th January 2000
Test beat WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Bossman to win the title; Kurt Angle downed Steve Blackman; The Acolytes overcame WWF World Champion Triple H and X-Pac by DQ; Jeff Hardy overturned Bubba Ray Dudley; Rikishi bested WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho by DQ; Kane toppled Albert; WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg rolled over The Rock and The Big Show.

Air Date: 24th January 2000
Edge and Christian battled Steve Blackman and Al Snow to a no-contest; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho pinned Hardcore Holly; Viscera and Crash Holly defeated Too Cool; Kane toppled X-Pac by DQ; Matt and Jeff Hardy overcame WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg by DQ; The Acolytes bested The Godfather and D-Lo Brown; The Big Bossman overturned WWF Hardcore Champion Test by DQ; The Rock and Rikishi rolled over WWF World Champion Triple H and The Big Show by DQ.

Air Date: 31st January 2000
WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg battled Al Snow and Steve Blackman to a no-contest; Christian overcame D-Von Dudley; The Big Show defeated Too Cool in a handicap match; The Rock pinned Kurt Angle; X-Pac overturned WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho in a non-title affair; Harvey Wippleman (in drag) defeated WWF Women’s Champion The Kat in a “lumberjill” match to win the title; Hardcore Holly and Crash Holly bested Viscera in a handicap match; Bubba Ray Dudley dropped Matt Hardy by DQ; WWF World Champion Triple H rolled over Cactus Jack by DQ.

Feb 2000

Air Date: 7th February 2000
WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn beat Edge and Christian; Kurt Angle bested Mark Henry by DQ; Hardcore Holly and Crash Holly defeated The Acolytes in a Hardcore match; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho downed Viscera by DQ; WWF Women’s Champion Jackie dropped Luna; The Dudley Boys overcame The Godfather and D-Lo Brown; WWF World Champion Triple H, X-Pac, Chris Benoit, Dean Malenko and Perry Saturn rolled over The Rock, Cactus Jack, Rikishi and Too Cool.

Air Date: 14th February 2000
D-Von Dudley overturned Edge; Road Dogg overturned Brian Christopher; The Godfather and D-Lo Brown defeated Steve Blackman and Al Snow; Chris Benoit rolled over The Rock; Scotty 2 Hotty beat Billy Gunn; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Essa Rios dropped Crash Holly; Tazz downed Hardcore Holly by DQ; Rikishi overcame Dean Malenko and Perry Saturn by DQ in a handicap match; Kane battled WWF World Champion Triple H, X-Pac and The Big Show to a no-contest in a handicap match.

Air Date: 21st February 2000
WWF European Champion Kurt Angle and Davey Boy Smith beat WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho and Chyna; Too Cool defeated Al Snow and Steve Blackman; Dean Malenko and Perry Saturn downed The Godfather and D-Lo Brown; Edge, Christian, Matt and Jeff Hardy overturned The Dudley Boys and WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg; The Acolytes and Mark Henry dropped Crash Holly, Hardcore Holly and Viscera; Chris Benoit bested Test; Rikishi pinned Eddie Guerrero in a no-DQ match; Tazz overturned The Big Bossman by DQ; WWF World Champion Triple H, The Big Show and X-Pac rolled over The Rock, Cactus Jack and Kane.

Air Date: 28th February 2000
Matt and Jeff Hardy defeated The Acolytes; Rikishi downed WWF Intercontinental and European Champion Kurt Angle by count-out; Edge and Christian beat Al Snow and Steve Blackman; The Rock pinned The Brooklyn Brawler; Chris Jericho bested Perry Saturn; Mark Henry battled WWF Hardcore Champion Crash Holly to a no-contest; Tazz overturned Chris Benoit by DQ; WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys battled X-Pac and Road Dogg to a no-contest; The Rock rolled over WWF World Champion Triple H and The Big Show by DQ in a handicap match.

Mar 2000

Air Date: 6th March 2000
The Big Show beat Kane by DQ; Mark Henry defeated D-Von Dudley; X-Pac and Road Dogg overcame Too Cool by DQ; Chris Jericho downed WWF Intercontinental and European Champion Kurt Angle by DQ; Rikishi bested WWF World Champion Triple H by DQ; Steve Blackman pinned Matt Hardy; Dean Malenko and Perry Saturn overcame Edge and Christian; WWF Hardcore Champion Crash Holly overturned Viscera; The Rock rolled over Chris Benoit in a cage match.

Air Date: 13th March 2000
Matt and Jeff Hardy toppled Al Snow and Steve Blackman; Mark Henry battled WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys to a no-contest in a handicap match; WWF European and Intercontinental Champion Kurt Angle beat Chris Jericho and Tazz in a no-DQ Triple Threat match; WWF World Champion Triple H, Road Dogg and X-Pac rolled over Rikishi and Too Cool; Kane pinned The Big Bossman; Dean Malenko beat WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Essa Rios to win the title; The Acolytes overturned Edge and Christian; Chris Benoit and Perry Saturn defeated Test and Val Venis; The Rock rolled over The Big Show.

Air Date: 20th March 2000
Matt and Jeff Hardy beat X-Pac and Road Dogg; The Godfather battled The Big Bossman to a no-contest; Too Cool downed Perry Saturn and WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko; Kane toppled Rikishi by DQ; Chris Jericho pinned Chris Benoit; Al Snow and Steve Blackman defeated WWF Hardcore Champion Crash Holly and Hardcore Holly; Edge and Christian overcame The Acolytes; Test toppled Val Venis; Matt Hardy and Jeff Hardy battled Edge and Christian to a no-contest; WWF World Champion Triple H rolled over The Rock and The Big Show in a Triple Threat match.

Air Date: 27th March 2000
Chris Benoit and Eddie Guerrero defeated Chris Jericho and Chyna; WWF Hardcore Champion Crash Holly battled Hardcore Holly to a no-contest; Rikishi downed Road Dogg by count-out; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko and Perry Saturn dropped Matt and Jeff Hardy; Kane overcame WWF Intercontinental and European Champion Kurt Angle by DQ; Test pinned Al Snow; The Acolytes battled The Big Bossman and Bull Buchanan to a no-contesy; X-Pac bested D-Von Dudley; The Rock and Vince McMahon rolled over The Big Show and Shane McMahon.

Apr 2000

Air Date: 3rd April 2000
Eddie Guerrero toppled WWF European Champion Chris Jericho to win the title; X-Pac and Road Dogg dropped Test and Albert; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit downed Tazz; The Big Show overturned Rikishi by DQ; Crash Holly overcame WWF Hardcore Champion Hardcore Holly to win the title; Kurt Angle bested Val Venis; Kane toppled Bull Buchanan; The Rock rolled over Shane McMahon.

Air Date: 10th April 2000
WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian downed X-Pac and Road Dogg; Kurt Angle bested The Godfather; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit, WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko and Perry Saturn defeated Tazz, Rikishi and Scotty 2 Hotty; WWF World Champion Triple H pinned Taka Michinoku; Matt and Jeff Hardy dropped Test and Albert; Val Venis overcame The Big Show by DQ; WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero overturned Chris Jericho; The Rock rolled over The Big Bossman and Bull Buchanan in a handicap cage match.

Air Date: 17th April 2000
Chris Jericho overturned WWF World Champion Triple H to ostensibly win the title (The match and result was later “stricken from the record”); Matt and Jeff Hardy dropped Essa Rios and WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero; WWF Hardcore Champion Crash Holly defeated Perry Saturn and ECW World Champion Tazz in a Triple Threat match; Al Snow and Steve Blackman downed The Dudley Boys; Scotty 2 Hotty pinned WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko to win the title; The Big Show bested WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit by DQ; WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian toppled The Big Bossman and Bull Buchanan by DQ; WWF World Champion Triple H, Road Dogg, and X-Pac rolled over Chris Jericho and The Acolytes.

Air Date: 24th April 2000
Rikishi bested Kurt Angle by DQ; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit dowed Road Dogg; Hardcore Holly and WWF Hardcore Champion Crash Holly defeated Matt and Jeff Hardy; Matt Hardy pinned WWF Hardcore Champion Crash Holly; The Big Show dropped The Big Bossman and Bull Buchanan by DQ in a handicap match; D-Lo Brown and Steve Blackman bested The Godfather and Al Snow; Val Venis toppled WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero in a non-title match; WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian overturned The Dudley Boys; WWF World Champion Triple H and WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit rolled over The Rock and Chris Jericho.

May 2000

Air Date: 1st May 2000
Test and Albert defeated The Dudley Boys; Too Cool dropped Matt and Jeff Hardy; The Kat and Jackie downed Terri Runnels and Ivory; Chris Jericho bested X-Pac by DQ; Rikishi and The Big Show overcame WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian by DQ; WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero pinned Essa Rios; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit bested Tazz; WWF Hardcore Champion Crash Holly battled Steve Blackman to a no-contest; WWF World Champion The Rock rolled over Shane McMahon in a cage match.

Air Date: 8th May 2000
WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian downed The Acolytes by DQ; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho dropped Kurt Angle; WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero dropped The Godfather; The Dudley Boys overturned Test, Albert, and Gerald Brisco in a handicap tables match; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho dropped The Big Show by count-out; Too Cool bested The Big Bossman and Bull Buchanon; The Hardy Boys overturned Perry Saturn and Dean Malenko; Rikishi toppled Pat Patterson, X-Pac, and Road Dogg by DQ in a handicap match; Chris Benoit overcame WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho to win the title by Montreal Finish. Triple H was guest enforcer.

Air Date: 15th May 2000
The Dudley Boys overturned Al Snow and Steve Blackman; Rikishi toppled Kurt Angle by DQ; The Godfather battled WWF Hardcore Champion Crash Holly to a no-contest (Cynthia Lynch overturned WWF Hardcore Champion Crash Holly to win the title; Crash Holly overcame WWF Hardcore Champion Cynthia Lynch to win the title); Hardcore Holly overcame Chris Jericho in a hardcore match; The Big Show defeated Test and Albert by DQ in a handicap match; Dean Malenko and Perry Saturn downed WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero and Chyna; WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian downed The Acolytes and The Hardy Boys in a three-way;  WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit overcame WWF World Champion The Rock in a non-title submission match by Montreal Finish.

Air Date: 22nd May 2000
Test and Albert overcame The Hardy Boys; WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero dropped D-Lo Brown; Kurt Angle, and WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian downed Too Cool and Rikishi; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit overcame Val Venis by DQ; The Dudley Boys overturned The Big Bossman and Bull Buchanon; Bradshaw pinned Crash Holly in a hardcore match; Perry Saturn and Dean Malenko dropped Essa Rios and The Godfather.

Air Date: 29th May 2000
D-Lo Brown, Perry Saturn, and Terri toppled The Godfather, WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko, and The Kat; WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero dropped Kurt Angle; Too Cool bested WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian to win the titles; Rikishi toppled Shane McMahon by DQ (KOTR Qualifier); Test, Albert, and Val Venis overturned Chris Jericho and The Hardy Boys; Hardcore Holly overcame WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit by DQ; Bull Buchanon downed Steve Blackman (KOTR Qualifier); The Rock battled The Undertaker to a no-contest in a lumberjack match.

Jun 2000

Air Date: 5th June 2000
Test, Albert, and Val Venis overturned Rikishi and WWF World Tag Champions Too Cool; Chris Benoit overcame Road Dogg (KOTR Qualifier); WWF Hardcore Champion Gerald Brisco dropped Crash Holly; The Rock rolled over The Undertaker and Kane in a three-way; Eddie Guerrero and Chyna defeated Dean Malenko and The Godfather; Hardcore Holly overcame Faarooq (KOTR Qualifier); Kurt Angle, Edge, and Christian downed Chris Jericho and The Dudley Boys; The Hardy Boys overturned The Big Bossman and Bull Buchanon; WWF World Champion Triple H pinned The Rock.

Air Date: 12th June 2000
X-Pac pinned Dean Malenko (KOTR Qualifier); WWF Women’s Champion Stephanie McMahon downed Lita; WWF World Tag Champions Too Cool bested Test and Albert by DQ; Vince and Shane McMahon downed The Dudley Boys in a tables match; Crash Holly overcame WWF Hardcore Champion Gerald Brisco to win the title; WWF World Champion Triple H bested Chris Jericho; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit overcame Matt Hardy; Jeff Hardy overcame Christian (KOTR Qualifier); The Rock battled Kane to a no-contest in a “No Holds Barred” match.

Air Date: 19th June 2000
Kurt Angle toppled Bubba Ray Dudley (KOTR 2000 – First Round); WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit overcame X-Pac (KOTR 2000 – First Round); WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero dropped Chyna (KOTR 2000 – First Round); Chris Jericho dropped Edge (KOTR 2000 – First Round); The Undertaker beat The Big Bossman and Bull Buchanon in a handicap match; Rikishi toppled Scotty 2 Hotty (KOTR 2000 – First Round); Kane toppled The Hardy Boys in a handicap match; WWF Hardcore Champion Crash Holly overcame Hardcore Holly by DQ (KOTR 2000 – First Round); Gerald Brisco beat WWF Hardcore Champion Crash Holly to win the title; Pat Patterson beat WWF Hardcore Champion Gerald Brisco to win the title; The Rock rolled over Test and Albert in a handicap match.

Air Date: 26th June 2000
WWF Intercontinental Champion Rikishi toppled Chris Benoit by DQ; X-Pac pinned Chris Jericho; Val Venis overturned WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero by DQ; Jerry Lawler defeated WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko in a non-title “Over The Top Rope, Off with the Top” match; The Acolytes won a tag team battle royal; The Undertaker and Kane toppled WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian in a non-title match; WWF World Champion The Rock rolled over Triple H and Kurt Angle in a three-way.

Jul 2000

Air Date: 3rd July 2000
Chris Benoit overcame WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero by DQ; WWF Intercontinental Champion Rikishi battled Triple H to a double count-out; Val Venis overturned Jeff Hardy; Chris Jericho and The Acolytes overturned X-Pac and Road Dogg in a handicap match; The Undertaker and Kane toppled WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian by DQ; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko dropped Crash Holly; The Dudley Boys overturned Kurt Angle and The Brooklyn Brawler; Test overcame The Godfather; WWF World Champion The Rock rolled over Shane McMahon by DQ.

Air Date: 10th July 2000
Test and Albert overcame Too Cool and The Hardy Boys in a three-way elimination match; Chris Jericho dropped Road Dogg; Rikishi toppled Kane and WWF Intercontinental Champion Val Venis by DQ in a handicap match; Edge toppled Bradshaw; Trish Stratus battled Lita to a no-contest in a streetfight; Triple H battled X-Pac to a no-contest; WWF World Champion The Rock rolled over Chris Benoit by DQ.

Air Date: 17th July 2000
The Dudley Boys battled The Hardy Boys to a no-contest; X-Pac and Road Dogg defeated Too Cool; Chris Benoit, Edge and Christian downed WWF World Champion The Rock and The Acolytes; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko dropped Chyna by count-out; Rikishi toppled Test; The Undertaker and Kane toppled Triple H and Kurt Angle.

Air Date: 24th July 2000
WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman downed Road Dogg; The Big Show and Lita defeated Shane and WWF Women’s Champion Stephanie McMahon by DQ; Bull Buchanon pinned The Godfather; The Undertaker battled Kurt Angle to a no-contest; Tazz defeated Scotty 2 Hotty; WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian downed The Dudley Boys and The Hardy Boys in a three-way; Trish Stratus overturned Lita in a strap match; WWF World Champion The Rock and Kane battled Chris Benoit and Kurt Angle to a no-contest.

Air Date: 31st July 2000
X-Pac and Road Dogg defeated Al Snow and WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman in a hardcore match; The Hardy Boys overturned WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian by DQ; Chris Benoit and Kurt Angle toppled The Dudley Boys; The Acolytes overturned Bull Buchanon and The Goodfather; Rikishi toppled Tazz; Eddie Guerrero and Chyna defeated WWF European Champion Perry Saturn and WWF Intercontinental Champion Val Venis; Kane battled The Big Show to a no-contest; WWF World Champion The Rock and Lita defeated Triple H and Trish Stratus.

Aug 2000

Air Date: 7th August 2000
Rikishi toppled X-Pac by DQ; The Dudley Boys overturned Bull Buchanon and The Goodfather by DQ; Too Cool and Chyna defeated Test, Albert, and WWF Intercontinental Champion Val Venis; WWF World Champion The Rock rolled over Chris Benoit in a no-DQ match; The Acolytes and Matt Hardy overcame The Big Show and WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian by DQ; Kurt Angle and Triple H both pinned Chris Jericho in a three-way.

Air Date: 14th August 2000
Test and Albert overcame Eddie Guerrero and WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman; The Goodfather and Bull Buchanon defeated The Hardy Boys and Lita in a handicap match; The Undertaker beat Chris Benoit by DQ; Tazz defeated Crash Holly; X-Pac and Road Dogg defeated Rikishi in a handicap match; Chris Jericho dropped WWF Intercontinental Champion Val Venis by DQ; WWF World Champion The Rock and The Dudley Boys battled Triple H, Kurt Angle, and Shane McMahon to a no-contest.

Air Date: 21st August 2000
The Hardy Boys overturned The Dudley Boys; WWF Intercontinental Champion Val Venis and Trish Stratus overturned Crash Holly and Ivory; Rikishi toppled Kane by DQ; Triple H and Kurt Angle toppled The Acolytes; Chris Jericho dropped WWF European Champion Perry Saturn by DQ; Shane McMahon downed WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman to win the title; WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian battled X-Pac and Road Dogg to a no-contest; Lita pinned WWF Women’s Champion Stephanie McMahon to win the title. The Rock was guest referee.

Air Date: 28th August 2000
Chris Jericho and The Acolytes overturned Chris Benoit, Test, and Albert; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chyna pinned Val Venis; Rikishi battled Steven Richards to a no-contest; WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman downed Tazz; WWF Women’s Champion Lita pinned Jackie; Triple H bested Eddie Guerrero by DQ; Perry Saturn and Terri toppled Al Snow and The Kat; WWF World Champion The Rock rolled over Kane.

Sep 2000

Air Date:  4th September 2000
Kane toppled Chris Benoit by DQ; The Dudley Boys overturned The Acolytes in a tables match; Eddie Guerrero dropped WWF Intercontinental Champion Chyna and Kurt Angle in a three-way; Triple H pinned Test; WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman downed The Big Bossman; Val Venis and Road Dogg defeated The Goodfather and Bull Buchanon; Jerry Lawler and Chris Jericho dropped Tazz and Mideon; WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian downed WWF World Champion The Rock and The Undertaker.

Air Date: 11th September 2000
The Hardy Boys overturned The Dudley Boys; WWF Women’s Champion Lita pinned Ivory; Too Cool bested WWF Intercontinental Champion Eddie Guerrero and Chyna; Chris Jericho dropped Triple H by DQ; The Goodfather and Bull Buchanon downed The Acolytes; WWF European Champion Al Snow pinned Tazz; Kurt Angle battled Test and Albert to a no-contest in a handicap match; WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman downed X-Pac; Kane and Chris Benoit overcame WWF World Champion The Rock and The Undertaker in a no-DQ match.

Air Date: 18th September 2000
Bull Buchanon and The Goodfather defeated The Dudley Boys; Kane toppled Chris Benoit by DQ; Triple H, Kurt Angle, and Stephanie McMahon downed Test, Albert, and Trish Stratus; Rikishi battled Chyna to a no-contest; WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian downed Too Cool; The Undertaker beat WWF World Champion The Rock in a non-title match.

Air Date: 25th September 2000
Kane battled Rikishi to a double-DQ; Tazz pinned Bubba Ray Dudley; WWF World Tag Champions The Hardy Boys overturned Edge and Christian in a ladder match; X-Pac defeated Chris Jericho in a “First Blood” match; WWF Intercontinental Champion Eddie Guerrero dropped Val Venis; WWF World Champion The Rock rolled over Chris Benoit.

Oct 2000

Air Date: 2nd October 2000
WWF World Tag Champions The Hardy Boys overturned Too Cool; Chris Jericho and The Dudley Boys overturned Tazz, Raven, and X-Pac; WWF World Champion The Rock and Rikishi toppled Kane and Chris Benoit; WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman downed Edge and Christian in a three-way; WWF Intercontinental Champion Eddie Guerrero and Chyna defeated Steven Richards and Val Venis; Kurt Angle toppled Triple H.

Air Date: 9th October 2000
WWF Women’s Champion Lita pinned Jackie in a hardcore match; WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman downed Raven; Chris Benoit and X-Pac defeated Triple H and Chris Jericho; WWF World Tag Champions The Hardy Boys toppled D-Lo Brown and Chaz; WWF European Champion Al Snow pinned Test; Val Venis and The Goodfather downed WWF Intercontinental Champion Eddie Guerrero and Chyna; WWF World Champion The Rock and Rikishi toppled Kurt Angle and Kane.

Air Date: 16th October 2000
Chris Jericho battled WWF Intercontinental Champion Eddie Guerrero to a no-contest; The Acolytes overturned Test and Albert ; William Regal bested WWF European Champion Al Snow to win the title; Triple H bested Kane by DQ; X-Pac defeated Billy Gunn by DQ; The Conquistadors (Edge and Christian) overturned The Dudley Boys; Val Venis beat Road Dogg; Kurt Angle, Edge, and Christian downed WWF World Champion The Rock and WWF World Tag Champions The Hardy Boys.

Air Date: 23rd October 2000
WWF Women’s Champion Lita defeated Trish Stratus in a “bra and panties” match; Chris Benoit overcame Road Dogg; Chris Jericho dropped WWF European Champion William Regal by DQ; Tazz and Raven downed Too Cool; Billy Gunn downed Val Venis; WWF World Tag Champions The Conquistadors overturned Edge in a handicap match; A three-way between WWF World Champion Kurt Angle, The Rock and Triple H went to a no-contest.

Air Date: 30th October 2000
WWF Intercontinental Champion Eddie Guerrero pinned Chyna; Bull Buchanon, The Goodfather, and Ivory defeated WWF Women’s Champion Lita and WWF World Tag Champions The Hardy Boys; Test overcame Crash Holly in a hardcore match; Steve Austin downed Rikishi in a cage match; Steven Richards pinned Billy Gunn; WWF World Champion Kurt Angle toppled Triple H by DQ; The Rock rolled over Chris Jericho.

Nov 2000

Air Date: 6th November 2000
The Undertaker overturned Val Venis; Test and Albert dropped Too Cool; Triple H, Chyna, Road Dogg and Billy Gunn overcame Chris Benoit, Eddie Guerrero, Perry Saturn and WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko; The Godfather and Bull Buchanon defeated WWF World Tag Champions Matt and Jeff Hardy to win the titles; WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman bested Kane; Jerry Lawler toppled Raven by DQ; The Dudley Boys downed Edge and Christian in a tables match; Steve Austin and Triple H battled WWF World Champion Kurt Angle and Rikishi to a no-contest.

Air Date: 13th November 2000
WWF World Champion Kurt Angle dropped Crash Holly; Chris Jericho and WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman defeated Kane and Test in a hardcore match; The Dudley Boys downed Tazz and Raven; The Undertaker, Hardcore and Crash Holly overturned WWF World Champion Kurt Angle, Edge and Christian; Lita, Matt and Jeff Hardy beat WWF Women’s Champion Ivory and WWF World Tag Champions The Goodfather and Bull Buchanan by DQ; WWF European Champion William Regal bested Road Dogg by DQ; Steve Austin, The Rock, Billy Gunn and Chyna toppled Chris Benoit, WWF Intercontinental Champion Eddie Guerrero, WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko and Perry Saturn.

Air Date: 20th November 2000
The Dudley Boys overturned WWF World Tag Champions Bull Buchanon and The Goodfather by DQ; WWF European Champion William Regal bested Hardcore Holly in a hardcore match; The Rock and Chris Jericho dropped Rikishi and Kane; Billy Gunn, Road Dogg, and K-Kwik rolled over WWF Intercontinental Champion Eddie Guerrero, WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko, and Perry Saturn; WWF Women’s Champion Ivory overcame Molly Holly; The Undertaker beat Edge and Christian in a handicap match; Steve Austin downed Chris Benoit.

Air Date: 27th November 2000
Hardcore Holly, Crash Holly, and Molly Holly overcame Trish Stratus, Test, and Albert; WWF Intercontinental Champion Billy Gunn downed Eddie Guerrero; The Rock rolled over Rikishi by DQ; The Undertaker beat Kane by DQ; Road Dogg and K-Kwik rolled over Val Venis and Steven Richards; Chris Jericho and  The Hardy Boys overturned Chris Benoit, WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko, and Perry Saturn; WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman downed Tiger Ali Singh; Steve Austin downed WWF World Champion Kurt Angle by DQ.

Dec 2000

Air Date: 4th December 2000
Rikishi toppled The Undertaker; WWF World Tag Champions Bull Buchanon and The Goodfather beat The Hardy Boys; Chris Benoit overcame Hardcore Holly; Edge and Christian downed Road Dogg and K-Kwik; WWF World Champion Kurt Angle toppled Chris Jericho; WWF Intercontinental Champion Billy Gunn downed Val Venis by DQ; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko dropped Lita; William Regal bested WWF European Champion Crash Holly to win the title.

Air Date: 11th December 2000
Dean Malenko and Perry Saturn defeated The Hardy Boys by count-out; Billy Gunn downed WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit by DQ; The Rock and The Dudley Boys overturned WWF World Champion Kurt Angle and WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian in a tables match; Chyna defeated Ivory by DQ; The Acolytes overturned Test and Albert; Jerry Lawler, Road Dogg, and K-Kwik rolled over Steven Richards, Bull Buchanon, and The Goodfather; Raven downed Al Snow in a hardcore match.

Air Date: 18th December 2000
WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit overcame Jeff Hardy; Val Venis, Bull Buchanon, and The Goodfather defeated Billy Gunn and The Dudley Boys; The Rock and The Undertaker beat WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian to win the titles; Chris Jericho dropped Perry Saturn; WWF European Champion William Regal bested Steve Austin by DQ; The Acolytes and Jackie toppled Test, Albert, and Trish Stratus; WWF World Champion Kurt Angle battled Vince McMahon to a no-contest.

Air Date: 25th December 2000
The Hardy Boys overturned WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit and Dean Malenko; WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman downed Raven and Hardcore Holly in a three-way; Raven downed WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman to win the title; Steve Austin downed Kane by DQ; Scotty 2 Hotty and Brian Christopher beat Test and Albert; WWF World Champion Kurt Angle, and WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian downed Chris Jericho and The Dudley Boys; The Goodfather and Bull Buchanon defeated The Acolytes; The Rock rolled over The Undertaker by DQ.
