Smackdown Results 2003

WWE SMACKDOWN RESULTS (2003)

Jan 2003

Air Date: 2nd January 2003
John Cena bested Rikishi; Bill DeMott overturned Chuck Palumbo; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Billy Kidman toppled Eddie Guerrero; Chris Benoit and Edge dropped Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas by DQ; Nunzio beat Crash Holly; Brock Lesnar rolled over Matt Hardy.

Air Date: 9th January 2003
The Big Show pinned Rikishi; John Cena bested Chavo Guerrero Jr; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Billy Kidman toppled Matt Hardy; Tajiri toppled Jamie Noble; Charlie Haas overcame Edge; Chris Benoit downed Shelton Benjamin by DQ; Bill DeMott overturned Shannon Moore; Eddie Guerrero bested Bull Buchanan; Brock Lesnar rolled over A-Train.

Air Date: 16th January 2003
WWE Tag Team Champions Eddie and Chavo Guerrero Jr overturned John Cena and Bull Buchanon; Bill DeMott overcame Rikishi; Nunzio downed Tajiri; Brock Lesnar and Rey Mysterio toppled The Big Show and Albert; Matt Hardy beat Sho Funaki; WWE World Champion Kurt Angle, Charlie Haas, and Shelton Benjamin bested Edge and Chris Benoit by DQ in a handicap match.

Air Date: 23rd January 2003
Chris Benoit toppled Charlie Haas; Rikishi pinned Bill DeMott; The Undertaker bested Albert; Edge toppled Shelton Benjamin; Nunzio beat Shannon Moore; WWE World Champion Kurt Angle dropped Rey Mysterio.

Air Date: 30th January 2003
Albert overcame Rey Mysterio; Eddie Guerrero downed John Cena; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Billy Kidman bested Shannon Moore; Bill DeMott overcame Rikishi; Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas rolled over Chris Benoit and Edge.

Feb 2003

Air Date: 6th February 2003
Rey Mysterio dropped Jamie Noble; Rikishi pinned Nunzio; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Billy Kidman bested Matt Hardy; Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas overcame WWE Tag Team Champions Eddie and Chavo Guerrero Jr to win the titles; Albert overcame Shannon Moore; WWE World Champion Kurt Angle rolled over Chris Benoit.

Air Date: 13th February 2003
Charlie Haas beat Edge; Rey Mysterio dropped Matt Hardy; Chris Benoit toppled Albert; Shelton Benjamin overcame Eddie Guerrero; Torrie Wilson bested Dawn Marie in a “bra and panties” match; Brock Lesnar dropped John Cena.

Air Date 20th February 2003
Chris Benoit toppled Albert; Rikishi pinned Johnny Stamboli; Matt Hardy and Shannon Moore toppled WWE Cruiserweight Champion Billy Kidman and Rey Mysterio; Nidia overcame Torrie Wilson in a “paddle-on-a-pole” encounter; Gauntlet match: Brock Lesnar bested Charlie Haas; Brock Lesnar toppled Shelton Benjamin; Brock Lesnar rolled over WWE World Champion Kurt Angle by DQ.

Air Date: 27th February 2003
Sho Funaki and Torrie Wilson bested Jamie Noble and Nidia; Eddie Guerrero beat Nunzio; Chris Benoit and Rhyno overturned WWE Cruiserweight Champion Matt Hardy and Shannon Moore; Rikishi toppled Chuck Palumbo by DQ; WWE World Champion Kurt Angle rolled over Brian Kendrick; Brock Lesnar dropped WWE Tag Team Champions Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas in a handicap match.

Mar 2003

Air Date: 6th March 2003
Rey Mysterio won a three-way over Tajiri and Jamie Noble; Eddie and Chavo Guerrero Jr overturned Chuck Palumbo and Johnny Stamboli; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Matt Hardy beat Billy Kidman; Albert dropped The Undertaker by DQ; Chris Benoit and Rhyno overturned WWE Tag Team Champions Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas in a non-title match; Brock Lesnar dropped Paul Heyman in a cage match.

Air Date: 13th March 2003
WWE Tag Team Champions Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas downed Billy Kidman and Rey Mysterio; Rikishi pinned Chuck Palumbo; The Big Show and Albert overcame Sho Funaki and Tajiri; Eddie and Chavo Guerrero Jr battled Chris Benoit and Rhyno to a no-contest; The Pittsburgh Penguin (Brian Kendrick) beat WWE Cruiserweight Champion Matt Hardy by count-out; WWE World Champion Kurt Angle toppled Brock Lesnar.

Air Date: 20th March 2003
Rhyno overturned Charlie Haas by DQ; Eddie Guuerero, Chavo Guerrero Jr and Rikishi defeated Johnny Stamboli, Nunzio, and Chuck Palumbo; Rey Mysterio dropped Jamie Noble; The Undertaker bested Bill DeMott; Brian Kendrick pinned Shannon Moore; Chris Benoit rolled over Shelton Benjamin.

Air Date: 27th March 2003
WWE Cruiserweight Champion Matt Hardy and Shannon Moore toppled Rey Mysterio and Brian Kendrick; Eddie Guerrero beat Charlie Haas; Torrie Wilson bested Nidia; The Big Show and Albert overcame Chris Benoit and Rhyno by DQ; Chavo Guerrero Jr dropped Shelton Benjamin; John Cena bested Rikishi.

Apr 2003

Air Date: 3rd April 2003
The Undertaker bested Rey Mysterio (WWE World Title #1 Contender Tournament – Quarter-Final); Brian Kendrick pinned Jamie Noble; Chris Benoit toppled Albert (WWE World Title #1 Contender Tournament – Quarter-Final); John Cena bested Eddie Guerrero (WWE World Title #1 Contender Tournament – Quarter-Final); WWE Tag Team Champions Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin bested Tajiri and Sho Funaki; Rhyno overturned The Big Show by DQ (WWE World Title #1 Contender Tournament – Quarter-Final).

Air Date: 10th April 2003
WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar dropped WWE Cruiserweight Champion Matt Hardy; Brian Kendrick and Torrie Wilson bested Jamie Noble and Nidia; Chris Benoit toppled Rhyno (WWE World Title #1 Contender Tournament – Semi-Final); Tajiri and Rey Mysterio dropped WWE Tag Team Champions Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin in a non-title match; Nathan Jones overturned Bill DeMott; John Cena bested The Undertaker (WWE World Title #1 Contender Tournament – Semi-Final).

Air Date: 17th April 2003
The Big Show and Albert overcame Rey Mysterio and Tajiri; Eddie Guerrero dropped Jamie Noble; Torrie Wilson bested Nidia; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Matt Hardy beat Brian Kendrick; Nathan Jones overturned Nunzio by DQ; John Cena bested Chris Benoit (WWE World Title #1 Contender Tournament – Final).

Air Date: 24th April 2003
John Cena bested Rhyno; Chris Benoit toppled Nunzio; Rey Mysterio dropped Crash Holly; Rikishi defeated Roddy Piper by DQ; Shelton Benjamin beat Chavo Guerrero Jr; Tajiri beat The Big Show by count-out; WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar dropped Albert by DQ.

May 2003

Air Date: 1st May 2003
Eddie and Chavo Guerrero Jr overturned WWE Cruiserweight Champion Matt Hardy and Shannon Moore; The Big Show toppled Sho Funaki; Chris Benoit battled Johnny Stamboli to a no-contest; Chuck Palumbo and Johnny Stamboli beat Chris Benoit and Rhyno; WWE Tag Team Champions Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas downed John Walters and Eric Stevens; Dawn Marie and Nidia beat Torrie Wilson and Sable.

Air Date: 8th May 2003
Eddie Guerrero beat WWE Cruiserweight Champion Matt Hardy in a non-title match; Brian Kendrick pinned Jamie Noble; Torrie Wilson bested Dawn Marie; WWE Tag Team Champions Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas downed Tajiri and Rikishi; Rhyno overturned John Cena; The Big Show pinned Chris Benoit.

Air Date: 15th May 2003
Chuck Palumbo beat Rikishi; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Matt Hardy beat Tajiri; Chris Benoit toppled John Cena by DQ; Shelton Benjamin bested Eddie Guerrero.

Air Date: 22nd May 2003
WWE Tag Team Champions Eddie Guerrero and Tajiri beat Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin by DQ; John Cena bested Spanky; Chris Benoit toppled WWE Cruiserweight Champion Matt Hardy in a non-title match; Rey Mysterio dropped Shannon Moore and Crash Holly in a handicap match; WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar dropped Johnny Stamboli by DQ; Sean O’Haire rolled over Mr America (a masked Hulk Hogan) by count-out.

Air Date: 29th May 2003
Nidia pinned Torrie Wilson; WWE Tag Team Champions Eddie Guerrero and Tajiri beat Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas by DQ; Sean O’Haire beat Chris Benoit; Doug and Danny Basham beat Rikishi and Spanky; The Big Show, Chuck Palumbo, and Johnny Stamboli defeated WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar and The Undertaker in a handicap match.

Jun 2003

Air Date: 5th June 2003
The Undertaker bested Chuck Palumbo; WWE Tag Team Champions Eddie Guerrero and Tajiri beat Doug and Danny Basham; John Cena bested Chris Benoit; Rikishi and Torrie Wilson bested Jamie Noble and Nidia; Rey Mysterio dropped WWE Cruiserweight Champion Matt Hardy to win the title.

Air Date: 12th June 2003
WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio dropped Matt Hardy; Billy Gunn bested Albert; The Undertaker toppled Johnny Stamboli; Vince McMahon bested Zach Gowen in an “arm wrestling” match; Doug and Danny Basham beat Chris Benoit and Rhyno; John Cena bested Sho Funaki; WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar battled The Big Show to a no-contest.

Air Date: 19th June 2003
The Undertaker bested Nunzio; Chris Benoit toppled Rhyno (WWE US Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio dropped Kanyon; Kurt Angle toppled Charlie Haas; Billy Gunn defeated Jamie Noble; Sean O’Haire beat Eddie Guerrero; WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar dropped The Big Show by DQ.

Air Date: 26th June 2003
John Cena bested Orlando Jordon; The Undertaker and The Acolytes overturned Nunzio, Chuck Palumbo, and Johnny Stamboli; Matt Hardy beat Rikishi (WWE US Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); WWE Tag Team Champions Eddie Guerrero and Tajiri beat Roddy Piper and Sean O’Haire; Ultimo Dragon bested Shannon Moore; The Big Show, Shelton Benjamin, and Charlie Haas downed WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar, Kurt Angle, and Mr America (Hulk Hogan).

Jul 2003

Air Date: 3rd July 2003
WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio dropped Nunzio; Chris Benoit and Rhyno overturned Doug and Danny Basham; Billy Gunn downed John Cena (WWE US Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin bested WWE Tag Team Champions Eddie Guerrero and Tajiri to win the titles; Albert overcame Orlando Jordon; Zach Gowen and Stephanie McMahon bested The Big Show in a “no holds barred” handicap match.

Air Date: 10th July 2003
WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio and Billy Kidman bested The Acolytes, Chuck Palumbo and Johnny Stamboli, and Doug and Danny Basham in a four-way elimination match; Chris Benoit and Rhyno overturned Matt Hardy and Shannon Moore; Eddie Guerrero overcame Ultimo Dragon (WWE US Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); Billy Gunn and Torrie Wilson bested Jamie Noble and Nidia; The Big Show and WWE Tag Team Champions Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas downed WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar in a ”falls count anywhere” non-title handicap match.

Air Date: 17th July 2003
Chris Benoit toppled Matt Hardy (WWE US Title Tournament – Semi-Final); Ultimo Dragon bested Jamie Noble; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio and Billy Kidman bested Los Conquistadores; Rhyno battled Sean O’Haire to a no-contest; Eddie Guerrero overcame Billy Gunn (WWE US Title Tournament – Semi-Final); Kurt Angle toppled The Big Show and WWE Tag Team Champions Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas in a no-DQ, “falls count anywhere” handicap match.

Air Date: 24th July 2003
WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio and Billy Kidman bested Matt Hardy and Shannon Moore; Billy Gunn beat Jamie Noble; Eddie Guerrero overcame Rhyno; The Acolytes overturned Chuck Palumbo and Johnny Stamboli; The Big Show and WWE Tag Team Champions Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas downed WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar, Kurt Angle, and Zach Gowen.

Air Date: 31st July 2003
WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio beat Shelton Benjamin; Chris Benoit toppled Doink; WWE US Champion Eddie Guerrero overcame Tajiri; Shannon Moore beat Zach Gowen; John Cena bested Orlando Jordon; WWE World Champion Kurt Angle and Brock Lesnar dropped The Big Show and Albert.

Aug 2003

Air Date: 7th August 2003
WWE US Champion Eddie Guerrero downed Chris Benoit by DQ; Chris Benoit and Tajiri beat WWE US Champion Eddie Guerrero and Rhyno; Nunzio bested Zach Gowen; John Cena toppled The Undertaker; Jamie Noble beat Doug Basham; Charlie Haas overcame WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio in a non-title match; Brock Lesnar battled Vince McMahon to a no-contest in a cage match.

Air Date: 14th August 2003
WWE Tag Team Champions Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin bested WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio and Billy Kidman; Danny Basham beat Billy Gunn; The Big Show toppled The Undertaker by count-out; Tajiri beat Rhyno; John Cena bested Zach Gowen; Spanky beat Brock Lesnar by DQ; Albert overcame Stephanie McMahon in a no count-out match.

Air Date: 21st August 2003
WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio dropped Matt Hardy; Doug and Danny Basham beat Jamie Noble and Billy Gunn; Zach Gowen bested Brock Lesnar by DQ; John Cena and Albert overcame The Undertaker and Orlando Jordon; WWE US Champion Eddie Guerrero and Rhyno overturned Chris Benoit and Tajiri; WWE World Champion Kurt Angle toppled The Big Show in a streetfight.

Air Date: 28th August 2003
WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio dropped Nunzio; Chris Benoit toppled Albert; WWE US Champion Eddie Guerrero overcame John Cena by DQ; The Undertaker bested Brock Lesnar and The Big Show in a no-DQ three-way.

Sep 2003

Air Date: 4th September 2003
WWE Tag Team Champions Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas downed The Acolytes; Chris Benoit toppled Albert; The Undertaker battled WWE World Champion Kurt Angle to a no-contest; Torrie Wilson bested Sable, Nidia, and Dawn Marie in a “Bourbon Street Bikini Contest; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio dropped Tajiri.

Air Date: 11th September 2003
Tajiri and Nunzio beat WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio and Billy Kidman; Shaniqua battled Dawn Marie to a no-contest; Chris Benoit toppled Rhyno; WWE US Champion Eddie Guerrero overcame John Cena in a “Parking Lot Brawl”; The Acolytes overturned Matt Hardy and Shannon Moore.

Air Date: 18th September 2003
Chris Benoit and WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio dropped Rhyno and Tajiri; Shaniqua defeated Torrie Wilson and Nidia in a handicap match; WWE US Champion Eddie Guerrero and Chavo Guerrero Jr overturned WWE Tag Team Champions Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin to win the titles; Brock Lesnar dropped WWE World Champion Kurt Angle in a 60-minute Ironman match to win the title. 5:4.

Air Date: 25th September 2003
WWE Tag Team Champions Eddie Guerrero and Chavo Guerrero Jr overturned Matt Hardy and Shannon Moore; Tajiri beat WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio to win the title; Bradshaw and Jamie Noble overcame Doug and Danny Basham by DQ; The Big Show toppled Arch Kincaid and John Walters in a handicap match; WWE US Champion Eddie Guerrero overcame Charlie Haas.

Oct 2003

Air Date: 2nd October 2003
WWE US Champion Eddie Guerrero beat Matt Hardy; Doug and Danny Basham overcame The Acolytes; Chris Benoit toppled Charlie Haas by DQ; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri beat Billy Kidman; Orlando Jordan dropped The Big Show by count-out; WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar and John Cena bested The Undertaker and Kurt Angle.

Air Date: 9th October 2003
WWE Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri beat Ultimo Dragon; The Undertaker bested Chuck Palumbo; The Big Show toppled Orlando Jordan; Shannon Moore beat Zach Gowen; Albert, Doug and Danny Basham beat Chris Benoit and Bradshaw in a handicap match; WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar dropped Paul London; The Big Show toppled Chavo Guerrero Jr by count-out.

Air Date: 16th October 2003
Chris Benoit toppled Doug Basham; Jamie Noble battled WWE Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri to a no-contest; WWE US Champion Eddie Guerrero overcame Rhyno; Rey Mysterio dropped Johnny Stamboli.

Air Date: 23rd October 2003
Kurt Angle and Chris Benoit battled John Cena and Albert to a no-contest; Rikishi and Scotty 2 Hotty beat Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas; Doug and Danny dropped WWE Tag Team Champions Eddie and Chavo Guerrero Jr to win the titles; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri beat Zach Gowen; The Undertaker bested WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar and WWE US Champion The Big Show in a “best of three falls” handicap match.

Air Date: 30th October 2003
Kurt Angle and Chris Benoit toppled John Cena and Albert; Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas downed Nunzio, Chuck Palumbo, and Johnny Stamboli in a handicap match; Ultimo Dragon bested Rey Mysterio; Torrie Wilson beat Dawn Marie in a “Trick or Treat Costume Contest”; The Acolytes overturned WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar and WWE US Champion The Big Show by DQ.

Nov 2003

Air Date: 6th November 2003
John Cena bested Rey Mysterio; WWE US Champion The Big Show toppled Bradshaw; Eddie Guerrero overcame WWE Tag Team Champions Doug and Danny Basham in a handicap match; Jamie Noble and Ultimo Dragon bested WWE Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri and Nunzio; Kurt Angle and Chris Benoit toppled WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar, Albert, Matt Morgan, and Nathan Jones by DQ in a handicap match.

Air Date: 13th November 2003
Kurt Angle toppled Nathan Jones by DQ; Akio and Sakoda defeated Rey Mysterio and Jamie Noble; WWE Tag Team Champions Doug and Danny Basham beat Chavo Guerrero Jr in a handicap match; Bradshaw defeated Albert; Chris Benoit and John Cena bested WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar and WWE US Champion The Big Show.

Air Date: 20th November 2003
Rey Mysterio dropped Akio; Matt Morgan bested Shannon Moore; Albert overcame Bradshaw; Chris Benoit toppled Nathan Jones; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri beat Jamie Noble; Eddie and Chavo Guerrero Jr overturned Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas; John Cena bested WWE US Champion The Big Show in a non-title match.

Air Date: 27th November 2003
Chris Benoit toppled Matt Morgan; Akio beat Jamie Noble; Nathan Jones overturned Shannon Moore; Eddie Guerrero dropped Charlie Haas; John Cena bested Albert; Chris Benoit and John Cena co-won a battle royal.

Dec 2003

Air Date: 4th December 2003
Rikishi and Scotty 2 Hotty beat WWE Tag Team Champions Doug and Danny Basham in a non-title match; WWE US Champion The Big Show toppled Shannon Moore; Jamie Noble beat Sakoda; Chris Benoit toppled John Cena; Chavo Guerrero Jr dropped Shelton Benjamin; WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar dropped Chris Benoit.

Air Date: 11th December 2003
Rhyno overturned Bradshaw; Shannon Moore toppled Albert; Eddie and Chavo Guerrero Jr overturned Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas; WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar dropped Rey Mysterio.

Air Date: 18th December 2003
WWE US Champion The Big Show toppled Orlando Jordan; WWE Tag Team Champions Doug and Danny Basham beat Rikishi and Scotty 2 Hotty, Eddie and Chavo Guerrero Jr, and Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas in a four-way; Rey Mysterio dropped Jamie Noble; Chris Benoit toppled Chuck Palumbo; Faarooq defeated Rhyno by DQ; WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar dropped Shannon Moore; Hardcore Holly and Shannon Moore toppled Albert and Matt Morgan.

Air Date: 25th December 2003
The Acolytes overturned Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin; Rikishi pinned Rhyno; Eddie Guerrero overcame Chris Benoit; Torrie Wilson bested Dawn Marie and Sable in a “Santa’s Little Helper” contest; John Cena bested WWE US Champion The Big Show in a non-title match.
