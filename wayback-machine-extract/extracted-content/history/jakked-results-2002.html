Jakked Results 2002

JAKKED RESULTS (2002)

Jan 2002


Air Date: 5th January 2002
Spike Dudley beat Scoot Andrews; Perry Saturn bested Vito DeNucci.

Air Date: 12th January 2002
The Hurricane defeated Sho Funaki; Crash Holly beat Prince Nana; Perry Saturn downed John Jirus (Xavier); Christian pinned Lo-Ki.

Air Date: 19th January 2002
The Big Bossman bested Shawn Stasiak; Perry Saturn bested Eddie Craven; Sho Funaki overturned Michael Shane.

Air Date: 26th January 2002
WWF European Champion Christian and Test overcame Scotty 2 Hotty and Albert; The Hurricane beat AJ Styles; Perry Saturn bested Sho Funaki; Crash Holly downed Oynx.

Feb 2002

Air Date: 2nd February 2002
WWF World Tag Champions Tazz and Spike Dudley beat Crash Holly and Sho Funaki. Jacqueline was guest referee; Val Venis overturned WWF Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri in a non-title match; The Big Bossman bested The Hurricane; Scotty 2 Hotty and Albert overcame Steven Perez and Pat Cusick.

Air Date: 9th February 2002
Christian downed The Hurricane; Scotty 2 Hotty and Albert overcame Crash Holly and Sho Funaki; WWF Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri beat Jason Partain.

Air Date: 16th February 2002
Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo defeated Scotty 2 Hotty and Albert; Val Venis overturned Crash Holly; Lance Storm defeated Sho Funaki; The Big Bossman bested Perry Saturn.

Air Date: 23rd February 2002
The Dudley Boys beat Sho Funaki and WWF Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri; Test overcame Crash Holly; Val Venis overturned Scotty 2 Hotty; The Big Bossman bested Michael Shane.

Mar 2002

Air Date: 2nd March 2002
Edge toppled Test; The Dudley Boys bested Tazz and Spike Dudley; WWF Hardcore Champion Goldust overcame WWF Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri; Scotty 2 Hotty and Albert beat Perry Saturn and Sho Funaki.

Air Date: 9th March 2002
Val Venis overturned Mike Anthony; Perry Saturn bested Paul London; Crash Holly downed Eddie Craven.

Air Date: 16th March 2002
The Hurricane dropped Sho Funaki; Lance Storm pinned The Godfather; Val Venis overturned Perry Saturn; WWF Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri beat Crash Holly.

Air Date: 23rd March 2002
The Acolytes overturned Curt Hennig and The Big Bossman Val Venis overturned Lance Storm; Test overcame WWF Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri in a non-title match; Scotty 2 Hotty and Albert overcame Crash Holly and Sho Funaki.

Air Date: 30th March 2002
Lance Storm pinned Val Venis; Tommy Dreamer dropped Raven; Test overcame Spike Dudley; WWF Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri and Sho Funaki overturned Billy Kidman and The Hurricane.

Apr 2002

Air Date: 6th April 2002
WWF Women’s Champion Jazz defeated Ivory; Faarooq pinned Lance Storm; Chavo Guerrero Jr and Hugh Morrus overturned The Hurricane and Sho Funaki.

Air Date: 13th April 2002
Sho Funaki overturned Lance Storm; Hugh Morrus overturned Christian; WWF Cruiserweight Champion Billy Kidman bested Chavo Guerrero Jr; Diamond Dallas Page battled Hardcore Holly to a double count-out.

Air Date: 20th April 2002
The Godfather dropped Val Venis; Christian downed Sho Funaki; Lance Storm battled Hugh Morrus to a double count-out.

Air Date: 27th April 2002
Chavo Guerrero Jr overturned The Hurricane; Lance Storm battled Hugh Morrus to a no-contest; WWF Women’s Champion Jazz defeated Ivory; Faarooq and Val Venis overturned The Godfather and Test.

May 2002

Air Date: 4th May 2002
The Godfather dropped Tazz; The Hurricane dropped Albert; Hugh Morrus overturned Sho Funaki; Chavo Guerrero Jr overturned Maven.

Air Date: 11th May 2002
Val Venis overturned Albert; Chavo Guerrero Jr overturned Sho Funaki; Billy Kidman bested Christian by DQ; Faarooq defeated The Godfather.

Air Date: 18th May 2002
Final Episode of WWF/E Jakked
Albert overcame Hugh Morrus; The Godfather dropped Sho Funaki; Billy Kidman bested Chavo Guerrero Jr; Test and Christian downed Faarooq and Mark Henry.

Jakked was replaced in syndication by WWE Velocity from 05/25/2002 onwards.
