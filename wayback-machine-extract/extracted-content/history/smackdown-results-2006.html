Smackdown Results 2006

WWE SMACKDOWN RESULTS (2006)

Jan 2006

Air Date: 6th January 2006
John Bradshaw Layfield pinned Matt Hardy in a “falls-count-anywhere” match; Mark Henry downed Rob Eckos, Rob Trusky and Gus Harlatcher in an elimination match; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Kid Kash bested Juventud Guerrera; Chris Benoit toppled Randy Orton by DQ (Best of Seven: Orton was subbing for Booker T); WWE Tag Team Champions Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Batista and Rey Mysterio in a cage match.

Air Date: 13th January 2006
Randy Orton pinned Chris Benoit. As a result of the match, Booker T was declared the WWE US Champion (Best of Seven Series Final. Orton was a permitted substitute for Booker); Kurt Angle won a 20-man battle royal by last eliminating Mark Henry to win the vacant World Heavyweight Title.

Air Date: 20th January 2006
Bobby Lashley beat John Bradshaw Layfield; Matt Hardy defeated Finlay by DQ; Mark Henry downed Rey Mysterio; The Gymini overcame Brian Kendrick and Paul London; Randy Orton toppled Orlando Jordan; World Heavyweight Champion Kurt Angle rolled over Daivari by DQ.

Air Date: 27th January 2006
Mark Henry pinned Rey Mysterio; Super Crazy and Psychosis defeated James and Chad Dick, and Nunzio and Vito in a three-way; Chris Benoit overturned Randy Orton in a “no-holds-barred” encounter;  Sho Funaki bested Finlay by reverse decision-DQ; John Bradshaw Layfield battled Scotty 2 Hotty to a no-contest; World Heavyweight Champion Kurt Angle rolled over WWE Tag Champions Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury in a handicap match.

Feb 2006

Air Date: 3rd February 2006
WWE Tag Champions Joey Mercury and Johnny Nitro defeated Super Crazy and Psychosis; Bobby Lashley downed Chad Dick; Tzuki dropped Octagoncito; Chris Benoit toppled WWE US Champion Finlay (a permitted substitute for Booker T) by DQ; Randy Orton and Mark Henry rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Kurt Angle and Rey Mysterio.

Air Date: 10th February 2006
Chris Benoit defeated Matt Hardy, John Bradshaw Layfield and Orlando Jordan in a four-way; WWE Tag Champions Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury downed Paul London and Brian Kendrick; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms dropped Nunzio; Octagoncito and Pequeno Violencia battled Mascarita Sagrada and Tsuki to a no-contest; The Undertaker overturned Mark Henry by DQ.

Air Date 17th February 2006
Rey Mysterio pinned Sylvan Grenier; The Boogeyman defeated Orlando Jordan; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms downed Scotty 2 Hotty; John Bradshaw Layfield and Finlay overturned Chris Benoit and Bobby Lashley; Matt Hardy bested Simon Dean; World Heavyweight Champion Kurt Angle and The Undertaker rolled over Mark Henry and WWE Tag Champions Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury in a handicap match.

Air Date: 24th February 2006
WWE Tag Champions Joey Mercury and Johnny Nitro defeated Matt Hardy and Tatanka; The Boogeyman beat Chad and James Dick in a Handicap match; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms downed Psychosis; WWE US Champion Chris Benoit, Bobby Lashley and Rey Misterio rolled over John Bradshaw Layfield, Randy Orton and Finlay.

Mar 2006

Air Date: 3rd March 2006
Tatanka pinned Booker T; Randy Orton bested Super Crazy; WWE Tag Champions Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury dropped Matt Hardy and Road Warrior Animal; Bobby Lashley battled Finlay to a double-DQ; The Undertaker rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Kurt Angle by DQ.

Air Date: 10th March 2006
Bobby Lashley battled Finlay to a no-contest (MITB Qualifier); Paul Burchill overturned William Regal; Kristal overturned Jillian Hall in a “bra and panties” match; WWE US Champion Chris Benoit toppled WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms in a non-title match; World Heavyweight Champion Kurt Angle and Rey Mysterio bested Randy Orton and Mark Henry by DQ.

Air Date: 17th March 2006
Finlay downed Bobby Lashley in a lumberjack match (MITB Qualifier); Paul Burchill and Kristal overturned William Regal and Jillian Hall; Booker T pinned Jeremy Young; Matt Hardy downed Road Warrior by reverse decision-DQ (MITB Qualifier); WWE US Champion Chris Benoit toppled Orlando Jordan; World Heavyweight Champion Kurt Angle, Rey Mysterio, and Randy Orton beat Mark Henry, and WWE Tag Champions Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury.

Air Date: 24th March 2006
Bobby Lashley won a battle royal; Finlay downed Rey Mysterio; WWE US Champion Chris Benoit toppled William Regal; Mark Henry downed World Heavyweight Champion Kurt Angle in a non-title match.

Air Date: 31st March 2006
WWE US Champion Chris Benoit toppled Road Warrior Animal; John Bradshaw Layfield pinned Daniel Cross; Paul Burchill overturned Booker T by count-out; Bobby Lashley, Matt Hardy, and Tatanka dropped Finlay, and WWE Tag Champions Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury; Super Crazy and Psychosis toppled Kid Kash and Jamie Noble; World Heavyweight Champion Kurt Angle bested Rey Mysterio.

Apr 2006

Air Date: 7th April 2006
Brian Kendrick and Paul London beat WWE Tag Champions Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury in a non-title match; Gunner Scott (Brent Albright) pinned Booker T; Chris Benoit toppled Simon Dean; World Heavyweight Champion Rey Mysterio bested Randy Orton; Paul Burchill overturned William Regal; The Undertaker battled Mark Henry to a no-contest.

Air Date: 14th April 2006
Brian Kendrick beat Johnny Nitro; Paul Burchill dropped Rasheed Brown; Matt Hardy downed Road Warrior Animal; WWE US Champion John Bradshaw Layfield toppled Chris Benoit in a cage match; The Gymini overcame Super Crazy and Psychosis; Kurt Angle bested Randy Orton (KOTR 2006 – First Round).

Air Date: 21st April 2006
Booker T pinned Matt Hardy (KOTR 2006 – First Round); Paul London downed Joey Mercury; Chris Benoit and Bobby Lashley dropped Finlay and Orlando Jordan; The Great Khali crushed Sho Funaki; The Gymini overturned Paul Burchill and William Regal.

Air Date: 28th April 2006
WWE US Champion John Bradshaw Layfield bested William Regal; Super Crazy, Brian Kendrick, and Paul London beat WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms, and WWE Tag Champions Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury; Bobby Lashley dropped Mark Henry by count-out (KOTR 2006 – First Round); Finlay pinned Gunner Scott; Kurt Angle battled World Heavyweight Champion Rey Mysterio to a no-contest.

May 2006

Air Date: 5th May 2006
Booker T pinned Gunner Scott; Brian Kendrick and Paul London battled WWE Tag Champions Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury to a no-contest; Finlay downed Chris Benoit (KOTR 2006 – First Round); The Gymini overturned Sho Funaki and Scotty 2 Hotty; Super Crazy, Nunzio, and Kid Kash battled to a no-contest in a three-way; Mark Henry downed World Heavyweight Champion Rey Mysterio in a non-title match.

Air Date: 12th May 2006
Super Crazy downed Kid Kash and Nunzio in a three-way; The Gymini overturned Joey Ryan and Tom Wellington; Bobby Lashley dropped Finlay (KOTR 2006 – Semi-Final); Mark Henry downed Paul Burchill; The Great Khali pinned World Heavyweight Champion Rey Mysterio in a non-title match.

Air Date: 19th May 2006
Paul London, Brian Kendrick, and Jillian Hall overturned Melina, and WWE Tag Champions Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury; Bobby Lashley dropped Jared Steel; Chris Benoit and Gunner Scott defeated Booker T and Finlay; Mark Henry downed Matt Hardy; World Heavyweight Champion Rey Mysterio battled Kane to a no-contest.

Air Date: 26th May 2006
Bobby Lashley dropped WWE US Champion John Bradshaw Layfield to win the title; Tatanka dropped Simon Dean; Chris Benoit battled Mark Henry to a no-contest; WWE Tag Champions Brian Kendrick and Paul London beat Nunzio and Vito; Finlay downed Paul Burchill; World Heavyweight Champion Rey Mysterio bested John Bradshaw Layfield in a “Title vs. Career” encounter.

Jun 2006

Air Date: 2nd June 2006
Kurt Angle bested World Heavyweight Champion Rey Mysterio by count-out; Matt Hardy downed Nunzio; Jillan Hall overturned Kristal; Mark Henry downed Paul Burchill; Finlay downed Caden Matthews; WWE US Champion Bobby Lashley dropped Booker T.

Air Date: 9th June 2006
Super Crazy overturned Brian Kendrick; Vito pinned Nunzio; WWE US Champion Bobby Lashley dropped William Regal by count-out; Mark Henry downed Raymond Roe; Mr Kennedy downed Scotty 2 Hotty; Finlay downed World Heavyweight Champion Rey Mysterio in a non-title match.

Air Date: 16th June 2006
World Heavyweight Champion Rey Mysterio bested WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms in a non-title match; Mr Kennedy downed Nick Berk; The Great Khali overturned Super Crazy and Psychosis in a handicap match; Finlay and William Regal overturned Matt Hardy and Gunner Scott; Vito overcame Scott Wright; WWE US Champion Bobby Lashley dropped King Booker.

Air Date: 23rd June 2006
WWE Tag Champions Paul London and Brian Kendrick defeated Super Crazy and Psychosis; Tatanka battled Simon Dean to a no-contest; Finlay downed Gunner Scott; King Booker defeated WWE US Champion Bobby Lashley by DQ; Mr Kennedy downed Sho Funaki; World Heavyweight Champion Rey Mysterio bested Mark Henry by DQ.

Air Date: 30th June 2006
WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms battled Super Crazy to a no-contest; Jamie Noble and Kid Kash overcame Malik Yamah and Marty Garner; Finlay downed Matt Hardy; Mr Kennedy downed Gunner Scott; Vito overcame Simon Dean; WWE US Champion Bobby Lashley dropped King Booker in a cage match.

Jul 2006

Air Date: 7th July 2006
King Booker won a battle royal; Mr Kennedy downed Super Crazy; The Great Khali battled Tatanka to a no-contest; Vito overcame Psychosis; Jamie Noble and Kid Kash overcame Scotty 2 Hotty and Sho Funaki; WWE US Champion Bobby Lashley dropped WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms in a non-title match.

Air Date: 14th July 2006
Matt Hardy downed Mr Kennedy; Jamie Noble overcame Paul London; Finlay downed WWE US Champion Bobby Lashley to win the title; World Heavyweight Champion Rey Mysterio bested William Regal.

Air Date: 21st July 2006
Kid Kash overcame Brian Kendrick; Ashley and Jillian Hall overturned Michelle McCool and Kristal; Bobby Lashley dropped WWE US Champion Finlay by DQ; Super Crazy downed Psychosis; World Heavyweight Champion Rey Mysterio bested King Booker.

Air Date: 28th July 2006
Mr Kennedy downed Batista by count-out; Vito overcame Simon Dean; Sylvan beat Tatanka; Michelle McCool pinned Jillian Hall; WWE US Champion Finlay downed William Regal; Sylvester Terkay defeated Matt Hardy; World Heavyweight Champion King Booker rolled over Rey Mysterio.

Aug 2006

Air Date: 4th August 2006
William Regal overturned WWE US Champion Finlay by DQ; Elijah Burke  defeated Scott Wright; Kristal overturned Ashley; Tatanka dropped Sylvan; Batista dropped Mr Kennedy; Vito overcame The Brooklyn Brawler; KC James and Idol Stevens defeated Scotty 2 Hotty and Sho Funaki; The Undertaker rolled over World Heavyweight Champion King Booker by DQ.

Air Date: 11th August 2006
WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms downed Tatanka; KC James and Idol Stevens defeated WWE Tag Champions Paul London and Brian Kendrick in a non-title match; Mr Kennedy battled Rey Mysterio to a no-contest; Vito overcame Sylvan; Sylvester Terkay defeated Dustin Starr; Batista and Bobby Lashley dropped WWE US Champion Finlay and William Regal.

Air Date: 18th August 2006
Batista dropped Sylvan; Sylvester Terkay pinned Scotty 2 Hotty; Mr Kennedy downed Tatanka; The Undertaker rolled over The Great Khali in a “last man standing” match; Vito overcame Scott Fowler; Bobby Lashley dropped WWE US Champion Finlay by DQ.

Air Date: 25th August 2006
Mr Kennedy downed Matt Hardy; Sylvester Terkay pinned Tatanka; Paul London dropped KC James; Kristal overturned Jillian Hall; Vito overcame Sylvan; Batista and Bobby Lashley dropped World Heavyweight Champion King Booker, WWE US Champion Finlay, and William Regal in a handicap match.

Sep 2006

Air Date: 1st September 2006
Matt Hardy downed WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms in a non-title match; The Miz beat Tatanka; Mr Kennedy downed Bobby Lashley and WWE US Champion Finlay in a three-way to win the title; Vito overcame William Regal by count-out; WWE Tag Champions Paul London and Brian Kendrick defeated Jamie Noble and Kid Kash.

Air Date: 8th September 2006
Finlay downed Rey Mysterio; WWE US Champion Mr Kennedy downed Lawrence Tylwe; Ashley, and WWE Tag Champions Paul London and Brian Kendrick defeated Michelle McCool, KC James, and Idol Stevens; Bobby Lashley dropped William Regal; The Miz beat Matt Hardy; Chavo Guerrero Jr pinned Tatanka; World Heavyweight Champion King Booker pinned Batista.

Air Date: 15th September 2006
WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms downed Matt Hardy; The Miz beat Scotty 2 Hotty; Brian Kendrick defeated Idol Stevens and Kid Kash in a three-way; WWE US Champion Mr Kennedy downed Rey Mysterio; Vito pinned William Regal; Sylvester Terkay dropped Todd Smith; Bobby Lashley dropped Finlay by DQ.

Air Date: 22nd September 2006
Batista and Bobby Lashley dropped William Regal and Finlay; Matt Hardy downed WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms in a non-title match; The Miz beat Sho Funaki; Rey Mysterio battled Chavo Guerrero Jr to a no-contest; WWE Tag Champions Paul London and Brian Kendrick defeated Kid Kash and Jamie Noble, and KC James and Idol Stevens in a three-way; Sylvan beat Tatanka; The Undertaker rolled over World Heavyweight Champion King Booker by DQ.

Air Date: 29th September 2006
Rey Mysterio bested Elijah Burke; KC James and Idol Stevens downed Kid Kash and Jamie Noble; Chavo Guerrero Jr pinned Matt Hardy; The Miz beat Tatanka; Sylvan beat Jimmy Wang Yang; WWE World Champion John Cena, Batista, and Bobby Lashley dropped World Heavyweight Champion King Booker, Finlay, and William Regal.

Oct 2006

Air Date: 6th October 2006
Rey Mysterio and Matt Hardy downed Chavo Guerrero Jr and WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms; Elijah Burke overturned Tatanka; Finlay downed World Heavyweight Champion King Booker in a non-title match; Batista battled Bobby Lashley to a no-contest; Sylvan beat Jimmy Wang Yang; Michelle McCool bested Ashley; Batista and Bobby Lashley dropped World Heavyweight Champion King Booker and Finlay by DQ.

Air Date: 13th October 2006
Chris Benoit bested WWE US Champion Mr Kennedy to win the title; Vito pinned William Regal; MVP battled Kane to a no-contest; Rey Mysterio bested Jamie Noble; Jimmy Wang Yang, and WWE Tag Champions Paul London and Brian Kendrick overcame Sylvan, KC James and Idol Stevens; Batista dropped Bobby Lashley and Finlay in a three-way.

Air Date: 20th October 2006
Kane overturned MVP by DQ; Elijah Burke pinned Vito; William Regal and Dave Taylor defeated Scotty 2 Hotty and Sho Funaki ; Chavo Guerrero Jr bested Rey Mysterio in an “I Quit” match; The Undertaker rolled over WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms in a non-title match; Batista dropped World Heavyweight Champion King Booker by DQ.

Air Date: 27th October 2006
William Regal and Dave Taylor defeated Bobby Lashley and Tatanka; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms downed Matt Hardy in a non-title match; Mr Kennedy bested Kane in a no-DQ match; Kristal won a battle royal. The Miz was guest referee; WWE World Champion John Cena and Batista dropped World Heavyweight Champion King Booker and ECW World Champion The Big Show.

Nov 2006

Air Date: 3rd November 2006
WWE Tag Champions Paul London and Brian Kendrick bested KC James and Idol Stevens; The Undertaker and Kane overturned Mr Kennedy and MVP by count-out; The Undertaker and Kane overturned Mr Kennedy and MVP by DQ; The Undertaker and Kane overturned Mr Kennedy and MVP in a no-DQ, no count-out match; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms downed Jimmy Wang Yang; The Miz and Kristal overturned Vito and Layla; Bobby Lashley dropped Jamie Noble.

Air Date: 10th November 2006
World Heavyweight Champion King Booker pinned Bobby Lashley; Matt Hardy and Jimmy Wang Yang defeated WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms and Sylvan; WWE US Champion Chris Benoit toppled KC James; The Boogeyman bested TJ Dalton; Batista dropped Finlay.

Air Date: 17th November 2006
The Boogeyman battled The Miz and Kristal to a no-contest in a handicap match; WWE US Champion Chris Benoit toppled Tatanka; William Regal overturned Paul London; MVP pinned Kane in a streetfight; Matt Hardy downed WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms in a non-title match; Batista and Bobby Lashley dropped World Heavyweight Champion King Booker and Finlay.

Air Date: 24th November 2006
MVP bested Kane in a cage match; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms downed Jimmy Wang Yang; William Regal dropped Brian Kendrick; Finlay downed WWE US Champion Chris Benoit in a non-title match; The Boogeyman battled The Miz to a no-contest.

Dec 2006

Air Date: 1st December 2006
Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury beat WWE Tag Champions Paul London and Brian Kendrick in a non-title match; World Heavyweight Champion Batista dropped King Booker and Finlay in a three-way; Chavo Guerrero Jr pinned WWE US Champion Chris Benoit in a non-title match; Kristal overturned Layla; The Undertaker battled MVP to a no-contest.

Air Date: 8th December 2006
William Regal and Dave Taylor defeated WWE Tag Champions Paul London and Brian Kendrick in a non-title match; World Heavyweight Champion Batista dropped Finlay by DQ; Jimmy Wang Yang overcame Jamie Noble; WWE US Champion Chris Benoit toppled Sylvan; The Miz beat Scotty 2 Hotty; Kane overturned Mr Kennedy by DQ.

Air Date: 15th December 2006
Jimmy Wang Yang, and WWE Tag Champions Paul London and Brian Kendrick dropped WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms, William Regal and Dave Taylor; Matt Hardy downed Joey Mercury; The Undertaker and Kane battled Mr Kennedy and MVP to a double count-out; Chavo Guerrero Jr bested Sho Funaki; Vito pinned Sylvan; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Finlay.

Air Date: 22nd December 2006
World Heavyweight Champion Batista dropped Sylvan; WWE US Champion Chris Benoit toppled Chavo Guerrero Jr by DQ; Johnny Nitro pinned Matt Hardy; Ashley and Layla dropped Kristal and Jillian Hall; The Undertaker and Kane overturned King Booker and Finlay.
