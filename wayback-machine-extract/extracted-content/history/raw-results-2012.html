Raw Results 2012

WWE RAW RESULTS (2012)

Jan 2012

Air Date: 2nd January 2012
World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes in a non-title match; Wade Barrett pinned Santino Marella; Dolph Ziggler dropped WWE World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk by count-out; Brie and Nikki Bella downed Eve Torres and Kelly Kelly; John Cena, Zack Ryder and The Big Show rolled over Mark Henry and Jack Swagger in a handicap elimination match.

Air Date 9th January 2012
Sheamus and Santino Marella bested Wade Barrett and Jinder Mahal; World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan dropped Kofi Kingston; Brodus Clay overcame Curt Hawkins; WWE World Champion CM Punk rolled over Jack Swagger; John Cena battled Dolph Ziggler to a no-contest.

Air Date: 16th January 2012
WWE World Tag Champions Primo and Epico downed Kofi Kingston and Evan Bourne; Jack Swagger defeated WWE US Champion Zack Ryder to win the title; Kelly Kelly and Alicia Fox beat The Bella Twins; R-Truth won a battle royal over Sheamus, The Miz and Wade Barrett; Brodus Clay dropped JTG; Mark Henry, Dolph Ziggler and David Otunga rolled over WWE World Champion CM Punk, World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan and Chris Jericho by DQ.

Air Date: 23rd January 2012
Dolph Ziggler and Jack Swagger bested WWE World Champion CM Punk and John Cena; Kane battled Zack Ryder to a no-contest in a falls-count-anywhere encounter; Sheamus dropped Jinder Mahal; Brodus Clay downed Heath Slater; R-Truth rolled over The Miz.

Air Date: 30th January 2012
Randy Orton downed Dolph Ziggler; Brodus Clay defeated Tyler Reks; World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan toppled WWE World Champion CM Punk by DQ; Kofi Kingston overturned The Miz; WWE Divas Champion Beth Phoenix bested Eve Torres.

Feb 2012

Air Date: 6th February 2012
The Big Show bested World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan by count-out; Sheamus dropped David Otunga; Randy Orton and The Great Khali toppled Wade Barrett and Cody Rhodes; Kelly Kelly, Eve Torres, Tamina and Alicia Fox overturned WWE Divas Champion Beth Phoenix, Natalya and The Bella Twins; Chris Jericho rolled over WWE World Champion CM Punk, Dolph Ziggler, The Miz, R-Truth and Kofi Kingston in a non-title six-way match.

Air Date: 13th February 2012
Chris Jericho downed Kofi Kingston; Randy Orton defeated The Big Show by DQ; R-Truth beat Dolph Ziggler; Tamina beat Brie Bella; WWE World Champion CM Punk toppled The Miz.

Air Date: 20th February 2012
Sheamus pinned Mark Henry; Kofi Kingston and R-Truth overcame WWE World Tag Champions Primo and Epico in a non-title match; David Otunga overcame Ezekiel Jackson; World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan bested Santino Marella; The Bella Twins beat Aksana and Kelly Kelly; Chris Jericho won a battle royal.

Air Date: 27th February 2012
World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan bested WWE World Champion CM Punk by DQ; Kelly Kelly beat Nikki Bella; John Cena toppled The Miz; WWE World Tag Champions Epico and Primo overcame R-Truth and Kofi Kingston, and WWE US Champion Jack Swagger and Dolph Ziggler in a Triple Threat encounter; Sheamus and The Big Show toppled Cody Rhodes and Mark Henry.

Santino Marella beat WWE US Champion Jack Swagger to win the title; Eve Torres downed Alicia Fox; Chris Jericho and World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan bested WWE World Champion CM Punk and Sheamus; The Big Show dropped The Miz; Kane toppled R-Truth.

Apr 2012

Air Date: 2nd April 2012
WWE US Champion Santino Marella overturned Jack Swagger and Dolph Ziggler in a three-way; Lord Tensai bested Alex Riley; Mark Henry downed WWE World Champion CM Punk by count-out; Kofi Kingston dropped Cody Rhodes; The Miz pinned Zack Ryder.

Air Date: 9th April 2012
Brodus Clay and WWE US Champion Santino Marella defeated Dolph Ziggler and Jack Swagger; R-Truth bested Cody Rhodes; Lord Tensai toppled Yoshi Tatsu; Mark Henry dropped WWE World Champion CM Punk by DQ; Alberto Del Rio overturned Zack Ryder; John Cena rolled over David Otunga.

Air Date: 16th April 2012
WWE World Champion CM Punk toppled Mark Henry in a no-DQ match; WWE US Champion Santino Marella overturned David Otunga; Daniel Bryan toppled Kofi Kingston; Brodus Clay downed Dolph Ziggler by DQ; WWE Intercontinental Champion The Big Show and The Great Khali downed WWE World Tag Champions Primo and Epico in a non-title match; Lord Tensai rolled over John Cena in an Extreme Rules match.

Air Date: 23rd April 2012
Chris Jericho downed Kofi Kingston; Lord Tensai dropped R-Truth; WWE Intercontinental Champion The Big Show and The Great Khali bested Alberto Del Rio and Cody Rhodes; Nikki Bella overcame WWE Divas Champion Beth Phoenix to win the title in a “lumberjill” match; Mark Henry pinned World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus in a non-title match. Daniel Bryan was guest referee; Zack Ryder and Santino Marella overturned WWE World Tag Champions Primo and Epico in a non-title match; Brodus Clay and Hornswoggle toppled Dolph Ziggler and Jack Swagger by DQ.

Air Date: 30th April 2012
The Miz beat Santino Marella in a “Beat The Clock” match (04:18); WWE Divas Champion Layla overcame Brie Bella and Nikki Bella in a three-way; Chris Jericho battled The Big Show to a draw in a “Beat The Clock” match (04:18); Brodus Clay pinned JTG; Randy Orton toppled Jack Swagger in a “Beat The Clock” match (04:16); Kofi Kingston and R-Truth bested WWE World Tag Champions Primo and Epico to win the titles; Kane battled The Great Khali to a draw in a “Beat The Clock” match (04:16); Daniel Bryan rolled over Jerry Lawler in a “Beat The Clock” match (02:20).

May 2012

Air Date: 7th May 2012
WWE Intercontinental Champion The Big Show dropped Cody Rhodes by count-out; Dolph Ziggler bested Kofi Kingston; WWE Divas Champion Layla and Kelly Kelly downed Natalya and Maxine; Chris Jericho and Alberto Del Rio toppled Randy Orton and World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus; Brodus Clay overturned The Miz; Daniel Bryan and Lord Tensai rolled over WWE World Champion CM Punk in a handicap match.

Air Date: 14th May 2012
WWE World Champion CM Punk and WWE US Champion Santino Marella dropped Daniel Bryan and WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes; Beth Phoenix beat Alicia Fox; Kane bested The Big Show; Brodus Clay and WWE World Tag Champions R-Truth and Kofi Kingston downed The Miz, Dolph Ziggler and Jack Swagger; Chris Jericho toppled Randy Orton by DQ.

Air Date: 21st May 2012
John Cena beat David Otunga; Randy Orton bested Alberto Del Rio by DQ; Daniel Bryan toppled Kane by DQ; WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian downed Jinder Mahal; Beth Phoenix overcame Kelly Kelly; John Cena and World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus battled Dolph Ziggler, Jack Swagger and Tensai to a no-contest.

Air Date: 28th May 2012
Alberto Del Rio bested WWE US Champion Santino Marella in a non-title match; WWE World Tag Champions R-Truth and Kofi Kingston defeated Dolph Ziggler and Jack Swagger; Daniel Bryan bested WWE World Champion CM Punk in a non-title match; WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian toppled The Miz; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus rolled over David Otunga.

Jun 2012

Air Date: 4th June 2012
World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus bested Dolph Ziggler; Sin Cara pinned Hunico; Ryback squashed Arthur Rosenberg and Stan Stansky in a handicap match; Kane defeated WWE World Champion CM Punk in a non-title match; WWE World Tag Champions Kofi Kingston and R-Truth toppled Tyler Reks and Curt Hawkins; John Cena bested Tensai; John Cena rolled over Michael Cole in a no-DQ encounter.

Air Date: 11th June 2012
World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus defeated Tensai; Beth Phoenix and Ricardo Rodriguez downed Layla and Santino Marella; Dolph Ziggler overturned The Great Khali, Jack Swagger and WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian in a four-way elimination match; Ryback squashed Willard Fillmore and Rutherford Hayes in a handicap match; The Big Show dropped Kofi Kingston in a cage match; Sin Cara pinned Curt Hawkins; Vader bested Heath Slater; WWE World Champion CM Punk and AJ overturned Kane and Daniel Bryan.

Air Date: 18th June 2012
WWE World Champion CM Punk and World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus bested Daniel Bryan and Kane; Dolph Ziggler toppled Jack Swagger; Alberto Del Rio defeated WWE US Champion Santino Marella in a non-title match; Primo and Epico overturned Titus O’Neil and Darren Young by count-out; John Cena rolled over David Otunga and John Laurinaitis in a handicap match.

Air Date: 25th June  2012
Daniel Bryan rolled over WWE World Champion CM Punk and Kane in a non-title three-way elimination match; The Big Show pinned Brodus Clay; WWE US Champion Santino Marella defeated Jack Swagger; Sid Vicious downed Heath Slater; Dolph Ziggler battled Alberto Del Rio to a no-contest in a”contract-on-a-pole” match; AJ won a divas battle royal; John Cena toppled Chris Jericho by DQ.

Jul 2012

Air Date: 2nd July 2012
Kofi Kingston, R-Truth, Christian and Santino Marella dropped Titus O’Neil, Darren Young, Cody Rhodes and David Otunga; Sheamus and AJ bested Dolph Ziggler and Vickie Guerrero; Heath Slater overcame Doink; The Big Show beat Kane in a “no rules” match; Tyson Kidd overturned Tensai; John Cena and CM Punk battled Chris Jericho and Daniel Bryan to a double count-out.

Air Date: 9th July 2012
World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus defeated Jack Swagger; Dolph Ziggler and Tensai downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian and Tyson Kidd; Brodus Clay dropped Drew McIntyre; John Cena and Kane toppled Chris Jericho and The Big Show by DQ; Sin Cara overcame Heath Slater; Michael Cole beat Jerry Lawler by DQ; WWE World Champion CM Punk and AJ bested Daniel Bryan and Eve Torres.

Air Date: 16th July 2012
WWE World Tag Champions Kofi Kingston and R-Truth defeated Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; Alberto Del Rio bested Zack Ryder; Rikishi pinned Heath Slater; Daniel Bryan and AJ bested The Miz and Eve Torres; Brodus Clay downed JTG; WWE World Champion CM Punk toppled The Big Show by DQ.

Air Date: 23rd July 2012 – “Raw 1000”
Rey Mysterio, Sin Cara and World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus overturned Chris Jericho, Dolph Ziggler and Alberto Del Rio; Brodus Clay downed Jack Swagger; The Miz toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian to win the title; Lita dropped Heath Slater in a no-DQ match; John Cena rolled over WWE World Champion CM Punk by DQ.

Air Date: 30th July 2012
Alberto Del Rio beat WWE US Champion Santino Marella in a non-title match; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus defeated Daniel Bryan in a streetfight; Titus O’Neil downed Kofi Kingston; Randy Orton pinned Heath Slater; Christian and Chris Jericho dropped Dolph Ziggler and WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz; Tyson Kidd overcame Tensai by DQ; John Cena battled The Big Show to a no-contest.
