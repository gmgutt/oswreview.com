Smackdown Results 2000

WWF SMACKDOWN RESULTS (2000)

Jan 2000

Air Date: 6th January 2000
X-Pac dropped The Big Show by DQ; Hardcore and Crash Holly bested WWF Co-Intercontinental Champions Chris Jericho and Chyna; The Acolytes overcame Billy Gunn by DQ in a handicap match; Kane pinned Road Dogg; Kurt Angle rolled over The Rock by DQ; Edge, Christian and Jeff Hardy overturned Al Snow and The Dudley Boys; Test toppled WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Bossman in a non-title match; Taka Michinoku and Sho Funaki defeated Pete Gas, Rodney Gas and Joey Abs in a handicap match; Rikishi bested WWF World Champion Triple H by DQ.

Air Date: 13th January 2000
WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg beat Bradshaw in a no-DQ handicap match; Rikishi and Too Cool defeated Al Snow, Hardcore Holly and Crash Holly; Test downed Gangrel by DQ; Matt and Jeff Hardy dropped Albert and WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Bossman; Edge and Christian overcame Kurt Angle and Steve Blackman; WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg overturned Faarooq in a no-DQ handicap match; The Godfather and D-Lo Brown bested The Headbangers; Kane pinned WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho in a non-title match; WWF World Champion Triple H and X-Pac rolled over The Rock and The Big Show.

Air Date: 20th January 2000
X-Pac downed WWF Hardcore Champion Test in a non-title match; Al Snow and Steve Blackman defeated Kurt Angle and Davey Boy Smith; Matt Hardy bested D-Von Dudley by DQ; The Big Bossman battled Albert to a no-contest; Rikishi and Too Cool bested WWF Co-Intercontinental Champions Chris Jericho and Chyna, and Hardcore Holly by DQ; Viscera bested Crash Holly; Gangrel overcame Edge by count-out; Kane overturned The Rock and The Big Show in a lumberjack over-the-top-rope Triple Threat elimination match.

Air Date: 27th January 2000
Al Snow defeated Billy Gunn by DQ; Edge and Christian dropped The Dydley Boys; Viscera overcame Hardcore Holly; Kane battled The Big Show to a no-contest; Too Cool battled The Godfather and D-Lo Brown to a no-contest; WWF Hardcore Champion Test defeated Gangrel; Tazz overturned Rodney Gas and Pete Gas in a handicap match; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho dropped Crash Holly; The Rock battled Rikishi to a no-contest.

Feb 2000

Air Date: 3rd February 2000
WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho overturned Gangrel; X-Pac pinned Dean Malenko; Jackie defeated WWF Women’s Champion Harvey Wippleman to win the title; Kurt Angle overcame The Rock and Tazz in a Triple Threat match; WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg downed Eddie Guerrero and Perry Saturn; Al Snow and Steve Blackman beat Crash and Hardcore Holly; Rikishi bested The Big Show by DQ; WWF World Champion Triple H rolled over Chris Benoit.

Air Date: 10th February 2000
Kurt Angle bested WWF European Champion Val Venis to win the title; Perry Saturn bested Brian Christopher; Edge and Christian defeated The Dudley Boys; Dean Malenko downed Scotty 2 Hotty; The Rock and Cactus Jack dropped WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg in a non-title match; Gangrel and Luna beat Albert and WWF Women’s Champion Jackie; Rikishi toppled Chris Benoit by DQ; Al Snow and Steve Blackman defeated Matt and Jeff Hardy; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho bested Crash and Hardcore Holly in a no-DQ handicap match; Kane rolled over WWF World Champion Triple H and X-Pac by DQ in a handicap match.

Air Date: 17th February 2000
Kane rolled over WWF World Champion Triple H by DQ; Chyna toppled WWF European Champion Kurt Angle by DQ; Tazz overcame Gangrel; Cactus Jack battled X-Pac to a no-contest in a hardcore match; Dean Malenko and Perry Saturn downed Too Cool; Eddie Guerrero overturned Rikishi by forfeit; Edge and Christian dropped Albert and The Big Bossman; Mark Henry defeated Crash and Hardcore Holly in a no-DQ handicap match; The Rock and The Big Show battled WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg to a no-contest.

Air Date: 24th February 2000
Crash Holly beat WWF Hardcore Champion Test to win the title; Kane battled Chris Benoit to a double count-out; Dean Malenko, Perry Saturn, Al Snow and Steve Blackman toppled Edge, Christian, Matt and Jeff Hardy; WWF World Champion Triple H, X-Pac and Road Dogg rolled over Rikishi and Too Cool; The Godfather and D-Lo Brown bested The Big Bossman and Albert; Billy Gunn overturned Bubba Ray Dudley by DQ; The Big Show and WWF European Champion Kurt Angle rolled over The Rock and WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho by DQ.

Mar 2000

Air Date 2nd March 2000
Chris Benoit, Dean Malenko and Perry Saturn beat Chris Jericho and Too Cool; Jeff Hardy downed WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Essa Rios by DQ; WWF Hardcore Champion Crash Holly dropped Tazz; X-Pac and Road Dogg overturned Edge and Christian; Test overcame Viscera; WWF Intercontinental and European Champion Kurt Angle toppled Sgt Slaughter; WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys bested Kane by DQ in a handicap match; The Rock and Rikishi rolled over WWF World Champion Triple H and The Big Show.

Air Date: 9th March 2000
The Rock toppled Chris Benoit and Perry Saturn in a handicap match; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Essa Rios defeated Christian by DQ; Tazz downed WWF Intercontinental and European Champion Kurt Angle by DQ; Road Dogg and X-Pac dropped Kane in a handicap match; Steve Blackman overcame Jeff Hardy; WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys overturned The Rock in a handicap tables match; WWF Women’s Champion Jackie pinned Ivory; WWF Hardcore Champion Crash Holly battled Albert to a no-contest; WWF World Champion Triple H and The Big Show rolled over Rikishi in a handicap match.

Air Date: 16th March 2000
WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys overcame Edge and Christian; The Godfather pinned WWF World Champion Triple H in a non-title match; The Acolytes bested Al Snow and Steve Blackman; Rikishi dropped The Big Show; Chris Jericho and Tazz overturned WWF Intercontinental and European Champion Kurt Angle and Bob Backlund by DQ; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko rolled over Brian Christopher; Matt and Jeff Hardy battled Chris Benoit and Perry Saturn to a double count-out; The Rock toppled X-Pac by DQ.

Air Date: 23rd March 2000
WWF Intercontinental and European Champion Kurt Angle battled Chris Benoit to a double-DQ; The Godfather toppled Bull Buchanan by DQ;  Al Snow and Steve Blackman defeated WWF Hardcore Champion Crash Holly and Hardcore Holly by count-out; The Rock dropped Bubba Ray Dudley; WWF Women’s Champion Jackie pinned Lita; The Big Show overturned Rodney Gas, Pete Gas and Joey Abs in a handicap match; Rikishi and Too Cool rolled over Eddie Guerrero, Perry Saturn and WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko.

Air Date: 30th March 2000
Kane and Rikishi defeated WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys in a non-title match; Tazz battled WWF Hardcore Champion Crash Holly to a no-contest; Road Dogg and X-Pac bested Edge and Christian; The Rock rolled over WWF Intercontinental and European Champion Kurt Angle in a non-title match; Chris Jericho toppled Eddie Guerrero by DQ; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko bested Scotty 2 Hotty; Matt and Jeff Hardy overcame Test and Albert; Stephanie McMahon overturned WWF Women’s Champion Jackie to win the title.

Apr 2000

Air Date: 6th April 2000
WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit downed Rikishi; The Dudley Boys dropped Test and Albert; Tazz battled Perry Saturn to a no-contest; The Big Bossman and Bull Buchanan defeated Al Snow and Steve Blackman; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko overcame Taka Michinoku; The Big Show pinned The Godfather; WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian toppled Hardcore Holly and Kurt Angle; The Rock battled Road Dogg and X-Pac to a no-contest in a handicap match.

Air Date: 13th April 2000
Rikishi overcame WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero by DQ; Matt and Jeff Hardy downed WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit and WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko; Perry Saturn pinned WWF Hardcore Champion Crash Holly to win the title; Tazz pinned WWF Hardcore Champion Perry Saturn to win the title; Crash Holly pinned WWF Hardcore Champion Tazz to win the title; WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian dropped Kurt Angle and The Big Show by count-out; Bubba Ray Dudley overcame Albert; The Kat and Mae Young defeated Terri and The Fabulous Moolah; X-Pac and Dogg bested Chris Jericho in a handicap match; The Rock and The Acolytes battled WWF World Champion Triple H, The Big Bossman and Bull Buchanan to a no-contest.

Air Date: 20th April 2000
Road Dogg overturned Edge; The Dudley Boys defeated WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero and Essa Rios; The Big Show battled Rikishi to a double count-out; WW World Champion Triple H toppled ECW World Champion Tazz; Test and Albert overcame The Godfather and D-Lo Brown; X-Pac bested Christian; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit overturned Kurt Angle by DQ; Chris Jericho toppled The Rock in a lumberjack match.

Air Date: 27th April 2000
Essa Rios and Val Venis defeated WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero and Chyna; Dean Malenko bested WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Scotty 2 Hotty to win the title; Test and Albert dropped Hardcore and Crash Holly; Chris Jericho and Tazz toppled WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit and Perry Saturn; Christian beat Road Dogg by reverse decision-DQ; Rikishi and The Big Show toppled Kurt Angle, The Big Bossman and Bull Buchanan; Crash Holly pinned WWF Hardcore Champion Matt Hardy to win the title; The Godfather dropped Steve Blackman by DQ.

May 2000

Air Date: 4th May 2000
Chris Jericho rolled over WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit to win the title; WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero and Chyna downed Essa Rios and Lita; Kurt Angle toppled Hardcore Holly; Rikishi defeated WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian by count-out in a handicap match; Matt and Jeff Hardy downed Test and Albert; The Big Show battled Bull Buchanan to a no-contest; WWF Hardcore Champion Crash Holly overcame Dean Malenko; WWF World Champion The Rock and Earl Hebner rolled over Triple H, X-Pac, and Road Dogg in a handicap match.

Air Date: 11th May 2000
Test and Albert overcame The Dudley Boys; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit overcame The Godfather; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko dropped Chyna; Kurt Angle, and WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian downed Chris Jericho and The Hardy Boys; Hardcore Holly battled WWF Hardcore Champion Davey Boy Smith to a no-contest; (Crash Holly overcame WWF Hardcore Champion Davey Boy Smith to win the title); The Dudley Boys overturned The Big Show in a handicap match; WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero dropped Perry Saturn; Triple H, X-Pac, and Road Dogg defeated Rikishi and Too Cool in an elimination match.

Air Date: 18th May 2000
Kurt Angle toppled Scotty 2 Hotty; Perry Saturn battled WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko to a no-contest. Eddie Guerrero was guest referee; Brian Christopher beat Christian; Chris Jericho and Val Venis overturned WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit and Hardcore Holly by DQ; Rikishi toppled Edge by DQ; Shane McMahon downed The Big Show; (Gerald Brisco dropped WWF Hardcore Champion Crash Holly to win the title); WWF World Champion The Rock battled The Dudley Boys to a no-contest in a handicap lumberjack tables match.

Air Date: 25th May 2000
Eddie Guerrero and Chyna defeated WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian by DQ; Faarooq pinned Crash Holly; Hardcore Holly overcame Chris Jericho and Val Venis in a three-way; Matt Hardy overcame Essa Rios; Too Cool and The Dudley Boys overturned Test, Albert, X-Pac, and Road Dogg; Kurt Angle toppled WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit by DQ; Bull Buchanon downed Al Snow; The Undertaker battled Shane McMahon to a no-contest.

Jun 2000

Air Date: 1st June 2000
The Hardy Boys overturned WWF World Tag Champions Too Cool by DQ; Kurt Angle toppled Bradshaw (KOTR Qualifier); WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit overcame D-Lo Brown; Terri Runnels overturned The Kat in an arm-wrestling match; The Undertaker beat X-Pac and Road Dogg in a handicap match; Chris Jericho dropped Hardcore Holly;  The Rock rolled over Edge and Christian in a handicap match; Crash Holly overcame Albert (KOTR Qualifier); Kane toppled WWF World Champion Triple H in a non-title match.

Air Date: 8th June 2000
Chris Jericho dropped Test (KOTR Qualifier); WWF World Tag Champions Too Cool bested The Godfather and WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit overcame Jeff Hardy; The Dudley Boys overturned Kurt Angle, Edge, and Christian in a handicap match; Lita won a battle royal; WWF Women’s Champion Stephanie McMahon downed Lita. Tori was guest referee; WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero dropped Matt Hardy (KOTR Qualifier); Rikishi toppled Val Venis by DQ; The Rock, The Undertaker, and Kane toppled WWF World Champion Triple H, Road Dogg, and X-Pac.

Air Date: 15th June 2000
Edge toppled Brian Christopher (KOTR Qualifier); Test and Albert overcame The Hardy Boys; Scotty 2 Hotty overcame D-Lo Brown (KOTR Qualifier); Kane toppled WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit by DQ; Edge and Christian battled The Dudley Boys to a double count-out; Bull Buchanon downed Chris Jericho; The Rock rolled over Kurt Angle by DQ.

Air Date: 22nd June 2000
Test, Albert, and Trish Stratus overturned Lita and The Hardy Boys; Brian Christopher beat Christian; The Big Bossman downed WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero by DQ; Rikishi toppled WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit to win the title; Chris Jericho dropped Hardcore Holly; Jerry Lawler and The Kat overcame Dean Malenko and Terri; Road Dogg pinned D-Von Dudley; WWF World Champion Triple H, Shane McMahon, and Vince McMahon downed The Rock, Kane, and The Undertaker.

Air Date: 29th June 2000
Val Venis and Chris Benoit overcame WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero and Rikishi by DQ; Kurt Angle toppled Hardcore Holly; Bull Buchanon downed The Godfather; Christian pinned Kane; The Undertaker beat Edge; Al Snow battled WWF Hardcore Champion Crash Holly to a no-contest (Steve Blackman downed WWF Hardcore Champion Crash Holly to win the title); Triple H, Road Dogg, and X-Pac defeated Chris Jericho and The Dudley Boys.

Jul 2000

Air Date: 6th July 2000
Kurt Angle toppled Chris Jericho; The Dudley Boys overturned D-Lo Brown and Chaz; Jackie and Ivory overcame Dean Malenko in a handicap match; Val Venis overturned WWF Intercontinental Champion Rikishi to win the title; WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman downed The Big Bossman; The Undertaker and Kane toppled Test and Albert; The Brooklyn Brawler, Taka Michinoku, and Sho Funaki toppled Triple H in a handicap elimination match; WWF World Champion The Rock, WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero, and Chyna defeated Chris Benoit and WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian.

Air Date: 13th July 2000
Too Cool and Rikishi toppled WWF Intercontinental Champion Val Venis, Test, and Albert; X-Pac and Road Dogg defeated The Hardy Boys; Dean Malenko bested Al Snow; Eddie Guerrero and Chyna defeated Chris Benoit by DQ in a handicap match; Faarooq pinned Christian; Kane toppled Kurt Angle; WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman downed Crash Holly; Triple H pinned The Undertaker in a no-DQ match.

Air Date: 20th July 2000
WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero dropped Dean Malenko and Perry Saturn in a three-way; Kane toppled Kurt Angle by DQ; WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman downed Al Snow; Rikishi and The Hardy Boys overturned Test, Albert, and Val Venis; Bull Buchanon pinned The Godfather; The Big Bossman battled The Undertaker to a no-contest; Triple H and Chris Benoit overcame WWF World Champion The Rock and Chris Jericho by DQ.

Air Date: 27th July 2000
WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman downed X-Pac; The Hardy Boys overturned D-Lo Brown and Chaz; Tazz defeated Brian Christopher by DQ; Kane and The Dudley Boys overturned The Big Show and WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian in a tables match; WWF European Champion Perry Saturn pinned Crash Holly; Chris Jericho and Lita defeated Chris Benoit and Trish Stratus; Kurt Angle toppled WWF World Champion The Rock by DQ.

Aug 2000

Air Date: 3rd August 2000
X-Pac pinned Scotty 2 Hotty; Eddie Guerrero and Rikishi toppled WWF Intercontinental Champion Val Venis and Tazz; Matt Hardy overcame Edge by DQ; Chris Benoit and The Big Show defeated WWF World Champion The Rock and Kane; WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman downed Perry Saturn; The Goodfather and Bull Buchanon bested The Acolytes; Road Dogg pinned Brian Christopher; Triple H, Kurt Angle, and WWF Women’s Champion Stephanie McMahon downed The Dudley Boys and Lita in a no-DQ match.

Air Date: 10th August 2000
Chris Benoit and WWF Intercontinental Champion Val Venis overturned Chris Jericho and Eddie Guerrero; Rikishi toppled X-Pac; WWF European Champion Perry Saturn and Terri Runnels overturned Al Snow and The Kat; The Dudley Boys overturned WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian by DQ; Tazz battled Crash Holly to a no-contest; Bull Buchanon and The Goodfather defeated Too Cool; WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman downed Albert; WWF World Champion The Rock and The Undertaker beat Triple H and Kurt Angle.

Air Date: 17th August 2000
WWF European Champion Perry Saturn and Terri toppled Rikishi and The Kat; WWF Intercontinental Champion Val Venis overturned Eddie Guerrero; The Acolytes and Crash Holly overcame Sho Funaki, Taka Michinoku, and The Big Bossman; WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman downed Chris Benoit; WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian downed D-Von Dudley and Jeff Hardy; Too Cool battled X-Pac and Road Dogg to a no-contest; Triple H pinned WWF World Champion The Rock in a non-title match.

Air Date: 24th August 2000
Rikishi toppled The Goodfather; Eddie Guerrero, Chyna, and Steve Blackman downed Test, Albert, and Trish Stratus; The Undertaker beat X-Pac and Road Dogg in a handicap match; WWF European Champion Perry Saturn downed Al Snow; Chris Jericho and The Hardy Boys overturned Chris Benoit and WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian; WWF World Champion The Rock and WWF Women’s Champion Lita defeated Kurt Angle and Stephanie McMahon by DQ. 

Air Date: 31st August 2000
Road Dogg pinned Bull Buchanon; WWF Women’s Champion Lita, and The Hardy Boys overturned Jackie, and WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian; Al Snow defeated WWF European Champion Perry Saturn to win the title; The Undertaker beat Chris Benoit by DQ; Chris Jericho dropped Tazz; Sho Funaki and Taka Michinoku defeated The Dudley Boys; Val Venis overturned Rikishi; Kurt Angle toppled Eddie Guerrero.

Sep 2000

Air Date: 7th September 2000
X-Pac pinned Chris Jericho; Chris Benoit overcame Rikishi; Triple H bested Test and Albert in a handicap match; WWF Intercontinental Champion Eddie Guerrero dropped Road Dogg; Too Cool bested Bull Buchanon and The Goodfather; The Undertaker beat Kurt Angle by DQ; WWF World Champion The Rock rolled over Christian and Kane in a three-way.

Air Date: 14th September 2000
The Acolytes overturned Kane and Chris Benoit in an “Acolytes Rules” match; Bull Buchanon, The Goodfather, and Val Venis overturned Too Cool and Rikishi; Test and Albert overcame Kurt Angle and Triple H; WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman downed Perry Saturn; X-Pac and WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian downed Chris Jericho and The Hardy Boys; The Dudley Boys overturned The Undertaker and WWF World Champion The Rock in a tables match. 

Air Date: 21st September 2000
Val Venis pinned Scott 2 Hotty; WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman downed Taka Michinoku; Jerry Lawler and Chris Jericho dropped Tazz and X-Pac by DQ; The Acolytes overturned Bull Buchanon and The Goodfather; WWF World Champion The Rock, The Undertaker, and Triple H bested Kurt Angle, Chris Benoit, and Kane.

Air Date: 28th September 2000
WWE Women’s Champion Lita beat Jackie; WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman defeated Road Dogg; Rikishi and Too Cool downed Kane, Test, and Albert; Raven and Tazz dropped Chris Jericho and Jerry Lawler; Chyna bested Steven Richards; WWF European Champion Al Snow battled X-Pac to a no-contest; Kurt Angle and Chris Benoit rolled over WWF World Champion The Rock and Triple H.

Oct 2000

Air Date: 5th October 2000
D-Lo Brown, Chaz, and Jackie toppled WWF Women’s Champion Lita and WWF World Tag Champions The Hardy Boys; WWF European Champion Al Snow pinned X-Pac; The Goodfather, Bull Buchanon, and Val Venis overcame WWF Intercontinental Champion Eddie Guerrero and The Acolytes; Tazz and Raven downed The Dudley Boys by DQ; Kurt Angle, Edge, and Christian downed Rikishi and Too Cool; WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman downed Albert; Chris Benoit overcame Chris Jericho by DQ; Kane toppled WWF World Champion The Rock by DQ.

Air Date: 12th October 2000
The Dudley Boys overturned D-Lo Brown and Chaz in a tables match; WWF Intercontinental Champion Eddie Guerrero dropped Chris Jericho and X-Pac in a three-way; WWF European Champion Al Snow pinned Albert; Triple H and Road Dogg defeated Chris Benoit and Perry Saturn; WWF World Tag Champions The Hardy Boys downed Tazz and Raven; Kurt Angle toppled Kane; WWF World Champion The Rock rolled over Val Venis.

Air Date: 19th October 2000
The Conquistadors (Edge and Christian under masks) won a tag team battle royal;  WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman downed Tazz; WWF European Champion William Regal bested Al Snow and Perry Saturn in a three-way; Road Dogg pinned Raven; Chris Jericho, and WWF World Tag Champions The Hardy Boys battled X-Pac, Edge, and Christian to a no-contest; WWF Women’s Champion Lita defeated Trish Stratus by DQ; Kurt Angle toppled Triple H, Chris Benoit, and WWF World Champion The Rock in a non-title four-way match.

Air Date: 26th October 2000
WWF World Tag Champions The Hardy Boys bested The Dudley Boys by DQ; Chris Benoit and Dean Malenko dropped Triple H and Road Dogg; Chris Jericho dropped WWF World Champion Kurt Angle by DQ; Albert overcame Crash Holly in a hardcore match; The Rock, Billy Gunn, and Chyna toppled Val Venis, Bull Buchanon, and The Goodfather; Raven downed Al Snow; Rikishi toppled Too Cool in a handicap match.

Nov 2000

Air Date: 2nd November 2000
Crash Holly overcame Test and Albert in a handicap hardcore match; Ivory overcame WWF Women’s Champion Lita, Trish Stratus, and Jackie in a four-way to win the title; Chris Jericho dropped Rikishi by DQ; Billy Gunn and Chyna defeated Val Venis and WWF Intercontinental Champion Eddie Guerrero; Al Snow pinned Tazz; Chris Benoit overcame Triple H; The Rock rolled over WWF World Champion Kurt Angle by DQ.

Air Date: 9th November 2000
Billy Gunn downed WWF Intercontinental Champion Eddie Guerrero by DQ; Matt and Jeff Hardy dropped Edge and Christian; The Undertaker defeated Chris Jericho, Kane and Chris Benoit in a Fatal Four Way; WWF World Tag Champions The Goodfather and Bull Buchanan overturned The Dudley Boys; Crash Holly and Steve Blackman bested Test and Albert in a hardcore match; The Rock and Steve Austin battled Triple H and Rikishi to a no-contest.

Air Date: 16th November 2000
Hardcore and Crash Folly beat Test and Albert; Too Cool defeated Tazz and Raven; Steve Austin bested WWF Intercontinental Champion Eddie Guerrero in a non-title match; The Dudley Boys, and Matt and Jeff Hardy downed Steven Richards, Val Venis, and WWF World Tag Champions The Goodfather and Bull Buchanon; WWF World Champion Kurt Angle and Kane toppled Chris Jericho and The Undertaker in a no-DQ match; Road Dogg and K-Kwick dropped WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko and Perry Saturn; The Rock rolled over Chris Benoit by DQ.

Air Date: 23rd November 2000
Billy Gunn downed WWF Intercontinental Champion Eddie Guerrero to win the title; Test and Albert overcame Hardcore and Crash Holly in a hardcore match; Val Venis overturned K-Kwik ; The Rock rolled over WWF European Champion William Regal in a non-title match; Raven downed Tazz; Molly Holly overcame Trish Stratus; Chris Jericho and Steve Austin battled Kane and Chris Benoit to a no-contest.

Air Date: 30th November  2000
Eddie Guerrero, Dean Malenko, and Perry Saturn downed Lita and The Hardy Boys; WWF World Tag Champions Bull Buchanon and The Goodfather defeated Road Dogg and K-Kwik; WWF European Champion William Regal bested Scotty 2 Hotty; Chris Benoit and Kane toppled Chris Jericho and WWF Intercontinental Champion Billy Gunn; D-Lo Brown, Chaz, and Tiger Ali Singh bested The Dudley Boys and WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman in a tables match; Hardcore Holly overcame Raven in a hardcore match; The Rock and The Undertaker beat WWF World Champion Kurt Angle, Edge and Christian in a handicap match.

Dec 2000

Air Date: 7th December 2000
Val Venis and WWF Women’s Champion Ivory overcame WWF Intercontinental Champion Billy Gunn and Chyna; Molly Holly overcame Trish Stratus by DQ; Chris Jericho and Hardcore Holly overcame Kane and WWF European Champion William Regal; Road Dogg and K-Kwik rolled over Edge and Christian; WWF World Champion Kurt Angle, Steve Austin, The Rock, and The Undertaker battled to a no-contest in a four-way.

Air Date: 14th December 2000
WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian downed The Dudley Boys; WWF Women’s Champion Ivory overcame Lita; Val Venis defeated Billy Gunn by DQ; The Undertaker beat Raven in a hardcore match; The Hardy Boys overturned WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit and Perry Saturn; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko dropped Chris Jericho; The Rock and Steve Austin downed WWF World Champion Kurt Angle and WWF European Champion William Regal.

Air Date: 21st December 2000
The Dudley Boys overturned D-Lo Brown and Chaz; WWF Women’s Champion Ivory pinned Jackie; Chris Jericho, and The Hardy Boys overturned WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit, Dean Malenko, and Perry Saturn; WWF European Champion William Regal battled Hardcore Holly to a no-contest. Steve Austin was guest referee; Crash and Molly Holly overcame Taka Michinoku and Sho Funaki; WWF Hardcore Champion Steve Blackman downed Raven; Billy Gunn downed Steven Richards; Edge and Christian downed WWF World Tag Champions The Rock and The Undertaker to win the titles. Kurt Angle was guest referee.

Air Date: 28th December 2000
WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian downed Chris Jericho in a handicap match; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit overcame Matt Hardy; WWF World Champion Kurt Angle toppled Bubba Ray Dudley; Bull Buchanon, The Goodfather, and Ivory overcame The Acolytes and Jackie; WWF Hardcore Champion Raven downed Jeff Hardy; Test and Albert overcame Too Cool; The Rock and The Undertaker beat Rikishi, William Regal, and Kane in a handicap match.
