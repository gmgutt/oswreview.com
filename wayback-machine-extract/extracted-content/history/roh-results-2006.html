ROH Results 2006

Jan 2006

14th January 2006: “ROH Hell Freezes Over”: Phildelphia, PA – 900
Austin Aries beat Ricky Reyes; ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinness overcame Tony Mamaluke; Alex Shelley and Jimmy Rave downed Claudio Castagnoli and Azireal; Bryan Danielson defeated FIP Champion Homicide and Roderick Strong in a no-DQ, three-way to win the title; Christopher Daniels pinned Jay Lethal; Jack Evans overcame Adam Pearce, Jason Blade, Kid Mikaze, Sal Rinauro and Trik Davis in a six-way match; Samoa Joe bested BJ Whitmer; AJ Styles toppled Matt Sydal; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson rolled over Chris Hero.

27th January 2006: “ROH Tag Wars 2006”: Dayton, OH – 600
Jack Evans, Matt Sydal and Jimmy Yang overcame Adam Pearce, BJ Whitmer and Jimmy Jacobs (Trios Tournament – Semi-Final); Alex Shelley, Abyss and Jimmy Rave overturned Tony Mamaluke, Jay Firy and Sal Rinauro (Trios Tournament – Semi-Final); Claudio Castagnoli downed Chad Collyer; Ace Steel battled Sterling Keenan to a no-contest; ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinness beat Delirious; Low Ki dropped Christopher Daniels; Abyss, Alex Shelley and Jimmy Rave toppled Matt Sydal, Jack Evans and Jimmy Yang (Trios Tournament – Final); ROH Tag Champions Austin Aries and Roderick Strong rolled over Bryan Danielson and Jay Lethal.

28th January 2006: “ROH Dissension”: Cleveland, OH – 600
Adam Pearce beat Jay Fury; Jimmy Yang bested Jay Lethal; Delirious, Sal Rinauro and Tony Mamaluke overturned Abyss, Alex Shelley and Jimmy Rave; Low Ki defeated Jack Evans; Claudio Castagnoli and Ace Steel downed ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinness and Chad Collyer; ROH Tag Champions Austin Aries and Roderick Strong toppled Jimmy Jacobs and BJ Whitmer; Christopher Daniels bested Matt Sydal; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson rolled over AJ Styles.

Feb 2006

11th February 2006: “ROH Unscripted II”: Long Island, NY – 500
Adam Pearce overcame Mitch Franklin and Pelle Primeau in a three-way; Colt Cabana defeated Grim Reefer; Ricky Reyes downed Kid Mikaze; ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinness dropped Austin Aries; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson toppled Xavier by DQ; Jimmy Yang bested Azrieal, Jason Blade and Jerrelle Clark in a four-way; Roderick Strong overturned BJ Whitmer; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson and CM Punk rolled over Adam Pearce and Jimmy Rave.

25th February 2006: “ROH 4th Anniversary Show”: Edison, NJ
Jay and Mark Briscoe overcame Jason Blade and Kid Mikaze, and Tony Mamaluke and Sal Rinauro; Adam Pearce beat Claudio Castagnoli, Jay Fury and Azireal in a four-way; Samoa Joe bested Jay Lethal; Christopher Daniels battled BJ Whitmer to a no-contest; Homicide defeated Colt Cabana in a Ghetto Fight; Bobby Dempsey overturned Mitch Franklin; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson dropped Jimmy Rave; Ricky Reyes bested Jack Evans; ROH Tag Champions Austin Aries and Roderick Strong rolled over AJ Styles and Matt Sydal.

Mar 2006

11th March 2006: “ROH Arena Warfare”: Philadelphia, PA
Homicide and Ricky Reyes downed Mark and Jay Briscoe, Marcos and Dunn, and Jason Blade and Kid Mikaze in a four-way; Roderick Strong dropped Jimmy Yang; Austin Aries downed Matt Sydal; Jake and Dave Crist overcame Tony Mamaluke and Sal Rinauro; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson bested Alex Shelley; Colt Cabana overturned Christopher Daniels; Samoa Joe toppled Colt Cabana.

25th March 2006: “ROH Best in the World”: New York, NY
Jimmy Rave toppled Pelle Primeau; Jimmy Yang dropped Jimmy Rave; Allison Danger defeated Daize Haze, Lacey, and Mercedes Martinez in a four-way; Chris Hero and Necro Butcher beat Jason Blade and Kid Makaze; Adam Pearce battled Chris Hero to a no contest; Christopher Daniels downed Alex Shelley; ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinness downed Claudio Castagnoli; Austin Aries downed Ricky Reyes by DQ; Jack Evans and Roderick Strong dropped Mark and Jay Briscoe; KENTA and Naomichi Marufuji beat ROH Champion Bryan Danielson and Samoa Joe.

30th March 2006: “ROH Dragon Gate Challenge”: Detroit, MI
Homicide toppled Colt Cabana in a “falls count anywhere” match; Ricky Reyes downed Chad Collyer; BJ Whitmer pinned Jimmy Jacobs; Ryo Saito beat Jimmy Yang; Alex Shelley and Jimmy Rave defeated ROH Champion Bryan Danielson and Delirious; Claudio Castagnoli beat Shane Hagadorn; AJ Styles and Matt Sydal defeated Dragon Kid and Genki Horiguchi; CIMA, Naruki Doi, and Masato Yoshino defeated Jack Evans, and ROH Tag Team Champions Austin Aries and Roderick Strong; Christopher Daniels rolled over Samoa Joe.

31st March 2006: “ROH Supercard of Honor”: Chicago Ridge, IL – 1,100
Ricky Reyes downed Flash Flanagan, Delirious, and Shane Hagadorn in a four-way; Jimmy Rave and Alex Shelley defeated Jimmy Yang and Claudio Castagnoli; Ace Steel dropped Chad Collyer in a first blood match; AJ Styles and Matt Sydal bested Jack Evans and Austin Aries; Dragon Kid, Genki Horiguchi, and Ryo Saito overturned CIMA, Naruki Doi, and Masato Yoshino in a “Dragon Gate Invitational”; MsChif won a six-way over Lacey, Allison Danger, Cheerleader Melissa, Rain, and Daizee Haze; Homicide toppled Mitch Franklin; Samoa Joe toppled Christopher Daniels and Jimmy Jacobs in a three-way; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson bested Roderick Strong.

Apr 2006

1st April 2006: “ROH Better than our Best”: Chicago Ridge, IL – 1,500
Jack Evans downed Ace Steel, Matt Sydal, Jimmy Jacobs, Jake Crist, and Dave Crist in a six-way; Delirious downed Ricky Reyes; Jimmy Rave, Alex Shelley, and Masato Yoshino defeated Dragon Kid, Genki Horiguchi, and Ryo Saito; Samoa Joe toppled AJ Styles, Christopher Daniels, and Jimmy Yang in a four-way; ROH Tag Team Champions Austin Aries and Roderick Strong dropped Naruki Doi and CIMA; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson bested Lance Storm; Colt Cabana overturned Homicide in a streetfight.

22nd April 2006: “ROH The 100th Show”: Philadelphia, PA – 1,000
Christopher Daniels downed Claudio Castagnoli; Delirious downed Jimmy Jacobs, Jimmy Yang, and Jimmy Rave in a four-way; ROH Tag Champions Austin Aries and Roderick Strong beat Homicide and Ricky Reyes; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson bested Colt Cabana; Derrick Dempsey defeated Pelle Primeau; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson rolled over Delirious; Jay and Mark Briscoe overturned AJ Styles and Matt Sydal; Chris Hero, Necro Butcher, and Super Dragon toppled Samoa Joe, BJ Whitmer, and Adam Pearce.

28th April 2006: “ROH Weekend of Champions – Night 1”: Dayton, OH – 500
Colt Cabana overturned Jimmy Jacobs; Dave and Jake Christ overcame Spud and Jay Fury; Delirious fought Jimmy Rave to a draw; ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinness downed Christopher Daniels by count-out; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson bested Jimmy Yang; ROH Tag Team Champions Austin Aries and Roderick Strong dropped Samoa Joe and Matt Sydal; Necro Butcher, Nate Webb, Claudio Castagnoli, and Super Dragon defeated Colt Cabana, Ace Steel, BJ Whitmer, and Adam Pearce in a streetfight.

29th April 2006: “ROH Weekend of Champions – Night 2”: Cleveland, OH
Colt Cabana and Conrad Kennedy III beat Dave Crist and Jake Crist; Delirious downed Chris Sabin; Jimmy Jacobs defeated Jimmy Yang, Trik Davis, Spud, Jay Fury, and Flash Flanagan in a six-way; Samoa Joe toppled Claudio Castagnoli by DQ; BJ Whitmer pinned Super Dragon; Christopher Daniels downed Matt Sydal; ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinness toppled ROH Champion Bryan Danielson by count-out in a “Pure Wrestling Rules” match; ROH Tag Team Champions Austin Aries and Roderick Strong dropped Alex Shelley and Jimmy Rave.

May 2006

12th May 2006: “ROH How We Roll”: Uniondale, NY – 600
Matt Sydal pinned Jimmy Rave; Delirious downed Nigel McGuinness and Kikutaro in a three-way; Alex Shelley and Jimmy Rave toppled Homicide and Ricky Reyes; Chris Hero and Claudio Castagnoli beat Adam Pearce and BJ Whitmer; Samoa Joe toppled Apocalypse; Mark Briscoe toppled Roderick Strong; Jay Briscoe beat Austin Aries; NWA World Champion Christian Cage and Colt Cabana overturned ROH Champion Bryan Danielson and Christopher Daniels.

13th May 2006: “ROH Ring of Homicide”: Edison, NJ
Colt Cabana overturned Kikutaro; Homicide and Ricky Reyes defeated Dunn and Marcos; Jimmy Rave toppled Jimmy Yang; ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinness downed Jay Lethal; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson bested Delirious;  Christopher Daniels downed Matt Sydal; ROH Tag Team Champions Austin Aries and Roderick Strong dropped Mark and Jay Briscoe; Samoa Joe fought Necro Butcher to a no contest; Homicide toppled Necro Butcher.

June 2006

3rd June 2006: “ROH Destiny”: East Windsor, CT – 600
Davey Richards downed Jimmy Rave; Shane Hagadorn bested Derrick Dempsey; Sara Del Ray defeated Daizee Haze; Colt Cabana overturned Adam Pearce, Delirious, and Jason Blade in a four-way; Christopher Daniels downed BJ Whitmer; Shingo Takagi defeated Ricky Reyes; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson bested Homicide; ROH Tag Team Champions Austin Aries and Roderick Strong dropped Mark and Jay Briscoe.

17th June 2006: “ROH In Your Face”: New York, NY – 900
Mark and Jay Briscoe toppled Jason Blade and Sterling James Keenan; Jake Crist pinned Marcos; Ricky Reyes downed David Crist; Davey Richards downed Jimmy Rave; ROH Tag Team Champions Austin Aries and Roderick Strong dropped Colt Cabana and Nigel McGuinness; BJ Whitmer fought Jimmy Jacobs to a no-contest; Shane Hagadorn bested Mitch Franklin; Adam Pearce toppled Claudio Castagnoli by DQ; Homicide toppled Chris Hero; KENTA overturned ROH Champion Bryan Danielson and Samoa Joe in a non-title three-way.

23rd June 2006: “ROH Throwdown”: Detroit, MI
Gutter beat Bobby Dempsey; CJ Otis defeated Rhett Titus; Dave and Jake Crist overcame Keith Walker and Shane Hagadorn; Mark and Jay Briscoe toppled Ace Steel and Colt Cabana; Delirious downed Davey Richards, Jimmy Rave, and Samoa Joe in a four-way; Adam Pearce toppled Necro Butcher by DQ; Homicide toppled Claudio Castagnoli; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson bested BJ Whitmer and Jimmy Jacobs in a three-way elimination match; KENTA overturned Roderick Strong.

24th June 2006: “ROH Chi-Town Struggle”: Chicago Ridge, IL
Jake and David Crist overcame Shane Hagadorn and Trik Davis; Roderick Strong dropped Jimmy Jacobs; Mark and Jay Briscoe toppled Jimmy Rave and Conrad Kennedy III; Samoa Joe toppled Delirious; Claudio Castagnoli, Necro Butcher, and Nate Webb defeated Adam Pearce, Ace Steel, and BJ Whitmer in a bunkhouse match; ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinness downed Homicide by count-out; KENTA overturned Austin Aries; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson bested Colt Cabana.

July 2006

15th July 2006: “ROH Death Before Dishonor IV”: Philadelphia, PA – 1,100
Delirious downed Seth Delay; Jimmy Rave and Sal Rinauro defeated Colt Cabana and Jay Lethal; ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinness downed Roderick Strong by count-out; Jay and Mark Briscoe toppled Dave Crist and Jake Crist; AJ Styles downed Davey Richards; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson bested Sonjay Dutt by stoppage; Samoa Joe, BJ Whitmer, ROH Champion Bryan Danielson, Adam Pearce, Ace Steel, and Homicide rolled over Claudio Castagnoli, CZW Heavyweight Champion Chris Hero, Nate Webb, Necro Butcher, and Eddie Kingston in a “Cage of Death” match.

28th July 2006: “ROH War of the Wire II”: Dayton, OH – 600
Jimmy Jacobs downed Trik Davis; Davey Richards and Jerrelle Clark defeated Jimmy Rave and Sal Rinauro; ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinness downed Colt Cabana by count-out; Matt Sydal defeated Christopher Daniels; David and Jake Crist overcame Alex Payne and Pelle Primeau; Austin Aries downed ROH Champion Bryan Danielson, Delirious, and Homicide in a non-title four-way match; Mark and Jay Briscoe toppled Roderick Strong and Jack Evans; BJ Whitmer defeated Necro Butcher in a “no rope barbed wire” match.

29th July 2006: “ROH Generation Now”: Cleveland, OH – 500
Alex Payne, Bobby Dempsey, and Rhett Titus downed Pelle Primeau, CJ Otis, and Trik Davis; Shane Hagadorn bested Egotistico Fantastico; Delirious downed Claudio Castagnoli; Jimmy Rave and Sal Rinauro defeated Colt Cabana and Ace Steel; Jay Briscoe pinned Jimmy Jacobs; Homicide toppled Mark Briscoe; Davey Richards, Jerrelle Clark, Jake Crist, and Dave Crist overturned Austin Aries, Roderick Strong, Jack Evans, and Matt Sydal; BJ Whitmer defeated Chris Banks; Christopher Daniels overcame Christian Cage; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson bested ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinness.

Aug 2006

4th August 2006: “ROH Time to Man Up”: Uniondale, NY – 700
ROH Champion Bryan Danielson bested Jack Evans; Christopher Daniels and Matt Sydal defeated Jake and Dave Crist; Colt Cabana overturned Claudio Castagnoli; ROH Tag Team Champions Austin Aries and Roderick Strong dropped Jimmy Rave and Sal Rinauro, Homicide and Ricky Reyes, and Adam Pearce and BJ Whitmer in a four-way “Ultimate Endurance” match; ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinness downed Delirious; Mark and Jay Briscoe toppled KENTA and Davey Richards; Samoa Joe toppled AJ Styles.

5th August 2006: “ROH Fight of the Century”: Edison, NJ – 800
Colt Cabana overturned Sal Rinauro; Shane Hagadorn bested Bobby Dempsey; ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinness downed Christopher Daniels, Claudio Castagnoli, and Jay Lethal in a four-way; ROH Tag Team Champions Austin Aries and Roderick Strong dropped Mark and Jay Briscoe, Jake and Dave Crist, and Jack Evans and Matt Sydal in a four-way “Ultimate Endurance” match; Homicide and BJ Whitmer defeated Steve Corino and Adam Pearce by DQ; KENTA overturned Davey Richards; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson battled Samoa Joe to a draw.

12th August 2006: “ROH Unified”: Liverpool, England – 1,700
Matt Sydal beat Colt Cabana, Spud, and Jonny Storm in a four-way; Davey Richards downed Jimmy Rave; BJ Whitmer beat Claudio Castagnoli; Colt Cabana beat Chris Hero; Doug Williams and Jody Fleisch beat SUWA and Go Shiozaki; FWA Champion Robbie Brookside overturned Chad Collyer; ROH Tag Team Champions Austin Aries and Roderick Strong dropped Jay and Mark Briscoe; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson bested ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinness to unify the titles.

13th August 2006: “ROH Anarchy in the UK”: Broxbourne, England
Go Shiozaki beat BJ Whitmer; Jonny Storm defeated Jody Fleisch and Spud in a three-way; Nigel McGuinness, Colt Cabana, and Robbie Brookside toppled Chris Hero, Claudio Castagnoli, and Chad Collyer; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson bested SUWA; Doug Williams downed Jimmy Rave; Mark and Jay Briscoe toppled Matt Sydal and Davey Richards; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson bested Roderick Strong.

25th August 2006: “ROH Epic Encounter II”: St Paul, MN – 500
Mark and Jay Briscoe toppled Jake and Dave Crist; Homicide toppled Jimmy Jacobs; Christopher Daniels downed Claudio Castagnoli; Colt Cabana and Davey Richards downed Jimmy Rave and Sal Rinauro; Matt Sydal defeated Delirious; ROH Tag Team Champions Austin Aries and Roderick Strong dropped Samoa Joe and BJ Whitmer; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson battled Nigel McGuinness to a draw in a “best-of-three-falls” encounter (1:1).

26th August 2006: “ROH Gut Check”: Chicago Ridge, IL
Davey Richards downed Alex Payne; Jimmy Rave, Nigel McGuinness, and Sal Rinauro defeated Delirious, Jake Crist and David Crist; Jimmy Jacobs downed BJ Whitmer; Mark and Jay Briscoe toppled Davey Richards and Homicide; Samoa Joe toppled Claudio Castagnoli; ROH Tag Team Champions Austin Aries and Roderick Strong dropped Christopher Daniels and Matt Sydal; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson fought Colt Cabana to a draw in a “best-of-three-falls” encounter (1:1).

Sep 2006

15th September 2006: “ROH Glory by Honor V – Night 1”: East Windsor, CT – 500
Dunn and Marcos downed Pelle Primeau and Rhett Titus; Chris Hero and Claudio Castagnoli beat Dunn and Marcos; Jack Evans downed Colt Cabana, Jimmy Jacobs, and Ricky Reyes in a four-way; Nigel McGuinness downed Christopher Daniels; Austin Aries downed Davey Richards; Delirious downed Shane Hagadorn; Samoa Joe toppled Roderick Strong; KENTA and Naomichi Marufuji beat Mark and Jay Briscoe.

16th September 2006: “ROH Glory by Honor V – Night 2”: New York, NY (Hammerstein Ballroom) – 1,200
Davey Richards downed Jack Evans; Adam Pearce toppled Delirious; Jimmy Jacobs downed Christopher Daniels and Colt Cabana in a three-way; Samoa Joe and Homicide toppled Mark and Jay Briscoe; Chris Hero and Claudio Castagnoli beat ROH Tag Team Champions Austin Aries and Roderick Strong to win the titles; GHC World Champion Naomichi Marufuji beat Nigel McGuinness; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson bested KENTA.

Oct 2006

6th October 2006: “ROH Survival of the Fittest 2006”: Cleveland, OH – 400
Matt Sydal beat Davey Richards; Delirious downed Jimmy Rave; Austin Aries defeated Christopher Daniels; Jay and Mark Briscoe toppled Homicide and Roderick Strong; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson battled Samoa Joe to a draw; Dave Crist and Jake Crist overturned Alex Payne and Rhett Titus; ROH Tag Champions Chris Hero and Claudio Castagnoli toppled Colt Cabana and Jimmy Jacobs; Delirious won the “Survival of the Fittest” five-way elimination match over Matt Sydal, Mark Briscoe, Jay Briscoe, and Austin Aries.

7th October 2006: “ROH Motor City Madness”: Detroit, MI – 400
Delirious defeated Zach Gowen; Daizee Haze and MsChif beat Allison Danger and Lacey; Claudio Castagnoli overcame Davey Richards; Christopher Daniels and Matt Sydal defeated Colt Cabana and Jimmy Jacobs; Matt Sydal bested Chris Hero. Pelle Primeau won a six-way over Jimmy Rave, Dave Crist, Jake Crist, Shane Hagadorn, and Matt Cross; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson bested Austin Aries; Jay and Mark Briscoe rolled over Homicide and Samoa Joe in a “falls count anywhere” streetfight.

27th October 2006: “ROH Suffocation”: Dayton, OH – 400
Matt Cross beat Pelle Primeau; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson defeated Sal Rinauro; Shingo Takagi downed Jimmy Rave; Dave Crist and Jake Crist dropped Colt Cabana and Jimmy Jacobs; Christopher Daniels beat Brent Albright; Roderick Strong won a four-way over Chris Hero, Mark Briscoe and Tank Tolland; Austin Aries toppled Claudio Castagnoli; Davey Richards bested Jay Briscoe; Delirious overturned Matt Sydal; Homicide and Samoa Joe rolled over Steve Corino and Adam Pearce.

28th October 2006: “ROH Irrestible Forces”: Chicago Ridge, IL
Adam Pearce and Shane Hagadorn defeated Dave and Jake Crist; Shingo Takagi downed Roderick Strong; Colt Cabana and Jimmy Jacobs dropped Jimmy Rave and Sal Rinauro; Steve Corino overcame Delirious; Christopher Daniels defeated Austin Aries; Brent Albright beat Trik Davis; Matt Sydal toppled Jay Briscoe; Davey Richards overturned Mark Briscoe; Samoa Joe rolled over ROH Champion Bryan Danielson in a non-title no-DQ encounter.

Nov 2006

3rd November 2006: “ROH Honor Reclaims Boston”: Braintree, MA – 600
Bobby Dempsey, Mitch Franklin, and Rhett Titus beat Alex Payne, Ernie Osiris, and Thomas Penmanship; Shane Hagadorn overcame Pelle Primeau; Nigel McGuinness downed John Walters; Mercedes Martinez pinned Daizee Haze; Christopher Daniels downed Chris Hero; Matt Sydal pinned Claudio Castagnoli; Steve Corino, Mark Briscoe, and Jay Briscoe toppled Samoa Joe, Homicide, and BJ Whitmer; Colt Cabana overturned Jimmy Rave; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson bested Delirious; Austin Aries and Roderick Strong dropped Davey Richards and KENTA.

4th November 2006: “ROH The Bitter End”: Philadelphia, PA – 1,000
Christopher Daniels defeated Matt Cross; Ricky Reyes defeated Shane Hagadorn; Pelle Primeau won a four-way over Tank Toland, BJ Whitmer and Colt Cabana; Nigel McGuinness and Samoa Joe defeated ROH Champion Bryan Danielson and Jimmy Rave; Jay and Mark Briscoe bested Davey Richards and Delirious; ROH Tag Champions Chris Hero and Claudio Castagnoli toppled Austin Aries and Roderick Strong by DQ;  KENTA rolled over Matt Sydal; Homicide downed Steve Corino in a won a “Fight Without Honor”.

24th November 2006: “ROH Black Friday Fallout”: Uniondale, NY – 500
Lacey won a four-way over Nikki Roxx, Allison Danger and Daizee Haze; Nigel McGuinness won a gauntlet match over Jimmy Jacobs, BJ Whitmer, Brent Albright, Jason Blade, and Pelle Primeau; ROH Tag Champions Chris Hero and Claudio Castagnoli bested Austin Aries and Roderick Strong in a no-DQ encounter; Shingo Takagi defeated Mark Briscoe; Jimmy Rave overcame Homicide; Samoa Joe downed Jay Briscoe; Christopher Daniels and Matt Sydal rolled over Davey Richards and Delirious.

25th November 2006: “ROH Dethroned”: Edison, NJ – 800
Pelle Primeau and Rhett Titus beat Alex Payne and Grim Reefer; Jagged defeated Mitch Franklin; Brent Albright dropped El Generico; Shane Hagadorn defeated Bobby Dempsey;; Delirious defeated Jason Blade FIP Champion Roderick Strong toppled Shingo Takagi; Christopher Daniels and Matt Sydal overcame ROH Tag Champions Chris Hero and Claudio Castagnoli to win the titles; Nigel McGuinness bested Jimmy Rave; Davey Richards downed Austin Aries; Samoa Joe and Homicide rolled over Jay and Mark Briscoe in a “falls count anywhere” elimination streetfight.

Dec 2006

8th December 2006: “ROH The Chicago Spectacular – Night 1”: Chicago Ridge, IL – 800
Bobby Dempsey and CJ Otis beat Ernie Osiris and Mitch Franklin; Eric Priest and Chandler McClure defeated Rhett Titus and Alex Payne; Pelle Primeau downed Angel Armoni; Tank Toland dropped Kikutaro; Jay and Mark Briscoe bested Dave Crist and Jake Crist; Homicide toppled Brent Albright by DQ; BJ Whitmer won a four-way over Davey Richards, Matt Cross, and Christopher Daniels; Jimmy Rave beat Nigel McGuinness; Delirious and Ace Steel overcame Adam Pearce and Shane Hagadorn; Colt Cabana overturned Jimmy Jacobs by DQ; Austin Aries and Roderick Strong bested Shingo Takagi and Matt Sydal in a “Dragon Gate Rules” match; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson rolled over Samoa Joe in a cage match.

9th December 2006: “ROH The Chicago Spectacular – Night 2”: Chicago Ridge, IL
Alex Payne and Bobby Dempsey beat Johnny Dynamo and Rhett Titus; Eric Priest and Chandler McClure overcame Ernie Osiris and Mitch Franklin; Ace Steel overturned Tank Toland; Jake Crist won a six-way over Kikutaro, Pelle Primeau, Trik Davis, Dave Crist, and CJ Otis; Homicide defeated Shane Hagadorn in a strap match; Samoa Joe bested Mark Briscoe; Austin Aries and Roderick Strong toppled Christopher Daniels and Matt Cross ; Homicide downed Adam Pearce in a cage match; Jay Briscoe rolled over Davey Richards; Delirious, Nigel McGuinness, Colt Cabana, and BJ Whitmer bested ROH Champion Bryan Danielson, Jimmy Jacobs, Jimmy Rave, and Shingo Takagi in an elimination match.

22nd December 2006: “ROH International Challenge”: Hartford, CT – 500
Jason Blade defeated Pelle Primeau; Austin Aries downed Eddie Edwards; Adam Pearce dropped Delirious; BJ Whitmer and Daizee Haze beat Jimmy Jacobs and Mercedes Martinez; Colt Cabana rolled over Brent Albright; Chris Hero and Claudio Castagnoli bested Jay and Mark Briscoe; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson won a four-way over Nigel McGuinness, Jimmy Rave, and Homicide; FIP Champion Roderick Strong defeated Davey Richards by count-out; ROH Tag Champions Christopher Daniels and Matt Sydal toppled CIMA and Shingo Takagi.

23rd December 2006: “ROH Final Battle 2006”: New York, NY – 1,300
Jimmy Rave won a four-way over El Generico, Christopher Daniels, and Davey Richards; Adam Pearce defeated Ricky Reyes; Jimmy Jacobs and Brent Albright dropped Colt Cabana and BJ Whitmer; Jay and Mark Briscoe downed Chris Hero and Claudio Castagnoli; Jimmy Rave bested Nigel McGuinness; CIMA, Shingo Takagi, and Matt Sydal toppled Austin Aries, Roderick Strong and Delirious in a “Dragon Gate Rules” match; Homicide rolled over ROH Champion Bryan Danielson to win the title.
