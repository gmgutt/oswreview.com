ROH on Sinclair Results 2013

ROH ON SINCLAIR TV RESULTS 2013

Jan 2013

Air Date: 5th January 2013
Featured the Strong/Elgin, Hardy/Cole, and Richards & Edwards vs. Fish & O’Reilly matches from ROH Final Battle 2012. There were no original matches broadcast.

Air Date: 12th January 2013
Featured the Lethal/Rhino, Bennett/Lynn, and Briscoes vs. Jacobs & Corino vs. Coleman & Alexander matches from ROH Final Battle 2012. There were no original matches broadcast.

Air Date: 19th January 2013
Kyle O’Reilly and Bobby Fish beat Corey Hollis and Mike Posey; QT Marshall defeated Mike Sydal (Top Prospects Tournament – First Round); ROH Tag Champions Mark and Jay Briscoe downed Caprice Coleman and Cedric Alexander.

Air Date: 26th January 2013
Silas Young dropped Adam Page (Top Prospects Tournament – First Round); Matt Hardy bested Rhett Titus; ROH TV Champion Adam Cole toppled BJ Whitmer.

Feb 2013

Air Date: 2nd February 2013
Tadarius Thomas overcame Antonio Thomas (Top Prospects Tournament – First Round); Roderick Strong defeated Grizzly Redwood; Michael Elgin downed Mike Bennett; Gauntlet tag match: Davey Richards and Eddie Edwards beat Harlem and Lance Bravado, Davey Richards and Eddie Edwards beat Jimmy Jacobs and Steve Corino, Kyle O’Reilly and Bobby Fish beat Davey Richards and Eddie Edwards.

Air Date: 9th February 2013
Matt Taven defeated ACH (Top Prospects Tournament – First Round); MsChif downed Athena; ROH Champion Kevin Steen, Jimmy Jacobs, Steve Corino and Rhino rolled over ROH Tag Champions Mark and Jay Briscoe, Cedric Alexander and Caprice Coleman.

Air Date: 16th February 2013
Tadarius Thomas defeated QT Marshall (Top Prospect Tournament – Semi-Final); Caprice Coleman & Cedric Alexander battled Brian Fury and Nick Westgate to a no-contest; Matt Tavern downed Silas Young (Top Prospect Tournament – Semi-Final); Jay Lethal bested Jimmy Jacobs.

Air Date: 23rd February 2013
Matt Taven beat Tadarius Thomas (Top Prospect Tournament – Final); Roderick Strong, Kyle O’Reilly and Bobby Fish downed Michael Elgin, Davey Richards and Eddie Edwards.

Mar 2013

Air Date: 2nd March 2013
Rhino and Steve Corino bested Asylum and Burns; ROH Champion Kevin Steen toppled Grizzly Redwood; ROH Tag Champions Mark and Jay Briscoe rolled over Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin in a “losers must disband” encounter.

Air Date: 9th March 2013
Eddie Edwards bested Rhett Titus; MsChif defeated Mia Yim; Michael Elgin overturned Matt Hardy, Davey Richards and Roderick Strong in a four-way.

Air Date: 16th March 2013
Davey Richards and Eddie Edwards rolled over ACH and Tadarius Thomas; BJ Whitmer defeated QT Marshall; Jay Lethal bested Roderick Strong.

Air Date: 23rd March 2013
Michael Elgin bested Silas Young; Charlie Haas toppled Grizzly Redwood; Adam Cole overturned Matt Hardy by DQ. 

Air Date: 30th March 2013
Rhett Titus and Cliff Compton dropped Caprice Coleman and Cedric Alexander; Jimmy Jacobs overcame Mike Mondo; Rhino, Matt Hardy and Steve Corino toppled Jay Lethal, Michael Elgin and BJ Whitmer. 

Apr 2013

Air Date: 6th April 2013
Tadarius Thomas and ACH beat Mike Sydal and Adam Page; Athena won a four-way over MsChif, Cherry Bomb and Scarlett Bordeaux; Roderick Strong defeated Pepper Parks; ROH World Tag Champions Kyle O’Reilly and Bobby Fish downed Rocky Romero and Alex Koslov.

Air Date: 13th April 2013
Bobby Fish defeated Eddie Edwards; RD Evans and QT Marshall downed Mike Posey and Corey Hollis; Mark Briscoe won a five-way over BJ Whitmer, Caprice Coleman, Cedric Alexander and Mike Mondo.

Air Date: 20th April 2013
Roderick Strong bested Mike Bennett, Tadarius Thomas battled Jorge Santi to a no-contest; ROH TV Champion Matt Taven toppled ACH.

Air Date: 27th April 2013
Jimmy Jacobs, Cliff Compton and Rhett Titus overcame Caprice Coleman, Cedric Alexander and BJ Whitmer; Jay Lethal defeated Rhett Titus; ROH Champion Jay Briscoe and Adam Cole bested Matt Hardy and Rhino.
