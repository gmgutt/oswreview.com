ROH Results 2005

Jan 2005

15th January 2005: “ROH It All Begins”: Cambridge, MA – 700
Spanky beat Alex Shelley; Azrieal downed Deranged by DQ; Roderick Strong overcame Alex Law, Evan Starsmore, Shane Hagadorn and Ricky Landell in a handicap match; Jay Lethal and Josh Daniels defeated Diablo Santiago and Oman Tortuga; ROH Tag Champions Rocky Romero and Ricky Reyes bested CM Punk and Steve Corino, and The Carnage Crew in a three-way Ultimate Endurance match; Samoa Joe toppled Nigel McGuinness; Homicide bested Bryan Danielson; ROH Champion Austin Aries rolled over Colt Cabana.

Feb 2005

19th February 2005: “ROH Do or Die IV”: Elizabeth, NJ – 500
Lacey overcame Allison Danger; Buff-E and Mace defeated Phil Davis and Sean Davis; Vordell Walker beat Cheech; Matt Turner and Shane Hagadorn downed Jerk Jackson and Scott Cardinal; Alex Law and Ricky Landell dropped Anthony Franco and Davey Andrews; Eddie Vegas won a four-way over Arik Cannon, El Generico, and Josh Daniels; B-Boy toppled Kevin Steen; FIP Champion Homicide rolled over Antonio Banks.

19th February 2005: “ROH 3rd Anniversary Celebration – Night 1”: Elizabeth, NJ – 1,100
Steve Corino beat Roderick Strong; Jay Lethal bested Jimmy Rave; ROH Pure Champion John Walters defeated Jay Lethal; Billy Ken Kid downed Ebessan; Alex Shelley dropped Jimmy Jacobs; Spanky overcame CM Punk; BJ Whitmer and Dan Maff overcame ROH Tag Champions Rocky Romero and Ricky Reyes to win the titles; Samoa Joe overturned Ebessan Jack; Homicide toppled Bryan Danielson; ROH Champion Austin Aries bested Colt Cabana in a cage match; The Ring Crew Express rolled over Azrieal and Dixie, Roderick Strong and Jack Evans, The Carnage Crew, and Deranged and Izzy in a five-way scramble cage match.

25th February 2005: “ROH 3rd Anniversary Celebration – Night 2”: Dayton, OH – 700
Matt Sydal and Fast Eddie beat The Ring Crew Express; Homicide downed Puma; Colt Cabana defeated Nigel McGuinness; ROH Tag Champions Dan Maff and BJ Whitmer overcame Delirious and Jimmy Jacobs; James Gibson dropped Spanky; Daizee Haze overturned Traci Brooks, Lacey and Allison Danger in a four-way; CM Punk bested Alex Shelley; Bryan Danielson and Samoa Joe toppled ROH Champion Austin Aries and Jack Evans; Jimmy Rave rolled over AJ Styles.

26th February 2005: “ROH 3rd Anniversary Celebration – Night 3”: Chicago Ridge, IL – 1,000
Lacey beat Allison Danger, Traci Brooks and Daizee Haze in a four-way; Fast Eddie bested Matt Sydal; The Ring Crew overcame Delirious and Golden Vampire XII; Alex Shelley overturned Jack Evans; Nigel McGuinness and Colt Cabana toppled ROH Tag Champions Dan Maff and BJ Whitmer in a non-title match; Bryan Danielson bested Homicide in a falls-count-anywhere match; Spanky defeated Jimmy Jacobs; James Gibson downed Puma; Jimmy Rave bested CM Punk; ROH Champion Austin Aries rolled over Samoa Joe.

Mar 2005

5th March 2005: “ROH Trios Tournament”: Philadelphia, PA – 600
ROH Champion Austin Aries, Jack Evans, and Roderick Strong overturned Shane Hagadorn, Davey Andrews, and Anthony Franco (Trios Tournament – First Round); CM Punk, Colt Cabana, and Steve Corino overcame Jimmy Rave, Oman Tortuga, and The Weapon of Mass Destruction #3 (Trios Tournament – First Round); Homicide, Ricky Reyes, and Rocky Romero overcame Dunn, Marcos, and El Generico (Trios Tournament – First Round); Samoa Joe, Bryan Danielson, and Vordell Walker defeated Spanky, James Gibson, and Nigel McGuinness (Trios Tournament – First Round); Austin Aries, Jack Evans, and Roderick Strong overturned CM Punk, Colt Cabana, and Steve Corino (Trios Tournament – Semi-Final); Homicide, Ricky Reyes, and Rocky Romero overcame Samoa Joe, ROH Champion Bryan Danielson, and Vordell Walker (Trios Tournament – Semi-Final); Azrieal defeated Kevin Steen, B-Boy, Deranged, Izzy, and Dixie in a six-way; Jay Lethal toppled ROH Pure Champion John Walters to win the title; Homicide, Ricky Reyes, and Rocky Romero overcame ROH Champion Austin Aries, Jack Evans, and Roderick Strong (Trios Tournament – Final).

12th March 2005: “ROH Back to Basics”: Woodbridge, CT – 500
Colt Cabana beat Delirious; Dunn and Marcos downed Ricky Landell and Alex Law; Spanky defeated Jack Evans; Azrieal and Dixie downed Izzy and Deranged; Homicide downed Roderick Strong; Allison Danger defeated Daizee Haze, Cindy Rogers, and Lacey in a four-way; ROH Tag Champions Dan Maff and BJ Whitmer defeated Nigel McGuinness and Colt Cabana; James Gibson beat Rocky Romero; Samoa Joe and ROH Pure Champion Jay Lethal rolled over CM Punk and Spanky.

Apr 2005

2nd April 2005: “ROH Best of Amercian Super Juniors Tournament”: Asbury, NJ – 500
Bryan Danielson bested Spanky (Best of American Super Juniors Tournament – First Round); Dragon Soldier B beat Matt Sydal (Best of American Super Juniors Tournament – First Round); Black Tiger IV defeated Alex Shelley (Best of American Super Juniors Tournament – First Round);James Gibson bested Roderick Strong (Best of American Super Juniors Tournament – First Round); Jimmy Rave and Fast Eddie downed CM Punk and Colt Cabana in a no-DQ encounter; Dragon Soldier B defeated James Gibson (Best of American Super Juniors Tournament – Semi-Final);Black Tiger IV beat Bryan Danielson (Best of American Super Juniors Tournament – Semi-Final); BJ Whitmer and Jimmy Jacobs dropped Samoa Joe and Jay Lethal to win the vacant ROH Tag Titles; Dragon Soldier B defeated Black Tiger IV (Best of American Super Juniors Tournament – Final);  Jack Evans and Roderick Strong won a “tag team scramble” match over Dixie and Azrieal, Izzy and Deranged, and Dunn and Marcos; ROH Champion Austin Aries dropped Homicide.

16th April 2005: “ROH Stalemate”: Dorchester, MA – 500
Vordell Walker beat Kevin Steen; Roderick Strong bested El Generico; Deranged and Cheech defeated Dixie and Azrieal; ROH Pure Champion Jay Lethal defeated Spanky; CM Punk toppled Mike Kruel;  Samoa Joe and Alex Shelly won a “Double Stakes” four-way over Nigel McGuiness and Colt Cabana; ROH Champion Austin Aries battled James Gibson to a double-pinfall draw; Bryan Danielson bested Homicide in a lumberjack match (Best of Five Series – match #4).

May 2005

7th May 2005: “ROH Manhattan Mayhem”: New York, NY – 800
Izzy and Deranged defeated Azrieal and Dixie; Nigel McGuiness dropped Colt Cabana; James Gibson bested Black Tiger; ROH Tag Champions BJ Whitmer and Jimmy Jacobs dropped Jack Evans and Roderick Strong; Samoa Joe downed ROH Pure Champion Jay Lethal to win the title; Fast Eddie downed Davey Andrews; Jimmy Rave downed CM Punk in a “dog collar” match; ROH Champion Austin Aries dropped Alex Shelley; Low Ki and Homicide downed ROH Pure Champion Samoa Joe and Jay Lethal.

13th May 2005: “ROH Final Showdown”: Dayton, OH – 600
Alex Shelley defeated Roderick Strong; Matt Stryker defeated Masked Chicago Superstar; Jack Evans dropped Samoa Joe, Ebessan, and Delirious in a four-way; ROH Tag Champions BJ Whitmer and Jimmy Jacobs dropped CM Punk and Ace Steel; Jimmy Rave downed Matt Sydal; Colt Cabana and Doug Williams dropped Nigel McGuinness and Chad Collyer; ROH Champion Austin Aries dropped James Gibson; Bryan Danielson rolled over Homicide in a cage match (Best of Five Series – match #5).

14th May 2005: “ROH Nowhere to Run”: Chicago Ridge, IL –700
James Gibson bested BJ Whitmer; Matt Sydal defeated Matt Stryker, The Masked Green Bay Superstar, and Ace Steel in a four-way; Chad Collyer defeated Jimmy Jacobs; Homicide downed Doug Williams; Roderick Strong and Jack Evans dropped Delirious and Alex Shelley; Nigel McGuinness dropped Colt Cabana; ROH Champion Austin Aries dropped Bryan Danielson; CM Punk defeated Jimmy Rave in a cage match.

June 2005

4th June 2005: “ROH New Frontiers”: Buffalo, NY – 550
Jimmy Rave and Fast Eddie downed Sal Rinuero and El Generico; Homicide downed Kevin Steen; Roderick Strong overturned Alex Shelley; ROH Tag Champions BJ Whitmer and Jimmy Jacobs dropped Dunn and Marcos; Davey Andrews dropped Shane Hagadorn, and The Masked New York Superstar in a three way; Nigel McGuiness and Chad Collyer defeated CM Punk and Colt Cabana; ROH Pure Champion Samoa Joe downed James Gibson; ROH Champion Austin Aries dropped Spanky.

12th June 2005: “ROH The Future is Now”: New York, NY – 800
Colt Cabana rolled over Jimmy Rave; The Carnage Crew defeated Dunn and Marcos in a streetfight; Azrieal defeated BJ Whitmer, Jack Evans, Izzy, Deranged and Fast Eddie in a six-way; Homicide downed James Gibson; CM Punk defeated Roderick Strong; ROH Pure Champion Samoa Joe downed Nigel McGuiness; Low Ki rolled over ROH Champion Austin Aries in a non-title match.

18th June 2005: “ROH Death before Dishonor III”: Morristown, NJ – 900
ROH Tag Champions BJ Whitmer and Jimmy Jacobs dropped Jimmy Rave and Fast Eddie; Lacey defeated Cindy Rogers; Carnage Crew defeated Dunn and Marcos in an “Anything Goes” match; ROH Pure Champion Samoa Joe downed Colt Cabana; AJ Styles dropped Petey Williams; Izzy and Deranged defeated Roderick Strong and Jack Evans Nigel McGuiness dropped James Gibson, Homicide and Azrieal in a four-way; Low Ki battled Jay Lethal to a no-contest; CM Punk defeated ROH Champion Austin Aries to win the title.

July 2005

8th July 2005: “ROH Sign of Dishonor”: Uniondale, NY –850
Jay Lethal and Dixie downed The Heartbreak Express; Roderick Strong beat Azrieal; ROH Tag Champions BJ Whitmer and Jimmy Jacobs dropped Lacey’s Angels; AJ Styles dropped Jimmy Rave; Nigel McGuinness downed Vordell Walker; Austin Aries won a four-way over Samoa Joe, Homicide, and James Gibson ; Christopher Daniels dropped Colt Cabana; ROH Champion CM Punk rolled over Jay Lethal.

9th July 2005: “ROH Escape from New York”: New York, NY – 1,000
The Ring Crew Express and Dixie downed Lacey’s Angels and Vordell Walker; Matt Striker beat Mike Kruel; The Carnage Crew defeated ROH Tag Champions BJ Whitmer and Jimmy Jacobs to win the titles; Jimmy Rave won a four-way over Alex Shelley, James Gibson, and Azrieal; ROH Pure Champion Samoa Joe downed Austin Aries; Nigel McGuiness downed Colt Cabana in a “European Rules” match; Homicide downed Jay Lethal; ROH Champion CM Punk rolled over Roderick Strong.

16th July 2005: “ROH Fate of an Angel”: Woodbridge, CT – 1,000
Nigel McGuinness downed Claudio Castignoli; Austin Aries bested El Generico; Homicide won a four-way over Kevin Steen, Dixie, and Azrieal; ROH Tag Champions The Carnage Crew won an “Ultimate Endurance” match over Lacey’s Angels, The Embassy, and Dunn and Marcos; AJ Styles dropped Roderick Strong; ROH Pure Champion Samoa Joe downed Jimmy Rave; Matt Hardy defeated Christopher Daniels; ROH Champion CM Punk defeated James Gibson.

23rd July 2005: “ROH The Homecoming”: Philadelphia, PA – 700
El Generico overcame Kevin Steen; Spanky won a four-way over Nigel McGuiness, Azrieal, and Deranged; BJ Whitmer and Jimmy Jacobs dropped ROH Tag Champions The Carnage Crew to win the titles; Homicide, Low Ki, and Ricky Reyes dropped ROH Pure Champion Samoa Joe, Jay Lethal, and James Gibson;  Alex Shelley and Fast Eddie Vegas dropped Roderick Strong and Austin Aries; Jimmy Rave downed AJ Styles in a street fight ROH Champion CM Punk battled Christopher Daniels to a draw.

Aug 2005

12th August 2005: “ROH Redemption”: Dayton, OH – 700
Ace Steel won a four-way over Matt Stryker, Delirious, and Sterling James Keenan; Colt Cabana beat Spanky; ROH Tag Team Champions BJ Whitmer and Jimmy Jacobs overturned Nigel McGuinness and Chad Collyer; Austin Aries, Roderick Strong, and Matt Sydal defeated Jimmy Rave, Alex Shelley, and Abyss; Low Ki battled Jay Lethal to a no-contest; Matt Hardy beat Homicide
James Gibson won a four-way over ROH Champion CM Punk, ROH Pure Champion Samoa Joe, and Christopher Daniels.

13th August 2005: “ROH Punk: The Final Chapter”: Chicago Ridge, IL – 1,200
Nigel McGuinness won a four-way over Alex Shelley, Matt Sydal, and Delirious; Chad Collyer defeated Ace Steel; Austin Aries bested Jimmy Rave; ROH Pure Champion Samoa Joe and Jay Lethal beat Homicide and Low Ki by DQ; ROH Tag Team Champions BJ Whitmer and Jimmy Jacobs overcame ROH Champion James Gibson and Spanky; Roderick Strong overturned Matt Hardy; Colt Cabana beat CM Punk in a ”best-of-three-falls” encounter.

20th August 2005: “ROH Do or Die V”: Morristown, NJ
Sal Rinauro beat Antonio Blanca; Steve Madison bested Davey Andrews; Claudio Castagnoli defeated Rainman; Jerrelle Clark beat Jay Fury; Smash Bradley and The Dempsey Brothers dropped The Heatbreak Express and Shane Hagadorn; Lacey beat Sumie Sakai; The Outcast Killaz beat Pelle Primeau and EC Negro; IWC Super Indy Champion Sterling James Keenan beat John McChesney; Sal Rinauro overcame Steve Madison, Claudio Castagnoli and Jerrelle Clark in a four-way to win an ROH contract.

20th August 2005: “ROH Night of the Grudges II”: Morristown, NJ – 600
The Carnage Crew beat Dunn and Marcos in a weapons match; Azrieal beat Jerrelle Clark; Jay Lethal beat Ricky Reyes; Colt Cabana rolled over Nigel McGuinness in a ‘soccer riot’ match; ROH Champion James Gibson, Homicide and Spanky battled to a no-contest in a three-way; Austin Aries and Roderick Strong overturned Jimmy Rave and Puma; ROH Champion James Gibson bested Spanky; ROH Pure Champion Samoa Joe toppled Christopher Daniels.

27th August 2005: “ROH Dragon Gate Invasion”: Buffalo, NY
Ricky Reyes downed Puma; Chad Collyer won a four-way over Kevin Steen, Davey Andrews, and Jimmy Jacobs; Homicide overturned El Generico; Curry Man bested Shingo Takagi; Nigel McGuinness dropped ROH Pure Champion Samoa Joe to win the title; Dunn and Marcos dropped Matt Turner and Shane Hagadorn; Spanky and Jimmy Rave overturned Austin Aries and Roderick Strong; CIMA rolled over AJ Styles; ROH Champion James Gibson rolled over Colt Cabana.

Sep 2005

17th September 2005: “ROH Glory by Honor IV”: Lake Grove, NY – 600
Low Ki defeated Jay Lethal in a “Fight Without Honor”; Austin Aries dropped Azrieal; ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinness toppled Roderick Strong; Colt Cabana beat Homicide by DQ; Jay Lethal defeated Low Ki; Davey Andrews downed Eric Matlock; Samoa Joe won a four-way over Ricky Reyes, Adam Pearce and BJ Whitmer; Bryan Danielson bested ROH Champion James Gibson to win the title; AJ Styles downed Jimmy Rave.

24th September 2005: “ROH Survival of the Fittest 2005”: Boston, MA
Jay Lethal defeated Sal Rinauro; Colt Cabana beat Ricky Reyes; Austin Aries bested Jimmy Rave by DQ; Samoa Joe overturned Milano Collection AT; Roderick Strong overturned Jerrelle Clark; Christopher Daniels downed James Gibson; ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinness toppled BJ Whitmer; Tony DeVito and Shane Hagadorn beat Derek Dempsey and Pelle Primeau; Roderick Strong won the “Survival Of The Fittest” match over Samoa Joe, Colt Cabana, Jay Lethal, Christopher Daniels, and Austin Aries.

Oct 2005

1st October 2005: “ROH Joe vs. Kobashi”: New York, NY – 800
Claudio Castagnoli overturned Colt Cabana; Christopher Daniels dropped Azrieal and Matt Sydal in a three-way; Tony Mamaluke and Sal Rinauro toppled ROH Tag Champions BJ Whitmer and Jimmy Jacobs to win the titles; ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinnes bested Jay Lethal; Roderick Strong beat Jimmy Rave; Ricky Reyes downed Pelle Primeau; James Gibson bested Jimmy Yang; Jack Evans dropped Homicide; Kenta Kobashi beat Samoa Joe.

2nd October 2005: “ROH Unforgettable”: Philadelphia, PA – 1,000
ROH Tag Champions Tony Mamaluke and Sal Rinauro overcame Dunn and Marcos; Jay Lethal won a non-title four-way over Davey Andrews, Claudio Castagnoli and ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinness; Matt Sydal defeated Jimmy Rave; Ricky Reyes downed Derek Dempsey; Roderick Strong overturned James Gibson; BJ Whitmer and Jimmy Jacobs dropped Izzy and Deranged; Colt Cabana beat Jack Evans; Christopher Daniels downed Jimmy Yang; Kenta Kobashi and Homicide downed Samoa Joe and Low Ki.

14th October 2005: “ROH Enter the Dragon”: Cleveland, OH – 600
Claudio Castagnoli beat ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinness in a non-title match; Ricky Reyes dropped Matt Stryker; ROH Tag Champions Tony Mamaluke and Sal Rinauro overcame BJ Whitmer and Jimmy Jacobs; Roderick Strong beat Jimmy Yang; Steve Corino and Colt Cabana beat Homicide and Low Ki; Davey Andrews and Shane Hagadorn battled Bobby Dempsey and Pelle Primeau to a no-contest; Abyss downed Jack Evans; Jay Lethal and Samoa Joe downed Jimmy Rave and Alex Shelley; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson bested Austin Aries.

15th October 2005: “ROH Buffalo Stampede”: Buffalo, NY
BJ Whitmer beat Davey Andrews; Claudio Castagnoli defeated Sterling James Keenan; Jay Lethal defeated Jimmy Jacobs; ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinness rolled over Samoa Joe; ROH Tag Champions Sal Rinauro and Tony Mamaluke overcame Homicide and Ricky Reyes; Jimmy Rave, Alex Shelley and Abyss downed Austin Aries, Roderick Strong and Jack Evans in a no-DQ match; Low Ki defeated Colt Cabana; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson rolled over Steve Corino.

29th October 2005: “ROH This Means War”: Woodbridge, CT – 500
Alex Shelley battled Claudio Castagnoli to a draw; Azrieal and Davey Andrews downed Jason Blade and Kid Mikaze; Ricky Reyes downed Mitch Franklin; BJ Whitmer won a non-title four-way over Nosawa, Kikutaro and ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinness; Jay Lethal beat Curry Man; Colt Cabana beat B-Boy; AJ Styles dropped Austin Aries; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson bested Roderick Strong.

Nov 2005

4th November 2005: “ROH Showdown in Motown”: Detroit, MI – 600
BJ Whitmer defeated Delirious; Davey Andrews downed Derek Dempsey and Shane Hagadorn in a three-way; ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinness rolled over Claudio Castagnoli; Roderick Strong and Jack Evans dropped Chad Collyer and Sal Rinauro; Christopher Daniels won a four-way over Samoa Joe, Adam Pearce and Jimmy Jacobs; Daizee Haze overturned Allison Danger; Austin Aries bested Alex Shelley; AJ Styles and Matt Sydal defeated Jimmy Rave and Abyss; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson toppled Chris Sabin.

5th November 2005: “ROH Vendetta”: Chicago Ridge, IL – 950
Ace Steel and Delirious downed Chad Collyer and ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinness; Jimmy Jacobs overcame Sal Rinauro; BJ Whitmer defeated Claudio Castagnoli; Samoa Joe downed Christopher Daniels; Adam Pearce overturned Davey Andrews; Homicide battled Colt Cabana to a no-contest; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson bested Roderick Strong; Jimmy Rave, Alex Shelley, Abyss, and Prince Nana beat Austin Aries, AJ Styles, Jack Evans, and Matt Sydal.

19th November 2005: “ROH A Night of Tribute”: Uniondale, NY – 500
Dunn and Marcos downed Jason Blade and Kid Mikaze; Ricky Reyes downed Eric Matlock; Jimmy Rave downed Davey Andrews’ Christopher Daniels downed AJ Styles and Matt Sydal in a three-way; Homicide overturned Colt Cabana; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson beat Pelle Primeau; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson beat Azrieal; Milano Collection AT dropped Claudio Castagnoli; Austin Aries and Roderick Strong overturned Samoa Joe and Jay Lethal; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson bested Christopher Daniels.

Dec 2005

3rd December 2005: “ROH Steel Cage Warfare”: New York, NY – 950
Kid Mizake and Jason Blade downed The Ring Crew Express; Davey Andrews dropped Pelle Primeau; ROH Tag Champions Tony Mamaluke and Sal Rinauro overcame Colt Cabana and Milano Collection; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson beat Rocky Romero; Jay Lethal beat Samoa Joe; Steve Corino overcame Homicide; Austin Aries, Roderick Strong, Matt Sydal, and Jack Evans rolled over Jimmy Rave, Alex Shelley, Abyss, and Prince Nana in a cage elimination match.

17th December 2005: “ROH Final Battle 2005”: Edison, NJ – 600
Jimmy Rave downed Milano Collection AT; Colt Cabana beat Azrieal; ROH Pure Champion Nigel McGuinness dropped Claudio Castagnoli by DQ; Alex Shelley beat Steve Corino; Jay Lethal won a four-way over Samoa Joe, Christopher Daniels, and BJ Whitmer; Ricky Reyes toppled Davey Andrews; Austin Aries and Roderick Strong overturned ROH Tag Champions Sal Rinauro and Tony Mamaluke to win the titles; ROH Champion Bryan Danielson bested Naomichi Marufuji; GHC Jr Champion KENTA rolled over Low Ki.
