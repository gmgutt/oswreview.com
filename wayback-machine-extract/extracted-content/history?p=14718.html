WWE Bragging Rights Results

WWE BRAGGING RIGHTS RESULTS

25th October 2009: “WWE Bragging Rights 2009”: Pittsburgh, PA – 7,981
($449,465 live gate)
Dark match: ECW Champion Christian beat Paul Burchill.
PPV Broadcast: WWE US Champion The Miz toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion John Morrison in a non-title match; Beth Phoenix, Natalya, and WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool overcame Gail Kim, Melina, and Kelly Kelly; Chris Jericho, Kane, Matt Hardy, R-Truth, Finlay, DH Smith, and Tyson Kidd defeated Triple H, Shawn Michaels, The Big Show, Jack Swagger, Cody Rhodes, Kofi Kingston, and Mark Henry; World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker bested Batista, CM Punk and Rey Mysterio in a four-way; John Cena rolled over WWE World Champion Randy Orton in a 60-minute “falls-count-anywhere, no-DQ” Iron Man match (6:5) to win the title.

24th October 2010: “WWE Bragging Rights 2010”: Minneapolis, MN – 7,702
($495,766 live gate)
Dark match: MVP beat Chavo Guerrero.
PPV Broadcast: WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler in a non-title match; John Cena and David Otunga bested WWE Unified World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Drew McIntyre to win the titles; Ted Dibiase Jr downed Goldust; WWE Divas Champion Layla (a permitted substitute for Michelle McCool) dropped Natalya; World Heavyweight Champion Kane overturned The Undertaker in a “Buried Alive” match; Alberto Del Rio, Edge, Jack Swagger, Kofi Kingston, Rey Mysterio, The Big Show and Tyler Reks overturned CM Punk, Ezekiel Jackson, John Morrison, R-Truth, Santino Marella, Sheamus and The Miz in an elimination match; Wade Barrett rolled over WWE World Champion Randy Orton by DQ.
