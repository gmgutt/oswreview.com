WWE Over The Limit Results

WWE OVER THE LIMIT RESULTS

23rd May 2010: “WWE Over The Limit 2010”: Detroit, MI – 11,000
($675,000 live gate)
Dark match: MVP beat Chavo Guerrero Jr.
PPV Broadcast: Kofi Kingston pinned WWE Intercontinental Champion Drew McIntyre to win the title; R-Truth defeated Ted Dibiase Jr; Rey Mysterio downed CM Punk in a “SES membership vs. Hair” encounter; WWE Unified World Tag Champions DH Smith and Tyson Kidd dropped The Miz and Chris Jericho; Randy Orton battled Edge to a double count-out; The Big Show bested World Heavyweight Champion Jack Swagger by DQ; WWE Divas Champion Eve Torres toppled Maryse; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Batista in an “I Quit” match.

22nd May 2011: “WWE Over The Limit 2011”: Seattle, WA – 6,500
($410,000 live gate)
Dark match: Daniel Bryan beat Drew McIntyre.
PPV Broadcast: R-Truth downed Rey Mysterio; Ezekiel Jackson overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett by DQ; Sin Cara pinned Chavo Guerrero; WWE Divas Champion Brie Bella defeated Kelly Kelly; WWE World Tag Champions The Big Show and Kane overturned CM Punk and Mason Ryan; World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton toppled Christian; Jerry Lawler bested Michael Cole in a “Kiss my Foot” encounter; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over The Miz in an I Quit match.

20th May 2012: “WWE Over The Limit 2012”: Raleigh, NC – 8,000
Youtube pre-show: (44,049 views): Kane defeated Zack Ryder.
PPV Broadcast: Christian won a battle royal; WWE World Tag Champions R-Truth and Kofi Kingston downed Jack Swagger and Dolph Ziggler; WWE Divas Champion Layla overturned Beth Phoenix; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus dropped Randy Orton, Chris Jericho and Alberto Del Rio in a four-way; Brodus Clay bested The Miz; Christian overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz to win the title; WWE World Champion CM Punk toppled Daniel Bryan; Ryback squashed Camacho; John Laurinaitis rolled over John Cena in a no-DQ, no-count-out encounter.
