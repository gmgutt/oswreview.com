Hellooooooooo and welcome to the 3rd Annual Golden Nogger awards! Voting is now open!
If this is your first time, every year we do an ‘awards show’ where we hotdog and grandstand about the last 12 months in OSW! You guys vote in the categories (and so choose the winners), which will remain open until the day we record the show (later this month), and we’ll have a video where we have a laugh revealing the results!  Every day we’ll have a new poll to vote in, culminating with the Lifetime Achievement Award (WORZEL won last time)

The Rules (RULES!):
• If a wrestler is nominated one year, he/she cannot be nominated next year (i.e. Animated Alberto Del Rio is not eligible for “Roidy Magoo” this year, since he was nominated last year, but will be eligible again next time)
• Nominations will be taken from episodes released since the last Golden Noggers, including both tattoo reviews (i.e. Royal Rumble 2001 – IWA: King of the Deathmatch)

Next up will be ROIDY MAGOO of the year! So which do you think will win Favourite Intro vs which did you vote for?
