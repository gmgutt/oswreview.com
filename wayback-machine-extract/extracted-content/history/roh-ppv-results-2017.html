ROH PPV Results 2017

ROH PPV RESULTS 2017

 

10th March 2017: “ROH 15th Anniversary Show”: Las Vegas, NV – 900 sellout
Shown live on PPV
Dark match: Will Ferrara beat Shaheem Ali.
PPV Broadcast: Jay White downed Kenny King; Kazarian overcame Cheeseburger, Chris Sabin, Hangman Page, Punisher Martinez and Silas Young in a six-way; Jay Lethal defeated Bobby Fish; ROH 6-Man Tag Champions Matt Taven, TK O’Ryan and Vinny Marseglia defeated Dalton Castle, Brandon Tate and Brent Tate; ROH TV Champion Marty Scurll defeated Lio Rush; Bully Ray, Jay Briscoe and Mark Briscoe downed Hanson, Raymond Rowe and Davey Boy Smith Jr; ROH Tag Champions Matt Hardy and Jeff Hardy defeated Baretta and Rocky Romero, and The Young Bucks in a three-way streetfight; Christopher Daniels overturned ROH Champion Adam Cole to win the title.

1st April 2017: “ROH Supercard of Honor XI”: Lakeland, FL – 3,500 sellout
Shown live on iPPV only
Set new attendance record for the promotion
Dark matches: Marcela beat La Amapola; Jenny Rose and Mandy León overcame Faye Jackson and Sumie Sakai; Kelly Klein overcame Deonna Purrazzo.
iPPV Broadcast: ROH TV Champion Marty Scurll defeated Adam Cole; Beer City Bruiser and Silas Young defeated Matt Taven and Vinny Marseglia; ROH 6-Man Tag Champions Mark Briscoe, Jay Briscoe and Bully Ray defeated Tama Tonga, Tanga Roa and Hangman Page; Jay Lethal rolled over Cody Rhodes in a bullrope match; Chris Sabin and Alex Shelley toppled Will Ferrara and Cheeseburger, and Shane Taylor and Rhett Titus in a three-way; Punishment Martinez defeated Kazarian; Bobby Fish toppled Silas Young by DQ; Will Ospreay and Volador Jr rolled over Dragón Lee and Jay White; ROH Champion Christopher Daniels defeated Dalton Castle; The Young Bucks overturned ROH Tag Champions Matt Hardy and Jeff Hardy in a ladder match to win the titles.

12th May 2017: “ROH War of the Worlds Tour – Night 3”: New York City, NY – 1,800 sellout
Shown live on PPV / Shown live on NJPW World (Japan only)
The Marty Scurll vs. Matt Sydal encounter did not air on the NJPW World broadcast.
Dark matches: Matt Taven and Vinny Marseglia beat Sho and Yohei; Caprice Coleman, Rhett Titus and Shane Taylor bested Cheeseburger, Gedo and Will Ferrara.
PPV Broadcast: Dalton Castle downed Bobby Fish, Kushida and Silas Young in a four-way; Hangman Page downed Kazarian; IWGP World Tag Champions Raymond Rowe and Hanson overcame Jonathan Gresham and Chris Sabin, and Evil and Sanada in a three-way; Will Ospreay toppled Jay White; ROH 6-Man Tag Champions Bully Ray, Mark Briscoe and Jay Briscoe defeated Hirooki Goto, Rocky Romero and Baretta; ROH TV Champion Marty Scurll defeated Matt Sydal; ROH Tag Champions The Young Bucks defeated IWGP Intercontinental Champion Tetsuya Naito and Bushi; Hiroshi Tanahashi dropped Adam Cole; ROH Champion Christopher Daniels defeated Cody Rhodes and Jay Lethal in a three-way.
