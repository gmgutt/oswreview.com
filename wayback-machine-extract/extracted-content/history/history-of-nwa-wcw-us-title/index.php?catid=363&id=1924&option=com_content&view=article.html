WCW US TITLE / NWA US TITLE (Undisputed Version)
The NWA Mid-Atlantic US Title is renamed to the WCW US Title in January 1991. The NWA continues to recognise the reigns as being part of the lineage of the NWA Undisputed US Title until September 1993.
07/14/1991 VACANT Baltimore, MD
The title is declared vacant after Luger wins the WCW World Title.
08/25/1991 Sting 86 Atlanta, GA
Defeated Steve Austin in a tournament final to win the vacant title.
11/19/1991 Rick Rude 419 Savannah, GA
01/11/1993 VACANT
The title is declared vacant when Rude suffers an injury.
01/11/1993 Dustin Rhodes 138 Atlanta, GA
Defeated Ricky Steamboat in a tournament final to win the title.
05/29/1993 VACANT
The title is declared vacant after a controversial match between Dustin Rhodes and Rick Rude.
08/30/1993 Dustin Rhodes (2) 119 Atlanta, GA
Defeated Rick Rude in a rematch to win the vacant title.
