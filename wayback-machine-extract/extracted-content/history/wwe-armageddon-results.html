WWE Armageddon Results

WWF/E ARMAGEDDON RESULTS

12th December 1999: “WWF Armageddon 1999”: Fort Lauderdale, FL – 15,749 sellout (13,981 paid)
($402,413 live gate)
($102,044 merchandise sales)
The Acolytes won an 8-team battle royal; Kurt Angle pinned Steve Blackman; Miss Kitty beat Jackie, BB and WWF Women’s Champion Ivory in a swimming pool evening gown match to win the title; Hardcore and Crash Holly dropped Viscera and Rikishi; Val Venis downed WWF European Champion Davey Boy Smith and D-Lo Brown in a Triple Threat match to win the title; Kane toppled X-Pac in a cage match; Chris Jericho rolled over WWF Intercontinental Champion Chyna to win the title; The Rock and Mankind downed WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn by DQ; WWF World Champion The Big Show pinned WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Bossman; Triple H bested Vince McMahon in a “no-holds-barred” match.

10th December 2000: “WWF Armageddon 2000”: Birmingham, AL – 14,920 (11,924 paid)
($573,990 live gate)
($83,977 merchandise sales)
Heat: (12/10): Scotty 2 Hotty beat D-Lo Brown.
PPV Broadcast: WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko, Eddie Guerrero, and Perry Saturn defeated Lita and The Hardy Boys in an elimination match; WWF European Champion William Regal bested Hardcore Holly; Val Venis pinned Chyna; Chris Jericho dropped Kane in a “Last Man Standing” match; Edge and Christian downed WWF World Tag Champions The Goodfather and Bull Buchanon, Road Dogg and K-Kwik, and The Dudley Boys in a four-way to win the titles; Chris Benoit overcame WWF Intercontinental Champion Billy Gunn to win the title; WWF Women’s Champion Ivory overcame Trish Stratus and Molly Holly in a three-way; WWF World Champion Kurt Angle toppled Steve Austin, Rikishi, The Undertaker, The Rock, and Triple H in a six-way “Hell in a Cell” match.

15th December 2002: “WWE Armageddon 2002”: Fort Lauderdale, FL – 9,000
($500,000 live gate)
Heat: (12/15): Jeff Hardy beat D-Lo Brown.
PPV Broadcast: Booker T and Goldust overcame Lance Storm and William Regal, The Dudley Boys, and WWE World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and Christian in a four-way elimination match to win the titles; Edge dropped Albert by DQ; Chris Benoit toppled Eddie Guerrero; Batista pinned Kane; WWE Women’s Champion Victoria toppled Trish Stratus and Jacquelyn in a three-way; Kurt Angle toppled WWE World Champion The Big Show to win the title; Triple H rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Shawn Michaels in a “Three Stages of Hell” encounter to win the title.

14th December 2003: “WWE Armageddon 2003”: Orlando, FL – 9,000
($450,000 live gate)
Heat: (12/14): Rico beat Jon Heidenreich.
PPV Broadcast: Booker T overturned Mark Henry; Randy Orton bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam to win the title. Mick Foley was guest referee; Chris Jericho and Christian downed Trish Stratus and Lita; Shawn Michaels overturned Batista; Ric Flair and Batista overturned WWE World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys, Scott Steiner and Test, Rob Conway and Rene Dupree, The Hurricane and Rosey, Mark Jindrak and Garrison Cade, and Lance Storm and Val Venis to win the titles in a “tag team turmoil” match; WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly downed Ivory; Triple H rolled over Kane and World Heavyweight Champion Bill Goldberg in a three-way to win the title.

12th December 2004: “WWE Armageddon 2004”: Atlanta, GA – 4,500
($300,000 live gate)
Heat: (12/12): Billy Kidman and Akio defeated Chavo Guerrero Jr and Paul London.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Tag Team Champions Rob Van Dam and Rey Mysterio dropped Kenzo Suzuki and Rene Dupree; Kurt Angle toppled “Santa Claus” in a “Kurt Angle Invitational”; Daniel Puder beat The Miz in a boxing match; Doug and Danny Basham beat Charlie Haas and Hardcore Holly; WWE US Champion John Cena bested Jesus in a streetfight; Dawn Marie pinned Jackie Gayda. Charlie Haas was guest referee; The Big Show toppled Kurt Angle, Mark Jindrak, and Luther Reigns in a handicap match; Sho Funaki overturned WWE Cruiserweight Champion Spike Dudley to win the title; WWE World Champion John Bradshaw Layfield rolled over Eddie Guerrero, The Undertaker, and Booker T in a no-DQ four-way match.

18th December 2005: “WWE Armageddon 2005”: Providence, RI – 8,000
($450,000 live gate)
Heat: (12/18): Jamie Noble bested Sho Funaki.
PPV Broadcast: John Bradshaw Layfield beat Matt Hardy; Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury downed Super Crazy and Psychosis; Chris Benoit bested Booker T (Best of Seven Series – match #4); Bobby Lashley dropped William Regal and Paul Burchill in a handicap match; Kid Kash overturned WWE Cruiserweight Champion Juventud Guerrera to win the title; WWE World Tag Champions Kane and The Big Show downed WWE Tag Champions Batista and Rey Mysterio in a non-title match; The Undertaker rolled over Randy Orton in a “Hell in a Cell” encounter.

17th December 2006: “WWE Armaggedon 2006”: Richmond, VA – 8,200 (7,100 paid)
($423,500 live gate)
Dark match: Oleg Prudius toppled Scotty 2 Hotty.
PPV Broadcast: Kane overturned MVP in an Inferno match; WWE Tag Champions Paul London and Brian Kendrick defeated Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury, WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy and Matt Hardy, and William Regal and Dave Taylor in a four-way ladder match; The Boogeyman bested The Miz; WWE US Champion Chris Benoit toppled Chavo Guerrero Jr; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms downed Jimmy Wang Yang; The Undertaker rolled over Mr Kennedy in a “Last Ride” match; WWE World Champion John Cena and World Heavyweight Champion Batista dropped King Booker and Finlay.

16th December 2007: “WWE Armageddon 2007”: Pittsburgh, PA – 12,500 sellout (11,000 paid)
($675,000 live gate)
Dark match: Jesse and Festus overturned WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz in a non-title match.
PPV Broadcast: Rey Mysterio toppled WWE US Champion MVP by count-out; Big Daddy V and Mark Henry dropped ECW Champion CM Punk and Kane; Shawn Michaels toppled Mr Kennedy; WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Triple H; Finlay overturned The Great Khali; Chris Jericho bested WWE World Champion Randy Orton by DQ; WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix overturned Mickie James; Edge toppled World Heavyweight Champion Batista and The Undertaker to win the title in a no-DQ three-way.

14th December 2008: “WWE Armageddon 2008”: Buffalo, NY – 12,500 (11,000 paid)
($670,000 live gate)
Dark match: WWE World Tag Champions The Miz and John Morrison beat Festus and Jesse.
PPV Broadcast: Vladimir Kozlov defeated ECW Champion Matt Hardy in a non-title match; CM Punk bested Rey Mysterio (WWE Intercontinental Title #1 Contender Tournament – Final); Finlay dropped Mark Henry in a “Belfast Brawl”; Batista pinned Randy Orton; Kelly Kelly, Maria, WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool and Mickie James downed Jillian Hall, Maryse, Natalya and Victoria; World Heavyweight Champion John Cena toppled Chris Jericho; Jeff Hardy rolled over WWE World Champion Edge and Triple H in a three-way to win the title.
