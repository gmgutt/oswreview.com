WWE NXT Live Specials 2016

WWE NXT LIVE SPECIALS (2016)

1st April 2016: “WWE NXT Takeover: Dallas”: Dallas, TX – 9,000 sellout
Shown live on WWE Network
Dark matches: Manny Andrade beat Christopher Girard; Apollo Crews defeated Elias Sampson.
WWE Network Live Broadcast: Jason Jordan and Chad Gable downed WWE NXT Tag Champions Scott Dawson and Dash Wilder to win the titles; Austin Aries toppled Baron Corbin; Shinsuke Nakamura pinned Sami Zayn; Asuka bested WWE NXT Women’s Champion Bayley to win the title; WWE NXT Champion Finn Balor rolled over Samoa Joe.

8th June 2016: “WWE NXT Takeover: The End”: Winter Park, FL – 450 sellout
Shown live on WWE Network
Ember Moon overcame Peyton RoyceAndrade Almas overturned Tye Dillinger; Dash Wilder and Scott Dawson overcame WWE NXT Tag Champions Chad Gable and Jason Jordan to win the titles; Shinsuke Nakamura beat Austin Aries; WWE NXT Women’s Champion Asuka defeated Nia Jax; WWE NXT Champion Samoa Joe defeated Finn Balor in a cage match.

20th August 2016: “WWE NXT Takeover: Brooklyn II”: Brooklyn, NY – 14,540 sellout
($691,707 live gate)
NXT: (08/24): Tye Dillinger rolled over Wesley Blake; Rezar and Akam defeated Shane Thorne and Nick Miller.
WWE Network Live Special: Austin Aries overturned No Way Jose; Ember Moon overcame Billie Kay; Bobby Roode downed Andrade Almas; WWE NXT Tag Champions Scott Dawson and Dash Wilder defeated Johnny Gargano and Tommaso Ciampa; WWE NXT Women’s Champion Asuka defeated Bayley; Shinsuke Nakamura beat WWE NXT Champion Samoa Joe to win the title.

19th November 2016: “WWE NXT Takeover: Toronto”: Toronto, ONT – 11,000 (9,500 paid)
Shown live on WWE Network
NXT: (11/23): Kona Reeves battled Rich Swann to a no-contest; Aliyah, Ember Moon and Liv Morgan overcame Billie Kay, Daria Berenato and Peyton Royce.
WWE Network Live Broadcast: Bobby Roode downed Tye Dillinger; Akam and Rezar rolled over Nick Miller and Shane Thorne (Dusty Rhodes Tag Team Classic Tournament – Final); Johnny Gargano and Tommaso Ciampa beat WWE NXT Tag Champions Scott Dawson and Dash Wilder in a “best-of-three-falls” match to win the title; WWE NXT Women’s Champion Asuka defeated Mickie James; Samoa Joe downed WWE NXT Champion Shinsuke Nakamura to win the title.
