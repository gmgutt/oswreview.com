WWE Main Event Results 2013

WWE MAIN EVENT RESULTS (2013)

Jan 2013

Air Date: 2nd January 2013
WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro bested The Great Khali. Gauntlet match: WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett defeated Yoshi Tatsu; WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett downed JTG; WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett dropped Justin Gabriel; Kofi Kingston beat Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett in a non-title match.

Air Date: 9th January 2013
Sheamus bested Dolph Ziggler by count-out; Wade Barrett defeated Zack Ryder.

Air Date: 16th January 2013
Randy Orton bested WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro by DQ; Jimmy and Jey Uso overcame Titus O’Neil and Darren Young.

Air Date: 23rd January 2013
Ryback downed WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro by count-out; Cody Rhodes and Damien Sandow dropped Jimmy and Jey Uso.

Air Date: 30th January 2013
Ryback dropped WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro by count-out; Tensai overturned Titus O’Neill.

Feb 2013

Air Date: 6th February 2013
Sheamus defeated Cody Rhodes; Damien Sandow overcame Daniel Bryan; Naomi and Cameron downed Tamina Snuka and Aksana.

Air Date: 13th February 2013
World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio bested Dolph Ziggler.

Air Date: 20th February 2013
The Big Show defeated Jimmy and Jey Uso in a handicap match; The Big Show pinned Brodus Clay; The Big Show dropped The Great Khali by count-out; Justin Gabriel bested Titus O’Neil.

Air Date: 27th February 2013
Sheamus defeated Cody Rhodes; Mark Henry downed Justin Gabriel.

Mar 2013

Air Date: 6th March 2013
Randy Orton bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett in a non-title match; Cody Rhodes defeated Zack Ryder; Sin Cara downed WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro in a non-title match.

Air Date: 13th March 2013
The Big Show beat World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio by count-out; Jimmy and Jey Uso defeated Drew McIntyre and Heath Slater; WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett downed Justin Gabriel.

Air Date: 20th March 2013
WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett defeated R-Truth; Layla and Natalya downed Alicia Fox and Aksana; Jimmy and Jey Uso dropped Titus O’Neil and Darren Young.

Air Date: 27th March 2013
Kane pinned WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro in a non-title match; The Bella Twins defeated Naomi and Cameron.

Apr 2013

Air Date: 3rd April 2013
The Miz overcame Darren Young and Titus O’Neil in a handicap match.

Air Date: 10th April 2013
Seth Rollins, Roman Reigns and Dean Ambrose beat Kofi Kingston, Brodus Clay and Tensai; Big E Langston defeated Zack Ryder; Jack Swagger downed Yoshi Tatsu.

Air Date: 17th April 2013
Justin Gabriel won a battle royal; WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett bested Justin Gabriel.

Air Date: 24th April 2013
Mark Henry bested Jimmy Uso; Mark Henry toppled Jey Uso; Mark Henry overcame Santino Marella; The Great Khali overturned Mark Henry by count-out.

May 2013

Air Date: 1st May 2013
WWE US Champion Kofi Kingston defeated Antonio Cesaro; Justin Gabriel downed Heath Slater.

Air Date: 8th May 2013
Randy Orton pinned Antonio Cesaro; Jimmy Uso, Jey Uso and Justin Gabriel overcame Heath Slater, Drew McIntyre and Jinder Mahal.

Air Date: 15th May 2013
Kane defeated Seth Rollins by DQ; Sin Cara downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett in a non-title match; Cody Rhodes dropped Justin Gabriel.

Air Date: 22nd May 2013
Alberto Del Rio defeated Big E Langston; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso downed Drew McIntyre and Jinder Mahal.

Air Date: 29th May 2013
Sheamus defeated WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett in a non-title match; Antonio Cesaro downed Justin Gabriel; Brodus Clay, Tensai and R-Truth dropped Heath Slater, Drew McIntyre and Jinder Mahal.

Jun 2013

Air Date: 5th June 2013
Sheamus toppled Antonio Cesaro; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso downed Cody Rhodes and Damien Sandow; Justin Gabriel dropped Curt Hawkins.

Air Date: 12th June 2013
The Miz defeated Cody Rhodes; Damien Sandow downed Sin Cara; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso dropped Brodus Clay and Tensai.

Air Date: 19th June 2013
WWE World Tag Champions Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns, and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose dropped Justin Gabriel and Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso; Antonio Cesaro bested Sin Cara; Kaitlyn beat Aksana.

Air Date: 26th June 2013
Chris Jericho defeated Cody Rhodes; Christian downed WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose by DQ; Titus O’Neil and Darren Young dropped Brodus Clay and Tensai.

Jul 2013

Air Date: 3rd July 2013
Christian defeated Damien Sandow; Jack Swagger downed Sin Cara; Big E Langston dropped Curt Hawkins.

Air Date: 10th July 2013
Dolph Ziggler bested Antonio Cesaro by DQ; The Great Khali, Brodus Clay and Tensai defeated Cody Rhodes, Damien Sandow and Heath Slater.

Air Date: 17th July 2013
Christian defeated Fandango; Natalya and Layla beat Alicia Fox and Aksana; Heath Slater battled Justin Gabriel to a no-contest.

Air Date: 24th July 2013
Sheamus toppled Jack Swagger; Sin Cara defeated Drew McIntyre; Damien Sandow downed Justin Gabriel.

Air Date: 31st July 2013
Wade Barrett dropped R-Truth; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso defeated Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; Mark Henry dropped Drew McIntyre.

Aug 2013

Air Date: 7th August 2013
WWE World Tag Champions Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns, and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose defeated Jimmy and Jey Uso, and Mark Henry; Natalya downed Aksana; WWE Intercontinental Champion Curtis Axel dropped Sin Cara.

Air Date: 14th August 2013
World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio bested Cody Rhodes; Jimmy and Jey Uso downed Heath Slater and Drew McIntyre; Big E Langston overcame Justin Gabriel.

Air Date: 21st August 2013
WWE World Tag Champions Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns, and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose defeated Jimmy Uso, Jey Uso and Kofi Kingston; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee overcame Naomi; The Great Khali toppled Jack Swagger; Bray Wyatt bested Justin Gabriel.

Air Date: 28th August 2013
WWE Intercontinental Champion Curtis Axel defeated Cody Rhodes; Titus O’Neil and Darren Young downed Jack Swagger and Antonio Cesaro; Kofi Kingston dropped Fandango by DQ.

Sep 2013

Air Date: 4th September 2013
Fandango bested Justin Gabriel; R-Truth beat Damien Sandow; Luke Harper and Erick Rowan rolled over Titus O’Neil and Darren Young.

Air Date: 11th September 2013
Kofi Kingston rolled over Antonio Cesaro; Titus O’Neil and Darren Young beat Heath Slater and Jinder Mahal; Big E Langston rolled over R-Truth.

Air Date: 18th September 2013
WWE Intercontinental Champion Curtis Axel beat Dolph Ziggler; Kofi Kingston rolled over Fandango; Damien Sandow beat Justin Gabriel.

Air Date: 25th September 2013
Jimmy and Jey Uso toppled Big E Langston and Fandango; The Big Show beat Damien Sandow; WWE Intercontinental Champion Curtis Axel beat Justin Gabriel.

Oct 2013

Air Date: 2nd October 2013
Damien Sandow beat Santino Marella; Erick Rowan and Luke Harper overcame Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; WWE Intercontinental Champion Curtis Axel beat Zack Ryder; Antonio Cesaro and Jack Swagger overcame Tensai and Brodus Clay.

Air Date: 9th October 2013
Heath Slater, Jinder Mahal and Drew McIntyre downed Brodus Clay and Tensai in a handicap match; Tamina Snuka defeated Aksana; Big E Langston rolled over Zack Ryder.

Air Date: 16th October 2013
WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose downed Dolph Ziggler; Brodus Clay, Tensai and Santino Marella defeated Heath Slater, Drew McIntyre and Jinder Mahal; Damien Sandow beat R-Truth; The Great Khali dropped Fandango.

Air Date: 23rd October 2013
Seth Rollins toppled Goldust; Jack Swagger and Antonio Cesaro bested Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; Naomi and Cameron rolled over Alicia Fox and Aksana; Damien Sandow beat Dolph Ziggler.

Air Date: 30th October 2013
Ryback overturned Kofi Kingston; Santino Marella defeated Heath Slater; Fandango bested The Great Khali; Los Matadores toppled Los Locales (Tyson Kidd and Ricardo Rodriguez).

Nov 2013

Air Date: 6th November 2013
Luke Harper and Erick Rowan rolled over Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; Nikki Bella defeated Alicia Fox; Jimmy and Jey Uso toppled Hunico and Camacho.

Air Date: 13th November 2013
Natalya defeated WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee by DQ; Justin Gabriel beat Tyson Kidd; Heath Slater and Drew McIntyre downed Titus O’Neil and Darren Young.

Air Date: 20th November 2013
Tamina Snuka defeated Naomi in a “lumberjill” match; Alberto Del Rio bested Santino Marella; Fandango bested Kofi Kingston.

Air Date: 27th November 2013
Alberto Del Rio bested Dolph Ziggler; Damien Sandow beat Santino Marella; Kofi Kingston rolled over Fandango.

Dec 2013

Air Date: 4th December 2013
Goldust beat Ryback by DQ; Jimmy and Jey Uso toppled Drew McIntyre and Jinder Mahal; Damien Sandow beat R-Truth.

Air Date: 11th December 2013
Curtis Axel beat Dolph Ziggler; Natalya defeated Alicia Fox; R-Truth and Xavier Woods toppled Brodus Clay and Tensai.

Air Date: 18th December 2013
R-Truth and Xavier Woods toppled Jinder Mahal and Drew McIntyre; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee downed Kaitlyn; The Miz beat Curtis Axel.

Air Date: 25th December 2013
Goldust defeated Jack Swagger; The Bella Twins and Natalya downed WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee, Tamina Snuka and Aksana; Fandango dropped Kofi Kingston.
