WWE Main Event Results 2014

WWE MAIN EVENT RESULTS (2014)

Jan 2014

Air Date: 1st January 2014
Damien Sandow beat Kofi Kingston; Naomi and Cameron rolled over Alicia Fox and Rosa Mendes.

Air Date: 8th January 2014
Jack Swagger and Antonio Cesaro bested R-Truth and Xavier Woods; Alberto Del Rio bested Justin Gabriel; WWE World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Goldust beat Jinder Mahal and Drew McIntyre; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee downed Kaitlyn.

Air Date: 15th January 2014
Kofi Kingston dropped Curtis Axel; Damien Sandow beat Sin Cara; Titus O’Neil and Darren Young defeated Heath Slater, Jinder Mahal and Drew McIntyre in a handicap match; Los Matadores and Torito bested Heath Slater, Jinder Mahal and Drew McIntyre.

Air Date: 22nd January 2014
R-Truth beat Damien Sandow; The Bella Twins defeated Aksana and Alicia Fox; Alberto Del Rio downed Sin Cara.

Air Date: 29th January 2014
Alberto Del Rio defeated R-Truth; Natalya downed Tamina Snuka; WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E Langston, Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso dropped Drew McIntyre, Heath Slater, and Jinder Mahal.

 

Feb 2014

Air Date: 5th February 2014
The Miz beat Fandango; Alberto Del Rio defeated Zack Ryder; Big E Langston downed Curtis Axel.

Air Date: 12th February 2014
Bray Wyatt, Luke Harper and Erick Rowan defeated Los Matadores and Sin Cara; Natalya downed Aksana; WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E Langston dropped Drew McIntyre; WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E Langston overcame Jinder Mahal.

Air Date: 19th February 2014
Seth Rollins, Roman Reigns and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose defeated Los Matadores and Sin Cara; Cameron rolled over Aksana; Kofi Kingston toppled Curtis Axel by DQ.

Air Date: 26th February 2014
Cody Rhodes, Goldust, Los Matadores and Torito dropped Curtis Axel, Ryback, Heath Slater, Drew McIntyre and Jinder Mahal; Aksana and Alicia Fox overturned Natalya and Eva Marie; Darren Young overturned Titus O’Neil; Sin Cara beat Damien Sandow.

Mar 2014

Air Date: 4th March 2014
Daniel Bryan toppled Kane; Nikki Bella defeated Alicia Fox; WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso overturned Los Matadores.

Air Date: 11th March 2014
Los Matadores overturned Ryback and Curtis Axel; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee downed Natalya; WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose downed Mark Henry.

Air Date: 18th March 2014
Cody Rhodes and Goldust rolled over Jack Swagger and Cesaro; Naomi and Cameron toppled Layla and Alicia Fox; The Big Show beat Titus O’Neil.

Air Date: 25th March 2014
Dolph Ziggler won a three-way over Alberto Del Rio and Sheamus; Summer Rae, Layla, Tamina Snuka, Aksana and Alicia Fox beat Natalya, Naomi, Cameron, Eve Marie and Emma; WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E downed Dolph Ziggler.

Apr 2014

Air Date: 1st April 2014
Sheamus overturned The Miz; Tamina Snuka defeated Nikki Bella.

Air Date: 8th April 2014
WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose, Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns overturned Bray Wyatt, Luke Harper and Erick Rowan; Jack Swagger bested Dolph Ziggler; Alexander Rusev beat Sin Cara.

Air Date: 15th April 2014
Los Matadores battled Jinder Mahal and Drew McIntyre to a no-contest; Wade Barrett rolled over Sin Cara; Tamina Snuka won a battle royal; The Big Show beat Jack Swagger.

Air Date: 22nd April 2014
WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E downed Alberto Del Rio; Hornswoggle downed El Torito; Sheamus overturned Titus O’Neil; Ryback beat Jimmy Uso.

Air Date: 29th April 2014
Goldust rolled over Alberto Del Rio; WWE Divas Champion Paige downed Alicia Fox; Wade Barrett rolled over Kofi Kingston; Bray Wyatt rolled over Sheamus.

May 2014

Air Date: 6th May 2014
Dolph Ziggler bested Jack Swagger; Naomi, Cameron and Natalya defeated Alicia Fox, Aksana and Tamina Snuka; Goldust rolled over Curtis Axel.

Air Date: 13th May 2014
Dolph Ziggler bested Damien Sandow; Emma defeated Alicia Fox; WWE US Champion Sheamus battled Cesaro to a double count-out.

Air Date: 20th May 2014
Damien Sandow beat R-Truth; Naomi dropped Aksana.

Air Date: 27th May 2014
Curtis Axel beat Cody Rhodes; R-Truth beat Damien Sandow; Natalya defeated Brie Bella; Erick Rowan and Luke Harper bested WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso.

Jun 2014

Air Date: 3rd June 2014
Luke Harper and Erick Rowan toppled Kofi Kingston and Goldust; WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett battled to a no-contest with Rob Van Dam.

Air Date: 10th June 2014
Jimmy Uso overcame Luke Harper; Rusev beat Kofi Kingston; Seth Rollins overturned Dolph Ziggler.

Air Date: 17th June 2014
Rusev beat Santino Marella; Naomi downed WWE Divas Champion Paige in a non-title match; Bray Wyatt, Erick Rowan and Luke Harper bested WWE US Champion Sheamus and WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso.

Air Date: 24th June 2014
Seth Rollins, WWE Intercontinental Champion Bad News Barrett and Jack Swagger bested Dolph Ziggler, Rob Van Dam and Kofi Kingston; Bo Dallas overturned Fandango; Ryback and Curtis Axel beat R-Truth and Xavier Woods.

Jul 2014

Air Date: 1st July 2014
Luke Harper and Erick Rowan toppled R-Truth and Xavier Woods; Naomi and Cameron toppled Alicia Fox and Nikki Bella; Dolph Ziggler, Big E and Rob Van Dam overcame Ryback, Curtis Axel and Cesaro.

Air Date: 8th July 2014
WWE US Champion Sheamus overturned Alberto Del Rio in a last man standing match; Rosa Mendes, Natalya, Summer Rae, Eve Marie and Naomi downed Nikki Bella in a handicap match.

Air Date: 15th July 2014
Emma beat Cameron; Big E and Kofi Kingston toppled Curtis Axel and Ryback; WWE US Champion Sheamus overturned The Miz.

Air Date: 22nd July 2014
Seth Rollins overturned Fandango; Ryback beat Jey Uso; Kofi Kingston and Big E defeated Titus O’Neil and Heath Slater; Jack Swagger bested Rusev by DQ.

Air Date: 29th July 2014
WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso overturned Ryback and Curtis Axel; Heath Slater and Titus O’Neil beat Tyson Kidd and Zack Ryder; Dean Ambrose defeated Alberto Del Rio by DQ.

Aug 2014

Air Date: 5th August 2014
Heath Slater and Titus O’Neil beat Goldust and Stardust; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee defeated Summer Rae; Jack Swagger bested Alberto Del Rio.

Air Date: 12th August 2014
Seth Rollins overturned Kofi Kingston; WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso won a three-way over Rob Van Dam and Sheamus, and Curtis Axel and Ryback.

Air Date: 19th August 2014
WWE US Champion Sheamus defeated Curtis Axel; Nikki Bella downed Emma; Heath Slater and Titus O’Neill dropped Los Matadores; The Big Show rolled over Erick Rowan.

Air Date: 26th August 2014
Rob Van Dam beat Seth Rollins by count-out; Adam Rose downed Curtis Axel; Goldust and Stardust rolled over Los Matadores.

Sep 2014

Air Date: 2nd September 2014
WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler overturned Damien Sandow; Layla and Summer Rae downed Natalya and Rose Mendes; Goldust and Stardust rolled over Los Matadores.

Air Date: 9th September 2014
Seth Rollins toppled Jack Swagger; Cesaro bested Zack Ryder; Adam Rose downed Titus O’Neil; WWE NXT Tag Champions The Ascension overcame Los Matadores.

Air Date: 16th September 2014
The Miz and Damien Mizdow beat WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler and R-Truth; Brie Bella defeated Cameron; Seth Rollins toppled Big E.

Air Date: 23rd September 2014
Los Matadores toppled Heath Slater and Titus O’Neil; Paige downed Naomi; Kofi Kingston overcame Bo Dallas.

Air Date: 30th September 2014
Cesaro bested Jack Swagger; Tyson Kidd beat Kofi Kingston; Brie Bella defeated Summer Rae and Layla in a handicap match; Bo Dallas toppled Mark Henry by DQ.

Oct 2014

Air Date: 7th October 2014
WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler overturned Bo Dallas; Cameron, Summer Rae, Nikki Bella and Layla defeated Brie Bella in a handicap match; Tyson Kidd beat Jack Swagger; Jey Uso bested Stardust.

Air Date: 14th October 2014
R-Truth beat Tyson Kidd; Cesaro bested Justin Gabriel; Rusev beat Big E.

Air Date: 21st October 2014
Tyson Kidd beat R-Truth; Big E downed Bo Dallas; Seth Rollins toppled Jack Swagger.

Air Date: 28th October 2014
Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso toppled The Miz and Damien Mizdow; Paige downed Natalya; WWE US Champion Sheamus toppled Bo Dallas.

Nov 2014

Air Date: 4th November 2014
Jey Uso bested Stardust; Curtis Axel beat Justin Gabriel; Tyson Kidd beat Sami Zayn; Bray Wyatt rolled over Sin Cara.

Air Date: 11th November 2014
Adam Rose downed Stardust; Alicia Fox beat Cameron; Tyson Kidd beat Sami Zayn; Dean Ambrose downed Justin Gabriel.

Air Date: 18th November 2014
Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso toppled WWE World Tag Champions Goldust & Stardust in a non-title match; Alicia Fox beat Paige; Tyson Kidd & Natalya defeated Adam Rose & The Bunny; Bray Wyatt rolled over Jack Swagger.

Air Date: 25th November 2014
WWE World Tag Champions The Miz & Damien Mizdow beat Los Matadores; Fandango bested Adam Rose; Natalya defeated Layla; Dean Ambrose downed Kane.

Dec 2014

Air Date: 2nd December 2014
Jack Swagger overturned Titus O’Neil; Goldust and Stardust rolled over Adam Rose and The Bunny; Fandango bested R-Truth; Jimmy Uso bested The Miz.

Air Date: 9th December 2014
No original matches broadcast. Was a preview show for TLC 2014.

Air Date: 16th December 2014
Kofi Kingston and Xavier Woods toppled Cesaro and Tyson Kidd; Paige downed Emma; Kane downed Erick Rowan.

Air Date: 23rd December 2014
Tyson Kidd and Cesaro bested Los Matadores; Brie Bella defeated Summer Rae; Big E downed Stardust; Luke Harper overturned Jack Swagger.

Air Date: 30th December 2014
WWE NXT Women’s Champion Charlotte downed Sasha Banks; Titus O’Neil beat Justin Gabriel; WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso rolled over Goldust and Stardust.
