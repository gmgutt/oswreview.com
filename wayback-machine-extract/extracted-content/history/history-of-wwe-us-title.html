HISTORY OF WWE US TITLE

See also: History of WWWF US Title

See also: History of NWA/WCW US Title

NB: WWE claims this title shares its lineage with the NWA (Mid Atlantic)/WCW US Title, although many historians link its lineage with the WWWF US Title. Because of this dispute, we have elected to recognise it as creating a separate and distinct title lineage.
