WWE Cruiserweight Classic Results

WWE CRUISERWEIGHT CLASSIC RESULTS

Jul 2016

Air Date: 13th July 2016
Gran Metalik defeated Alejandro Saez (Cruiserweight Classic – 1st Round); Ho Ho Lun overcame Ariya Daivari (Cruiserweight Classic – 1st Round); Cedric Alexander rolled over Clement Petiot (Cruiserweight Classic – 1st Round); Kota Ibushi dropped Sean Maluta (Cruiserweight Classic – 1st Round).

Air Date: 20th July 2016
Tajiri dropped Damian Slater (Cruiserweight Classic – 1st Round); TJ Perkins overturned Da Mack (Cruiserweight Classic – 1st Round); Lince Dorado bested Mustafa Ali (Cruiserweight Classic – 1st Round); Akira Tozawa beat Kenneth Johnson (Cruiserweight Classic – 1st Round).

Air Date: 27th July 2016
Zack Sabre Jr rolled over Tyson Dux (Cruiserweight Classic – 1st Round); Drew Gulak defeated Harv Sihra (Cruiserweight Classic – 1st Round); Tony Nese downed Anthony Bennett (Cruiserweight Classic – 1st Round); Brian Kendrick defeated Raúl Mendoza (Cruiserweight Classic – 1st Round).

Aug 2016

Air Date: 3rd August 2016
Rich Swann overcame Jason Lee (Cruiserweight Classic – 1st Round); Noam Dar rolled over Gurv Sihra (Cruiserweight Classic – 1st Round); Jack Gallagher rolled over Fabian Aichner (Cruiserweight Classic – 1st Round); Johnny Gargano bested Tommaso Ciampa (Cruiserweight Classic – 1st Round).

Air Date: 10th August 2016
Gran Metalik defeated Tajiri (Cruiserweight Classic – 2nd Round); Kota Ibushi dropped Cedric Alexander (Cruiserweight Classic – 2nd Round).

Air Date: 17th August 2016
Akira Tozawa beat Jack Gallagher (Cruiserweight Classic – 2nd Round); Noam Dar rolled over Ho Ho Lun (Cruiserweight Classic – 2nd Round); Brian Kendrick defeated Tony Nese (Cruiserweight Classic – 2nd Round).

Air Date: 24th August 2016
Rich Swann overcame Lince Dorado (Cruiserweight Classic – 2nd Round); Zack Sabre Jr rolled over Drew Gulak (Cruiserweight Classic – 2nd Round); TJ Perkins overturned Johnny Gargano (Cruiserweight Classic – 2nd Round).

Air Date: 31st August 2016
Gran Metalik defeated Akira Tozawa (Cruiserweight Classic – Quarter-Final); Kota Ibushi dropped Brian Kendrick (Cruiserweight Classic – Quarter-Final).

Sep 2016

Air Date: 7th September 2016
Zack Sabre Jr rolled over Noam Dar (Cruiserweight Classic – Quarter-Final); TJ Perkins overturned Rich Swann (Cruiserweight Classic – Quarter-Final).

Air Date: 14th September 2016
Gran Metalik defeated Zack Sabre Jr (Cruiserweight Classic – Semi-Final); TJ Perkins overturned Kota Ibushi (Cruiserweight Classic – Semi-Final); Johnny Gargano and Tommaso Ciampa beat Noam Dar and Cedric Alexander; TJ Perkins overturned Gran Metalik to become the first WWE Cruiserweight Champion (Cruiserweight Classic – Final).
