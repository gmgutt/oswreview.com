WWF Superstars Results 1995

WWF SUPERSTARS RESULTS (1995)

Jan 1995

Air Date: 7th January 1995
The 1-2-3 Kid and Bob Holly downed Well Dunn (WWF World Tag Title Tournament – Quarter-Finals); Bob Backlund dropped Bob Knight; Mabel bested Mike Sharpe; Davey Boy Smith overturned Mike Khoury; Mantaur beat Walt Snow.

Air Date: 14th January 1995
Tatanka and Bam Bam Bigelow overturned The New Headshrinkers (WWF World Tag Title Tournament – Semi-Finals); Jeff Jarrett bested Mike Moraldo; Duke Droese overcame Chris Kanyon; The 1-2-3 Kid and Bob Holly toppled The Heavenly Bodies (WWF World Tag Title Tournament – Semi-Finals).

Air Date: 21st January 1995
WWF Intercontinental Champion Razor Ramon defeated Charlie Hunter; Henry Godwinn downed Rich Myers; Adam Bomb dropped Mark Starr; King Kong Bundy bested Nick Barberri; Aldo Montoya beat Nick Torentino.

Air Date: 28th January 1995
Men on a Mission won a squash; Doink pinned Kwang; Owen Hart won a squash; Kama downed Matt Hardy; Lex Luger bested Ray Hudson.

Feb 1995

Air Date: 4th February 1995
The Undertaker overturned Henry Godwinn; Mantaur downed Gary Scott; The Bushwhackers defeated Reno Riggins and Adam Croom; King Kong Bundy dropped Bill Weaver; Duke Droese beat The Brooklyn Brawler; Jacob and Eli Blu overcame Butch Long and Jason Ahrndt.

Air Date: 11th February 1995
Tatanka pinned Gary Sabaugh; The Heavenly Bodies defeated Raven Clark and Buck Quartermaine; Shawn Michaels dropped Adam Bomb; Man Mountain Rock downed Bill Payne; Fatu and Sionne defeated Well Dunn; Bob Backlund beat Lee Tobin; Davey Boy Smith overcame Reno Riggins.

Air Date: 18th February 1995
Hakushi defeated Buck Quartermaine; WWF World Tag Champions The Smoking Gunns battled The New Headshrinkers to a double count-out; Razor Ramon downed Bill Payne; Kwang dropped Rick Santana.

Air Date: 25th February 1995
Davey Boy Smith won a battle royal; WWF Intercontinental Champion Jeff Jarrett defeated Ben Jordan; Man Mountain Rock downed Bob Cook; Owen Hart dropped Gary Scott.

Mar 1995

Air Date: 4th March 1995
The 1-2-3 Kid defeated Bob Cook; Kama won a squash; Henry Godwinn downed Leroy Howard; Men on a Mission dropped Dennis Knight and The Black Phantom; King Kong Bundy pinned Butch Long;  Lex Luger rolled over Sonny Pruitt.

Air Date: 11th March 1995
Razor Ramon pinned Mantaur; Doink beat Charlie Hunter; Bob Backlund defeated Ken Raper; Bam Bam Bigelow bested Bob Holly.

Air Date: 18th March 1995
Adam Bomb defeated Tom Prichard; Jacob and Eli Blu downed Well Dunn; Lex Luger dropped King Kong Bundy by DQ; Man Mountain Rock beat The Black Phantom; Kama toppled Leroy Howard.

Air Date: 25th March 1995
Tatanka beat Davey Boy Smith by count-out; WWF World Tag Champions The Smoking Gunns overcame Rip Rogers and Chic Donovan; Hakushi defeated Aldo Montoya; Henry Godwinn downed Mark Starr.

Apr 1995

Air Date: 1st April 1995
Wrestlemania preview show. No original matches broadcast.

Air Date: 8th April 1995
Men on a Mission beat Buck Quartermaine and Bill Payne; The 1-2-3 Kid defeated Mike Khoury; Mantaur downed Rich Myers; Aldo Montoya upset WWF Intercontinental Champion Jeff Jarrett in a non-title match; Jean Pierre Lafitte pinned Scott Taylor.

Air Date: 15th April 1995
Lex Luger and Davey Boy Smith dropped Bert Centeno and Tony DeVito; Bob Holly overcame The Black Phantom; The Smoking Gunns overturned Hakushi and Kwang; Adam Bomb bested Mike Bell; Henry Godwinn toppled Nick Barberry.

Air Date: 22nd April 1995
WWF World Tag Champions Yokozuna and Owen Hart beat John Crystal and Leroy Howard; Razor Ramon pinned Mark Starr; Kama bested Tony Roy; WWF Intercontinental Champion Jeff Jarrett toppled Aldo Montoya; Duke Droese overcame Roy Raymond.

Air Date: 29th April 1995
Sid Vicious downed Aldo Montoya; Lex Luger and Davey Boy Smith defeated The Brooklyn Brawler and The Black Phantom; Hakushi dropped Kwang; Jean Pierre Lafitte overcame Gary Scott; Bret Hart rolled over Jimmy Del Ray.

May 1995

Air Date: 6th May 1995
WWF World Tag Champions Owen Hart and Yokozuna toppled The New Headshrinkers; Adam Bomb dropped Rock Werner; Bertha Faye overcame Jessica Rivera; Hunter Hearst Helmsley pinned Sonny Rogers; Tatanka pinned Kevin Kruger; The Undertaker overturned Dave Sigfrid.

Air Date: 13th May 1995
Razor Ramon pinned Todd Becker;  Rad Radford overcame Jerry Lynn; Doink beat The Brooklyn Brawler; Men on a Mission overturned Buck Zumhoff and Tommy Ferrara; WWF World Champion Diesel toppled Henry Godwinn.

Air Date: 20th May 1995
The Smoking Gunns beat Bob Cook and Mike Khoury; Jean Pierre Lafitte defeated John Chrystal; Man Mountain Rock downed Bill Payne; Mantaur dropped Gary Scott; Razor Ramon overcame Jacob Blu (KOTR Qualifier).

Air Date: 27th May 1995
Men on a Mission beat Russ Greenberg and Tim McNeany; Kama bested Duke Droese (KOTR Qualifier); Shawn Michaels pinned Tom Pritchard; Hunter Hearst Helmsley overcame Brian Walsh; Techno Team 2000 dropped Barry Horowitz and The Brooklyn Brawler.

Jun 1995

Air Date: 3rd June 1995
Lex Luger and Davey Boy Smith overturned Mike Bell and Gus Kantarakis; Skip downed Scott Taylor; The Roadie beat Doink (KOTR Qualifier); Bob Holly overcame Raymond Roy; Rad Radford defeated Burt Centeno; The Undertaker rolled over Tony DeVito.

Air Date: 10th June 1995
Sid Vicious and Tatanka bested John Chrystal and Mike Khoury; Hakushi overturned David Haskins by count-out; Razor Ramon pinned Bill Payne; Kama overcame Buck Quartermaine; The Smoking Gunns beat Iron Mike Sharpe and Duane Gill; Jerry Lawler rolled over Aldo Montoya.

Air Date: 17th June 1995
Henry Godwinn defeated Bret Hart by count-out; Jacob and Eli Blu downed John Smith and Gus Kantarakis; Man Mountain Rock dropped Kenny Kendall; WWF Intercontinental Champion Jeff Jarrett bested John Syznowski; Jean Pierre Lafitte overcame Reginald Walker; Savio Vega bested Bob Cook.

Air Date: 24th June 1995
Shawn Michaels downed The Brooklyn Brawler by count-out; Men on a Mission overcame Jerry Flynn and Mike McReynold; The Undertaker overcame Barry Horowitz; Skip pinned John Chrystal; Bam Bam Bigelow toppled Mantaur; Hunter Hearst Helmsley overcame Mike Khoury.

Air Date: 8th July 1995

Air Date: 15th July 1995
Man Mountain Rock beat The Black Phantom; WWF World Tag Champions Owen Hart and Yokozuna toppled Mike Khoury and John Crystal; Hunter Hearst Helmsley dropped Duke Droese; Aldo Montoya bested Tony DeVito; Waylon Mercy downed Jerry Flynn.

Air Date: 22nd July 1995
Jacob and Eli Blu overturned The 1-2-3 Kid and Savio Vega; Rad Radford toppled Scott Taylor; Skip pinned Russ Greenberg; Adam Bomb bested Kenny Kendall.

Air Date: 29th July 1995
Fatu beat Rad Radford; King Mabel pinned Gary Scott; Razor Ramon, Savio Vega and The 1-2-3 Kid overcame Jacob Blu, Eli Blu and Uncle Zebakiah; Waylon Mercy beat Major Yates.

Aug 1995

Air Date: 12th August 1995

Air Date: 19th August 1995

Air Date: 26th August 1995

Air Date: 16th September 1995

Air Date: 30th September 1995
