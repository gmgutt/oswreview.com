Raw Results 2017

WWE RAW RESULTS (2017)

Jan 2017

Air Date: 2nd January 2017
Seth Rollins overturned WWE Universal Champion Kevin Owens by DQ; Karl Anderson overcame Cesaro; Braun Strowman overcame Sami Zayn in a “Last Man Standing” match; Xavier Woods overturned Titus O’Neil; Drew Gulak defeated Cedric Alexander; WWE US Champion Roman Reigns defeated Chris Jericho; TJ Perkins overturned Brian Kendrick; Jinder Mahal and Rusev defeated Big Cass in a handicap match; Bayley toppled Nia Jax.

Air Date: 9th January 2017
Braun Strowman battled Seth Rollins to a double countout; Jack Gallagher rolled over Drew Gulak; Big Cass overturned Jinder Mahal; Neville downed Lince Dorado; Sheamus overturned Luke Gallows; WWE Raw Women’s Champion Charlotte Flair and Nia Jax defeated Bayley and Sasha Banks; Kofi Kingston overcame Titus O’Neil; Chris Jericho and WWE Universal Champion Kevin Owens overturned WWE US Champion Roman Reigns in a handicap match (Jericho won the US Title).

Air Date: 16th January 2017
Enzo Amore and Big Cass overturned Rusev and Jinder Mahal; Ariya Daivari dropped Lince Dorado; Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows overturned WWE Raw Tag Champions Cesaro and Sheamus by DQ; Big E downed Titus O’Neil; Cedric Alexander rolled over Brian Kendrick; Braun Strowman, WWE US Champion Chris Jericho and WWE Universal Champion Kevin Owens overturned Roman Reigns, Seth Rollins and Sami Zayn.

Air Date: 23rd January 2017
Luke Gallows overturned Cesaro; Sami Zayn overcame Seth Rollins; Jack Gallagher, TJ Perkins and Mustafa Ali dropped Drew Gulak, Tony Nese and Ariya Daivari; Braun Strowman, Jinder Mahal, Rusev and Titus O’Neil defeated Big E, Kofi Kingston, Big Cass and Enzo Amore; Roman Reigns overturned WWE US Champion Chris Jericho by DQ; Nia Jax defeated Ray Lyn; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rich Swann overcame Noam Dar.

Air Date: 30th January 2017
Sami Zayn overcame WWE US Champion Chris Jericho in a non-title match; Tony Nese downed Mustafa Ali; Cesaro, Sheamus and Bayley toppled WWE Raw Tag Champions Luke Gallows and Karl Anderson, and WWE Raw Women’s Champion Charlotte Flair; Braun Strowman overcame WWE Universal Champion Kevin Owens by DQ; Nia Jax defeated Sasha Banks by referee stoppage; Enzo Amore and Big Cass overturned Rusev and Jinder Mahal in a Texas Tornado match.

Feb 2017

Air Date: 6th February 2017
Nia Jax defeated Bayley; Braun Strowman overcame CC Bonan, Rob Kelly, Dusty Wallace and James Stock in a handicap match; Akira Tozawa beat Drew Gulak; WWE Raw Tag Champions Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows defeated Cesaro and Sheamus by DQ; Big E and Xavier Woods overturned Primo and Epico; WWE US Champion Chris Jericho defeated Sami Zayn; TJ Perkins, Jack Gallagher and Cedric Alexander rolled over WWE Cruiserweight Champion Neville, Noam Dar and Tony Nese; Samoa Joe downed Roman Reigns.

Air Date: 13th February 2017
Roman Reigns overturned Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows by DQ in a handicap match; Kofi Kingston overcame Bo Dallas; Jack Gallagher rolled over Noam Dar; Braun Strowman overcame Mark Henry; Sami Zayn overcame Rusev; Akira Tozawa beat Ariya Daivari; Cesaro bested Enzo Amore; Bayley toppled WWE Raw Women’s Champion Charlotte Flair to win the title.

Air Date: 20th February 2017
Enzo Amore and Big Cass overturned Cesaro and Sheamus; WWE Raw Tag Champions Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows overturned Roman Reigns by DQ in a handicap match; Kofi Kingston and Big E downed Rusev and Jinder Mahal; Nia Jax defeated Sarah Pierce; Sasha Banks overturned Charlotte Flair; WWE Universal Champion Kevin Owens overturned Sami Zayn; Braun Strowman overcame The Big Show.

Air Date: 27th February 2017
Big E and Xavier Woods overturned Jinder Mahal and Rusev; Akira Tozawa beat Noam Dar; Charlotte Flair and Nia Jax defeated Sasha Banks and WWE Raw Women’s Champion Bayley; Big Cass overturned Luke Gallows; Sheamus overturned Titus O’Neil; The Big Show defeated Primo and Epico in a handicap match; TJ Perkins and Jack Gallagher rolled over Tony Nese and WWE Cruiserweight Champion Neville; Samoa Joe downed Cesaro.

Mar 2017

Air Date: 6th March 2017
Kevin Owens overturned Sami Zayn; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Neville defeated Rich Swann; Enzo Amore and Big Cass defeated WWE Raw Tag Champions Luke Gallows and Karl Anderson by DQ; Akira Tozawa beat Ariya Daivari; Kofi Kingston and Big E downed Primo and Epico; Sasha Banks overturned WWE Raw Women’s Champion Bayley in a non-title match; Samoa Joe downed Chris Jericho by count-out.

Air Date: 13th March 2017
Sasha Banks overturned Dana Brooke; Tony Nese and Brian Kendrick defeated TJ Perkins and Akira Tozawa; Chris Jericho and Sami Zayn overcame Kevin Owens and Samoa Joe by DQ; Enzo Amore and Big Cass battled Cesaro and Sheamus to a no-contest; Roman Reigns overturned Jinder Mahal; Austin Aries overturned Ariya Daivari; The Big Show defeated Titus O’Neil; Bayley toppled Nia Jax by DQ.

Air Date: 20th March 2017
Samoa Joe downed Sami Zayn; Charlotte Flair rolled over Dana Brooke; Brian Kendrick defeated TJ Perkins; Nia Jax defeated WWE Raw Women’s Champion Bayley in a non-title, no-DQ match; Sheamus and Cesaro bested Karl Anderson, Luke Gallows, Enzo Amore and Big Cass in a handicap match; Austin Aries overturned Tony Nese; Braun Strowman battled Roman Reigns to a no-contest.

Air Date: 27th March 2017
WWE Raw Women’s Champion Bayley and Sasha Banks overturned Charlotte Flair and Nia Jax; Austin Aries overturned Noam Dar; An “Over The Top Rope Challenge” between The Big Show, Bo Dallas, Curtis Axel, Epico, Goldust, Jinder Mahal, Primo and R-Truth ended in a no-contest; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Neville downed Jack Gallagher; Sami Zayn overcame Kevin Owens in a no-DQ match.

Apr 2017

Air Date: 3rd April 2017
WWE Raw Tag Champions Matt Hardy and Jeff Hardy defeated Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Neville downed Mustafa Ali; Dash Wilder and Scott Dawson overcame Big E and Xavier Woods; WWE Raw Women’s Champion Bayley, Sasha Banks and Dana Brooke downed Charlotte Flair, Nia Jax and Emma; Cesaro and Sheamus overturned Enzo Amore and Big Cass; Sami Zayn overcame Jinder Mahal; Seth Rollins and Finn Balor rolled over WWE US Champion Kevin Owens and Samoa Joe.

Air Date: 10th April 2017
Scott Dawson and Dash Wilder rolled over Xavier Woods and Big E; TJ Perkins overturned Austin Aries; Nia Jax defeated Charlotte Flair; Finn Balor rolled over Jinder Mahal; Sami Zayn overcame The Miz; Cesaro, Sheamus, and WWE Raw Tag Champions Matt Hardy and Jeff Hardy toppled Primo, Epico, Luke Gallows and Karl Anderson; WWE Intercontinental Champion Dean Ambrose downed WWE US Champion Kevin Owens.

Air Date: 17th April 2017
Samoa Joe downed Chris Jericho; Luke Gallows and Karl Anderson overcame Enzo Amore and Big Cass; TJ Perkins overturned Jack Gallagher; Alexa Bliss overturned Mickie James, Nia Jax and Sasha Banks in a four-way; Finn Balor rolled over Curt Hawkins; Jeff Hardy toppled Cesaro; The Big Show battled Braun Strowman to a no-contest.

Air Date: 24th April 2017
Matt Hardy toppled Sheamus; Jack Gallagher and Austin Aries overturned Neville and TJ Perkins; Kalisto bested Braun Strowman in a dumpster match; Dana Brooke downed Alicia Fox; Finn Balor, Big Cass and Seth Rollins overturned Samoa Joe, Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows; Sasha Banks overturned Alexa Bliss by count-out; Apollo Crews overturned Curt Hawkins; Chris Jericho and Dean Ambrose battled to a no-contest with The Miz in a handicap match.

May 2017

Air Date: 1st May 2017
WWE Raw Women’s Champion Alexa Bliss, Emma, Nia Jax and Alicia Fox defeated Bayley, Sasha Banks, Dana Brooke and Mickie; Luke Gallows overturned Enzo Amore; Jack Gallagher, Rich Swann and Akira Tozawa beat Tony Nese, Brian Kendrick and Noam Dar; Apollo Crews overturned Heath Slater; Austin Aries overturned TJP; The Miz defeated Finn Balor and Seth Rollins in a three-way.
