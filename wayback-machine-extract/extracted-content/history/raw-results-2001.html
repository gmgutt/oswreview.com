Raw Results 2001

WWF RAW RESULTS (2001)

 

Jan 2001


Air Date: 1st January 2001
The Undertaker beat Rikishi; The Acolytes and Billy Gunn downed The Goodfather, Val Venis, and Bull Buchanon; WWF Hardcore Champion Raven battled Tazz to a no-contest (WWF Hardcore Champion Raven pinned Crash Holly); Chris Jericho and The Dudley Boys overturned WWF World Champion Kurt Angle, WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian in a tables match; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit overcame Test; The Hardy Boys overturned Taka Michinoku and Sho Funaki; Kane toppled The Rock; WWF European Champion William Regal bested Steve Austin. Stephanie McMahon was guest referee.

Air Date: 8th January 2001
Chris Jericho and The Hardy Boys defeated WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit, Perry Saturn, and Dean Malenko; Billy Gunn pinned Val Venis in a lumberjack match; The Rock, Jack Doan, and Teddy Long toppled Kane and Rikishi by DQ in a handicap match; WWF Hardcore Champion Raven overcame Steve Blackman; Albert and Trish Stratus bested Test in a handicap match; Steve Austin battled WWF World Champion Kurt Angle to a no-contest.

Air Date: 15th January 2001
Lita and The Hardy Boys defeated Steven Richards, Val Venis, and WWF Women’s Champion Ivory; WWF Hardcore Champion Raven downed Test; The Undertaker and Rikishi bested The Rock and Kane; Hardcore Holly battled Steve Blackman to a no-contest; Trish Stratus battled Jackie to a no-contest in a spanking match; Tazz, Taka Michinoku, and Sho Funaki beat K-Kwik and Too Cool; Steve Austin and The Acolytes rolled over WWF World Champion Kurt Angle and WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian.

Air Date: 22nd January 2001
Test pinned WWF European Champion William Regal to win the title; The Acolytes overcame The Hardy Boys; The Rock and WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho bested The Big Show and Chris Benoit; WWF Women’s Champion Ivory overcame Molly Holly; Kane toppled Rikishi by DQ; D-Lo Brown and Chaz defeated WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys by DQ; Al Snow overturned WWF Hardcore Champion Raven to win the title; Raven pinned WWF Hardcore Champion Al Snow to win the title; Triple H and Stephanie McMahon rolled over WWF World Champion Kurt Angle and Trish Stratus.

Air Date: 29th January 2001
WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys defeated Sho Funaki and Taka Michinoku; Val Venis overcame Steve Blackman; The Hardy Boys overturned The Acolytes, and Bull Buchanan and The Goodfather in a three-way; WWF Hardcore Champion Raven pinned Crash Holly; The Undertaker bested Haku; Lita battled Jackie to a no-contest; The Rock rolled over Chris Benoit, The Big Show, and WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho in a four-way.

Feb 2001

Air Date: 5th February 2001
WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho bested Matt Hardy; Edge and Christian defeated Sho Funaki and Taka Michinoku; WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys toppled The Undertaker and Kane by DQ; Rikishi and Haku overturned Too Cool; Chris Benoit dropped Al Snow; The Rock and WWF World Champion Kurt Angle rolled over Steve Austin and Triple H in a no-DQ match.

Air Date: 12th February 2001
WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho dropped Eddie Guerrero by DQ; Lita and The Hardy Boys dropped Chris Benoit, Perry Saturn, and Terri; The Undertaker and Kane battled Edge and Christian to a no-contest; Triple H pinned Billy Gunn; X-Pac toppled Brian Christopher; WWF World Champion Kurt Angle and WWF Hardcore Champion Raven bested The Big Show and Essa Rios; The Rock rolled over Rikishi.

Air Date: 19th February 2001
The Undertaker bested Christian and D-Von Dudley in a three-way; Lita beat Dean Malenko; Eddie Guerrero battled X-Pac to a no-contest; Chris Jericho was guest referee; Rikishi and Haku overturned The Hardy Boys; WWF Hardcore Champion Raven battled Crash Holly to a no-contest; The Rock and Steve Austin rolled over WWF World Champion Kurt Angle and Chris Benoit.

Air Date: 26th February 2001
X-Pac and Justin Credible beat Chris Benoit and Eddie Guerrero; Lita overturned Molly Holly; WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys overcame Rikishi and Haku; Vince McMahon and Trish Stratus battled William Regal and Stephanie McMahon to a no-contest; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Dean Malenko bested Taka Michinoku; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho toppled Raven; Jerry Lawler and The Acolytes downed Val Venis, The Goodfather, and Bull Buchanan; WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Show won a three-way over Kurt Angle and The Rock.

Mar 2001

Air Date: 5th March 2001
WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho bested Eddie Guerrero; WWF Women’s Champion Ivory beat Lita; The Undertaker and Kane toppled Haku and Rikishi; The Hardy Boys beat WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys to win the titles; WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Show toppled Al Snow; Steve Austin and WWF World Champion The Rock rolled over Kurt Angle and Triple H.

Air Date: 12th March 2001
WWF World Tag Champions The Hardy Boys beat X-Pac and Justin Credible by DQ; D-Von Dudley beat Christian; Chris Benoit toppled Eddie Guerrero; Steven Richards, Val Venis, The Goodfather, and Bull Buchanon bested WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho in a non-title handicap match; WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Show battled The Undertaker to a no-contest; WWF European Champion Test overcame Raven; WWF World Champion The Rock defeated Kurt Angle by DQ.

Air Date: 19th March 2001
Raven bested WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Show to win the title; Edge and Christian beat WWF World Tag Champions The Hardy Boys to win the titles; Tazz battled Steven Richards to a no-contest; Triple H battled WWF European Champion Test to a no-contest; X-Pac and Justin Credible beat Brian Christopher and Steve Blackman; The Dudley Boys overturned WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian to win the titles; Kurt Angle, Chris Benoit, and William Regal overcame WWF World Champion The Rock and WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho in a handicap match.

Air Date: 26th March 2001
Tazz defeated Val Venis in a lumberjack match; Molly Holly battled William Regal to a no-contest; Chris Benoit and The Hardy Boys beat Kurt Angle, Edge, and Christian; X-Pac pinned WWF European Champion Test in a non-title match; The Big Show toppled WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho in a non-title match; WWF World Champion The Rock and Steve Austin bested The Undertaker and Kane.

Apr 2001

Air Date: 2nd April 2001
Test and The Acolytes overturned WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero, Dean Malenko, and Perry Saturn; Trish Stratus battled Stephanie McMahon to a no-contest in a “whipping” match; Rhyno overturned WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Crash Holly in a non-title match;  WWF Hardcore Champion Kane toppled Val Venis; Kurt Angle and William Regal overcame WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho in a handicap match; X-Pac  beat Billy Gunn; The Rock battled WWF World Champion Steve Austin to a no-contest in a cage match.

Air Date: 9th April 2001
WWF Hardcore Champion Kane toppled The Big Show; Rhyno overturned WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Crash Holly and Molly Holly in a handicap match; The Undertaker bested The Big Show; Bubba Ray, D-Von, and Spike Dudley beat X-Pac, Albert, and Justin Credible; Chris Jericho battled Chris Benoit to a no-contest. William Regal was guest referee; Val Venis dropped Raven; Lita and The Hardy Boys rolled over WWF World Champion Steve Austin, WWF Intercontinental Champion Triple H, and Stephanie McMahon.

Air Date: 16th April 2001
Edge and Christian downed Hardcore and Crash Holly; Bubba Ray Dudley beat Justin Credible; WWF Hardcore Champion Kane toppled Steven Richards; Chris Jericho bested Kurt Angle by DQ; Chris Benoit overcame William Regal; Raven, Test, and Billy Gunn bested Eddie Guerrero, Perry Saturn, and Dean Malenko; The Big Show toppled Sho Funaki and Taka Michinoku in a handicap match; Triple H rolled over WWF Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy to win the title.

Air Date: 23rd April 2001
WWF Hardcore Champion Rhyno overturned Spike Dudley; Taka Michinoku and Sho Funaki overturned The Goodfather and Bull Buchanon; Chris Benoit and Chris Jericho bested Kurt Angle and William Regal in a submission match; The Big Show toppled Test by DQ; Perry Saturn, Dean Malenko, and WWF European Champion Eddie Guerrero overcame Raven, Brian Christopher, and Steve Blackman; Trish Stratus overcame Ivory; WWF World Champion Steve Austin, WWF Intercontinental Champion Triple H, Edge, and Christian downed The Hardy Boys and WWF World Tag Champions The Undertaker and Kane.

Air Date: 30th April 2001
WWF European Champion Matt Hardy beat Edge; X-Pac pinned Spike Dudley; WWF Hardcore Champion Rhyno overturned Chris Jericho; The Acolytes overturned Crash and Hardcore Holly; Test overcame WWF Intercontinental Champion Triple H by DQ; WWF Women’s Champion Chyna pinned Trish Stratus; The Undertaker bested WWF World Champion Steve Austin by DQ.

May 2001

Air Date: 7th May 2001
Bradshaw defeated Kurt Angle by count-out; Edge, Christian, and Rhyno overturned Eddie Guerrero, WWF European Champion Matt Hardy, and Jeff Hardy; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Jerry Lynn won a four-way over Crash Holly, Brian Christopher, and Taka Michinoku; Chris Jericho bested William Regal in a cage match; Lita beat Molly Holly; Spike, Bubba Ray, and D-Von Dudley overcame X-Pac, Albert, and Justin Credible in a tables match; WWF World Champion Steve Austin rolled over Rikishi.

Air Date: 14th May 2001
Rikishi defeated Kurt Angle by DQ; The Hardy Boys beat Perry Saturn and Dean Malenko; WWF Hardcore Champion Rhyno overturned Crash Holly; Chris Jericho and Chris Benoit toppled Edge and Christian; WWF Women’s Champion Chyna and Lita beat Ivory and Molly Holly; The Big Show, The Goodfather, and Bull Buchanon bested The Acolytes and Test; Kane toppled WWF World Tag Champions Steve Austin and Triple H in a handicap match by DQ.

Air Date: 21st May 2001
The Big Show toppled WWF Hardcore Champion Rhyno to win the title; The Acolytes overturned Dean Malenko and Perry Saturn; WWF European Champion Matt Hardy beat X-Pac; Hardcore and Crash Holly downed The Dudley Boys; Eddie Guerrero and Jeff Hardy beat Edge and Christian; WWF Intercontinental Champion Kane toppled Kurt Angle; Chris Benoit and Chris Jericho bested WWF World Tag Champions Steve Austin and Triple H to win the titles.

Air Date: 28th May 2001
The Hardy Boys beat X-Pac and Justin Credible; Chris Benoit toppled Rhyno; Steve Blackman and Trish Stratus overcame Perry Saturn and Terri; Chris Jericho bested WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Show to win the title; Eddie Guerrero overcame Albert; The Dudley Boys toppled Hardcore and Crash Holly in a tables match; WWF World Champion Steve Austin rolled over Chris Benoit.

Jun 2001

Air Date: 4th June 2001
WWF Intercontinental Champion Kane toppled Christian; Jeff Hardy beat Matt Hardy (KOTR Qualifier); Kurt Angle, Hardcore and Crash Holly downed Bubba Ray, D-Von, and Spike Dudley; Chris Benoit toppled The Big Show; Rhyno overturned Tazz (KOTR Qualifier); Trish Stratus overcame Terri in a “bra and panties” match; WWF World Champion Steve Austin bested Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 11th June 2001
Christian overturned WWF Intercontinental Champion Kane in a non-title match (KOTR Qualifier); The Hardy Boys downed Justin Credible and X-Pac; The Big Show and Rhyno overturned Chris Jericho in a handicap match; Perry Saturn overcame Steve Blackman (KOTR Qualifier); Bubba Ray, D-Von, and Spike Dudley beat Hardcore, Crash, and Molly Holly; Kurt Angle toppled Chris Benoit in a cage match.

Air Date: 18th June 2001
Kurt Angle toppled WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Jeff Hardy in a non-title match (KOTR 2001 – Quarter-Final); Tazz defeated Hardcore Holly; Rhyno overturned Tajiri (KOTR 2001 – Quarter-Final); Edge dropped Perry Saturn (KOTR 2001 – Quarter-Final); WWF European Champion Matt Hardy beat Albert by DQ; Christian rolled over The Big Show (KOTR 2001 – Quarter-Final); Spike Dudley and WWF World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and Chris Benoit toppled WWF World Champion Steve Austin and The Dudley Boys.

Air Date: 25th June 2001
Rhyno overturned WWF Hardcore Champion Test to win the title; X-Pac pinned WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Jeff Hardy to win the title; Tazz defeated Steven Richards; WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys overturned The Undertaker and WWF Intercontinental Champion Kane; The Big Show overcame WWF European Champion Matt Hardy by DQ; Edge pinned Billy Gunn; Chris Jericho bested Tajiri.

Jul 2001

Air Date: 2nd July 2001
The Undertaker downed WWF Intercontinental Champion Albert by DQ; Molly Holly dropped Crash Holly; Tajiri beat Scotty 2 Hotty; Chris Jericho bested The Dudley Boys in a handicap match; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion X-Pac defeated Christian; Rhyno overturned Test; Matt Hardy and Lita beat The Big Show and Trish Stratus; WCW World Champion Booker T battled Buff Bagwell to a no-contest.

Air Date: 9th July 2001
The Undertaker battled Diamond Dallas Page to a no-contest in a streetfight; The Acolytes overturned WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys to win the titles;  WWF Intercontinental Champion Albert beat Rhyno; The Big Show toppled Jeff Hardy; WCW World Champion Booker T rolled over Kurt Angle; Chris Jericho and Kane battled Lance Storm and WWF Hardcore Champion Mike Awesome to a no-contest; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion X-Pac pinned Scotty 2 Hotty; Team WWF (Billy Gunn, The Big Show, Hardcore Holly, WWF World Tag Champions The Acolytes) and Team WCW (Kanyon, Shawn Stasiak, Mark Jindrak, and WCW World Tag Champions Sean O’Haire and Chuck Palumbo) battled Team ECW (Tazz, The Dudley Boys, Tommy Dreamer, Rob Van Dam, Justin Credible, Rhyno, Lance Storm, Raven, and WWF Hardcore Champion Mike Awesome) to a no-contest.

Air Date: 16th July 2001
Chuck Palumbo beat Faarooq; Mike Awesome defeated Edge; Tazz downed William Regal by DQ; WCW World Champion Booker T overturned Chris Jericho; Kurt Angle toppled Raven in a hardcore match; The Dudley Boys overturned The Hardy Boys; Trish Stratus overcame Terri; The Undertaker and Kane battled Diamond Dallas Page and Rhyno to a no-contest.

Air Date: 23rd July 2001
WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Matt Hardy; Lance Storm pinned WWF Intercontinental Champion Albert to win the title; Tajiri beat Raven; The Undertaker bested Rhyno; Chris Jericho toppled Kanyon; Torrie Wilson bested Trish Stratus in a “Paddle on a Pole” match; WCW World Champion Booker T and The Dudley Boys overturned Kurt Angle, Edge, and Christian in an elimination tables match.

Air Date: 30th July 2001
Chris Jericho bested Tazz; WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Tajiri; Hurricane Helms and Torrie Wilson bested Matt Hardy and Lita; Booker T pinned WCW World Champion Kurt Angle in a no-DQ match to win the title; WWF Intercontinental Champion Lance Storm and WCW US Champion Kanyon bested Edge and Christian; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion X-Pac pinned WCW Cruiserweight Champion Billy Kidman to win the title; The Undertaker and Kane rolled over The Dudley Boys in a tables match.

Aug 2001

Air Date: 6th August 2001
WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Kane; Torrie Wilson and Stacy Keibler dropped Jacquelyn in a handicap match; Tajiri beat WWF Light Heavyweight and WCW Cruiserweight Champion X-Pac to win the WWF Light Heavyweight title; Chris Jericho and Kurt Angle downed The Dudley Boys; WWF Intercontinental Champion Lance Storm pinned Christian; WWF World Champion Steve Austin toppled WWF European Champion Matt Hardy in a non-title match; The Rock rolled over Shane McMahon in a streetfight.

Air Date: 13th August 2001
WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Tajiri beat Albert; Edge and Christian downed WWF Intercontinental Champion Lance Storm and Justin Credible; WCW World Tag Champions The Undertaker and Kane toppled Sean O’Haire and Chuck Palumbo in a cage match; Test overcame Spike Dudley; WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam battled Kurt Angle to a no-contest; Jeff Hardy pinned WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam to win the title; WCW World Champion Booker T and Rhyno rolled over The Rock and Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 20th August 2001
Lita, Molly Holly, and Jacquelyn toppled Ivory, Stacy Kiebler, and Torrie Wilson; The Acolytes, The Big Show, Billy Gunn, Scotty 2 Hotty, and Spike Dudley beat Sean O’Haire, Chuck Palumbo, Hugh Morrus, Tommy Dreamer, and The Dudley Boys; Test bested Chris Jericho; WCW World Champion The Rock pinned Lance Storm; WWF European Champion Matt Hardy beat Christian; Tajiri beat Booker T by DQ; Sara Calaway rolled over Diamond Dallas Page.

Air Date: 27th August 2001
The Undertaker bested Albert; Hurricane Helms overturned WWF European Champion Matt Hardy to win the title; Test overcame Bradshaw; WCW World Champion The Rock defeated Rhyno; Hugh Morrus overturned WWF Intercontinental Champion Edge by DQ; The Big Show toppled Booker T by DQ; Chris Jericho and Kurt Angle toppled WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam and Tazz.

Sep 2001

Air Date: 3rd September 2001
The Undertaker bested Test by DQ; The Dudley Boys overturned The Big Show and Tajiri; Spike Dudley beat Shawn Stasiak; WCW World Champion The Rock pinned Christian; WWF European Champion The Hurricane and Ivory beat Matt Hardy and Lita; WWF Intercontinental Champion Edge toppled Lance Storm; Chris Jericho and Jeff Hardy beat WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam and Rhyno.

Air Date: 10th September 2001
Christian downed Billy Gunn; Lita and The Hardy Boys beat Ivory, Lance Storm, and Hurricane Helms; Chris Jericho and The Acolytes overturned Rhyno and The Dudley Boys; Booker T overturned The Undertaker; Tajiri beat WCW US Champion Kanyon to win the title; Kurt Angle toppled WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam to win the title; Rob Van Dam rolled over WWF Hardcore Champion Kurt Angle to win the title; WCW World Champion The Rock defeated Test and Stephanie McMahon in a handicap match.

Air Date: 17th September 2001
The Dudley Boys overturned WWF and WCW World Tag Champions The Undertaker and Kane to win The WWF World Tag Titles; Lance Storm defeated Jeff Hardy; Perry Saturn bested Shawn Stasiak; Booker T and Shane McMahon bested Tazz in a handicap match; WWF European Champion The Hurricane dropped Spike Dudley; Christian downed WCW US Champion Tajiri by DQ; Test and Stephanie McMahon bested WCW World Champion The Rock in a non-title handicap match; Kurt Angle and Chris Jericho bested WWF World Champion Steve Austin and WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam.

Air Date: 24th September 2001
Bradshaw and WCW World Tag Champions The Undertaker and Kane toppled Test and WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys; Tazz downed Tajiri; WCW US Champion Rhyno overturned Jeff Hardy; The Big Show, Spike Dudley, and Molly Holly downed WWF European Champion The Hurricane, Lance Storm, and Ivory; WWF World Champion Kurt Angle toppled Booker T; Chris Jericho bested WWF Intercontinental Champion Christian by DQ; Kanyon bested Matt Hardy; William Regal pinned WWF Light Heavyweight and WCW Cruiserweight Champion X-Pac in a non-title match; WCW World Champion The Rock pinned WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam.

Oct 2001

Air Date: 1st October 2001
WWF European Champion The Hurricane dropped Spike Dudley; WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam, Booker T, and Test overcame Chris Jericho, The Undertaker, and Kane; WCW World Champion The Rock defeated The Dudley Boys in a handicap tables match; Tazz and Stacy Keibler dropped Tajiri and Torrie Wilson; William Regal overcame Lance Storm; WWF World Champion Kurt Angle toppled Shane McMahon.

Air Date: 8th October 2001
The Big Show, Spike Dudley, and Tajiri beat Tazz and The Dudley Boys; The Hardy Boys defeated WCW World Tag Champions Booker T and Test to win the titles; Edge dropped WCW US Champion Rhyno by DQ; WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam and Shane McMahon bested WCW World Champion The Rock and Chris Jericho; WWF Light Heavyweight  and WCW Cruiserweight Champion X-Pac pinned Scotty 2 Hotty; Molly Holly downed Lita; Steve Austin bested WWF World Champion Kurt Angle to win the title.

Air Date: 15th October 2001
WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys beat The Big Show in a handicap match; The Acolytes and Jacquelyn defeated The Hurricane, Lance Storm, and Molly Holly; WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over WCW World Champion The Rock by DQ; WCW Cruiserweight Champion Billy Kidman bested Tazz; Chris Jericho bested WCW US Champion Rhyno by DQ; Edge toppled WWF Light Heavyweight Champion X-Pac in a non-title match; WWF World Champion Steve Austin and Booker T overturned Kurt Angle and The Undertaker.

Air Date: 22nd October 2001
Lita and Trish Stratus overcame Ivory and Molly Holly; Tajiri beat WCW Cruiserweight Champion Billy Kidman to win the title; Kurt Angle toppled WCW US Champion Rhyno to win the title; Bradshaw pinned WWF European Champion The Hurricane to win the title; The Undertaker and Kane toppled Booker T and Test; WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over The Big Show; WCW World Champion Chris Jericho and The Rock defeated WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys to win the titles.

Air Date: 29th October 2001
WWF Intercontinental Champion Edge toppled WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam; The Hurricane and Molly Holly downed Tajiri and Torrie Wilson; WWF World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and The Rock defeated Booker T and Test; Lita beat Stacy Keibler; WCW US Champion Kurt Angle toppled William Regal; WCW World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys overturned The Undertaker and Kane; Shane McMahon bested Vince McMahon in a streetfight.

Nov 2001

Air Date: 5th November 2001
Ivory beat Lita; Test overcame WWF Intercontinental Champion Edge to win the title; WWF European Champion Christian downed The Hurricane; The Undertaker bested WCW US Champion Kurt Angle by DQ; The Acolytes and Jacquelyn toppled The Dudley Boys and Stacy Keibler in a tables match; WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam battled Booker T to a no-contest; The Rock pinned WCW World Champion Chris Jericho to win the title.

Air Date: 12th November 2001
Edge toppled WCW US Champion Kurt Angle to win the title; The Hardy Boys beat WWF World Tag Champions Booker T and Test to win the titles; WWF World Champion Steve Austin bested Tazz; Scotty 2 Hotty and Albert battled WCW World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys to a no-contest; WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over The Undertaker; The Big Show toppled Christian; WCW World Champion The Rock rolled over William Regal.

Air Date: 19th November 2001
WWF Women’s Champion Trish Stratus overcame Lita; WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys overturned WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam in a non-title handicap tables match; WCW World Champion The Rock pinned Kurt Angle; Kane toppled Chris Jericho by DQ; William Regal overcame Tazz.

Air Date: 26th November 2001
WWF European Champion Christian downed Jeff Hardy; WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over D-Von Dudley; WWF Women’s Champion Trish Stratus overcame Stacy Keibler in a “bra and panties” match; WWF Intercontinental Champion Edge dropped Test by DQ; William Regal pinned The Big Show; WCW World Champion The Rock and Kane toppled Kurt Angle and Chris Jericho.

Dec 2001

Air Date: 3rd December 2001
Kane toppled Bubba Ray Dudley; Albert overcame Test; The Hardy Boys beat Spike Dudley and Tajiri; WWF World Champion Steve Austin bested Chris Jericho; WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over WWF European Champion Christian; William Regal pinned Bradshaw; WCW World Champion The Rock and WWF Women’s Champion Trish Stratus overcame Kurt Angle and Vince McMahon.

Air Date: 10th December 2001
WWF Hardcore Champion The Undertaker bested Spike Dudley; Rikishi defeated Kurt Angle by count-out; William Regal pinned Kane; WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys bested The Rock and WWF Women’s Champion Trish Stratus; Matt Hardy beat Lita and Jeff Hardy in a handicap match; The Big Show toppled Lance Storm; WWF Undisputed World Champion Chris Jericho bested Steve Austin in a cage match.

Air Date: 17th December 2001
WWF Hardcore Champion The Undertaker bested Jeff Hardy; WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys beat Scotty 2 Hotty and Albert; WWF European Champion Christian downed Tazz; Lance Storm defeated The Rock by count-out; Kurt Angle and Test overcame WWF Intercontinental Champion Edge and Rikishi; Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo defeated The Big Show and Tajiri; Rob Van Dam rolled over WWF World Champion Chris Jericho by DQ. Ric Flair was guest referee.

Air Date: 24th December 2001
Rikishi defeated Test by DQ; Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo defeated The Acolytes; Rob Van Dam rolled over Lance Storm; Torrie Wilson bested Stacy Keibler; WWF European Champion Christian downed The Hurricane; Booker T overcame Maven; Tajiri beat Bubba Ray Dudley; WWF World Champion Chris Jericho bested The Rock and Kurt Angle in a three-way.

Air Date: 31st December 2001
Year-in-Review highlight show.
