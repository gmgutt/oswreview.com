ROH PPV/iPPV Results 2014

ROH PPV/iPPV RESULTS 2014

10th May 2014: “NJPW/ROH Global Wars”: Toronto, ONT – 1,500 sellout
Shown on iPPV (Ustream)
Dark match: Tadarius Thomas beat Romantic Touch.
iPPV Broadcast: Michael Bennett pinned ACH; Michael Elgin defeated Takaaki Watanabe; Mark and Jay Briscoe won a three-way over Bobby Fish and Kyle O’Reilly, and BJ Whitmer and Jimmy Jacob; Cedric Alexander toppled Roderick Strong; IWGP Jr and ROH Tag Champions The Young Bucks won a three-way over Rocky Romero and Alex Koslov, and Alex Shelley and Kushida (5th defense); Hiroshi Tanahashi and Jushin Liger toppled IWGP Intercontinental Champion Shinsuke Nakamura and Jado; ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal won a four-way over Matt Taven, Tommaso Ciampa, and Silas Young; IWGP World Champion AJ Styles and Karl Anderson bested Kazuchika Okada and Gedo; ROH Champion Adam Cole rolled over Kevin Steen.

17th May 2014: “NJPW/ROH War of the Worlds”: New York, NY (Hammerstein Ballroom) – 1,800 sellout
Shown on iPPV(Ustream)
Dark match: Caprice Coleman beat Adam Page.
iPPV Broadcast: Tommaso Ciampa, Matt Taven and ACH defeated Rocky Romero, Alex Koslov and Takaaki Watanabe; Roderick Strong and BJ Whitmer downed Gedo and Jado; ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal dropped Kushida; IWGP World Tag Champions Karl Anderson and Doc Gallows overcame Mark and Jay Briscoe (4th defense); IWGP Intercontinental Champion Shinsuke Nakamura bested Kevin Steen; Hiroshi Tanahashi toppled Michael Bennett; Bobby Fish and Kyle O’Reilly overturned ROH and IWGP Jr Tag Champions The Young Bucks to win the ROH Tag Titles. Only the ROH Tag belts were at stake; ROH Champion Adam Cole defeated Jushin Liger; IWGP World Champion AJ Styles rolled over Kazuchika Okada and Michael Elgin in a three-way (1st defense).

22nd June 2014: “ROH Best in the World 2014”: Nashville, TN – 1,000
Shown on PPV/iPPV
(12,000 PPV Buys)
ROH on Sinclair: (07/19): War Machine beat The Tate Twins; Adam Page defeated The Romantic Touch.
PPV Broadcast: ACH won a six-way over Tommaso Ciampa, Takaaki Watanabe, Caprice Coleman, Tadarius Thomas and BJ Whitmer; ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal downed Matt Tavern. Truth Martini was handcuffed to the ring post for the match; Alexander dropped Roderick Strong; Mark and Jay Briscoe toppled Matt Hardy and Michael Bennett in a no-DQ match; Kevin Steen pinned Silas Young; ROH Tag Champions Bobby Fish and Kyle O’Reilly bested Christopher Daniels and Kazarian; Michael Elgin rolled over ROH Champion Adam Cole to win the title.

6th September 2014: “ROH All Star Extravaganza 2014”: Toronto, ONT – 1,100
Shown on iPPV only
Mark Briscoe beat Hanson; Moose and RD Evans won a four-way over Caprice Coleman and Takakki Watanabe, BJ Whitmer and Adam Page, and Josh Alexander and Ethan Gabriel Owens; Christopher Daniels and Kazarian dropped Jimmy Jacobs and Roderick Strong; AJ Styles pinned Adam Cole; ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal bested Cedric Alexander; Jay Briscoe rolled over ROH Champion Michael Elgin to win the title; ROH Tag Champions Bobby Fish and Kyle O’Reilly toppled The Young Bucks in a “best-of-three-falls” match.

7th December 2014: “ROH Final Battle 2014”: New York, NY (Terminal Five) – 1,000 sellout
Shown on PPV/iPPV
Hanson won a four-way over Mark Briscoe, Caprice Coleman and Jimmy Jacobs; Roderick Strong overcame Adam Page; Michael Elgin dropped Tommaso Ciampa; The Young Bucks and ACH downed Christopher Daniels, Kazarian and Cedric Alexander; Moose pinned RD Evans; ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal toppled Matt Sydal; ROH Tag & IWGP Jr Tag Champions Kyle O’Reilly and Bobby Fish bested Alex Shelley and Kushida; ROH Champion Jay Briscoe rolled over Adam Cole.
