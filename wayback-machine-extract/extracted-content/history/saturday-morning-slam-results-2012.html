Brodus Clay beat Curt Hawkins.

WWE World Tag Champions Kofi Kingston and R-Truth downed Primo and Epico.

Air Date: 22nd September 2012
Zack Ryder toppled William Regal.

Air Date: 29th September 2012
Santino Marella beat Heath Slater.

Oct 2012

Air Date: 6th October 2012
Rey Mysterio pinned Michael McGillicutty.

WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz bested Justin Gabriel; Sin Cara pinned Jinder Mahal.

Air Date: 20th October 2012
Zack Ryder beat Justin Gabriel; Daniel Bryan toppled Tyson Kidd.

Air Date: 27th October 2012
Brodus Clay downed Epico; WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro dropped Derrick Bateman.

Nov 2012


Air Date: 3rd November 2012
Sin Cara beat El Local; Brodus Clay defeated Cody Rhodes.

Air Date: 10th November 2012
Jimmy and Jey Uso beat Darren Young and Titus O’Neil; The Great Khali overcame Primo.

Air Date: 17th November 2012
R-Truth beat William Regal; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston downed Zack Ryder.

Air Date: 24th November 2012
Justin Gabriel beat El Local; Heath Slater and Drew McIntyre bested Jimmy and Jey Uso.

Dec 2012

Air Date: 1st December 2012
Jinder Mahal bested Ted Dibiase Jr; Jey and Jimmy Uso defeated Justin Gabriel and Tyson Kidd.

Air Date: 8th December 2012
Sheamus defeated Titus O’Neil; John Cena beat Heath Slater.

Air Date: 15th December 2012
Tyson Kidd and Justin Gabriel beat Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; Ryback squashed Robbie Matthews and Yanni Grecco in a handicap match.

Air Date: 22nd December 2012

Air Date: 29th December 2012
No original matches broadcast. Featured Daniel Bryan vs. Tyson Kidd from the 10/20 broadcast, and John Cena vs. Heath Slater from the 12/08 broadcast.
