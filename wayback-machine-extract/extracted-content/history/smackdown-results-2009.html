Smackdown Results 2009

WWE SMACKDOWN RESULTS (2009)

Jan 2009

Air Date: 2nd January 2009
Finlay and The Great Khali beat Curt Hawkins and Zack Ryder; Ezekiel Jackson and Brian Kendrick defeated Festus and Jesse; Vladimir Kozlov downed The Hurricane; Kizarny dropped MVP; WWE World Champion Jeff Hardy and ECW Champion Matt Hardy rolled over Edge and The Big Show.

Air Date: 9th January 2009
Triple H beat John Morrison in a tables match; The Undertaker defeated Shelton Benjamin; The Bella Twins downed Michelle McCool and Victoria; Triple H dropped The Miz and Chavo Guerrero in a handicap match; WWE Tag Champions Carlito and Primo bested Ezekiel Jackson and Brian Kendrick; The Big Show rolled over Triple H in a “last man standing” match.

Air Date: 16th January 2009
Matt Hardy and Finlay defeated ECW World Champion Jack Swagger and Mark Henry; R-Truth beat Brian Kendrick; MVP overcame The Big Show in a “Last Man Standing” match; Michelle McCool overcame Victoria.

Air Date: 23rd January 2009
The Undertaker bested WWE US Champion Shelton Benjamin in a non-title match; The Great Khali pinned Chavo Guerrero; Brie Bella and Nikki Bella battled Natalya and Michelle McCool to a no-contest; Edge toppled Matt Hardy in a no-DQ match; WWE World Tag Champions The Miz and John Morrison toppled WWE Tag Champions Carlito and Primo Colon in a non-title match; Vladimir Kozlov and The Big Show rolled over Triple H in a handicap match.

Air Date: 30th January 2009
R- Truth defeated Brian Kendrick; MVP downed Chavo Guerrero; Umaga pinned Jimmy Wang Yang; The Undertaker bested Mark Henry (Elimination Chamber Qualifier); The Big Show dropped Festus (Elimination Chamber Qualifier);  Triple H rolled over The Great Khali and Vladimir Kozlov in a three-way (Elimination Chamber Qualifier).

Feb 2009

Air Date: 6th February 2009
Vladimir Kozlov won a battle Royal (Elimination Chamber Qualifier); MVP overcame WWE US Champion Shelton Benjamin in a non-title match; Brian Kendrick and WWE World Tag Champions The Miz and John Morrison toppled R-Truth and WWE Tag Champions Carlito and Primo Colon; Michelle McCool dropped Eve Torres; Umaga defeated Sho Funaki; Triple H and The Undertaker rolled over WWE World Champion Edge and The Big Show.

Air Date: 13th February 2009
MVP beat Chavo Guerrero by DQ; WWE World Tag Champions The Miz and John Morrison defeated WWE Tag Champions Primo and Carlito Colon in a non-title match; R-Truth bested Ezekiel Jackson; Maria pinned Michelle McCool: Triple H, The Undertaker, The Big Show and Vladimir Kozlov battled to a no-contest in a four-way.

Air Date: 20th February 2009
Michelle McCool and WWE Divas Champion Maryse defeated Eve Torres and Maria; MVP and R-Truth downed Chavo Guerrero and WWE US Champion Shelton Benjamin; Umaga dropped Scotty Goldman; Jeff Hardy rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Edge by DQ.

Air Date: 27th February 2009
WWE Tag Champions Carlito and Primo Colon beat WWE World Tag Champions The Miz and John Morrison in a non-title match; Vladimir Kozlov defeated The Undertaker; Chavo Guerrero bested WWE US Champion Shelton Benjamin in a non-title match; The Big Show rolled over John Cena.

Mar 2009

Air Date: 6th March 2009
MVP downed Matt Hardy (MITB Qualifier); WWE Divas Champion Maryse downed Eve Torres; WWE US Champion Shelton Benjamin defeated Jeff Hardy by DQ (MITB Qualifier); WWE World Champion Triple H battled Umaga to a no-contest.

Air Date: 13th March 2009
WWE World Tag Champions The Miz and John Morrison bested WWE Tag Champions Carlito and Primo Colon in a non-title match; Chavo Guerrero and WWE US Champion Shelton Benjamin toppled R-Truth and MVP; WWE Women’s Champion Melina and Maria downed WWE Diva’s Champion Maryse and Michelle McCool; Finlay defeated Brian Kendrick (MITB Qualifier); The Undertaker toppled Vladimir Kozlov.

Air Date: 20th March 2009
WWE World Champion Triple H pinned Vladimir Kozlov; MVP overcame WWE US Champion Shelton Benjamin to win the title; Shawn Michaels toppled Kane; Jeff Hardy downed Brian Kendrick in an “Extreme Rules” match; The Undertaker rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion John Bradshaw Layfield in a non-title match.

Air Date: 27th March 2009
Kane, Finlay, Christian and Shelton Benjamin beat WWE US Champion MVP, Kofi Kingston, CM Punk and Mark Henry; The Big Show defeated Jesse and Festus in a handicap match; Rey Mysterio toppled Chavo Guerrero; Matt Hardy overturned R-Truth; WWE Tag Champions Primo and Carlito Colon, and Brie Bella rolled over WWE World Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz, and Nikki Bella; Michelle McCool rolled over WWE Divas Champion Maryse by DQ.

Apr 2009

Air Date: 3rd April 2009
Gail Kim and Maria dropped Michelle McCool and WWE Divas Champion Maryse; WWE US Champion MVP bested Shelton Benjamin; Jeff Hardy rolled over Ezekiel Jackson.

Air Date: 10th April 2009
The Big Show pinned Kofi Kingston; The Great Khali beat Santino Marella; Gail Kim bested Michelle McCool; Ted Dibiase Jr and Cody Rhodes toppled WWE Unified World Tag Champions Carlito and Primo in a non-title match; Matt Hardy overturned Jeff Hardy in a “stretcher” match.

Air Date: 17th April 2009
The Big Show beat Jeff Hardy; Gail Kim defeated WWE Diva’s Champion Maryse in a non-title match; John Morrison downed R-Truth; The Undertaker dropped Shelton Benjamin; Dolph Ziggler bested WWE US Champion MVP in a non-title match; Batista rolled over Ted Dibiase Jr.

Air Date: 24th April 2009
Matt Hardy and Kane defeated Jeff Hardy and CM Punk; The Big Show dropped The Undertaker by stoppage; WWE Divas Champion Maryse pinned Gail Kim; WWE US Champion MVP downed Chavo Guerrero Jr; Batista and Shane McMahon bested Ted Dibiase Jr and Cody Rhodes.

May 2009

Air Date: 1st May 2009
John Morrison bested Shelton Benjamin; Jeff Hardy toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Rey Mysterio, Kane, and Chris Jericho in a four-way elimination match; WWE US Champion MVP overturned Dolph Ziggler; CM Punk pinned World Heavyweight Champion Edge in a non-title match.

Air Date: 8th May 2009
R Truth defeated Mike Knox; Michelle McCool downed Gail Kim; John Morrison dropped Charlie Haas; CM Punk bested World Heavyweight Champion Edge by count-out; The Great Khali toppled Dolph Ziggler by DQ; Jeff Hardy pinned Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 15th May 2009
CM Punk and John Morrison defeated Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas; Jeff Hardy overturned Ricky Ortiz; WWE Women’s Champion Melina and Gail Kim downed Michelle McCool and Alicia Fox; Dolph Ziggler pinned Jimmy Wang Yang; R Truth dropped Mike Knox; World Heavyweight Champion Edge rolled over Chris Jericho by DQ.

Air Date: 22nd May 2009
John Morrison and Cryme Tyme beat Shelton Benjamin, Charlie Haas and Ricky Ortiz; Michelle McCool bested Gail Kim; CM Punk toppled Chris Jericho by DQ; Dolph Ziggler overcame R-Truth; Jeff Hardy toppled World Heavyweight Champion Edge in a non-title match.

Air Date: 29th May 2009
The Great Khali and R-Truth beat Mike Knox and Dolph Ziggler; WWE Women’s Champion Melina defeated Alicia Fox; John Morrison downed Umaga by DQ; Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas dropped Cryme Tyme; Eve Torres overcame Layla; World Heavyweight Champion Edge and Chris Jericho bested Jeff Hardy in a handicap match.

Jun 2009

Air Date: 5th June 2009
John Morrison beat Shelton Benjamin; Chris Jericho downed R-Truth; CM Punk dropped Umaga; Michelle McCool, Layla and Alicia Fox defeated Gail Kim, WWE Women’s Champion Melina and Eve Torres. Maria was guest referee; The Great Khali toppled Dolph Ziggler; World Heavyweight Champion Edge rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Rey Mysterio in a non-title match.

Air Date: 12th June 2009
Dolph Ziggler beat The Great Khali by count-out; Michelle McCool and Layla downed Eve Torres and WWE Women’s Champion Melina; John Morrison and R-Truth bested Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin; World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho in a non-title match; Edge rolled over Jeff Hardy by DQ.

Air Date: 19th June 2009
Jeff Hardy beat WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho by count-out; The Great Khali defeated Dolph Ziggler by DQ; WWE Women’s Champion Melina downed Alicia Fox; Edge dropped John Morrison; Cryme Tyme and R-Truth toppled Charlie Haas, Shelton Benjamin and Ricky Ortiz; World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk bested Rey Mysterio.

Air Date 26th June 2009
R-Truth defeated Shelton Benjamin; Alicia Fox and Michelle McCool downed Gail Kim and WWE Women’s Champion Melina; John Morrison toppled World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk in a non-title match; Edge and WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho rolled over Rey Mysterio and Jeff Hardy in a cage match. CM Punk was guest referee.

Jul 2009

Air Date: 3rd July 2009
Finlay beat Ricky Ortiz; Dolph Ziggler defeated R-Truth; Kane bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Rey Mysterio in a non-title match; Cryme Tyme downed DH Smith and Tyson Kidd; Melina and Maria dropped WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool and Layla,;WWE Unified World Tag Champions Edge and Chris Jericho bested World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk and Jeff Hardy.

Air Date: 10th July 2009
Kane defeated Jeff Hardy; Dolph Ziggler downed John Morrison; Melina and Eve Torres dropped WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool and Layla; World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk battled The Great Khali to a no-contest; DH Smith and Tyson Kidd bested Cryme Tyme; WWE Intercontinental Champion Rey Mysterio rolled over Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 17th July 2009
John Morrison bested World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk in a non-title match; Kane toppled R-Truth; DH Smith, Tyson Kidd and Natalya beat Cryme Tyme and Eve Torres; Jeff Hardy and WWE Intercontinental Champion Rey Mysterio rolled over Chris Jericho and Dolph Ziggler by DQ.

Air Date: 24th July 2009
Jeff Hardy bested Chris Jericho; Dolph Ziggler toppled Finlay by count-out; The Great Khali overcame Mike Knox; Eve Torres overturned Natalya; R-Truth dropped Charlie Haas; WWE Intercontinental Champion Rey Mysterio rolled over World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk by DQ.

Air Date: 31st July 2009
Cryme Tyme defeated DH Smith and Tyson Kidd; Melina and Eve Torres downed WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool and Layla; WWE Intercontinental Champion Rey Mysterio and Finlay dropped Dolph Ziggler and Mike Knox; The Great Khali battled Charlie Haas to a no-contest; World Heavyweight Champion Jeff Hardy rolled over John Morrison.

Aug 2009

Air Date: 7th August 2009
John Morrison defeated Tyson Kidd; Slam Master J downed Charlie Haas; Dolph Ziggler won a four-way over Finlay, Mike Knox, and R-Truth; JTG dropped Chris Jericho; The Great Khali overturned Ricky Ortiz; World Heavyweight Champion Jeff Hardy rolled over CM Punk. Matt Hardy was guest enforcer.

Air Date: 14th August 2009
Dolph Ziggler defeated Finlay; CM Punk downed John Morrison; Melina dropped Layla; The Big Show bested JTG; DH Smith and Tyson Kidd overturned Jeff Hardy in a handicap match.

Air Date: 21st August 2009
Dolph Ziggler and WWE Unified World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and The Big Show bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Rey Mysterio and Cryme Tyme; Finlay toppled Mike Knox by DQ; Maria and Melina defeated Layla and Natalya; World Heavyweight Champion Jeff Hardy, Matt Hardy and John Morrison rolled over CM Punk, DH Smith and Tyson Kidd.

Air Date: 28th August 2009
John Morrison and Matt Hardy beat DH Smith and Tyson Kidd; Melina beat Layla; WWE Intercontinental Champion Rey Mysterio bested Kane by DQ; Chris Jericho battled Shad Gaspard to a no-contest; WWE Unified World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and The Big Show dropped Cryme Tyme; World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk rolled over Jeff Hardy in a “loser leaves WWE” cage match.

Sep 2009

Air Date: 4th September 2009
The Great Khali and Finlay beat Kane and Mike Knox; John Morrison defeated WWE Intercontinental Champion Rey Mysterio to win the title; Eve Torres and Maria downed Layla and Natalya; Matt Hardy toppled World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk by DQ.

Air Date: 11th September 2009
Finlay beat Mike Knox; WWE Unified World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and The Big Show defeated Cryme Tyme; WWE Intercontinental Champion John Morrison downed Charlie Haas; Layla dropped Melina; World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk rolled over Matt Hardy.

Air Date: 18th September 2009
WWE Intercontinental Champion John Morrison bested Mike Knox; Melina defeated WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool in a non-title match; DH Smith and Tyson Kidd downed Cryme Tyme; The Great Khali dropped Kane by DQ; Batista bested Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 25th September 2009
WWE Intercontinental Champion John Morrison and Finlay defeated Dolph Ziggler and Mike Knox; Kane downed Slam Master J; Batista dropped The Big Show by DQ; DH Smith toppled JTG; World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk rolled over The Undertaker by count-out.

Oct 2009

Air Date: 2nd October 2009
Batista bested Kane; The Miz and Dolph Ziggler overcame WWE US Champion Kofi Kingston and WWE Intercontinental Champion John Morrison; WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool overcame Melina in a lumberjack match; The Undertaker, WWE World Champion John Cena, Triple H and Shawn Michaels rolled over Randy Orton, Ted Dibiase Jr, Cody Rhodes and World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk.

Air Date: 9th October 2009
Rey Mysterio bested Chris Jericho; WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool defeated Eve Torres; WWE Intercontinental Champion John Morrison dropped Dolph Ziggler; Kane and Drew McIntyre toppled Matt Hardy and R-Truth; CM Punk rolled over Batista by count-out.

Air Date: 16th October 2009
Dolph Ziggler won a three-way over Finlay and Mike Knox; Cryme Tyme bested DH Smith and Tyson Kidd; Eric Escobar toppled Matt Hardy; Drew McIntyre overturned R-Truth by count-out; Rey Mysterio pinned Batista.

Air Date: 23rd October 2009
Tyson Kidd, DH Smith, Finlay, Matt Hardy and R-Truth defeated JTG, Eric Escobar, Drew McIntyre and Dolph Ziggler in a handicap match; WWE Intercontinental Champion John Morrison dropped Mike Knox; Batista and Rey Mysterio toppled Chris Jericho and Kane; Mickie James overturned Layla; World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker rolled over CM Punk in a submission match.

Air Date: 30th October 2009
Dolph Ziggler beat WWE Intercontinental Champion John Morrison by count-out; Beth Phoenix defeated Jenny Brooks; Matt Hardy downed Eric Escobar; Mickie James won a Halloween costume contest over WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool, Natalya and Layla; CM Punk bested Scott Armstrong; Chris Jericho toppled Kane.

Nov 2009

Air Date: 6th November 2009
Dolph Ziggler, DH Smith and Tyson Kidd beat WWE Intercontinental Champion John Morrison and Cryme Tyme; Beth Phoenix downed Brittany Carter; Drew McIntyre bested Jimmy Wang Yang; Rey Mysterio toppled Mike Knox; R-Truth dropped CM Punk; Matt Hardy rolled over Batista by DQ.

Air Date: 13th November 2009
CM Punk bested R-Truth; Natalya downed Mickie James; Finlay battled Drew McIntyre to a double-DQ; Beth Phoenix defeated Lisa Taylor; WWE Intercontinental Champion John Morrison battled Dolph Ziggler to a double count-out; World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker bested Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 20th November 2009
Batista pinned Matt Hardy; Drew McIntyre defeated Finlay; WWE Intercontinental Champion John Morrison bested Dolph Ziggler in a “best-of-three-falls” encounter; Mickie James downed Layla; Tyson Kidd dropped Rey Mysterio by DQ; WWE Unified World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and The Big Show battled World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker and Kane to a no-contest.

Air Date: 27th November 2009
DH Smith and Tyson Kidd beat Cryme Tyme; Matt Hardy defeated CM Punk by DQ; Batista downed Kane by count-out; WWE Intercontinental Champion John Morrison dropped Eric Escobar; World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker rolled over Chris Jericho by DQ.

Dec 2009

Air Date: 4th December 2009
R- Truth defeated CM Punk by DQ; Kane downed Mike Knox; Mickie James won a three-way over Beth Phoenix and Natalya; DH Smith and Tyson Kidd dropped Eric Escobar in a handicap match; Drew McIntyre pinned WWE Intercontinental Champion John Morrison in a non-title match; WWE Unified World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and The Big Show rolled over Cryme Tyme.

Air Date: 11th December 2009
CM Punk and Luke Gallows defeated Matt Hardy and R-Truth; WWE Unified World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and The Big Show downed Eric Escobar in a handicap match; Kane dropped Mike Knox; Mickie James and Maria toppled WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool and Layla; Batista bested Rey Mysterio in a streetfight.

Air Date: 18th December 2009
WWE Intercontinental Champion Drew McIntyre, CM Punk and Luke Gallows beat John Morrison, Matt Hardy and R-Truth; Kane dropped Eric Escobar; The Great Khali defeated Chris Jericho by count-out; DH Smith and Tyson Kidd downed Jimmy Wang Yang and Slam Master J; Beth Phoenix overcame Maria; Rey Mysterio bested Batista.

Air Date: 25th December 2009
R-Truth, Matt Hardy, John Morrison and Finlay beat Dolph Ziggler, WWE Intercontinental Champion Drew McIntyre, CM Punk and Luke Gallows; World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker bested Rey Mysterio by DQ; Cryme Tyme defeated Bryce Andrews and Pat Buck; Mickie James and Maria downed Layla and Beth Phoenix; WWE Unified World Tag Champions Shawn Michaels and Triple H toppled DH Smith and Tyson Kidd.
