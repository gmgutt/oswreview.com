WWE Elimination Chamber Results

WWE ELIMINATION CHAMBER RESULTS

21st February 2010: “WWE Elimination Chamber 2010”: St Louis, MO – 17,628 sellout (14,000 paid)
($850,000 live gate)
Dark match: Christian bested Ezekiel Jackson
PPV Broadcast: John Cena defeated WWE World Champion Sheamus, Kofi Kingston, Randy Orton, Ted Dibiase Jr and Triple H in an Elimination Chamber match to win the title; Batista pinned WWE World Champion John Cena in an impromptu match to win the title;  WWE Intercontinental Champion Drew McIntyre downed Kane; Michelle McCool and Layla dropped Gail Kim and Maryse; WWE US Champion The Miz toppled MVP; Chris Jericho rolled over World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker, Rey Mysterio, R-Truth, John Morrison and CM Punk in an Elimination Chamber match to win the title.

20th February 2011: “WWE Elimination Chamber 2011”: Oakland, CA – 11,500
($700,000 live gate)
Dark match: WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan bested Ted Dibiase Jr.
PPV Broadcast: Alberto Del Rio toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston in a non-title match; World Heavyweight Champion Edge defeated Kane, Drew McIntyre, Wade Barrett, Rey Mysterio and The Big Show in an Elimination Chamber match; Heath Slater and Justin Gabriel overcame WWE World Tag Champions Santino Marella and Vladimir Kozlov to win the titles; WWE World Champion The Miz pinned Jerry Lawler; John Cena rolled over CM Punk, John Morrison, Sheamus, R-Truth and Randy Orton in an Elimination Chamber match.

19th February 2012: “WWE Elimination Chamber 2012”: Milwaukee, WI – 15,306 sellout (12,000 paid)
($750,000 live gate)
Dark match: Hunico beat Alex Riley.
PPV Broadcast: WWE World Champion CM Punk defeated The Miz, Chris Jericho, R-Truth, Kofi Kingston and Dolph Ziggler in an Elimination Chamber match; WWE Divas Champion Beth Phoenix bested Tamina Snuka; World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan toppled Santino Marella, The Big Show, Wade Barrett, Cody Rhodes and The Great Khali in an Elimination Chamber match; WWE US Champion Jack Swagger overturned Justin Gabriel; John Cena rolled over Kane in an ambulance match.

17th February 2013: “WWE Elimination Chamber 2013”: New Orleans, LA – 13,000 sellout (12,000 paid)
You-Tube pre-show: Brodus Clay and Tensai beat Cody Rhodes and Damien Sandow.
PPV Broadcast: World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio bested The Big Show; WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro downed The Miz by DQ; Jack Swagger won an “Elimination Chamber” match over Randy Orton, Chris Jericho, Mark Henry, Daniel Bryan and Kane;  Dean Ambrose, Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns dropped John Cena, Ryback and Sheamus;  Dolph Ziggler toppled Kofi Kingston; WWE Divas Champion Kaitlyn defeated Tamina Snuka; WWE World Champion The Rock rolled over CM Punk.

23rd February 2014: “WWE Elimination Chamber 2014”: Minneapolis, MN – 11,000 sellout
($700,000 live gate)
Pre-show: Cody Rhodes and Goldust beat Ryback and Curtis Axel.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E Langston overcame Jack Swagger; WWE World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn overturned Jimmy and Jey Uso; Titus O’Neill toppled Darren Young; Bray Wyatt, Luke Harper and Erick Rowan rolled over Roman Reigns, Seth Rollins and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose; Cameron bested WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee by DQ; Batista pinned Alberto Del Rio; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton won an Elimination Chamber match over Daniel Bryan, John Cena, Cesaro, Christian and Sheamus.


31st May 2015: “WWE Elimination Chamber 2015”: Corpus Christi, TX – 7,000 sellout
Shown live on WWE Network / Only shown on PPV in international markets
Kick-off: Stardust beat Zack Ryder.
WWE Network Special: WWE World Tag Champions Big E, Kofi Kingston & Xavier Woods won an “Elimination Chamber” match over Titus O’Neill & Darren Young, The Ascension, Kalisto & Sin Cara, Los Matadores, and Cesaro & Tyson Kidd; WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella won a three-way over Naomi and Paige; WWE NXT Champion Kevin Owens bested WWE US Champion John Cena in a non-title match; Neville overcame Bo Dallas; Ryback won the vacant WWE Intercontinental Title in an “Elimination Chamber” match over Dolph Ziggler, King Barrett, Mark Henry, R-Truth and Sheamus; Dean Ambrose rolled over WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins by DQ.

12th February 2017: “WWE Elimination Chamber 2017”: Phoenix, AZ – 11,000
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Mojo Rawley toppled Curt Hawkins.
PPV Broadcast: Becky Lynch defeated Mickie James; Apollo Crews and Kalisto bested Dolph Ziggler in a handicap match; WWE Smackdown Tag Champions Jason Jordan and Chad Gable won a Tag Team Turmoil match over Simon Gotch and Aiden English, Fandango and Tyler Breeze, Heath Slater and Rhyno, Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso, and Konnor and Viktor; Natalya battled Nikki Bella to a double count-out; Randy Orton overcame Luke Harper; Naomi dropped WWE Smackdown Women’s Champion Alexa Bliss to win the title; Bray Wyatt won an Elimination Chamber match over WWE World Champion John Cena, AJ Styles, Baron Corbin, WWE Intercontinental Champion Dean Ambrose and The Miz to win the title.
