ECW PPV RESULTS (WWE)

12th June 2005: “ECW One Night Stand 2005”: New York, NY (Hammerstein Ballroom) – 2,500 sellout
($484,000 live gate)
Lance Storm bested Chris Jericho; Super Crazy downed Yoshihiro Tajiri and Little Guido in a three-way; Rey Mysterio toppled Psicosis; Sabu bested Rhyno; Chris Benoit overturned Eddie Guerrero; Mike Awesome defeated Masato Tanaka; The Dudley Boys rolled over Tommy Dreamer and The Sandman.

11th June 2006: “ECW One Night Stand 2006”: New York, NY (Hammerstein Ballroom) – 2,460 sellout
($350,000 live gate)
Taz toppled Jerry Lawler; Kurt Angle bested Randy Orton; Little Guido and Tony Mamaluke defeated Yoshihiro Tairi and Super Crazy; World Heavyweight Champion Rey Mysterio battled Sabu to a no-contest; Edge, Mick Foley and Lita overcame Terry Funk, Tommy Dreamer and Beulah McGillicutty in a streetfight; Balls Mahoney bested Masato Tanaka; Rob Van Dam rolled over WWE World Champion John Cena to win the title.

3rd December 2006: “ECW December to Dismember 2006”: August, GA – 4,800 (3,600 paid)
Dark match: Stevie Richards beat Rene Dupree.
PPV Broadcast: Matt and Jeff Hardy overcame Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury; Balls Mahoney downed Matt Striker; Sylvester Terkay and Elijah Burke overcame Little Guido and Tony Mamaluke; Daivari dropped Tommy Dreamer; Kevin Thorn and Ariel bested Mike Knox and Kelly Kelly; Bobby Lashley rolled over ECW World Champion The Big Show, Test, Rob Van Dam, Hardcore Holly and CM Punk in an “extreme” Elimination Chamber match to win the title.
