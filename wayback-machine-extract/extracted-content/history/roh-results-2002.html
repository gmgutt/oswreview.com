ROH Results 2002

Feb 2002

23rd February 2002: “ROH The Era of Honor Begins”: Philadelphia, PA
Mafia and Monsta Mack downed Buff E and Mace; The Amazing Red overcame Jay Briscoe; Xavier overturned Scoot Andrews; Danny Drake and Mike Tobin dropped Homicide and Boogaloo by DQ; Quiet Storm defeated Amazing Red, Jose Maximo, Joel Maximo, Brian XL and Chris Divine in a six-man elimination match; Prince Nana pinned Eric Tuttle; Spanky and Ikaika Loa bested Oz and Michael Shane; Super Crazy toppled Eddie Guerrero to capture the IWA IC title; Low Ki rolled over Christopher Daniels and Bryan Danielson in a three-way.

Mar 2002

30th March 2002: “ROH The Round Robin Challenge”: Philadelphia, PA
Christopher Daniels bested Bryan Danielson (Round Robin Challenge); Mafia and Monsta Mack dropped Prince Nana and Eric Tuttle; Christian York and Joey Matthews downed CW Anderson and Elax; Xavier overturned James Maitato; Homicide and Boogaloo defeated Danny Drake and Mike Tobin; Low Ki toppled Christopher Daniels (Round Robin Challenge); Paul London beat Chris Marvel by stoppage; Spanky overcame Jay Briscoe; Jose and Joel Maximo defeated Amazing Red and Brian XL, and Chris Divine and Quiet Storm in a three-way elimination match; Bryan Danielson rolled over Low Ki. Ken Shamrock was the guest referee (Round Robin Challenge).

Apr 2002

27th April 2002: “ROH Night of Appreciation”: Philadelphia, PA
Mafia and Monsta Mack downed Simply Luscious, Prince Nana, Buff E and Mace in a handicap match; Jay Briscoe overcame Tony Mamaluke; Chris Divine and Quiet Storm defeated Christopher York and Joey Matthews; James Maritato dropped Scoot Andrews and Xavier; Low Ki overturned AJ Styles; HC Loc and Tony DeVito bested Marcos and Dunn; Donovan Morgan overcame Christopher Daniels; Spanky toppled Paul London, Bryan Danielson, John Hope and Michael Shane in a five man gauntlet match; WWF Intercontinental Champion Eddie Guerrero and Amazing Red rolled over Joel and Jose Maximo.

May 2002

No shows held

June 2002

22nd June 2002: “ROH Road to the Title”: Philadelphia, PA
Bryan Danielson downed Bio-Hazard (ROH Title Tournament – First Round); Spanky overcame Paul London (ROH Title Tournament – First Round); Douglas Williams overturned Jay Briscoe (ROH Title Tournament – First Round); Jody Fleisch pinned Jonny Storm (ROH Title Tournament – First Round); Low Ki dropped Prince Nana by stoppage (ROH Title Tournament – First Round); The Amazing Red bested Xavier (ROH Title Tournament – First Round); Christopher Daniels toppled Scoot Andrews (ROH Title Tournament – First Round); AJ Styles beat Jerry Lynn (ROH Title Tournament – First Round); Spanky defeated Jody Fleisch (ROH Title Tournament – Second Round); Douglas Williams rolled over Bryan Danielson (ROH Title Tournament – Second Round); Low Ki toppled The Amazing Red (ROH Title Tournament – Second Round); Christopher Daniels bested AJ Styles(ROH Title Tournament – Second Round).

July 2002

27th July 2002: “ROH Crowning a Champion”: Philadelphia, PA
Tony Mamaluke downed Jeremy Lopez; Christian York and Joey Matthews downed Jacob’s Ladder and Prince Nana; Michael Shane and Bio-Hazard dropped Paul London and Don Juan; Mafia and Monsta Mack defeated Chris Devine and Quiet Storm; James Maritato overcame Jay Briscoe; Homicide and Boogaloo toppled HC Loc and Tony DeVito in a Bunkhouse Brawl match; NWA-TNA X Division Champion AJ Styles beat Adam Jacobs and David Young in a three-way elimination match; Scoot Andrews overturned Xavier; Low Ki rolled over Christopher Daniels, Douglas Williams, and Spanky in a 60-minute Ironman match to win the ROH Title (ROH Title Tournament – Final).

Aug 2002

24th August 2002: “ROH Honor Invades Boston”: Wakefield, MA
The Amazing Red overcame Quiet Storm; Joel Maximo, Jose Maximo and Chris Divine downed Izzy, Dixie and Brian XL in a scramble match; ROH Champion Low Ki downed Chris Divine; Mace and Buff E dropped Dunn and Marcos; Scoot Andrews pinned Mike Tobin; Xavier overturned Scoot Andrews; Michael Shane toppled Paul London; Bryan Danielson battled Donovan Morgan to a draw; Mafia and Monster Mack defeated HC Loc and Tony DeVito in a Boston Massacre match; Bio-Hazard beat Don Juan; Alex Arion bested Maverick Wild; Homicide and Boogaloo downed Tony Mamaluke and James Maritato; Mark Briscoe toppled Jay Briscoe; ROH Champion Low Ki rolled over AJ Styles.

Sep 2002

21st September 2002: “ROH Unscripted”: Philadelphia, PA
Christopher Daniels and Donovan Morgan defeated Jose and Joel Maximo (ROH Tag Title Tournament – Quarter-Finals); Dick Togo and Ikuto Hidaka downed Tony Mamaluke and James Maritato (ROH Tag Title Tournament – Quarter-Finals); Tony Mamaluke overcame James Maritato; Bryan Danielson and Michael Modest dropped Quiet Storm and Chris Divine (ROH Tag Title Tournament – Quarter-Finals); Christopher Daniels and Donovan Morgan toppled Dick Togo and Ikuto Hidaka (ROH Tag Title Tournament – Semi-Finals); Alex Arion overturned Dunn; Xavier pinned ROH Champion Low Ki to win the title; Takao Omori beat Sonny Siaki; Jay Briscoe toppled The Amazing Red; Paul London bested Michael Shane in a streetfight; Christopher Daniels and Donovan Morgan rolled over Bryan Danielson and Michael Modest to capture the ROH Tag Title (ROH Tag Title Tournament – Finals).

Oct 2002

5th October 2002:“ROH Glory by Honor”: Philadelphia, PA
Homicide downed Jose Maximo, Joel Maximo, Dixie, Izzy, Quiet Storm and Chris Devine in a scramble match; Johnny Kashmere and Trent Acid defeated Homicide and Steve Corino; Buff E, Mace and Allison Danger dropped Christian York, Joey Matthews and Alexis Laree; Tony Mamaluke beat James Maritato; The Amazing Red overcame Ikuto Hidaka; Fast Eddie overturned Don Juan; Steve Corino toppled Rudy Boy Gonzalez in a Texas Death match; Low Ki bested Samoa Joe; Jay Briscoe rolled over ROH Champion Xavier in a non-title match.

Nov 2002

9th November 2002: “ROH All Star Extravaganza”: Philadelphia, PA
Jose and Joel Maximo defeated Dixie and Joey Matthews, Quiet Storm and Chris Devine, and Mafia and Monster Mack in a scramble match; Bryan Danielson dropped CM Punk, Michael Shane, Amazing Red and Paul London in a gauntlet match; Samoa Joe and ROH Tag Champions Christopher Daniels and Donovan Morgan downed Low Ki, Douglas Williams and Homicide; Alexis Laree overcame Allison Danger; HC Loc and Tony DeVito bested Dunn and Marcos; ROH Champion Xavier pinned Jay Briscoe; AJ Styles overturned Bryan Danielson; Shinjiro Otani and Masato Tanaka rolled over Steve Corino and Low Ki.

16th November 2002: ROH Scramble Madness”: Wakefield, MA
Mark Briscoe and Christopher Daniels toppled Jay Briscoe and The Amazing Red; Alexis Laree bested Mace; ROH Champion Xavier pinned Jeremy Lopez; Mafia and Monster Mack beat Tony Mamaluke and Matt Thompson; The Amazing Red, Jose and Joel Maximo, Chris Devine and Quiet Storm overturned Joey Matthews, Izzy, Deranged, Slim J and Angel Dust in a scramble match; Samoa Joe defeated Dunn and Marcos in a handicap match; Paul London and Rudy Boy Gonzalez downed Bio-Hazard and Michael Shane; HC Loc, Tony DeVito and Masada dropped Alex Arion, Don Juan and Fast Eddie; Homicide overcame Samoa Joe; AJ Styles bested Christopher Daniels; Bryan Danielson rolled over Doug Williams in an 30 minute Ironman match (1-0).

Dec 2002

7th December 2002: “ROH Night of the Butcher”: Philadelphia, PA
Michael Shane overcame Jeremy Lopez; Colt Cabana pinned CM Punk; Paul London beat EZ Money (ROH Title #1 Contender Tournament – Semi-Final); Bryan Danielson bested Chad Collyer (ROH Title #1 Contender Tournament – Semi-Final); Jose and Joel Maximo downed Mafia and Monster Mack, Jay Briscoe and Amazing Red, and Joey Matthews and Deranged in a scramble match; Chris Devine and Quiet Storm dropped Jeff Starr and Shockwave; Paul London toppled Bryan Danielson (ROH Title #1 Contender Tournament – Final); ROH Champion Xavier bested AJ Styles; Homicide and Abdullah the Butcher rolled over HC Loc and Tony DeVito in a Bunkhouse Brawl.

28th December  2002: “ROH Final Battle 2002”: Philadelphia, PA
Dark match: Homicide overcame Jay Lethal.
CM Punk overturned Colt Cabana; Simply Luscious pinned Alexis Laree; Deranged and Angel Dust defeated Mafia and Monster Mack, and Johnny Kashmere and Trent Acid in a scramble match; ROH Tag Champions Christopher Daniels and Donovan Morgan beat Joel and Jose Maximo in a best-of-three-falls match; Jody Fleisch downed The Amazing Red; ROH Champion Xavier toppled Paul London; Bryan Danielson, Samoa Joe, Low Ki and Steve Corino battled to a four-way 45-minute draw.
