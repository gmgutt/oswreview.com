STATE OF YOUR UNION ADDRESS!

Hey yo, here’s what’s going on with OSW!

• Our new episode (December to Dismember) dropped on Thursday! http://OSWreview.com/episodes

Here’s our (official) schedule for the rest of 2014:
– The Happening
– Simon Deakin’s Tattoo Review
– Monty Motörsäg’s Tattoo Review

• We haven’t decided on a new storyline but sure we’ll get these done first. A bit later we’ll ask what you guys want to see and we’ll pick from that!

• If you’re wondering why we’re reviewing The Happening, as mentioned in January’s Q&A, we’re dying to review it. Two of us say it’s shit, one thinks it’s amazing. Guess which one! We’ve been arguing about it for years. We put it off for the ECW series and now’s a good a time as ever. Should come out around mid-November, as always I’ll keep you updated.

• OOC HAS GONE TO AMERICA, so you’re stuck with me and V1 for a little while. Time-permitting we’ll do a Brucie Bonus wrestling review

• NEW VIDEOS! Myself & V1 have been doing written videogame/movie reviews, and a lot of people have asked for audio or video versions. When V1’s PS4 broke over the Summer, he completed Metal Gear 1-4. So, I recorded a podcast with him reviewing the games – I’ll splice them to video and see what ye think! It’ll be 4 separate reviews, with much quicker turnaround so it won’t interfere with OSW. If you’re wondering why in general, we love videogames, it’s just something we’d like to try out, in an effort to bring you more content more frequently.

• If you’re watching an episode of OSW, live tweet me! It’s awesome reading your thoughts as you’re watching. @OSWreview

• On Monday I asked you guys what you’d like to see from OSW and the most popular comment was “don’t change a thing” (oh you!) and “review One Night In Chyna” (Oh you &*$^!) Lol… I really appreciate how cool you guys & gals are, I know the internet is a wretched hive of scum and villany, but we’ve managed to find the absolute best of it. Please never change!

• It’s super-awesome seeing pictures of people in our tee! We mark out every time someone sends us a photo. Please don’t feel bad if you don’t get one – this isn’t our job or anything, it’s just something cool we wanted to do. If you could tell one of your friends about us then that’d be sweet We won’t be printing any more ECW tees, so if you do want to get one, please do before your size is out! tshirt.OSWreview.com

Love,

Jay

the union, U.P.Y.O.R.S.
