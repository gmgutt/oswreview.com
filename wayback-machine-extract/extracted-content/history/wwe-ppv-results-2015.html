25th January 2015: “WWE Royal Rumble 2015”: Philadelphia, PA – 14,000 sellout
Shown on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Tyson Kidd and Cesaro toppled Kofi Kingston and Big E.
PPV Broadcast: The Ascension bested Road Dogg and Billy Gunn; WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso downed The Miz and Damien Mizdow; The Bella Twins dropped Natalya and Paige; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Brock Lesnar won a three-way over John Cena and Seth Rollins; Roman Reigns won the 30-man Royal Rumble, last eliminating Rusev.


22nd February 2015: “WWE Fast Lane 2015”: Memphis, TN – 10,300
Shown on PPV/WWE Network
Seth Rollins, Kane and The Big Show beat Ryback, Dolph Ziggler and Erick Rowan; Goldust pinned Stardust; Cesaro and Tyson Kidd downed WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso to win the titles; WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella defeated Paige; WWE Intercontinental Champion Bad News Barrett overcame Dean Ambrose by DQ; WWE US Champion Rusev bested John Cena by stoppage; Roman Reigns rolled over Daniel Bryan.

29th March 2015: “WWE Wrestlemania 31”: Santa Clara, CA – 67,000 (57,800 paid)
($12,600,000 live gate – includes service charges)
($3,300,000 merchandise sales – includes Axxess events)
Shown on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: WWE World Tag Champions Cesaro and Tyson Kidd won a four-way over Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso, Los Matadores, and Big E and Kofi Kingston; The Big Show won the “Andre The Giant” memorial battle royal.
PPV Broadcast: Daniel Bryan won a 7-man ladder match over WWE Intercontinental Champion Bad News Barrett, Dolph Ziggler, Dean Ambrose, Luke Harper, R-Truth, and Stardust; Randy Orton downed Seth Rollins; Triple H toppled Sting in a no-DQ match; AJ Lee and Paige bested The Bella Twins; John Cena rolled WWE US Champion Rusev to win the title; The Undertaker pinned Bray Wyatt; Seth Rollins won the WWE World Heavyweight Title over Roman Reigns and champion Brock Lesnar in what turned into a three-way match when Rollins cashed in his MITB briefcase.

26th April 2015: “WWE Extreme Rules 2015”: Chicago, IL – 11,000 sellout
Shown on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Neville pinned Bad News Barrett.
PPV Broadcast: Dolph Ziggler toppled Sheamus in a “Kiss Me Arse” match; Kofi Kingston and Big E bested WWE World Tag Champions Tyson Kidd and Cesaro to win the titles; Dean Ambrose pinned Luke Harper in a streetfight; WWE US Champion John Cena beat Rusev in a chain match; WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella downed Naomi; Roman Reigns dropped Big Show in a “last man standing” match; WWE Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins rolled over Randy Orton in a cage match.


17th May 2015: “WWE Payback 2015”: Baltimore, MD – 10,000 sellout
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Kick-off show: R-Truth beat Stardust; The Ascension overcame Curtis Axel & Damien Sandow.
PPV Broadcast: Sheamus pinned Dolph Ziggler; WWE World Tag Champions Big E & Kofi Kingston defeated Tyson Kidd & Cesaro in a “best-of-three-falls” match; Bray Wyatt downed Ryback; WWE US Champion John Cena dropped Rusev in a “I Quit” match; Naomi & Tamina Snuka bested The Bella Twins; Neville toppled King Barrett by count-out; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins won a four-way over Dean Ambrose, Roman Reigns & Randy Orton.


31st May 2015: “WWE Elimination Chamber 2015”: Corpus Christi, TX – 7,000 sellout
Shown live on WWE Network / Only shown on PPV in international markets
Kick-off: Stardust beat Zack Ryder.
WWE Network Special: WWE World Tag Champions Big E, Kofi Kingston & Xavier Woods won an “Elimination Chamber” match over Titus O’Neill & Darren Young, The Ascension, Kalisto & Sin Cara, Los Matadores, and Cesaro & Tyson Kidd; WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella won a three-way over Naomi and Paige; WWE NXT Champion Kevin Owens bested WWE US Champion John Cena in a non-title match; Neville overcame Bo Dallas; Ryback won the vacant WWE Intercontinental Title in an “Elimination Chamber” match over Dolph Ziggler, King Barrett, Mark Henry, R-Truth and Sheamus; Dean Ambrose rolled over WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins by DQ.


14th June 2015: “WWE Money in the Bank 2015”: Columbus, OH – 13,000 sellout (12,000 paid)
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: R-Truth beat King Barrett.
PPV Broadcast: Sheamus won the “Money in the Bank” ladder match over Dolph Ziggler, Kane, Kofi Kingston, Neville, Randy Orton and Roman Reigns; WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella downed Paige; The Big Show dropped WWE Intercontinental Champion Ryback by DQ; WWE US Champion John Cena bested WWE NXT Champion Kevin Owens; Darren Young & Titus O’Neill overturned WWE World Tag Champions Big E & Xavier Woods to win the titles; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins rolled over Dean Ambrose in a ladder match.


19th July 2015: “WWE Battleground 2015”: St Louis, MO – 11,000
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: King Barrett beat R-Truth.
PPV Broadcast: Randy Orton pinned Sheamus; WWE World Tag Champions Titus O’Neill and Darren Young downed Big E and Kofi Kingston; Bray Wyatt toppled Roman Reigns; Charlotte won a three-way over Brie Bella and Sasha Banks; WWE US Champion John Cena bested Kevin Owens; Brock Lesnar rolled over WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins by DQ.


23rd August 2015: “WWE Summerslam 2015”: Brooklyn, NY – 13,589 sellout
($1,300,000 live gate)
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Sheamus rolled over Randy Orton; Big E & Kofi Kingston won a four-way over WWE World Tag Champions Darren Young & Titus O’Neil, Los Matadores and The Lucha Dragons to win the titles; Dolph Ziggler battled Rusev to a double count-out; Neville & Stephen Amell beat King Barrett & Stardust; WWE Intercontinental Champion Ryback beat The Big Show and The Miz in a three-way; Dean Ambrose & Roman Reigns rolled over Bray Wyatt & Luke Harper; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins beat WWE US Champion John Cena to win the title; Becky Lynch, Charlotte & Paige won a three-way elimination match over Alicia Fox, Brie Bella & Nikki Bella, and Naomi, Sasha Banks & Tamina; Kevin Owens rolled over Cesaro; The Undertaker overturned Brock Lesnar by stoppage.


20th September 2015: “WWE Night of Champions 2015”: Houston, TX – 11,000 sellout
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Dark match: Stardust and The Ascension beat Neville, Kalisto and Sin Cara
PPV Broadcast: Kevin Owens overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion Ryback to win the title; Dolph Ziggler pinned Rusev; The Dudley Boys downed WWE World Tag Champions Big E and Kofi Kingston by DQ; Charlotte dropped WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella to win the title; Bray Wyatt, Luke Harper and Braun Strowman bested Roman Reigns, Dean Ambrose and Chris Jericho; John Cena toppled WWE US Champion Seth Rollins to win the title; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins rolled over Sting.


25th October 2015: “WWE Hell in a Cell 2015”: Los Angeles, CA – 14,184 sellout
($1,324,625 live gate)
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Dolph Ziggler, Cesaro and Neville beat Rusev, King Barrett and Sheamus.
PPV Broadcast: Alberto Del Rio defeated WWE US Champion John Cena to win the title; Roman Reigns pinned Bray Wyatt in a “Hell in a Cell” match; WWE World Tag Champions Kofi Kingston and Big E bested The Dudley Boys; WWE Divas Champion Charlotte beat Nikki Bella; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins toppled Kane; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens overcame Ryback; Brock Lesnar rolled over The Undertaker in a “Hell in a Cell” match.


22nd November 2015: “WWE Survivor Series 2015”: Atlanta, GA – 11,836 sellout
($858,831 live gate)
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Kickofff: Goldust, Neville, The Dudley Boys and Titus O’Neil beat Bo Dallas, Konnor, Stardust, Viktor and The Miz in a “Survivor Series” elimination match.
PPV Broadcast: Roman Reigns defeated Alberto Del Rio (WWE World Heavyweight Title Tournament – Semi-Final); Dean Ambrose downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens in a non-title match (WWE World Heavyweight Title Tournament – Semi-Final); Ryback, Kalisto, Sin Cara, Jey Uso and Jimmy Uso dropped King Barrett, Sheamus, Big E, Kofi Kingston and Xavier Woods in a “Survivor Series” elimination match; WWE Divas Champion Charlotte bested Paige; Tyler Breeze toppled Dolph Ziggler; The Undertaker and Kane defeated Bray Wyatt and Luke Harper; Roman Reigns rolled over Dean Ambrose to win the vacant WWE World Heavyweight Title (WWE World Heavyweight Title Tournament – Final); Sheamus bested WWE World Heavyweight Champion Roman Reigns to win the title.


13th December 2015: “WWE TLC 2015”: Boston, MA – 11,900 sellout
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Sasha Banks beat Becky Lynch.
PPV Broadcast: WWE World Tag Champions Big E & Kofi Kingston won a three-way ladder match over Sin Cara & Kalisto, and Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso; Rusev pinned Ryback; WWE US Champion Alberto Del Rio bested Jack Swagger in a chairs match; Bray Wyatt, Braun Strowman, Erick Rowan & Luke Harper beat Tommy Dreamer, The Dudleys & Rhyno in an elimination tables match; Dean Ambrose pinned WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens to win the title; WWE Divas Champion Charlotte toppled Paige; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus bested Roman Reigns in a TLC match.
