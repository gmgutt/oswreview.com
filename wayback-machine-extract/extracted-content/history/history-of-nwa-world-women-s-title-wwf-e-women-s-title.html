NWA WORLD WOMEN’S TITLE (Original Version)
1950-1953 Mildred Burke
Was officially recognised by the NWA as their women’s champion between 1950 and 1953 when she was reigning World Women’s Champion.
08/20/1954 June Byers 760 Atlanta, GA
Recognised as NWA World Women’s Champion from this date, when as World Women’s Champion Byers defeated WWWA World Champion Mildred Burke. 
1956 VACANT
The title is declared vacant after June Byers announces her plans to retire.
