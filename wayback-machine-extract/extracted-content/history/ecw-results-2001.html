ECW Results 2001

7th January 2001: “ECW Guilty as Charged 2001”: New York, NY – 2,500 sellout
Dark match: Bilvis Wesley pinned Mike Bell.
PPV Broadcast: Jerry Lynn and Cyrus defeated Joey Matthews and Christian York; ECW World Tag Champions Danny Doring and Roadkill beat EZ Money and Julio Dinero; Nova pinned Chris Hamrick; Tommy Dreamer overcame CW Anderson in an I Quit Match; Mikey Whipwreck and Yoshihiro Tajiri downed Little Guido and Tony Mamaluke, and Super Crazy and Kid Kash in a Three Way elimination match; Simon Diamond and Johnny Swinger battled Balls Mahoney and Chilly Willy to a no-contest; The Sandman rolled over ECW World Champion Steve Corino and Justin Credible in a Three Way, tables, ladders and canes match to win the title; ECW TV Champion Rhino downed ECW World Champion The Sandman to win the title; Rob Van Dam bested Jerry Lynn.

12th January 2001: Poplar Bluff, MO – 1,200
Michael Shane defeated Oz; Prodigy beat Phoenix; Christian York and Joey Matthews downed Little Guido and Tony Mamaluke; Jack Victory pinned CW Anderson in a streetfight; Yoshihiro Tajiri overturned Super Crazy; ECW World Tag Champions Danny Doring and Nova (a permitted substitute for Roadkill) defeated Julio Dinero and EZ Money; Tommy Dreamer overcame Justin Credible; ECW World Champion Rhino downed The Sandman

13th January 2001: Pine Bluff, AR – 1,300
The final ECW show under Paul Heyman ownership
Nova downed Tom Marquez; Christian York and Joey Matthews defeated Julio Dinero and EZ Money; Super Crazy pinned Yoshihiro Tajiri; ECW World Tag Champions Tommy Dreamer (a permitted substitute for Roadkill) and Danny Doring toppled Little Guido and Tony Mamaluke; Jack Victory overcame CW Anderson in a streetfight; Michael Shane beat Oz; ECW World and TV Champion Rhino downed Spike Dudley; The Sandman pinned Justin Credible.
