WWE New Year’s Revolution Results

WWE NEW YEAR’S REVOLUTION RESULTS

9th January 2005: “WWE New Year’s Revolution 2005”: San Juan, PR – 15,764 sellout
($825,000 live gate)
Dark match: The Hurricane and Rosey beat Rob Conway and Sylvan Grenier.
PPV Broadcast: WWE World Tag Champions Eugene and William Regal defeated Christian and Tyson Tomko; Trish Stratus downed WWE Women’s Champion Lita to win the title; WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin dropped Maven; Muhammad Hassan overcame Jerry Lawler; Kane bested Gene Snitsky; Triple H won an Elimination Chamber match over Randy Orton, Chris Jericho, Chris Benoit, Batista, and Edge to win the vacant World Heavyweight Title. Shawn Michaels was guest referee.

8th January 2006: “WWE New Year’s Revolution 2006”: Albany, NY – 11,000
($577,000 live gate)
Heat: (01/08): Chavo Guerrero Jr beat Gene Snitsky.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion Ric Flair defeated Edge by DQ; WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus downed Mickie James; Jerry Lawler dropped Gregory Helms; Triple H pinned The Big Show; Shelton Benjamin overcame Viscera; Ashley won a “bra-and-panties- gauntlet” match; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Shawn Michaels, Kurt Angle, Carlito, Chris Masters and Kane in an Elimination Chamber match; Edge pinned WWE World Champion John Cena to win the title.

7th January 2007: “WWE New Year’s Revolution 2007”: Kansas City, MO – 10,000
($560,000 live gate)
Dark match: Vladimir Kozlov beat Eugene.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Johnny Nitro in a cage match; Cryme Tyme overcame Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas, Robbie and Rory McAllister, and Super Crazy and Jim Duggan in a “tag team turmoil” match; Kenny Dykstra overturned Ric Flair; WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James dropped Victoria; Triple H and Shawn Michaels battled WWE World Tag Champions Edge and Randy Orton to a no-contest; Chris Masters toppled Carlito; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Umaga.
