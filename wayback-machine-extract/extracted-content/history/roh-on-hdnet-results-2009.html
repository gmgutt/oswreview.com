Mar 2009

Air Date: 28th March 2009
Kevin Steen overcame Eddie Edwards; Sara Del Ray beat Daizee Haze; Claudio Castagnoli overturned Alex Payne; ROH Champion Nigel McGuinness toppled Jay Briscoe.

Air Date: 18th April 2009

Jon Davis and Kory Chavis defeated Grizzly Redwood and Andy Ridge; ROH Champion Nigel McGuinness bested Alex Payne; Kevin Steen and Jay Briscoe toppled Chris Hero and Eddie Edwards; Tyler Black battled Bryan Danielson to a draw.

Air Date: 30th May 2009

Air Date: 20th June 2009

Air Date: 27th June 2009
