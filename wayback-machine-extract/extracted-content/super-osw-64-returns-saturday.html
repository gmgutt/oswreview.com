Super OSW 64 Returns this Saturday!

YO! Super OSW 64 returns this Saturday! The show I’d been covering for isn’t returning to the same timeslot so they’ve asked me to fill another programming hole, giggity. Another 2 hours of fun-filled frolics with videogame music! It’ll be airing LIVE on PiledriverWrestling.net at 1pm BST! I’m taking requests (no limitations!) just leave a comment below with your name & hometown and I’ll give you a shoutout on the show! Please include a youtube link to the song as it’ll make it SO much easier for me and I’ll be more likely to choose it!
– Jay
