Raw Results 1998

WWF RAW RESULTS (1998)

Jan 1998

Air Date: 5th January 1998
Ken Shamrock dropped Faarooq; Jeff Jarrett overcame Barry Windham; Skull and Eight-Ball defeated Sniper and Recon; Owen Hart bested Savio Vega; Marc Mero downed Tom Brandi by DQ; Flash Funk overturned TAFKA Goldust by DQ; WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg rolled over The Headbangers.

Air Date: 12th January 1998
WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg dropped The Godwinns, Recon and Sniper, and The Headbangers in a Fatal Four Way; Kurrgan downed Jimmy Cicero and Lance Diamond in a handicap elimination match; Vader dropped Marc Mero by DQ; Ken Shamrock and Mark Henry battled WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock and D-Lo Brown to a no contest; Skull and Eight-Ball defeated NWA Tag Team Champions The Rock and Roll Express by DQ; Mankind battled TAFKA Goldust to a no-contest; Savio Vega and Jesus Castillo bested WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku and Scott Taylor.

Air Date: 19th January 1998
Chainz, Skull, and Eight-Ball battled Faarooq, Kama, and D-Lo Brown to a double-DQ; Marc Mero beat Tom Brandi; The Quebecers toppled Cactus Jack and Terry Funk by DQ; NWA North American Champion Jeff Jarrett pinned Bradshaw; Owen Hart, WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku and The Headbangers overcame Los Boricuas; WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock downed Ahmed Johnson; WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg toppled The Godwinns.

Air Date: 26th January 1998
Ken Shamrock toppled Mark Henry by DQ; Jeff Jarrett and Barry Windham overcame The Legion of Doom; Vader pinned TAFKA Goldust; WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn overcame Cactus Jack and Terry Funk by DQ; El Pantera downed Brian Christopher; The Quebecers dropped The Headbangers; Owen Hart overturned TAFKA Goldust to win the WWF European title. Goldust was a permitted substitute for WWF European Champion Triple H.

Feb 1998

Air Date: 2nd February 1998
Cactus Jack battled Terry Funk to a no-contest; WWF European Champion Owen Hart overturned Billy Gunn by DQ; Marc Mero downed Mosh; Chainz beat Faarooq by count-out; Justin Bradshaw and Flash Funk bested Jeff Jarrett and Barry Windham; Steve Austin rolled over Road Dogg by DQ.

Air Date: 9th February 1998
Jeff Jarrett and Barry Windham defeated The Legion of Doom by DQ; The Godwinns downed The Quebecers by DQ; Pantera and Brian Christopher dropped WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku and Aguila; Faarooq and WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock bested Ken Shamrock and Chainz; Steve Blackman beat Recon; The Headbangers toppled Marc Mero and TAFKA Goldust.

Air Date: 21st February 1998
The Quebecers defeated The Legion of Doom by count-out; Ken Shamrock toppled Sniper; NWA Tag Team Champions Ricky Morton and Robert Gibson bested The Headbangers by DQ; WWF European Champion Owen Hart overturned NWA North American Champion Jeff Jarrett by DQ; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku and Aguila downed Brian Christopher and Piratita Morgan; Steve Blackman rolled over Faarooq; Cactus Jack and Terry Funk beat TAKFA Goldust and Marc Mero.

Air Date: 23rd February 1998
WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg downed The Legion of Doom; Ken Shamrock toppled Jeff Jarrett; Kurrgan, Sniper and Recon defeated Chainz, Eight-Ball and Skull; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku battled Barry Windham to a no-contest; Bradshaw pinned TAFKA Goldust; Steve Blackman bested WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock in a non-title match; WWF European Champion Owen Hart overturned Marc Mero by DQ.

Mar 1998

Air Date: 2nd March 1998
Skull and Eight-Ball overcame WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn by count-out; Marc Mero pinned Tom Brandi; Mark Henry downed WWF European Champion Owen Hart by DQ; NWA Tag Champions The Headbangers and WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku defeated The Rock ‘n’ Roll Express and Barry Windham; Steve Blackman dropped Kama by DQ; Jeff Jarrett bested Flash Funk; Steve Austin battled Kane to a no-contest.

Air Date: 9th March 1998
Ken Shamrock and Steve Blackman defeated WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock and Faarooq by DQ; Barry Windham downed WWF European Champion Owen Hart by count-out; Aguila beat Brian Christopher by DQ; Cactus Jack and Terry Funk overcame The Quebecers; NWA Tag Team Champions The Headbangers dropped The Rock ‘n’ Roll Express by DQ; Marc Mero bested TAFKA Goldust by DQ; Triple H battled Savio Vega to a no-contest.

Air Date: 17th March 1998
Ken Shamrock toppled D-Lo Brown by DQ; Jeff Jarrett downed Tom Brandi; NWA Tag Team Champions The Headbangers defeated Ricky Morton and Robert Gibson; Terry Funk beat Billy Gunn by DQ; Triple H dropped WWF European Champion Owen Hart by stoppage; Luna battled Sable to a no-contest.

Air Date: 23rd March 1998
Cactus Jack and Terry Funk beat The Quebecers by DQ; Jeff Jarrett pinned Steve Blackman; Skull and Eight-Ball battled Bob Holly and Bart Gunn to a double-DQ; Chainz dropped Faarooq; Bradshaw bested Barry Windham; Steve Austin rolled over WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock in a non-title match.

Air Date: 30th March 1998
The Legion of Doom bested Jose Estrada and Jesus Castillo; Kurrgan overcame Chainz; Jeff Jarrett downed Aguila; Ken Shamrock and Steve Blackman dropped Faarooq and WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock; Marc Mero pinned WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku in a non-title match; Bob Holly and Bart Gunn overcame NWA Tag Team Champions The Headbangers to win the titles; Road Dogg and Billy Gunn overcame Cactus Jack and Terry Funk in a steel cage match to win The vacant WWF tag team titles.

Apr 1998

Air Date: 6th April 1998
NWA World Champion Dan Severn bested Flash Funk; Steve Blackman downed Brian Christopher by DQ; Owen Hart overturned WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock by DQ; Luna pinned Matt Knowles; Ken Shamrock toppled Marc Mero by DQ; WWF European Champion Triple H and WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg rolled over Chainz, Skull and Eight-Ball.

Air Date: 13th April 1998
Skull and Eight-Ball battled Savio Vega and Jose Estrada to a no-contest in a chain match; Brian Christopher and Scott Taylor battled Aguila and Pantera to a no-contest; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku downed Jeff Jarrett by DQ; Terry Funk and Scorpio overcame The Quebecers; Ken Shamrock and Steve Blackman battled Bob Holly and Bart Gunn to a double-DQ; Owen Hart pinned Billy Gunn; WWF World Champion Steve Austin battled Vince McMahon to a no-contest.

Air Date: 20th April 1998
Faarooq beat Kama in a streetfight; Dan Severn downed Mosh; Bradshaw dropped TAFKA Goldust by DQ; Terry Funk and Scorpio defeated NWA Tag Team Champions Bob Holly and Bart Gunn in a non-title match; WWF European Champion Triple H and WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg overcame Owen Hart and The Legion of Doom; Dude Love overturned Steve Blackman by Montreal finish.

Air Date: 27th April 1998
Ken Shamrock and Owen Hart battled WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock and Mark Henry to a no-contest; Terry Funk and Scorpio dropped The Headbangers by DQ; Bradshaw toppled Jeff Jarrett by DQ; Skull and Eight-Ball overturned WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg in a non-title match; The Undertaker bested Barry Windham; WWF World Champion Steve Austin battled TAFKA Goldust to a no-contest. Gerald Briscoe was guest referee.

May 1998

Air Date: 4th May 1998
WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock and Owen Hart overturned Steve Blackman and Faarooq; Dan Severn downed Savio Vega; The Legion of Doom, Skull and Eight-Ball battled WWF European Champion Triple H, Chyna and WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg to a no-contest; Marc Mero dropped Jeff Jarrett by count-out; Steve Blackman and Faarooq beat WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock and Owen Hart by count-out; Mick Foley pinned Terry Funk in a “falls-count-anywhere” match. Pat Patterson was guest referee.

Air Date: 11th May 1998
Vader beat Barry Windham; Eight-Ball downed Road Warrior Hawk; Faarooq dropped Jeff Jarrett; WWF European Champion Triple H battled Owen Hart to a no-contest; Kaientai defeated Terry Funk and Scorpio by DQ in a handicap match; WWF World Champion Steve Austin and Vince McMahon battled WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock and D-Lo Brown to a no-contest.

Air Date: 18th May 1998
Val Venis dropped Scorpio; Terry Funk beat Marc Mero; Skull and Eight-Ball overcame The Legion of Doom; Dude Love overturned Dustin Runnels; The Headbangers defeated Dick Togo and Mens Teioh by DQ; WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn overturned WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock and Owen Hart; WWF World Champion Steve Austin battled Pat Patterson and Gerald Brisco to a no-contest in a handicap streetfight.

Air Date: 25th May 1998
The Legion of Doom and Droz overcame Skull, Eight-Ball and Chainz; NWA World Champion Dan Severn downed Owen Hart by DQ; Golga pinned Thrasher; Vader dropped Jeff Jarrett by DQ; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku beat Dick Togo; Faarooq bested Marc Mero; WWF European Champion Triple H battled WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock to a double count-out.

Jun 1998

Air Date: 1st June 1998
The Legion of Doom and Droz battled Chainz, Skull and Eight-Ball to a no-contest in a streetfight; Val Venis defeated Papi Chulo; Marc Mero beat Steve Blackman (KOTR Qualifier);  WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock, Owen Hart and D-Lo Brown dropped WWF European Champion Triple H and WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn in an elimination match; Jeff Jarrett bested Faarooq (KOTR Qualifier); WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku pinned Sho Funaki; Mark Henry overcame Terry Funk (KOTR Qualifier); Kane toppled The Undertaker.

Air Date: 8th June 1998
Ken Shamrock toppled Kama (KOTR Qualifier); Faarooq and Steve Blackman defeated Jeff Jarrett and Marc Mero; Owen Hart overturned Scorpio (KOTR Qualifier); Chainz beat Droz; Vader battled Mark Henry to a no-contest; Dan Severn dropped D-Lo Brown (KOTR Qualifier); Val Venis battled Dustin Runnels to a no-contest; WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg overcame The Legion of Doom, and Skull and Eight-Ball in a Triple Threat.

Air Date: 15th June 1998
WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock downed Vader (KOTR Qualifier); Droz beat Jeff Jarrett; Val Venis defeated Chainz; Dustin Runnels dropped Marc Mero; WWF European Champion Triple H dropped X-Pac by count-out (KOTR Qualifier); Owen Hart and Mark Henry overturned Ken Shamrock and Dan Severn; Kane and Mankind won a 10-team tag battle royal; WWF World Champion Steve Austin and The Undertaker battled Kane and Mankind to a no-contest in a “Hell-in-a-Cell” match.

Air Date: 22nd June 1998
Ken Shamrock toppled Mark Henry (KOTR 1998 – Quarter Final); X-Pac pinned Dustin Runnels; Jeff Jarrett downed Marc Mero (KOTR 1998 – Quarter Final); Kane toppled Road Dogg; Edge bested Jose Estrada by count-out; Dan Severn beat Owen Hart (KOTR 1998 – Quarter Final); WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock downed WWF European Champion Triple H in a non-title match (KOTR 1998 – Quarter-Final); Mankind overturned Billy Gunn.

Air Date: 29th June 1998
Steven Regal beat Droz; Steve Blackman overcame Marco Mero in a “Brawl for All” match; Val Venis pinned Dick Togo; Ken Shamrock toppled Owen Hart and Triple H in a Triple Threat match; Bradshaw downed Mark Canterbury in a “Brawl for All” match; Steve Austin rolled over WWF World Champion Kane to win the title.

Jul 1998

Air Date: 6th July 1998
Savio Vega beat Brakkus in a “Brawl for All” match; Ken Shamrock toppled Jeff Jarrett by DQ; Vader battled Bradshaw to a no-contest; Skull and Eight-Ball overturned The Headbangers; D-Lo Brown bested Terry Funk; Road Warrior Hawk battled Droz to a draw in a “Brawl for All” match; Val Venis overcame Dustin Runnells by DQ; Ken Shamrock toppled Mabel; Kane toppled Mankind.

Air Date: 13th July 1998
The Undertaker bested Vader; Bart Gunn overcame Bob Holly in a “Brawl for All” match; X-Pac and WWF European Champion Triple H dropped Owen Hart and WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock; Steve Blackman pinned Marc Mero; Kane and Mankind overturned WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn to win the titles; Kaientai overcame Brian Christopher, Scott Taylor and WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku; Dan Severn beat The Godfather in a “Brawl for All” match; Road Dogg and Billy Gunn battled WWF World Tag Champions Kane and Mankind to a no-contest. Steve Austin and The Undertaker were the guest referees.

Air Date: 20th July 1998
D-Lo Brown pinned WWF European Champion Triple H to win the title; Steve Williams defeated Pierre in a “Brawl for All” match; Road Warrior Animal battled Skull to a no-contest; Steve Blackman downed Jeff Jarrett; Scorpio dropped Eight-Ball in a “Brawl for All” match; Owen Hart overturned Faarooq; WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock downed X-Pac by DQ; WWF World Tag Champions Kane and Mankind overturned WWF World Champion Steve Austin by DQ in a handicap match.

Air Date: 27th July 1998
Vader defeated WWF European Champion D-Lo Brown by count-out; Bart Gunn overcame Steve Williams in a “Brawl for All” match (Quarter Final); Owen Hart overturned Dan Severn by DQ; Faarooq and Scorpio battled Skull and Eight-Ball to a no-contest; Triple H and X-Pac dropped WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock by count-out in a Triple Threat; Brakus pinned Jesus Castillo; Val Venis dropped Brian Christopher; Mark Henry and Kama overcame The Legion of Doom; WWF World Tag Champions Steve Austin and The Undertaker bested Billy Gunn and Road Dogg.

Aug 1998

Air Date: 3rd August 1998
Golga beat Marc Mero; The Godfather downed Scorpio in a “Brawl for All” match (Quarter Final); Kane and Mankind overturned Billy Gunn and Road Dogg; Road Warrior Hawk pinned Jeff Jarrett; Triple H dropped X-Pac; Val Venis and Taka Michinoku battled Dick Togo and Sho Funaki to a no-contest; Dan Severn toppled WWF European Champion D-Lo Brown by DQ; WWF World Tag Champions Steve Austin and The Undertaker bested WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock and Owen Hart.

Air Date: 10th August 1998
Luna beat Jackie; Droz defeated Savio Vega in a “Brawl for All” match (Quarter Final); Southern Justice battled Road Warrior Animal and Droz to a no-contest; Vader battled The Godfather to a no-contest; Bradshaw downed Marc Mero in a “Brawl for All” match (Quarter Final); Kane and Mankind overturned Billy Gunn and Road Dogg, WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock and WWF European Champion D-Lo Brown and WWF World Tag Champions Steve Austin and The Undertaker to win the titles.

Air Date: 17th August 1998
Dan Severn battled Owen Hart and Ken Shamrock to a no-contest in a Triple Threat match; Bart Gunn overcame The Godfather in a “Brawl for All” match (Semi Final); Gangrel pinned Brian Christopher; Skull and Eight-Ball downed Faarooq and Scorpio; WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock, WWF European Champion D-Lo Brown, The Godfather and Mark Henry battled Triple H, X-Pac, Billy Gunn and Road Dogg to a no-contest in a streetfight; Sable beat Jackie by DQ in an “arm wrestling” match; Bradshaw overturned Droz in a “Brawl for All” match (Semi Final); Kaientai rolled over Val Venis in a gauntlet match.

Air Date: 24th August 1998
Ken Shamrock toppled Dan Severn by DQ; Kurrgan bested Marc Mero by DQ; Road Dogg and Billy Gunn overcame Southern Justice; Kane battled Mankind to a no-contest in a “Hell in a Cell” match; X-Pac toppled Gangrel by DQ; Bart Gunn overcame Bradshaw in a “Brawl for All” match (Final).

Sep 1998

Air Date: 5th September 1998
Ken Shamrock and Steve Blackman battled Skull and Eight-Ball to a no-contest; Val Venis battled Vader to a no-contest; The Rock and Mark Henry dropped WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg by DQ; Southern Justice overturned The Headbangers; X-Pac battled WWF European Champion D-Lo Brown to a no-contest; Edge battled Marc Mero to a no-contest; Kurrgan and The Giant Silva dropped Road Warrior Animal and Droz; Brian Christopher and Scott Taylor defeated Miguel Perez and Jesus Castillo; Jeff Jarrett toppled Scorpio by DQ.

Air Date: 12th September 1998
Edge downed Jeff Jarrett by DQ; Bradshaw beat Droz; Marc Mero pinned Miguel Perez; Skull and Eight-Ball overcame Kurrgan and Golga; Southern Justice dropped Brian Christopher and Scott Taylor; Vader bested Dustin Runnels; D-Lo Brown and Mark Henry defeated The Headbangers by DQ; WWF Intercontinental Champion Triple H, X-Pac and WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg rolled over Kaientai.

Air Date: 14th September 1998
Jeff Jarrett defeated Road Dogg; The Rock downed Kane; WWF Intercontinental Champion Triple H dropped Owen Hart; Mankind battled The Undertaker to a no-contest in a streetfight; Gangrel battled Edge to a no-contest; Mark Henry overcame X-Pac and Chyna in a handicap match; Sable beat Jackie in an evening gown match; WWF World Champion Steve Austin battled Ken Shamrock to a no-contest.

Air Date: 21st September 1998
WWF Tag Team Champion Billy Gunn overcame Jeff Jarrett; The Headbangers battled The Oddities to a no-contest; Skull and Eight-Ball battled Mark Canterbury and Dennis Knight to a no-contest; The Undertaker and Kane toppled WWF World Champion Steve Austin and Billy Gunn; Al Snow pinned Sgt Slaughter in a “Boot Camp” match; Val Venis downed Owen Hart by DQ; X-Pac overcame WWF European Champion D-Lo Brown to win the title; Jackie pinned Sable to win the vacant WWE Women’s title; The Rock, Mankind, and Ken Shamrock battled to a no-contest in a Triple Threat match.

Air Date: 28th September 1998
Southern Justice downed WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg by DQ; Owen Hart pinned Dan Severn; D-Lo Brown dropped Edge, Marc Mero, Jeff Jarrett, Gangrel and Droz in a six-way; Faarooq beat Mark Henry; Kurrgan and Golga dropped The Headbangers; WWF European Champion X-Pac bested Val Venis; The Rock, Ken Shamrock, and Mankind overturned The Undertaker and Kane in a handicap match.

Oct 1998

Air Date: 5th October 1998
D-Lo Brown pinned WWF European Champion X-Pac to win the title; The Headbangers battled The Insane Clown Posse to a no-contest; Marc Mero bested Vader; Edge dropped Owen Hart by forfeit; Val Venis overcame Gangrel by count-out; Jeff Jarrett defeated Al Snow by DQ; Ken Shamrock toppled Kane; Road Dogg beat Mark Henry; The Undertaker bested The Rock.

Air Date: 12th October 1998
WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn battled Road Warrior Animal and Droz to a no-contest; Ken Shamrock defeated Steve Blackman (WWF Intercontinental Title Tournament – Quarter-Finals); Val Venis downed Marc Mero (WWF Intercontinental Title Tournament – Quarter-Finals); Mankind overturned Mark Henry (WWF Intercontinental Title Tournament – Quarter-Finals); X-Pac dropped Jeff Jarrett (WWF Intercontinental Title Tournament – Quarter-Finals); Ken Shamrock toppled Val Venis (WWF Intercontinental Title Tournament – Semi-Finals); X-Pac bested Mankind (WWF Intercontinental Title Tournament – Semi-Finals); Ken Shamrock rolled over X-Pac to win the vacant WWF Intercontinental Title (WWF Intercontinental Title Tournament – Final); Steve Austin and The Rock battled Kane and The Undertaker to a no-contest.

Air Date: 19th October 1998
WWF European Champion X-Pac pinned WWF Intercontinental Champion Ken Shamrock in a non-title match; The Headbangers overcame Road Warrior Animal and Droz; Steve Blackman dropped Jeff Jarrett by DQ; The Rock downed D-Lo Brown; Val Venis toppled Mankind; The Undertaker battled Kane to a no-contest in a casket match.

Air Date: 26th October 1998
WWF European Champion X-Pac defeated Steve Blackman by DQ; The Rock downed Droz; Kane toppled Gangrel by DQ; The Godfather battled Tiger Ali Singh to a double-DQ; Kaientai overcame The Oddities and The Insane Clown Posse by DQ; Marc Mero bested Goldust by DQ; WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg overturned Mankind and Al Snow; Steve Austin rolled over WWF Intercontinental Champion Ken Shamrock in a non-title match.

Nov 1998

Air Date: 2nd November 1998
Edge, Gangrel and Christian battled X-Pac and WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg to a no-contest; Road Warrior Hawk battled Droz to a no-contest; Kurrgan and Golga beat Al Snow and Mankind; Goldust battled Steven Regal to a no-contest; The Rock downed WWF Intercontinental Champion Ken Shamrock by DQ; Val Venis dropped Jeff Jarrett by DQ; The Headbangers battled D-Lo Brown and Mark Henry to a no-contest.

Air Date: 9th November 1998
X-Pac battled The Undertaker to a no-contest; Val Venis downed Steve Blackman by DQ; Mosh bested Road Dogg and D-Lo Brown in a Triple Threat. Shane McMahon was guest referee; Goldust defeated Jeff Jarrett by DQ; WWF Hardcore Champion Mankind overturned WWF Intercontinental Champion Ken Shamrock in a hardcore match; Tiger Ali Singh beat Al Snow; Kane toppled Edge by DQ; The Rock downed Mark Henry. Shane McMahon was the guest referee.

Air Date: 16th November 1998
WWF European Champion X-Pac and WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg overturned Golga, Kurrgan and Giant Silva; Val Venis pinned Mark Henry; Steve Blackman and Goldust downed Jeff Jarrett and The Blue Blazer; The Godfather battled Steven Regal to a no -contest; WWF Intercontinental Champion Ken Shamrock battled The Big Bossman to a double-DQ; Edge and Gangrel dropped Animal and Droz by count-out; Steve Austin rolled over WWF World Champion The Rock by DQ.

Air Date: 23rd November 1998
The Headbangers overturned Golga and Kurrgan; Steve Blackman defeated The Blue Blazer; Edge and Gangrel downed Mark Henry and D-Lo Brown; Goldust battled Marc Mero to a no-contest; WWF Hardcore Champion Mankind overturned The Big Bossman and WWF Intercontinental Champion Ken Shamrock in a non-title Triple Threat; Duane Gill overcame WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Christian to win the title; Tiger Ali Singh battled The Godfather to a no-contest; Scorpio and Bob Holly dropped WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn in a non-title match; WWF World Champion The Rock downed WWF European Champion X-Pac.

Air Date: 30th November 1998
WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg overturned Gangrel and Edge by DQ; Goldust bested Jeff Jarrett by DQ; The Big Bossman overcame WWF Hardcore Champion Mankind in a ladder match to win the title; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Duane Gill pinned Marc Mero; WWF Intercontinental Champion Ken Shamrock toppled WWF European Champion X-Pac by DQ; Val Venis defeated Tiger Ali Singh by DQ; WWF World Champion The Rock downed Al Snow.

Dec 1998

Air Date: 7th December 1998
D-Lo Brown overturned Jeff Jarrett; The Headbangers dropped Edge and Gangrel by DQ; Goldust pinned Owen Hart; The Acolytes battled Val Venis and The Godfather to a no-contest; Steve Blackman toppled Tiger Ali Singh; Mark Henry bested Droz; WWF Intercontinental Champion Ken Shamrock and The Big Bossman downed Triple H and X-Pac by DQ; Steve Austin and Mankind battled WWF World Champion The Rock and The Undertaker by DQ.

Air Date: 14th December 1998
Val Venis and The Godfather beat Edge and Gangrel; Goldust overturned The Blue Blazer (Owen Hart) by DQ; Mark Henry and D-Lo Brown defeated Al Snow and Bob Holly; WWF Intercontinental Champion Ken Shamrock and WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Bossman toppled WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg to win the titles; Jeff Jarrett downed Steve Blackman in a “guitar-on-a-pole” match; Kane fought Mankind to a no-contest; WWF World Champion the Rock rolled over Triple H.

Air Date: 21st December 1998
Al Snow beat Gangrel; Billy Gunn pinned WWF Intercontinental Champion Ken Shamrock in a non-title match; Steve Blackman downed The Blue Blazer by DQ; Road Dogg pinned WWF Hardcore Champion the Big Bossman to win the title; Bob Holly and Scorpio overcame The Acolytes by DQ; Mankind toppled Shane McMahon by DQ; The Headbangers beat D-Lo Brown in a handicap match; WWF World Champion The Rock and Test rolled over Triple H and X-Pac by DQ.

Air Date: 28th December 1998
Val Venis downed WWF Hardcore Champion Road Dogg by DQ; Edge dropped Al Snow by DQ; WWF European Champion X-Pac battled The Big Bossman to a no-contest; Steve Blackman and Goldust defeated Owen Hart and Jeff Jarrett; Triple H rolled over Ken Shamrock by DQ; Kane bested Billy Gunn by DQ; WWF Hardcore Champion Road Dogg toppled Mankind.
