205 Live Results 2017

WWE 205 LIVE RESULTS (2017)

Jan 2017

Air Date: 3rd January 2017
Tajiri dropped Sean Maluta; Jack Gallagher rolled over Tony Nese by DQ; Mustafa Ali dropped Noam Dar; Neville downed TJ Perkins.

Air Date: 10th January 2017
Noam Dar rolled over Cedric Alexander; Brian Kendrick defeated Sean Maluta; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rich Swann overcame Tony Nese.

Air Date: 17th January 2017
Drew Gulak defeated Cedric Alexander; Tony Nese downed Mustafa Ali; Jack Gallagher rolled over Ariya Daivari in an “I Forfeit” match.

Air Date: 24th January 2017
TJ Perkins overturned Tony Nese; Brian Kendrick defeated Tripp Bradshaw; Neville downed Cedric Alexander.

Air Date: 31st January 2017
Tony Nese downed Lince Dorado; Akira Tozawa beat Aaron Solow; Cedric Alexander and Jack Gallagher rolled over WWE Cruiserweight Champion Neville and Noam Dar.

Feb 2017

Air Date: 7th February 2017
Mustafa Ali dropped Ariya Daivari; Brian Kendrick defeated Lince Dorado; Jack Gallagher rolled over Cedric Alexander, Mustafa Ali, Noam Dar and TJ Perkins in a five-way elimination match.

Air Date: 14th February 2017
Rich Swann overcame Noam Dar; Gran Metalik defeated Drew Gulak; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Neville downed TJ Perkins.

Air Date: 21st February 2017
Brian Kendrick defeated Akira Tozawa by count-out; Noam Dar rolled over Mustafa Ali; Jack Gallagher rolled over Tony Nese.

Air Date: 28th February 2017
Noam Dar rolled over Lince Dorado; Tony Nese, Drew Gulak and Ariya Daivari dropped TJ Perkins, Mustafa Ali and Cedric Alexander.

Mar 2017

Air Date: 7th March 2017
Rich Swann and Jack Gallagher rolled over Noam Dar and Ariya Daivari; Akira Tozawa beat A Brian Kendrick (Arik Cannon); Austin Aries overturned Tony Nese.

Air Date: 14th March 2017
Mustafa Ali dropped Drew Gulak; Noam Dar and Ariya Daivari dropped Rich Swann and Jack Gallagher; Austin Aries overturned Akira Tozawa, Brian Kendrick, TJ Perkins and Tony Nese in a five-way.

Air Date: 21st March 2017
Tony Nese downed TJ Perkins; Akira Tozawa beat Alex Reynolds; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Neville downed Mustafa Ali.

Air Date: 28th March 2017
Rich Swann overcame Ariya Daivari; Mustafa Ali dropped Brandon Scott; Brian Kendrick defeated Akira Tozawa.

Apr 2017

Air Date: 4th April 2017
Akira Tozawa beat Brian Kendrick; Rich Swann overcame Oney Lorcan; Austin Aries overturned Jack Gallagher, Mustafa Ali and TJ Perkins in a four-way.

Air Date: 11th April 2017
Mustafa Ali dropped Brian Kendrick; Rich Swann overcame Johnny Ocean; TJ Perkins overturned Jack Gallagher.

Air Date: 18th April 2017
Akira Tozawa beat Tony Nese; Ariya Daivari dropped Mustafa Ali; Austin Aries overturned TJ Perkins.

Air Date: 25th April 2017
Rich Swann and Akira Tozawa beat Brian Kendrick and Noam Dar; Tony Nese downed Mustafa Ali by DQ; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Neville downed Jack Gallagher.

May 2017

Air Date: 2nd May 2017
TJP defeated Lince Dorado; Drew Gulak defeated Mustafa Ali; Noam Dar rolled over Rich Swann.
