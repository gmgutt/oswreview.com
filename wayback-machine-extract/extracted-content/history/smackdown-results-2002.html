Smackdown Results 2002

WWF/E SMACKDOWN RESULTS (2002)

Jan 2002

Air Date: 3rd January 2002
WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys overcame Taz and Spike Dudley; Jazz defeated Molly Holly; WWF Hardcore Champion The Undertaker toppled The Big Show; WWF World Champion Chris Jericho and Kurt Angle bested WWF Intercontinental Champion Edge and Rob Van Dam; Test downed Tajiri; The Rock dropped Booker T.

Air Date: 10th January 2002
WWF World Tag Champions Tazz and Spike Dudley defeated Christian and Lance Storm; WWF Intercontinental Champion Edge beat The Big Bossman; The Rock and Rob Van Dam downed WWF World Champion Chris Jericho and Test; Rikishi battled Booker T to a no-contest; Steve Austin battled Kurt Angle to a no-contest.

Air Date: 17th January 2002
William Regal pinned Rob Van Dam; Billy Gunn beat Tajiri; Diamond Dallas Page toppled The Big Bossman; Rikishi defeated Christian and Lance Storm in a handicap “over-the-top-rope-challenge”; Test overcame WWF Intercontinental Champion Edge by DQ; Steve Austin and Triple H rolled over Kurt Angle and Booker T.

Air Date: 24th January 2002
Rob Van Dam rolled over WWF Intercontinental Champion William Regal in a non-title match; Rikishi pinned The Big Bossman. Jacqueline was guest referee; WWF World Tag Champions Tazz and Spike Dudley beat Albert and Scotty 2 Hotty; Steve Austin bested Booker T (WWF Undisputed Title #1 Contender – Semi-Final); The Dudley Boys overturned Kane and The Big Show in a tables match; WWF Women’s Champion Trish Stratus overcame Jazz by DQ; Kurt Angle toppled The Rock (WWF Undisputed Title #1 Contender – Semi-Final.)

Air Date: 31st January 2002
WWF World Champion Chris Jericho bested Tazz. Jacqueline was guest referee; Diamond Dallas Page toppled WWF European Champion Christian to win the title; Rob Van Dam and Edge dropped The Dudley Boys; The Acolytes beat Booker T and Test; The Rock and Triple H rolled over The Undertaker and Kurt Angle by DQ.

Feb 2002

Air Date: 7th February 2002
The Rock pinned Booker T; WWF Intercontinental Champion William Regal and The Dudley Boys overturned Edge, Rikishi, and Rob Van Dam; Maven bested WWF Hardcore Champion The Undertaker to win the title; WWF Undisputed World Champion Chris Jericho and Kurt Angle toppled Triple H in a handicap match.

Air Date: 14th February 2002
Spike Dudley beat Booker T; Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo defeated The Acolytes; WWF Intercontinental Champion William Regal pinned Val Venis; Goldust overcame WWF Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri in a non-title match; Kane toppled WWF Undisputed World Champion Chris Jericho by DQ; Triple H and WWF Hardcore Champion Maven bested Kurt Angle and The Undertaker by DQ.

Air Date: 21st February 2002
Kane toppled Kurt Angle by DQ; Edge dropped Christian; Rob Van Dam, Matt Hardy and Jeff Hardy beat Lance Storm and The Dudley Boys; Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo defeated WWF World Tag Champions Spike Dudley and Tazz to win the titles.

Air Date: 28th February 2002
WWF Women’s Champion Jazz and The Dudley Boys overturned Lita and The Hardy Boys; The Big Show toppled WWF Intercontinental Champion William Regal in a non-title match; WWF Hardcore Champion Maven bested Goldust; Al Snow battled The Undertaker to a no-contest; Goldust overcame WWF Hardcore Champion Maven to win the title; Booker T overturned Scotty 2 Hotty; Triple H and Kane toppled WWF World Champion Chris Jericho and Kurt Angle.

Mar 2002


Air Date: 7th March 2002
WWF Hardcore Champion Goldust overcame Rob Van Dam; Booker T overturned WWF Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri in a non-title match; The Undertaker bested Al Snow and Maven in a handicap match; WWF Undisputed World Champion Chris Jericho bested Kane; Lita beat Stacy Keibler; The Rock defeated Scott Hall by DQ.

Air Date: 14th March 2002
Kurt Angle bested Rob Van Dam; Billy Gunn bested Bradshaw, Bubba Ray Dudley, and Jeff Hardy in a four-way; WWF Hardcore Champion Al Snow battled The Big Show to aa no-contest (Maven pinned WWF Hardcore Champion Al Snow to win the title); Test and Booker T overturned Edge and WWF Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri; The Undertaker bested David Flair by DQ.

Air Date: 21st March 2002
Booker T overturned Edge; Lita and Trish Stratus overcame WWF Women’s Champion Jazz and Ivory; William Regal overcame WWF European Champion Diamond Dallas Page to win the title; Test overcame WWF Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri in a non-title match; The Hurricane battled Molly Holly to a no-contest; WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo beat The Hardy Boys; The Rock defeated Kevin Nash by DQ.

Air Date: 28th March 2002
Diamond Dallas Page battled Booker T to a no-contest; WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo beat The Dudley Boys; Hulk Hogan, The Rock, and Kane toppled Kevin Nash, Scott Hall, and X-Pac; Chris Jericho bested Matt Hardy; WWF Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Test; Raven bested WWF Hardcore Champion Maven to win the title; Kurt Angle and Vince McMahon bested WWF World Champion Triple H and Ric Flair.

Apr 2002

Air Date: 4th April 2002
WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo beat Scotty 2 Hotty and Albert; Christian downed Diamond Dallas Page; Edge dropped Kurt Angle by DQ; Billy Kidman bested WWF Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri to win the title; Hardcore Holly downed Maven; The Rock pinned Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 11th April 2002
Tajiri beat The Hurricane; Albert overcame Scotty 2 Hotty; Chris Jericho bested Edge; Al Snow dropped Chuck Palumbo; WWF World Champion Triple H rolled over Kurt Angle.

Air Date: 18th April 2002
Al Snow, Maven, and Rikishi defeated Albert and WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo; Tajiri and Chavo Guerrero Jr overturned WWF Cruiserweight Champion Billy Kidman and The Hurricane; Faarooq pinned Test; Hardcore Holly downed Diamond Dallas Page; WWF World Champion Triple H and Hulk Hogan battled Chris Jericho and Kurt Angle to a no-contest.

Air Date: 25th April 2002
Al Snow, Maven, and Billy Kidman bested WWF Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri and WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo; Randy Orton rolled over Hardcore Holly; Mark Henry beat Christian; Kurt Angle and Albert overcame Edge and Rikishi; Chris Jericho bested Triple H.

May 2002

Air Date: 2nd May 2002
WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo beat Rikishi and The Hurricane; Randy Orton rolled over Hardcore Holly by DQ; Kurt Angle and Lance Storm defeated Edge and Val Venis; Christian and Test overcame Mark Henry and Faarooq; WWF Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri dropped Billy Kidman; WWF World Champion Hulk Hogan bested Chris Jericho in a no-DQ match.

Air Date: 9th May 2002
The Hurricane dropped WWE Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri by count-out; Test overcame Mark Henry; D-Von Dudley beat Triple H; Rico and WWE Tag Team Champions Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo defeated Al Snow, Maven, and Rikishi; Lance Storm defeated Randy Orton. Hardcore Holly was guest referee; WWE World Champion Hulk Hogan and Edge battled Chris Jericho and Kurt Angle to a no-contest.

Air Date: 16th May 2002
Val Venis and Randy Orton rolled over Lance Storm and Hardcore Holly; D-Von Dudley beat Maven; Rikishi pinned Rico; The Hurricane dropped Billy Kidman and WWE Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri in a three-way to win the title; Kurt Angle and Chris Jericho bested Triple H and Edge.

Air Date: 23rd May 2002
Triple H rolled over Lance Storm; Test overcame Randy Orton; Edge and Maven bested Kurt Angle and Christian; D-Von Dudley beat Rikishi; WWE Cruiserweight Champion The Hurricane dropped Chavo Guerrero Jr; Chris Jericho bested Mark Henry.

Air Date: 30th May 2002
WWE Tag Team Champions Rikishi and Rico defeated Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo; Christian downed Maven; Chris Jericho bested Faarooq; WWE World Champion The Undertaker bested Randy Orton; Tajiri and Chavo Guerrero Jr overturned Billy Kidman and WWE Cruiserweight Champion The Hurricane; Test overcame Triple H; Edge toppled Kurt Angle in a cage match.

Jun 2002

Air Date: 6th June 2002
Triple H and Hulk Hogan co-won a 20-man battle royal ; Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo defeated WWE World Tag Champions Rikishi and Rico in an elimination match to win the titles; Val Venis overturned Christian (KOTR Qualifier); Kurt Angle toppled Hardcore Holly; Chris Jericho bested Edge by forfeit (KOTR Qualifier);  Lance Storm pinned Billy Kidman; Triple H rolled over Hulk Hogan.

Air Date: 13th June 2002
Test overcame WWE Cruiserweight Champion The Hurricane (KOTR Qualifier); Hardcore Holly downed Tajiri (KOTR Qualifier); Billy Kidman and Val Venis overturned Chris Jericho and Lance Storm; Faarooq pinned D-Von Dudley; WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus and Linda Miles overturned Ivory and Jackie Gayda; WWE World Champion The Undertaker and Kurt Angle toppled Triple H by DQ in a handicap match.

Air Date: 20th June 2002
Rikishi pinned Christian; Jamie Noble beat Billy Kidman; Triple H rolled over Billy Gunn; Chris Jericho bested Val Venis (KOTR 2002 – Quarter-Final); Test overcame Hardcore Holly (KOTR 2002 – Quarter-Final); Kurt Angle toppled WWE World Champion The Undertaker in a non-title match.

Air Date: 27th June 2002
Kurt Angle toppled John Cena; WWE World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo defeated Hardcore Holly and Val Venis; Lance Storm pinned Mark Henry; Rikishi defeated Test by DQ; Billy Kidman and The Hurricane dropped WWE Cruiserweight Champion Jamie Noble and Tajiri; D-Von Dudley and Batista overturned Faarooq and Randy Orton; Hulk Hogan bested Chris Jericho by DQ.

Jul 2002

Air Date: 4th July 2002
Lance Storm beat Rikishi; D-Von Dudley and Batista overturned Randy Orton and Val Venis; Hulk Hogan and Edge dropped WWE World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo to win the titles; Chris Jericho bested John Cena; Torrie Wilson bested Stacy Keibler in a “bra and panties” match; WWE World Champion The Undertaker battled Kurt Angle to a draw.

Air Date: 11th July 2002
WWE World Tag Champions Hulk Hogan and Edge dropped Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo; WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly downed Torrie Wilson; D-Von Dudley and Batista overturned Randy Orton and Mark Henry; Rikishi pinned Test; Billy Kidman and The Hurricane dropped WWE Cruiserweight Champion Jamie Noble and Tajiri; WWE World Champion The Undertaker and John Cena bested Kurt Angle and Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 18th July 2002
John Cena beat Chris Jericho by DQ; Chavo Guerrero Jr defeated The Hurricane; Hardcore Holly and Val Venis overturned Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo; Rikishi and WWE World Tag Champions Hulk Hogan and Edge dropped Test, Christian, and Lance Storm; Billy Kidman bested Tajiri; The Rock defeated Kurt Angle by DQ.

Air Date: 25th July 2002
Mark Henry beat Kurt Angle by DQ; Val Venis dropped D-Von Dudley; Rikishi pinned Albert; Hulk Hogan and WWE World Champion The Rock defeated WWE World Tag Champions Christian and Lance Storm by DQ; Rey Mysterio dropped Chavo Guerrero Jr; John Cena bested Test; Edge dropped Chris Jericho in a cage match.

Aug 2002

Air Date: 1st August 2002
Rey Mysterio dropped Tajiri; Brock Lesnar beat Mark Henry; Hulk Hogan bested Kurt Angle by DQ; Rikishi pinned D-Von Dudley; John Cena bested Rico;  WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit and Eddie Guerrero overcame WWE World Champion The Rock and Edge.

Air Date: 8th August 2002
John Cena bested Kurt Angle by DQ; Mark Henry beat D-Von Dudley; Rikishi pinned Batista; The Hurricane and Shannon Moore toppled Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo; Edge, Rey Mysterio, and John Cena bested Kurt Angle, WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit, and Eddie Guerrero; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Jamie Noble and Nidia defeated Billy Kidman and Torrie Wilson; Brock Lesnar rolled over Hulk Hogan by stoppage.

Air Date: 15th August 2002
Billy Gunn, Chuck Palumbo, and Rico defeated The Hurricane, Shannon Moore, and Hardcore Holly; Kurt Angle toppled Mark Henry; Brock Lesnar beat Rikishi; Nidia defeated Torrie Wilson; Edge and Rey Mysterio dropped Eddie and Chavo Guerrero Jr by DQ; WWE World Champion The Rock pinned WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit.

Air Date: 22nd August 2002
The Hurricane and Shannon Moore toppled WWE Cruiserweight Champion Jamie Noble and Tajiri; Chavo Guerrero Jr dropped Matt Hardy; Billy Kidman bested Kurt Angle by count-out; Rikishi and John Cena bested D-Von Dudley and Batista; WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly downed Nidia; WWE World Champion The Rock and Edge dropped WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit and Eddie Guerrero.

Air Date: 29th August 2002
Eddie Guerrero overcame Edge; D-Von Dudley beat John Cena;  Rey Mysterio dropped Rico; Rikishi pinned Eddie Guerrero; Shannon Moore beat Tajiri ; Chris Benoit toppled Rikishi; WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar beat Matt Hardy; The Undertaker bested Chris Benoit and Kurt Angle in a three-way.

Sep 2002


Air Date: 5th September 2002
Rey Mysterio dropped Billy Gunn; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Jamie Noble beat Shannon Moore; WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar beat Randy Orton; Mark Henry beat Tajiri; Batista overturned D-Von Dudley; Hardcore Holly downed Matt Hardy; Crash Holly downed The Hurricane; The Undertaker, Edge, and Rikishi defeated Kurt Angle, Eddie Guerrero, and Chris Benoit.

Air Date: 12th September 2002
WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar beat Hardcore Holly; Eddie and Chavo Guerrero Jr overturned Edge and John Cena; Kurt Angle toppled Rey Mysterio; Chris Benoit toppled Rikishi by DQ; Torrie Wilson bested Nidia; Matt Hardy beat The Undertaker by count-out.

Air Date: 19th September 2002
Rey Mysterio and Torrie Wilson bested WWE Cruiserweight Champion Jamie Noble and Nidia; WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar beat John Cena; Eddie Guerrero and Chavo Guerrero Jr overturned Edge and Rikishi; Matt Hardy beat The Hurricane; Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo defeated Kurt Angle and Chris Benoit.

Air Date: 26th September 2002
Rikishi pinned Chavo Guerrero Jr; Tajiri and Jamie Noble toppled Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo; Edge toppled Eddie Guerrero in a no-DQ match; The Undertaker bested Matt Hardy; Rey Mysterio dropped Kurt Angle and Chris Benoit in a three-way.

Oct 2002

Air Date: 3rd October 2002
Eddie and Chavo Guerrero Jr overturned Rikishi and Mark Henry (WWE Tag Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); Matt Hardy beat The Undertaker in a “falls count anywhere” match; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Jamie Noble beat Crash Holly; Edge toppled Kurt Angle; Faarooq and D-Von Dudley beat Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo (WWE Tag Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); Torrie Wilson bested Dawn Marie in a bikini contest; Rey Mysterio dropped Chris Benoit.

Air Date: 10th October 2002
Eddie Guerrero beat Rikishi; Kurt Angle and Chris Benoit toppled Billy Kidman and John Cena (WWE Tag Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); Billy Gunn pinned  D-Von Dudley; Torrie Wilson bested Dawn Marie in a Lingerie contest Edge and Rey Mysterio dropped Tajiri and WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar (WWE Tag Title Tournament – Quarter-Final).

Air Date: 17th October 2002
Edge and Rey Mysterio dropped Ron Simmons and D-Von Dudley (WWE Tag Title Tournament – Semi-Final);  WWE Cruiserweight Champion Jamie Noble toppled Nidia. Tajiri was guest referee; John Cena bested Billy Kidman; WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar beat Chuck Palumbo; Matt Hardy and Dawn Marie toppled Rikishi and Torrie Wilson; Kurt Angle and Chris Benoit toppled Eddie and Chavo Guerrero Jr (WWE Tag Title Tournament – Semi-Final).

Air Date: 24th October 2002
Rikishi pinned Matt Hardy; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Jamie Noble beat Tajiri; Edge and Rey Mysterio dropped Eddie and Chavo Guerrero Jr; Bily Kidman bested John Cena; Chris Benoit toppled Kurt Angle.

Air Date: 31st October 2002
Edge toppled Chris Benoit; The Big Show toppled Rikishi; Matt Hardy beat Tajiri; Eddie Guerrero overcame Kurt Angle; Rey Mysterio battled WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar to a no-contest.

Nov 2002

Air Date: 7th November 2002
Billy Kidman and Torrie Wilson bested WWE Cruiserweight Champion Jamie Noble and Nidia; Rikishi pinned John Cena; The Big Show toppled Matt Hardy; Edge and Rey Mysterio dropped WWE Tag Team Champions Kurt Angle and Chris Benoit in a “best-of-three-falls” encounter; WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar dropped Eddie Guerrero.

Air Date: 14th November 2002
Edge toppled Chavo Guerrero Jr; Matt Hardy and John Cena bested Rikishi and Tajiri; Eddie Guerrero overcame Rey Mysterio; Billy Kidman bested WWE Cruiserweight Champion Jamie Noble in a non-title match; Chris Benoit battled Kurt Angle to a no-contest.

Air Date: 21st November 2002
Rey Mysterio dropped Jamie Noble; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Billy Kidman bested Tajiri; Chris Benoit toppled Chavo Guerrero Jr; Kurt Angle toppled Eddie Guerrero; Rikishi pinned John Cena; WWE World Champion The Big Show beat Edge by DQ.

Air Date: 28th November 2002
Matt Hardy and John Cena beat Edge and Rey Mysterio; Tajiri dropped Chuck Palumbo; WWE Tag Team Champions Eddie and Chavo Guerrero Jr overturned Kurt Angle and Chris Benoit; Torrie Wilson bested Dawn Marie in a “Pilgrim” match; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Billy Kidman bested Crash Holly; WWE World Champion The Big Show battled The Fabulous Moolah to a no-contest.

Dec 2002

Air Date: 5th December 2002
Albert overcame Rey Mysterio; Crash Holly downed Jamie Noble; Bill DeMott overcame Sho Funaki; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Billy Kidman bested Chavo Guerrero Jr; Kurt Angle toppled Chris Benoit, Eddie Guerrero, and Edge in a four-way elimination match.

Air Date: 12th December 2002
Bull Buchanon beat Rikishi; Bill DeMott overcame Shannon Moore; WWE Tag Team Champions Eddie and Chavo Guerrero Jr overturned Chris Benoit and Billy Kidman; Crash Holly downed Jamie Noble by DQ; WWE World Champion The Big Show and Albert overcame Kurt Angle and Edge.

Air Date: 19th December 2002
Eddie Guerrero beat WWE Cruiserweight Champion Billy Kidman in a non-title match; John Cena beat Chuck Palumbo; Brock Lesnar dropped Shannon Moore; Bill DeMott and Crash Holly downed Jamie Noble and Nunzio; Edge toppled Albert; Chris Benoit toppled WWE World Champion Kurt Angle by DQ.

Air Date: 26th December 2002
Bill DeMott overcame Crash Holly; WWE Tag Team Champions Eddie and Chavo Guerrero Jr overturned Edge and WWE Cruiserweight Champion Billy Kidman; Rikishi pinned Bull Buchanon; Chris Benoit toppled The Big Show.
