NXT Results 2015

NXT RESULTS (2015)

Jan 2015

Air Date: 1st January 2015
WWE NXT Tag Champions Sin Cara and Kalisto bested Jason Jordan and Tye Dillinger; Blue Pants beat Carmella; Baron Corbin overcame CJ Parker; Finn Balor and Hideo Itami dropped The Ascension.

Air Date: 8th January 2015
Hideo Itami dropped Curtis Axel; Sasha Banks toppled Alexa Bliss; Tyler Breeze downed Chad Gable; WWE NXT Tag Champions Sin Cara and Kalisto bested Aiden English and Simon Gotch.

Air Date: 14th January 2015
Finn Balor overturned Tyson Kidd; Baron Corbin overcame Bull Dempsey; Sasha Banks and Becky Lynch beat WWE NXT Women’s Champion Charlotte and Natalya; WWE NXT Champion Sami Zayn overcame Adrian Neville.

Air Date: 21st January 2015
Finn Balor toppled Curtis Axel (WWE NXT Title #1 Contender Tournament – 1st Round); WWE NXT Women’s Champion Charlotte downed Sasha Banks by DQ; Wesley Blake and Buddy Murphy beat Aiden English and Simon Gotch; Hideo Itami dropped Tyler Breeze (WWE NXT #1 Contender Tournament – 1st Round).

Air Date: 28th January 2015
Wesley Blake and Buddy Murphy beat WWE NXT Tag Champions Sin Cara and Kalisto to win the titles; Adrian Neville downed Tyson Kidd (WWE NXT Title #1 Contender Tournament – 1st Round); WWE NXT Women’s Champion Charlotte and Bayley battled to a no-contest with Sasha Banks and Becky Lynch; Baron Corbin overcame Bull Dempsey (WWE NXT Title #1 Contender Tournament – 1st Round).

Feb 2015

Air Date: 4th February 2015
Carmella defeated Emma; Adrian Neville downed Baron Corbin (WWE NXT Title #1 Contender Tournament – Semi-Final); Bayley beat Becky Lynch; Finn Balor toppled Hideo Itami (WWE NXT Title #1 Contender Tournament – Semi-Final).

There was no show on 2/11. It was pre-empted by the live broadcast of NXT Takeover: Rival

Air Date: 18th February 2015
Rhyno bested Elias Sampson; Enzo Amore and Colin Cassady beat Aiden English and Simon Gotch; WWE NXT Women’s Champion Sasha Banks overcame Blue Pants; WWE NXT Champion Kevin Owens overcame Adrian Neville.

Air Date: 25th February 2015
Hideo Itami dropped Bull Dempsey; Sin Cara and Kalisto bested Jason Jordan and Tye Dillinger; Baron Corbin rolled over Tye Dillinger; Becky Lynch beat Bayley; Finn Balor toppled Brian Kendrick.

Mar 2015

Air Date: 4th March 2015
Tyler Breeze downed Adam Rose; WWE NXT Tag Champions Wesley Blake and Buddy Murphy beat Angelo Dawkins and Sawyer Fulton; Baron Corbin rolled over Tony Briggs; Solomon Crowe downed Bull Dempsey; WWE NXT Women’s Champion Sasha Banks overcame Charlotte.

Air Date: 11th March 2015
Enzo Amore and Colin Cassady beat Sin Cara and Kalisto; Alexa Bliss overcame Carmella; Alex Riley beat CJ Parker; Tyler Breeze downed Hideo Itami.

Air Date: 18th March 2015
Tyler Breeze downed Kalisto; Wesley Blake downed Colin Cassady; Alexa Bliss dropped WWE NXT Women’s Champion Sasha Banks by count-out; WWE NXT Champion Kevin Owens dropped Alex Riley.

Air Date: 25th March 2015
WWE NXT Women’s Champion Sasha Banks dropped Alexa Bliss; WWE NXT Champion Kevin Owens dropped Finn Balor.

Apr 2015

Air Date: 1st April 2015
Rhyno beat Jesus De Leon; Jason Jordan toppled Tye Dillinger; Bayley overcame Emma; WWE NXT Tag Champions Murphy and Blake downed Sin Cara and Kalisto; Tyler Breeze downed Hideo Itami in a “best-of-three-falls” match.

Air Date: 8th April 2015
Hideo Itami beat Adrian Neville (Andre The Giant Memorial Battle Royal Qualifying Tournament – Semi-Final); Finn Balor defeated Tyler Breeze (Andre The Giant Memorial Battle Royal Qualifying Tournament – Semi-Final); Hideo Itami bested Finn Balor (Andre The Giant Memorial Battle Royal Qualifying Tournament – Final).

Air Date: 15th April 2015
Solomon Crowe downed CJ Parker; Baron Corbin toppled Steve Cutler; Enzo Amore and Colin Cassady overcame Angelo Dawkins and Sawyer Fulton; Dana Brooke downed Blue Pants; Sami Zayn toppled Rhyno.

Air Date: 22nd April 2015
Finn Balor overturned Tye Dillinger; Becky Lynch won a three-way over Charlotte and Bayley; Hideo Itami dropped CJ Parker; Rhyno won a squash; Kevin Owens dropped Alex Riley.

Air Date: 29th April 2015
Enzo Amore and Colin Cassady overturned WWE NXT Tag Champions Murphy and Blake in a non-title match; Dana Brooke downed Bayley; Hideo Itami dropped Adam Rose; Becky Lynch beat Sarah Dobson; Sami Zayn toppled Alex Riley by DQ.

May 2015

Air Date: 6th May 2015
Charlotte downed Emma; Rhyno bested Bull Dempsey; Hideo Itami dropped Tyler Breeze.

Air Date: 13th May 2015
Bayley overturned Jessica McKay; Baron Corbin toppled Solomon Crowe; WWE NXT Women’s Champion Sasha Banks overcame KC Cassidy; Alexa Bliss overcame Carmella; Kalisto bested Tye Dillinger; Tyler Breeze and Adam Rose downed Finn Balor and Hideo Itami.

There was no show on 5/20. It was pre-empted by the live broadcast of NXT Takeover: Unstoppable

Air Date: 27th May 2015
Emma defeated Bayley; WWE NXT Tag Champions Blake & Murphy beat Elias Sampson & Mike Rallis; Finn Balor overturned Tye Dillinger; WWE NXT Champion Kevin Owens overcame Solomon Crowe.

Jun 2015

Air Date: 3rd June 2015
Tyler Breeze downed Adam Rose; Alexa Bliss overcame Carmella; Aiden English & Simon Gotch bested Jason Jordan & Marcus Louis; Finn Balor overturned Rhyno.

Air Date: 10th June 2015
Zack Ryder & Mojo Rawley beat Elias Sampson & Mike Rallis; Emma defeated Blue Pants; Tyler Breeze downed Bull Dempsey; Becky Lynch bested Jessie; Baron Corbin toppled Angelo Dawkins; Samoa Joe downed Scott Dawson.

Air Date: 17th June 2015
Colin Cassady, Enzo Amore & Carmella defeated WWE NXT Tag Champions Murphy & Blake, & Alexa Bliss; Charlotte downed Cassie; WWE NXT Champion Kevin Owens battled to a no-contest with Samoa Joe

Air Date: 24th June 2015
Colin Cassady & Enzo Amore beat Jason Jordan & Sylvester Lefort; Dana Brooke downed KC; Mojo Rawley & Zack Ryder toppled Angelo Dawkins & Sawyer Fulton; Finn Balor toppled Rhyno.

Jul 2015

Aiden English & Simon Gotch overturned Dash Wilder & Scott Dawson; Baron Corbin overturned Tucker Night; Emma beat Carmella; Tyler Breeze downed Tye Dillinger; Finn Balor & Samoa Joe downed WWE NXT Champion Kevin Owens & Rhyno.

Air Date: 8th July 2015
Charlotte & WWE NXT Women’s Champion Sasha Banks overcame Dana Brooke & Emma; Solomon Crowe downed Marcus Louis; Aiden English & Simon Gotch beat Colin Cassady & Enzo Amore.

Air Date: 15th July 2015
Chad Gable & Jason Jordan bested Elias Sampson & Steve Cutler; Samoa Joe downed Axel Tischer; WWE NXT Tag Champions Blake & Murphy beat Angelo Dawkins & Sawyer Fulton; WWE NXT Women’s Champion Sasha Banks rolled over Charlotte.

Air Date: 22nd July 2015
Eva Marie downed Cassie; Baron Corbin dropped Mr. 450; Samoa Joe downed Mike Rawlins; Bayley overcame Emma; Aiden English & Simon Gotch  defeated Angelo Dawkins & Sawyer Fulton.

Air Date: 29th July 2015
Dash Wilder & Scott Dawson defeated Colin Cassady & Enzo Amore; Baron Corbin dropped Jesse Sorensen; Chad Gable & Jason Jordan dropped Elias Sampson & Manny Garcia; Charlotte downed Dana Brooke; Kevin Owens toppled Martin Stone; WWE NXT Tag Champions Blake & Murphy beat Aiden English & Simon Gotch.

Aug 2015

Air Date: 5th August 2015
Bayley overcame Charlotte; Baron Corbin dropped Steve Cutler; Tyler Breeze downed Aaron Solo; Mojo Rawley & Zack Ryder rolled over Dash Wilder & Scott Dawson; Samoa Joe downed Rhyno.

Air Date: 12th August 2015

Air Date: 19th August 2015

Air Date: 26th August 2015
Colin Cassady, Enzo Amore, Mojo Rawley & Zack Ryder beat Chad Gable, Dash Wilder, Jason Jordan & Scott Dawson; Eva Marie downed Carmella; Bull Dempsey beat Elias Samson; Emma defeated Becky Lynch, Charlotte and Dana Brooke in a four-way.

Sep 2015

Air Date: 2nd September 2015
WWE NXT Women’s Champion Sasha Banks toppled Gionna Daddio; Baron Corbin & Rhyno dropped The Ascension; Alexa Bliss beat Blue Pants; Apollo Crews defeated Alexander Wolf; Eva Marie overcame Billie Kay; Chad Gable & Jason Jordan downed Neville & Solomon Crowe.

Air Date: 9th September 2015

Air Date: 16th September 2015

Air Date: 23rd September 2015
Eva Marie downed Carmella by count-out; Tyler Breeze downed Bull Dempsey; WWE NXT Tag Champions Aiden English and Simon Gotch defeated Blake and Murphy.

Air Date: 30th September 2015
WWE NXT Champion Finn Balor and Samoa Joe downed Enzo Amore and Colin Cassady (Dusty Rhodes Tag Classic Tournament – Quarter-Final); Apollo Crews rolled over Johnny Gargano; Dana Brooke and Emma defeated Peyton Royce and Billie Kay; Tyler Breeze downed Tommaso Ciampa.
