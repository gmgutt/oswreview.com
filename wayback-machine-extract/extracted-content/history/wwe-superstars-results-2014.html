WWE Superstars Results 2014

WWE SUPERSTARS RESULTS (2014)

Jan 2014

Air Date: 3rd January 2014
Jack Swagger downed Tyson Kidd; Jimmy and Jey Uso bested Titus O’Neil and Darren Young.

Air Date: 10th January 2014
Natalya defeated Summer Rae; Ryback toppled Dolph Ziggler.

Air Date: 17th January 2014
Xavier Woods beat Brodus Clay; Titus O’Neil and Darren Young defeated Jinder Mahal and Drew McIntyre.

Air Date: 24th January 2014
Brie Bella beat Alicia Fox; Los Matadores and Sin Cara defeated Heath Slater, Jinder Mahal and Drew McIntyre.

Air Date: 31st January 2014
Natalya beat Summer Rae; Los Matadores downed Jinder Mahal and Drew McIntyre.

 

Feb 2014

Air Date: 7th February 2014
Tamina Snuka beat Natalya; Jimmy Uso, Jey Uso and Los Matadores bested Drew McIntyre, Jinder Mahal, Heath Slater and Curt Hawkins.

Air Date: 14th February 2014
WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E Langston defeated Drew McIntyre; Natalya downed Tamina Snuka.

Air Date: 21st February 2014
Cody Rhodes toppled Curtis Axel; The Bella Twins and Natalya beat Summer Rae, Tamina Snuka and Eva Marie. 

Air Date: 28th February 2014
Kofi Kingston rolled over Damien Sandow; Goldust, Cody Rhodes and Los Matadores toppled Ryback, Curtis Axel, Drew McIntyre and Jinder Mahal.

Air Date: 21st March 2014
Natalya defeated Alicia Fox; R-Truth and Xavier Woods overturned Jinder Mahal and Drew McIntyre.

Air Date: 28th March 2014
Emma defeated Summer Rae; R-Truth and Xavier Woods overturned Drew McIntyre and Jinder Mahal.
