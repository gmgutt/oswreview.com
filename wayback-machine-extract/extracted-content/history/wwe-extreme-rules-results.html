WWE Extreme Rules Results

WWE EXTREME RULES RESULTS

7th June 2009: “WWE Extreme Rules 2009”: New Orleans, LA – 9,214
($448,990 live gate)
Dark match: Mickie James and Kelly Kelly beat Beth Phoenix and Rosa Mendes.
PPV Broadcast: WWE US Champion Kofi Kingston overcame MVP, William Regal, and Matt Hardy in a four-way; Chris Jericho defeated WWE Intercontinental Champion Rey Mysterio in a “no-holds-barred” match to win the title; CM Punk downed Umaga in a strap match; Tommy Dreamer overturned ECW Champion Christian and Jack Swagger in a three-way “extreme rules” match to win the title; Santino Marella won a handicap “Hog Pen” match over Vickie Guerrero and Chavo Guerrero Jr; Batista bested WWE World Champion Randy Orton in a cage match to win the title; John Cena toppled The Big Show in a submission match; Jeff Hardy rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Edge in a ladder match to win the title; CM Punk pinned World Heavyweight Champion Jeff Hardy to win the title.

25th April 2010: “WWE Extreme Rules 2010”: Baltimore, MD – 9,000 sellout
($550,000 live gate)
Dark match: Kofi Kingston downed Dolph Ziggler.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Unified World Tag Champions The Miz and The Big Show bested John Morrison and R-Truth by DQ; WWE Unified World Tag Champions The Miz and The Big Show downed MVP and Mark Henry; DH Smith and Tyson Kidd overcame WWE Unified World Tag Champions The Miz and The Big Show in a non-title match; CM Punk dropped Rey Mysterio; JTG beat Shad Gaspard in a strap match; World Heavyweight Champion Jack Swagger toppled Randy Orton in an Extreme Rules match; Sheamus downed Triple H in a streetfight; Beth Phoenix pinned WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool in an extreme makeover match to win the title; Edge bested Chris Jericho in a cage match; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Batista in a last man standing match.

1st May 2011: “WWE Extreme Rules 2011”: Tampa, FL – 10,000
($630,000 live gate)
Dark match: Sin Cara bested Tyson Kidd.
PPV Broadcast: Randy Orton downed CM Punk in a Last Man Standing match; Kofi Kingston defeated WWE US Champion Sheamus in a tables match to win the title; Michael Cole and Jack Swagger overturned  Jerry Lawler and Jim Ross in a “Country Whipping” match; Rey Mysterio toppled Cody Rhodes in a falls-count-anywhere encounter; Layla overcame Michelle  McCool in a no-DQ, no count-out, “Loser leaves WWE” match;  Christian defeated Alberto Del Rio in a ladder match to win the vacant World Heavyweight Title; WWE World Tag Champions Kane and The Big Show downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett and Ezekiel Jackson in a lumberjack match; John Cena rolled over WWE World Champion The Miz and John Morrison in a Three Way Steel Cage Match to win the title.

29th April 2012: “WWE Extreme Rules 2012”: Chicago, IL – 14,817 sellout (11,500 paid)
($700,000 live gate)
Youtube pre-show: (63,000 views) WWE US Champion Santino Marella beat The Miz.
PPV Broadcast: Randy Orton bested Kane in a falls-count-anywhere match; Brodus Clay defeated Dolph Ziggler; Cody Rhodes overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion The Big Show in a tables match to win the title; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus toppled Daniel Bryan in a “best-of-three-falls” encounter; Ryback squashed Aaron Relic and Jay Hatton in a handicap match; WWE World Champion CM Punk dropped Chris Jericho in a streetfight; Layla beat WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella to win the titles; John Cena rolled over Brock Lesnar in an “Extreme Rules” match.

19th May 2013: “WWE Extreme Rules 2013”: St Louis, MO – 14,500 sellout
Pre-show: The Miz downed Cody Rhodes.
PPV Broadcast: Chris Jericho defeated Fandango; Dean Ambrose beat WWE US Champion Kofi Kingston to win the title; Sheamus overcame Mark Henry in a strap match; Alberto Del Rio bested Jack Swagger in an “I Quit” match; Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns overturned WWE World Tag Champions Daniel Bryan and Kane to win the titles in a tornado match; Randy Orton pinned The Big Show in an “Extreme Rules” match; WWE World Champion John Cena battled Ryback to a no-contest in a “last man standing” match; Brock Lesnar rolled over Triple H in a cage match.

4th May 2014: “WWE Extreme Rules 2014”: East Rutherford, NJ – 14,500 sellout (12,900 paid)
Shown on PPV / WWE Network
Pre-show: El Torito beat Hornswoggle in a “WeeLC” encounter.
PPV Broadcast: Cesaro won a three-way elimination match over Jack Swagger and Rob Van Dam; Rusev defeated R-Truth and Xavier Woods in a handicap match; Wade Barrett downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E to win the title; Roman Reigns, WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose and Seth Rollins bested Triple H, Batista and Randy Orton; Bray Wyatt dropped John Cena in a cage match; WWE Divas Champion Paige toppled Tamina Snuka; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan rolled over Kane in an “Extreme Rules” match.

26th April 2015: “WWE Extreme Rules 2015”: Chicago, IL – 11,000 sellout
Shown on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Neville pinned Bad News Barrett.
PPV Broadcast: Dolph Ziggler toppled Sheamus in a “Kiss Me Arse” match; Kofi Kingston and Big E bested WWE World Tag Champions Tyson Kidd and Cesaro to win the titles; Dean Ambrose pinned Luke Harper in a streetfight; WWE US Champion John Cena beat Rusev in a chain match; WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella downed Naomi; Roman Reigns dropped Big Show in a “last man standing” match; WWE Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins rolled over Randy Orton in a cage match.

22nd May 2016: “WWE Extreme Rules 2016”: Newark, NJ – 12,576 sellout
($1,058,400 live gate)
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show:  Baron Corbin overcame Dolph Ziggler in a no-DQ match.
PPV Broadcast: Luke Gallows and Karl Anderson overcame Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso in a Texas Tornado match; Rusev defeated WWE US Champion Kalisto to win the title; WWE Tag Champions Big E and Xavier Woods defeated Simon Gotch and Aiden English; WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz defeated Cesaro, Kevin Owens and Sami Zayn in a four-way; Dean Ambrose downed Chris Jericho in an Asylum match; WWE Women’s Champion Charlotte defeated Natalya in a submission match; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Roman Reigns defeated AJ Styles in an “Extreme Rules” match.
