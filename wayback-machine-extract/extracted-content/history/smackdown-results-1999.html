Smackdown Results 1999

WWF SMACKDOWN RESULTS (1999)

Apr 1999

Air Date: 29th April 1999
TV Special on UPN.
The Blue Blazer beat Val Venis; The Big Show downed Test; D-Lo Brown defeated Droz by DQ; WWF World Tag Champions Kane and X-Pac dropped Road Dogg and Billy Gunn; Ken Shamrock bested Bradshaw in a streetfight; Mankind downed The Big Bossman; WWF World Champion Steve Austin and The Rock battled The Undertaker and Triple H to a no-contest.

Aug 1999

Air Date: 26th August 1999
First weekly show on UPN
Billy Gunn toppled WWF Intercontinental Champion Jeff Jarrett in a non-title match; WWF World Tag Champions The Undertaker and The Big Show dropped X-Pac and Kane, and The Acolytes in a Triple Threat match; The Big Bossman bested WWF Hardcore Champion Al Snow to win the title; Road Dogg toppled Chris Jericho by DQ; Shane McMahon defeated Mankind; Tori overcame WWF Women’s Champion Ivory in a non-title “Evening Gown” match; WWF World Champion Triple H bested The Rock. Shawn Michaels was guest referee.

Sep 1999

Air Date: 2nd September 1999
Chyna dropped Billy Gunn; Tony Chimel defeated Howard Finkel in a tuxedo match; X-Pac battled Chris Jericho to a no-contest; WWF World Champion Triple H bested Gillberg; The Rock battled Mankind to a no-contest; Ken Shamrock bested Val Venis; Hardcore and Crash Holly, Viscera and Mideon, The Acolytes, Matt and Jeff Hardy, Edge and Christian, Albert and Droz, and Stevie Richards and The Blue Meanie battled to a no-contest in a tag team gauntlet match; WWF World Tag Champions The Rock and Mankind downed WWF World Champion Triple H and Shane McMahon.

Air Date: 9th September 1999
Davey Boy Smith bested WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Bossman to win the title; Edge and Christian defeated Matt and Jeff Hardy; Hardcore Holly battled Chyna to a no-contest; Bradshaw bested Bubba Ray Dudley by DQ; WWF World Champion Triple H pinned Kane; Chris Jericho battled Ken Shamrock to a no-contest; The Undertaker and The Big Show rolled over WWF World Tag Champions Mankind and The Rock to win the titles in a “Buried Alive” match.

Air Date: 16th September 1999
Shane McMahon defeated Joey Abs. Shawn Stasiak was guest referee; WWF European Champion Mark Henry downed Steve Blackman; Mr Hughes toppled Ken Shamrock by DQ. El Dopo (A masked Howard Finkel) was the guest referee; The Undertaker won a 5-man battle royal; Billy Gunn and Chyna overturned Hardcore and Crash Holly; Al Snow bested The Big Bossman in a “Pepper-on-a-Pole” match; WWF Intercontinental Champion Jeff Jarrett battled Test to a no-contest; Vince McMahon rolled over WWF World Champion Triple H to win the title. Shane McMahon was guest referee.

Air Date: 23rd September 1999
The Big Show downed Triple H in a “Chokeslam” match; X-Pac downed WWF European Champion Mark Henry by count-out; Triple H bested Kane in an Inferno match; Road Dogg and Billy Gunn toppled WWF World Tag Champions The Rock and Mankind to win the titles; Mideon and Viscera overcame Triple H in a Casket match; Hardcore Holly overturned Al Snow; Triple H bested Mankind in a “Boiler Room Brawl”; Chris Jericho toppled Ken Shamrock in a “First Blood” match; Triple H bested The Rock in a “Brahma Bull Strap Match”.

Air Date: 30th September 1999
Road Dogg battled Chris Jericho to a no-contest; Edge and Christian downed Matt and Jeff Hardy; The Big Show battled WWF European Champion D-Lo Brown to a no-contest; X-Pac and Kane dropped The Dudley Boyz and The Acolytes in a Triple Threat elimination match; Davey Boy Smith battled WWF World Champion Triple H to a no-contest.

Oct 1999

Air Date: 7th October 1999
X-Pac and Kane downed Viscera and Mideon; Hardcore Holly bested Road Dogg; Matt and Jeff Hardy defeated Edge and Christian in a Texas Tornado match; WWF Women’s Champion Ivory overcame Miss Kitty in a mud wrestling match; Mankind battled Davey Boy Smith to a no-contest; The Big Show toppled The Big Bossman by DQ; The Rock rolled over Val Venis.

Air Date: 14th October 1999
The Acolytes beat Kane in a handicap match; Chris Jericho bested Mr Hughes; The Big Bossman defeated WWF Hardcore Champion Al Snow and The Big Show in a Triple Threat match to win the title; Val Venis pinned Stevie Richards; Matt and Jeff Hardy overcame Edge and Christian; The Godfather bested Mideon; Mankind and The Rock rolled over WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn to win the titles.

Air Date: 21st October 1999
Test, Edge and Christian defeated Davey Boy Smith, Joey Abs, Rodney and Pete Gas in a handicap match; WWF World Champion Triple H bested Al Snow; Mankind battled The Rock to a no-contest; WWF Women’s Champion The Fabulous Moolah battled Mae Young to a no-contest; WWF World Tag Champions Hardcore and Crash Holly dropped Matt and Jeff Hardy; The Big Show battled The Big Bossman to a no-contest; The Godfather and Mark Henry defeated Viscera and Mideon; Steve Austin rolled over Val Venis.

Air Date: 28th October 1999
Too Cool beat Edge and Christian; Stevie Richards pinned Chris Jericho; Davey Boy Smith overcame WWF European Champion D-Lo Brown to win the title; The Dudley Boys bested Kane and X-Pac; Matt and Jeff Hardy defeated Viscera and Mark Henry; Al Snow overturned WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Bossman in a non-title match; Mankind downed Val Venis.

Nov 1999

Air Date: 4th November 1999
Mankind and Al Snow toppled WWF World Tag Champions Hardcore and Crash Holly to win the titles; D-Lo Brown overcame Test; Matt and Jeff Hardy downed Too Cool; Edge dropped WWF European Champion Davey Boy Smith by DQ; The Godfather bested Chris Jericho; The Big Show battled The Big Bossman to a double count-out; WWF World Champion Triple H, Road Dogg, Billy Gunn and X-Pac rolled over Steve Austin, The Rock, Kane and Shane McMahon in an elimination match.

Air Date 11th November 1999
Hardcore Holly beat Edge; The Dudley Boys defeated The Godfather and D-Lo Brown; Val Venis battled WWF European Champion Davey Boy Smith to a no-contest; WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Bossman bested Faarooq; Brian Christopher overcame Jeff Hardy;  Tori battled WWF Women’s Champion Ivory to a no-contest; The Rock, Shane McMahon, Kane and Test rolled over WWF World Champion Triple H, X-Pac and WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg in an elimination match. Steve Austin was the guest enforcer.

Air Date: 18th November 1999
Brian Christopher and Scotty 2 Hotty defeated Edge and Christian; Kurt Angle beat Gangrel; WWF European Champion Davey Boy Smith bested The Godfather; WWF World Champion The Big Show downed Hardcore Holly; Kane toppled Viscera; WWF Women’s Champion Ivory beat Jackie and Luna in a three-way hardcore match; Chris Jericho overturned Mark Henry; WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg toppled Matt and Jeff Hardy; The Rock rolled over The Big Bossman.

Air Date: 25th November 1999
WWF World Champion The Big Show pinned Chris Jericho; Kurt Angle bested D-Lo Brown; Val Venis toppled WWF European Champion Davey Boy Smith by DQ; WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Bossman beat Kane; The Rock and Mankind downed Hardcore and Crash Holly; Rikishi pinned Joey Abs; Jackie overcame Ivory in a “Turkey Bowl” match. Miss Kitty was guest referee; The Godfather dropped Al Snow; WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn toppled Matt and Jeff Hardy in a cage match; Triple H and X-Pac rolled over Test and Shane McMahon.

Dec 1999

Air Date: 2nd December 1999
Viscera defeated Kane by count-out; The Dudley Boys downed Edge and Christian; WWF European Champion Davey Boy Smith dropped D-Lo Brown by DQ; Chris Jericho bested Mankind; Kurt Angle and Steve Blackman overturned The Headbangers; Rikishi overcame Crash Holly; The Rock pinned Al Snow; WWF World Champion The Big Show, Matt Hardy, and Jeff Hardy rolled over X-Pac and WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn.

Air Date: 9th December 1999
Road Dogg toppled Chris Jericho; The Dudley Boys bested Kurt Angle and Steve Blackman; Christian dropped WWF European Champion Davey Boy Smith by DQ; The Big Bossman and Prince Albert overturned Matt and Jeff Hardy by DQ; Mankind downed Al Snow by DQ; X-Pac pinned Test; The Rock rolled over Billy Gunn; Kane battled Triple H to a no-contest.

Air Date: 16th December 1999
WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho bested Albert; Kurt Angle dropped Test by DQ; Matt Hardy toppled Jeff Hardy; X-Pac downed Tori; Mankind downed Al Snow in a “falls count anywhere” match; Edge pinned Christian; The Acolytes beat The Mean Street Posse in a handicap match; Rikishi and Too Cool dropped Viscera, Hardcore Holly, and Crash Holly; WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn toppled Gerald Brisco and Pat Patterson; The Rock battled WWF World Champion The Big Show to a no-contest in a lumberjack match.

Air Date: 23rd December 1999
WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho defeated Hardcore Holly by DQ; Rikishi pinned Viscera; Test bested Road Dogg; Kurt Angle downed Edge; The Rock rolled over Al Snow in a cage match; WWF Women’s Champion The Kat dropped Ivory in a no-DQ match; Christian and Jeff Hardy beat Taka Michinoku and Sho Funaki; Kane toppled Billy Gunn; Mark Henry overturned Bubba Ray Dudley; Mankind bested WWF World Champion The Big Show by DQ.

Air Date: 30th December 1999
WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Bossman and Albert defeated Test in a handicap match; Kurt Angle bested Rikishi; Jeff Hardy pinned Al Snow; Crash and Hardcore Holly downed The Fabulous Moolah and Mae Young; The Rock rolled over Kane in a “no-holds-barred” match; Chyna battled WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho to a double-pinfall draw; Faarooq toppled Bubba Ray Dudley by DQ; Viscera and Jackie defeated Gangrel and Luna; Triple H, X-Pac, and WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn toppled WWF World Champion The Big Show in a handicap match.
