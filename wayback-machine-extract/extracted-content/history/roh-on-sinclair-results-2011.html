ROH on Sinclair Results 2011

ROH ON SINCLAIR TV RESULTS 2011

Sep 2011

Air Date: 24th September 2011
Adam Cole and Kyle O’Reilly overturned The Bravado Brothers; ROH Tag Champions Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin overcame Chris Hero and Claudio Castagnoli.

Oct 2011

Air Date: 1st October 2011
Mike Bennett defeated Jimmy Jacobs; ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal downed El Generico.

Air Date: 8th October 2011
Tommaso Ciampa beat Andy Ridge; ROH Champion Davey Richards rolled over Roderick Strong.

Air Date: 15th October 2011
Jay and Mark Briscoe toppled Kenny King and Rhett Titus; Eddie Edwards rolled over Michael Elgin.

Air Date: 22nd October 2011
TJ Perkins dropped Mike Mondo; ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal battled Mike Bennett to a draw.

Air Date: 29th October 2011
Roderick Strong defeated Kyle O’Reilly; Tommaso Ciampa overturned Alex Silva; Jay and Mark Briscoe downed Kenny King and Rhett Titus.

Nov 2011

Air Date: 5th November 2011
Michael Elgin beat Shiloh Jonze; ROH Tag Champions Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin overcame Caprice Coleman and Cedric Alexander.

Air Date: 12th November 2011
The Young Bucks bested The Bravado Brothers; ROH Champion Davey Richards and Eddie Edwards rolled over Roderick Strong and Michael Elgin.

Air Date: 19th November 2011
Mike Mondo beat Alex Silva; Mike Bennett overcame Jamin Olivencia; ROH Champion Davey Richards rolled over Kyle O’Reilly.

Air Date: 26th November 2011
Jay and Mark Briscoe battled Caprice Coleman and Cedric Alexander to a no-contest; Michael Elgin bested Raphael Constantine and Sean Casey in a handicap match; ROH TV Champion Jay  Lethal fought El Generico to a draw.

Dec 2011

Air Date: 3rd December 2011
Tommaso Ciampa pinned Shiloh Jonze; The Young Bucks toppled Adam Cole and Kyle O’Reilly.

Air Date: 10th December 2011
Cedric Alexander and Caprice Coleman beat The Bravado Brothers; TJ Perkins downed Chris Silvio; Rhett Titus and Kenny King toppled ROH Tag Champions Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin in a non-title match.

Air Date: 17th December 2011
Eddie Edwards defeated Andy Ridge; ROH Champion Davey Richards pinned Michael Elgin.

Air Date: 24th December 2011
Was a “Best Of” show featuring El Generico vs. Jay Lethal (10/01), Haas and Benjamin vs. Hero & Castagnoli (09/24), and The Briscoes vs. King & Titus (10/29).

Air Date: 31st December 2011
Was a “Best Of” show featuring Davey Richards vs. Roderick Strong (10/08), Roderick Strong vs. Kyle O’Reilly (10/29), and Richards & Edwards vs. Strong & Elgin (11/12).
