HISTORY OF WCW SIX-MAN TAG TITLE

See also: History of NWA Six-Man Tag Title (Mid-Atlantic Version)

DATE WRESTLERS DAYS HELD LOCATION
02/17/1991 The Junkyard Dog, Ricky Morton and Tommy Rich 106 Atlanta, GA
Defeated Dutch Mantel, Buddy Landell and Dr X to become the first champions.
06/03/1991 Jimmy Garvin, Michael Hayes and Badstreet 63 Birmingham, AL
Defeated The Junkyard Dog and Tommy Rich in a handicap match to win the titles.
08/05/1991 Dustin Rhodes, Tom Zenk and Big Josh 64 St Joseph, MO
10/08/1991 Richard Morton, Terrance Taylor and Tommy Rich 54 Montgomery, AL
11/1991 TITLE RETIRED
