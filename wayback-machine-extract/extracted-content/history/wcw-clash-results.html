1988

27th March 1988: “NWA Clash of the Champions”: Greensboro, NC – 6,000
Shown live on TBS
(5.8 TV Rating – 12.6 share – 2,561,000 homes)
(The Ric Flair vs. Sting main event drew a 7.1 rating with a 15 share in 3,138,000 homes. The final 15 minutes were seen in 3,447,000 homes)
NWA TV Champion Mike Rotunda pinned Jimmy Garvin in an amateur wrestling match; NWA US Tag Champions Bobby Eaton and Stan Lane bested Bobby Fulton and Tommy Rogers by reverse decision-DQ; NWA US Champion Dusty Rhodes and The Road Warriors overcame Ivan Koloff and The Powers of Pain in a barbed wire match; Lex Luger and Barry Windham overturned NWA World Tag Champions Arn Anderson and Tully Blanchard to win the titles; Sting battled NWA World Champion Ric Flair to a draw (7.1).

8th June 1988:  “NWA Clash of The Champions II: Miami Mayhem”: Miami, FL – 2,400
Shown live on TBS 
(4.7 TV Rating – 8.2 Share – 2,128,000 homes)
NWA US Champion Barry Windham overturned Brad Armstrong; NWA US Tag Champions Tommy Rogers and Bobby Fulton downed The Sheepherders; Ron and Jimmy Garvin defeated NWA TV Champion Mike Rotundo and Rick Steiner; Nikita Koloff defeated Al Perez by DQ; Sting and Dusty Rhodes battled NWA World Tag Champions Arn Anderson and Tully Blanchard to a double-DQ (6.0 rating – 2,660,000 homes).

7th September 1988: “NWA Clash of the Champions III: Fall Brawl”: Albany, GA – 3,700
Shown live on TBS
(5.4 TV Rating – 8.9 share – 2,548,000 homes)
Brad Armstrong battled NWA TV Champion Mike Rotunda to a draw (3.5 TV Rating); Nikita Koloff and Steve Williams overcame The Sheepherders; Dusty Rhodes overcame Kevin Sullivan by pinning Gary Hart;  Ricky Morton downed Ivan Koloff in a Russian chain match, Sting toppled NWA US Champion Barry Windham by DQ (6.5 TV Rating – 3,068,000 homes with around 6,500,000 viewers).

7th December 1988: “NWA Clash of the Champions IV: Seasons Beatings”: Chattanooga, TN – 8,000 (3,000 paid)
Shown live on TBS
(4.5 TV Rating –  6.8 share – 2,170,000 homes)
Tommy Rogers and Bobby Fulton downed Ron Simmons and Eddie Gilbert to win the vacant NWA US Tag Titles (NWA US Tag Title Tournament – Final) (3.7 rating); Steve Williams overcame The Italian Stallion; Ivan Koloff pinned Paul Jones; Road Warrior Animal defeated Dusty Rhodes by  DQ; NWA World Champion Ric Flair and NWA US Champion Barry Windham overturned Bobby Eaton and Stan Lane (5.2 rating – 2,300,000 homes).
