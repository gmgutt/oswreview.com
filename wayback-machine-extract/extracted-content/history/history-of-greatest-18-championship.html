HISTORY OF GREATEST 18 CHAMPIONSHIP

See also: History of WWF World Martial Arts Heavyweight Title

DATE WRESTLER LOCATION
09/29/1990 Riki Choshu –
Awarded. Choshu wins the IWGP World Title on 01/04/1992 and the belts are defended together. The title belt was the former WWWF World Martial Arts Title, but the titles do not share the same lineage.
08/16/1992 The Great Muta (Keiji Muto) Fukuoka, Japan
09/23/1992 TITLE RETIRED  
Muta vacates the belt shortly after winning it and the title is retired.
