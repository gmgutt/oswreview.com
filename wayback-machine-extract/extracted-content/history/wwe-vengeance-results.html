WWE Vengeance Results

WWE VENGEANCE RESULTS

9th December 2001: “WWE Vengeance 2001”: San Diego, CA – 11,800 sellout  (10,699 paid)
($550,580 live gate)
($100,696 merchandise sales)
Heat: (12/09): The Acolytes overturned Chuck Palumbo and Billy Gunn.
PPV Broadcast: Scotty 2 Hotty and Albert overcame Test and WWF European Champion Christian;; WWF Intercontinental Champion Edge toppled William Regal; Jeff Hardy dropped Matt Hardy. Lita was guest referee; WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys beat Kane and The Big Show; The Undertaker bested WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam to win the title; WWF Women’s Champion Trish Stratus overcame Jacquelyn; WWF World Champion Steve Austin bested Kurt Angle; Chris Jericho toppled WCW World Champion The Rock to win the title; WCW World Champion Chris Jericho rolled over WWF World Champion Steve Austin to unify the titles.

21st July 2002: “WWE Vengeance 2002”: Detroit, MI – 12,000
Heat: (07/21): Goldust overcame Steven Richards.
PPV Broadcast: Bubba Ray Dudley and Spike Dudley beat Chris Benoit and Eddie Guerrero in a tables match; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Jamie Noble dropped Billy Kidman; WWE European Champion Jeff Hardy downed William Regal; John Cena bested Chris Jericho; WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Brock Lesnar by DQ; Booker T overturned The Big Show in a no-DQ match; Christian and Lance Storm defeated WWE World Tag Champions Hulk Hogan and Edge to win the titles; The Rock rolled over WWE World Champion The Undertaker and Kurt Angle in a three-way to win the title.

27th July 2003: “WWE Vengeance 2003”: Denver, CO – 9,500
($475,000 live gate)
Heat: (07/27): Ultimo Dragon bested Kanyon.
PPV Broadcast: Eddie Guerrero overcame Chris Benoit  to win the vacant WWE US Title (WWE US Title Tournament – Final;); Jamie Noble toppled Billy Gunn; Bradshaw won the “APA Invitational Bar Room Brawl”; WWE Tag Team Champions Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas downed WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio and Billy Kidman; Sable toppled Stephanie McMahon in a no count-out encounter; The Undertaker bested John Cena; Vince McMahon toppled Zach Gowen; Kurt Angle toppled WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar and The Big Show in a three-way to win the title.

11th July 2004: “WWE Vengeance 2004”: Hartford, CT – 7,000
($370,000 live gate)
Heat: (07/11): Tyson Tomko beat Val Venis.
PPV Broadcast: Tajiri and Rhyno defeated Garrison Cade and Jonathan Coachman; Batista overturned Chris Jericho; WWE World Tag Champions Rob Conway and Sylvain Grenier dropped Eugene and Ric Flair by DQ; Matt Hardy beat Kane in a no-DQ match; Edge toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Randy Orton to win the title; Victoria toppled Molly Holly; World Heavyweight Champion Chris Benoit rolled over Triple H.

26th June 2005: “WWE Vengeance 2005”: Las Vegas, NV – 9,850 (8,218 paid)
($455,600 live gate)
Heat: (06/26): WWE World Tag Champions The Hurricane and Rosey beat The Heart Throbs.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion Carlito bested Shelton Benjamin; Victoria beat Christy Hemme; Kane pinned Edge; Shawn Michaels toppled Kurt Angle; WWE World Champion John Cena won a three-way over Chris Jericho and Christian; World Heavyweight Champion Batista downed Triple H in a “Hell in a Cell” match.

25th June 2006: “WWE Vengeance 2006”: Charlotte, NC – 6,800
($400,000 live gate)
Dark match: Val Venis toppled Rob Conway.
PPV Broadcast: Randy Orton beat Kurt Angle; Umaga toppled Eugene; Ric Flair bested Mick Foley in a “best of three falls” encounter; Johnny Nitro dropped WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin and Carlito in a three-way to win the title; WWE and ECW World Champion Rob Van Dam downed Edge; Imposter Kane (Drew Hankinson) bested Kane; John Cena rolled over Sabu in a lumberjack match; Shawn Michaels and Triple H toppled WWE World Tag Champions Kenny, Mitch, Nicky, Mikey, and Johnny in a non-title handicap match.

24th June 2007: “WWE Vengeance: Night of Champions 2007”: Houston, TX – 15,000 sellout
($800,000 live gate)
Dark match: Super Crazy downed Carlito.
PPV Broadcast: WWE World Tag Champions Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch defeated Matt and Jeff Hardy; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr pinned Jimmy Wang Yang; Johnny Nitro bested CM Punk to win the vacant ECW title; WWE Intercontinental Champion Santino Marella downed Umaga by DQ; WWE US Champion MVP toppled Ric Flair; WWE Tag Champions Deuce and Domino overcame Jimmy Snuka and Sgt. Slaughter; World Heavyweight Champion Edge overturned Batista by count-out; Candice Michelle pinned WWE Women’s Champion Melina to win the title; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over King Booker, Mick Foley, Randy Orton, and Bobby Lashley in a five-way.


There was no event in 2008, 2009 or 2010

 

23rd October 2011: “WWE Vengeance 2011”: San Antonio, TX – 8,000
($450,000 live gate)
Dark match: Wade Barrett beat Daniel Bryan; WWE World Tag Champions Evan Bourne and Kofi Kingston dropped Dolph Ziggler and Jack Swagger; WWE US Champion Dolph Ziggler bested Zack Ryder; WWE Divas Champion Beth Phoenix overcame Eve Torres; Sheamus bested Christian; R-Truth and The Miz toppled CM Punk and Triple H; Randy Orton pinned WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes in a non-title match; World Heavyweight Champion Mark Henry battled The Big Show to a no-contest; WWE World Champion Alberto Del Rio rolled over John Cena in a “Last Man Standing” match.
