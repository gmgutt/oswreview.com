WWF Superstars Results 1997

WWF SUPERSTARS RESULTS (1997)

Jan 1997

Air Date: 5th January 1997
Doug Furnas and Phil LaFon defeated Pierroth and Cibernetico by DQ; Flash Funk pinned Salvatore Sincere; The Sultan downed Matt Hardy; WWF Intercontinental Champion Hunter Hearst Helmsley pinned Freddie Joe Floyd; The Undertaker rolled over The Goon.

Air Date: 12th January 1997
Goldust beat The Executioner; Hector Garza defeated TL Hooper; Rocky Maivia downed Dr X; Bret Hart dropped The Sultan by DQ.

Air Date: 19th January 1997
Royal Rumble 1997 preview show.

Air Date: 26th January 1997
Faarooq and Crush defeated Doug Furnas and Phil LaFon; The Godwinns and WWF World Tag Champions Owen Hart and Davey Boy Smith in a non-title four-way;  Ahmed Johnson toppled WWF Intercontinental Champion Hunter Hearst Helmsley by DQ.

Feb 1997

Air Date: 2nd February 1997
Vader beat Ced Mann; Perro Aguayo bested Abismo Negro; The Undertaker battled Marc Mero to a double count-out.

Air Date: 9th February 1997
The Sultan downed Goldust by count-out; Hector Garza and Octagon defeated Heavy Metal and Guerra; Farooq dropped Ken Reha; Steve Austin battled Vader to a double count-out.

Air Date: 16th February 1997
Highlight show only. No original matches.

Air Date: 23rd February 1997
Goldust beat TL Hopper; The Godwinns overcame The Headbangers by DQ; The Headbangers downed The New Blackjacks by DQ; Crush overturned Davey Boy Smith.

Mar 1997

Air Date: 2nd March 1997
Bart Gunn beat TL Hopper; Bradshaw downed Henry Godwinn; The Headbangers defeated Freddie Joe Floyd and Barry Horowitz; Crush and Savio Vega dropped Goldust and Miguel Perez Jr.

Air Date: 9th March 1997
Salvatore Sincere defeated Alex Porteau; Doug Furnas and Phil LaFon downed Aldo Montoya and Bob Holly; Ahmed Johnson dropped Leif Cassidy.

Superstars became a highlight show from 03/16/1997 onwards and did not feature any additional material until its eventual cancellation in 2001.
