HISTORY OF WWE SMACKDOWN WOMEN’S TITLE

DATE WRESTLER DAYS HELD LOCATION
09/11/2016 Becky Lynch 84 Richmond, VA
Defeated Natalya, Naomi, Nikki Bella, Carmella, and Alexa Bliss in a six-way elimination match to become the first champion.
12/04/2016 Alexa Bliss 70 Dallas, TX
02/12/2017 Naomi 9 Phoenix, AZ
02/21/2017 VACANT
The title is declared vacant after Naomi is sidelined with a knee injury.
02/21/2017 Alexa Bliss (2) 40 Ontario, CA
Defeated Becky Lynch to win the vacant title.
04/02/2017 Naomi (2) – Orlando, FL
Defeated Alexa Bliss, Becky Lynch, Mickie James, Natalya, and Carmella in a six-way to win the title.
