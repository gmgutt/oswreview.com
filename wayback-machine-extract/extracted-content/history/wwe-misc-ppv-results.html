($148,000 live gate)
PPV Broadcast: Adrian Adonis toppled Corporal Kirschner (Wrestling Classic Tournament – First Round); Dynamite Kid dropped Nikolai Volkoff (Wrestling Classic Tournament – First Round); Randy Savage pinned Ivan Putski (Wrestling Classic Tournament – First Round); Ricky Steamboat bested Davey Boy Smith by stoppage (Wrestling Classic Tournament – First Round); The Junkyard Dog overturned The Iron Sheik (Wrestling Classic Tournament – First Round); Moondog Spot overcame Terry Funk by count-out (Wrestling Classic Tournament – First Round); WWF Intercontinental Champion Tito Santana defeated Don Muraco (Wrestling Classic Tournament – First Round); Paul Orndorff beat Bob Orton Jr by DQ (Wrestling Classic Tournament – First Round); Dynamite Kid downed Adrian Adonis (Wrestling Classic Tournament – Quarter-Final); Randy Savage toppled Ricky Steamboat (Wrestling Classic Tournament – Quarter-Final); The Junkyard Dog dropped Moondog Spot (Wrestling Classic Tournament – Quarter-Final); WWF Intercontinental Champion Tito Santana battled Paul Orndorff to a double count-out (Wrestling Classic Tournament – Quarter-Final); WWF World Champion Hulk Hogan bested Roddy Piper by DQ; Randy Savage pinned Dynamite Kid (Wrestling Classic Tournament – Semi-Final); The Junkyard Dog rolled over Randy Savage by count-out (Wrestling Classic Tournament – Final).

27th December 1989: “WWF No Holds Barred: The Match/The Movie”
Broadcast of No Holds Barred movie
(Taped on 12/12/1989 in Nashville, TN): WWF World Champion Hulk Hogan and Brutus Beefcake rolled over Randy Savage and Zeus in a cage match.

3rd December 1991: “WWF Tuesday in Texas”: San Antonio, TX- 8,000 sellout
($100,000 live gate)
Tuesday in Texas PPV Broadcast: (12/03): WWF Intercontinental Champion Bret Hart downed Skinner; Randy Savage bested Jake Roberts; Davey Boy Smith pinned The Warlord; Ted Dibiase and Repo Man defeated Tito Santana and Virgil; Hulk Hogan rolled over WWF World Champion The Undertaker to win the title.

14th February 1999: “WWF St Valentine’s Day Massacre”: Memphis, TN – 19,028 sellout (17,977 paid)
($316,618 live gate)
($143,215 merchandise sales)
Goldust pinned The Blue Meanie; Hardcore Holly defeated Al Snow in a Hardcore match to capture the vacant WWF Hardcore Title; The Big Bossman beat Mideon; WWF World Tag Champions Jeff Jarrett and Owen Hart dropped Mark Henry and D-Lo Brown; Val Venis toppled WWF Intercontinental Champion Ken Shamrock to win the title. Billy Gunn was guest referee; Kane and Chyna bested Triple H and WWF European Champion X-Pac; The Rock battled WWF World Champion Mankind to a double knock-out in a Last Man Standing encounter; Steve Austin rolled over Vince McMahon in a cage match.

22nd July 2001: “WWF Invasion”: Cleveland, OH – 17,019 sellout  (15,535 paid)
($848,060 live gate)
($146,452 merchandise sales)
Heat: (07/22): Chavo Guerrero Jr overturned Scotty 2 Hotty.
PPV Broadcast: Edge and Christian downed Mike Awesome and Lance Storm; Earl Hebner beat Nick Patrick. Mick Foley was guest referee; WWF World Tag Champions The Acolytes overturned WCW World Tag Champions Sean O’Haire and Chuck Palumbo in a non-title match; WCW Cruiserweight Champion Billy Kidman bested WWF Light Heavyweight Champion X-Pac in a non-title match; Raven bested William Regal; Hugh Morrus, Shawn Stasiak, and Chris Kanyon toppled WWF Intercontinental Champion Albert, The Big Show, and Billy Gunn; Tajiri beat Tazz; Rob Van Dam rolled over WWF Hardcore Champion Jeff Hardy to win the title; Lita and Trish Stratus overcame Torrie Wilson and Stacy Keibler in a “bra and panties” match. Mick Foley was guest referee; WCW World Champion Booker T, Rhyno, Diamond Dallas Page, and The Dudley Boys rolled over WWF World Champion Steve Austin, Chris Jericho, Kurt Angle, The Undertaker, and Kane.

3rd December 2006: “WWE/ECW December to Dismember 2006”: Augusta, GA – 4,800 (3,600 paid)
Dark match: Stevie Richards toppled Rene Dupree.
PPV Broadcast:  Matt Hardy and WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury; Balls Mahoney dropped Matt Striker; Sylvester Terkay and Elijah Burke overcame Little Guido and Tony Mamaluke; Daivari overturned Tommy Dreamer; Kevin Thorn and Ariel overturned Mike Knox and Kelly Kelly; Bobby Lashley dropped ECW World Champion The Big Show, Rob Van Dam, Test, CM Punk, and Hardcore Holly in an “Extreme Elimination Chamber” match to win the title.

28th June 2009: “WWE The Bash 2009”: Sacramento, CA – 11,946 (10,412 paid)
($670,360 live gate)
Dark match: R-Truth beat Shelton Benjamin.
PPV Broadcast: ECW Champion Tommy Dreamer won a scramble match over Christian, Jack Swagger, Finlay, and Mark Henry; Rey Mysterio toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho in a “mask vs. title” match to win the title; Dolph Ziggler defeated The Great Khali in a no-DQ encounter; Edge and Chris Jericho overcame WWE Unified World Tag Champions Carlito and Primo Colon, and Cody Rhodes and Ted Dibiase Jr in a three-way to win the titles; Michelle McCool downed WWE Women’s Champion Melina to win the title;  Jeff Hardy bested World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk by DQ; John Cena overtured The Miz; WWE World Champion Randy Orton rolled over Triple H in a “best-of-three-falls (normal, falls-count-anywhere, stretcher) encounter, (2:1).

13th September 2009: “WWE Breaking Point 2009”: Montreal, QUE – 12,000
($730,000 live gate)

PPV Broadcast: WWE Unified World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and The Big Show beat MVP and Mark Henry; WWE US Champion Kofi Kingston pinned The Miz; Ted Dibiase and Cody Rhodes bested Triple H and Shawn Michaels in a “falls-count-anywhere” submissions match; Kane overturned The Great  Khali in a “Singapore cane” match; ECW Champion Christian defeated William Regal; John Cena bested WWE Word Champion Randy Orton in an “I Quit” match; World Heavyweight Champion  CM Punk rolled over The Undertaker in a submission match by Montreal finish.

20th June 2010: “WWE Fatal Four Way 2010”: Uniondale, NY – 10,000
($650,000 live gate)
