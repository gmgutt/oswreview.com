13th June 2009: “ROH Manhattan Mayhem III”: New York, NY (Hammerstein Ballroom) – 1,450
Ric Flair was originally advertised to referee the Aries vs. Lynn vs. Black main event. However Flair refused to be involved in the match as clips were going to air on HDNet and WWE had expressed its desire that he not appear on TV for ROH. Flair ended up cutting up a promo on the show.
The show opened up with a ten-bell salute for Mitsuharu Misawa.
Nick and Matt Jackson beat Rhett Titus and Kenny King; Necro Butcher defeated Jimmy Rave; Roderick Strong bested Sonjay Dutt; Jimmy Jacobs beat Tyler Black in a “First Blood” match; Colt Cabana overcame Bryan Danielson, Claudio Castagnoli and D-Lo Brown in a four-way; ROH Tag Champions Eddie Edwards and Davey Richards toppled Kevin Steen and El Generico in a submission match; Jay Briscoe overturned Guido Maritato; Austin Aries rolled over ROH Champion Jerry Lynn and Tyler Black in a three-way elimination match to win the title. Nigel McGuinness was guest ringside enforcer.
