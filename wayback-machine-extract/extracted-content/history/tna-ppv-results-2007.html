TNA PPV Results 2007

TNA PPV RESULTS 2007

14th January 2007: “TNA Final Resolution 2007”: Orlando, FL – 900 
Dark match: Jason and Johnny Riggs beat Frankie Kazarian and Johnny Devine.
Pre-show: Lance Hoyt pinned Chase Stevens.
PPV Broadcast: Rhino dropped AJ Styles in a “last man standing” match; Chris Sabin overcame TNA X-Division Champion Christopher Daniels and Jerry Lynn in a three-way to win the title; Alex Shelley toppled Austin Starr; James Storm overturned Petey Williams; NWA World Tag Champions Homicide and Hernandez downed Team 3D by DQ; Kurt Angle (3) rolled over Samoa Joe (2) in a 30-minute Ironman match. Christian Cage bested NWA World Champion Abyss and Sting in a three-way elimination match to win the title.

11th February 2007: “TNA Against All Odds 2007”: Orlando, FL – 900 (0 paid)
Johnny Devine and Kazarian defeated Sonjay Dutt and Jay Lethal; NWA World Tag Champions Homicide and Hernandez downed Team 3D in a streetfight; Senshi pinned Austin Starr; Christy Hemme beat The Fat Oily Guy in a “Tuxedo streetfight”; Lance Hoyt overcame Dale Torborg; AJ Styles pinned Rhino in a chain match; TNA X-Division Champion Chris Sabin bested Jerry Lynn; James Storm and Jackie Moore overturned Petey Williams and Gail Kim; Sting rolled over Abyss in a “Prison Yard” match; NWA World Champion Christian Cage toppled Kurt Angle.

11th March 2007: “TNA Destination X 2007”: Orlando, FL – 900
NWA World Tag Champions Homicide and Hernandez downed Team 3D in a streetfight; James Storm and Jackie Moore defeated Petey Williams and Gail Kim in a “double bullrope” match; Senshi beat Austin Starr in a “chicken wing” match; BG and Kip James overcame Romeo Roselli and Antonio Thomas; TNA X-Division Champion Chris Sabin overturned Jerry Lynn in a “best of three falls” encounter; Rhino dropped AJ Styles in an “Elevation X” match; Kurt Angle bested Scott Steiner; Sting rolled over Abyss in a “Last Rites” match; NWA World Champion Christian Cage toppled Samoa Joe.

15th April 2007: “TNA Lockdown 2007”: St Joseph, MO – 6,000
All matches were held inside a steel cage.
Dark match:  Kip and BG James defeated Frankie Kazarian and Havoc.
PPV Broadcast: TNA X-Division Champion Chris Sabin overcame Shark Boy, Alex Shelley, Jay Lethal and Sonjay Dutt in an elimination cage match; Robert Roode toppled Petey Williams; Gail Kim overcame Jackie Moore; Senshi dropped Austin Starr. Bob Backlund was guest referee; James Storm bested Chris Harris at 9:05 in a blindfold match; Christopher Daniels pinned Jerry Lynn; Team 3D overturned NWA World Tag Champions Hernandez and Homicide in an “electrified” cage match to win the titles; Kurt Angle, Samoa Joe, Rhino, Jeff Jarrett and Sting rolled over NWA World Champion Christian Cage, Abyss, Travis Tomko, Scott Steiner and AJ Styles in “Lethal Lockdown”.

13th May 2007: “TNA Sacrifice 2007”: Orlando, FL – 900 (0 paid)
TNA X-Division Champion Chris Sabin overcame Sonjay Dutt and Jay Lethal in a three-way; Robert Roode pinned Jeff Jarrett; Christopher Daniels beat Rhino; Doug Basham and Damaja defeated Kip James in a handicap match; Chris Harris dropped James Storm in a “Texas Death” match; Jerry Lynn toppled Tiger Mask IV, Alex Shelley and Senshi in a four-way; NWA World Tag Champions Team 3D overturned Scott Steiner and Travis Tomko, and Homicide and Hernandez in a three-way; NWA World Champion Christian Cage battled Kurt Angle and Sting to a no-contest in a three-way.

17th June 2007: “TNA Slammiversary 2007”: Nashville, TN – 3,500
Dark match: Traci Brooks defeated Charley.
PPV Broadcast: Rhino and Senshi beat Hernandez and Homicide; Jay Lethal pinned TNA X Division Champion Chris Sabin to win the title; Frank Wycheck and Jerry Lynn downed James Storm and Ron Killings; Bob Backlund dropped Alex Shelley; BG and Kip James toppled Doug Basham and Damaja; Eric Young overcame Robert Roode; TNA Tag Team Champions Team 3D overturned Road Warrior Animal and Rick Steiner; Sting bested Christopher Daniels; Abyss toppled Tomko in a no-DQ match; Kurt Angle rolled over Samoa Joe, AJ Styles, Christian Cage and Chris Harris in a “King of the Mountain” match to win the vacant TNA World Title.

15th July 2007: “TNA Victory Road 2007”: Orlando, FL – 900 (0 paid)
Dark match: Havok and Martyr defeated Brute Issei and Akira Raijin.
PPV Broadcast: Christopher Daniels won an “Ultimate X” gauntlet match; BG and Kip James defeated Damaja and Doug Basham; James Storm pinned Rhino; Chris Sabin and Alex Shelley downed Bob Backlund and Jerry Lynn; Eric Young and Gail Kim dropped Robert Roode and Traci Brooks; Christian Cage bested Chris Harris; Sting and Abyss toppled AJ Styles and Tomko; TNA World Champion Kurt Angle and TNA X Division Champion Samoa Joe overcame TNA Word Tag Champions Team 3D. By virtue of the win, Samoa Joe (who pinned Ray) won the tag titles.

12th August 2007: “TNA Hardcore Justice 2007”: Orlando, FL – 900 (0 paid)
Jay Lethal and Sonjay Dutt defeated Christopher Daniels and Senshi, and Chris Sabin and Alex Shelley in a three-way; Frankie Kazarian beat Raven; James Storm pinned Rhino in a streetfight; Hernandez and Homicide downed BG and Kip James; Robert Roode toppled Eric Young; Chris Harris beat Black Reign by DQ; Rick and Scott Steiner overcame Team 3D; Sting, Abyss and Andrew Martin rolled over Christian Cage, Tomko and AJ Styles in a cage match; TNA World Champion Kurt Angle pinned TNA X Division Champion and TNA Tag Team Champion Samoa Joe to win both titles.

9th September 2007: “TNA No Surrender 2007”: Orlando, FL –  900 (0 paid)
Ron Killings and Adam Jones defeated TNA World Tag Champions Kurt Angle and Sting to win the titles; Rhino pinned James Storm; Robert Roode overcame Frankie Kazarian; Jay Lethal bested TNA X-Division Champion Kurt Angle to win the title; Chris Harris overturned Black Reign; AJ Styles and Alex Shelley co-won a 20-man tag team battle royal; AJ Styles and Tomko downed Alex Shelley and Chris Sabin; Christian Cage bested Samoa Joe by DQ; TNA World Champion Kurt Angle rolled over Abyss.

14th October 2007: “TNA Bound For Glory 2007”: Duluth, GA – 4,000 
Dark match: Chris Sabin and Alex Shelley beat Joey Matthews and Johnny Swinger.
PPV Broadcast: Homicide and Hernandez downed Elix Skipper and Senshi in an “Ultimate X” encounter; Eric Young won a reverse battle royal; AJ Styles and Tomko defeated TNA World Tag Champions Ron Killings and Consequences Creed (a permitted substitute for Adam Jones) to win the titles; TNA X Division Champion Jay Lethal pinned Christopher Daniels; Rick and Scott Steiner defeated Team 3D in a tables match; Gail Kim won a gauntlet match to become the first TNA Knockouts Champion; Samoa Joe downed Christian Cage; Abyss toppled Raven, Rhino and Black Reign in a four-way “Monster’s Ball” match; Sting rolled over TNA World Champion Kurt Angle to win the title.

11th November 2007: “TNA Genesis 2007”: Orlando, FL – 900 (0 paid)
Abyss downed Black Reign in a “Shop of Horrors” match; Alex Shelley and Chris Sabin overcame Team 3D; TNA Knockouts Champion Gail Kim defeated Angel Williams, ODB and Roxxi Laveaux in a four-way; TNA X Division Champion Jay Lethal pinned Sonjay Dutt; TNA World Tag Champions AJ Styles and Tomko bested Rick and Scott Steiner; Samoa Joe toppled Robert Roode; Frankie Kazarian dropped Christian Cage in a ladder match; TNA World Champion Kurt Angle and Kevin Nash rolled over Sting and Booker T. 

2nd December 2007: “TNA Turning Point 2007”: Orlando, FL – 900 (0 paid)
Johnny Devine and Team 3D overturned Jay Lethal, Alex Shelley and Chris Sabin in a tables match; Velvet Sky and Angelina Love beat ODB and Roxxi Levoux; Eric Young pinned James Storm; Scott Steiner, Senshi, BG James and Petey Williams defeated Chris Harris, Christopher Daniels, Elix Skipper, Hernandez, Homicide, Jimmy Rave, Kip James, Lance Hoyt, Shark Boy and Sonjay Dutt in a “Feast of Fired” battle royal; TNA Knockouts Champion Gail Kim dropped Awesome Kong by DQ; Abyss and Raven bested Black Reign and Rellik in a “10,000 Tacks” Match; Booker T and Frankie Kazarian bested Christian Cage and Robert Roode; Samoa Joe, Kevin Nash and Eric Young rolled over TNA World Champion Kurt Angle and TNA World Tag Champions AJ Styles and Tomko.
