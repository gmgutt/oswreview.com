WWE NXT Live Specials 2015

WWE NXT LIVE SPECIALS (2015)

11th February 2015: “NXT Takeover: Rival”: Winter Park, FL – 400 sellout
Shown live on WWE Network
Dark match: Colin Cassady and Enzo Amore beat Aiden English and Simon Gotch.
Live Broadcast: Hideo Itami pinned Tyler Breeze; Baron Corbin defeated Bull Dempsey in a no-DQ match; WWE NXT Tag Champions Wesley Blake and Buddy Murphy downed Kalisto and Sin Cara; Finn Balor bested Adrian Neville (WWE NXT Title #1 Contender Tournament – Final); Sasha Banks won a four-way over WWE NXT Women’s Champion Charlotte, Bayley and Becky Lynch to win the title; Kevin Owens rolled over WWE NXT Champion Sami Zayn by stoppage to win the title.


20th May 2015: “WWE NXT Takeover: Unstoppable”: Winter Park, FL – 400 sellout
Shown live on WWE Network
Dark match: Aiden English & Simon Gotch beat Scott Dawson & Dash Wilder.
WWE Network Live Broadcast: Finn Balor bested Tyler Breeze; Charlotte & Bayley beat Emma & Dana Brooke; Baron Corbin pinned Rhyno; WWE NXT Tag Champions Blake & Murphy downed Enzo Amore & Colin Cassady; WWE NXT Women’s Champion Sasha Banks rolled over Becky Lynch; WWE NXT Champion Kevin Owens battled to a no contest with Sami Zayn.


22nd August 2015: “WWE NXT Takeover: Brooklyn”: Brooklyn, NY – 13,589 sellout
Shown live on WWE Network
NXT: (08/26 – 90 minute special): Colin Cassady, Enzo Amore, Mojo Rawley & Zack Ryder beat Chad Gable, Dash Wilder, Jason Jordan & Scott Dawson; Eva Marie downed Carmella; Bull Dempsey beat Elias Samson; Emma defeated Becky Lynch, Charlotte and Dana Brooke in a four-way.
NXT Takeover: Brooklyn: (08/22): Jushin Liger overturned Tyler Breeze; Aiden English & Simon Gotch defeated WWE NXT Tag Champions Blake & Murphy to win the titles; Apollo Crews rolled over Tye Dillinger; Samoa Joe downed Baron Corbin; Bayley beat WWE NXT Women’s Champion Sasha Banks to win the title; WWE NXT Champion Finn Balor rolled over Kevin Owens in a ladder match.


7th October 2015: “WWE NXT Takeover: Respect”: Orlando, FL – 400 sellout
Shown live on WWE Network
Dark match: Bull Dempsey pinned Sawyer Fulton.
WWE Network Broadcast: Finn Balor and Samoa Joe dropped Scott Dawson and Dash Wilder (Dusty Rhodes Classic Tag Tournament – Semi-Final); Baron Corbin and Rhyno downed Jason Jordan and Chad Gable (Dusty Rhodes Classic Tag Tournament – Semi-Final); Asuka toppled Dana Brooke; Apollo Crews pinned Tyler Breeze; Samoa Joe and WWE NXT Champion Finn Balor bested Rhyno and Baron Corbin (Dusty Rhodes Classic Tag Tournament – Final); WWE NXT Women’s Champion Bayley defeated Sasha Banks in a 30 minute Iron Man match. 3:2.

16th December 2015: “NXT Takeover: London”: London, England (SSE Wembley Arena) – 7,000 sellout
Shown live on WWE Network
NXT: (12/23): Jason Jordan & Chad Gable won a four-way over Blake & Murphy, Mojo Rawley & Zack Ryder, and Aiden English and Simon Gotch; Elias Sampson downed Bull Dempsey.
NXT Takeover: London: (12/16): Asuka dropped Emma; WWE NXT Tag Champions Dash Wilder & Scott Dawson bested Enzo Amore & Colin Cassady; Baron Corbin pinned Apollo Crews; WWE NXT Women’s Champion Bayley overcame Nia Jax; WWE NXT Champion Finn Balor rolled over Samoa Joe.
NXT: (12/23): Sami Zayn defeated Tye Dillinger.
