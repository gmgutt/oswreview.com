History of WWF Light Heavyweight Title

HISTORY OF WWF LIGHT HEAVYWEIGHT TITLE

See also: History of J-Crown

See also: History of WCW/WWF/WWE Cruiserweight Title

NB: The Title was defended as part of the J-Crown between 08/05/1996 and 11/05/1997

NB: The title was created as part of a working relationship between the WWF and UWA. When the UWA folded in 1995, it was used by Michinoku Pro and NJPW briefly before forming part of the J-Crown. WWF, who owned the title, took back possession of the title in November 1997 to start their own Light Heavyweight Division.
