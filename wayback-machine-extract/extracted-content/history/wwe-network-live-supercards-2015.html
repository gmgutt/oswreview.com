WWE Network Live Supercards 2015

WWE NETWORK LIVE SUPERCARDS (2015)

See also: WWE PPV Results 2015

See also: WWE NXT Live Specials 2015


31st May 2015: “WWE Elimination Chamber 2015”: Corpus Christi, TX – 7,000 sellout
Shown live on WWE Network / Only shown on PPV in international markets
Kick-off: Stardust beat Zack Ryder.
WWE Network Special: WWE World Tag Champions Big E, Kofi Kingston & Xavier Woods won an “Elimination Chamber” match over Titus O’Neill & Darren Young, The Ascension, Kalisto & Sin Cara, Los Matadores, and Cesaro & Tyson Kidd; WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella won a three-way over Naomi and Paige; WWE NXT Champion Kevin Owens bested WWE US Champion John Cena in a non-title match; Neville overcame Bo Dallas; Ryback won the vacant WWE Intercontinental Title in an “Elimination Chamber” match over Dolph Ziggler, King Barrett, Mark Henry, R-Truth and Sheamus; Dean Ambrose rolled over WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins by DQ.


4th July 2015: “WWE Beast in the East”: Tokyo, Japan (Sumo Hall) – 6,500
Shown live on WWE Network
The show also aired as an iPPV in Japan for 1,000 yen
Matches airing only on iPPV in Japan: Cesaro beat Diego; The Lucha Dragons defeated Big E & Xavier Woods.
Beast in the East: (07/04): Chris Jericho toppled Neville; WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella won a three-way over Tamina Snuka & Paige; Brock Lesnar pinned Kofi Kingston; Finn Balor bested WWE NXT Champion Kevin Owens to win the title; John Cena & Dolph Zigger rolled over Kane & Wade Barrett.


3rd October 2015: “WWE Live from Madison Square Garden”: New York, NY (MSG) – 16,224 sellout
Shown live on WWE Network
Dark match: Zack Ryder defeated Bo Dallas; Mark Henry pinned Brad Maddox.
WWE Network Broadcast: Randy Orton and Dolph Ziggler overturned Sheamus and Rusev; Neville dropped Stardust; Nikki Bella, Brie Bella and Alicia Fox downed WWE Divas Champion Charlotte, Becky Lynch and Paige; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens toppled Chris Jericho; The Dudley Boys beat WWE World Tag Champions Big E and Kofi Kingston by DQ; Brock Lesnar bested The Big Show; WWE US Champion John Cena pinned WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins in a cage match. Only the US Title was on the line.
