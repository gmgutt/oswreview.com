WWE PPV Results 2006

WWE PPV RESULTS (2006)

See also: WWE PPV Statistics 2006

8th January 2006: “WWE New Year’s Revolution 2006”: Albany, NY – 11,000
($577,000 live gate)
Heat: (01/08): Chavo Guerrero Jr beat Gene Snitsky.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion Ric Flair defeated Edge by DQ; WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus downed Mickie James; Jerry Lawler dropped Gregory Helms; Triple H pinned The Big Show; Shelton Benjamin overcame Viscera; Ashley won a “bra-and-panties- gauntlet” match; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Shawn Michaels, Kurt Angle, Carlito, Chris Masters and Kane in an Elimination Chamber match; Edge pinned WWE World Champion John Cena to win the title.

29th January 2006: “WWE Royal Rumble 2006”: Miami, FL – 14,500 sellout
($800,000 live gate)
Heat: (01/29): Finlay beat Brian Kendrick.
PPV Broadcast: Gregory Helms defeated WWE Cruiserweight Champion Kid Kash, Sho Funaki, Jamie Noble, Paul London, and Nunzio to win the title in a six-way match; Mickie James downed Ashley. Trish Stratus was guest referee; The Boogeyman dropped John Bradshaw Layfield; Rey Mysterio won the 30-man Royal Rumble by last eliminating Randy Orton; John Cena toppled WWE World Champion Edge to win the title; World Heavyweight Champion Kurt Angle rolled over Mark Henry.

19th February 2006: “WWE No Way Out 2006”: Baltimore, MD – 11,000 sellout
($575,000 live gate)
Heat: (02/19): The Boogeyman defeated Simon Dean.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms overcame Scotty 2 Hotty, Psychosis, Nunzio, Super Crazy, Brian Kendrick, Paul London and Kid Kash in a nine-way match;  John Bradshaw Layfield dropped Bobby Lashley; Tatanka and Matt Hardy overturned WWE Tag Champions Joey Mercury and Johnny Nitro in a non-title match; Chris Benoit toppled WWE US Champion Booker T to win the title; Randy Orton bested Rey Mysterio; World Heavyweight Champion Kurt Angle rolled over The Undertaker.

2nd April 2006: “WWE Wrestlemania 22”: Chicago, IL – 14,300 sellout (13,600 paid)
($2,500,000 live gate)
Dark match: Viscera won a battle royal.
PPV Broadcast: WWE World Tag Champions Kane and The Big Show beat Carlito and Chris Masters; Rob Van Dam won a “Money in the Bank” ladder match over Matt Hardy, Finlay, Bobby Lashley, Ric Flair, and WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin; John Bradshaw Layfield pinned WWE US Champion Chris Benoit to win the title; Edge overcame Mick Foley in a hardcore match; The Boogeyman bested Booker T and Sharmell in a handicap match; Mickie James toppled WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus to win the title; The Undertaker rolled over Mark Henry in a casket match; Shawn Michaels toppled Vince McMahon in a “no holds barred” match; Rey Mysterio bested World Heavyweight Champion Kurt Angle and Randy Orton in a three-way to win the title; Torrie Wilson beat Candice Michelle in a “Playboy pillow fight”; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Triple H.

30th April 2006: “WWE Backlash 2006”: Lexington, KY – 14,000
($480,000 live gate)
Heat: (04/30): Goldust dropped Rob Conway.
PPV Broadcast: Carlito pinned Chris Masters; Umaga toppled Ric Flair; Trish Stratus toppled WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James by DQ; Rob Van Dam downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin to win the title; The Big Show battled Kane to a no-contest; Vince McMahon and Shane McMahon beat Shawn Michaels in a “no holds barred” handicap match; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Triple H and Edge in a no-DQ three-way.

21st May 2006: “WWE Judgment Day 2006”: Phoenix, AZ – 14,000 sellout (9,800 paid)
($560,000 live gate)
Dark match: Matt Hardy downed Simon Dean
PPV Broadcast: Brian Kendrick and Paul London beat WWE Tag Champions Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury to win the titles; Chris Benoit toppled Finlay; Jillian Hall overturned Melina; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms downed Super Crazy; Mark Henry downed Kurt Angle by count-out; Booker T pinned Bobby Lashley (KOTR 2006 – Final); The Great Khali overturned The Undertaker; World Heavyweight Champion Rey Mysterio bested WWE US Champion John Bradshaw Layfield.

11th June 2006: “ECW One Night Stand 2006”: New York, NY (Hammerstein Ballroom) – 2,460 sellout
($350,000 live gate)
PPV Broadcast: Tazz defeated Jerry Lawler; Kurt Angle bested Randy Orton; Little Guido and Tony Mamaluke downed Super Crazy and Tajiri; World Heavyweight Champion Rey Mysterio battled Sabu to a no-contest; Lita, Edge and Mick Foley downed Terry Funk, Tommy Dreamer, and Beulah McGillicutty; Balls Mahoney downed Masato Tanaka; Rob Van Dam downed WWE World Champion John Cena to win the title.

25th June 2006: “WWE Vengeance 2006”: Charlotte, NC – 6,800
($400,000 live gate)
Dark match: Val Venis toppled Rob Conway.
PPV Broadcast: Randy Orton beat Kurt Angle; Umaga toppled Eugene; Ric Flair bested Mick Foley in a “best of three falls” encounter; Johnny Nitro dropped WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin and Carlito in a three-way to win the title; WWE and ECW World Champion Rob Van Dam downed Edge; Imposter Kane (Drew Hankinson) bested Kane; John Cena rolled over Sabu in a lumberjack match; Shawn Michaels and Triple H toppled WWE World Tag Champions Kenny, Mitch, Nicky, Mikey, and Johnny in a non-title handicap match.

23rd July 2006: “WWE Great American Bash 2006”: Indianapolis, IN – 9,750 (8,750 paid)
($490,400 live gate)
Dark match: Sho Funaki beat Simon Dean.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Tag Champions Brian Kendrick and Paul London beat Kid Kash and Jamie Noble; WWE US Champion Finlay downed William Regal; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms downed Matt Hardy; The Undertaker rolled over ECW World Champion The Big Show in a non-title “Punjabi Prison” match; Ashley downed Kristal, Michelle McCool, and Jillian Hall in a four-way “bra and panties” match; Mr Kennedy toppled Batista by DQ; King Booker rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Rey Mysterio to win the title.

20th August 2006: “WWE Summerslam 2006”: Boston, MA – 16,168 sellout (13,500 paid)
(800,000 live gate)
Dark match: Carlito pinned Rob Conway.
PPV Broadcast: Chavo Guerrero Jr beat Rey Mysterio; ECW World Champion The Big Show beat Sabu in an “extreme rules” match; Hulk Hogan beat Randy Orton; Ric Flair bested Mick Foley in an “I Quit” match; Batista dropped World Heavyweight Champion King Booker by DQ; Shawn Michaels and Triple H toppled Vince and Shane McMahon; WWE World Champion Edge rolled over John Cena.

17th September 2006: “WWE Unforgiven 2006”: Toronto, ONT – 16,105 sellout (15,000 paid)
($800,000 live gate)
Dark match: Super Crazy downed Shelton Benjamin.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion Johnny Nitro pinned Jeff Hardy; Kane battled Umaga to a double count-out; WWE World Tag Champions Mikey and Kenny  defeated Robbie and Rory McAllister; Shawn Michaels and Triple H toppled ECW World Champion The Big Show, Vince and Shane McMahon in a handicap “Hell in a Cell” match; Trish Stratus toppled WWE Women’s Champion Lita to win the title; Randy Orton beat Carlito; John Cena rolled over WWE World Champion Edge in a TLC encounter to win the title.

8th October 2006: “WWE No Mercy 2006”: Raleigh, NC – 9,000
($400,000 live gate)
Dark match: Jimmy Wang Yang beat Sylvan.
PPV Broadcast: Matt Hardy downed WWE Cruiserwight Champion Gregory Helms in a non-title match; WWE Tag Champions Paul London and Brian Kendrick defeated KC James and Idol Stevens; MVP pinned Marty Garner; WWE US Champion Mr Kennedy downed The Undertaker by DQ; Rey Mysterio bested Chavo Guerrero Jr in a “falls count anywhere” match; Chris Benoit toppled William Regal; World Heavyweight Champion King Booker rolled over Batista, Bobby Lashley, and Finlay in a four-way.

5th November 2006: “WWE Cyber Sunday 2006”: Cincinatti, OH – 7,000
($400,000 live gate)
Dark match: Super Crazy downed Rob Conway.
PPV Broadcast: Umaga bested Kane; Cryme Tyme overcame Robbie and Rory McAllister, Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch, and Viscera and Charlie Haas in a four-way  “Texas Tornado” match; WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Carlito; Edge and Randy Orton beat Triple H and Shawn Michaels. Eric Bischoff was guest referee; Lita dropped Mickie James in a lumberjill match to win the vacant WWE Women’s Title (WWE Women’s Title Tournament – Final); Ric Flair and Roddy Piper overturned WWE World Tag Champions Kenny and Mikey to win the titles; World Heavyweight Champion King Booker rolled over WWE World Champion John Cena and ECW World Champion The Big Show in a no-DQ, three-way match.

 

26th November 2006: “WWE Survivor Series 2006”: Philadelphia, PA  – 15,400 sellout (14,400 paid)
($931,000 live gate)
Dark match: Carlito pinned Charlie Haas.
PPV Broadcast: Ric Flair, Ron Simmons, Dusty Rhodes, and Sgt. Slaughter beat Kenny, Mikey, Nicky, and Johnny in a “Survivor Series” elimination match;  WWE US Champion Chris Benoit toppled Chavo Guerrero Jr; Mickie James toppled WWE Women’s Champion Lita to win the title; Triple H, Shawn Michaels, CM Punk, Matt Hardy, and WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed WWE World Tag Champions Edge and Randy Orton, Mike Knox, WWE Crusierweight Champion Gregory Helms, and Johnny Nitro in a “Survivor Series” elimination match; Mr Kennedy downed The Undertaker in a first blood match; WWE World Champion John Cena, Kane, Bobby Lashley, Rob Van Dam, and Sabu rolled over ECW World Champion The Big Show, Umaga, Finlay, MVP, and Test in a “Survivor Series” elimination match; Batista dropped World Heavyweight Champion King Booker to win the title.

3rd December 2006: “WWE/ECW December to Dismember 2006”: Augusta, GA – 4,800 (3,600 paid)
Dark match: Stevie Richards toppled Rene Dupree.
PPV Broadcast:  Matt Hardy and WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury; Balls Mahoney dropped Matt Striker; Sylvester Terkay and Elijah Burke overcame Little Guido and Tony Mamaluke; Daivari overturned Tommy Dreamer; Kevin Thorn and Ariel overturned Mike Knox and Kelly Kelly; Bobby Lashley dropped ECW World Champion The Big Show, Rob Van Dam, Test, CM Punk, and Hardcore Holly in an “Extreme Elimination Chamber” match to win the title.

17th December 2006: “WWE Armaggedon 2006”: Richmond, VA – 8,200 (7,100 paid)
($423,500 live gate)
Dark match: Oleg Prudius toppled Scotty 2 Hotty.
PPV Broadcast: Kane overturned MVP in an Inferno match; WWE Tag Champions Paul London and Brian Kendrick defeated Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury, WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy and Matt Hardy, and William Regal and Dave Taylor in a four-way ladder match; The Boogeyman bested The Miz; WWE US Champion Chris Benoit toppled Chavo Guerrero Jr; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms downed Jimmy Wang Yang; The Undertaker rolled over Mr Kennedy in a “Last Ride” match; WWE World Champion John Cena and World Heavyweight Champion Batista dropped King Booker and Finlay.
