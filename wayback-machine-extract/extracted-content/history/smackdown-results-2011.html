Smackdown Results 2011

WWE SMACKDOWN RESULTS (2011)

Jan 2011

Air Date: 7th January 2011
World Heavyweight Champion Edge overturned Kane in a Last Man Standing match; Kofi Kingston toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler to win the title; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston pinned Dolph Ziggler; Dolph Ziggler overcame Drew McIntyre, The Big Show and Cody Rhodes in a Fatal Four Way; Michelle McCool downed Kelly Kelly; Alberto Del Rio bested Rey Mysterio in a best-of-three-falls encounter.

Air Date: 14th January 2011
Rey Mysterio  and R-Truth bested  Alberto Del Rio and Cody Rhodes; Trent Barreta pinned Drew McIntyre; Wade Barrett battled The Big Show to a no-contest; Beth Phoenix downed Michelle McCool; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston dropped Jack Swagger in a non-title match.

Air Date: 21st January 2011
Alberto Del Rio toppled R-Truth; Beth Phoenix pinned Layla; Rey Mysterio bested Cody Rhodes; Jack Swagger overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston in a non-title 3-minute amateur wrestling challenge; Drew McIntyre downed Trent Barretta; Justin Gabriel rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Edge in a non-title match.

Air Date: 28th January 2011
Michelle McCool and Layla dropped Kelly Kelly and Kaitlyn; Drew McIntyre pinned JTG; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston won a battle royal; Rey Mysterio and WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston downed Alberto Del Rio and Kane; The Big Show downed Heath Slater; Randy Orton and World Heavyweight Champion Edge toppled WWE World Champion The Miz and Dolph Ziggler.

Feb 2011

Air Date: 4th February 2011
Heath Slater and Justin Gabriel defeated WWE Tag Team Champions Santino Marella and Vladimir Kozlov in a non-title match; Drew McIntyre pinned WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston in a non-title match; Rey Mysterio toppled Jack Swagger; Kane crushed Chavo Guerrero Jr; Wade Barrett toppled The Big Show; World Heavyweight Champion Edge and Kelly Kelly defeated Dolph Ziggler, Michelle McCool and Layla in a handicap match where the World Heavyweight Title was at stake.

Air Date: 11th February 2011
Alberto Del Rio rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston in a non-title match; Justin Gabriel overcame Vladimir Kozlov; Drew McIntyre pinned Chris Masters; Wade Barrett bested Rey Mysterio; Kane downed JTG; WWE Divas Champion Eve dropped Layla in a non-title match; World Heavyweight Champion Edge toppled Dolph Ziggler. Vickie Guerrero was guest referee.

Air Date: 18th February 2011
John Cena, World Heavyweight Champion Edge, Randy Orton, Rey Mysterio Jr, John Morrison and R-Truth defeated CM Punk, Dolph Ziggler, Kane, Drew McIntyre, Wade Barrett, & Sheamus; Maryse and Layla overcame WWE Divas Champion Eve and Beth Phoenix; WWE World Champion The Miz toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston in a non-title match; WWE World Tag Champions Santino Marella and Vladimir Lozlov downed Heath Slater and Justin Gabriel by DQ; Edge rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Dolph Ziggler to win the title.

Air Date: 25th February 2011
Rey Mysterio bested Kane; Rosa Mendes beat Layla by DQ; Jack Swagger toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston in a non-title match; The Big Show pinned Wade Barrett; World Heavyweight Champion Edge and Kelly Kelly rolled over Drew McIntyre and Vickie Guerrero.

Mar 2011

Air Date: 4th March 2011
World Heavyweight Champion Edge toppled Drew McIntyre; WWE World Tag Champions Heath Slater and Justin Gabriel overcame Santino Marella and Vladimir Kozlov; Michelle McCool and Layla beat Beth Phoenix and Rosa Mendes; The Big Show downed Kane by DQ; Jack Swagger defeated JTG.

Air Date: 11th March 2011
Kane dropped Wade Barrett by DQ; Kane and The Big Show overcame Wade Barrett and Heath Slater by Q; Cody Rhodes overturned JTG; Layla pinned Kaitlyn; Rey Mysterio toppled Drew McIntyre; World Heavyweight Champion Edge and Christian rolled over Alberto Del Rio and Brodus Clay.

Air Date: 18th March 2011
World Heavyweight Champion Edge pinned Brodus Clay; WWE US Champion Sheamus toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston in a non-title match; Cody Rhodes beat Trent Barretta; Layla overturned Kelly Kelly; The Big Show and Kane dropped WWE World Tag Champions Heath Slater and Justin Gabriel by DQ; Jack Swagger downed Chris Masters; Rey Mysterio defeated Ted Dibiase Jr; Christian rolled over Alberto Del Rio in a cage match.

Air Date: 25th March 2011
Rey Mysterio defeated CM Punk by DQ; World Heavyweight Champion Edge dropped Drew McIntyre; Michelle McCool and Layla overcame Kelly Kelly and Rosa Mendes; Wade Barrett toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston to win the title; Jack Swagger overturned R-Truth; Christian rolled over Alberto Del Rio.

Apr 2011

Air Date: 1st April 2011
Kofi Kingston toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett by DQ; Cody Rhodes pinned Chris Masters.

Air Date: 8th April 2011
Kofi Kingston, The Big Show, Kane and Santino Marella downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett, Ezekiel Jackson and WWE World Tag Champions Heath Slater and Justin Gabriel in a best-of-three-falls encounter; Cody Rhodes pinned Trent Barretta; Kelly Kelly and Beth Phoenix dropped Michelle McCool and Layla; Alberto Del Rio toppled Christian.

Air Date: 15th April 2011
Ezekiel Jackson bested Kofi Kingston; Rey Mysterio toppled Drew McIntyre; Kelly Kelly dropped Layla; Christian won a 20 man battle royal.

Air Date: 22nd April 2011
Rey Mysterio pinned Cody Rhodes; Jack Swagger downed Trent Barretta; The Big Show and Kane dropped WWE World Tag Champions Heath Slater and Justin Gabriel to win the titles; Drew McIntyre bested Chris Masters; WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett rolled over Kofi Kingston.

Air Date: 29th April 2011
WWE US Champion Sheamus battled Kofi Kingston to a no-contest; Sin Cara bested Jack Swagger; WWE World Tag Champions The Big Show and Kane downed Justin Gabriel and Heath Slater; Rey Mysterio bested Mark Henry by DQ; Michelle McCool battled Layla to a double count-out; Christian and Randy Orton rolled over Alberto Del Rio and Brodus Clay.

May 2011

Air Date: 6th May 2011
Sheamus pinned Daniel Bryan; Ezekiel Jackson overcame The Big Show; Layla downed Alicia Fox; Sin Cara bested Tyson Kidd; Randy Orton rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Christian to win the title.

Air Date: 13th May 2011
Sin Cara pinned Daniel Bryan; Kane toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett by DQ; Cody Rhodes downed Ted Dibiase Jr; World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton and Christian rolled over Sheamus and Mark Henry.

Air Date: 20th May 2011
Christian bested Sheamus by DQ; Ted DiBiase Jr defeated Trent Barretta; WWE World Tag Champions Kane and The Big Show, and Ezekiel Jackson overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett, Justin Gabriel and Heath Slater; WWE Divas Champion Brie Bella downed Natalya Neidhart; Daniel Bryan dropped Chavo Guerrero Jr; World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton rolled over Mark Henry by DQ.

Air Date: 27th May 2011
Sin Cara defeated Chavo Guerrero; Daniel Bryan downed Cody Rhodes; Ezekiel Jackson dropped Heath Slater by DQ; Kane overcame The Great Khali in a “Monsters Brawl” encounter; Alicia Fox and Tamina overturned AJ and Kaitlyn; Sheamus bested Christian and Mark Henry in a three-way.

Jun 2011

Air Date: 3rd June 2011
Christian overcame Mark Henry; Alicia Fox and Tamina defeated AJ and Kaitlyn; Jinder Mahal downed Yoshi Tatsu; Ezekiel Jackson dropped WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett by count-out; Daniel Bryan bested Ted Dibiase Jr; World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton rolled over Sheamus. Christian was guest referee.

Air Date: 10th June 2011
Ezekiel Jackson, and Jimmy and Jey Uso overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett, Heath Slater and Justin Gabriel; Jinder Mahal dropped Trent Baretta; AJ beat Tamina; Sin Cara overturned Ted Dibiase Jr by DQ; Sin Cara and Daniel Bryan toppled Cody Rhodes and Ted Dibiase Jr; Sheamus rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton in a non-title, no-DQ encounter.

Air Date: 17th June 2011
Daniel Bryan, Sin Cara and Ezekiel Jackson downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett, Ted Dibiase Jr and Cody Rhodes; Vladimir Kozlov dropped Jinder Mahal; Tamina, Rosa Mendes and Alicia Fox defeated Natalya, AJ and Kaitlyn; Jimmy and Jey Uso bested Heath Slater and Justin Gabriel; Christian rolled over Sheamus.

Air Date: 24th June 2011
Sin Cara pinned Ted Dibiase Jr; Cody Rhodes downed Daniel Bryan; WWE Intercontinental Champion Ezekiel Jackson bested Wade Barrett; Heath Slater and Justin Gabriel overturned Jimmy and Jey Uso; Kane toppled Christian by DQ; Christian and Mark Henry rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton and Kane.

Jul 2011

Air Date: 1st July 2011
World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton bested Mark Henry by count-out; Daniel Bryan toppled Ted Dibiase Jr; Christian pinned Sin Cara; Cody Rhodes overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion Ezekiel Jackson in a non-title match; Kane pinned Wade Barrett.

Air Date: 8th July 2011
Cody Rhodes and Ted Dibiase Jr overcame Daniel Bryan and Ezekiel Jackson; Jinder Mahal beat Trent Barretta; AJ defeated Tamina; Sin Cara pinned Tyson Kidd; Jimmy and Jey Uso overturned Heath Slater and Justin Gabriel; World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton battled Sheamus to a double-DQ.

Air Date: 15th July 2011
WWE Intercontinental Champion Ezekiel Jackson overcame Ted Dibiase Jr; Cody Rhodes beat Daniel Bryan; Sin Cara downed Sheamus; WWE Divas Champion Kelly Kelly bested Rosa Mendes; Justin Gabriel toppled Heath Slater; Kane rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton by count-out.

Air Date: 22nd July 2011
World Heavyweight Champion Christian bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Ezekiel Jackson; Daniel Bryan dropped Heath Slater; Sheamus battled Wade Barrett to a double count-out; Randy Orton rolled over Kane in a streetfight.

Air Date: 29th July 2011
World Heavyweight Champion Christian defeated John Morrison; Alicia Fox, Tamina and Rosa Mendes downed AJ, Kaitlyn and Natalya; Mark Henry squashed Bobby Howard; WWE World Tag Champions Michael McGillicutty and David Otunga overcame Jimmy and Jey Uso; Ted Dibiase Jr and Cody Rhodes bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Ezekiel Jackson in a non-title handicap match; R-Truth rolled over Randy Orton by DQ.

Aug 2011

Air Date: 5th August 2011
Sheamus overcame The Great Khali; Natalya beat AJ; Daniel Bryan bested Tyson Kidd; WWE Intercontinental Champion Ezekiel Jackson defeated Zack Ryder; Mark Henry downed Vladimir Kozlov; JTG and WWE World Tag Champions David Otunga and Michael McGillicutty dropped Jimmy Uso, Jey Uso and Trent Baretta; World Heavyweight Champion Christian and R-Truth rolled over Randy Orton and John Morrison.

Air Date: 12th August 2011
Sheamus beat World Heavyweight Champion Christian by count-out; Beth Phoenix and Natalya defeated AJ and Kaitlyn; Imposter Sin Cara (Negro) downed Tyson Kidd; Alberto Del Rio bested Daniel Bryan; Cody Rhodes overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion Ezekiel Jackson to win the title; Mark Henry squashed Johnny Curtis; Randy Orton pinned The Great Khali.

Air Date: 19th August 2011
WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes beat Ezekiel Jackson; Justin Gabriel defeated Tyson Kidd; WWE World Champion Alberto Del Rio downed Daniel Bryan; WWE Divas Champion Kelly Kelly and AJ bested Natalya and Alicia Fox; Mark Henry won a battle royal.

Air Date: 26th August 2011
Christian beat Daniel Bryan; Imposter Sin Cara (Negro) downed Heath Slater (The match was taped three times); World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton bested Ted Dibiase Jr; WWE Divas champion Kelly Kelly beat Tamina; Ezekiel Jackson defeated The Great Khali; Sheamus toppled Mark Henry by count-out.

Air Date: 30th August 2011
John Cena beat Wade Barrett; Imposter Sin Cara (Negro) defeated Daniel Bryan; Beth Phoenix and Natalya downed WWE Divas Champion Kelly Kelly and Alicia Fox; Sheamus overturned The Great Khali; World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton rolled over Christian in a cage match.

Sep 2011

Air Date: 9th September 2011
Mark Henry beat Ezekiel Jackson; Imposter Sin Cara (Negro) defeated Tyson Kidd; WWE Divas Champion Kelly Kelly downed Natalya; Sheamus bested Wade Barrett by DQ; WWE World Tag Champions Kofi Kingston and Evan Bourne overcame The Great Khali and Jinder Mahal; World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes in a non-title match.

Air Date: 16th September 2011
Daniel Bryan bested Imposter Sin Cara (Negro) by DQ; Beth Phoenix overcame AJ; Sheamus and Justin Gabriel bested Wade Barrett and Christian; The Great Khali toppled Heath Slater by DQ; R-Truth rolled over Evan Bourne.

Air Date: 23rd September 2011
Sheamus pinned Heath Slater; Wade Barrett defeated Justin Gabriel; Beth Phoenix and Natalya toppled AJ and Kaitlyn; WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes bested Randy Orton by DQ; The Great Khali overcame Jinder Mahal; Imposter Sin Cara (Negro) dropped Daniel Bryan (Sin Cara had started the match but was eliminated by the Imposter); WWE World Tag Champions Evan Bourne and Kofi Kingston overturned Jimmy and Jey Uso; World Heavyweight Champion Mark Henry rolled over Christian in a lumberjack match.

Air Date: 30th September 2011
World Heavyweight Champion Mark Henry defeated The Great Khali; Jack Swagger downed Evan Bourne; Sin Cara (Azul) bested Heath Slater; WWE Divas Champion Kelly Kelly overcame Natalya; Sheamus overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes by DQ; Zack Ryder dropped JTG; Randy Orton battled Christian to a double count-out.

Oct 2011

Air Date: 7th October 2011
WWE World Champion Alberto Del Rio defeated Sin Cara (Azul); WWE US Champion Dolph Ziggler and Jack Swagger overcame WWE World Tag Champions Evan Bourne and Kofi Kingston in a non-title match; WWE Divas Champion Beth Phoenix bested Alicia Fox; Ezekiel Jackson overturned Jinder Mahal; Randy Orton and Sheamus rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes and Sheamus.

Air Date: 14th October 2011
Randy Orton won a 41 man battle royal; WWE Divas Champion Beth Phoenix defeated Kelly Kelly; Imposter Sin Cara (Negro) downed Justin Gabriel; WWE World Champion Alberto Del Rio bested Sheamus; Kofi Kingston and Zack Ryder toppled Dolph Ziggler and Jack Swagger; Randy Orton rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Mark Henry by DQ.

Air Date: 21st October 2011
Mark Henry defeated John Morrison; Wade Barrett downed Daniel Bryan; Sheamus and Zack Ryder dropped Christian and WWE US Champion Dolph Ziggler; Sin Cara (Azul) bested Imposter Sin Cara (Negro) in a “Mask vs. Mask” match; The Big Show bested WWE World Champion Alberto Del Rio by DQ.

Air Date: 28th October 2011
The Miz and R-Truth bested WWE World Tag Champions Kofi Kingston and Evan Bourne in a non-title match; Wade Barrett defeated Trent Baretta; Randy Orton bested WWE US Champion Dolph Ziggler in a non-title match; Daniel Bryan overcame Tyson Kidd; CM Punk rolled over Christian.

Nov 2011

Air Date: 4th November 2011
Randy Orton bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes in a non-title streetfight; Ted Dibiase Jr defeated Tyson Kidd; Alicia Fox overcame Natalya; Wade Barrett downed Sheamus; Sin Cara toppled Epico (Orlando Colon) by DQ; World Heavyweight Champion Mark Henry rolled over Daniel Bryan by DQ.

Air Date: 11th November 2011
Sheamus bested Christian; Ted Dibiase Jr defeated Jinder Mahal; World Heavyweight Champion Mark Henry overturned Daniel Bryan; The Big Show squashed Andy Baker, Tom LaRuffa and Zak Zodiac in a handicap match; Alicia Fox bested Tamina; Hunico and Epico defeated Jimmy and Jey Uso; Wade Barrett dropped Randy Orton.

Air Date: 18th November 2011
WWE US Champion Dolph Ziggler bested Sin Cara; Mason Ryan defeated Jack Swagger; Ted Dibiase Jr downed Derrick Bateman; Kofi Kingston beat Hunico; WWE Divas Champion Beth Phoenix and Natalya overcame AJ and Kaitlyn; Randy Orton and Sheamus toppled Wade Barrett and WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes by DQ.

Air Date: 25th November 2011
Daniel Bryan bested World Heavyweight Champion Mark Henry to win the title (the result was later overturned as Henry had not been “medically cleared” to wrestle); Hunico defeated Justin Gabriel; WWE Divas Champion Beth Phoenix and Natalya downed AJ and Kaitlyn; Sheamus and Zack Ryder dropped Dolph Ziggler and Jack Swagger; Ted Dibiase Jr toppled Heath Slater; Daniel Bryan overturned Randy Orton, WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes and Wade Barrett in a four-way.

Air Date: 29th November 2011
Brie Bella won a “mistletoe-on-a- pole match over AJ, Alicia Fox, Kaitlyn, Natalya, Aksana, Tamina, Rosa Mendes and Nikki Bella; Justin Gabriel beat Jinder Mahal; Kofi Kingston defeated Tyson Kidd; Randy Orton bested David Otunga; Hornswoggle won a battle Royal; World Heavyweight Champion Mark Henry toppled Daniel Bryan in a cage match.

Dec 2011

Air Date: 9th December 2011
Wade Barrett beat Ezekiel Jackson; WWE Divas Champion Beth Phoenix and Natalya defeated AJ and Kaitlyn; Zack Ryder defeated Heath Slater; Ted Dibiase Jr bested Jinder Mahal; Sheamus pinned David Otunga; WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes toppled Daniel Bryan by DQ; Randy Orton rolled over WWE US Champion Dolph Ziggler in a non-title match.

Air Date: 16th December 2011
WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes bested Daniel Bryan; Alicia Fox defeated Natalya; Epico and Primo downed Jimmy and Jey Uso; The Big Show pinned Jack Swagger; Ted Dibiase Jr downed Heath Slater; Sheamus dropped Jinder Mahal; Randy Orton and Zack Ryder bested WWE US Champion Dolph Ziggler and Wade Barrett.

Air Date: 23rd December 2011
WWE US Champion Zack Ryder pinned WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes in a non-title match; Sheamus overturned The Miz; Kofi Kingston bested Primo; World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan battled Dolph Ziggler to a no-contest; The Big Show and World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan toppled Dolph Ziggler and Jack Swagger.

Air Date: 30th December 2011
Jinder Mahal bested Ted Dibiase Jr; Kaitlyn and Alicia Fox overturned Natalya and Tamina; Hunico overcame Justin Gabriel; The Big Show pinned David Otunga; Ezekiel Jackson dropped Drew McIntyre; Sheamus downed Heath Slater; Randy Orton battled Wade Barrett to a no-contest in a “falls-count-anywhere” encounter.
