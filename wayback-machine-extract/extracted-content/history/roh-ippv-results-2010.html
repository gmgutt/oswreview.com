ROH iPPV Results 2010

ROH iPPV RESULTS 2010

3rd April 2010: “ROH The Big Bang 2010: Charlotte, NC – 800
1,250 iPPV buys
Shown on ippv (GoFightLive.tv).
Dark match: Grizzly Redwood, Harlem Bravado and Lance Bravado overcame McKnight, Ernesto Osiris and Sabre.
iPPV Broadcast: Phil Shatter beat Zack Salvation; Davey Richards downed Kenny King; Necro Butcher bested Erick Stevens in an “anything-goes” encounter; Cassandro dropped Rhett Titus; Colt Cabana and El Generico overturned Steve Corino and Kevin Steen by DQ; Chris Hero and Claudio Castagnoli bested ROH Tag Champions Mark and Jay Briscoe to win the titles; ROH Champion Tyler Black rolled over Austin Aries and Roderick Strong in a three-way; Blue Demon Jr and Magno defeated Super Parka and Misterioso.

19th June 2010: “ROH Death Before Dishonor VIII”: Toronto, ONT – 900
1,300 iPPV Buys
Shown on ippv (GoFightLive.tv).
Dark matches: Harlem Bravado and Lance Bravado beat Mike Sydal and Sebastian Suave; Bobby Dempsey and Grizzly Redwood defeated Ernesto Osiris and Rip Impact.
iPPV Broadcast: Kevin Steen downed El Generico; Kenny King and Rhett Titus dropped Cheech and Cloudy; Delirious overcame Austin Aries by DQ; Roderick Strong won a gauntlet match over Colt Cabana, Steve Corino, Shawn Daivari, Eddie Edwards and Tyson Dux; Christopher Daniels pinned Kenny Omega; ROH Tag Champions Chris Hero and Claudio Castagnoli overturned Mark and Jay Briscoe in a no-DQ match; ROH Champion Tyler Black rolled over Davey Richards.

11th September 2010: “ROH Glory By Honor IX”: New York, NY (Manhattan Center) – 1,300 sellout
1,400 iPPV Buys
Shown on ippv (GoFightLive.tv).
Kenny King beat Jay Briscoe; Mark Briscoe defeated Rhett Titus; Erick Stevens and Necro Butcher overturned Balls Mahoney and Grizzly Redwood; Colt Cabana and El Generico downed Kevin Steen and Steve Corino in a “Double Chain” match; ROH TV Champion Eddie Edwards bested Shawn Daivari; Christopher Daniels overturned Austin Aries; ROH Tag Champions Chris Hero and Claudio Castagnoli toppled Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin; Roderick Strong rolled over ROH Champion Tyler Black to win the title in a no-DQ encounter. Terry Funk was guest ringside enforcer.

18th December 2010: “ROH Final Battle 2010”: New York, NY (Manhattan Center) – 1,300 sellout
1,900 iPPV buys
Shown on ippv (GoFightLive.tv).
Kenny King and Rhett Titus downed Adam Cole and Kyle O’Reilly; Colt Cabana defeated TJ Perkins; Sara Del Rey and Serena Deeb overcame Awesome Kong and Daizee Haze; Eddie Edwards beat Sonjay Dutt; Homicide overturned ROH TV Champion Christopher Daniels in a non-title match; Jay Briscoe, Mark Briscoe and Mike Briscoe toppled Shane Hagadorn and ROH Tag Champions Chris Hero and Claudio Castagnoli; ROH Champion Roderick Strong bested Davey Richards; El Generico rolled over Kevin Steen in a “Career vs. Mask” streetfight.
