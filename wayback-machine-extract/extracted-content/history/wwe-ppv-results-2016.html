WWE PPV Results 2016

WWE PPV RESULTS (2016)

See also: WWE Network Statistics

24th January 2016: “WWE Royal Rumble 2016”: Orlando, FL – 12,000 sellout
Shown on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Jack Swagger and Mark Henry won a four-way over The Dudley Boys, The Ascension, and Darren Young and Damien Sandow.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion Dean Ambrose defeated Kevin Owens in a “last man standing” match; WWE World Tag Champions Big E and Kofi Kingston downed Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso; Kalisto bested WWE US Champion Alberto Del Rio to win the title; WWE Divas Champion Charlotte toppled Becky Lynch; Triple H won the 30-man Royal Rumble, last eliminating Dean Ambrose. By virtue of his victory, Triple H was awarded the WWE World Heavyweight Title.

21st February 2016: “WWE Fast Lane 2016”: Cleveland, OH – 14,406
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: WWE US Champion Kaliston defeated Alberto Del Rio in a “best-of-three-falls” match.
PPV Broadcast: Becky Lynch and Sasha Banks overcame Tamina and Naomi; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens bested Dolph Ziggler; The Big Show, Kane and Ryback overturned Luke Harper, Erick Rowan and Braun Strowman; WWE Divas Champion Charlotte downed Brie Bella; AJ Styles toppled Chris Jericho; Curtis Axel pinned R-Truth; Roman Reigns won a three-way over Brock Lesnar and Dean Ambrose.

3rd April 2016: “WWE Wrestlemania 32”: Dallas, TX – 93,730 sellout
($17,000,000 live gate)
($4,550,000 merchandise sales)
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: WWE US Champion Kalisto pinned Ryback; Brie Bella, Paige, Natalya, Alicia Fox and Eva Marie defeated Lana, Naomi, Tamina, Emma and Summer Rae; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso downed The Dudley Boys.
PPV Broadcast: Zack Ryder defeated WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens, Sami Zayn, Dolph Ziggler, Stardust, The Miz and Sin Cara in a ladder match to win the title; Chris Jericho pinned AJ Styles; Sheamus, Rusev and Alberto Del Rio dropped Big E, Kofi Kingston and Xavier Woods; Brock Lesnar bested Dean Ambrose in a “no-holds-barred” streetfight; WWE Divas Champion Charlotte won a three-way over Sasha Banks and Becky Lynch to win the WWE Women’s Title; The Undertaker defeated Shane McMahon in a “Hell in a Cell” match; Baron Corbin won the 20-man “Andre The Giant Memorial” battle royal; The Rock toppled Erick Rowan; Roman Reigns rolled over WWE World Heavyweight Champion Triple H to win the title.

1st May 2016: “WWE Payback 2016”: Chicago, IL – 12,540 sellout
($1,081,970 live gate)
Shown on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Dolph Ziggler rolled over Baron Corbin; WWE US Champion Kalisto defeated Ryback.
PPV Broadcast: Aiden English and Simon Gotch battled Enzo Amore and Colin Cassady to a no-contest (WWE Tag Title #1 Contender Tournament – Final); Kevin Owens overturned Sami Zayn; WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz defeated Cesaro; Dean Ambrose downed Chris Jericho; WWE Women’s Champion Charlotte defeated Natalya; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Roman Reigns defeated AJ Styles.

22nd May 2016: “WWE Extreme Rules 2016”: Newark, NJ – 12,576 sellout
($1,058,400 live gate)
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show:  Baron Corbin overcame Dolph Ziggler in a no-DQ match.
PPV Broadcast: Luke Gallows and Karl Anderson overcame Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso in a Texas Tornado match; Rusev defeated WWE US Champion Kalisto to win the title; WWE Tag Champions Big E and Xavier Woods defeated Simon Gotch and Aiden English; WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz defeated Cesaro, Kevin Owens and Sami Zayn in a four-way; Dean Ambrose downed Chris Jericho in an Asylum match; WWE Women’s Champion Charlotte defeated Natalya in a submission match; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Roman Reigns defeated AJ Styles in an “Extreme Rules” match.

19th June 2016: “WWE Money in the Bank 2016”: Las Vegas, NV – 12,000
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Goldust and R-Truth defeated Tyler Breeze and Fandango; Kalisto and Sin Cara beat Bubba Ray Dudley and D-Von Dudley.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Tag Champions Kofi Kingston and Big E defeated Simon Gotch and Aiden English, Big Cass and Enzo Amore, and Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows in a four-way; Baron Corbin overcame Dolph Ziggler; Dana Brooke and WWE Women’s Champion Charlotte downed Natalya and Becky Lynch; Apollo Crews overturned Sheamus; AJ Styles overturned John Cena; Dean Ambrose won the “Money in the Bank” ladder match over Alberto del Río, Cesaro, Chris Jericho, Kevin Owens and Sami Zayn; WWE US Champion Rusev defeated Titus O’Neil; Seth Rollins overturned WWE World Heavyweight Champion Roman Reigns to win the title; Dean Ambrose downed WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins to win the title.

24th July 2016: “WWE Battleground 2016”: Washington, DC – 13,500 sellout
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Tyler Breeze and Fandango bested Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso.
PPV Broadcast: Sasha Banks and Bayley toppled WWE Women’s Champion Charlotte and Dana Brooke; Bray Wyatt, Erick Rowan and Braun Strowman overcame Big E, Xavier Woods and Kofi Kingston; WWE US Champion Rusev defeated Zack Ryder; Sami Zayn overcame Kevin Owens; Natalya beat Becky Lynch; Darren Young battled WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz to a double-DQ; John Cena, Big Cass and Enzo Amore downed AJ Styles, Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Dean Ambrose defeated Roman Reigns and Seth Rollins in a three-way.

21st August 2016: “WWE Summerslam 2016”: Brooklyn, NY – 13,400 sellout
($1,506,514 live gate)
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Kick-off Show: Jason Jordan, Chad Gable, Jimmy Uso, Jey Uso, Mojo Rawley and Zack Ryder rolled over Konnor, Fandango, Tyler Breeze, Aiden English, Simon Gotch and Viktor; Sami Zayn and Neville downed Bubba Ray Dudley and D-Von Dudley; Sheamus overturned Cesaro (Best of 7 Series – Match #1).
PPV Broadcast: Chris Jericho and Kevin Owens overturned Enzo Amore and Big Cass; Charlotte downed WWE Women’s Champion Sasha Banks to win the title; WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz defeated Apollo Crews; AJ Styles overturned John Cena; Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows overturned WWE Tag Champions Kofi Kingston and Xavier Woods by DQ; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Dean Ambrose defeated Dolph Ziggler; Nikki Bella, Alexa Bliss and Natalya beat Carmella, Becky Lynch and Naomi; Finn Balor rolled over Seth Rollins to become the first WWE Universal Champion; Brock Lesnar rolled over Randy Orton by stoppage.

11th September 2016: “WWE Backlash 2016”: Richmond, VA – 7,000
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Baron Corbin overcame Apollo Crews.
PPV Broadcast: Becky Lynch defeated Alexa Bliss, Carmella, Naomi, Natalya and Nikki Bella in a six-way to become the first WWE Smackdown Women’s Champion; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso bested Zack Ryder and Mojo Rawley (WWE Smackdown Tag Title Tournament – Second Chance Semi-Final); WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz defeated Dolph Ziggler; Kane downed Bray Wyatt in a “No Holds Barred” match; Heath Slater and Rhyno bested Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso to become the first WWE Smackdown Tag Champions (WWE Smackdown Tag Title Tournament – Final); AJ Styles overturned WWE World Champion Dean Ambrose to win the title.

25th September 2016: “WWE Clash of Champions 2016”: Indianapolis, IN – 7,500
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Nia Jax defeated Alicia Fox.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Raw Tag Champions Kofi Kingston and Big E defeated Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows; WWE Cruiserweight Champion TJ Perkins defeated Brian Kendrick; Cesaro battled Sheamus to a no-contest (Best of 7 Series – Match #7); Chris Jericho bested Sami Zayn; WWE Raw Women’s Champion Charlotte defeated Bayley and Sasha Banks in a three-way; Roman Reigns overturned WWE US Champion Rusev to win the title; WWE Universal Champion Kevin Owens defeated Seth Rollins.

9th October 2016: “WWE No Mercy 2016”: Sacramento, CA – 12,000 sellout
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Chad Gable, Jason Jordan, Mojo Rawley and Zack Ryder rolled over Simon Gotch, Aiden English, Konnor and Viktor.
PPV Broadcast: WWE World Champion AJ Styles defeated Dean Ambrose and John Cena in a three-way; Nikki Bella beat Carmella; WWE Smackdown Tag Champions Heath Slater and Rhyno defeated Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso; Baron Corbin overcame Jack Swagger; Dolph Ziggler rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz to win the title; Naomi dropped Alexa Bliss; Bray Wyatt defeated Randy Orton.

30th October 2016: “WWE Hell in a Cell 2016”: Boston, MA – 13,000 sellout
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Sin Cara, Lince Dorado and Cedric Alexander rolled over Tony Nese, Drew Gulak and Ariya Daivari.
PPV Broadcast: WWE US Champion Roman Reigns defeated Rusev in a “Hell in a Cell” match;
Bayley toppled Dana Brooke; Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows overturned Enzo Amore and Big Cass; WWE Universal Champion Kevin Owens defeated Seth Rollins in a “Hell in a Cell” match; Brian Kendrick defeated WWE Cruiserweight Champion TJ Perkins to win the title; Sheamus and Cesaro bested WWE Raw Tag Champions Big E and Xavier Woods by DQ; Charlotte Flair rolled over WWE Raw Women’s Champion Sasha Banks to win the title in a “Hell in a Cell” match.

20th November 2016: “WWE Survivor Series 2016”: Toronto, ONT – 15,000 sellout (14,000 paid)
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Kickoff: Rich Swann, TJ Perkins and Noam Dar rolled over Drew Gulak, Ariya Daivari and Tony Nese.
PPV Broadcast: Kane downed Luke Harper; WWE Raw Women’s Champion Charlotte Flair, Alicia Fox, Sasha Banks, Bayley and Nia Jax defeated Natalya, Carmella, Alexa Bliss, Naomi and WWE Smackdown Women’s Champion Becky Lynch in a “Survivor Series” elimination match; WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz defeated Sami Zayn; Big E, Epico, Primo, Big Cass, Enzo Amore, Karl Anderson, Luke Gallows, Sheamus, Cesaro and Kofi Kingston overcame Fandango, Mojo Rawley, Zack Ryder, Jey Uso, Jimmy Uso, Chad Gable, Jason Jordan, Rhyno, Heath Slater and Tyler Breeze in a “Survivor Series” elimination match; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Brian Kendrick defeated Kalisto by DQ; Shane McMahon, Randy Orton, Bray Wyatt, WWE World Champion AJ Styles and Dean Ambrose downed Seth Rollins, Braun Strowman, WWE US Champion Roman Reigns, WWE Universal Champion Kevin Owens and Chris Jericho in a “Survivor Series” elimination match; Goldberg defeated Brock Lesnar.

4th December 2016: “WWE TLC 2016”: Dallas, TX – 12,500
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Chad Gable, Jason Jordan, Apollo Crews, Mojo Rawley and Zack Ryder rolled over Aiden English, Curt Hawkins, Viktor, Konnor and Simon Gotch.
PPV Broadcast: Bray Wyatt and Randy Orton overcame WWE Smackdown Tag Champions Heath Slater and Rhyno to win the title; Nikki Bella beat Carmella in a no-DQ match; WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz defeated Dolph Ziggler in a ladder match; Baron Corbin overcame Kalisto in a chairs match; Alexa Bliss overturned WWE Smackdown Women’s Champion Becky Lynch to win the title in a tables match; WWE World Champion AJ Styles defeated Dean Ambrose in a TLC match.

18th December 2016: “WWE Roadblock 2016”: Pittsburgh, PA – 8,000
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Rusev defeated Big Cass by count-out.
PPV Broadcast: Cesaro and Sheamus overturned WWE Raw Tag Champions Big E and Kofi Kingston to win the titles; Sami Zayn overcame Braun Strowman; Seth Rollins overturned Chris Jericho; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rich Swann defeated Brian Kendrick and TJ Perkins in a three-way; Charlotte Flair rolled over WWE Raw Women’s Champion Sasha Banks to win the title in a 30-minute Iron Man match to win the title; WWE Universal Champion Kevin Owens defeated WWE US Champion Roman Reigns by DQ.
