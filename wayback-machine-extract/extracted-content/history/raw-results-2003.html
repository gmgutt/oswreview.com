Raw Results 2003

WWE RAW RESULTS (2003)

Jan 2003

Air Date: 6th January 2003
Rico, Rosey, Jamal, and Batista overturned The Dudley Boys in a no-DQ handicap match; WWE Women’s Champion Victoria and Molly Holly overcame Trish Stratus and Jackie; Lance Storm and William Regal toppled WWE World Tag Champions Booker T and Goldust to win the titles; Test downed Christopher Nowinski; Rob Van Dam and Kane bested Chris Jericho and Christian.

Air Date: 13th January 2003
WWE Women’s Champion Victoria beat Trish Stratus and Jacquelyn in a three-way hardcore match; Test and Maven bested D-Lo Brown and Christopher Nowinski; William Regal overcame Jerry Lawler by DQ; Jeff Hardy beat Raven; Booker T battled Lance Storm to a no-contest; The Hurricane toppled Steven Richards; Chris Jericho bested Rob Van Dam, Kane, and Batista in an “Over-the-top-rope Challenge”.

Air Date: 20th January 2003
Rob Van Dam rolled over Jeff Hardy; Lance Storm and William Regal overcame WWE World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys to win the titles; The Hurricane and Trish Stratus overcame Steven Richards and WWE Women’s Champion Victoria; Booker T and Goldust overcame Rosey and Jamal; Chris Jericho battled Test to a no-contest; D-Lo Brown beat Tommy Dreamer in a “Singapore Cane” match; Scott Steiner dropped Batista by DQ.

Air Date: 27th January 2003
Booker T overturned Jeff Hardy; D-Lo Brown beat The Hurricane; WWE Women’s Champion Victoria dropped Trish Stratus in a streetfight; WWE World Tag Champions Lance Storm and William Regal overcame The Dudley Boys in a tables match; Matt Cappotelli battled John Hennigan to a no-contest; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H and Batista rolled over Rob Van Dam and Kane.

Feb 2003

Air Date: 3rd February 2003
Rosey and Jamal defeated The Dudley Boys. Rico was guest referee; WWE Women’s Champion Victoria beat Molly Holly; WWE World Tag Champions Lance Storm and William Regal overcame Booker T and Goldust; Rob Van Dam rolled over Kane by DQ; D-Lo Brown beat Maven; Scott Steiner dropped Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 10th February 2003
Test beat Christian; Booker T overturned D-Lo Brown; Jazz defeated Molly Holly; Rob Van Dam and Kane toppled Rosey and Jamal; Bubba Ray, D-Von, and Spike Dudley beat Chief Morley in a handicap match; Batista pinned Tommy Dreamer; Chris Jericho bested Jeff Hardy.

Air Date: 17th February 2003
Rob Van Dam pinned Lance Storm; WWE Women’s Champion Victoria and Jazz defeated Jacquelyn and Molly Holly; Rodney Mack beat Al Snow; Shawn Michaels and Jeff Hardy beat Chris Jericho and Christian in a no-DQ match; The Hurricane dropped Christopher Nowinski; Rosey, Jamal, and Rico defeated Spike Dudley in a handicap match; Scott Steiner and Booker T overturned World Heavyweight Champion Triple H and Batista; Eric Bischoff rolled over Jim Ross in a no-DQ match.

Air Date: 24th February 2003
Jazz defeated Jacquelyn; Kane beat Lance Storm; Scott Steiner and Booker T overturned Randy Orton and Batista; Test and Stacy Keibler dropped Chris Jericho and Christian by DQ; Christopher Nowinski defeated Jeff Hardy by reverse decision-DQ; Jerry Lawler dropped Chief Morley in a no-DQ match; Booker T won a battle royal.

Mar 2003

Air Date: 3rd March 2003
Christian downed Jeff Hardy; Chief Morley beat Spike Dudley; Trish Stratus and Jacquelyn toppled WWE Women’s Champion Victoria and Jazz; Chris Jericho bested Test; Booker T overturned Scott Steiner; Rob Van Dam and Kane toppled Al Snow and Tommy Dreamer in a hardcore match.

Air Date: 10th March 2003
Chris Jericho and Christian downed Rob Van Dam and Kane; Jeff Hardy beat Rico; Trish Stratus battled Jazz to a no-contest; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H rolled over Maven; Chief Morley and Lance Storm defeated D-Von Dudley in a handicap match; The Hurricane dropped The Rock in a no-DQ match.

Air Date: 17th March 2003
Rob Van Dam and Kane toppled Chief Morley and Lance Storm; Maven bested Rico; The Hurricane dropped Rodney Mack by DQ; Steven Richards and WWE Women’s Champion Victoria toppled Trish Stratus and Jazz; Chris Jericho and Christian downed Test and Scott Steiner; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H rolled over Goldust; Steve Austin bested Eric Bischoff in a no-DQ match.

Air Date: 24th March 2003
Trish Stratus and Jeff Hardy beat WWE Women’s Champion Victoria and Steven Richards; Scott Steiner dropped Christian; Booker T and Goldust overcame World Heavyweight Champion Triple H and Ric Flair; Rob Van Dam and Kane toppled The Dudley Boys.

Air Date: 31st March 2003
World Heavyweight Champion Triple H rolled over The Hurricane; Scott Steiner dropped Christopher Nowinski; Maven bested Rosey; Booker T overturned Chris Jericho by DQ; Jeff Hardy beat Steven Richards; Rob Van Dam and Kane toppled The Dudley Boys, and WWE World Tag Champions Lance Storm and Chief Morley in a three-way elimination match to win the titles.

Apr 2003

Air Date: 7th April 2003
WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus overcame Jazz; Chief Morley beat Rob Van Dam; Scott Steiner battled Christopher Nowinski to a no-contest; The Rock pinned Jeff Hardy; The Dudley Boys overturned Kane in a handicap match; Goldust overcame Steven Richards; Shawn Michaels and Booker T overturned World Heavyweight Champion Triple H and Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 14th April 2003
Chris Jericho bested Test; Victoria and Jazz defeated WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus and Ivory; WWE World Tag Champions Rob Van Dam and Kane toppled Lance Storm and Chief Morley in a no-DQ match; Goldust overcame Christian by DQ; Booker T and The Hurricane dropped World Heavyweight Champion Triple H and Ric Flair.

Air Date: 21st April 2003
Chris Jericho bested The Hurricane; Rodney Mack beat Sean Evans; Scott Steiner and Test downed Rosey and Jamal; Spike Dudley and WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus battled The Dudley Boys to a no-contest; Booker T overturned World Heavyweight Champion Triple H by DQ Shawn Michaels was guest referee.

Air Date: 28th April 2003
The Hurricane dropped Chief Morley; Rodney Mack pinned White Boy #2; WWE World Tag Champions Rob Van Dam and Kane toppled World Heavyweight Champion Triple H and Ric Flair; Scott Steiner and Test beat Rico and Christopher Nowinski; Booker T overturned Christian by DQ; Eric Bischoff pinned Trish Stratus in a no-DQ match.

May 2003

Air Date: 5th May 2003
Jerry Lawler pinned Chief Morley; WWE World Tag Champions Rob Van Dam and Kane toppled Scott Steiner and Test; Booker T overturned Lance Storm; Sylvain Grenier and Rene Dupree downed Tommy Dreamer and Spike Dudley; WWE Women’s Champion Jazz downed Trish Stratus; Bill Goldberg battled Christian to a no-contest.

Air Date: 12th May 2003
WWE World Tag Champions Rob Van Dam and Kane toppled The Legion of Doom; Rodney Mack beat Mike Phoenix and Ken Phoenix in a handicap match; Bill Goldberg pinned Christian in a cage match; Ric Flair overturned The Hurricane; Scott Steiner, Test, and Goldust overcame Christopher Nowinski, Sylvain Grenier, and Rene Dupree; Trish Stratus beat Victoria in a hardcore match; Kevin Nash rolled over Chris Jericho by DQ.

Air Date: 19th May 2003
The Dudley Boys overturned Rosey and Jamal; WWE World Tag Champions Rob Van Dam and Kane toppled Chris Jericho and WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian by DQ; Rodney Mack beat Spike Dudley; Bill Golberg pinned Lance Storm; Sylvain Grenier and Rene Dupree downed Scott Steiner and Test; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H rolled over Ric Flair.

Air Date: 26th May 2003
Booker T overturned Test; Val Venis overturned Steven Richards; Goldust overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian in a non-title match; Rodney Mack beat Bubba Ray Dudley; Sylvain Grenier dropped Rob Van Dam in a flag match; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H rolled over Shawn Michaels and Ric Flair in a handicap match.

Jun 2003

Air Date: 2nd June 2003
Trish Stratus, Ivory, and Jacquelyn toppled WWE Women’s Champion Jazz, Molly Holly, and Victoria; Scott Steiner beat Steven Richards; Rene Dupree pinned Kane; Booker T and Goldust overcame Chris Jericho and WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H, Ric Flair, and Randy Orton rolled over Kevin Nash, Shawn Michaels, and The Hurricane.

Air Date: 9th June 2003
Bill Goldberg pinned Rosey; Rob Van Dam rolled over Rene Dupree; Trish Stratus and Ivory beat WWE Women’s Champion Jazz and Victoria; Scott Steiner beat Lance Storm; Randy Orton bested The Hurricane; Rodney Mack dropped D-Von Dudley.

Air Date: 16th June 2003
The Dudley Boys and Ivory beat Christopher Nowinski, Rodney Mack, and WWE Women’s Champion Jazz; Bill Goldberg and Booker T overturned Chris Jericho and WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian; Garrison Cade toppled Lance Storm; Rico beat Spike Dudley; Randy Orton bested Maven; WWE World Tag Champions Sylvain Grenier and Rene Dupree downed Rob Van Dam and Kane by DQ.

Air Date: 23rd June 2003
WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian and Test overcame Booker T and Scott Steiner; Maven bested Christopher Nowinski; The Dudley Boys overturned Chris Jericho and Lance Storm; WWE World Tag Champions Sylvain Grenier and Rene Dupree downed The Hurricane and Sgt Slaughter; Shawn Michaels and Kevin Nash defeated Ric Flair and Randy Orton; Bill Goldberg pinned Rodney Mack; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H rolled over Kane in a “Title vs. Mask” encounter.

Air Date: 30th June 2003
Gail Kim won a battle royal to win the WWE Women’s Title; Booker T battled WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian to a double-pinfall draw; Chris Jericho and Test overcame Scott Steiner and Stacy Keibler in a no-DQ match; Rico beat Maven; Randy Orton bested Tommy Dreamer; WWE World Tag Champions Sylvain Grenier and Rene Dupree downed The Hurricane and Spike Dudley; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H rolled over Rob Van Dam in a “falls count anywhere” encounter.

Jul 2003

Air Date: 7th July 2003
Booker T overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian to win the title in a no-DQ, no count-out encounter; Rosey toppled The Hurricane; Val Venis and WWE Women’s Champion Gail Kim defeated Steven Richards and Victoria; Test overcame Kevin Nash; Chris Jericho bested Mark Jindrak; Molly Holly downed Trish Stratus; Rob Van Dam and Bubba Ray Dudley beat Ric Flair and Randy Orton.

Air Date: 14th July 2003
Kevin Nash, Scott Steiner, and Trish Stratus overcame Test, Steven Richards, and Victoria; Maven bested Lance Storm; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H, Ric Flair, and Randy Orton rolled over Bubba Ray, D-Von, and Spike Dudley in an elimination match; Rodney Mack beat Rosey; WWE Intercontinental Champion Booker T overturned Christian; WWE Women’s Champion Gail Kim pinned Molly Holly.

Air Date: 21st July 2003
Victoria and Molly Holly downed WWE Women’s Champion Gail Kim and Trish Stratus; Randy Orton bested Val Venis; Chris Jericho bested Shawn Michaels; WWE Intercontinental Champion Booker T overturned Test; Rob Van Dam battled Kane to a no-contest.

Air Date: 28th July 2003
WWE Intercontinental Champion Booker T and Scott Steiner beat Test and Christian; Bill Goldberg pinned Steven Richards; Val Venis overturned Rico; Kevin Nash defeated Chris Jericho by DQ; WWE World Tag Champions Sylvain Grenier and Rene Dupree downed Mark Jindrak and Garrison Cade; Molly Holly downed WWE Women’s Champion Gail Kim to win the title.

Aug 2003

Air Date: 4th August 2003
Bubba Ray Dudley beat Rene Dupree; Randy Orton rolled over Scott Steiner; Christian downed The Hurricane; Bill Goldberg defeated Ric Flair by DQ; Trish Stratus overcame WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly by DQ; Rob Van Dam rolled over Chris Jericho; Eric Bischoff pinned Shane McMahon in a “no-holds-barred” match.

Air Date: 11th August 2003
Rico and Jackie Gayda defeated Scott Steiner and Stacy Keibler; The Dudley Boys overturned WWE World Tag Champions Sylvain Grenier and Rene Dupree by DQ; WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian downed Spike Dudley; Eric Bischoff defeated Kane by count-out; WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly downed Trish Stratus and Gail Kim in a three-way; The Hurricane dropped Rodney Mack; Bill Goldberg pinned Ric Flair in a no-DQ match. Randy Orton was guest referee.

Air Date: 18th August 2003
WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly downed Trish Stratus by DQ; Test overcame Scott Steiner; Chris Jericho bested Kevin Nash in a “hair vs. hair” match; Rosey beat Rodney Mack; Rob Van Dam battled WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian to a no-contest; Bill Goldberg pinned Randy Orton.

Air Date: 25th August 2003
Trish Stratus overcame Gail Kim; Rob Conway and WWE Tag Team Champions Sylvain Grenier and Rene Dupree downed Bubba Ray, D-Von, and Spike Dudley; Rodney Mack and Mark Henry beat Garrison Cade and Mark Jindrak; Randy Orton bested Maven; WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian downed Jerry Lawler; Shane McMahon battled Chris Jericho to a no-contest.

Sep 2003

Air Date: 1st September 2003
Jonathan Coachman bested Jerry Lawler; WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian downed Chris Jericho; WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly and Gail Kim defeated Trish Stratus and Ivory; WWE World Tag Champions Sylvain Grenier and Rene Dupree downed The Hurricane and Rosey; Test and Stacy Keibler dropped Steven Richards and Victoria in a no-DQ match; Bill Goldberg, Shawn Michaels, and Maven bested World Heavyweight Champion Triple H, Ric Flair, and Randy Orton.

Air Date: 8th September 2003
Kane toppled Rob Van Dam in a cage match; Lance Storm pinned Rico; WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly and Gail Kim defeated Trish Stratus and Jacquelyn; Mark Henry, Rodney Mack, Rob Conway, and WWE World Tag Champions Sylvain Grenier and Rene Dupree downed The Hurricane, Rosey, Bubba Ray, D-Von, and Spike Dudley; Scott Steiner dropped Steven Richards; Bill Goldberg and Randy Orton battled World Heavyweight Champion Triple H and Ric Flair to a no-contest.

Air Date: 15th September 2003
Rob Van Dam battled Chris Jericho to a no-contest; Rob Conway beat Spike Dudley; The Fabulous Moolah downed Victoria; Rodney Mack and Mark Henry beat Lance Storm and Goldust; WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly and Gail Kim defeated Trish Stratus in a handicap match; Val Venis overturned Test; Jerry Lawler dropped Al Snow.

Air Date: 22nd September 2003
Rob Van Dam rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian by DQ; Mark Henry beat Tommy Dreamer in a hardcore match; Lita and Trish Stratus overcame WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly and Gail Kim; The Hurricane and Rosey beat Rene Dupree and Rob Conway; Triple H, Ric Flair, and Randy Orton rolled over Maven, Garrison Cade, and Mark Jindrak; World Heavyweight Champion Bill Goldberg pinned Chris Jericho. Eric Bischoff was guest “ringside” referee.

Air Date: 29th September 2003
WWE World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys overturned Test and Scott Steiner; Maven, Mark Jindrak, and Garrison Cade toppled Sylvain Grenier, Rene Dupree, and Rob Conway; Jim Ross overturned Jonathan Coachman in a “Country Whipping” match; Kane toppled The Hurricane; World Heavyweight Champion Bill Goldberg and Shawn Michaels overturned Ric Flair and Randy Orton by DQ; Rob Van Dam rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian to win the title in a ladder match.

Oct 2003

Air Date: 6th October 2003
Kane toppled Rosey; Lita beat Gail Kim; Scott Steiner beat Spike Dudley; WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam and Lance Storm defeated Chris Jericho and Christian; Ric Flair and Randy Orton rolled over Mark Jindrak and Garrison Cade; Maven and Trish Stratus overcame Steven Richards and Victoria; World Heavyweight Champion Bill Goldberg defeated Mark Henry by DQ.

Air Date: 13th October 2003
WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Scott Steiner by DQ; Maven bested Rico; Booker T, Shane McMahon, and WWE World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys overturned Chris Jericho, Christian, Rene Dupree, and Rob Conway in a tables match; Trish Stratus, Lita, and Ivory beat WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly, Victoria, and Gail Kim; World Heavyweight Champion Bill Goldberg and Shawn Michaels overturned Ric Flair, Randy Orton, and Mark Henry in a handicap match.

Air Date: 20th October 2003
WWE World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys overturned Rene Dupree and Rob Conway; Booker T overturned Randy Orton by count-out; Chris Jericho and Scott Steiner dropped WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam and Lance Storm; WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly and Victoria beat Trish Stratus and Lita; Ric Flair overturned Maven; Shawn Michaels battled World Heavyweight Champion Bill Goldberg to a no-contest.

Air Date: 27th October 2003
Booker T overturned Rico; Mark Henry beat Lance Storm by DQ; The Hurricane and Jon Heidenreich overcame Rob Conway and Rene Dupree; Shane McMahon bested Test in a streetfight; Lita beat Victoria, Trish Stratus, and Gail Kim in a four-way; Mark Jindrak and Garrison Cade toppled Ric Flair and Randy Orton; Chris Jericho bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam to win the title; Rob Van Dam rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho to win the title in a cage match.

Nov 2003

Air Date: 3rd November 2003
Chris Jericho and Christian bested Booker T and WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam; Batista pinned Maven; Mark Jindrak and Garrison Cade toppled WWE World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys in a non-title match; Lance Storm pinned Rico; Jon Heidenreich and Trish Stratus overcame Steven Richards and Victoria; Shawn Michaels overturned Mark Henry. Steve Austin was guest ringside enforcer.

Air Date: 10th November 2003
WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Christian; Rob Conway and Rene Dupree downed The Hurricane and Rosey; WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly and Gail Kim defeated Lita and Terri; Shawn Michaels overturned Randy Orton; WWE World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys overturned Scott Steiner and Mark Henry by DQ; Chris Jericho overturned Booker T by reverse decision-DQ. Jonathan Coachman was guest referee; World Heavyweight Champion Bill Goldberg defeated Batista by DQ.

Air Date: 17th November 2003
Booker T overturned Mark Henry; Mark Jindrak and Garrison Cade toppled Rob Conway and Rene Dupree; WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Ric Flair by DQ; WWE World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys overturned Test and Scott Steiner; Eric Bischoff and WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly downed Matt Hardy and Lita; Val Venis overcame Rico; Triple H, Randy Orton, and Batista overturned World Heavyweight Champion Bill Goldberg in a non-title handicap match.

Air Date: 24th November 2003
Victoria toppled Lita in a cage match; Randy Orton rolled over Sgt Slaughter; Bubba Ray Dudley and Garrison Cade toppled Matt Hardy and Christian; World Heavyweight Champion Bill Goldberg defeated Triple H by DQ; Mark Henry beat Booker T in a “Salt Lake City Streetfight”; Test and Scott Steiner dropped WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam in a non-title “Singapore Cane” handicap match; Trish Stratus overcame Jackie Gayda in a “bra and panties” match; Shawn Michaels and Chris Jericho bested Ric Flair and Batista.

Dec 2003

Air Date: 1st December 2003
WWE World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys overturned Mark Jindrak and Garrison Cade; Booker T overturned Test; Lance Storm and Val Venis overturned Rob Conway and Rene Dupree; Chris Jericho and Trish Stratus overcame Rico and Jackie Gayda; Matt Hardy beat Christian; Jerry Lawler dropped Jonathan Coachman; World Heavyweight Champion Bill Goldberg, Shawn Michaels, and WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Kane, Batista, and Randy Orton.

Air Date: 8th December 2003
WWE World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys overturned Chris Jericho and Christian; Batista pinned WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam; Mark Jindrak and Garrison Cade toppled Lance Storm and Val Venis; Mark Henry, Scott Steiner, and Test overcame Booker T, The Hurricane, and Rosey; WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly and Victoria toppled Trish Stratus and Lita; World Heavyweight Champion Bill Goldberg rolled over Kane by DQ in a lumberjack match.

Air Date: 15th December 2003
Chris Jericho and Christian battled Trish Stratus and Lita to a no-contest; Booker T and Maven bested Matt Hardy and Mark Henry; Shawn Michaels and Rob Van Dam rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Triple H, and WWE World Tag Champions Ric Flair and Batista in a handicap match; Rico overcame Jon Heidenreich; Kane toppled Chris Jericho by DQ.

Air Date: 22nd December 2003
Year-in-Review highlight show

Air Date: 29th December 2003
Rob Van Dam rolled over Scott Steiner; Eddie Kramer and Russell Simpson bested The Dudley Boys by DQ. Ric Flair was guest referee; WWE Intercontinental Champion Randy Orton rolled over Booker T; Trish Stratus, Lita, and Stacy Keibler dropped WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly, Victoria, and Jackie Gayda; Shawn Michaels battled World Heavyweight Champion Triple H to a double-pinfall draw.
