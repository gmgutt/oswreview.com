WWE Battleground Results

WWE BATTLEGROUND RESULTS

6th October 2013: “WWE Battleground 2013”: Buffalo, NY – 11,700
Pre-show: Dolph Ziggler beat Damien Sandow.
PPV Broadcast: World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio defeated Rob Van Dam in a hardcore match; Jack Swagger and Antonio Cesaro downed The Great Khali and Santino Marella; WWE Intercontinental Champion Curtis Axel toppled R-Truth; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee overturned Brie Bella; Cody Rhodes and Goldust dropped WWE World Tag Champions Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns in a non-title match; Bray Wyatt overcame Kofi Kingston; CM Punk bested Ryback; Randy Orton battled Daniel Bryan to a no-contest.

20th July 2014: “WWE Battleground 2014”: Tampa, FL – 11,000
Shown on WWE Network/PPV
Pre-show: Adam Rose beat Fandango; Cameron defeated Naomi.
PPV Broadcast: WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso downed Luke Harper and Erick Rowan in a “best-of-three-falls” encounter; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee defeated Paige; Rusev beat Jack Swagger by count out; Chris Jericho bested Bray Wyatt; The Miz won a battle royal to win the vacant WWE Intercontinental Title, last eliminating Dolph Ziggler; WWE World Heavyweight Champion John Cena rolled over Kane, Randy Orton, and Roman Reigns in a four-way.


19th July 2015: “WWE Battleground 2015”: St Louis, MO – 11,000
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: King Barrett beat R-Truth.
PPV Broadcast: Randy Orton pinned Sheamus; WWE World Tag Champions Titus O’Neill and Darren Young downed Big E and Kofi Kingston; Bray Wyatt toppled Roman Reigns; Charlotte won a three-way over Brie Bella and Sasha Banks; WWE US Champion John Cena bested Kevin Owens; Brock Lesnar rolled over WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins by DQ.

24th July 2016: “WWE Battleground 2016”: Washington, DC – 13,500 sellout
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Tyler Breeze and Fandango bested Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso.
PPV Broadcast: Sasha Banks and Bayley toppled WWE Women’s Champion Charlotte and Dana Brooke; Bray Wyatt, Erick Rowan and Braun Strowman overcame Big E, Xavier Woods and Kofi Kingston; WWE US Champion Rusev defeated Zack Ryder; Sami Zayn overcame Kevin Owens; Natalya beat Becky Lynch; Darren Young battled WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz to a double-DQ; John Cena, Big Cass and Enzo Amore downed AJ Styles, Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Dean Ambrose defeated Roman Reigns and Seth Rollins in a three-way.
