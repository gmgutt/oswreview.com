NXT Results 2017

NXT RESULTS (2017)

Jan 2017

Air Date: 4th January 2017
Dash Wilder and Scott Dawson overcame Riddick Moss and Tino Sabbatelli; Buddy Murphy and Tye Dillinger rolled over Bobby Roode and Elias Samson; Ember Moon overcame Billie Kay and Liv Morgan in a three-way; WWE NXT Tag Champions Johnny Gargano and Tommaso Ciampa defeated Nick Miller and Shane Thorne; WWE NXT Champion Shinsuke Nakamura defeated Samoa Joe in a cage match.

Air Date: 11th January 2017
Billie Kay and Peyton Royce downed Macey Evans and Sarah Bridges; Elias Samson overcame Jonathan Cruz; Andrade Almas overturned Oney Lorcan; WWE NXT Tag Champions Johnny Gargano and Tommaso Ciampa defeated Dash Wilder and Scott Dawson.

Air Date: 18th January 2017
Nikki Cross overturned Kennadi Brink; Roderick Strong defeated Steve Cutler; Nick Miller and Shane Thorne downed Dash Wilder and Scott Dawson.

Air Date: 25th January 2017
Ember Moon overcame Liv Morgan; No Way Jose downed Kona Reeves; Eric Young defeated Chris Atkins.

Feb 2017

Air Date: 1st February 2017
Ember Moon overcame Aliyah; Tyler Bate downed Oney Lorcan; No Way Jose downed Elias Samson.

Air Date: 8th February 2017
Alexander Wolfe and Killian Dain overcame Gurv Sihra and Harv Sihra; Liv Morgan overcame Billie Kay; Dash Wilder and Scott Dawson overcame Otis Dozovic and Tucker Knight; Alexander Wolfe, Eric Young and Killian Dain overcame No Way Jose, Roderick Strong and Tye Dillinger.

Air Date: 15th February 2017
WWE NXT Tag Champions Akam and Rezar rolled over Garrison Spears and Lance Anoai; Billie Kay and Peyton Royce downed Ember Moon and Liv Morgan; WWE United Kingdom Champion Tyler Bate defeated Trent Seven.

Air Date: 22nd February 2017
Peyton Royce downed Ember Moon and Liv Morgan in a three-way; Pete Dunne downed Mark Andrews; WWE NXT Champion Bobby Roode downed No Way Jose.

Mar 2017

Air Date: 1st March 2017
Patrick Clark defeated Sean Maluta; WWE NXT Women’s Champion Asuka defeated Peyton Royce; WWE NXT Tag Champions Akam and Rezar battled Tommaso Ciampa and Johnny Gargano to a no-contest.

Air Date: 8th March 2017
Andrade Almas overturned Ho Ho Lun; Ember Moon overcame Billie Kay; Shinsuke Nakamura beat TJ Perkins.

Air Date: 15th March 2017
Nikki Cross overturned Macey Estrella; WWE NXT Tag Champions Akam and Rezar rolled over Gabriel Ealy and Uriel Ealy; WWE NXT Champion Bobby Roode defeated Kassius Ohno.

Air Date: 22nd March 2017
Andrade Almas overturned Oney Lorcan; Asuka beat Priscilla Zuniga; Eric Young, Killian Dain and Alexander Wolfe battled No Way Jose, Tye Dillinger and Roderick Strong to a no-contest.

Air Date: 29th March 2017
Dash Wilder rolled over Akam and Johnny Gargano in a three-way; Otis Dozovic and Tucker Knight defeated Jonathan Ortagun and Mike Marshall; Kassius Ohno bested Elias Samson in a “Loser Leaves NXT” match.

Apr 2017

Air Date: 5th April 2017
Tucker Knight and Otis Dozovic defeated Harv Sihra and Gurv Sihra; Peyton Royce downed Aliyah; Oney Lorcan overcame El Vagabundo.

Air Date: 12th April 2017
Aleister Black defeated Corey Hollis; Johnny Gargano and Tommaso Ciampa beat Dylan Miley and Michael Richard Blais; Ruby Riot defeated Kimberly Frankele; Drew McIntyre downed Oney Lorcan.

Air Date: 19th April 2017
Andrade Almas overturned Danny Burch; Aliyah and Liv Morgan overcame Billie Kay and Peyton Royce; Tye Dillinger rolled over Eric Young in a cage match.

Air Date: 26th April 2017
Drew McIntyre downed Andrade Almas; Aleister Black defeated Kona Reeves; WWE United Kingdom Champion Tyler Bate defeated Jack Gallagher.

May 2017

Air Date: 3rd May 2017
Hideo Itami dropped Kona Reeves; Killian Dain overcame Danny Burch; Otis Dozovic and Tucker Knight defeated Hector Kunsman and Ricardo Watts; A women’s battle royal ended in a no-contest.
