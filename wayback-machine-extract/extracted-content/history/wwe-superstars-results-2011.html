WWE Superstars Results 2011

WWE SUPERSTARS RESULTS (2011)

Jan 2011

Air Date: 6th January 2011
Yoshi Tatsu overcame Zack Ryder; Tyson Kidd and Ted Dibiase Jr toppled WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan and Mark Henry; Tyler Reks beat JTG; Chris Masters bested Curt Hawkins.

Air Date: 13th January 2011
Gail Kim dropped Alicia Fox; DH Smith, Primo Colon, Yoshi Tatsu and Darren Young overturned William Regal, Zack Ryder and Jimmy and Jey Uso; Chris Masters and JTG downed Curt Hawkins and Chavo Guerrero Jr.

Air Date: 20th January 2011
Zack Ryder and Primo Colon beat DH Smith and Darren Young; Yoshi Tatsu bested Tyson Kidd; JTG beat Chavo Guerrero; Chris Masters defeated Curt Hawkins.

Air Date: 27th January 2011
Zack Ryder and Primo Colon defeated DH Smith and Yoshi Tatsu; R-Truth overcame Tyson Kidd; Tyler Reks beat Trent Baretta.

Feb 2011

Air Date: 3rd February 2011
David Hart Smith and Yoshi Tatsu downed Primo Colon and Zack Ryder; Melina pinned Tamina; Curt Hawkins and Tyler Reks bested Trent Barretta and JTG.

Air Date: 10th February 2011
Natalya Neidhart defeated Alicia Fox; The Great Khali beat Jimmy and Jey Uso in a handicap match; Tyler Reks beat Curt Hawkins; Jack Swagger overturned Trent Barretta.

Air Date: 17th February 2011
Jimmy and Jey Uso dropped DH Smith and Yoshi Tatsu; WWE World Tag Champions Santino Marella and Vladimir Kozlov defeated Zack Ryder and Primo; Chris Masters defeated Tyler Reks.

Air Date: 24th February 2011
Santino Marella pinned Tyson Kidd; WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan defeated Ted Dibiase Jr; WWE World Tag Champions Justin Gabriel and Heath Slater downed Trent Barretta and JTG; Chris Masters defeated Tyler Reks.

Mar 2011

Air Date: 3rd March 2011
R-Truth defeated William Regal; Santino Marella and Vladimir Kozlov overcame Primo and Zack Ryder; Chris Masters dropped Curt Hawkins.

Air Date: 10th March 2011
Zack Ryder dropped Vladimir Kozlov; Mark Henry, The Great Khali and Natalya Neidhart defeated William Regal, Tyson Kidd and Melina; Chris Masters bested Tyler Reks.

Air Date: 17th March 2011
Santino Marella and Vladimir Kozlov downed Jimmy and Jey Uso; WWE Divas Champion Eve, Natalya Neidhart, Gail Kim and Tamina overcame Melina, Alicia Fox, Brie and Nikki Bella; Tyler Reks dropped JTG.

Air Date: 24th March 2011
The Great Khali and Yoshi Tatsu bested Jimmy and Jey Uso; Daniel Bryan and Gail Kim toppled Tyson Kidd and Melina; Chris Masters bested Tyler Reks.

Air Date: 31st March 2011
Trent Baretta beat Curt Hawkins; WWE Divas Champion Eve, Natalya Neidhart and Gail Kim defeated Melina, Brie and Nikki Bella.

Air Date: 7th April 2011

Air Date: 14th April 2011

Air Date: 21st April 2011
Maryse overcame Natalya Neidhart; Mark Henry downed Ted Dibiase Jr; Tyler Reks dropped JTG.

Air Date: 28th April 2011
Zack Ryder beat DH Smith; Natalya Neidhart and Gail Kim overturned Alicia Fox and Melina; Tyson Kidd overcame Trent Barretta.

Air Date: 5th May 2011

Air Date: 12th May 2011

Air Date: 19th May 2011

Air Date: 26th May 2011
Santino Marella beat Zack Ryder; Curt Hawkins downed JTG; Tyson Kidd pinned Trent Barreta.

Jun 2011

Air Date: 2nd June 2011

Air Date: 9th June 2011

Air Date: 16th June 2011

Air Date: 23rd June 2011
Gail Kim beat Melina; WWE World Tag Champions David Otunga and Michael McGillicutty overcame Vladimir Kozlov and Santino Marella; AJ, Natalya and Kaitlyn beat Tamina, Rosa Mendes and Alicia Fox.

Air Date: 30th June 2011
Beth Phoenix defeated Gail Kim; Chris Masters overcame JTG; Alicia Fox beat Kaitlyn; Jimmy and Jey Uso, and Trent Barretta overcame Tyson Kid, Justin Gabriel and Heath Slater.

Jul 2011

Air Date: 7th July 2011

Air Date: 14th July 2011

Air Date: 21st July 2011
Justin Gabriel bested Tyson Kidd; Alicia Fox overturned Natalya; Zack Ryder and Santino Marella overcame WWE World Tag Champions Michael McGillicutty and David Otunga in a non-title match; Evan Bourne dropped Primo.

Air Date: 28th July 2011
Heath Slater beat Trent Barreta; WWE World Tag Champions Michael McGillicutty and David Otunga overcame Chris Masters and Santino Marella; Alex Riley bested Jack Swagger.

Air Date: 25th August 2011
