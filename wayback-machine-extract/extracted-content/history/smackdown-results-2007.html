Smackdown Results 2007

WWE SMACKDOWN RESULTS (2007)

Jan 2007

Air Date: 5th January 2007
Kane bested Chavo Guerrero Jr (Beat the Clock); Paul London battled Brian Kendrick to a draw (Beat the Clock); King Booker toppled WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms in a non-title match (Beat the Clock); Ashley pinned Jillian Hall; Tatanka battled Jimmy Wang Yang to a draw (Beat the Clock); Mr Kennedy dropped WWE US Champion Chris Benoit in a non-title match (Beat the Clock).

Air Date: 12th January 2007
Matt Hardy battled Finlay to a draw (Beat the Clock); Tatanka beat Jimmy Wang Yang; Mr Kennedy dropped WWE US Champion Chris Benoit in a non-title match; WWE Tag Champions Brian Kendrick and Paul London defeated William Regal and Dave Taylor; MVP battled Vito to a draw (Beat the Clock); The Undertaker battled The Miz to a draw (Beat the Clock).

Air Date: 19th January 2007
Matt Hardy downed Joey Mercury; MVP, William Regal and Dave Taylor defeated Vito and WWE Tag Champions Brian Kendrick and Paul London; WWE US Champion Chris Benoit dropped Chavo Guerrero Jr in a no-DQ match; Deuce and Domino overcame Adam Roberts and Patrick Bentley; Kane bested The Miz; Mr Kennedy dropped The Undertaker by DQ.

Air Date: 26th January 2007
World Heavyweight Champion Batista rolled over WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms in a non-title match; WWE US Champion Chris Benoit dropped The Miz; Deuce and Domino overcame Adam Evans and John Robinson; Finlay battled King Booker to a no-contest; Melina, Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury defeated Ashley and WWE Tag Champions Paul London and Brian Kendrick; Kane bested MVP by DQ;  A battle royal between WWE US Champion Chris Benoit, MVP, King Booker, Finlay, and The Miz  ended in a no-contest.

Feb 2007

Air Date: 2nd February 2007
WWE US Champion Chris Benoit dropped Finlay; Deuce and Dominio defeated WWE Tag Champions Brian Kendrick and Paul London in a non-title match; Mr Kennedy dropped Vito; Ashley beat Jillian Hall and Kristal in a bikini contest; The Miz pinned Matt Hardy.

Air Date: 9th February 2007
The Undertaker rolled over The Miz; Deuce and Domino overcame WWE Tag Champions Paul London and Brian Kendrick in a non-title match; Joey Mercury pinned Matt Hardy; Finlay beat The Boogeyman; Daivari beat WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms in a non-title match; World Heavyweight Champion Batista rolled over Mr Kennedy.

Air Date: 16th February 2007
The Boogeyman pinned Finlay; Deuce beat Brian Kendrick; WWE World Tag Champions John Cena and Shawn Michaels toppled Mr Kennedy and MVP; Scotty 2 Hotty battled WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms to a no-contest; World Heavyweight Champion Batista and The Undertaker rolled over Edge and Randy Orton.

Air Date: 23rd February 2007
Matt Hardy, WWE Tag Champions Paul London and Brian Kendrick overcame Joey Mercury, Deuce and Domino; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr pinned Scotty 2 Hotty; King Booker toppled Kane in a “falls count anywhere” match (MITB Qualifier); Finlay and Hornswoggle overturned The Boogeyman and Little Boogeyman.

Mar 2007

Air Date: 2nd March 2007
Matt Hardy downed Joey Mercury (MITB Qualifier); Jimmy Wang Yang, Shannon Moore, and Scotty 2 Hotty overcame WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr, Daivari, and Gregory Helms; Ashley pinned Jillian Hall; Finlay downed WWE US Champion Chris Benoit and MVP in a non-title three-way (MITB Qualifier); ECW Champion Bobby Lashley toppled Mr Kennedy.

Air Date: 9th March 2007
King Booker toppled Matt Hardy; World Heavyweight Champion Batista rolled over Kane; Mr Kennedy dropped CM Punk; The Undertaker rolled over Finlay.

Air Date: 16th March 2007
Matt Hardy downed Mr Kennedy; MVP pinned El Grande Latte; WWE Women’s Champion Melina, Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury defeated Ashley and WWE Tag Champions Paul London and Brian Kendrick; Kane battled Daivari to a no-contest; The Undertaker rolled over King Booker by DQ

Air Date: 23rd March 2007
WWE Tag Champions Paul London and Brian Kendrick overcame Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury; Jimmy Wang Yang overturned WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr in a non-title match; MVP defeated Cedric Von Haussen; ECW Champion Bobby Lashley toppled Randy Orton and Mr Kennedy in a handicap tables match; WWE US Champion Chris Benoit dropped Gregory Helms; The Undertaker rolled over King Booker and Finlay by DQ in a handicap match.

Air Date: 30th March 2007
Matt Hardy battled The Great Khali to a no-contest; Finlay beat Mr Kennedy; MVP pinned Viko Batomango; Jeff Hardy downed Randy Orton; Ashley, Torrie Wilson, Maria, Mickie James, and Candice Michelle defeated WWE Women’s Champion Melina, Michelle McCool, Jillian Hall, Kristal, and Victoria; CM Punk overturned King Booker.

Apr 2007

Air Date: 6h April 2007
Mr Kennedy dropped Jeff Hardy; The Miz pinned WWE US Champion Chris Benoit in a non-title match; Matt Hardy downed King Booker; WWE Tag Champions Paul London and Brian Kendrick overcame WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr and Gregory Helms.

Air Date: 13th April 2007
WWE Tag Champions Paul London and Brian Kendrick overcame Deuce and Domino by DQ; MVP pinned WWE US Champion Chris Benoit in a non-title match; Kane bested Daivari; World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker and Batista rolled over Mr Kennedy and Finlay.

Air Date: 20th April 2007
Batista rolled over Finlay by DQ; WWE US Champion Chris Benoit and WWE World Tag Champions Matt and Jeff Hardy downed MVP, WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr, and Gregory Helms; Deuce and Domino overcame WWE Tag Champions Brian Kendrick and Paul London to win the titles; Kane bested William Regal and Dave Taylor in a handicap match by count-out; World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker rolled over Mr Kennedy by DQ.

Air Date: 27th April 2007
Kane and The Boogeyman defeated William Regal and Dave Taylor; Deuce pinned Brian Kendrick; MVP overcame Hamlet McBeth; WWE US Champion Chris Benoit dropped MVP by count-out; Jillian Hall pinned Michelle McCool; World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker and Batista rolled over Mr Kennedy and Finlay in a no-DQ match.

May 2007

Air Date: 4th May 2007
The Boogeyman defeated William Regal; Brian Kendrick overcame Domino; Matt Hardy downed Mr Kennedy; Jimmy Wang Yang beat Gregory Helms; Finlay dropped The Miz by count-out; MVP toppled Kane.

Air Date: 11th May 2007
WWE US Champion Chris Benoit and Matt Hardy downed MVP and Finlay; Michelle McCool beat Jillian Hall; Paul London pinned Domino; Kane dropped Dave Taylor; World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker battled Batista to a draw in a cage match; Edge pinned World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker to win the title.

Air Date: 18th May 2007
MVP pinned Matt Hardy; Jimmy Wang Yang, Paul London and Brian Kendrick overcame WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr, and WWE Tag Champions Deuce and Domino; Mark Henry beat Sho Funaki; WWE US Champion Chris Benoit dropped Finlay by DQ; The Miz pinned Christian York; Kane rolled over William Regal.

Air Date: 25th May 2007
Paul London and Brian Kendrick overcame William Regal and Dave Taylor by DQ; Matt Hardy downed The Miz; Jimmy Wang Yang pinned WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr in a non-title match; Batista rolled over Mark Henry, Finlay, and Kane in a four-way.

Jun 2007


Air Date: 1st June 2007
Chris Benoit and Matt Hardy downed WWE US Champion MVP and The Miz; Michelle McCool beat Jillian Hall; Hornswoggle pinned Little Boogeyman; Mark Henry defeated Kane by count-out; WWE Tag Champions Deuce and Domino overcame Paul London and Brian Kendrick, and William Regal and Dave Taylor in a three-way.

Air Date: 8th June 2007
Mark Henry pinned The Boogeyman; Jimmy Wang Yang, Paul London and Brian Kendrick overcame WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr, Daivari, and Jamie Noble; World Heavyweight Champion Edge bested Chris Benoit; WWE Tag Champions Deuce and Domino overcame Matt Hardy in a handicap match; Batista rolled over WWE US Champion MVP by DQ.

Air Date: 15th June 2007
WWE Tag Champions Deuce and Domino overcame Paul London and Brian Kendrick; Matt Hardy downed William Regal; Batista rolled over WWE US Champion MVP in a non-title match; Jimmy Wang Yang defeated Daivari, Shannon Moore, and Jamie Noble in a four-way; Ric Flair bested World Heavyweight Champion Edge by DQ.

Air Date: 22nd June 2007
WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr and Jamie Noble defeated Jimmy Wang Yang and Shannon Moore; Mark Henry downed Sho Funaki by stoppage; Matt Hardy dropped Finlay; Chris Masters toppled Danny Shanley; Batista and Ric Flair bested World Heavyweight Champion Edge and WWE US Champion MVP.

Air Date: 29th June 2007
Ric Flair bested Carlito; Matt Hardy downed Lance Cade; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr defeated Sho Funaki and Jamie Noble in a three-way; WWE Tag Champions Deuce and Domino overcame Cryme Tyme; Kane bested WWE US Champion MVP in a non-title match; Mickie James dropped Victoria; The Great Khali toppled Eugene; Batista rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Edge in a non-title match.

Jul 2007

Air Date: 6th July 2007
Matt Hardy downed WWE US Champion MVP in a non-title match; Chris Masters toppled Jimmy Wang Yang; Brett and Brian Major defeated Jeremy Young and Mike Fox; Finlay overcame Ric Flair; Chuck Palumbo pinned Kenny Dykstra; Deuce battled Eugene to a no-contest.

Air Date: 13th July 2007
Matt Hardy downed Chris Masters; Finlay pinned Jimmy Wang Yang; Brett and Brian Major defeated WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr and Jamie Noble; Chuck Palumbo beat Luke Hawks; Mark Henry bested Eugene; Torrie Wilson defeated Victoria by count-out.

Air Date: 20th July 2007
Victoria beat Torrie Wilson; The Great Khali won a battle royal to win the vacant World Heavyweight Title; Matt Hardy downed Kenny Dykstra; Batista battled Kane to a no-contest.

Air Date: 27th July 2007
Batista rolled over Deuce; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Hornswoggle defeated Jamie Noble by count-out; Chavo Guerrero Jr pinned Eugene; Matt Hardy and Ric Flair bested WWE US Champion MVP and Chris Masters; Mark Henry downed Joey Blalock; Kane toppled Dave Taylor; Jimmy Wang Yang and Torrie Wilson dropped Kenny Dykstra and Victoria.

Aug 2007

Air Date: 3rd August 2007
Kane bested Chris Masters; Mark Henry defeated Nate Nickerson; Matt Hardy downed WWE US Champion MVP by count-out; Jamie Noble pinned Shannon Moore; Jimmy Wang Yang overcame Kenny Dykstra; Batista and Ric Flair bested WWE Tag Champions Deuce and Domino by DQ.

Air Date: 10th August 2007
Batista rolled over Domino; Chavo Guerrero beat Eugene; Mark Henry defeated Sterling Keenan; Victoria pinned Torrie Wilson; Matt Hardy downed Finlay by count-out; Kane bested Sylvan; World Heavyweight Champion The Great Khali rolled over Ric Flair.

Air Date: 17th August 2007
WWE Tag Champions Deuce and Domino overcame Brett and Brian Majors; Finlay defeated Jamie Noble by DQ; Chavo Guerrero Jr downed Shannon Moore; Mark Henry dropped Greg Cardona; Michelle McCool beat Victoria; World Heavyweight Champion The Great Khali rolled over Kane.

Air Date: 24th August 2007
Chuck Palumbo pinned Chris Masters; Sho Funaki beat Chavo Guerrero Jr by DQ; Jamie Noble defeated Jimmy Wang Yang; WWE Tag Champions Deuce and Domino overcame Eugene and Shannon Moore; Mark Henry downed Brad Allen; Batista battled Finlay to a no-contest.

Air Date: 31st August 2007
Finlay downed Kane (Championship Competition – Semi-Final); Matt Hardy and WWE US Champion MVP defeated WWE Tag Champions Deuce and Domino to win the titles; Rey Mysterio downed Batista by DQ (Championship Competition – Semi-Final); Rey Mysterio toppled Finlay (Championship Competition – Final).

Sep 2007

Air Date: 7th September 2007
Matt Hardy pinned Deuce; Chuck Palumbo overcame Kenny Dykstra; Batista and Kane bested World Heavyweight Champion The Great Khali and Finlay; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Hornswoggle overturned Jamie Noble by count-out; Rey Mysterio rolled over Chavo Guerrero Jr in an “I Quit” match.

Air Date: 14th September 2007
Michelle McCool and Chuck Palumbo downed Victoria and Kenny Dykstra; Shannon Moore overcame Jamie Noble; Domino bested WWE US Champion MVP in a non-title match; Finlay rolled over Kane in a “Belfast Brawl”; Batista rolled over World Heavyweight Champion The Great Khali by DQ.

Air Date: 21st September 2007
Jimmy Wang Yang beat Jamie Noble; Deuce and Domino overcame WWE Tag Champions MVP and Matt Hardy in a non-title match; World Heavyweight Champion Batista rolled over Mark Henry by DQ.

Air Date: 28th September 2007
Victoria pinned Torrie Wilson; Chuck Palumbo downed Kenny Dykstra; Kane bested WWE US Champion MVP by DQ; Jamie Noble toppled Shannon Moore; The Undertaker rolled over Mark Henry.

Oct 2007

Air Date: 5th October 2007
Chuck Palumbo beat Ace Steel; Jamie Noble dropped Jimmy Wang Yang; WWE Tag Champions MVP and Matt Hardy downed Deuce and Domino; Kane bested Kenny Dykstra; Jesse and Festus overturned Mike Tolar and Chad Collyer; Rey Mysterio toppled The Great Khali by DQ.

Air Date: 12th October 2007
Chuck Palumbo beat Chris Masters; Jesse and Festus overturned Jay Adams and Conrad Carnes; Drew McIntyre pinned Brett Major; The Undertaker and Kane bested WWE Tag Champions MVP and Matt Hardy in a non-title match; The Great Khali defeated Jamie Noble; Jamie Wang Yang beat Deuce; Rey Mysterio battled Finlay to a double-DQ.

Air Date: 19th October 2007
Kane bested The Great Khali by DQ; Chris Masters toppled Chuck Palumbo; Matt Hardy downed Finlay; Drew McIntyre overcame Brian Major; Jimmy Wang Yang and Shannon Moore defeated Deuce and Domino; The Undertaker rolled over Jamie Noble; WWE US Champion MVP bested Rey Mysterio by DQ.

Air Date: 26th October 2007
Kane bested Mark Henry; WWE Tag Champions MVP and Matt Hardy downed Rey Mysterio and Finlay; World Heavyweight Champion Batista rolled over Jamie Noble; Big Daddy V defeated John Morrison and The Mizin a three-way; Jesse and Festus overturned Joe Hennig and Steve Fender.

Nov 2007

Air Date: 2nd November 2007
Festus beat Domino; Rey Mysterio toppled Jamie Noble; Kane bested WWE US Champion MVP in a non-title match; Mick Foley pinned Jonathan Coachman. Hornswoggle was guest referee; Chuck Palumbo overturned Kenny Dykstra; World Heavyweight Champion Batista and The Undertaker rolled over The Great Khali and Mark Henry by DQ.

Air Date: 9th November 2007
Brett and Brian Major won a tag team battle royal; Victoria dropped Michelle McCool; Finlay downed Rey Mysterio; ECW Champion CM Punk overturned Jamie Noble by DQ; WWE Tag Champions MVP and Matt Hardy downed Brett and Brian Major; The Undertaker rolled over The Great Khali in a “no holds barred” match.

Air Date: 16th November 2007
WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Finlay by DQ; WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix and Victoria defeated Torrie Wilson and Mickie James; John Morrison and The Miz dropped WWE Tag Champions MVP and Matt Hardy to win the titles; WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz bested WWE US Champion MVP and Matt Hardy; Rey Mysterio toppled Mr Kennedy; World Heavyweight Champion Batista rolled over Mark Henry in a no-DQ match.

Air Date: 23rd November 2007
WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz defeated Brian and Brett Major; Kane bested Big Daddy V by DQ; WWE US Champion MVP bested Rey Mysterio; Finlay and Hornswoggle toppled Mark Henry in a handicap match; Jamie Noble toppled Chuck Palumbo; Torrie Wilson overturned Victoria.

Air Date: 30th November 2007
Finlay and Hornswoggle toppled Deuce and Domino; ECW Champion CM Punk overturned Kenny Dykstra; Rey Mysterio and Kane bested WWE US Champion MVP and Big Daddy V; Jamie Noble pinned Chuck Palumbo; World Heavyweight Champion Batista battled Edge to a no-contest.

Dec 2007

Air Date: 7th December 2007
WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz defeated Jesse and Festus; Batista rolled over Elijah Burke; Jamie Noble toppled Chuck Palumbo; Finlay downed Deuce; Kane bested Edge by DQ.

Air Date: 14th December 2007
Edge toppled Sho Funaki; Layla and Victoria overturned Kelly Kelly and Michelle McCool; Rey Mysterio toppled Kenny Dykstra; World Heavyweight Champion Batista rolled over Kane in a “last man standing” match; Festus beat The Miz; Hornswoggle battled The Great Khali to a no-contest; The Undertaker rolled over Mark Henry and Big Daddy V in a handicap match.

Air Date: 21st December 2007
Finlay and Hornswoggle toppled The Great Khali and Runjin Singh; ECW Champion CM Punk, Rey Mysterio, and Kane bested WWE US Champion MVP, Mark Henry, and Big Daddy V; Festus beat Deuce; Batista rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Edge, Curt Hawkins and Zack Rider by DQ in a handicap match.
