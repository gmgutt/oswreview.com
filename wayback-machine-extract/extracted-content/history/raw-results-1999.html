Raw Results 1999

WWF RAW RESULTS (1999)

Jan 1999

Air Date: 4th January 1999
Steve Blackman overcame WWF Intercontinental Champion Ken Shamrock in a non-title match; Goldust bested Mark Henry by DQ; The Godfather battled Test to a no-contest; Triple H toppled Mankind; Edge battled D-Lo Brown to a no-contest; Kane battled Pat Patterson and Gerald Brisco in a handicap match; WWF Hardcore Champion Road Dogg pinned Al Snow; Mankind rolled over WWF World Champion The Rock to win the title in a no-DQ match.

Air Date: 11th January 1999
Owen Hart and Jeff Jarrett rolled over Billy Gunn and Road Dogg; Luna Vachon downed Gillberg; WWF European Champion X-Pac bested Al Snow; Kane toppled WWF World Champion Mankind by DQ; Triple H overturned Edge; Chyna won a 10-man Royal Rumble by last eliminating Vince McMahon.

Air Date: 18th January 1999
WWF Hardcore Champion Road Dogg beat Gangrel; Test overcame Billy Gunn; Steve Blackman downed Dan Severn by DQ; WWF World Champion Mankind dropped The Big Bossman by DQ; Al Snow pinned Goldust; Chyna overturned Pat Patterson and Gerald Brisco in a handicap match; Kane rolled over The Rock by DQ.

Air Date: 25th January 1999
Billy Gunn downed Goldust; Droz defeated George Steele; Owen Hart and Jeff Jarrett toppled WWF World Tag Champions Ken Shamrock and The Big Bossman to win the titles; Test dropped Val Venis; Al Snow and WWF Hardcore Champion Road Dogg overturned Edge and Gangrel in a hardcore match; WWF World Champion The Rock rolled over Triple H in an I Quit match.

Feb 1999

Air Date: 1st February 1999
Val Venis overcame Billy Gunn by DQ; The Big Bossman pinned D-Lo Brown; Droz beat Kurrgan; The Undertaker, Mideon and Viscera battled Edge, Christian and Gangrel to a no-contest; The Acolytes overturned WWF Hardcore Champion Road Dogg and Al Snow; Triple H rolled over Kane in a cage match.

Air Date: 13th February 1999
D-Lo Brown beat Jeff Jarrett; Gillberg defeated Goldust; The Godfather dropped Viscera by DQ; X-Pac downed Kane by DQ; The Rock bested Steve Blackman; Vince McMahon, WWF Intercontinental Champion Ken Shamrock, Test, Kane, Chyna and The Big Bossman rolled over Steve Austin in a gauntlet match.

Air Date: 15th February 1999
Debra and Jeff Jarrett battled Ivory and D-Lo Brown to a no-contest; WWF Intercontinental Champion Val Venis bested Billy Gunn; Kane and Shane McMahon toppled Triple H and WWF European Champion X-Pac. By virtue of a pre-match stipulation, McMahon was awarded the WWF European title; WWF Hardcore Champion Hardcore Holly downed Steve Blackman; The Big Bossman, Test and Ken Shamrock battled Mideon and The Acolytes to a no-contest; The Rock rolled over WWF World Champion Mankind in a ladder match to win the title.

Air Date: 22nd February 1999
The Public Enemy downed Edge and Gangrel by DQ; Billy Gunn battled Ken Shamrock to a no-contest; WWF World Tag Champions Owen Hart and Jeff Jarrett defeated D-Lo Brown in a handicap match; WWF World Champion The Rock battled The Big Show to a no-contest; Steve Blackman beat Droz; Goldust pinned WWF Intercontinental Champion Val Venis in a non-title match; WWF Hardcore Champion Hardcore Holly pined Bart Gunn; X-Pac toppled Chyna; The Undertaker dropped Kane in an Inferno Match.

Mar 1999

Air Date: 1st March 1999
WWF Intercontinental Champion Val Venis downed Goldust and Ken Shamrock by count-out in a Triple Threat match; Triple H and X-Pac battled WWF World Tag Champions Owen Hart and Jeff Jarrett to a double-DQ; WWF World Champion The Rock pinned Road Dogg; Droz defeated Steve Blackman in a Kendo Stick Match; Mankind battled The Undertaker to a double count-out; Ivory toppled Debra by DQ; Steve Austin battled Kane to a no-contest.

Air Date: 8th March 1999
D-Lo Brown beat Owen Hart in a streetfight; Billy Gunn and Road Dogg toppled Al Snow and WWF Hardcore Champion Hardcore Holly; Ken Shamrock bested Goldust; Test pinned X-Pac; The Godfather downed Steve Blackman by forfeit; Tori defeated Luna by DQ; Mankind downed Steve Austin by count-out. The Big Show was guest referee.

Air Date: 15th March 1999
Road Dogg toppled WWF Intercontinental Champion Val Venis to win the title; WWF European Champion Shane McMahon beat Gerald Brisco and Pat Patterson in a handicap match; WWF World Tag Champions Owen Hart and Jeff Jarrett dropped The Public Enemy; The Big Bossman battled Mideon to a no-contest in a cage match; Billy Gunn toppled WWF Hardcore Champion Hardcore Holly to win the title; Steve Austin and Mankind battled WWF World Champion The Rock and The Big Show to a no-contest.

Air Date: 22nd March 1999
WWF World Tag Champions Owen Hart and Jeff Jarrett battled Gangrel and Edge to a no-contest; WWF Intercontinental Champion Road Dogg battled WWF Hardcore Champion Billy Gunn to a no-contest; WWF Women’s Champion Sable beat Ivory; Mankind downed WWF World Champion The Rock by DQ; Kane battled Goldust to a no-contest; X-Pac battled WWF European Champion Shane McMahon to a no-contest in a streetfight; The Undertaker and The Acolytes battled The Big Bossman, Test and Ken Shamrock to a no-contest; Steve Austin rolled over The Big Show. Mankind was guest referee.

Air Date: 29th March 1999
Ivory and Tori defeated WWF Women’s Champion Sable and Jackie; The Big Show beat Test; WWF Hardcore Champion Hardcore Holly bested Steve Williams; The Rock pinned Billy Gunn; Ken Shamrock toppled Gangrel; Goldust overcame WWF Intercontinental Champion Road Dogg to win the title; WWF World Tag Champions Owen Hart and Jeff Jarrett downed The Legion of Doom; X-Pac rolled over Triple H by DQ.

Apr 1999

Air Date: 5th April 1999
Kane and X-Pac downed WWF World Tag Champions Jeff Jarrett and Owen Hart to win the titles; Al Snow overcame WWF Hardcore Champion Hardcore Holly in a non-title match; Billy Gunn and Road Dogg toppled Edge and Gangrel; Ken Shamrock battled Viscera to a no-contest; Mankind downed Val Venis; The Godfather battled WWF Intercontinental Champion Goldust to a double count-out; The Big Show toppled The Rock and Triple H by DQ in a handicap match.

Air Date: 12th April 1999
The Big Show downed Christian; Val Venis battled Billy Gunn to a no-contest; Owen Hart and Jeff Jarrett defeated The Acolytes; The Godfather bested WWF Intercontinental Champion Goldust to win the title; Hardcore Holly dropped D-Lo Brown; WWF World Tag Champions X-Pac and Kane rolled over Triple H and Test.

Air Date: 19th April 1999
Road Dogg toppled Owen Hart; The Acolytes beat Edge and Christian by DQ; WWF Intercontinental Champion The Godfather overcame WWF Hardcore Champion Hardcore Holly; Ken Shamrock downed The Big Bossman in a no-holds-barred match. Mankind downed Triple H by DQ; Billy Gunn toppled Jeff Jarrett by DQ; The Big Show overturned Viscera by DQ.

Air Date: 26th April 1999
WWF World Tag Champions Kane and X-Pac downed Edge and Gangrel; D-Lo Brown beat Val Venis; Triple H pinned Billy Gunn; Mankind and The Big Show dropped Test and The Big Bossman; WWF Intercontinental Champion The Godfather overcame Jeff Jarrett; The Rock rolled over Shane McMahon by DQ.

May 1999


Air Date: 3rd May 1999
Billy Gunn toppled X-Pac; Pat Patterson and Gerald Brisco downed Pete Gas and Rodney by count-out; Test dropped The Big Bossman, Mideon and Viscera in a handicap match; The Acolytes beat Mankind in a handicap hardcore match; Vince McMahon rolled over Shane McMahon; Triple H bested Ken Shamrock; The Big Show toppled The Undertaker by DQ; Nicole Bass overcame Debra in a nightgown match; WWF World Champion Steve Austin battled The Rock to a no-contest in a lumberjack match.

Air Date: 10th May 1999
Billy Gunn battled Kane to a no-contest; The Big Show battled Paul Bearer to a no-contest; Debra beat WWF Women’s Champion Sable to win the title in an “evening gown” match; The Big Bossman bested Test in a nightstick match; Cactus Jack dropped Viscera and Mideon in a handicap hardcore match; Bradshaw battled Farooq to a no-contest in a lumberjack match; Pat Patterson and Gerald Brisco defeated Rodney and Pete Gas in a “Loser leaves WWF” match; Jeff Jarrett bested Val Venis; Ken Shamrock battled Chyna to a no-contest; WWF World Champion Steve Austin, The Rock and Vince McMahon rolled over The Undertaker, Triple H and Shane McMahon. Shawn Michaels was guest referee.

Air Date: 17th May 1999
Road Dogg, and WWF World Tag Champions X-Pac and Kane battled Mark Henry, D-Lo Brown and Billy Gunn to a no-contest; WWF Intercontinental Champion The Godfather and Val Venis dropped Jeff Jarrett and The Blue Blazer (Owen Hart); Test defeated Meat by DQ; The Undertaker dropped The Rock in a casket match; Michael Hayes, and Matt and Jeff Hardy battled Gangrel, Edge and Christian to a double-DQ; The Big Show pinned The Big Bossman; WWF World Champion Steve Austin rolled over Triple H by DQ.

Air Date: 24th May 1999
“Owen Hart Tribute Show”: Jeff Jarrett beat Test; WWF World Tag hampions X-Pac and Kane toppled Edge and Gangrel; Matt and Jeff Hardy downed Taka Michinoku and Sho Funaki; Ken Shamrock dropped Hardcore Holly; Mankind overcame Billy Gunn by count-out; D-Lo Brown and Mark Henry beat The Acolytes; Road Dogg battled WWF Intercontinental Champion The Godfather to a no-contest; Triple H bested WWF Hardcore Champion Al Snow in a non-title match; The Big Show defeated Goldust; The Rock rolled over Val Venis

Air Date: 31st May 1999
The Big Show downed Billy Gunn by count-out; Chaz Warrington defeated Christian; Jeff Jarrett overcame WWF Intercontinental Champion The Godfather to win the title; WWF World Champion The Undertaker dropped Vince McMahon by DQ; Road Dogg toppled The Big Bossman by DQ; The Acolytes beat WWF World Tag Champions X-Pac and Kane to win the titles; Val Venis pinned Ken Shamrock; Triple H bested Mankind in a no-DQ falls-count-anywhere match; Steve Austin rolled over WWF World Champion The Undertaker by DQ.

Jun 1999

Air Date: 7th June 1999
WWF World Tag Champions The Acolytes beat Edge and Gangrel; X-Pac and Kane downed Shane McMahon by DQ in a handicap match; The Rock dropped Triple H by DQ in a “cast” match; WWF Women’s Champion Debra downed Nicole Bass in a swimsuit contest; The Godfather pinned Billy Gunn; WWF Hardcore Champion Al Snow beat Droz; Vince McMahon toppled Ken Shamrock in a “Lion’s Den” match; The Big Show battled WWF World Champion The Undertaker to a no-contest.

Air Date: 14th June 1999
Billy Gunn toppled Road Dogg in a “Dog Pound” match; Ivory downed WWF Women’s Champion Debra to win the title; Test defeated The Big Bossman by DQ in a “Blind Date” match; X-Pac battled The Big Show to a no-contest; Ken Shamrock overturned WWF Intercontinental Champion Jeff Jarrett in a non-title “Straight Jacket” match; Pat Patterson and Gerald Brisco battled Viscera and Mideon to a no-contest; WWF World Tag Champions The Acolytes beat Kane in a handicap match; The Rock rolled over WWF World Champion The Undertaker and Triple H.

Air Date: 21st June 1999
WWF Intercontinental Champion Jeff Jarrett defeated Ken Shamrock and Test in a Triple Threat match; The Rock bested Edge; Viscera dropped Mark Henry in a slam match; Steve Austin rolled over The Big Bossman. Shawn Michaels was guest referee; Billy Gunn and WWF World Tag Champions The Acolytes beat Kane, X-Pac and Road Dogg; The Big Show toppled Hardcore Holly in a hardcore match; Vince and Shane McMahon battled Pat Patterson and Gerald Brisco to a no-contest; WWF World Champion The Undertaker dropped Triple H by DQ.

Air Date: 28th June 1999
Chaz beat Meat; Hardcore Holly bested Kane; The Rock dropped Triple H by DQ; The Godfather battled Edge to a no-contest; Bradshaw pinned Billy Gunn; WWF Intercontinental Champion Jeff Jarrett bested X-Pac; Steve Austin rolled over WWF World Champion The Undertaker to win the title.

Jul 1999

Air Date: 5th July 1999
Matt and Jeff Hardy defeated WWF World Tag Champions The Acolytes to win the titles; The Godfather downed Gangrel; Test dropped Joey Abs by DQ; WWF Hardcore Champion Al Snow pinned D-Lo Brown; Road Dogg battled Val Venis to a no-contest; WWF Intercontinental Champion Jeff Jarrett bested Chaz; Edge overcame The Big Bossman; Billy Gunn overturned Meat; Kane toppled The Big Show and Hardcore Holly in a handicap match; The Rock rolled over Triple H in a cage match.

Air Date: 12th July 1999
The Godfather and Val Venis downed WWF World Tag Champions Matt and Jeff Hardy by DQ; The Rock, X-Pac and Road Dogg toppled Triple H, Billy Gunn and Chyna; Al Snow overcame Droz in a “hardcore evening dress” match; Test overturned The Mean Street Posse by DQ in a gauntlet match. Test pinned Pete Gas; Test pinned Rodney Gas; Test downed Joey Abs by DQ; WWF World Champion Steve Austin and The Big Show rolled over The Undertaker and Kane.

Air Date: 19th July 1999
Road Dogg battled Chyna to a no-contest in a dog collar match; WWF World Tag Champions Matt and Jeff Hardy overcame Val Venis and The Godfather, The Acolytes, and Albert and Droz in a Fatal Four Way elimination match; WWF Intercontinental Champion Jeff Jarrett bested Christian; Billy Gunn toppled The Rock; Al Snow battled WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Bossman to a no-contest; Edge and D-Lo Brown downed Gangrel and WWF European Champion Mideon; Test defeated Steve Blackman by DQ; The Big Show and Hardcore Holly battled X-Pac and Kane to a no-contest; The Undertaker battled Triple H to a no-contest.

Air Date: 26th July 1999
WWF World Tag Champions The Acolytes beat Edge and Christian; Val Venis and The Godfather downed Albert and Droz in a streetfight; Kane dropped The Undertaker and The Big Show by DQ in a handicap match; Triple H bested Ken Shamrock by stoppage; The Rock battled Billy Gunn and Chyna to a no-contest in a handicap match.

Aug 1999

Air Date: 2nd August 1999
Edge defeated Gangrel in a Bloodbath match; WWF European Champion D-Lo Brown pinned WWF Intercontinental Champion Jeff Jarrett to unify the titles; The Undertaker and The Big Show toppled Road Dogg and Kane;WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Bossman bested Viscera; Test downed Rodney; Steve Blackman battled Val Venis to a no-contest; WWF World Champion Steve Austin battled The Undertaker to a no-contest in a No-Holds-Barred match.

Air Date: 9th August 1999
Gangrel bested Christian; Val Venis pinned Jeff Jarrett; Kane and X-Pac dropped WWF World Tag Champions The Acolytes to win the titles; Test and Ken Shamrock downed Steve Blackman and Joey Abs; Road Dogg toppled WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Bossman in a non-title match; The Rock overcame The Big Show by DQ; Chyna defeated Triple H and The Undertaker in a no-DQ, falls count anywhere, Triple Threat match. Shawn Michaels was guest referee.

Air Date: 16th August 1999
Road Dogg toppled Al Snow;  Chyna bested Triple H; Test defeated Steve Blackman by DQ; WWF World Tag Champions X-Pac and Kane dropped The Acolytes; Mankind downed Chyna; Mankind battled Triple H to a double-pin no-contest in a no-holds-barred, falls-count-anywhere match. Shane McMahon and Shawn Michaels were guest referees.

Air Date: 23rd August 1999
WWF Hardcore Champion Al Snow battled Road Dogg to a no-contest; The Acolytes battled WWF World Tag Champions The Undertaker and The Big Show to a no-contest; D-Lo Brown downed WWF European Champion Mark Henry by DQ; The Rock pinned Gangrel; Crash Holly battled Hardcore Holly to a no-contest; Mideon and Viscera dropped X-Pac and Kane; Triple H rolled over WWF World Champion Mankind to win the title.

Air Date: 30th August 1999
Pete Gas, Joey Abs and Rodney Gas defeated Mankind by DQ in a handicap match. Shane McMahon was guest referee; The Big Show bested The Rock in a falls-count-anywhere match; X-Pac pinned Taka Michinoku; The Acolytes, Matt and Jeff Hardy, Crash and Hardcore Holly, and Edge and Christian battled to a no-contest in a Fatal Four Way elimination match; WWF Intercontinental Champion Jeff Jarrett and WWF European Champion Mark Henry downed D-Lo Brown and Billy Gunn; Ken Shamrock toppled Gangrel; Mankind and The Rock rolled over WWF World Tag Champions The Big Show and The Undertaker to win the titles.

Sep 1999

Air Date: 6th September 1999
WWF World Tag Champions The Rock and Mankind downed WWF World Champion Triple H and Kane; WWF Intercontinental Champion Jeff Jarrett defeated Jackie; Edge and Christian dropped The Acolytes; The Big Show bested Val Venis; Test, Gerald Brisco and Pat Patterson overcame The Mean Street Posse; Hardcore and Crash Holly beat Matt and Jeff Hardy; D-Lo Brown pinned Steve Blackman; WWF Women’s Champion Ivory overturned Tori in a hardcore match; WWF World Champion Triple H bested Billy Gunn.

Air Date: 13th September 1999
Luna beat WWF Intercontinental Champion Jeff Jarrett by DQ; D-Von Dudley downed Faarooq in a strap match; Test bested Joey Abs; The Godfather defeated Chaz; Chris Jericho dropped Gotch Gracie (a masked Mr Hughes) in a cage match; The Undertaker, Mankind, The Big Show, Kane and The Rock battled to a no-contest in a five-way match; D-Lo Brown and Val Venis overturned Steve Blackman and WWF European Champion Mark Henry; WWF World Champion Triple H bested Steve Austin by DQ.

Air Date: 20th September 1999
Chris Jericho defeated Billy Gunn; The Dudley Boyz downed Matt and Jeff Hardy; Test and Stephanie McMahon dropped WWF Intercontinental Champion Jeff Jarrett and Debra; The Rock and Mankind downed Mideon, Viscera and The Big Show in a handicap match to win the WWF World Tag Titles in a “darkside rules” match; Shawn Stasiak beat Steve Blackman; Hardcore Holly overcame The Big Bossman in a hardcore match; Shane McMahon and Test rolled over Triple H and Chyna by DQ.

Air Date: 27th September 1999
The Big Show defeated Chris Jericho by DQ; WWF European Champion D-Lo Brown downed Steve Blackman by DQ; WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg toppled X-Pac and Kane by DQ; The Fabulous Moolah and Mae Young overturned WWF Women’s Champion Ivory in a handicap evening gown match; Chyna and Debra bested Tom Pritchard and WWF Intercontinental Champion Jeff Jarrett; The Rock battled WWF World Champion Triple H to a no-contest.

Oct 1999

Air Date: 4th October 1999
The Acolytes beat WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg, Kane and X-Pac, and Hardcore and Crash Holly in a non-title Fatal Four Way; Edge and Christian bested Matt and Jeff Hardy; The Fabulous Moolah and Mae Young defeated Miss Kitty and Ivory; Chyna downed Davey Boy Smith by DQ; The Dudley Boyz dropped Mankind and Stevie Richards; The Rock rolled over Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 11th October 1999
Billy Gunn bested Crash Holly; WWF Women’s Champion Ivory dropped Mae Young by DQ; Edge and Christian battled Matt and Jeff Hardy to a double count-out; X-Pac pinned Faarooq; The Headbangers downed Chris Jericho and Mr Hughes; The Godfather bested Mark Henry in a “Ho-Jack” match; The Big Show beat The Big Bossman by DQ; Steve Austin and Jim Ross battled WWF World Champion Triple H and Chyna to a no-contest; The Rock and Mankind battled Val Venis and Davey Boy Smith to a no-contest.

Air Date: 18th October 1999
X-Pac and Kane downed The Dudley Boys; Viscera overcame The Godfather; Test defeated Davey Boy Smith by DQ; Val Venis bested Al Snow; WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Bossman toppled The Big Show by forfeit; Billy Gunn and Road Dogg toppled The Acolytes; Hardcore and Crash Holly rolled over WWF World Tag Champions The Rock and Mankind to win the titles.

Air Date: 25th October 1999
Viscera beat The Godfather; Edge and Christian downed WWF World Tag Champions Hardcore and Crash Holly by DQ; Ivory overcame WWF Women’s Champion The Fabulous Moolah to win the title; The Big Show battled Albert to a no contest; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chyna and WWF European Champion D-Lo Brown dropped Chris Jericho and Stevie Richards; Mankind downed WWF World Champion Triple H by DQ; Kane defeated Bubba Ray Dudley by DQ; Test battled Davey Boy Smith to a no-contest in a cage match; Steve Austin and The Rock rolled over WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg by DQ.

Nov 1999

Air Date: 1st November 1999
The Rock battled Road Dogg to a no-contest; Matt and Jeff Hardy, and Edge and Christian overcame Too Cool and WWF World Tag Champions Hardcore Holly and Crash Holly; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chyna defeated Stevie Richards; Steve Austin rolled over Billy Gunn; Test downed Val Venis by count-out; Kane battled X-Pac to a no-contest; The Headbangers dropped The Dudley Boys; WWF World Champion Triple H bested Shane McMahon. Kane was the guest enforcer. The Rock was the guest time keeper and Steve Austin was guest commentator.

Air Date: 8th November 1999
Road Dogg and Billy Gunn toppled WWF World Tag Champions Mankind and Al Snow to win the titles; The Godfather downed Val Venis by count-out; Edge and Christian, and The Headbangers overcame Davey Boy Smith and The Mean Street Posse; The Rock pinned X-Pac; The Big Bossman downed Kane; Crash and Hardcore Holly downed Too Cool; WWF World Champion Triple H bested Test by count-out. Shane McMahon was guest referee. Vince McMahon was guest time keeper.

Air Date: 15th November 1999
Mankind downed Val Venis; Kurt Angle bested The Godfather; Gangrel defeated Chris Jericho; WWF World Champion The Big Show dropped WWF European Champion Davey Boy Smith by DQ; Road Dogg toppled Al Snow; Test beat Billy Gunn; Triple H bested Kane by DQ; WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Bossman rolled over The Rock in a hardcore match.

Air Date: 22nd November 1999
Crash Holly and Hardcore Holly defeated Mankind and Al Snow; Kurt Angle downed Mark Henry; Chris Jericho dropped The Godfather; Edge and Christian bested The Dudley Boys; X-Pac toppled Jeff Hardy; WWF World Champion The Big Show beat Kane by count-out; Brian Christopher and Scotty 2 Hotty overcame Val Venis and WWF European Champion Davey Boy Smith by DQ; Test overturned Road Dogg. Shane McMahon was guest referee; The Rock and Mankind downed WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Bossman and Albert; Triple H battled The Acolytes to a no contest in a “no-holds-barred” handicap match.

Air Date: 29th November 1999
Viscera and WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Bossman defeated Kane and WWF World Champion The Big Show; Edge downed Matt Hardy; Brian Christopher and Scotty 2 Hotty dropped Hardcore and Crash Holly; Test bested Triple H; Kurt Angle rolled over Val Venis; X-Pac and WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn toppled The Rock, Kane and Mankind by DQ.

Dec 1999

Air Date: 6th December 1999
Too Cool bested WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg by DQ; Kurt Angle and Steve Blackman toppled Matt and Jeff Hardy; Val Venis battled D-Lo Brown to a no-contest; The Godfather beat Albert; Rikishi defeated Hardcore Holly; Triple H and X-Pac downed Test and Kane; WWF World Champion The Big Show pinned Viscera; The Rock and Mankind downed Al Snow and Chris Jericho by DQ.

Air Date: 13th December 1999
WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho bested X-Pac; Mark Henry pinned The Godfather; The Rock and Mankind downed The Dudley Boys; WWF Women’s Champion The Kat dropped Tori in a “chocolate pool” match; The Acolytes won a handicap match over The Mean Street Posse; Edge and Christian toppled Matt and Jeff Hardy in a cage match; WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn toppled Kane in a three-way match; WWF World Champion The Big Show overturned WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Bossman and Albert in a handicap match; WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn battled Triple H and Test to a no contest.

Air Date: 20th December 1999
WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg beat Test in a handicap match; Jeff Hardy and Christian bested Matt Hardy and Edge; Kurt Angle overcame Viscera; The Dudley Boys, The Acolytes, and Mae Young and The Fabulous Moolah battled to a no contest in a three-way; A boiler room brawl between Mankind and Santa Claus (Mean Street Posse) went to a no-decision; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho dropped The Godfather; Hardcore Holly toppled Val Venis in a “Holiday Topless Top Rope” match; Al Snow bested The Rock in a “Brahma Bullrope” match; Rikishi and Too Cool overturned The Mean Street Posse; WWF World Champion The Big Show rolled over Kane in a “falls count anywhere” match.

Air Date: 27th December 1999
Chyna beat Crash Holly; The Dudley Boys won a four-way over The Acolytes, Edge and Christian, and D-Lo Brown and The Godfather. Joey Abs was guest referee; Kurt Angle bested The Headbangers in a handicap match; Al Snow toppled WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho by DQ; The Rock rolled over Mankind in a “pink-slip-on-a-pole” encounter; Ivory and Albert won a four-way over Val Venis and WWF Women’s Champion The Kat, Gangrel and Luna, and Viscera and Jackie; X-Pac pinned Jeff Hardy; Test bested Kane by DQ; WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn toppled Too Cool; WWF World Champion The Big Show pinned Triple H in a no-DQ encounter.
