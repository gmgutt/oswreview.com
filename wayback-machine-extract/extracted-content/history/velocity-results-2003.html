Velocity Results 2003

VELOCITY RESULTS (2003)

Jan 2003

Air Date: 4th January 2003
Jamie Noble and Nunzio beat Jeff Michaelis and Jim Lucas; A-Train dropped Derek Selsvold; Sho Funaki overcame Charles Schipporeit; Chavo Guerrero Jr downed Tajiri.

Air Date: 11th January 2003
Nunzio beat Crash Holly; Diamondback (Brian Kendrick) defeated Sho Funaki; Bill DeMott overturned Mike Wellington; Chuck Palumbo overturned Keiji Sakoda.

Air Date: 18th January 2003
Jamie Noble beat Bryan Danielson; Chuck Palumbo pinned Xavier; The Jet (Spanky) dropped Shannon Moore.

Air Date: 25th January 2003
Chavo Guerrero Jr overturned Sho Funaki; Jamie Noble beat Jeff Starr; Chuck Palumbo pinned The River Rat (Brian Kendrick); WWE Cruiserweight Champion Billy Kidman bested Matt Hardy by DQ.

Air Date: 8th February 2003

Air Date: 15th February 2003
Bill DeMott overcame Sho Funaki; Shannon Moore toppled Mike Knox; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Billy Kidman bested Jamie Noble.

Air Date: 22nd February 2003
WWE Cruiserweight Champion Billy Kidman bested Shannon Moore; Bill DeMott overcame Sho Funaki; Nunzio and Chuck Palumbo beat Nova and Johnny Jeter; Eddie and Chavo Guerrero Jr overturned WWE Tag Team Champions Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas by count-out.

Air Date: 29th March 2003

Air Date: 26th April 2003

Air Date: 31st May 2003
