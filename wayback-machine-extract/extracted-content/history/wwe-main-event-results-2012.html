WWE Main Event Results 2012

WWE MAIN EVENT RESULTS (2012)

Oct 2012

Air Date: 3rd October 2012
Debut edition of the 1 hour show on the Ion Network
WWE World Champion CM Punk rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus; Santino Marella and Zack Ryder overcame Tyson Kidd and Justin Gabriel (WWE World Tag Title #1 Contender Tournament – First Round).

Air Date: 10th October 2012
The Big Show bested Randy Orton; Kofi Kingston downed Michael McGillicutty.

Air Date: 17th October 2012
Kofi Kingston defeated WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz to win the title; Dolph Ziggler bested Zack Ryder.

Air Date: 24th October 2012
Ryback bested Dolph Ziggler; Alberto Del Rio toppled Sin Cara.

Air Date: 31st October 2012
Randy Orton, Rey Mysterio and Sin Cara bested Alberto Del Rio, Darren Young and Titus O’Neill; Wade Barrett dropped Tyson Kidd.

Nov 2012

Air Date: 7th November 2012
Sheamus rolled over Wade Barrett; R-Truth overcame Heath Slater by DQ.

Air Date: 14th November 2012
WWE World Tag Champions Kane and Daniel Bryan bested Cody Rhodes and Damien Sandow; Santino Marella defeated David Otunga.

Air Date: 21st November 2012
Dolph Ziggler bested The Miz; Wade Barrett toppled Justin Gabriel.

Air Date: 28th November 2012
John Cena bested Damien Sandow; WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro toppled Zack Ryder.

Dec 2012

Air Date: 5th December 2012
Kofi Kingston battled WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro to a no-contest; Kofi Kingston and R-Truth bested Wade Barrett and WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro.

Air Date: 12th December 2012
Ryback bested Alberto Del Rio; Jinder Mahal, Heath Slater and Drew McIntyre overturned Justin Gabriel and Tyson Kidd in a handicap match.

Air Date: 19th December 2012
WWE World Tag Champions Kane and Daniel Bryan bested Cody Rhodes and Damien Sandow; Titus O’Neill and Darren Young defeated Santino Marella and Zack Ryder.

Air Date: 26th December 2012
The Great Khali won a battle royal; Tyson Kidd and Justin Gabriel defeated Jinder Mahal and Drew McIntyre.
