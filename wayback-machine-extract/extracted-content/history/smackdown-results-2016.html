Smackdown Results 2016

WWE SMACKDOWN RESULTS (2016)

Jan 2016

Air Date: 7th January 2016
Kalisto bested WWE US Champion Alberto del Río in a non-title match; Dolph Ziggler, Goldust, R-Truth and Neville dropped Big E, Kofi Kingston, Xavier Woods and The Miz; WWE Divas Champion Charlotte defeated Becky Lynch; WWE Intercontinental Champion Dean Ambrose battled Kevin Owens to a double count-out.

Air Date: 14th January 2016
Bubba Ray Dudley and D-Von Dudley defeated Erick Rowan and Luke Harper in a tables match; Heath Slater, Bo Dallas, Adam Rose and Curtis Axel beat Goldust, Damien Sandow, Zack Ryder and Jack Swagger; Alberto del Río toppled WWE US Champion Kalisto to win the title; Becky Lynch beat Brie Bella; WWE Intercontinental Champion Dean Ambrose and Neville battled to a no-contest with Sheamus and Kevin Owens.

Air Date: 21st January 2016
Jimmy Uso, Jey Uso and Dolph Ziggler rolled over Big E, Kofi Kingston and Xavier Woods; Becky Lynch rolled over Alicia Fox; Bray Wyatt beat Ryback; Titus O’Neil overcame Stardust; Roman Reigns beat Rusev, Sheamus, King Barrett and Alberto del Río by DQ in a handicap match.

Air Date: 28th January 2016
Jey Uso, Jimmy Uso, Dolph Ziggler and Titus O’Neil bested Big E, Kofi Kingston, Xavier Woods and The Miz; WWE US Champion Kalisto toppled Neville; WWE Divas Champion Charlotte overcame Natalya; AJ Styles overturned Curtis Axel; Roman Reigns, Dean Ambrose and Chris Jericho rolled over Bray Wyatt, Luke Harper and Erick Rowan by DQ.

Feb 2016

Air Date: 4th February 2016
Roman Reigns overturned Rusev by DQ; WWE US Champion Kalisto bested Kevin Owens; Ryback defeated Erick Rowan; AJ Styles overturned The Miz; Big E, Kofi Kingston and Xavier Woods overturned Adam Rose, Heath Slater and Curtis Axel; WWE Divas Champion Charlotte dropped Alicia Fox; WWE Intercontinental Champion Dean Ambrose and Roman Reigns overturned Alberto del Rio and Rusev.

Air Date: 11th February 2016
Jimmy Uso, Jey Uso and Big Show defeated Big E, Kofi Kingston and Xavier Woods; Jack Swagger rolled over Tyler Breeze; WWE Divas Champion Charlotte defeated Paige. Summer Rae was guest referee; WWE Intercontinental Champion Dean Ambrose defeated Kevin Owens; Goldust defeated Bo Dallas; R-Truth defeated Bo Dallas; Dolph Ziggler rolled over Rusev; Roman Reigns overturned Alberto del Rio in a streetfight.

Air Date: 18th February 2016
Sheamus, Alberto del Río and Rusev defeated Kalisto, Sin Cara and Dolph Ziggler; Sasha Banks overturned Tamina Snuka; Chris Jericho bested The Miz; WWE Divas Champion Charlotte downed Natalya; Roman Reigns and Dean Ambrose downed The Dudley Boys by DQ.

Air Date: 25th February 2016
Alberto del Rio, Sheamus, Rusev and King Barrett rolled over Sin Cara, WWE US Champion Kalisto, Neville and Dolph Ziggler; D-Von Dudley defeated Jimmy Uso; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens defeated The Big Show by count-out; Becky Lynch battled Natalya to a no-contest; R-Truth rolled over Heath Slater; Chris Jericho, AJ Styles and Mark Henry defeated Big E, Kofi Kingston and Xavier Woods.

Mar 2016

Air Date: 3rd March 2016
Sheamus and Rusev beat Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso; Dolph Ziggler rolled over The Miz; Becky Lynch battled to a no-contest with Sasha Banks; AJ Styles overturned Kofi Kingston; Dean Ambrose downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens in a non-title match.

Air Date: 10th March 2016
Sami Zayn and Neville downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens and The Miz; Brie Bella defeated Summer Rae; King Barrett and Sheamus overturned Sin Cara and WWE US Champion Kalisto; Dean Ambrose, Dolph Ziggler, Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso overturned Bray Wyatt, Luke Harper, Erick Rowan and Braun Strowman.

Air Date: 17th March 2016
Dolph Ziggler overcame The Miz; Bubba Ray Dudley beat Goldust; Kofi Kingston toppled King Barrett; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens overturned AJ Styles.

Air Date: 24th March 2016
Sasha Banks overturned WWE Divas Champion Charlotte in a non-title match; Sheamus overturned Kofi Kingston; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso overturned The Ascension; Dolph Ziggler and Sami Zayn toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens and The Miz; Bubba Ray Dudley; AJ Styles overturned Tyler Breeze.

Air Date: 31st March 2016
AJ Styles overturned Heath Slater; D-Von Dudley beat Jey Uso; Dean Ambrose downed Erick Rowan.

Apr 2016

Air Date: 7th April 2016
Aiden English and Simon Gotch rolled over Sin Cara and Kalisto; Natalya defeated Summer Rae; WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz beat Zack Ryder; Apollo Crews overturned Curtis Axel; Dean Ambrose downed Tyler Breeze; AJ Styles and Cesaro bested Chris Jericho and Kevin Owens.

Air Date: 14th April 2016
Baron Corbin toppled Zack Ryder; Enzo Amore and Colin Cassady beat The Ascension (WWE World Tag Title #1 Contender Tournament – 1st Round); AJ Styles overturned Alberto Del Rio; The Vaudevillains overturned Goldust and Fandango (WWE World Tag Title #1 Contender Tournament – 1st Round); Sami Zayn toppled Chris Jericho by DQ.

Air Date: 21st April 2016
Ryback dropped WWE US Champion Kalisto in a non-title match; AJ Styles overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz in a non-title match; Paige and Natalya defeated Naomi and Tamina; R-Truth rolled over Fandango. Goldust was guest referee; Chris Jericho and Kevin Owens overturned Sami Zayn and Dean Ambrose.

Air Date: 28th April 2016
Sami Zayn, Cesaro and WWE US Champion Kalisto bested Sheamus, Rusev and Alberto del Río by count-out; Baron Corbin overcame Damien Sandow; Enzo Amore and Colin Cassady toppled Bo Dallas and Curtis Axel; Apollo Crews overturned Stardust; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Roman Reigns overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz.

May 2016

Air Date: 5th May 2016
Sami Zayn overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz by DQ; Aiden English and Simon Gotch defeated Bo Dallas and Curtis Axel; Natalya and Becky Lynch defeated WWE Women’s Champion Charlotte and Emma; Rusev defeated Zack Ryder; Luke Gallows and Karl Anderson overcame Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso by DQ.

Air Date: 12th May 2016
Rusev defeated Sin Cara; Dana Brooke downed Becky Lynch; Aiden English defeated Kofi Kingston; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso bested Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows by DQ; R-Truth and Tyler Breeze downed Goldust and Fandango; Kevin Owens and WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz defeated Sami Zayn and Cesaro.

Air Date: 19th May 2016
Kevin Owens overturned Cesaro; Dana Brooke downed Paige; Dolph Ziggler rolled over Sheamus; Big E, Kofi Kingston, Xavier Woods and Big Cass overturned D-Von Dudley, Bubba Ray Dudley, Aiden English and Simon Gotch; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Roman Reigns overturned Luke Gallows by DQ.

Air Date: 26th May 2016
Alberto del Río bested Zack Ryder (MITB Qualifier); WWE US Champion Rusev defeated Kalisto; Natalya beat Dana Brooke by DQ; Tyler Breeze and Fandango bested R-Truth and Goldust; WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz defeated Cesaro.

Jun 2016

Air Date: 2nd June 2016
Becky Lynch defeated WWE Women’s Champion Charlotte by DQ; Bubba Ray Dudley and D-Von Dudley toppled Goldust and R-Truth; Dean Ambrose and Sami Zayn overcame Kevin Owens and Alberto del Río; WWE US Champion Rusev defeated Jack Swagger; AJ Styles overturned Kofi Kingston.

Air Date: 9th June 2016
Enzo Amore and Big Cass battled Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows to a no-contest; Kevin Owens and Alberto del Río bested Cesaro and Sami Zayn; Becky Lynch defeated Dana Brooke; Baron Corbin overcame Kalisto; Chris Jericho bested Dean Ambrose.

Air Date: 16th June 2016
Kofi Kingston overcame Aiden English, Big Cass and Luke Gallows in a four-way; Baron Corbin overcame Zack Ryder; AJ Styles overturned Xavier Woods; WWE Women’s Champion Charlotte downed Natalya; Dean Ambrose, Cesaro and Sami Zayn overcame Kevin Owens, Chris Jericho and Alberto del Río.

Air Date: 23rd June 2016
Cesaro bested Alberto del Río; Sheamus overturned Apollo Crews by count-out; AJ Styles overturned Jimmy Uso; Big E and Kofi Kingston overcame Aiden English and Simon Gotch; Dana Brooke downed Becky Lynch; Seth Rollins overturned Sami Zayn.

Air Date: 30th June 2016
Cesaro bested Alberto del Río, Apollo Crews and Sheamus in a four-way; WWE US Champion Rusev defeated Cesaro; Dana Brooke downed Billie Kay; Braun Strowman and Erick Rowan overcame Mike Duwaddy and Aaron Hale; Sasha Banks overturned Summer Rae; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Dean Ambrose downed WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz.

Jul 2016

Air Date: 7th July 2016
Seth Rollins overturned Jey Uso; Zack Ryder rolled over Sheamus; AJ Styles overturned Enzo Amore; WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz defeated Kalisto; Sami Zayn overcame Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 14th July 2016
Sasha Banks overturned Dana Brooke; Dolph Ziggler rolled over WWE US Champion Rusev; AJ Styles and Karl Anderson overcame Enzo Amore and Big Cass; Zack Ryder rolled over Alberto del Río; Kalisto bested Tyler Breeze; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Dean Ambrose downed Kevin Owens by DQ; Seth Rollins and Kevin Owens overturned WWE World Heavyweight Champion Dean Ambrose and Sami Zayn.

Air Date: 19th July 2016
John Cena beat Luke Gallows; Darren Young and Zack Ryder rolled over WWE US Champion Rusev and WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz; Bray Wyatt defeated Xavier Woods; WWE Women’s Champion Charlotte and Dana Brooke downed Sasha Banks in a handicap match; Chris Jericho bested Cesaro; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Dean Ambrose defeated Seth Rollins.

Air Date: 26th July 2016
Apollo Crews won a battle royal; Becky Lynch defeated Natalya; Randy Orton overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz in a non-title match; Dolph Ziggler rolled over AJ Styles, Apollo Crews, Baron Corbin, Bray Wyatt and John Cena in a six-way.

Aug 2016

Air Date: 2nd August 2016
Apollo Crews overturned Baron Corbin and Kalisto in a three-way; Chad Gable and Jason Jordan overcame Simon Gotch and Aiden English; Randy Orton overcame Fandango by DQ; Dolph Ziggler rolled over Bray Wyatt.

Air Date: 9th August 2016
Chad Gable and Jason Jordan overcame Mikey O’Shea and Mike Vega; Alexa Bliss overturned Becky Lynch; Randy Orton overcame Alberto del Río by DQ; Rhyno bested Heath Slater; Carmella beat Natalya; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Dean Ambrose and Dolph Ziggler rolled over Bray Wyatt and Erick Rowan.

Air Date: 16th August 2016
Jimmy Uso, Jey Uso, Mojo Rawley, Zack Ryder, Jason Jordan and Chad Gable downed Aiden English, Viktor, Konnor, Fandango, Tyler Breeze and Simon Gotch; Heath Slater rolled over Randy Orton by DQ; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Dean Ambrose downed Erick Rowan; Becky Lynch and Carmella beat Natalya and Alexa Bliss; John Cena beat Alberto del Río.

Air Date: 23rd August 2016
Becky Lynch defeated Alexa Bliss; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso bested Konnor and Viktor (WWE Smackdown Tag Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); Jason Jordan and Chad Gable downed Tyler Breeze and Fandango (WWE Smackdown Tag Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); AJ Styles overturned Dolph Ziggler.

Air Date: 30th August 2016
Mojo Rawley and Zack Ryder rolled over Simon Gotch and Aiden English (WWE Smackdown Tag Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); AJ Styles overturned Apollo Crews; Alexa Bliss and Natalya beat Becky Lynch and Naomi; Heath Slater and Rhyno bested Mosh and Thrasher (WWE Smackdown Tag Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); Baron Corbin overcame WWE World Champion Dean Ambrose by DQ.

Sep 2016

Air Date: 6th September 2016
WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz defeated Apollo Crews; Carmella, Alexa Bliss and Natalya beat Nikki Bella, Becky Lynch and Naomi; Jason Jordan and Chad Gable downed Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso (WWE Smackdown Tag Title Tournament –Semi-Final); Heath Slater and Rhyno bested Mojo Rawley and Zack Ryder (WWE Smackdown Tag Title Tournament –Semi-Final).

Air Date: 13th September 2016
Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso bested Mojo Rawley and Zack Ryder; Alexa Bliss overturned Carmella, Naomi, Natalya and Nikki Bella in a five-way; WWE Smackdown Tag Champions Heath Slater and Rhyno defeated Konnor and Viktor; John Cena and Dean Ambrose downed WWE World Champion AJ Styles and WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz.

Air Date: 20th September 2016
Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso bested Chad Gable and Jason Jordan; Baron Corbin overcame Apollo Crews; WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz defeated Dolph Ziggler; Randy Orton overcame Erick Rowan; Nikki Bella and Naomi dropped Natalya and Carmella by DQ; Dean Ambrose downed John Cena.

Air Date: 27th September 2016
Jimmy Uso, Jey Uso, Konnor and Viktor rolled over Chad Gable, Jason Jordan, and WWE Smackdown Tag Champions Heath Slater and Rhyno; Natalya and Carmella beat Naomi and Nikki Bella; WWE World Champion AJ Styles defeated Dean Ambrose; Randy Orton and Dolph Ziggler rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz and Bray Wyatt.

Oct 2016

Air Date: 4th October 2016
Kane downed Bray Wyatt by count-out; Nikki Bella beat Alexa Bliss by DQ; Carmella and Alexa Bliss overturned WWE Smackdown Women’s Champion Becky Lynch and Nikki Bella; Zack Ryder and Mojo Rawley toppled Simon Gotch and Aiden English; Jason Jordan overcame Jey Uso; Jack Swagger rolled over Baron Corbin.

Air Date: 11th October 2016
WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler rolled over Kenny and Mikey in a handicap match; Naomi dropped Carmella; Jimmy Uso bested Chad Gable; James Ellsworth defeated WWE World Champion AJ Styles in a non-title match. Dean Ambrose was guest referee; Bray Wyatt and Luke Harper rolled over Kane and Randy Orton.

Air Date: 18th October 2016
Randy Orton overcame Luke Harper by DQ; Alexa Bliss overturned Naomi; The Miz, Mikey and Kenny toppled Heath Slater, Rhyno and WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler; Baron Corbin overcame Jack Swagger; James Ellsworth defeated WWE World Champion AJ Styles by DQ.

Air Date: 25th October 2016
Bray Wyatt defeated Kane in a no-DQ match; Mojo Rawley and Zack Ryder rolled over Konnor and Viktor; Nikki Bella beat Natalya; WWE Smackdown Tag Champions Heath Slater and Rhyno defeated Kenny and Mikey; WWE World Champion AJ Styles overturned Dean Ambrose by DQ.

Nov 2016

Air Date: 1st November 2016
Randy Orton overcame Kane in a no-DQ match; Carmella and Alexa Bliss overturned Nikki Bella and Becky Lynch; Jason Jordan and Chad Gable downed Kenny and Mikey; WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler defeated Curt Hawkins; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso bested Mosh and Thrasher; Dean Ambrose downed WWE World Champion AJ Styles in a non-title match.

Air Date: 8th November 2016
Tyler Breeze and Fandango bested Simon Gotch and Aiden English; Naomi dropped Natalya; WWE Smackdown Women’s Champion Becky Lynch defeated Alexa Bliss; Curt Hawkins overturned Apollo Crews; Bray Wyatt, Luke Harper and Randy Orton overcame Dean Ambrose, Kane and James Ellsworth.

Air Date: 15th November 2016
The Miz defeated WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler to win the title; Kalisto bested Oney Lorcan; Carmella battled Nikki Bella to a no-contest; Zack Ryder, Chad Gable, Jason Jordan, Tyler Breeze, Fandango, Jey Uso, Jimmy Uso and Mojo Rawley toppled Thrasher, Simon Gotch, Aiden English, Mikey, Kenny, Viktor, Konnor and Mosh.

Air Date: 22nd November 2016
WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz defeated Kalisto; Chad Gable and Jason Jordan won a Tag Team Turmoil match over Aiden English and Simon Gotch, Tyler Breeze and Fandango, Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso, Konnor and Viktor, and Mojo Rawley and Zack Ryder; WWE Smackdown Women’s Champion Becky Lynch defeated Natalya; Baron Corbin overcame Kane by DQ; James Ellsworth defeated WWE World Champion AJ Styles in a non-title ladder match.

Air Date: 29th November 2016
Zack Ryder and Mojo Rawley toppled Fandango and Tyler Breeze; Kalisto and Dolph Ziggler rolled over Baron Corbin and WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz by DQ; Kane downed Luke Harper; Bray Wyatt and Randy Orton overcame Chad Gable and Jason Jordan.

Dec 2016

Air Date: 6th December 2016
WWE Smackdown Tag Champions Randy Orton and Bray Wyatt defeated Heath Slater and Rhyno; Zack Ryder and Mojo Rawley toppled Konnor and Viktor; Baron Corbin overcame Kalisto; Chad Gable downed Tyler Breeze; WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz defeated Dean Ambrose.

Air Date: 13th December 2016
Carmella beat Natalya; Zack Ryder won a battle royal; Becky Lynch defeated WWE Smackdown Women’s Champion Alexa Bliss by count-out; Dolph Ziggler rolled over Dean Ambrose, Luke Harper and The Miz in a four-way.

Air Date: 20th December 2016
Heath Slater and Rhyno bested Simon Gotch and Aiden English; WWE Champion AJ Styles defeated James Ellsworth; WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz defeated Apollo Crews; Dean Ambrose downed Luke Harper; La Luchadora beat Alexa Bliss; Mojo Rawley toppled Curt Hawkins; Baron Corbin battled Dolph Ziggler to a double countout.

Air Date: 27th December 2016
Jason Jordan and Chad Gable downed Heath Slater and Rhyno, Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso, and WWE Smackdown Tag Champions Luke Harper and Randy Orton to win the titles; WWE Smackdown Women’s Champion Alexa Bliss defeated Becky Lynch; WWE World Champion AJ Styles defeated Baron Corbin and Dolph Ziggler in a three-way.
