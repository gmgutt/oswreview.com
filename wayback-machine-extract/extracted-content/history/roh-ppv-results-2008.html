ROH PPV Results 2008

ROH PPV RESULTS 2008

16th March 2008: “ROH Take No Prisoners”: Philadelphia, PA – 900
Shown on PPV on 05/30/2008
Dark matches: Davey Richards beat Dingo; Sara Del Rey defeated Kylie Pierce; Ruckus and Jigsaw dropped Adam Pearce and BJ Whitmer; Luke Williams downed Shane Hagadorn;
PPV Broadcast: Tyler Black won a four-way over Claudio Castagnoli, Delirious and Go Shiozaki (ROH Title #1 Contender Tournament – Final); Kevin Steen defeated FIP Champion Roderick Strong in a non-title match; Mark and Jay Briscoe downed Joey Matthews and Necro Butcher in a streetfight; Brent Albright pinned Erick Stevens; Bryan Danielson bested Austin Aries; ROH Champion Nigel McGuinness rolled over Tyler Black.

7th June 2008: “ROH Respect is Earned 2008”: Philadelphia, PA – 600
Shown on PPV on 08/01/2008
Dark matches: Mitch Franklin and Shane Hagadorn beat Amasis and Ophidian; Rhett Titus overturned Ernie Osiris to win the “Top of the Class” trophy; Jigsaw defeated Eddie Kingston; Necro Butcher rolled over Jay Briscoe in a “falls-count-anywhere” match.
PPV Broadcast: Kevin Steen and El Generico defeated Ruckus and Jigsaw; Claudio Castagnoli pinned Davey Richards; Brent Albright, Delirious and Pelle Primeau downed Chris Hero, Adam Pearce and Eddie Edwards; FIP Champion Roderick Strong dropped Erick Stevens in a streetfight; ROH Champion Nigel McGuinness bested Go Shiozaki; ROH Tag Team Champions Tyler Black and Jimmy Jacobs toppled Bryan Danielson and Austin Aries.

26th July 2008: “ROH New Horizons”: Detroit, MI – 750
Shown on PPV on 09/26/2008
Dark matches: Alex Payne, Ernie Osiris and Kyle Durden beat Ninja White, Ninja Yellow and Rocky Fontaine; D-Ray 3000 defeated Shane Hollister; Silas Young beat Kenny Omega; Ruckus won an “Honor Rumble”.
PPV Broadcast: Mark and Jay Briscoe defeated Mitch Franklin and Silas Young; Erick Stevens downed Delirious, Ruckus and Shane Hagadorn in a four-way; Kevin Steen dropped Necro Butcher; ROH Champion Nigel McGuinness pinned Claudio Castagnoli; Roderick Strong and Naomichi Marufuji overturned Chris Hero and Go Shiozaki. Lance Storm was the guest enforcer; Bryan Danielson bested Tyler Black.

19th September 2008: “ROH Driven 2008”: Boston, MA
Shown on PPV on 11/14/2008
Dark matches: Ernie Osiris beat Frankie Arion; Bob Evans defeated Alex Payne; Chasyn Rance defeated Grizzly Redwood; Daizee Haze pinned Madison Eagles; Rhett Titus beat Josh Daniels.
PPV Broadcast: Austin Aries downed Delirious; Sara Del Ray dropped Jessie McKay; Brent Albright and Erick Stevens overcame Adam Pearce and Eddie Edwards; Chris Hero overturned Jerry Lynn; Bryan Danielson bested Claudio Castagnoli and Go Shiozaki in a three-way elimination match; Mark and Jay Briscoe defeated Jigsaw and Ruckus, Jason Blade and Kenny King, and Necro Butcher in a “scramble” match; ROH Champion Nigel McGuinness toppled Roderick Strong; El Generico and Kevin Steen rolled over ROH Tag Team Champions Jimmy Jacobs and Tyler Black to win the titles.

22nd November 2008: “ROH Rising Above 2009”: Chicago Ridge, IL
Shown on PPV on 01/16/2009
Dark matches: Dash Phoenix and Tweek Phoenix overturned Aaron Scott and Grizzly Redwood; Samoa Joe rolled over Tyler Black.
PPV Broadcast: ROH Tag Champions Kevin Steen and El Generico beat Mark and Jay Briscoe; Shimmer Champion MsChif defeated Sara Del Ray; Claudio Castagnoli overcame Silas Young, Alex Payne and Sami Callihan in a four-way; Davey Richards, Go Shiozaki and Chris Hero overturned Roderick Strong, Ace Steel and Brent Albright; Austin Aries toppled Jimmy Jacobs in an I Quit match; ROH Champion Nigel McGuinness rolled over Bryan Danielson.
