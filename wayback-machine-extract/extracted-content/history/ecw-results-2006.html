ECW Results 2006

ECW RESULTS (2006)

Jun 2006

Air Date: 13th June 2006
The Sandman beat The Zombie (Tim Arson); Kurt Angle bested Justin Credible; Sabu won an “extreme battle royal”.

Air Date: 20th June 2006
Sabu defeated Tony Mamaluke; The Sandman downed Macho Libre (Tony DeVito); ECW World and WWE World Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Edge and Randy Orton.

Air Date: 27th June 2006
Sabu beat Roadkill in an “extreme rules” match; Mike Knox downed Danny Doring; ECW World and WWE World Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Kurt Angle.

Jul 2006

Air Date: 4th July 2006
Mike Knox beat Little Guido; Test downed Al Snow; The Big Show overturned ECW World Champion Rob Van Dam in an “extreme rules” match to win the title.

Air Date: 11th July 2006
Test downed Tommy Dreamer; Justin Credible bested Sabu by DQ; ECW World Champion The Big Show toppled Ric Flair in an “extreme rules” match.

Air Date: 18th July 2006
Mike Knox overcame The Sandman in an “extreme rules” match; Sabu toppled Steven Richards; ECW World Champion The Big Show battled The Undertaker to a no-contest.

Air Date: 25th July 2006
The Sandman beat Mike Knox by DQ; The Vampire downed Little Guido; Justin Credible overcame Balls Mahoney by DQ; ECW World Champion The Big Show pinned Kane in an “extreme rules” match.

Aug 2006

Air Date: 1st August 2006
Test and Mike Knox defeated Tommy Dreamer and The Sandman in an “extreme rules” match; CM Punk dropped Justin Credible; Kurt Angle bested The Brooklyn Brawler; Batista dropped ECW World Champion The Big Show by DQ.

Air Date: 8th August 2006
Mike Knox defeated Tommy Dreamer; Mike Knox and Test downed The Sandman by DQ in a handicap match; Kevin Thorn dropped Al Snow; Sabu battled Kurt Angle to a no-contest.

Air Date: 15th August 2006
CM Punk defeated Justin Credible; Test and Mike Knox dropped Little Guido and Tony Mamaluke; Sabu bested Rob Van Dam in a ladder match.

Air Date: 22nd August 2006
Tommy Dreamer, The Sandman, and Torrie Wilson beat Mike Knox, Test, and Kelly Kelly; Kevin Thorn dropped Balls Mahoney in an “extreme rules” match; CM Punk defeated CW Anderson; Rob Van Dam downed Danny Doring; ECW World Champion The Big Show beat Sabu by DQ.

Air Date: 29th August 2006
Hardcore Holly downed Rob Van Dam by DQ; CM Punk defeated Stevie Richards; Paul Heyman beat Sabu in an “extreme rules” match.

Sep 2006

Air Date: 5th September 2006
Sabu and Rob Van Dam downed Test and Mike Knox in an “extreme rules” match; Stevie Richards toppled Balls Mahoney; Triple H and Shawn Michaels toppled ECW World Champion The Big Show by DQ in a handicap match.

Air Date: 12th September 2006
Rob Van Dam downed Hardcore Holly by DQ; CM Punk defeated Shannon Moore; Rene Dupree overcame Balls Mahoney; ECW World Champion The Big Show rolled over Sabu in an “extreme rules” match.

Air Date: 19th September 2006
Sabu, Tommy Dreamer, and The Sandman beat Matt Striker, Test, and Mike Knox by DQ; Kevin Thorn bested Balls Mahoney; CM Punk defeated Shannon Moore; World Heavyweight Champion King Booker rolled oved Rob Van Dam in an “extreme rules” match.

Air Date: 26th September 2006
Rob Van Dam downed Hardcore Holly in an “extreme rules” match; Ariel battled Francine to a no-contest in an “extreme catfight”; ECW World Champion The Big Show beat The Sandman.

Oct 2006

Air Date: 3rd October 2006
ECW World Champion The Big Show and Matt Striker overcame The Sandman and Sabu; Kevin Thorn bested Tommy Dreamer; CM Punk defeated Danny Doring; Test pinned Rob Van Dam in an “extreme rules” match.

Air Date: 10th October 2006
CM Punk defeated Rene Dupree; Tommy Dreamer pinned Kevin Thorn; Rob Van Dam, Sabu, and The Sandman beat ECW World Champion The Big Show, Matt Striker, and Test.

Air Date: 17th October 2006
The Sandman beat Matt Striker by count-out in a “Singapore-cane-on-a pole” match; CM Punk defeated Rene Dupree; Test pinned Balls Mahoney; Sabu dropped Shannon Moore; Rob Van Dam downed ECW World Champion The Big Show in a non-title match.

Air Date: 24th October 2006
CM Punk defeated Matt Striker by DQ; Test pinned Hardcore Holly; Rob Van Dam downed ECW World Champion The Big Show in a non-title ladder match.

Air Date: 31st October 2006
Sabu defeated Kevin Thorn; Daivari pinned Shannon Moore; ECW World Champion The Big Show and Test defeated Rob Van Dam and Hardcore Holly.

Nov 2006

Air Date: 7th November 2006
CM Punk defeated Mike Knox; Daivari bested Little Guido; Test pinned Tommy Dreamer; Rob Van Dam and Hardcore Holly battled ECW World Champion The Big Show and Paul Heyman to a no-contest.

Air Date: 14th November 2006
CM Punk bested Mike Knox; Tommy Dreamer defeated Daivari by DQ; Bobby Lashley dropped Hardcore Holly by DQ.

Air Date: 21st November 2006
Bobby Lashley dropped Matt Striker; WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy and Matt Hardy downed Little Guido and Tony Mamaluke; CM Punk defeated Kevin Thorn; Rob Van Dam downed Hardcore Holly in an “extreme rules” match.

Air Date: 28th November 2006
Rob Van Dam bested Sabu; Matt Hardy and WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Sylvester Terkay and Elijah Burke; CM Punk battled Test to a double count-out; Bobby Lashley dropped ECW World Champion The Big Show by DQ.

Dec 2006

Air Date: 5th December 2006
Rob Van Dam and CM Punk defeated Hardcore Holly and Test by DQ; Tommy Dreamer pinned Daivari; Kelly Kelly downed Ariel; ECW World Champion Bobby Lashley dropped The Big Show.

Air Date: 16th December 2006
Test bested Rob Van Dam; CM Punk toppled Hardcore Holly by DQ; Tommy Dreamer pinned Daivari; ECW World Champion Bobby Lashley dropped Doug Basham and Derrick Neikirk in a handicap match.

Air Date: 19th December 2006
ECW World Champion Bobby Lashley dropped Rene Dupree; Matt Striker overcame Balls Mahoney; CM Punk battled Hardcore Holly to a no-contest; The Great Khali overturned Shannon Moore; Test won a three-way over Rob Van Dam and Sabu.

Air Date: 26th December 2006
