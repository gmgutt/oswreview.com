WWF THE MAIN EVENT RESULTS

 

The Main Event
Air Date: 5th February 1988 (Live) – Indianapolis, IN
Randy Savage bested WWF Intercontinental Champion The Honkytonk Man by count-out; Andre The Giant pinned WWF World Champion Hulk Hogan to win the title; WWF World Tag Champions Rick Martel and Tito Santana defeated Bret Hart and Jim Neidhart.

The Main Event II
Air Date: 3rd February 1989 (Live) – Milwaukee, WI
Hulk Hogan and WWF World Champion Randy Savage rolled over The Big Bossman and Akeem; Ted Dibiase dropped Hercules.

The Main Event III
Air Date: 23rd February 1990 (Live) – Detroit, MI
WWF World Champion Hulk Hogan pinned Randy Savage. Buster Douglas was special outside referee; WWF Intercontinental Champion The Ultimate Warrior downed Dino Bravo.

The Main Event IV
Air Date: 23rd November 1990 (Taped 10/30/1990) – Fort Wayne, IN
WWF World Champion The Ultimate Warrior downed Ted Dibiase by DQ; Sgt Slaughter battled Nikolai Volkoff to a no-contest; Curt Hennig overcame The Big Bossman by count-out; Rick Martel toppled Tito Santana.

The Main Event V
Air Date: 1st February 1991 (Taped 01/28/1991) – Macon, GA
Hulk Hogan and Tugboat defeated Earthquake and Dino Bravo; Jim Duggan downed WWF World Champion Sgt Slaughter by DQ; The Legion of Doom dropped The Orient Express.
