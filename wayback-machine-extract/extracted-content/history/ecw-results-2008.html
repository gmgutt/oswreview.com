ECW Results 2008

ECW RESULTS (2008)

Jan 2008

Air Date: 1st January 2008
Kelly Kelly, Jimmy Wang Yang, and Shannon Moore downed Layla, WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz; Big Daddy V dropped Colin Delany; Shelton Benjamin overcame James Curtis; ECW Champion CM Punk overturned Chavo Guerrero Jr by count-out.

Air Date: 8th January 2008
WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz battled Jimmy Wang Yang and Shannon Moore to a draw in a 15-minute Ironman match; Mark Henry pinned Colin Delany; ECW Champion CM Punk bested Chavo Guerrero Jr by DQ.

Air Date: 15th January 2008
WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz defeated Robbie and Rory McAllister; Kane pinned Colin Delany; Shelton Benjamin downed Nunzio; Chavo Guerrero Jr dropped ECW Champion CM Punk by count-out.

Air Date: 22nd January 2008
Shelton Benjamin won a 5-man battle royal; Kelly Kelly defeated Lena and Layla in a dance contest; Kofi Kingston overcame David Owens; The Great Khali beat Colin Delany; Chavo Guerrero Jr pinned ECW Champion CM Punk to win the title in a no-DQ match.

Air Date: 29th January 2008
Kane defeated Shelton Benjamin by count-out; Victoria pinned Kelly Kelly; WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz downed Colin Delany in a handicap match; CM Punk pinned Elijah Burke; Kofi Kingston overcame Rob Echoes.

Feb 2008

Air Date: 5th February 2008
Victoria and Layla defeated Kelly Kelly and Michelle McCool; John Morrison pinned Tommy Dreamer; Kofi Kingston downed James Curtis; CM Punk dropped ECW Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr in a non-title “Gulf of Mexico Extreme Rules” match.

Air Date: 12th February 2008
Stevie Richards defeated Rory Fox; Kelly Kelly overcame Layla; Kofi Kingston downed Mike Knox; Tommy Dreamer pinned John Morrison; CM Punk dropped Mark Henry by DQ.

Air Date: 19th February 2008
Kofi Kingston overturned Jason Riggs; The Miz bested Colin Delaney; Stevie Richards pinned James Curtis; Ric Flair and CM Punk toppled Elijah Burke and Shelton Benjamin.

Air Date: 26th February 2008
Kofi Kingston and Kelly Kelly defeated Santino Marella and Layla; Stevie Richards pinned Mike Knox; Tommy Dreamer and Colin Delaney downed WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz in a non-title match; CM Punk dropped Shelton Benjamin and Elijah Burke in a three-way.

Mar 2008

Air Date: 4th March 2008
WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz overcame Tommy Dreamer and Colin Delaney by DQ; Shelton Benjamin pinned Stevie Richards; Kane downed James Curtis; ECW Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr overturned CM Punk.

Air Date: 11th March 2008
WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz overcame Tommy Dreamer and Colin Delaney in an “extreme rules” match; The Great Khali squashed Stevie Richards; Kofi Kingston dropped Deuce; Festus overcame Elijah Burke; CM Punk overturned Big Daddy V by count-out.

Air Date: 18th March 2008
Kane bested Elijah Burke; Kofi Kingston dropped James Curtis; CM Punk, Jesse and Festus defeated Shelton Benjamin and WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz; ECW Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr overturned Colin Delaney.

Air Date: 25th March 2008
The Great Khali, The Miz, Chuck Palumbo, Matt Striker, Mark Henry, Snitsky, Lance Cade, Trevor Murdoch, Brian Kendrick, Deuce, Domino, and Elijah Burke downed Kane, Tommy Dreamer, Hardcore Holly, Cody Rhodes, Kofi Kingston, Jesse, Festus, Jimmy Wang Yang, Shannon Moore, Jim Duggan, Val Venis, and Jamie Noble; Shelton Benjamin pinned Carlito; Mike Knox and Layla defeated Stevie Richards and Kelly Kelly; ECW Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr overturned Tommy Dreamer.

Apr 2008

Air Date: 1st April 2008
Mike Knox pinned Stevie Richards; The Big Show defeated Colin Delaney; ECW Champion Kane and CM Punk downed Chavo Guerrero Jr and Shelton Benjamin.

Air Date: 8th April 2008
CM Punk pinned The Miz; Elijah Burke beat Nunzio; Kofi Kingston dropped Domino; Tommy Dreamer overcame Colin Delaney.

Air Date: 15th April 2008
CM Punk, Kofi Kingston, Jimmy Wang Yang, and Shannon Moore defeated Deuce, Domino, Elijah Burke, and Shelton Benjamin; Mike Knox downed Colin Delaney; The Undertaker and Kane bested WWE Tag Champions The Miz and John Morrison in a non-title match.

Air Date: 22nd April 2008
Tommy Dreamer and Kelly Kelly beat Mike Knox and Layla; Jimmy Wang Yang pinned John Morrison; Kofi Kingston dropped Shelton Benjamin; Mark Henry overturned Nunzio.

Air Date: 29th April 2008
Kofi Kingston dropped Shelton Benjamin; Mike Knox pinned Tommy Dreamer; CM Punk defeated Chuck Palumbo by DQ; Chavo Guerrero Jr and Bam Neely downed ECW Champion Kane in a non-title handicap match.

May 2008

Air Date: 6th May 2008
Mike Knox pinned Tommy Dreamer in an “extreme rules” match; Kofi Kingston dropped Matt Striker; Colin Delaney beat Armando Estrada; ECW Champion Kane and CM Punk defeated Chavo Guerrero Jr and Bam Neely.

Air Date: 13th May 2008
The Miz pinned CM Punk; Michelle McCool, Kelly Kelly, and Cherry defeated Victoria, Natalya, and Layla; Mike Knox beat Colin Delaney; Armando Estrada downed Colin Delaney; John Morrison overcame ECW Champion Kane in a non-title match.

Air Date: 20th May 2008
The Miz overcame ECW Champion Kane by DQ; Shelton Benjamin pinned Kofi Kingston; Colin Delaney and Kelly Kelly beat Mike Knox and Layla; Armando Estrada defeated Colin Delaney; Chavo Guerrero Jr and John Morrison bested CM Punk and Tommy Dreamer.

Air Date: 27th May 2008
The Big Show bested Tommy Dreamer; Kofi Kingston dropped Mike Knox; Chavo Guerrero overcame CM Punk; ECW Champion Kane beat WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz by DQ in a handicap match.

Jun 2008

Air Date: 3rd June 2008
CM Punk won a four-way over Chavo Guerrero; John Morrison and Tommy Dreamer; Shelton Benjamin defeated Matt Sydal by count-out; WWE US Champion Matt Hardy toppled Armando Estrada; Colin Delaney downed Armando Estrada; ECW Champion Kane pinned CM Punk.

Air Date: 10th June 2008
Kofi Kingston and Evan Bourne defeated Mike Knox and Shelton Benjamin; Victoria downed Kelly Kelly by count-out; Finlay defeated Armando Estrada; Colin Delaney beat Armando Estrada; Hornswoggle overcame Armando Estrada; WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz overcame ECW Champion Kane and CM Punk.

Air Date: 17th June 2008
Hornswoggle beat Armando Estrada; Evan Bourne pinned Matt Stryker; Kofi Kingston defeated Mike Knox by DQ; CM Punk downed John Morrison.

Air Date: 24th June 2008
Matt Hardy and CM Punk overturned WWE Tag Champions The Miz and John Morrison in a non-title match; Kofi Kingston dropped Shelton Benjamin in an “extreme rules” encounter; Mike Knox beat Evan Bourne; Mark Henry toppled ECW Champion Kane in a non-title match.

Jul 2008

Air Date: 1st July 2008
Finlay, Hornswoggle and WWE US Champion Matt Hardy toppled WWE Tag Champions The Miz and John Morrison, and Chavo Guerrero; Atlas Ortiz beat Armando Estrada; ECW Champion Mark Henry downed Colin Delaney.

Air Date: 8th July 2008
Finlay defeated The Miz; Braden Walker (Chris Harris) downed Armando Estrada; Evan Bourne pinned Nunzio; ECW Champion Mark Henry battled Tommy Dreamer to a double count-out.

Air Date: 15th July 2008
Mike Knox defeated Shannon Moore; Evan Bourne downed Chavo Guerrero; Matt and Jeff Hardy toppled WWE Tag Champions The Miz and John Morrison.

Air Date: 22nd July 2008
Tommy Dreamer defeated Colin Delaney; Evan Bourne downed James Curtis; Matt Hardy won a four-way over Finlay, John Morrison and The Miz.

Air Date: 29th July 2008
Ricky Ortiz beat Chavo Guerrero by DQ; The Miz and John Morrison defeated Finlay and Hornswoggle; Matt Hardy toppled Colin Delaney.

Aug 2008

Air Date: 5th August 2008
Evan Bourne and Ricky Ortiz defeated Chavo Guerrero and Bam Neely; Armando Estrada downed Tommy Dreamer; Braden Walker dropped James Curtis.

Air Date: 12th August 2008
Tommy Dreamer defeated Colin Delaney in an “Extreme Rules” match; Evan Bourne pinned Bam Neely; Finlay defeated Armando Estrada; Matt Hardy and ECW Champion Mark Henry overturned The Miz and John Morrison.

Air Date: 19th August 2008
The Miz, John Morrison and Chavo Guerrero beat Evan Bourne, Super Crazy and Tommy Dreamer; Ricky Ortiz defeated Gavin Spears; ECW Champion Mark Henry bested Matt Hardy.

Air Date: 26th August 2008
Matt Hardy toppled John Morrison; The Miz overcame Evan Bourne; Chavo Guerrero bested Tommy Dreamer; Finlay defeated Mike Knox.

Sep 2008

Air Date: 2nd September 2008
Super Crazy beat Gavin Spears; Ricky Ortiz bested Ryan Braddock; Matt Hardy, Tommy Dreamer, Evan Bourne and Finlay defeated The Miz, John Morrison, ECW Champion Mark Henry and Chavo Guerrero.

Air Date: 9th September 2008
John Morrison beat Evan Bourne; Jack Swagger downed Josh Daniels; Matt Hardy and Finlay defeated Mark Henry and Mike Knox.

Air Date: 16th September 2008
ECW Champion Matt Hardy toppled Mike Knox; Evan Bourne dropped The Miz; Jack Swagger downed Chase Stevens; Mark Henry overcame Finlay.

Air Date: 23rd September 2008
Finlay defeated Bam Neely; Maryse overcame WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool in a non-title match; Mike Knox downed Chase Stevens; The Miz battled John Morrison to a double count-out.

Air Date: 30th September 2008
Jack Swagger beat Lenny Lane; Mike Knox defeated Tommy Dreamer; Mark Henry, The Miz and John Morrison overcame ECW Champion Matt Hardy, Evan Bourne and R-Truth.

Oct 2008

Air Date: 7th October 2008
Tommy Dreamer and Finlay defeated Jack Swagger and Mike Knox; Mark Henry overcame Jamie Noble; The Miz, John Morrison and WWE World Tag Champions Ted Dibiase Jr and Cody Rhodes overcame Evan Bourne, Kofi Kingston, CM Punk and Ricky Ortiz.

Air Date: 14th October 2008
Evan Bourne dropped Chavo Guerrero; Finlay defeated John Morrison.

Air Date: 21st October 2008
Jack Swagger downed Brett Badery; Shad Gaspard defeated The Miz by DQ; Evan Bourne won a three-way over Mark Henry and Finlay.

Air Date: 28th October 2008
Chavo Guerrero, Bam Neely and Mark Henry overcame ECW Champion Matt Hardy, Finlay and Evan Bourne; Jack Swagger downed Tommy Dreamer.

Nov 2008

Air Date: 4th November 2008
ECW Champion Matt Hardy toppled Bam Neely; Ricky Ortiz downed Jon Davis; Finlay defeated Mark Henry.

Air Date: 11th November 2008
The Miz, John Morrison and Jack Swagger downed Cryme Tyme and Tommy Dreamer; Mark Henry overcame Oliver Biney; ECW Champion Matt Hardy toppled Finlay.

Air Date: 18th November 2008
DJ Gabriel beat Sal Rinauro;  Jack Swagger downed Tommy Dreamer by DQ in an “Amateur Wrestling Challenge”; Mark Henry, WWE Intercontinental Champion William Regal and Cody Rhodes overcame ECW Champion Matt Hardy, Kofi Kingston and Finlay.

Air Date: 25th November 2008
Jack Swagger downed Tommy Dreamer in an “Extreme Rules” match; DJ Gabriel beat Jared Ganem; Mark Henry battled to a no-contest with Finlay.

Dec 2008

Air Date: 2nd December 2008
Jack Swagger downed Ricky Ortiz; DJ Gabriel beat Josh Daniels; ECW Champion Matt Hardy toppled MVP.

Air Date: 9th December 2008
The Miz and John Morrison defeated Kung Fu Naki and Jimmy Wang Yang; The Boogeyman downed Scott Reed; Mark Henry and Tony Atlas dropped Finlay and Hornswoggle.

Air Date: 16th December 2008
ECW Champion Matt Hardy toppled Chavo Guerrero; Finlay defeated Gavin Spears; WWE World Tag Champions The Miz and John Morrison, and Jack Swagger downed CM Punk, Kofi Kingston and Ricky Ortiz.

Air Date: 23rd December 2008
Best of 2008 show. No original matches broadcast.

Air Date: 30th December 2008
ECW Champion Matt Hardy defeated Mark Henry by DQ; DJ Gabriel pinned Paul Burchill; WWE World Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz downed Finlay and The Boogeyman in  no-DQ match.
