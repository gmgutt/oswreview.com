WWE TLC Results

WWE TLC RESULTS

13th December 2009: “WWE TLC 2009”: San Antonio, TX – 13,500 sellout (12,000 paid)
($770,000 live gate)
Dark match: R-Truth beat CM Punk.
PPV Broadcast: ECW Champion Christian defeated Shelton Benjamin in a ladder match; Drew McIntyre downed WWE Intercontinental Champion John Morrison to win the title; WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool dropped Mickie James; Sheamus toppled WWE World Champion John Cena in a tables match to win the title; World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker bested Batista in a “chairs” match; Randy Orton pinned Kofi Kingston; Triple and Shawn Michaels rolled over WWE Unified World Tag Champions in a TLC encounter to win the titles.

19th December 2010: “WWE TLC 2010”: Houston, TX – 11,500
Dark match: WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan beat Ted Dibiase Jr.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler defeated Kofi Kingston and Jack Swagger in a Triple Threat ladder match; WWE Divas Champion Natalya Neidhart and Beth Phoenix downed Michelle McCool and Layla in a tables match; WWE World Tag Champions Santino Marella and Vladimir Kozlov overcame Justin Gabriel and Heath Slater by DQ; John Morrison downed Sheamus in a ladder match; WWE World Champion The Miz bested Randy Orton in a tables match; Edge toppled World Heavyweight Champion Kane, Rey Mysterio and Alberto Del Rio in a Fatal Four Way TLC match to win the title; John Cena rolled over Wade Barrett in a chairs encounter.

 

18th December 2011: “WWE TLC 2011”: Baltimore, MD – 8,000
($500,000 live gate)
Dark match: Drew McIntyre beat Alex Riley.
PPV Broadcast: Zack Ryder defeated WWE US Champion Dolph Ziggler to win the title; WWE World Tag Champions Kofi Kingston and Evan Bourne downed Primo and Epico; Randy Orton dropped Wade Barrett in a tales match; WWE Divas Champion Beth Phoenix overcame Kelly Kelly; Triple H toppled Kevin Nash in a ladder match; Sheamus overturned Jack Swagger; The Big Show pinned World Heavyweight Champion Mark Henry in a chairs match to win the title; Daniel Bryan beat World Heavyweight Champion The Big Show to win the title; WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes bested  Booker T; WWE World Champion  CM Punk rolled over The Miz and Alberto Del Rio in a three-way TLC match.

16th December 2012: “WWE TLC 2012”: Brooklyn, NY – 15,748 sellout (12,500 paid)
Dark match: JTG beat David Otunga.
Pre-show: Eve Torres won a battle royal.
PPV Broadcast: Cody Rhodes and Damien Sandow overturned Rey Mysterio and Sin Cara in a “tables match; WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro downed R-Truth; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston dropped Wade Barrett; Dean Ambrose, Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns bested Ryback and WWE World Tag Champions Kane and Daniel Bryan in a “TLC” match; WWE Divas Champion Eve Torres downed Naomi; World Heavyweight Champion The Big Show defeated Sheamus in a “chairs” match; The Miz, Alberto Del Rio and The Brooklyn Brawler overcame Heath Slater, Jinder Mahal and Drew McIntyre; Dolph Ziggler rolled over John Cena in a ladder match.

15th December 2013: “WWE TLC 2013”: Houston, TX – 12,000 sellout
Pre-show: Fandango beat Dolph Ziggler.
PPV Broadcast: CM Punk bested Seth Rollins, Roman Reigns, and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose in a handicap match; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee downed Natalya; WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E Langston downed Damien Sandow; WWE World Tag Champions Goldust and Cody Rhodes won a four-way over Rey Mysterio and The Big Show, Jack Swagger and Antonio Cesaro, and Ryback and Curtis Axel; R-Truth pinned Brodus Clay; Kofi Kingston overcame The Miz in a no-DQ encounter; Bray Wyatt, Luke Harper, and Erick Rowan toppled Daniel Bryan in a handicap match; WWE World Champion Randy Orton rolled over World Heavyweight Champion John Cena in a TLC match to unify the titles.

14th December 2014: “WWE TLC 2014”: Cleveland, OH – 14,000
Shown on PPV / WWE Network
Pre-show: Kofi Kingston and Big E defeated Goldust and Stardust.
PPV Broadcast: Dolph Ziggler dropped WWE Intercontinental Champion Luke Harper in a ladder match to win the title; Jimmy and Jey Uso downed WWE World Tag Champions The Miz and Damien Mizdow by DQ; The Big Show toppled Erick Rowan in a stairs match; John Cena bested Seth Rollins in a tables match; WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella overturned AJ Lee; Ryback pinned Kane in a chairs match; WWE US Champion Rusev overcame Jack Swagger; Bray Wyatt rolled over Dean Ambrose in a TLC match.


13th December 2015: “WWE TLC 2015”: Boston, MA – 11,900 sellout
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Sasha Banks beat Becky Lynch.
PPV Broadcast: WWE World Tag Champions Big E & Kofi Kingston won a three-way ladder match over Sin Cara & Kalisto, and Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso; Rusev pinned Ryback; WWE US Champion Alberto Del Rio bested Jack Swagger in a chairs match; Bray Wyatt, Braun Strowman, Erick Rowan & Luke Harper beat Tommy Dreamer, The Dudleys & Rhyno in an elimination tables match; Dean Ambrose pinned WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens to win the title; WWE Divas Champion Charlotte toppled Paige; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus bested Roman Reigns in a TLC match.

4th December 2016: “WWE TLC 2016”: Dallas, TX – 12,500
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Chad Gable, Jason Jordan, Apollo Crews, Mojo Rawley and Zack Ryder rolled over Aiden English, Curt Hawkins, Viktor, Konnor and Simon Gotch.
PPV Broadcast: Bray Wyatt and Randy Orton overcame WWE Smackdown Tag Champions Heath Slater and Rhyno to win the title; Nikki Bella beat Carmella in a no-DQ match; WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz defeated Dolph Ziggler in a ladder match; Baron Corbin overcame Kalisto in a chairs match; Alexa Bliss overturned WWE Smackdown Women’s Champion Becky Lynch to win the title in a tables match; WWE World Champion AJ Styles defeated Dean Ambrose in a TLC match.
