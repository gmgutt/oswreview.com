WWE One Night Stand Results

ECW/WWE ONE NIGHT STAND RESULTS

12th June 2005: “ECW One Night Stand 2005”: New York, NY (Hammerstein Ballroom) – 2,500
($484,000 live gate)
PPV Broadcast: Lance Storm bested Chris Jericho; Super Crazy downed Yoshihiro Tajiri and Little Guido in a three-way; Rey Mysterio toppled Psicosis; Sabu bested Rhyno; Chris Benoit overturned Eddie Guerrero; Mike Awesome defeated Masato Tanaka; The Dudley Boys rolled over Tommy Dreamer and The Sandman.

11th June 2006: “ECW One Night Stand 2006”: New York, NY (Hammerstein Ballroom) – 2,460 sellout
($350,000 live gate)
PPV Broadcast: Tazz defeated Jerry Lawler; Kurt Angle bested Randy Orton; Little Guido and Tony Mamaluke downed Super Crazy and Tajiri; World Heavyweight Champion Rey Mysterio battled Sabu to a no-contest; Lita, Edge and Mick Foley downed Terry Funk, Tommy Dreamer, and Beulah McGillicutty; Balls Mahoney downed Masato Tanaka; Rob Van Dam downed WWE World Champion John Cena to win the title.

3rd June 2007: “WWE One Night Stand 2007”: Jacksonville, FL – 7,000 
($450,000 live gate)
Dark match: WWE Intercontinental Champion Santino Marella beat Chris Masters.
PPV Broadcast: Rob Van Dam toppled Randy Orton in a stretcher match; CM Punk, Tommy Dreamer, and The Sandman dropped Elijah Burke, Marcus Cor Von, and Matt Striker in a tables match; WWE World Tag Champions Matt and Jeff Hardy downed Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas in a ladder match; Mark Henry defeated Kane in a lumberjack match; Bobby Lashley overcame ECW Champion Vince McMahon in a streetfight; Candice Michelle overturned WWE Women’s Champion Melina in a non-title “pudding” match; World Heavyweight Champion Edge bested Batista in a cage match; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over The Great Khali in a “falls-count-anywhere” match.

1st June 2008: “WWE One Night Stand 2008”: San Diego, CA – 9,961 sellout
($586,045 live gate)
Dark match: WWE US Champion Matt Hardy beat Shelton Benjamin.
PPV Broadcast: Jeff Hardy defeated Umaga in a falls-count-anywhere match; The Big Show won a five-way “Singapore Cane” match over Chavo Guerrero, CM Punk, John Morrison, and Tommy Dreamer; John Cena downed JBL in a “First Blood” match; Beth Phoenix dropped Melina in a “I Quit” match; Batista bested Shawn Michaels in a “stretcher” match; WWE World Champion Triple H toppled Randy Orton in a “last man standing” match; Edge rolled over The Undertaker in a TLC match to win the vacant World Heavyweight Title.
