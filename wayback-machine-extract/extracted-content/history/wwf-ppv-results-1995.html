WWF PPV Results 1995

WWF PPV RESULTS (1995)

See also: WWF PPV Statistics 1994-1995

22nd January 1995: “WWF Royal Rumble 1995”: Tampa, FL – 10,000
($140,000 live gate)
Dark match: Buck Quartermaine downed The Brooklyn Brawler.
PPV Broadcast: Jeff Jarrett pinned WWF Intercontinental Champion Razor Ramon to win the title following a restart; The Undertaker overturned IRS; WWF World Champion Diesel battled Bret Hart to a draw; Bob Holly and The 1-2-3 Kid dropped Bam Bam Bigelow and Tatanka to win the vacant WWF World Tag Titles (WWF World Tag Title Tournament – Final); Shawn Michaels won the 30-man Royal Rumble, last eliminating Davey Boy Smith.

2nd April 1995: “WWF Wrestlemania XI”: Hartford, CT – 15,000 sellout
($750,000 live gate)
Lex Luger and Davey Boy Smith defeated Jacob and Eli Blu; Razor Ramon overturned WWF Intercontinental Champion Jeff Jarrett by DQ; The Undertaker dropped King Kong Bundy; Owen Hart and Yokozuna bested WWF World Tag Champions The Smoking Gunns to win the titles; Bret Hart toppled Bob Backlund in a “I Quit” match; WWF World Champion Diesel rolled over Shawn Michaels; Lawrence Taylor pinned Bam Bam Bigelow.

14th May 1995: “WWF In Your House”: Syracuse, NY – 7,000 (3,500 paid)
($51,648 live gate)
Dark matches: Jean Pierre Lafitte beat Bob Holly; Bam Bam Bigelow toppled Tatanka; The Undertaker rolled over Kama.
Raw: (06/05): Davey Boy Smith battled Owen Hart to a draw (KOTR Qualifier).
PPV Broadcast: Bret Hart dropped Hakushi; Razor Ramon bested WWF Intercontinental Champion Jeff Jarrett and The Roadie in a handicap match; Mabel overcame Adam Bomb (KOTR Qualifier); WWF World Tag Champions Owen Hart and Yokozuna defeated The Smoking Gunns; Jerry Lawler toppled Bret Hart; WWF World Champion Diesel rolled over Sid Vicious by DQ.

25th June 1995: “WWF King of the Ring 1995”: Philadelphia, PA – 16,590 (14,181 paid)
($311,680 live gate)
Sunday Night Slam: Savio Vega beat IRS (KOTR Qualifier).
PPV Broadcast: Savio Vega downed Yokozuna by count-out (KOTR Quarter-Final); The Roadie defeated Bob Holly (KOTR Quarter-Final); Shawn Michaels battled Kama to a draw (KOTR Quarter-Final); Mabel pinned The Undertaker (KOTR Quarter-Final); Savio Vega downed The Roadie (KOTR Semi-Final); Bret Hart toppled Jerry Lawler in a “Kiss my Foot” match; Mabel bested Savio Vega (KOTR Final); WWF World Champion Diesel and Bam Bam Bigelow rolled over Sid Vicious and Tatanka.

23rd July 1995: “WWF In Your House II”: Nashville, TN – 6,482
($74,930 live gate)
Dark matches: Skip beat Aldo Montoya; Bret Hart dropped Jean Pierre Lafitte; The Undertaker overturned Kama in a casket match.
PPV Broadcast: The Roadie pinned The 1-2-3 Kid; Men on a Mission toppled Razor Ramon and Savio Vega; Bam Bam Bigelow bested Henry Godwinn; Shawn Michaels overcame WWF Intercontinental Champion Jeff Jarrett to win the title; WWF World Tag Champions Owen Hart and Yokozuna defeated Lex Luger and Davey Boy Smith; WWF World Champion Diesel rolled over Sid Vicious in a lumberjack match.

27th August 1995: “WWF Summerslam 1995”: Pittsburgh, PA – 18,062 sellout (17,000 paid)
($353,705 live gate)
Hakushi pinned The 1-2-3 Kid; Hunter Hearst Helmsley downed Bob Holly; The Smoking Gunns defeated Jacob and Eli Blu; Barry Horowitz defeated Skip; Bertha Faye beat WWF Women’s Champion Alundra Blayze to win the title; The Undertaker bested Kama in a casket match; Bret Hart toppled Isaac Yankem by DQ; WWF Intercontinental Champion Shawn Michaels overturned Razor Ramon in a ladder match; WWF World Champion Diesel rolled over King Mabel.

24th September 1995: “WWF In Your House III”: Saginaw, MI – 5,146
($62,392 live gate)
Dark matches: Fatu defeated Hunter Hearst Helmsley; Goldust beat Bob Holly; Ahmed Johnson (Tony Norris) dropped Skip; The Undertaker overturned Mabel.
PPV Broadcast: Savio Vega defeated Waylon Mercy; Sid Vicious pinned Henry Godwinn; Davey Boy Smith dropped Bam Bam Bigelow; Dean Douglas bested Razor Ramon; Bret Hart toppled Jean Pierre Lafitte; WWF World Champion Diesel and WWF Intercontinental Champion Shawn Michaels rolled over WWF World Tag Champions Yokozuna and Davey Boy Smith (a permitted substitute for Owen Hart) to ostensibly win the titles.

22nd October 1995: “WWF In Your House IV”: Winnipeg, MAN – 10,339 (9,000 paid)
($93,422 live gate)
Dark matches: Bob Holly beat Rad Radford; Henry Godwinn defeated Sid Vicious; Bret Hart bested Isaac Yankem; Owen Hart and Yokozuna dropped Savio Vega and Bam Bam Bigelow.
PPV Broadcast: Hunter Hearst Helmsley beat Fatu; WWF World Tag Champions The Smoking Gunns overturned Razor Ramon and The 1-2-3 Kid; Goldust bested Marty Jannetty; Yokozuna battled King Mabel to a double count-out; Razor Ramon pinned WWF Intercontinental Champion Dean Douglas to win the title; Davey Boy Smith rolled over WWF World Champion Diesel by DQ.

19th November 1995: “WWF Survivor Series 1995”: Landover, MD – 14,500 (12,500 paid)
($250,000 live gate)
Dark match: WWF World Tag Champions The Smoking Gunns beat The Public Enemy.
PPV Broadcast: Skip, Rad Radford, Tom Prichard and The 1-2-3 Kid defeated Hakushi, Barry Horowitz, Bob Holly and Marty Jannetty In a Survivors Elimination match; Bertha Faye, Aja Kong, Lioness Asuka and Tomoko Watanabe downed WWF Women’s Champion Alundra Blayze, Kyoko Inoue, Sakie Hasegawa and Chaparita Asari In a Survivors Elimination match; Goldust pinned Bam Bam Bigelow; The Undertaker, Fatu, Henry Godwinn and Savio Vega dropped King Mabel, Hunter Hearst Helmsley, Jerry Lawler and Isaac Yankem In a Survivors Elimination match; Shawn Michaels, Ahmed Johnson, Davey Boy Smith and Sid Vicious bested WWF Intercontinental Champion Razor Ramon, Yokozuna, Owen Hart and Dean Douglas In a “Wildcard” Survivors Elimination match; Bret Hart rolled over WWF World Champion Diesel in a “no-DQ, count-out or time-limit” encounter to win the title.

17th December 1995: “WWF In Your House  V”: Hersey, PA – 7,289
($100,000 live gate)
Dark matches: Savio Vega beat Bob Backlund; Goldust defeated Duke Droese; WWF World Tag Champions The Smoking Gunns, Hakushi and Barry Horowitz overcame Yokozuna, Isaac Yankem, Skip and Zip.
PPV Broadcast: WWF Intercontinental Champion Razor Ramon and Marty Jannetty bested The 1-2-3 Kid and Sid Vicious; Ahmed Johnson defeated Buddy Landel; Hunter Hearst Helmsley downed Henry Godwinn in an “Arkansas Hog Pen” match; Owen Hart toppled Diesel by DQ; The Undertaker dropped King Mabel in a casket match; WWF World Champion Bret Hart rolled over Davey Boy Smith.
