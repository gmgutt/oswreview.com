WWE NXT Live Specials 2014

WWE NXT LIVE SPECIALS (2014)

27th February 2014: “WWE NXT ArRIVAL”: Winter Park, FL – 400 sellout (hundreds were turned away at the door)
Shown on WWE Network
Dark match: Mason Ryan beat Sylvester Lefort
NXT ArRIVAL: (02/27): Cesaro pinned Sami Zayn; Mojo Rawley defeated CJ Parker; WWE NXT Tag Champions Konnor and Viktor downed Too Cool; WWE NXT Women’s Champion Paige dropped Emma; Tyler Breeze battled Xavier Woods to a no-contest; Adrian Neville rolled over WWE NXT Champion Bo Dallas in a ladder match to win the title.

29th May 2014: “WWE NXT Takeover”: Winter Park, FL – 400 sellout
Shown on WWE Network
Adam Rose pinned Camacho; NXT Tag Champions Konnor and Viktor defeated Kalisto and El Local; Tyler Breeze bested Sami Zayn; Charlotte toppled Natalya (NXT Women’s Title Tournament – Final); NXT Champion Adrian Neville rolled over Tyson Kidd.

11th September 2014: “NXT Takeover: Fatal 4 Way”: Winter Park, FL – 400 sellout
Shown on WWE Network
Dark match: WWE Divas Champion Paige beat Becky Lynch.
Live Broadcast: Sin Cara and Kalisto defeated WWE NXT Tag Champions The Ascension to win the titles; Baron Corbin toppled CJ Parker; Enzo Amore pinned Sylvester Lefort in a “hair vs. hair” match; Bull Dempsey dropped Mojo Rawley; WWE NXT Women’s Champion Charlotte rolled over Bayley; WWE NXT Champion Adrian Neville won a four-way over Sami Zayn, Tyson Kidd and Tyler Breeze.

11th December 2014: “WWE NXT Takeover: Revolution”: Winter Park, FL – 400 sellout
Shown on WWE Network
Dark match: Bull Dempsey beat Steve Cutler.
Live Broadcast: Kevin Owens defeated CJ Parker; WWE NXT Tag Champions Sin Cara and Kalisto overcame Aiden English and Simon Gotch; Baron Corbin downed Tye Dillinger; Finn Balor and Hideo Itami bested Konnor and Rick Victor; WWE NXT Women’s Champion Charlotte toppled Sasha Banks; Sami Zayn rolled over WWE NXT Champion Adrian Neville to win the title.
