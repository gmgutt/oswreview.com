OSW Update March 18th

• Latest OSW is our brucie bonus WWE/FLINTSTONES crossover! http://oswreview.com/episodes/

If you notice an episode isn’t playing, try turning off HD, Adblock, and try a different browser. If it’s still not working, let us know!

• The next OSW is No Way Out 2001, it’s in production but won’t be ready for a while yet. It’s a whopper show! I’ll keep you posted when editing is nearer completion.

• I reviewed a sweet horror film for the site! House At The End of Time (La casa del fin de los tiempos). It’s about a woman going back to her house, 30 years after her husband was murdered and son disappeared in front of her eyes.
http://oswreview.com/reviews/

• I was interviewed by Wrestle Radio Australia earlier in March, and it’s now up on the iTunes feed! In it I review Resident Evil Remake and Telltale’s GoT, and drop some spoilers on upcoming OSW episodes!  Flintstones audio coming in a couple of days.

• Response to the WHAT BAR tee has been amazing! Thank you so much. We’ve sold out both our US & UK pre-orders but we’re getting more made. It’ll take a little time and I’ll keep you in the loop! UPDATE: The first batch is in the mail! UK/Ire should receive it by early next week!
