Raw Results 2013

WWE RAW RESULTS (2013)

Jan 2013

Air Date: 7th January 2013
John Cena downed Dolph Ziggler; Kaitlyn beat Eve Torres by count-out; Cody Rhodes and Damien Sandow dropped WWE World Tag Champions Kane and Daniel Bryan in a non-title match; Randy Orton pinned Heath Slater; WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett bested Santino Marella; WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro toppled The Great Khali; Sheamus overcame Jinder Mahal; WWE World Champion CM Punk rolled over Ryback in a TLC match; World Heavyweight Champion The Big Show overturned Kofi Kingston.

Air Date 14th January 2013
WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett downed Randy Orton; Kane pinned Damien Sandow; Kaitlyn bested WWE Divas Champion Eve Torres to win the title; WWE World Champion CM Punk toppled Brodus Clay; Heath Slater, Drew McIntyre and Jinder Mahal overcame Sheamus in an “Over The Top Rope” elimination challenge; Daniel Bryan bested Cody Rhodes; John Cena rolled over Dolph Ziggler in a cage match.

Air Date: 21st January 2013
Randy Orton bested WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro in a non-title match (Beat the Clock); The Big Show pinned Zack Ryder; Ryback overturned Heath Slater; Dolph Ziggler downed  The Miz (Beat the Clock); WWE Divas Champion Kaitlyn overcame Alicia Fox; WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett battled Sheamus to a draw (Beat the Clock); World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio dropped Tensai.

Air Date: 28th January 2013
Randy Orton pinned WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro in  a non-title match. The Miz was guest referee; Bo Dallas defeated WWE Intercontinental Champion  Wade Barrett in a non-title match; John Cena bested Cody Rhodes; WWE Divas Champion Kaitlyn battled Tamina Snuka to a no-contest in a “lumberjill” match;  Sheamus toppled Damien Sandow in a “tables” match; WWE World Tag Champions Kane and Daniel Bryan rolled over Chris Jericho and Dolph Ziggler.

Feb 2013

Air Date: 4th February 2013

Air Date: 11th February 2013

Air Date: 18th February 2013
Mark Henry beat Sin Cara; The Miz defeated WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro in a non-title, no-DQ encounter; World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio downed Dolph Ziggler; Brodus Clay, Tensai and Naomi overcame Primo, Epico and Rosa Mendes; Jack Swagger dropped Daniel Bryan; Dean Ambrose, Roman Reigns and Seth Rollins bested Chris Jericho, Sheamus and Ryback; Randy Orton rolled over Kane.

Air Date: 25th February 2013
Ryback beat Dolph Ziggler; Mark Henry defeated The Great Khali; Randy Orton downed WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro in a non-title match; R-Truth dropped Cody Rhodes; WWE World Tag Champions Kane and Daniel Bryan toppled Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; Jack Swagger bested The Miz; John Cena rolled over CM Punk.

Air Date: 11th March 2013
The Big Show downed Seth Rollins by DQ; Dolph Ziggler dropped Daniel Bryan; Billy Gunn and Road Dogg battled Cody Rhodes and Damien Sandow to a no-contest; Mark Henry bested Kofi Kingston; Ryback toppled Heath Slater; Word Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio overcame WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro in a non-title match; Randy Orton and Sheamus defeated Cody Rhodes and Damien Sandow; Chris Jericho battled to a no-contest with The Miz; Jack Swagger toppled Sin Cara; Kane bested CM Punk to a no-DQ match.

Air Date: 18th March 2013

Air Date: 25th March 2013

Apr 2013

Air Date: 15th April 2013

Air Date: 22nd April 2013

Air Date: 29th April 2013
