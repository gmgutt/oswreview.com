Shotgun Results 1998

SHOTGUN RESULTS (1998)

Jan 1998

Air Date: 3rd January 1998
Mark Henry beat D-Lo Brown by DQ; Vader toppled Julio Sanchez; Jeff Jarrett overcame Scott Taylor; The New Blackjacks downed Recon and Sniper by DQ; Steve Blackman overturned Jimmy Cicero.

Air Date: 10th January 1998
WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock toppled Chainz; Mosh overcame Phinneas Godwinn by DQ; The Jackyl beat Scott Taylor; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku pinned Pablo Marquez; Steve Blackman downed Nick Barberry.

Air Date: 17th January 1998
Owen Hart overturned Brian Christopher; Faarooq pinned Mark Shrader; Jose Estrada Jr and Jesus Castillo battled Recon and Sniper to a double-DQ; Steve Blackman defeated Al Phillips; Tom Brandi pinned Jimmy Cicero.

Air Date: 24th January 1998
The Legion of Doom toppled The Rock and Roll Express by DQ; Mark Henry squashed The Black Phantom; Steve Blackman downed Bob Bradley; Max Mini pinned El Torito; El Pantera dropped Chilongo.

Air Date: 31st January 1998
NWA North American Champion Jeff Jarrett bested Chainz; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku pinned Jesus Castillo; Kurrgan downed Michael Modest; Bradshaw beat Donovan Morgan; Nova and Mosaic dropped Tarantula and Batallion.

Feb 1998

Air Date: 7th February 1998
WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock downed Tom Brandi; Savio Vega and Jesus Castillo dropped The Godwinns; Brian Christopher and Flash Flanagan defeated WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku and El Pantera; Miguel Perez Jr and Jose Estrada Jr overcame The Quebecers.

Air Date: 14th February 1998
WWF European Champion Owen Hart overturned Jesus Castillo; WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn overcame Tom Brandi and Flash Funk; Kurrgan defeated Lance Jade; Skull and Eight-Ball downed NWA Tag Team Champions Ricky Morton and Robert Gibson by DQ; Bradshaw dropped Mark Kyle.

Air Date: 21st February 1998
Brian Christopher beat Tony Williams; Kurrgan defeated Cedman; Pantera downed Felino; Barry Windham dropped Tom Brandi; Mark Henry and D-Lo Brown bested Bill Irwin and Kit Carson; Chainz, Skull and Eight-Ball overcame Jesus Castillo, Jose Estrada and Savio Vega by DQ.

Air Date: 28th February 1998
Aguila defeated Piratta Morgan; Ricky Morton and Robert Gibson downed The Godwinns; Tom Brandi dropped Jesus Castillo; Skull and Eight-Ball overturned The Quebecers.

Mar 1998

Air Date: 7th March 1998
Aguila defeated Brian Christopher; Bradshaw downed Marc Mero; Flash Funk beat TAFKA Goldust; WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock and Faarooq beat Tom Brandi and Tarantula.

Air Date: 14th March 1998
D-Lo Brown bested Robert Gibson; Kurrgan dropped Jason Ahrndt; Jeff Jarrett toppled Tom Brandi; Skull and Eight-Ball overcame Jesus Castillo and Jose Estrada, Sniper and Recon, and Savio Vega and Miguel Perez in a Fatal Four Way.

Air Date: 21st March 1998
WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku defeated Christopher Daniels; Bob Holly and Bart Gunn overcame Ricky Morton and Robert Gibson; Barry Windham downed Steve Blackman; Jose Estrada Jr battled Savio Vega to a double-DQ.

Air Date: 28th March 1998
Was a preview show for WWF Wrestlemania XIV

Apr 1998

Air Date: 4th April 1998
Bradshaw beat Savio Vega; Skull and Eight-Ball dropped Brian Walsh and Roy Raymond; The Headbangers defeated Scott Taylor and Brian Christopher.

Air Date: 11th April 1998
TAFKA Goldust dropped Jose Estrada; NWA Tag Team Champions Bob Holly and Bart Gunn overcame Ricky Morton and Robert Gibson; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku downed Scott Taylor; Jeff Jarrett defeated Raymond Clark; The Legion of Doom overcame Frank Stalletto and Roy Raymond.

Air Date: 18th April 1998
Bradshaw pinned Harley Lewis; Brian Christopher and Scott Taylor defeated Ace Darling and Devon Storm; WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock, Mark Henry and D-Lo Brown downed Al Phillips, Mark Schraeder and Brian Knighton; The Headbangers battled The Quebecers to a double-DQ.

Air Date: 25th April 1998
NWA Tag Team Champions Bob Holly and Bart Gunn overcame Skull and Eight-Ball by DQ; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku pinned Jimmy Cicero; Jeff Jarrett downed Lance Diamond; The Quebecers bested Pouier and Buchanan.

May 1998

Air Date: 2nd May 1998
Marc Mero downed Ricky Morton; Brian Christopher and Scott Taylor defeated Matt Hardy and Jason Ardt; Steve Blackman pinned D-Lo Brown; The Legion of Doom dropped Jose Estrada Jr and Jesus Castillo.

Air Date: 9th May 1998
Papi Chulo beat Scott Taylor; Kama Mustafa and D-Lo Brown defeated Bob Starr and John Crystal; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku overturned David Jericho; NWA Tag Champions Bob Holly and Bart Gunn overcame The Headbangers.

Air Date: 16th May 1998
The Headbangers downed Jose Estrada and Jesus Castillo; Scott Taylor pinned Papi Chulo; Barry Windham beat Rich Myers; Savio Vega dropped Chainz; NWA Tag Team Champions Bob Holly and Bart Gunn overcame Steve Corino and Julio Sanchez by DQ.

Air Date: 23rd May 1998
Faarooq beat Mark Kyle; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku downed Sho Funaki by DQ; Brian Christopher dropped David Jericho; Jeff Jarrett defeated Chainz.

Air Date: 30th May 1998
Steve Blackman beat Jason Ahrndt; Brian Christopher and Scott Taylor defeated David Jericho and Papi Chulo; Mark Henry downed Jeff Hardy; Val Venis dropped Matt Hardy; WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn overcame Bob Holly and Bart Gunn.

Jun 1998

Air Date: 6th June 1998
Dick Togo and Sho Funaki downed Christopher Daniels and Steve Boz; Vader pinned Rex Regal; Scorpio beat Jesus Castillo; Brian Christopher and Scott Taylor dropped The Headbangers by DQ.

Air Date: 13th June 1998
WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku and Bradshaw defeated Mike Anthony and Steve Boz; Mark Canterbury and Dennis Knight downed Rex Royal and Chris Young; Kurrgan dropped Kevin Flynn; Bart Gunn and Bob Holly overcame Savio Vega and Jose Estrada Jr.

Air Date: 20th June 1998
Kama battled Faarooq to a double count-out; Kurrgan and Golga defeated Chris Young and Joe White; Savio Vega and Jesus Castillo downed Sho Funaki and Dick Togo; WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn overcame The Headbangers.

Air Date: 27th June 1998
Terry Funk, Bradshaw and Dustin Runnels downed Jerry Lawler, Brian Christopher and Scott Taylor; The Legion of Doom dropped Bart Gunn and Bob Holly; Droz bested Shawn Summers; Val Venis overcame Trench Phillips.

Jul 1998

Air Date: 4th July 1998
X-Pac defeated Jesus Castillo; Faarooq pinned Lou Marconi; Mens Teioh and Sho Funaki downed Papi Chulo and Sean Casey; Vader dropped Frank Stalletto; Terry Funk and Dustin Runnels overturned WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn by DQ .

Air Date: 11th July 1998
Faarooq and Scorpio dropped Brian Christopher and Scott Taylor; Droz defeated Bob Holly; Steve Blackman downed Lance Diamond; Edge pinned Jesus Castillo; Dan Severn overcame GI Will.

Air Date: 18th July 1998
Faarooq and Scorpio downed Savio Vega and Jesus Castillo; Edge pinned Pierre; Val Venis defeated Papi Chulo; Dustin Runnels beat Jeff Jarrett by DQ.

Air Date: 25th July 1998
Terry Funk and Bradshaw defeated Brian Christopher and Scott Taylor; Bob Holly and Bart Gunn overcame Devon Storm and Eric Sebrecia; Vader beat Mark Henry; The Headbangers overcame Golga and Kurrgan; The Godfather pinned Dustin Runnels.

Air Date: 29th August 1998

Air Date: 12th September 1998

Air Date: 26th September 1998

Air Date: 10th October 1998

Air Date: 24th October 1998
WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn overcame Skull and Eight-Ball; Edge downed Marc Mero; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Christian dropped Dick Togo; Kurrgan, Golga and Giant Silva overcame Kevin Quinn, Mike Anthony and Tom Voss; Gangrel defeated Scorpio.

Air Date: 31st October 1998

Air Date: 26th December 1998
