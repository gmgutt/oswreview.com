ROH PPV Results 2015

ROH PPV RESULTS 2015

1st March 2015: “ROH 13th Anniversary Show”: Las Vegas, NV – 1,000 sellout
Shown on PPV
(10,000 PPV Buys)
Dark match: Adam Page and Jimmy Jacobs beat Adam Thornstowe & Luster The Legend.
PPV Broadcast: Matt Sydal defeated Cedric Alexander; Moose downed Mark Briscoe; Matt Taven and Michael Bennett won a three-way over Karl Anderson, and Christopher Daniels and Frankie Kazarian; Roderick Strong dropped BJ Whitmer; ODB toppled Maria Kanellis; IWGP World Champion AJ Styles bested ACH; ROH Tag Champions Bobby Fish and Kyle O’Reilly rolled over The Young Bucks; ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal pinned AAA World Champion Alberto El Patron; ROH Champion Jay Briscoe won a four-way over Hanson, Michael Elgin and Tommaso Ciampa.


15th May 2015: “NJPW/ROH Global Wars 2015”: Toronto, ONT – 1,500 sellout
Shown live on iPPV
Dark match: Dalton Castle defeated Donovan Dijak.
iPPV Broadcast: Gedo & Moose beat Silas Young & Takaaki Watanabe; Kushida won a three-way over Chris Sabin and Kyle O’Reilly; IWGP World Tag Champions Matt Taven & Michael Bennett downed Jushin Liger & Matt Sydal; Kazuchika Okada pinned Cedric Alexander; ROH Tag Champions Christopher Daniels & Frankie Kazarian won a three-way over Adam Page & BJ Whitmer, and Berreta & Rocky Romero; Shinsuke Nakamura bested ACH; ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal bested Tetsuya Naito; Hiroshi Tanahashi toppled Michael Elgin; Roderick Strong, ROH Champion Jay Briscoe, Mark Briscoe & War Machine rolled over IWGP World Champion AJ Styles, Doc Gallows, Karl Anderson & IWGP Jr Tag Champions The Young Bucks.


19th June 2015: “ROH Best in the World 2015”: New York, NY (Terminal 5) – 1,100 sellout
Shown on PPV/iPPV
Dark match: Cheeseburger won a four-way over J Diesel, Will Ferrara & Romantic Touch.
PPV Broadcast: Mark Briscoe pinned Donovan Dijak; Adam Page & BJ Whitmer overcame Matt Sydal & ACH; Dalton Castle downed Silas Young; Hanson & Raymond Rowe overturmed Caprice Coleman & Cedric Alexander ; Roderick Strong won a three-way over Michael Elgin & Moose; IWGP World Champion AJ Styles & IWGP Jr Tag Champions The Young Bucks toppled Adam Cole, and IWGP World Tag Champions Michael Bennett and Matt Taven; ROH Tag Champions Christopher Daniels and Kazarian bested Bobby Fish & Kyle O’Reilly; ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal rolled over ROH Champion Jay Briscoe to win the title.


24th July 2015: “ROH Death Before Dishonor XIII”: Baltimore, MD – 900 sellout
Shown live on iPPV
Silas Young overcame Will Ferrara; Cedric Alexander beat Moose; Jay Briscoe & Mark Briscoe overturned Beretta & Rocky Romero; Adam Cole downed Dalton Castle; Adam Page downed ACH in a no-DQ match; ROH Tag Champions Christopher Daniels & Frankie Kazarian won a four-way over Matt Taven & Michael Bennett, Bobby Fish & Kyle O’Reilly, and War Machine; ROH Champion Jay Lethal battled Roderick Strong to a 60 minute draw.


18th September 2015: “ROH All Star Extravaganza 2015”: San Antonio, TX – 1,000 sellout
Shown on PPV
Dark match: Takaaki Watanabe and Will Ferrara beat Donovan Dijak and Gregory James.
PPV Broadcast: ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal defeated Bobby Fish; Silas Young pinned Dalton Castle; Rhett Titus and Kenny King toppled Mark Briscoe and Jay Briscoe; Moose defeated Cedric Alexander in a no-DQ match; ACH pinned Matt Sydal (Best of 5 – Match #3); Matt Taven and Michael Bennett won a three-way over ROH Tag Champions Christopher Daniels and Frankie Kazarian, and The Young Bucks to win the titles; AJ Styles won a four-way over Roderick Strong, Adam Cole and Michael Elgin; ROH Champion Jay Lethal rolled over Kyle O’Reilly.

18th December 2015: “ROH Final Battle 2015”: Philadelphia, PA (2300 Arena)
Shown live on PPV
Dark matches: Donovan Dijak & J Diesel won a squash; Cheeseburger beat Bob Evans.
PPV Broadcast: Kenny King & Rhett Titus won a three-way over Mark Briscoe & Jay Briscoe, and The Young Bucks; Silas Young beat Dalton Castle; Michael Elgin toppled Moose; Adam Cole pinned Kyle O’Reilly; ACH, Matt Sydal & Alex Shelly defeated Chris Sabin, Christopher Daniels & Frankie Kazarian; ROH TV Champion Roderick Strong downed Bobby Fish; Hanson & Ray Rowe dropped ROH Tag Champions Michael Bennett & Matt Taven to win the titles; ROH Champion Jay Lethal rolled over AJ Styles.
