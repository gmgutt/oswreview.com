About the OSW History Archives

The OSW History Archives are an ongoing project to create a comprehensive record of pro-wrestling related results, facts and figures.

Currently the project is still in the preliminary stages, with an estimated completion date of 2019.

As the project is still in its very early stages, you may experience some flaws with navigation, page design or dead links.

Any comments, suggestions, questions or corrections can be directed by e-mail to oswreviewhistory@gmail.com

Updates will also be available on our second twitter feed from spring 2018 onwards as new sections are posted: https://twitter.com/oswhistory
