WWE PPV Results 2008

WWE PPV RESULTS (2008)

See also: WWE PPV Statistics 2008

27th January 2008: “WWE Royal Rumble 2008”: New York, NY – 17,382 sellout
($1,383,980 live gate)
Dark match: Jimmy Wang Yang and Shannon Moore beat Deuce and Domino.
PPV Broadcast: Ric Flair bested WWE US Champion MVP in a non-title match; John Bradshaw Layfield toppled Chris Jericho by DQ; World Heavyweight Champion Edge pinned Rey Mysterio; WWE World Champion Randy Orton rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy; John Cena won the 30-man Royal Rumble by last eliminating Triple H.

17th February 2008: “WWE No Way Out 2008”: Las Vegas, NV – 13,306 sellout
($772,720 live gate)
Dark match: Kane beat Shelton Benjamin.
PPV Broadcast: ECW Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr pinned CM Punk; The Undertaker won an “Elimination Chamber” match over The Great Khali, Batista, WWE US Champion MVP, Finlay, and Big Daddy V; Ric Flair downed Mr Kennedy; World Heavyweight Champion Edge bested Rey Mysterio; John Cena toppled WWE World Champion Randy Orton by DQ; Triple H won an “Elimination Chamber” match over WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy, Shawn Michaels, John Bradshaw Layfield, Chris Jericho, and Umaga.

30th March 2008: “WWE Wrestlemania 24”: Orlando, FL – 74,635 sellout (63,100 paid)
($5,854,590 live gate)
Dark match: Kane won a battle royal.
PPV Broadcast: John Bradshaw Layfield pinned Finlay in a “Belfast Brawl”; CM Punk won a “Money in the Bank” ladder match over WWE US Champion MVP, WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho,  John Morrison, Shelton Benjamin, Mr Kennedy, and Carlito; Batista toppled Umaga; Kane bested ECW Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr to win the title; Shawn Michaels pinned Ric Flair; WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix and Melina downed Maria and Ashley in a lumberjack match; WWE World Champion Randy Orton rolled over Triple H and John Cena in a three-way; Floyd Mayweather overturned The Big Show in a “no holds barred” match; The Undertaker rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Edge to win the title.

27th April 2008: “WWE Backlash 2008”: Baltimore, MD –11,277 sellout (9,000 paid)
($624,195 live gate)
Dark match: WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz overcame Jimmy Wang Yang and Shannon Moore.
PPV Broadcast: Matt Hardy overcame WWE US Champion MVP to win the title; ECW Champion Kane dropped Chavo Guerrero Jr; The Big Show pinned The Great Khali; Shawn Michaels toppled Batista. Chris Jericho was guest referee; Beth Phoenix, Jillian Hall, Melina, Layla, Victoria, and Natalya Neidhart overturned WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James, Michelle McCool, Kelly Kelly, Ashley, and Cherry; World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker rolled over Edge; Triple H bested WWE World Champion Randy Orton, John Cena, and John Bradshaw Layfield in a no-DQ, no count-out, four-way elimination match to win the title.

18th May 2008: “WWE Judgment Day 2008”: Omaha, NE – 11,324 (10,663 paid) (83% full)
($653,740 live gate)
Dark match: WWE World Tag Champions Hardcore Holly and Cody Rhodes downed Carlito and Santino Marella.
PPV Broadcast: John Cena dropped John Bradshaw Layfield; WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz overcame ECW Champion Kane and CM Punk; Shawn Michaels toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho in a non-title match; WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James defeated Melina and Beth Phoenix in a three-way; The Undertaker bested Edge by count-out; Jeff Hardy pinned MVP; WWE World Champion Triple H rolled over Randy Orton in a cage match.

1st June 2008: “WWE One Night Stand 2008”: San Diego, CA – 9,961 sellout
($586,045 live gate)
Dark match: WWE US Champion Matt Hardy beat Shelton Benjamin.
PPV Broadcast: Jeff Hardy defeated Umaga in a falls-count-anywhere match; The Big Show won a five-way “Singapore Cane” match over Chavo Guerrero, CM Punk, John Morrison, and Tommy Dreamer; John Cena downed JBL in a “First Blood” match; Beth Phoenix dropped Melina in a “I Quit” match; Batista bested Shawn Michaels in a “stretcher” match; WWE World Champion Triple H toppled Randy Orton in a “last man standing” match; Edge rolled over The Undertaker in a TLC match to win the vacant World Heavyweight Title.

29th June 2008: “WWE Night of Champions 2008”: Dallas, TX – 12,766 sellout
($743,280 live gate)
Dark match:  Jeff Hardy beat MVP.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Tag Champions The Miz and John Morrisson defeated Finlay and Hornswoggle; WWE US Champion Matt Hardy downed Chavo Guerrero; Mark Henry dropped ECW Champion Kane and The Big Show in a three-way to win the title; Cody Rhodes and Ted Dibiase Jr won the WWE World Tag Titles from Hardcore Holly in a handicap match; Kofi Kingston pinned WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho to win the title; WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James bested Katie Lea; World Heavyweight Champion Edge toppled Batista; WWE World Champion Triple H rolled over John Cena.

20th July 2008: “WWE Great American Bash 2008”: Uniondale, NY – 12,454 sellout
($817,875 live gate)
Dark match: Umaga beat Mr Kennedy.
PPV Broadcast: Curt Hawkins and Zack Ryder won the WWE Tag Titles in a four-way over Finlay and Hornswoggle, The Miz and John Morrison, and Jesse and Festus; Shelton Benjamin toppled WWE US Champion Matt Hardy to win the title; ECW Champion Mark Henry dropped Tommy Dreamer; Chris Jericho bested Shawn Michaels by stoppage; Michelle McCool overcame Natalya to win the WWE Divas Title; World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk battled Batista to a double-DQ; JBL downed John Cena in a “NYC Parking Lot Brawl”; WWE World Champion Triple H rolled over Edge.

17th August 2008: “WWE Summerslam 2008”: Indianapolis, IN – 12,480 sellout
($802,810 live gate)
Dark match: The Big Show beat Bam Neely.
PPV Broadcast: MVP defeated Jeff Hardy; Santino Marella and Beth Phoenix downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston and WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James in a “Winner Takes All” encounter. By virtue of a pre-match stipulation, Marella won the IC title and Phoenix won the women’s title; Matt Hardy dropped ECW Champion Mark Henry by DQ; World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk bested JBL; WWE World Champion Triple H pinned The Great Khali; Batista toppled John Cena; The Undertaker rolled over Edge in a “Hell in a Cell” match.

7th September 2008: “WWE Unforgiven 2008”: Cleveland, OH – 8,707 (61% full)
($531,880 live gate)
Dark match: Evan Bourne beat John Morrison.
PPV Broadcast: Matt Hardy won the ECW Title in a “scramble” match over Mark Henry, Chavo Guerrero, Finlay, and The Miz; WWE World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Ted Dibiase Jr overturned Cryme Tyme; Shawn Michaels bested Chris Jericho by stoppage in an “unsanctioned” match; WWE World Champion Triple H toppled Jeff Hardy, MVP, Brian Kendrick, and Shelton Benjamin in a “scramble” match; WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool dropped Maryse; Chris Jericho won the World Heavyweight Title over Batista, Kane, Rey Mysterio, and JBL.

5th October 2008: “WWE No Mercy 2008”: Portland, OR – 9,527 (73% full)
($464,725 live gate)
Dark match: WWE Tag Champions Carlito and Primo beat The Miz and John Morrison.
PPV Broadcast: ECW Champion Matt Hardy downed Mark Henry; WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix dropped Candice Michelle; Rey Mysterio overturned Kane by DQ; Batista pinned JBL; The Big Show toppled The Undertaker by stoppage; WWE World Champion Triple H bested Jeff Hardy; World Heavyweight Champion Chris Jericho rolled over Shawn Michaels in a ladder match.

26th October 2008: “WWE Cyber Sunday 2008”: Phoenix, AZ – 7,981 (78% full)
($449,465 live gate)
Pre-show: WWE US Champion Shelton Benjamin beat R-Truth.
PPV Broadcast: Rey Mysterio defeated Kane in a “No Holds Barred”  encounter; ECW Champion Matt Hardy pinned Evan Bourne; The Miz and John Morrison downed Cryme Tyme; The Honkytonk Man dropped WWE Intercontinental Champion Santino Marella by DQ; The Undertaker toppled The Big Show in a “last man standing” match; WWE World Champion Triple H bested Jeff Hardy; Batista rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Chris Jericho to win the title. Steve Austin was guest referee.

23rd November 2008: ”WWE Survivor Series 2008”: Boston, MA – 12,498 sellout
($751,480 live gate)
Dark match: Brian Kendrick beat Kung Fu Naki.
PPV Broadcast: Shawn Michaels, Rey Mysterio, The Great Khali, and Cryme Tyme dropped JBL, Kane, John Morrison, The Miz, and MVP in a “Survivors elimination” match; WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix, Mickie James, Kelly Kelly, Jillian Hall, and Candice Michelle overcame Victoria, WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool, Natalya, Maria, and Maryse in a “Survivors elimination” match; The Undertaker toppled The Big Show in a casket match; Randy Orton, Cody Rhodes, WWE US Champion Shelton Benjamin, WWE Intercontinental Champion William Regal, and Mark Henry bested Batista, ECW Champion Mark Henry, R-Truth, and WWE World Tag Champions CM Punk and Kofi Kingston in a “Survivors elimination” match; Edge won a three-way over WWE World Champion Triple H and Vladimir Kozlov to win the title; John Cena rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Chris Jericho to win the title.

 

14th December 2008: “WWE Armageddon 2008”: Buffalo, NY – 12,500 (11,000 paid)
($670,000 live gate)
Dark match: WWE World Tag Champions The Miz and John Morrison beat Festus and Jesse.
PPV Broadcast: Vladimir Kozlov defeated ECW Champion Matt Hardy in a non-title match; CM Punk bested Rey Mysterio (WWE Intercontinental Title #1 Contender Tournament – Final); Finlay dropped Mark Henry in a “Belfast Brawl”; Batista pinned Randy Orton; Kelly Kelly, Maria, WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool and Mickie James downed Jillian Hall, Maryse, Natalya and Victoria; World Heavyweight Champion John Cena toppled Chris Jericho; Jeff Hardy rolled over WWE World Champion Edge and Triple H in a three-way to win the title.
