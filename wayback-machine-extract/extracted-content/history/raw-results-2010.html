Raw Results 2010

WWE RAW RESULTS (2010)

Jan 2010


Air Date: 4th January 2010
Maryse overcame Brie Bella (WWE Divas Title Tournament – Quarter Final); MVP dropped Jack Swagger, Mark Henry and Carlito in a Fatal Four Way; WWE Unified Tag Champions Triple H and Shawn Michaels bested Chris Jericho and The Big Show; WWE World Champion Sheamus toppled Evan Bourne; Randy Orton rolled over Kofi Kingston.

Air Date: 11th January 2010
Alicia Fox beat Kelly Kelly (WWE Divas Title Tournament – Quarter Finals); Cody Rhodes and Ted Dibiase Jr dropped Mark Henry and Evan Bourne; Santino Marella overcame Jack Swagger in an over-the-top-rope challenge; Randy Orton toppled John Cena and Kofi Kingston in a Three Way Dance; Eve pinned Katie Lea (WWE Divas Title Tournament – Quarter Finals); WWE Unified Tag Champions Triple H and Shawn Michaels rolled over Mike Tyson and Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 18th January 2010
WWE World Champion Sheamus pinned Evan Bourne; Mark Henry overcame Jack Swagger in an over-the-top-rope challenge; John Cena and Kofi Kingston bested Ted Dibiase Jr and Cody Rhodes; Eve and Gail Kim downed Maryse and Alicia Fox; Randy Orton toppled Chris Masters; Hornswoggle and WWE Unified Tag Champions Triple H and Shawn Michaels rolled over Jon Heder, WWE US Champion The Miz and The Big Show.

Air Date: 25th January 2010
WWE Unified Tag Champions Shawn Michaels and Triple H dropped Cody Rhodes and Ted Dibiase Jr; The Big Show squashed MVP; Maryse downed Eve (WWE Divas Title Tournament – Semi-Final); Kofi Kingston upset WWE US Champion The Miz in a non-title match; Carlito battled Kelly Kelly to a no-contest; Gail Kim bested Alicia Fox (WWE Divas Title Tournament – Semi-Final); WWE World Champion Sheamus toppled John Cena by DQ.

Feb 2010

Air Date: 1st February 2010
John Cena beat Cody Rhodes (Elimination Chamber qualifier); Triple H bested Jack Swagger (Elimination Chamber qualifier); Randy Orton pinned Shawn Michaels (Elimination Chamber qualifier); Ted Dibiase Jr downed Mark Henry (Elimination Chamber qualifier); Kofi Kingston toppled The Big Show by DQ (Elimination Chamber qualifier).

Air Date: 8th February 2010
WWE World Champion Sheamus dropped ECW Champion Christian in a non-title match; The Miz and The Big Show overcame WWE Unified Tag Champions Triple H and Shawn Michaels, and CM Punk and Luke Gallows in a Triple Threat elimination match to win the titles; Gail Kim pinned Jillian Hall; Cody Rhodes bested Randy Orton; John Cena battled Ted Dibiase Jr to a no-contest.

Air Date: 15th February 2010
WWE World Champion Sheamus bested Randy Orton by DQ; MVP and Mark Henry toppled WWE Unified World Tag Champions The Miz and The Big Show in a non-title match; Ted Dibiase Jr overturned Kofi Kingston; Triple H bested John Cena by DQ.

Air Date: 22nd February 2010
Maryse downed Gail Kim to win the vacant WWE Divas Title (WWE Divas Title Tournament – Final); Evan Bourne, Kofi Kingston and Yoshi Tatsu bested Randy Orton, Ted Dibiase Jr and Cody Rhodes; Christian beat Carlito (Money in the Bank qualifier); WWE Unified World Tag Champions The Miz and The Big Show toppled MVP and Mark Henry; John Cena rolled over WWE World Champion Batista by DQ.

Mar 2010

Air Date: 1st March 2010
Randy Orton bested Ted Dibiase by DQ; Jack Swagger downed Santino Marella (Money in the Bank Qualifier); MVP dropped Zack Ryder (Money in the Bank Qualifier); Eve beat WWE  Divas Champion Maryse, Kelly Kelly, Gail Kim, Jillian Hall and Alicia Fox in a 6-diva pyjama pillow fight match; WWE Unified World Tag Champions The Miz and The Big Show dropped Triple H and Shawn Michaels.

Air Date: 8th March 2010
Eve, Kelly Kelly and Gail Kim defeated WWE Divas Champion Maryse, Alicia Fox and Katie Lea; WWE Unified World Tag Champions The Miz and The Big Show battled John Morrison and R-Truth to a no-contest; Cody Rhodes and Ted Dibiase Jr toppled Randy Orton in a handicap match; Evan Bourne bested William Regal (Money in the Bank Qualifier); Vince McMahon rolled over John Cena in a no-DQ handicap gauntlet match.

Air Date: 15th March 2010
The Big Show pinned John Cena; Evan Bourne battled Sheamus to a no-contest; WWE Divas Champion Maryse beat Kelly Kelly; Shawn Michaels toppled World Heavyweight Champion Chris Jericho by count-out; Triple H bested Randy Orton by DQ; WWE World Champion Batista rolled over Kofi Kingston.

Air Date: 22nd March 2010
John Morrison beat WWE US Champion The Miz in a non-title match; Kofi Kingston downed Vladimir Kozlov (Money in the Bank Qualifier); Sheamus, Ted Dibiase Jr and Cody Rhodes bested Triple H and Randy Orton in a handicap match; Shawn Michaels pinned Kane in a no-DQ, no count-out encounter; WWE Divas Champion Maryse, WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool and Layla defeated Beth Phoenix, Gail Kim and Eve.

Air Date: 29th March 2010
Christian pinned Ted Dibiase Jr in a lumberjack match; Beth Phoenix, Mickie James, Kelly Kelly, Eve and Gail Kim defeated WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool, WWE Divas Champion Maryse, Vickie Guerrero, Alicia Fox and Layla; DH Smith and Tyson Kidd overcame WWE Unified World Tag Champions The Miz and The Big Show by count-out; WWE World Champion John Cena and Randy Orton rolled over Batista and Jack Swagger.

Apr 2010

Air Date: 5th April 2010
Sheamus pinned Kofi Kingston; Eve won a 8-diva battle royal; WWE Unified World Tag Champions The Miz and The Big Show bested WWE World Champion John Cena and Batista by count-out; Ted Dibiase Jr dropped Christian; Randy Orton toppled World Heavyweight Champion Jack Swagger in a non-title match; WWE Unified World Tag Champions The Miz and The Big Show bested WWE World Champion John Cena and David Otunga.

Air Date: 12th April 2010
Eve pinned WWE Divas Champion Maryse to win the title; WWE US Champion The Miz dropped DH Smith; Evan Bourne bested Carlito; WWE World Champion John Cena overcame David Otunga; Kofi Kingston toppled Sheamus by DQ; The Bella Twins beat Gail Kim and Kelly Kelly, and Rosa Mendes and Jillian Hall in a Triple Threat match; Randy Orton rolled over Batista by DQ.

Air Date: 19th April 2010
WWE Intercontinental Champion Drew McIntyre pinned Matt Hardy; The Undertaker toppled World Heavyweight Champion Jack Swagger in a non-title match; MacGruber and The Great Khali downed Vladimir Kozlov by count-out; Triple H, Edge and Rey Mysterio Jr rolled over Chris Jericho, CM Punk and Luke Gallows.

Air Date: 26th April  2010
DH Smith and Tyson Kidd overcame WWE Unified World Tag Champions The Miz and The Big Show to win the titles; Michelle McCool and Layla beat WWE Divas Champion Eve and Maryse; CM Punk downed Evan Bourne; Ted Dibiase Jr and Santino Marella co-won a 10-man battle royal; Chris Jericho bested Christian; World Heavyweight Champion Jack Swagger toppled John Morrison; Hornswoggle overcame Dolph Ziggler by count-out; Batista overturned Randy Orton and Sheamus in a Triple Threat match.

May 2010


Air Date: 3rd May 2010
Batista defeated Daniel Bryan (Beat the Clock); John Morrison downed Ted Dibiase Jr by count-out; John Morrison dropped Ted Dibiase Jr; John Cena dropped Wade Barrett (Beat the Clock); Maryse overcame Nikki Bella; Mark Henry overturned Zack Ryder; WWE Unified World Tag Champions DH Smith and Tyson Kidd bested Vladimir Kozlov and William Regal.

Air Date: 10th May 2010
Chris Jericho beat DH Smith; R-Truth defeated William Regal by DQ; Evan Bourne downed Zack Ryder; Tyson Kidd dropped WWE US Champion The Miz in a non-title match; Darren Young, Daniel Bryan, Michael Tarver, David Otunga, Wade Barrett, Justin Gabriel, Skip Sheffield, and Heath Slater overcame Goldust, Yoshi Tatsu, Santino Marella, and John Morrison in a handicap match; Randy Orton bested Edge and Ted Dibiase Jr in a handicap match.

Air Date: 17th May 2010
Edge defeated Christian; The Undertaker downed Edge by count-out; Ted Dibiase Jr beat Yoshi Tatsu; Bret Hart bested WWE US Champion The Miz in a no-DQ match to win the title; Michelle McCool, WWE Women’s Champion Layla, and Maryse overcame The Bella Twins and WWE Divas Champion Eve Torres; Randy Orton dropped World Heavyweight Champion Jack Swagger by DQ; Evan Bourne and Gail Kim overturned Zack Ryder and Alicia Fox; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Sheamus by DQ.

Air Date: 24th May 2010
Sheamus bested Mark Henry; Maryse and Alicia Fox downed WWE Divas Champion Eve Torres and Gail Kim; R-Truth beat The Miz to win the vacant US title; WWE Unified World Tag Champions DH Smith and Tyson Kidd defeated William Regal and Vladimir Kozlov; Edge won a non-title three-way over WWE World Champion John Cena and Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 31st May 2010
WWE US Champion R- Truth dropped Chris Jericho; Santino Marella and WWE Divas Champion Eve Torres overcame William Regal and Maryse; Daniel Bryan bested The Miz; WWE World Champion John Cena and Evan Bourne toppled Edge and Sheamus.

Jun 2010

Air Date: 7th June 2010
The Big Show beat Chris Jericho in a “bodyslam” challenge; WWE Unified World Tag Champions DH Smith and Tyson Kidd defeated The Great Khali and Hornswoggle; Maryse won a battle royal; Kane downed Sheamus by count-out; The Miz and Zack Ryder dropped WWE US Champion R-Truth and John Morrison; Randy Orton bested Edge by DQ (Edge’s arm was tied behind his back); Matt Hardy pinned Drew McIntyre; WWE World Champion John Cena battled CM Punk to a no-contest.

Air Date: 14th June 2010
The Miz won a four-way over WWE US Champion R-Truth; John Morrison, and Zack Ryder to win the title; Evan Bourne bested Chris Jericho by DQ; The Big Show and Mark Feuerstein toppled Ted Dibiase Jr and Virgil; Santino Marella overcame William Regal. Vladimir Kozlov was guest referee; WWE World Champion John Cena and Randy Orton battled Sheamus and Edge to a no-contest.

Air Date: 21st June 2010
Chris Jericho bested Evan Bourne; Natalya battled Tamina to a no-contest; John Morrison toppled Zack Ryder; The Great Khali and Eve Torres rolled over Primo and WWE Divas Champion Alicia Fox; WWE World Champion Sheamus battled John Cena to a no-contest. Vince McMahon was a guest referee.

Air Date: 28th June 2010
Vladimir Kozlov downed Santino Marella; Sheamus dropped Mark Henry; WWE Divas Champion Alicia Fox and Maryse bested Gail Kim and Eve Torres; Randy Orton, Evan Bourne, R-Truth, and John Morrison toppled Chris Jericho, Edge, WWE US Champion The Miz, and Ted Dibiase Jr.

Jul 2010

Air Date: 5th July 2010
Ted Dibiase Jr dropped John Morrison; Vladimir Kozlov and Santino Marella overturned The Great Khali and William Regal; Randy Orton and Evan Bourne bested Chris Jericho and Edge; WWE Divas Champion Alicia Fox toppled Eve Torres.

Air Date: 12th July 2010
WWE Divas Champion Alicia Fox defeated Gail Kim; Jimmy Uso, Jey Uso and Tamina downed WWE Unified World Tag Champions DH Smith and Tyson Kidd, and Natalya; Edge bested Randy Orton; WWE US Champion The Miz battled Mark Henry to a no-contest; The Great Khali, Santino Marella, Goldust and Vladimir Kozlov overcame William Regal, Zack Ryder, Primo and Doink; Wade Barrett, Skip Sheffield, David Otunga, Justin Gabriel, Michael Tarver and Heath Slater rolled over John Cena in a handicap match.

Air Date: 19th July 2010
Randy Orton won a three-way over Edge and Chris Jericho; Eve Torres defeated Maryse; WWE World Champion Sheamus bested Evan Bourne; Vladimir Kozlov and Santino Marella toppled William Regal and Zack Ryder; Wade Barrett overcame Mark Henry.

Air Date: 26th July 2010
Randy Orton bested Jey Uso; Edge battled The Great Khali to a no-contest; Wade Barrett, Skip Sheffield, Darren Young, Heath Slater, Michael Tarver, David Otunga and Justin Gabriel defeated Yoshi Tatsu, Jerry Lawler, Goldust, Mark Henry, Evan Bourne and WWE Unified World Tag Champions DH Smith and Tyson Kidd in an elimination match; WWE Divas Champion Alicia Fox downed Brie Bella; Ted Dibiase Jr toppled John Morrison; WWE World Champion Sheamus and The Miz rolled over Chris Jericho and John Cena.

Aug 2010

Air Date: 2nd August 2010
Edge battled Wade Barrett to a no-contest; WWE Divas Champion Alicia Fox, Jillian Hall and Tamina overcame Natalya, Gail Kim and Eve Torres; WWE World Champion Sheamus bested Goldust; John Cena toppled Chris Jericho in a “Loser leaves Team Cena” match; The Great Khali dropped Ted Dibiase Jr; Randy Orton bested WWE US Champion The Miz in a non-title match.

Air Date: 9th August 2010
WWE US Champion The Miz defeated Evan Bourne; Melina downed WWE Divas Champion Alicia Fox in a non-title match; Husky Harris, Michael McGillicutty and Alex Riley dropped Kaval, Percy Watson and Lucky Cannon; John Morrison and R-Truth overturned William Regal and Zack Ryder; The Bella Twins won a three-way over Jillian Hall and Maryse, and Eve Torres and Gail Kim; John Cena and Bret Hart battled Chris Jericho and Edge to a no-contest in a lumberjack match.

Air Date: 16th August 2010
Wade Barrett defeated Chris Jericho; Michael Tarver downed Daniel Bryan; Justin Gabriel dropped Randy Orton by count-out; Skip Sheffield and David Otunga overcame John Morrison and R-Truth; Jillian Hall, Maryse and Alicia Fox overturned WWE Divas Champion Melina, Eve Torres and Gail Kim; Heath Slater bested Edge by count-out; John Cena rolled over Darren Young.

Air Date: 23rd August 2010
Edge defeated R-Truth; Chris Jericho toppled The Great Khali; WWE Divas Champion Melina downed Jillian Hall; WWE US Champion The Miz dropped John Cena by DQ; Vladimir Kozlov and Santino Marella overcame Jimmy and Jey Uso; Randy Orton won a three-way over John Morrison and Ted Dibiase Jr; WWE World Champion Sheamus rolled over Zack Ryder.

Air Date: 30th August 2010
The Miz and Alex Riley won a three-way over Kaval and Daniel Bryan, and Kofi Kingston and Michael McGillicutty; WWE Divas Champion Melina and Eve Torres bested Layla and Michelle McCool; John Morrison and R-Truth battled Cody Rhodes and Drew McIntyre to a no-contest; Jack Swagger downed Evan Bourne; Wade Barrett, David Otunga, Heath Slater, Michael Tarver and Justin Gabriel rolled over John Cena, Randy Orton, WWE World Champion Sheamus, Chris Jericho and Edge in an elimination match.

Sep 2010

Air Date: 6th September 2010
John Morrison beat Chris Jericho; WWE Divas Champion Melina defeated Alicia Fox; John Cena toppled Justin Gabriel; Edge toppled The Great Khali by count-out; Edge toppled The Great Khali in an “over-the-top-rope-challenge”; Randy Orton rolled over Wade Barrett.

Air Date: 13th September 2010
Daniel Bryan battled WWE US Champion The Miz to a no-contest in a submission match; Daniel Bryan overturned Alex Riley in a submission match; Edge toppled Evan Bourne in a “bodyslam challenge”; Mark Henry overturned Edge in a “bodyslam challenge”; R-Truth and Eve Torres defeated Ted Dibiase Jr and Maryse in a “singing and dancing contest”; WWE World Champion Sheamus defeated John Morrison in a falls-count-anywhere encounter; Chris Jericho defeated DH Smith and Tyson Kidd in a handicap cage match; Goldust downed William Regal in a “trading places” match; Randy Orton rolled over John Cena in a tables match.

Air Date: 20th September 2010
WWE Unified World Tag Champions Drew McIntyre and Cody Rhodes dropped Santino Marella and Vladimir Kozlov; John Morrison beat Chris Jericho; WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan overturned Edge by DQ; WWE Divas Champion Layla (a permitted substitute for Michelle McCool) defeated Melina; R-Truth and Eve Torres defeated Ted Dibiase Jr and Maryse; Gauntlet match: John Cena toppled Heath Slater, John Cena toppled David Otunga, John Cena toppled Michael Tarver, John Cena toppled Justin Gabriel by DQ.

Air Date: 27th September 2010
The Miz and Alex Riley dropped WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan and John Morrison; Natalya won a women’s battle royal; The Great Khali overcame Sheamus by DQ; John Cena toppled Edge; Heath Slater and Justin Gabriel beat Evan Bourne and Mark Henry; WWE Unified World Tag Champions Drew McIntyre and Cody Rhodes dropped DH Smith and Tyson Kidd; WWE World Champion Randy Orton battled Chris Jericho to a no-contest.

Oct 2010

Air Date: 4th October 2010
Evan Bourne and Mark Henry bested John Cena and Michael Tarver; Natalya beat Alicia Fox; WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan toppled Sheamus by DQ; The Bella Twins overcame Layla and WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool; Wade Barrett won a 20 man battle royal.

Air Date: 11th October 2010
R-Truth downed Ted Dibiase Jr; John Morrison dropped Tyson Kidd; Santino Marella overcame Zack Ryder; WWE World Champion Randy Orton rolled over Justin Gabriel; Sheamus bested WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan in a non-title match; CM Punk bested Evan Bourne; The Miz toppled John Cena in a no-DQ match.

Air Date: 18th October 2010
WWE Unified World Tag Champions Drew McIntyre and Cody Rhodes toppled DH Smith and Tyson Kidd; Goldust defeated Zack Ryder; John Cena and WWE World Champion Randy Orton rolled over Michael McGillicutty and Husky Harris; Natalya beat Alicia Fox; The Big Show battled The Miz to a no-contest; The Big Show Show and Edge co-won a battle royal.

Air Date 25th October 2010
Justin Gabriel and Heath Slater overcame WWE Unified World Tag Champions John Cena and David Otunga to win the titles; WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler in a non-title match; Layla and WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool downed Gail Kim and Melina; The Miz dropped R-Truth; Santino Marella bested Sheamus; John Cena rolled over WWE World Champion Randy Orton by DQ.

Nov 2010

Air Date: 1st November 2010
WWE Tag Team Champions Justin Gabriel and Heath Slater defeated Tyson Kidd and DH Smith in a non-title match; Sheamus pinned Vladimir Kozlov; Ezekiel Jackson dropped Zack Ryder; The Big Show downed The Miz by DQ; WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan bested Ted Dibiase Jr in a non-title match; Natalya Neidhart pinned WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool in a non-title match; WWE World Champion Randy Orton and R-Truth rolled over Wade Barrett and David Otunga. John Cena was guest referee.

Air Date: 8th November 2010
Eve, Brie and Nikki Bella defeated Maryse, Tamina and Alicia Fox; Jimmy and Jey Uso overcame DH Smith and Tyson Kidd; Goldust pinned Ted Dibiase Jr; John Cena bested David Otunga; Sheamus rolled over Santino Marella by DQ; Wade Barrett, Husky Harris, Michael McGillicutty and WWE Tag Team Champions Heath Slater & Justin Gabriel rolled over WWE World Champion Randy Orton, R-Truth, The Miz, WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan and Mark Henry.

Air Date: 15th November 2010
WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler defeated Mark Henry in a non-title match; WWE Tag Team Champions Justin Gabriel and Heath Slater overcame DH Smith and Tyson Kidd; Ezekiel Jackson pinned The Brooklyn Brawler; John Cena bested Alex Riley; Santino Marella and Vladimir Kozlov overturned Jimmy and Jey Uso; Kofi Kingston pinned David Otunga; Wade Barrett dropped R-Truth; Alberto Del Rio rolled over Sgt Slaughter; Mae Young beat WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool and WWE Divas Champion Layla in an non-title, falls count anywhere match; WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan bested Jack Swagger in a non-title match.

Air Date: 22nd November 2010
Sheamus dropped R-Truth (KOTR Tournament – Qualifier); Ezekiel Jackson pinned Alex Riley (KOTR Tournament – Qualifier); WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan downed Ted Dibiase Jr in a non-title match (KOTR Tournament – Qualifier); WWE Divas Champion Natalya overcame Alicia Fox in a non-title match; John Morrison bested Tyson Kidd (KOTR Tournament – Qualifier); WWE World Champion Randy Orton rolled over Wade Barrett; The Miz toppled WWE World Champion Randy Orton to win the title.

Air Date: 29th November 2010
Alberto Del Rio downed WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan in a non-title match (KOTR Tournament – First Round); John Morrison dropped Cody Rhodes; (KOTR Tournament – First Round);  Ezekiel Jackson battled Drew McIntyre to a double count-out; (KOTR Tournament – First Round); Sheamus pinned Kofi Kingston (KOTR Tournament – First Round); Yoshi Tatsu and Mark Henry overcame WWE Tag Team Champions Justin Gabriel and Heath Slater in a non-title match; John Morrison bested Alberto Del Rio (KOTR Tournament – Semi-Final); WWE Divas Champion Natalya, Gail Kim and Melina overturned Maryse, Tamina and Alicia Fox; Sheamus rolled over John Morrison (KOTR Tournament – Final); WWE World Champion The Miz bested Jerry Lawler in a TLC match.

Dec 2010

Air Date: 6th December 2010
WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan and Brie Bella overcame Ted Dibiase Jr and Maryse; Tyson Kidd pinned DH Smith; Santino Marella and Vladimir Kozlov overturned WWE World Tag Champions Justin Gabriel and Heath Slater, Jimmy and Jey Uso, and Mark Henry and Yoshi Tatsu in a Fatal Four Way elimination match to win the titles;  WWE Divas Champion Natalya Neidhart defeated Melina; Randy Orton rolled over Alex Riley by DQ.

Air Date: 13th December 2010
The Big Show defeated Wade Barrett by count-out; WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan and Kofi Kingston downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler and Ted Dibiase Jr; Cody Rhodes pinned Mark Henry; WWE World Champion The Miz dropped Rey Mysterio; John Morrison battled Sheamus to a double-DQ; Michelle McCool won a 14-diva battle royal; Edge toppled Jack Swagger; Randy Orton bested Alex Riley and David Arquette in a handicap match; John Cena rolled over David Otunga.

Air Date: 20th December 2010
Melina defeated Eve and Alicia Fox in a Triple Threat; WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan bested William Regal; Santino Marella and Tamina toppled Ted Dibiase Jr and Maryse; John Cena dropped WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler in a non-title match; John Morrison, Randy Orton and Jerry Lawler rolled over WWE World Champion The Miz, Alex Riley and Sheamus.

Air Date: 27th December 2010
Santino Marella beat Ted Dibiase Jr; John Morrison dropped Alex Riley; Mark Henry overcame Tyson Kidd; Jerry Lawler bested WWE World Champion The Miz by count-out; Melina and Alicia Fox defeated Eve and Gail Kim; WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan overturned Zack Ryder; Randy Orton rolled over Sheamus.
