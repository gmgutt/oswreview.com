ROH iPPV Results 2012

ROH iPPV RESULTS 2012

4th March 2012: “ROH 10th Anniversary Show”: New York, NY (Hammerstein Ballroom) – 1,400 (1,300 paid)
2,000 iPPV buys
Shown on ippv (GoFightLive.tv).
Rhett Titus and Kenny King downed Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas; Mike Bennett downed Homicide; Michael Elgin and Roderick Strong overcame TJ Perkins and Amazing Red; ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal battled Tommaso Ciampa to a draw; ROH Tag Champions Jay and Mark Briscoe toppled The Young Bucks; Kevin Steen pinned Jimmy Jacobs in a no-DQ match; Eddie Edwards and Adam Cole rolled over ROH Champion Davey Richards and Kyle O’Reilly.

30th March 2012: “ROH Showdown in the Sun – Night 1”: Fort Lauderdale, FL – 875
1,500 iPPV buys
Shown on ippv (GoFightLive.tv).
Dark match: Bruce Santee beat Wes Brisco
iPPV Broadcast: ROH Tag Champions Mark and Jay Briscoe defeated Shane Haste and Mikey Nicholls; Adam Cole overcame Adam Pearce; Kenny King and Rhett Titus downed The Young Bucks in a tornado match; ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal dropped Kyle O’Reilly; Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas overturned Cedric Alexander and Caprice Coleman; Mike Bennett bested Lance Storm; Kevin Steen toppled El Generico in a Last Man Standing match; ROH Champion Davey Richards rolled over Roderick Strong and Eddie Edwards in a three-way.

31st March 2012: “ROH Showdown in the Sun – Night 2”: Fort Lauderdale, FL – 725
1,500 iPPV buys
Shown on ippv (GoFightLive.tv).
Jimmy Jacobs beat El Generico; Tommaso Ciampa defeated Cedric Alexander; TJ Perkins overcame Fire Ant; Kyle O’Reilly downed Adam Cole; The Young Bucks dropped Kenny King and Rhett Titus in a streetfight; ROH Tag Champions Mark and Jay Briscoe bested Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin; Kevin Steen toppled Eddie Edwards; Roderick Strong overturned ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal to win the title; ROH Champion Davey Richards rolled over Michael Elgin.

12th May 2012: “ROH Border Wars”: Toronto, ONT – 1,350 sellout
2,000 iPPV buys
Shown on ippv (ROHWrestling.com).
Dark match: Grizzly Redwood beat Delirious
iPPV Broadcast: Eddie Edwards beat Rhino; Rhett Titus, Kenny King and TJ Perkins overcame The Young Bucks and Mike Mondo; Jay Lethal defeated Tommaso Ciampa; Lance Storm downed Mike Bennett; Michael Elgin dropped Adam Cole; ROH TV Champion Michael Elgin overturned Finlay; Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin bested ROH Tag Champions Mark and Jay Briscoe in a “Fight without Honor” to win the titles; Kevin Steen rolled over ROH Champion Davey Richards to win the title.

24th June 2012: “ROH “Best in the World 2012 – Hostage Crisis”: New York, NY (Hammerstein Ballroom) – 1,200
1,450 iPPV orders
The show was offered free to those who had ordered the previous iPPV “Border Wars” and had not been able to access the full show. These comps are included in the orders total.
Mark and Jay Briscoe beat The Guardians of Truth (Chaz Warrington and Glen Ruth); Homicide downed Eddie Edwards; Adam Cole dropped Kyle O’Reilly in a “Hybrid Rules” encounter; Michael Elgin toppled Finlay; Mike Mondo pinned Mike Bennett; ROH TV Champion Roderick Strong overturned Jay Lethal and Tommaso Ciampa in a three-way; Rhett Titus and Kenny King bested ROH Tag Champions Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin to win the titles; ROH Champion Kevin Steen rolled over Davey Richards in an “anything goes” main event.

11th August 2012: “ROH Boiling Point 2012”: Providence, RI – 850 sellout
650 iPPV buys
Dark match: Harlem and Lance Bravado beat Jorge Santi and Mike Sydal.
iPPV Broadcast: Roderick Strong defeated Mike Mondo; QT Marshall overcame Antonio Thomas, Vinny Marseglia and Matt Taven in a four-way; ROH TV Champion Adam Cole pinned Brutal Bob Evans; Charlie Haas bested Michael Elgin; Mark and Jay Briscoe downed Steve Corino and Jimmy Jacobs in a “Relaxed Rules” encounter; Jay Lethal dropped Tommaso Ciampa in a “best-of-three-falls” encounter; Eddie Edwards and Sara Del Rey toppled Mike Bennett and Maria Kanellis; ROH Champion Kevin Steen rolled over CHIKARA champion Eddie Kingston in a no-DQ match.

15th September 2012: “ROH Death Before Dishonor X”: Chicago Ridge, IL – 625
Dark match: Mike Sydal beat Matt Sterling.
iPPV Broadcast: Steve Corino and Jimmy Jacobs overturned Cedric Alexander and Caprice Coleman (ROH Tag Title Tournament – Semi-Final); Tadarius Thomas downed Silas Young; Kyle O’Reilly defeated ACH; Charlie Haas and Rhett Titus dropped Mark and Jay Briscoe (ROH Tag Title Tournament – Semi-Final); Jay Lethal toppled Homicide; Roderick Strong and Michael Elgin bested Dave and Jake Crist; ROH TV Champion Adam Cole overcame Mike Mondo; Jimmy Jacobs and Steve Corino bested Charlie Haas and Rhett Titus to win the vacant ROH Tag Titles (ROH Tag Title Tournament – Finals); ROH Champion Kevin Steen rolled over Rhino in an “Anything Goes” main event.

13th October 2012: “ROH Glory by Honor XI”: Mississauga, ONT – 900
1,500 iPPV buys (1,200 paid – 350 first time orders)
Dark match: Chaz Montana and Rip Impact beat Mathieu St Jacques and Mike Rollins.
iPPV Broadcast: Caprice Coleman and Cedric Alexander beat Lance and Harlem Bravado; Mike Bennett defeated Mike Mondo; Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas downed BJ Whitmer and Rhett Titus; Jay Lethal bested Davey Richards; Tadarius Thomas dropped Rhino; ROH TV Champion Adam Cole overturned Eddie Edwards; ROH Tag Champions Steve Corino and Jimmy Jacobs toppled Mark and Jay Briscoe; ROH Champion Kevin Steen rolled over Michael Elgin.

16th December 2012: “ROH Final Battle 2012”: New York, NY (Hammerstein Ballroom) – 1,400
2,000 iPPV buys
Dark match: Grizzly Redwood beat QT Marshall.
iPPV Broadcast: Roderick Strong defeated Michael Elgin; Jay Lethal overturned Rhino; RD Evans downed Prince Nana; Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin beat Rhett Titus and BJ Whitmer in a streetfight; Mike Bennett pinned Jerry Lynn; Eddie Edwards and Davey Richards toppled Bobby Fish and Kyle O’Reilly; Matt Hardy dropped TV champion Adam Cole in a non-title match; Mark and Jay Briscoe bested ROH Tag Champions Jimmy Jacobs and Steve Corino, and Cedric Alexander and Caprice Coleman in a three-way; Kevin Steen rolled over El Generico in a ladder match.
