ECW Results 2010

ECW RESULTS (2010)

Jan 2010

Air Date: 5th January 2010
Shelton Benjamin downed Chavo Gurerrero Jr (ECW Homecoming Tournament); Ezekiel Jackson beat Vladimir Kozlov; CM Punk dropped Mark Henry (ECW Homecoming Tournament).

Air Date: 12th January 2010
Zack Ryder beat The Hurricane; Trent Barretta downed Goldust; Ezekiel Jackson won an eight man battle royal, last eliminating Kane.

Air Date: 19th January 2010
Caylen Croft and Trent Barretta downed Goldust and Yoshi Tatsu; ECW Champion Christian bested William Regal by DQ.

Air Date: 26th January 2010
Ezekiel Jackson and William Regal toppled ECW Champion Christian and Kane; Yoshi Tatsu, Goldust and The Hurricane overturned Zack Ryder, Trent Barretta and Caylen Croft.

Feb 2010

Air Date: 2nd February 2010
Vance Archer beat Shelton Benjamin; Yoshi Tatsu downed Trent Barretta; ECW Champion Christian toppled Zack Ryder by DQ.

Air Date: 9th February 2010
Yoshi Tatsu and Goldust beat Caylen Croft and Trent Barretta; Ezekiel Jackson downed Perry Wallace; Shelton Benjamin dropped Vance Archer in a no-DQ, no count-out match.

Air Date: 16th February 2010
Final broadcast of ECW
WWE Unified World Tag Champions The Miz and The Big Show dropped Goldust and Yoshi Tatsu; Ezekiel Jackson bested ECW Champion Christian to win the title in an Extreme Rules match.
