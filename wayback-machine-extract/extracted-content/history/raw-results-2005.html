Raw Results 2005

WWE RAW RESULTS (2005)

Jan 2005

Air Date: 3rd January 2005
Batista downed Chris Benoit; Edge dropped Chris Jericho; WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin pinned Sylvan Grenier; Trish Stratus overcame Victoria; Christian bested Eugene; Randy Orton rolled over Triple H.

Air Date: 10th January 2005
WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin pinned Maven; Muhammad Hassan overcame The Hurricane; Edge dropped Rhyno; Chris Benoit and Chris Jericho bested Christian and Tyson Tomko; Christy Hemme overturned Maria in a “lingerie pillow fight”. Candice Michelle was guest referee; Randy Orton rolled over Batista.

Air Date: 17th January 2005
Maven, and WWE World Tag Champions Rob Conway and Sylvan Grenier defeated WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin, Rosey, and The Hurricane; Shawn Michaels bested Christian; Batista downed Viscera; Chris Jericho toppled Chris Benoit; Kane battled Gene Snitsky to a no-contest in a “no holds barred” match.

Air Date: 24th January 2005
Shawn Michaels, Chris Benoit, and Chris Jericho defeated Edge, Christian, and Tyson Tomko; Viscera downed Tajiri in an “over-the-top-rope challenge”; Batista downed WWE World Tag Champions Rob Conway and Sylvan Grenier in a handicap match; Muhammad Hassan dropped Val Venis; Kane won a three-way over  Maven and Gene Snitsky; Randy Orton rolled over Ric Flair.

Air Date: 31st January 2005
WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin defeated Simon Dean; Maven downed The Hurricane; Chris Benoit and Chris Jericho battled WWE World Tag Champions Rob Conway and Sylvan Grenier to a double-DQ; Muhammad Hassan dropped Sgt Slaughter; Randy Orton and Shawn Michaels bested World Heavyweight Champion Triple H and Ric Flair; Kane rolled over Gene Snitsky in a cage match.

Feb 2005

Air Date: 7th February 2005
Chris Benoit bested Chris Jericho in a submission match; Batista downed Maven; William Regal and Tajiri defeated WWE World Tag Champions Rob Conway and Sylvan Grenier to win the titles; Shawn Michaels pinned Ric Flair; Randy Orton dropped Tyson Tomko; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H rolled over Edge.

Air Date: 14th February 2005
Muhammad Hassan beat Chris Jericho; WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin defeated Gene Snitsky by DQ; WWE World Tag Champions William Regal and Tajiri downed Rob Conway and Sylvan Grenier; Randy Orton bested Christian; Kane toppled Simon Dean; Edge battled Batista to a no-contest.

Air Date: 21st February 2005
WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus won a three-way over  Victoria and Molly Holly; Chris Jericho defeated Simon Dean; Kane pinned Tyson Tomko; Shawn Michaels and Randy Orton bested Edge and Christian; Chris Masters toppled Steven Richards; Gene Snitsky overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin by DQ.

Air Date: 28th February 2005
World Heavyweight Champion Triple H battled The Hurricane to a no-contest; Chris Benoit defeated Muhammad Hassan by DQ; WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin downed Gene Snitsky; Chris Masters beat John Walters; Chris Jericho bested Maven; Shawn Michaels toppled Edge in a streetfight.

Mar 2005

Air Date: 7th March 2005
World Heavyweight Champion Triple H pinned Rosey; Kane beat Christian; Edge dropped Chris Jericho; Chris Benoit bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin in a non-title match; Batista downed Ric Flair.

Air Date: 14th March 2005
Kane beat Christian and Tyson Tomko in a handicap match; WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin pinned Edge; Shawn Michaels and Marty Jannetty bested Rob Conway and Sylvan Grenier; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H toppled Chris Benoit; Batista downed Gene Snitsky by DQ.

Air Date: 21st March 2005
Shawn Michaels pinned Rob Conway; Christy Hemme and WWE World Tag Champions William Regal and Tajiri defeated Molly Holly, Simon Dean, and Maven; Edge and Christian downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin and Chris Jericho; Chris Benoit dropped Tyson Tomko; Batista downed Kane in a no-DQ lumberjack match.

Air Date: 28th March 2005
Chris Jericho, Chris Benoit, and WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin defeated Edge, Christian, and Tyson Tomko; Christy Hemme beat WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus in an “arm wrestling” match; WWE World Tag Champions William Regal and Tajiri won a three-way over Maven and Simon Dean, and Rob Conway and Sylvan Grenier; Shawn Michaels battled Muhammad Hassan to a no-contest.

Apr 2005

Air Date: 4th April 2005
WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin won a three-way over  Chris Jericho and Christian; WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus battled Christy Hemme to a no-contest; Chris Benoit pinned Edge; World Heavyweight Champion Batista downed Randy Orton.

Air Date: 11th April 2005
Christy Hemme and Victoria defeated WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus and Molly Holly; Triple H downed The Hurricane and Rosey in a handicap match; Chris Masters dropped Seth Skyfire; Daivari bested Shawn Michaels; Christian toppled Chris Benoit.

Air Date: 18th April 2005
Chris Benoit battled Edge to a no contest; WWE World Tag Champions William Regal and Tajiri defeated The Heart Throbs; Shawn Michaels downed Muhammad Hassan by DQ; WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin dropped Simon Dean; Jim Ross rolled over Triple H in a no-DQ match.

Air Date: 25th April 2005
Chris Jericho defeated Sylvan Grenier; Muhammad Hassan and Daivari downed WWE World Tag Champions William Regal and Tajiri in a non-title match; Edge dropped Val Venis; World Heavyweight Champion Batsita bested Christian. Jonathan Coachman was guest referee.

May 2005

Air Date: 2nd May 2005
Kane beat Christian (Gold Rush – Quarter-Final); Viscera defeated Simon Dean; Shawn Michaels downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin in a non-title match (Gold Rush – Quarter-Final); WWE World Tag Champions The Hurricane and Rosey dropped Rob Conway and Sylvan Grenier; Edge dropped Chris Jericho (Gold Rush – Quarter-Final); Chris Benoit toppled Triple H (Gold Rush – Quarter-Final).

Air Date: 9th May 2005
Chris Jericho defeated Daivari; Viscera pinned Sylvan Grenier; Kane bested Chris Benoit (Gold Rush – Semi-Final); Ric Flair overturned Tyson Tomko; WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin toppled Rob Conway; Edge dropped Shawn Michaels (Gold Rush – Semi-Final).

Air Date: 16th May 2005
Muhammad Hassan and Daivari dropped WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin and Chris Jericho; Ric Flair pinned Christian; Chris Benoit battled Tajiri to a no-contest in an “ECW Rules” match; Christy Hemme overcame Candice Michelle in a “lingerie pillow fight”; WWE World Tag Champions The Hurricane and Rosey overturned Maven and Simon Dean; Edge rolled over Kane (Gold Rush Tournament – Final).

Air Date: 23rd May 2005
Daivari and Muhammad Hassan defeated Shelton Benjamin in a handicap match; Chris Masters bested Steven Richards; Chris Jericho toppled Sylvan Grenier; Chris Benoit overturned Tazz in an “ECW Rules” encounter; World Heavyweight Champion Batista downed Edge.

Air Date: 30th May 2005
Rosey and The Hurricane bested The Heart Throbs; WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin won a three-way over Robert Conway and Sylvan Grenier; Edge toppled Chris Benoit; World Heavyweight Champion Batista rolled over Muhammad Hassan.

Jun 2005

Air Date: 6th June 2005
WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin overturned Muhammad Hassan and Daivari in a handicap match; Chris Masters bested Val Venis; Victoria and The Heart Throbs overcame Christy Hemme and WWE World Tag Champions The Hurricane and Rosey by DQ; Chris Benoit bested Gene Snitsky.

Air Date: 13th June 2005
Muhammad Hassan defeated WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin by DQ; WWE World Champion John Cena and Chris Jericho bested Christian and Tyson Tomko; Viscera toppled Maven; Kane bested Sylvan Grenier; Kane overturned Edge by DQ; World Heavyweight Champion Batista downed Triple H.

Air Date: 20th June 2005
Carlito bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin to win the title; Viscera overcame Simon Dean; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Muhammad Hassan; World Heavyweight Champion Batista bested Kurt Angle by DQ; Triple H and Kurt Angle toppled World Heavyweight Champion Batista and Shawn Michaels.

Air Date: 27th June 2005
The Big Show defeated Gene Snitsky; Kurt Angle downed Ric Flair; Hulk Hogan, Shawn Michaels and WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Chris Jericho, Christian, and Tyson Tomko.

Jul 2005

Air Date: 4th July 2005
Kane and The Big Show defeated Edge and Gene Snitsky; Viscera downed The Heart Throbs by DQ in a handicap match; Rene Dupree dropped Val Venis; Hulk Hogan and Shawn Michaels bested Kurt Angle and WWE Intercontinental Champion Carlito.

Air Date: 11th July 2005
Chris Masters beat Tajiri; Shelton Benjamin downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Carlito by count-out; Kurt Angle dropped Matt Striker; Kane toppled Edge by DQ.

Air Date: 18th July 2005
Shelton Benjamin bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Carlito by DQ in a “no count-out” match; Kurt Angle downed Matt Striker; Edge toppled Kane in a cage match; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Gene Snitsky in a lumberjack match.

Air Date: 25th July 2005
Eugene beat Kurt Angle in a “Kurt Angle Invitational Challenge”; Viscera and Cloacas defeated Antonio and Pocket Rocket; Chris Masters and Gene Snitsky downed Shelton Benjamin in a handicap match; Edge dropped Kane in a stretcher match; WWE Intercontinental Champion Carlito overturned WWE World Champion John Cena in a non-title match. Chris Jericho was guest referee.

Aug 2005

Air Date: 1st August 2005
The Big Show and Shelton Benjamin defeated Chris Masters and Gene Snitsky;
Eugene downed Tatanka by DQ; Rob Conway dropped Val Venis; WWE World Champion John Cena bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Carlito. Chris Jericho was guest referee;

Air Date: 8th August 2005
Eugene downed Kurt Angle by DQ; Chris Masters defeated Shelton Benjamin; Rob Conway pinned Viscera; Victoria and The Heart Throbs defeated Stacy Keibler and WWE World Tag Champions The Hurricane and Rosey; Chris Jericho toppled Chad Patton. Eric Bischoff was guest referee; Matt Hardy pinned Gene Snitsky.

Air Date: 15th August 2005
The Big Show downed The Heart Throbs in a handicap match; Eugene battled Rene Dupree to a no-contest; WWE World Champion John Cena beat Chris Jericho and WWE Intercontinental Champion Carlito in a handicap match; Edge downed Val Venis; Rob Conway dropped The Hurricane; Hulk Hogan rolled over Kurt Angle by DQ.

Air Date: 22nd August 2005
Kurt Angle bested Shelton Benjamin; Rob Conway pinned Matt Hardy; The Big Show downed Gene Snitsky by count-out; WWE World Champion John Cena beat Chris Jericho in a “loser-gets-fired” encounter.

Air Date: 29th August 2005
The Big Show downed Steve Madison and Buck Quartermaine in a handicap match; Victoria beat Ashley; Edge battled Matt Hardy to a no-contest in a streetfight; Tyson Tomko toppled Rosey; Chris Masters and WWE Intercontinental Champion Carlito toppled Shawn Michaels and Ric Flair.

Sep 2005


Air Date: 5th September 2005
Kurt Angle bested Eugene; Viscera and Val Venis defeated The Heart Throbs; Gene Snitsky pinned Matt Hardy; WWE World Champion John Cena beat Tyson Tomko; Torrie Wilson beat Ashley; Rob Conway downed Shelton Benjamin; Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch overturned WWE World Tag Champions The Hurricane and Rosey in a non-title match.

Air Date: 12th September 2005
The Big Show battled Edge to a no-contest; Edge and Gene Snitsky dropped Matt Hardy and The Big Show; Shelton Benjamin downed Kerwin White by DQ; Ric Flair bested Chris Masters by DQ; Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch overturned Eugene and Tajiri; WWE World Champion John Cena beat Kurt Angle and Tyson Tomko in a handicap match.

Air Date: 19th September 2005
WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus beat Torrie Wilson; Trevor Murdoch bested The Hurricane; Tyson Tomko toppled Eddie Craven by stoppage; WWE Intercontinental Champion Ric Flair overturned Carlito; WWE World Champion John Cena, The Big Show, Matt Hardy, and Shawn Michaels rolled over Kurt Angle, Chris Masters, Edge, and Gene Snitsky.

Air Date: 26th September 2005
Final episode to air on Spike TV.
WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus downed Victoria by DQ; The Big Show pinned Gene Snitsky in a no-DQ match; Kerwin White beat Shelton Benjamin; Val Venis and Viscera beat WWE World Tag Champions Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch by DQ; Rob Conway pinned Eugene; Carlito and Chris Masters defeated WWE World Champion John Cena and Shawn Michaels in a tables match.

Oct 2005

From 10/03/2005 onwards, Raw moved back to the USA Network.

Air Date: 3rd October 2005
Shawn Michaels battled Kurt Angle to a draw in a 30-minute Ironman match. 2:2; Edge downed  Matt Hardy in a ladder match; Triple H and WWE Intercontinental Champion Ric Flair dropped Carlito and Chris Masters; WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus and Ashley dropped Torrie Wilson, Victoria, and Candice Michelle in a handicap “bra and panties” match; World Heavyweight Champion Batista, WWE US Champion Chris Benoit, and Rey Mysterio battled John Bradshaw Layfield, Eddie Guerrero, and Christian to a no-contest; WWE World Champion John Cena beat Eric Bischoff in a no-DQ match.

Air Date: 10th October 2005
Rob Conway toppled Doink (Steve Lombardi); Chris Masters defeated Tajiri; WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus defeated Victoria; Kurt Angle, Edge and Carlito toppled WWE World Champion John Cena, Shawn Michaels, and The Big Show.

Air Date: 17th October 2005
The Big Show pinned Edge; Shawn Michaels defeated Carlito; Victoria, Torrie Wilson, and Candice Michelle bested WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus, Ashley, and Mickie James; Kane won a battle royal.

Air Date: 24th October 2005
Shawn Michaels won a three-way over The Big Show and Kane; Triple H battled Viscera to a no-contest; Mickie James beat Victoria; WWE World Tag Champions Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch overturned Rosey in a handicap match; Kurt Angle bested WWE World Champion John Cena in a non-title match. Mick Foley was guest referee.

Air Date: 31st October 2005
Kurt Angle bested Tajiri; Eugene bested Rob Conway by DQ; Triple H overturned Viscera; The Big Show and Kane toppled The Heart Throbs in a Texas Tornado match; Shawn Michaels defeated WWE World Champion John Cena by DQ.

Nov 2005

Air Date: 7th November 2005
Carlito toppled Shelton Benjamin; Victoria and Candice Michelle bested WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus and Mickie James; Gregory Helms defeated Rosey; WWE World Tag Champions The Big Show and Kane bested Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch in a hardcore match; WWE Intercontinental Champion Ric Flair downed Rob Conway; Kurt Angle and Chris Masters rolled over WWE World Champion John Cena and Shawn Michaels by DQ. Daivari was guest referee.

Air Date: 14th November 2005
WWE World Tag Champions The Big Show and Kane bested WWE Tag Champions Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury in a non-title match; Kurt Angle bested Shelton Benjamin; Rey Mysterio rolled over Shawn Michaels; Melina won a battle royal; Eugene overcame Simon Dean; WWE Intercontinental Champion Ric Flair downed William Regal; WWE World Champion John Cena beat Randy Orton by DQ.

Air Date: 21st November 2005
WWE Intercontinental Champion Ric Flair pinned Trevor Murdoch; Tajiri battled Rob Conway to a no-contest; Kurt Angle overcame Shelton Benjamin. Daivari was guest referee; Val Venis defeated Triple H by DQ; Candice Michelle overcame Mickie James; Shawn Michaels battled John Bradshaw Layfield to a no-contest in a lumberjack match.

Air Date: 28th November 2005
Shawn Michaels defeated Carlito; Kurt Angle battled Maria to a no-contest; WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus, Ashley, and Mickie James defeated Victoria, Candice Michelle, and Torrie Wilson; Trevor Murdoch overturned Shelton Benjamin; WWE World Tag Champions Kane and The Big Show downed Tyson Tomko and Gene Snitsky; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Kurt Angle and Chris Masters in a no-DQ submission match.

Dec 2005

Air Date: 5th December 2005
WWE World Tag Champions The Big Show and Kane bested Val Venis and Viscera, Tyson Tomko and Gene Snitsky, and The Heart Throbs in a four-way; Victoria beat Mickie James; Chavo Guerrero Jr pinned Lance Cade; Triple H overturned Tajiri; Kurt Angle and Carlito rolled over Shawn Michaels and Shelton Benjamin.

Air Date: 12th December 2005
Kurt Angle overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion Ric Flair in a non-title match; Carlito toppled Shelton Benjamin; Shawn Michaels defeated The Big Show by DQ; Chris Masters downed Viscera; Mickie James dropped Victoria; Kane overcame Triple H; WWE World Champion John Cena beat Daivari.

Air Date: 19th December 2005
The Big Show pinned Carlito; Mick Foley bested John Bradshaw Layfield in a “no holds barred” match; Gene Snitsky toppled Shelton Benjamin; WWE World Champion John Cena beat Chris Masters; WWE Intercontinental Champion Ric Flair overturned Jonathan Coachman; Candice Michelle and Maria beat WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus and Ashley; Shawn Michaels rolled over Triple H in a “Boot Camp” match.

Air Date: 26th December 2005
Shawn Michaels beat Snitsky (Beat the Clock); Chris Masters battled Chavo Guerrero Jr to a no-contest (Beat the Clock); Daivari defeated Kurt Angle by count-out (Beat the Clock); WWE World Champion John Cena bested Shelton Benjamin (Beat the Clock); Carlito toppled Victoria (Beat the Clock); Kane bested The Heart Breakers in a handicap match (Beat the Clock).
