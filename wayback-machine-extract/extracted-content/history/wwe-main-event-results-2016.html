WWE Main Event Results 2016

WWE MAIN EVENT RESULTS (2016)

Jan 2016

Air Date: 2nd January 2016
R-Truth overturned Heath Slater; Titus O’Neil defeated Adam Rose; Jack Swagger rolled over Stardust.

Air Date: 9th January 2016
Titus O’Neil defeated Stardust; Tyler Breeze dropped Zack Ryder; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso bested Bo Dallas and Curtis Axel.

Air Date: 16th January 2016
Stardust beat Fandango; Alicia Fox defeated Tamina; Jack Swagger and Mark Henry toppled The Ascension.

Air Date: 23rd January 2016
Jack Swagger and Mark Henry defeated Viktor and Konnor; Neville dropped Tyler Breeze; Curtis Axel and Heath Slater rolled over Bubba Ray Dudley and D-Von Dudley.

Air Date: 29th January 2016
Kevin Owens downed Jack Swagger; The Dudley Boys toppled The Ascension; Rusev dropped Ryback.

Feb 2016

Air Date: 5th February 2016
Jey Uso, Jimmy Uso, Darren Young and Titus O’Neil beat Stardust, Tyler Breeze, Viktor and Konnor; Jack Swagger rolled over Fandango; Naomi and Tamina Snuka downed Paige and Natalya.

Air Date: 12th February 2016
Zack Ryder rolled over Heath Slater; Natalya beat Alicia Fox; Mark Henry and Darren Young defeated Damien Sandow and Fandango.

Air Date: 19th February 2016
Neville and Zack Ryder rolled over Tyler Breeze and Stardust; Paige downed Summer Rae; Big E, Kofi Kingston and Xavier Woods overturned Heath Slater, Adam Rose and Curtis Axel.

Air Date: 27th February 2016
Bo Dallas overturned Darren Young; Naomi dropped Paige; Ryback defeated Tyler Breeze; Braun Strowman overcame Fandango.

Mar 2016

Air Date: 5th March 2016
Paige, Natalya and Brie Bella defeated Summer Rae, Naomi and Tamina; Ryback beat Heath Slater; Sin Cara and WWE US Champion Kalisto bested The Ascension; Stardust toppled Zack Ryder.

Air Date: 11th March 2016
Zack Ryder overcame Tyler Breeze; Ryback dropped Bo Dallas; Naomi and Tamina defeated Natalya and Paige.

Air Date: 18th March 2016
Jack Swagger overcame Tyler Breeze; Sin Cara and Kalisto bested The Ascension; Paige downed Summer Rae; Sami Zayn toppled Stardust.

Air Date: 25th March 2016
Zack Ryder overcame Stardust; Naomi dropped Paige; Ryback dropped Sin Cara; The Big Show and Kane downed Bo Dallas and Curtis Axel.

Apr 2016

Air Date: 1st April 2016
Ryback dropped Fandango.

Air Date: 8th April 2016
WWE World Tag Champions Big E and Kofi Kingston toppled The Ascension; Baron Corbin toppled Fandango; Paige downed Emma.

Air Date: 15th April 2016
WWE US Champion Kalisto bested Heath Slater; Apollo Crews overturned Bo Dallas; Kevin Owens overturned Dolph Ziggler.

Air Date: 22nd April 2016
Apollo Crews overturned Curtis Axel; Summer Rae downed Alicia Fox; The Big Show and Kane downed Erick Rowan and Braun Strowman.

Air Date: 26th April 2016
Luke Gallows and Karl Anderson overcame Zack Ryder and Fandango; Dolph Ziggler rolled over Viktor; Kevin Owens overturned Jack Swagger.

May 2016

Air Date: 3rd May 2016
Titus O’Neil defeated Damien Sandow; Apollo Crews overturned Viktor; Baron Corbin overcame Sin Cara; Dolph Ziggler rolled over Stardust.

Air Date: 10th May 2016
Big Cass overturned Curtis Axel; Apollo Crews overturned Stardust; Baron Corbin overcame Zack Ryder.

Air Date: 17th May 2016
Titus O’Neil and Jack Swagger rolled over Bo Dallas and Curtis Axel; Apollo Crews overturned Viktor; Alberto del Río bested Zack Ryder.

Air Date: 24th May 2016
Big E, Xavier Woods and Kofi Kingston overcame Heath Slater, Bo Dallas and Curtis Axel; Paige downed Summer Rae; Baron Corbin overcame Jack Swagger.

Air Date: 31st May 2016
Enzo Amore and Big Cass overturned Aiden English and Simon Gotch; Zack Ryder rolled over Viktor; Sasha Banks overturned Summer Rae.

Jun 2016

Air Date: 7th June 2016
Sheamus overturned Zack Ryder; Titus O’Neil defeated Viktor; WWE US Champion Rusev defeated Sin Cara.

Air Date: 14th June 2016
Becky Lynch defeated Summer Rae; Jack Swagger rolled over Viktor; Apollo Crews overturned Bubba Ray Dudley.

Air Date: 21st June 2016
Enzo Amore and Big Cass overturned Konnor and Viktor; Baron Corbin overcame Sin Cara; WWE US Champion Rusev defeated Zack Ryder.

Air Date: 28th June 2016
Kalisto and Sin Cara beat Viktor and Konnor; R-Truth and Goldust defeated Fandango and Tyler Breeze; Epico and Primo bested Jack Swagger and Zack Ryder; Enzo Amore and Big Cass overturned Bubba Ray Dudley and D-Von Dudley.

Jul 2016

Air Date: 5th July 2016
Cesaro bested Curtis Axel; Baron Corbin overcame Sin Cara; Goldust and R-Truth defeated Viktor and Konnor; WWE US Champion Rusev defeated Apollo Crews.

Air Date: 12th July 2016
Jack Swagger, Apollo Crews, Goldust and R-Truth defeated Simon Gotch, Aiden English, Viktor and Konnor; Becky Lynch defeated Alicia Fox; Sheamus overturned Titus O’Neil; Cesaro, Jey Uso and Jimmy Uso bested Alberto del Río, Bubba Ray Dudley and D-Von Dudley.

Air Date: 20th July 2016
Goldust, R-Truth and Apollo Crews overturned Bubba Ray Dudley, D-Von Dudley and Baron Corbin; Kalisto bested Viktor; Mark Henry and Jack Swagger rolled over Tyler Breeze and Fandango; Sheamus, Alberto del Río, Primo and Epico bested Titus O’Neil, Dolph Ziggler, Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso.

Air Date: 27th July 2016
Zack Ryder and Mojo Rawley toppled Aiden English and Simon Gotch; Tyler Breeze downed Jey Uso.

Aug 2016

Air Date: 3rd August 2016
Tyler Breeze downed Jey Uso; Zack Ryder and Mojo Rawley toppled Konnor and Viktor.

Air Date: 10th August 2016
Sin Cara beat Jinder Mahal; Mojo Rawley and Zack Ryder rolled over Tyler Breeze and Fandango.

Air Date: 17th August 2016
Kane downed Ricky Starks; Baron Corbin overcame Rhyno.

Air Date: 24th August 2016
Erick Rowan overcame Rhyno; Mojo Rawley and Zack Ryder rolled over Simon Gotch and Aiden English.

Air Date: 31st August 2016
Jey Uso bested Konnor; Chad Gable and Jason Jordan overcame Tyler Breeze and Fandango.
