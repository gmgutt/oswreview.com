04/16/2000 Chris Candido 29 Chicago, IL
Defeated Iaukea, Juventud Guerrera, Shannon Moore, Lash Leroux and Crowbar in a six-way match to win the title.
05/15/2000 Crowbar 7 Biloxi, MS

05/22/2000 Daffney 15 Grand Rapids, MI

07/31/2000 Lance Storm 14 Cincinnati, OH

10/02/2000 Mike Sanders 63 Daly City, CA
With Kevin Nash, defeated Skipper in a handicap powerbomb match to win the title.
12/04/2000 Chavo Guerrero Jr (2) 104 Lincoln, NE
