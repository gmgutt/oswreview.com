Saturday Morning Slam Results 2013

SATURDAY MORNING SLAM RESULTS (2013)

Jan 2013

Air Date: 5th January 2013
Kane defeated Jinder Mahal; Daniel Bryan downed Heath Slater.

Air Date: 12th January 2013
Randy Orton toppled Damien Sandow.

Air Date: 19th January 2013
Natalya beat Alicia Fox; Sheamus defeated Michael McGillicutty.

Air Date: 26th January 2013
Kofi Kingston downed Epico; The Miz dropped Darren Young.

Feb 2013

Air Date: 2nd February 2013
Dolph Ziggler defeated Zack Ryder; WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett downed Yoshi Tatsu.

Air Date: 9th February 2013
Brodus Clay and Kofi Kingston bested Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; Justin Gabriel pinned Curt Hawkins.

Air Date: 16th February 2013:
Sheamus defeated Cody Rhodes; Justin Gabriel downed Heath Slater.

Air Date: 23rd February 2013
WWE Divas Champion Kaitlyn bested Natalya; Jack Swagger dropped Jimmy Uso.

Mar 2013

Air Date: 2nd March 2013
Damien Sandow beat Zack Ryder; WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro defeated Justin Gabriel.

Air Date: 9th March 2013
Natalya beat AJ; Daniel Bryan defeated WWE Intercontinental Champion  Wade Barrett in a non-title match.

Air Date: 16th March 2013
Daniel Bryan dropped Dolph Ziggler; Brodus Clay and Tensai overcame Primo and Epico.

Air Date: 23rd March 2013
The Great Khali beat Primo and Epico in a handicap match; The Miz defeated Damien Sandow.

Air Date: 30th March 2013
Titus O’Neil and Darren Young dropped Tensai and Brodus Clay; Tensai and Brodus Clay bested Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; R-Truth toppled WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro in a non-title match.

Apr 2013

Air Date: 6th April 2013
Ryback pinned Damien Sandow; Kane bested Cody Rhodes.

Air Date: 13th April 2013
Drew McIntyre and Jinder Mahal dropped Zack Ryder and Justin Gabriel; Kofi Kingston downed Heath Slater; Mark Henry bested Daniel Bryan.

Air Date: 20th April 2013
Kofi Kingston downed Justin Gabriel.

Air Date: 27th April 2013
The Great Khali, Hornswoggle and Natalya overturned Primo, Epico and Rosa Mendes; Jimmy and Jey Uso toppled Damien Sandow and Cody Rhodes.

May 2013

Air Date: 4th May 2013
Jey Uso defeated Darren Young by DQ; Jimmy and Jey Uso downed Titus O’Neil and Darren Young.

Air Date: 11th May 2013
The final episode
Santino Marella defeated Jinder Mahal; Randy Orton and Sheamus downed Titus O’Neil and Darren Young.
