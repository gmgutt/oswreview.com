Smackdown Results 2017

WWE SMACKDOWN RESULTS (2017)

Jan 2017

Air Date: 3rd January 2017
Baron Corbin overcame Dolph Ziggler; Becky Lynch defeated La Luchadora; Carmella beat Aliyah; WWE Smackdown Tag Champions Chad Gable and Jason Jordan overcame Tyler Breeze and Fandango; Dean Ambrose downed WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz to win the title.

Air Date: 10th January 2017
Heath Slater and Rhyno bested Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso; Kalisto bested Dolph Ziggler; WWE Smackdown Tag Champions Chad Gable and Jason Jordan defeated Randy Orton and Bray Wyatt; Carmella beat CJ Lunde; John Cena beat Baron Corbin.

Air Date: 17th January 2017
Mojo Rawley toppled Tyler Breeze; WWE World Champion AJ Styles battled The Miz to a double count-out; WWE Intercontinental Champion Dean Ambrose downed Randy Orton; WWE Smackdown Women’s Champion Alexa Bliss defeated Becky Lynch in a cage match.

Air Date: 24th January 2017
WWE Smackdown Tag Champions Chad Gable and Jason Jordan defeated Fandango and Tyler Breeze; Randy Orton overcame Luke Harper; Mojo Rawley won a battle royal; Dolph Ziggler rolled over Kalisto; WWE Intercontinental Champion Dean Ambrose defeated The Miz in a lumberjack match.

Air Date: 31st January 2017
Randy Orton and Bray Wyatt defeated Luke Harper and WWE World Champion John Cena; Carmella beat Delilah Dawson; Dolph Ziggler rolled over Kalisto; Naomi and Becky Lynch defeated WWE Smackdown Women’s Champion Alexa Bliss and Mickie James; AJ Styles overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Dean Ambrose in a non-title match.

Feb 2017

Air Date: 7th February 2017
Baron Corbin overcame AJ Styles, WWE Intercontinental Champion Dean Ambrose and The Miz in a four-way; Apollo Crews overturned Dolph Ziggler; Aiden English, Jimmy Uso, Simon Gotch, Jey Uso, Viktor and Konnor rolled over Tyler Breeze, Heath Slater, Rhyno, Jason Jordan, Chad Gable and Fandango; WWE World Champion John Cena beat Randy Orton.

Air Date: 14th February 2017
WWE Smackdown Tag Champions Chad Gable and Jason Jordan overcame Konnor and Viktor; Mickie James overturned Becky Lynch; WWE World Champion Bray Wyatt defeated AJ Styles and John Cena in a three-way.

Air Date: 21st February 2017
Alexa Bliss overturned Becky Lynch to win the vacant WWE Smackdown Women’s Title; WWE Smackdown Tag Champions Jason Jordan and Chad Gable downed Tyler Breeze and Fandango; Natalya beat Nikki Bella in a “falls-count-anywhere” match; AJ Styles and Luke Harper co-won a battle royal.

Air Date: 28th February 2017
Becky Lynch defeated Mickie James in a “best-of-three-falls” match; AJ Styles overturned Luke Harper; Dolph Ziggler rolled over Apollo Crews in a “Chairs” match.

Mar 2017

Air Date: 7th March 2017
John Cena and Nikki Bella beat James Ellsworth and Carmella; Mickie James and WWE Smackdown Women’s Champion Alexa Bliss overturned Becky Lynch and Natalya; Randy Orton overcame AJ Styles.

Air Date: 14th March 2017
Becky Lynch defeated Natalya; Mickie James overturned Alexa Bliss; Mojo Rawley toppled Dolph Ziggler by count-out; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso bested WWE Smackdown Tag Champions Chad Gable and Jason Jordan in a non-title match.

Air Date: 21st March 2017
Jey Uso and Jimmy Uso bested WWE Smackdown Tag Champions Chad Gable and Jason Jordan to win the titles; Randy Orton overcame Baron Corbin; John Cena beat Fandango; Carmella beat Becky Lynch by DQ.

Air Date: 28th March 2017
Becky Lynch battled Carmella to a no-contest; Carmella and Alexa Bliss overturned Becky Lynch and Mickie James; Heath Slater, Rhyno, Mojo Rawley, Chad Gable and Jason Jordan overcame Tyler Breeze, Dolph Ziggler, Jey Uso, Jimmy Uso and Fandango; WWE World Champion Bray Wyatt defeated Luke Harper.

Apr 2017

Air Date: 4th April 2017
WWE Smackdown Women’s Champion Naomi defeated Alexa Bliss; Tye Dillinger rolled over Curt Hawkins; Baron Corbin overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion Dean Ambrose in a non-title streetfight; Luke Harper and WWE World Champion Randy Orton overcame Bray Wyatt and Erick Rowan.

Air Date: 11th April 2017
WWE World Champion Randy Orton overcame Erick Rowan by DQ; WWE Smackdown Tag Champions Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso defeated Chad Gable and Jason Jordan; Mojo Rawley toppled Jinder Mahal; Tye Dillinger rolled over Aiden English; AJ Styles overturned Baron Corbin and Sami Zayn in a three-way.

Air Date: 18th April 2017
Jinder Mahal defeated Dolph Ziggler, Erick Rowan, Luke Harper, Mojo Rawley and Sami Zayn in a six-way; Charlotte Flair rolled over WWE Smackdown Women’s Champion Naomi in a non-title match; Epico Colon and Primo Colon overcame Jason Jordan and Chad Gable; WWE US Champion Kevin Owens overturned Gary Gandy; AJ Styles overturned Baron Corbin by count-out.

Air Date: 25th April 2017
AJ Styles overturned Baron Corbin; Jason Jordan and Chad Gable downed Epico Colon and Primo Colon (Beat The Clock – 5:17); Randy Orton overcame Erick Rowan in a no-DQ match; Tyler Breeze and Fandango bested Konnor and Viktor (Beat The Clock – 2:40); Charlotte Flair battled WWE Smackdown Women’s Champion Naomi to a double-DQ.

May 2017

Air Date: 2nd May 2017
Jinder Mahal defeated Sami Zayn; Tye Dillinger rolled over Aiden English; Carmella and Natalya beat Charlotte Flair and WWE Smackdown Women’s Champion Naomi; Dolph Ziggler rolled over Sin Cara; Kevin Owens overturned WWE US Champion Chris Jericho to win the title.
