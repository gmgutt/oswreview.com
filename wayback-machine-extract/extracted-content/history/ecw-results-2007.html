ECW Results 2007

ECW RESULTS (2007)

Jan 2007

Air Date: 2nd January 2007
Test overturned Sabu; Elijah Burke and Sylvester Terkay overcame Little Guido and Tony Mamaluke; Kevin Thorn downed Balls Mahoney; ECW Champion Bobby Lashley battled Rob Van Dam to a no-contest.

Air Date: 9th January 2007
Hardcore Holly dropped CM Punk; The Great Khali pinned Tommy Dreamer in an “Extreme Rules” match; Kevin Thorn downed Shannon Moore; ECW Champion Bobby Lashley battled Rob Van Dam to a no-contest.

Air Date: 16th January 2007
CM Punk overturned Matt Striker; Marquis Cor Von defeated Cassidy Riley; Elijah Burke pinned The Sandman; ECW Champion Bobby Lashley toppled Rob Van Dam and Test in a three-way.

Air Date: 23rd January 2007
Rob Van Dam won a 5-man battle royal; Marcus Cor Von defeated Wes Adams; CM Punk overturned Elijah Burke; ECW Champion Bobby Lashley toppled Test in an “Extreme Rules” match.

Air Date: 30th January 2007
Matt Striker pinned CM Punk; Hardcore Holly dropped Rob Van Dam; ECW Champion Bobby Lashley toppled Test.

Feb 2007

Air Date: 6th February 2007
Kevin Thorn downed Tommy Dreamer; ECW Champion Bobby Lashley toppled Hardcore Holly; Marcus Cor Von defeated Balls Mahoney; Elijah Burke pinned Rob Van Dam.

Air Date: 13th February 2007
ECW Champion Bobby Lashley toppled Hardcore Holly in an “Extreme Rules” match; Marcus Cor Von and Kevin Thorn downed Tommy Dreamer and The Sandman; CM Punk overturned Mike Knox; Elijah Burke and Matt Striker defeated Rob Van Dam and Sabu.

Air Date: 20th February 2007
Rob Van Dam downed Kevin Thorn; CM Punk overturned Johnny Nitro (MITB Qualifier); Rene Dupree and Sylvan Grenier defeated Los Luchas; ECW Champion Bobby Lashley toppled Mr Kennedy and Hardcore Holly in a no-DQ three-way.

Air Date: 27th February 2007
Mr Kennedy dropped Sabu in an “Extreme Rules” match (MITB Qualifier); Balls Mahoney defeated Matt Striker by DQ; Marcus Cor Von and Kevin Thorn bested Rob Van Dam and Tommy Dreamer; CM Punk overturned Stevie Richards; ECW Champion Bobby Lashley toppled Hardcore Holly in a cage match.

Mar 2007

Air Date: 6th March 2007
Hardcore Holly dropped Balls Mahoney; Rob Van Dam pinned Elijah Burke.

Air Date: 13th March 2007
Tommy Dreamer won a 8-man battle royal; Snitsky downed Michael Deek and Danny Jaxx in a handicap match; ECW Champion Bobby Lashley toppled Randy Orton.

Air Date: 20th March 2007
Marcus Cor Von and Kevin Thorn downed Rob Van Dam and Sabu; Snitsky pinned Mike Tolar; CM Punk overturned Hardcore Holly; Elijah Burke dropped Tommy Dreamer; ECW Champion Bobby Lashley toppled Chris Masters.

Air Date: 27th March 2007
Rob Van Dam downed Elijah Burke; Snitsky pinned Balls Mahoney; King Booker, Randy Orton, Finlay, and Mr Kennedy dropped Edge, CM Punk, Matt Hardy and Jeff Hardy.

Apr 2007

Air Date: 3rd April 2007
CM Punk overturned Stevie Richards; Snitsky pinned Hardcore Holly; Elijah Burke, Matt Striker, Marcus Cor Von, and Kevin Thorn downed Rob Van Dam, Tommy Dreamer, Sabu, and The Sandman in an “Extreme Rules” match.

Air Date: 10th April 2007
Kevin Thorn downed Tommy Dreamer; CM Punk overturned Stevie Richards; Marcus Cor Von pinned Rob Van Dam.

Air Date: 17th April 2007
Snitsky pinned Nunzio; Tommy Dreamer and The Sandman downed Marcus Cor Von and Kevin Thorn; Rob Van Dam downed Elijah Burke.

Air Date: 24th April 2007
ECW Champion Bobby Lashley toppled Umaga by DQ; Snitsky pinned Balls Mahoney; Rob Van Dam, Sabu, Tommy Dreamer, and The Sandman downed Elijah Burke, Marcus Cor Von, Matt Striker, and Kevin Thorn in an elimination match.

May 2007

Air Date: 1st May 2007
CM Punk overturned Kevin Thorn; Brian and Brett Major defeated Matt Striker and Marcus Cor Von; Rob Van Dam downed The Sandman, Tommy Dreamer, and Sabu in a four-way “Extreme Rules” match.

Air Date: 8th May 2007
Marcus Cor Von bested CM Punk; Snitksy toppled The Sandman; Elijah Burke downed Brian Major; ECW Champion Vince McMahon, Shane McMahon, and Umaga defeated Rob Van Dam in a handicap match.

Air Date: 15th May 2007
Rob Van Dam downed Snitsky by DQ; CM Punk overturned Stevie Richards; Kevin Thorn downed Nunzio; Bobby Lashley toppled Elijah Burke, Matt Striker, and Marcus Cor Von in a handicap match.

Air Date: 22nd May 2007
Snitsky beat Tommy Dreamer; Kevin Thorn downed Wyatt Lara; Matt Striker pinned Brett Major; CM Punk and Rob Van Dam downed Elijah Burke and Marcus Cor Von by DQ.

Air Date: 29th May 2007
Elijah Burke pinned CM Punk in an “Extreme Rules” match; Kevin Thorn downed Balls Mahoney; Randy Orton bested Tommy Dreamer.

Jun 2007

Air Date: 5th June 2007
ECW Champion Bobby Lashley toppled Tommy Dreamer, The Sandman, and Balls Mahoney in a handicap “Extreme Rules” match; Kevin Thorn downed Stevie Richards; Elijah Burke and Marcus Cor Von defeated Brett and Brian Major; CM Punk overturned Matt Striker.

Air Date: 12th June 2007
Tommy Dreamer pinned Balls Mahoney in an “Extreme Rules” match; The Boogeyman defeated Matt Striker; Chris Benoit and CM Punk overturned Elijah Burke and Marcus Cor Von by DQ.

Air Date: 19th June 2007
Chris Benoit dropped Elijah Burke (ECW Title Tournament – Semi-Final); The Boogeyman pinned Sean Alexander; Kevin Thorn downed Tommy Dreamer; Johnny Nitro beat Nunzio; CM Punk overturned Marcus Cor Von (ECW Title Tournament – Semi-Final).

Air Date: 26th June 2007
WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over ECW Champion Johnny Nitro in a non-title match; CM Punk overturned Elijah Burke in a “best of three falls” match;

Jul 2007

Air Date: 3rd July 2007
ECW Champion Johnny Nitro beat Tommy Dreamer; CM Punk overturned Kevin Thorn.

Air Date: 10th July 2007
Elijah Burke pinned Balls Mahoney; CM Punk overturned Stevie Richards; The Miz downed Nunzio; ECW Champion Johnny Nitro dropped Tommy Dreamer in an “extreme rules” match.

Air Date: 17th July 2007
CM Punk overturned Elijah Burke; Big Daddy V pinned Ted Storm; Kevin Thorn downed Tommy Dreamer.

Air Date: 24th July 2007
ECW Champion John Morrison defeated Zokre; Stevie Richards pinned Kevin Thorn; The Miz downed Nunzio; Big Daddy V overcame Jimmy Cruz and Victor Calvio in a handicap match; CM Punk and Tommy Dreamer bested ECW Champion John Morrison and Elijah Burke.

Air Date: 31st July 2007
ECW Champion John Morrison pinned Joey Blalock; Stevie Richards beat Kevin Thorn; Big Daddy V downed Jeff Michaels, Brandon Gatson, and John Mason in a handicap match; The Miz overturned Balls Mahoney; CM Punk rolled over Tommy Dreamer and Elijah Burke in a three-way.

Aug 2007

Air Date: 7th August 2007
The Boogeyman pinned Jesse Guilmette; Elijah Burke downed Tommy Dreamer; The Miz dropped Balls Mahoney; CM Punk overturned ECW Champion John Morrison in a non-title match.

Air Date: 14th August 2007
Big Daddy V defeated CM Punk by count-out; Big Daddy V pinned Tommy Dreamer; Stevie Richards downed Kevin Thorn by reverse decision-DQ; The Boogeyman downed ECW Champion John Morrison by DQ.

Air Date: 21st August 2007
Big Daddy V beat Tommy Dreamer in an “Extreme Rules” match; Balls Mahoney downed Elijah Burke; ECW Champion John Morrison and The Miz overcame CM Punk and The Boogeyman.

Air Date: 28th August 2007
Kevin Thorn downed Stevie Richards; Elijah Burke pinned Balls Mahoney; CM Punk overturned Big Daddy V, The Miz, and The Boogeyman in a four-way.

Sep 2007

Air Date: 4th September 2007
The Boogeyman beat Matt Striker by DQ; The Miz downed Balls Mahoney; Kevin Thorn and Elijah Burke dropped Tommy Dreamer and Stevie Richards; CM Punk overturned ECW Champion John Morrison to win the title.

Air Date: 11th September 2007
The Miz overcame Tommy Dreamer; Matt Striker downed Nunzio; Mike Knox pinned Balls Mahoney; ECW Champion CM Punk and Stevie Richards dropped Elijah Burke and Kevin Thorn.

Air Date: 18th September 2007
Elijah Burke defeated Tommy Dreamer, Kevin Thorn, and Stevie Richards in a four-way; Balls Mahoney pinned Mike Knox; Big Daddy V downed The Boogeyman.

Air Date: 25th September 2007
Elijah Burke beat Kevin Thorn and Tommy Dreamer in a three-way; Mike Knox downed Nunzio; The Miz dropped Silas Young; ECW Champion CM Punk overturned Matt Striker.

Oct 2007

Air Date: 2nd October 2007
ECW Champion CM Punk overturned Mike Knox; Tommy Dreamer pinned Elijah Burke; Big Daddy V downed Tommy Dreamer

Air Date: 9th October 2007
Big Daddy V dropped Tommy Dreamer; Kevin Thorn downed James Curtis; The Miz defeated Balls Mahoney by count-out; John Morrison bested ECW Champion CM Punk in a non-title match.

Air Date: 16th October 2007
ECW Champion CM Punk and Kane bested Big Daddy V, John Morrison, and The Miz in a handicap match; Jesse and Festus overturned Elijah Burke and Nunzio; John Morrison battled The Miz to a no-contest.

Air Date: 23rd October 2007
Kane battled Big Daddy V to a no-contest; Elijah Burke pinned Nunzio; ECW Champion CM Punk and Balls Mahoney defeated John Morrison and The Miz.

Air Date: 30th October 2007
Elijah Burke pinned Jimmy Yang Wang; Tommy Dreamer bested Nunzio; John Morrison toppled The Miz; ECW Champion CM Punk overturned James Curtis; Mark Henry won a battle royal.

Nov 2007

Air Date: 6th November 2007
Kane bested Mark Henry by DQ; Jamie Noble toppled Nunzio; Layla pinned Kelly Kelly; Elijah Burke toppled Shannon Moore; ECW Champion CM Punk overturned John Morrison.

Air Date: 13th November 2007
Mr Kennedy and and WWE US Champion MVP bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy and Matt Hardy; Kelly Kelly and Michelle McCool beat Melina and Layla; ECW Champion CM Punk overturned Jamie Noble.

Air Date: 20th November 2007
ECW Champion CM Punk overturned Kenny Dykstra; Shelton Benjamin bested Tommy Dreamer; Jesse and Festus overturned Deuce and Domino; Kevin Thorn downed Nunzio; Kane bested Big Daddy V and Matt Striker in a handicap match.

Air Date: 27th November 2007
Kelly Kelly downed Layla; WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz defeated Jesse and Festus; Shelton Benjamin bested Shannon Moore; Big Daddy V overcame Kane in an “Extreme Rules” match.

Dec 2007

Air Date: 6th December 2007
WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz defeated Brett and Brian Major; Balls Mahoney and Kelly Kelly downed Kenny Dykstra and Victoria; Shelton Benjamin bested Jimmy Wang Yang; Kevin Thorn downed Jeff Lewis; ECW Champion CM Punk overturned Mark Henry by DQ.

Air Date: 11th December 2007
ECW Champion CM Punk and Kane bested Deuce and Domino; WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz defeated Jimmy Wang Yang and Shannon Moore; Layla and Victoria beat Kelly Kelly in a handicap match; World Heavyweight Champion Batista rolled over Elijah Burke.

Air Date: 18th December 2007
Jimmy Wang Yang and Shannon Moore toppled WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz in a non-title match; Shelton Benjamin bested Colin Delaney; Kenny Dykstra pinned Nunzio; ECW Champion CM Punk overturned WWE US Champion MVP by DQ.
