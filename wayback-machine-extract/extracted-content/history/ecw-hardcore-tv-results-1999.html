ECW Hardcore TV Results 1999

ECW HARDCORE TV RESULTS (1999)

Jan 1999

Air Date: 8th January 1999
Show was a preview of the Guilty as Charged PPV which would occur on the 1/10.

Air Date: 15th January 1999
Show was a review of the Guilty as Charged PPV which occurred on the 1/10.

Air Date: 22nd January 1999
Sid pinned Skull von Crush; Super Crazy bested Yoshihiro Tajiri; ECW World Champion Taz battled Chris Candido to a no-contest; ECW World Champion Taz toppled Shane Douglas.

Air Date: 29th January 1999
Also included highlights of the Taz vs. Shane Douglas match from 1/22.
ECW World Tag Champions Rob Van Dam and Sabu bested Axl Rotten and Balls Mahoney. 

Feb 1999

Air Date: 5th February 1999
Lance Storm beat Spike Dudley; Yoshihiro Tajiri toppled Super Crazy; ECW World Champion Taz rolled over El Diablo.

Air Date: 12th February 1999
ECW TV Champion Rob Van Dam beat Ulf Herman; Super Crazy bested Yoshihiro Tajiri; Antifaz del Norte overturned Little Guido; Spike Dudley beat Big Dick Dudley

Air Date: 19th February 1999
Chris Chetti beat Little Guido.

Air Date: 26th February 1999
Super Crazy bested Yoshihiro Tajiri; Sabu battled Skull Von Crush to a no-contest. 

Mar 1999

Air Date: 5th March 1999
Featured no new matches. Featured interview segments and the Sabu vs. Terry Funk barbed wire match from 1997.

Air Date: 12th March 1999
Axl Rotten and Balls Mahoney overcame Danny Doring and Roadkill, and Tracy Smothers and Little Guido in a three-way elimination match; Lance Storm pinned Shane Douglas; Sabu toppled Jerry Lynn.

Air Date: 19th March 1999
Spike Dudley battled Mustafa Saed to a no-contest; ECW World Tag Champions Sabu and Rob Van Dam bested The Dudley Boys, and Shane Douglas and Tommy Dreamer in a three-way elimination match.

Air Date: 26th March 1999
Yoshihiro Tajiri beat Little Guido; Super Crazy toppled Antifaz del Norte.

Apr 1999

Air Date: 2nd April 1999
Chris Chetti and Nova defeated Danny Doring and Roadkill; Jerry Lynn battled Tracy Smothers to a no-contest; Lance Storm dropped Tommy Dreamer.

Air Date: 9th April 1999
ECW TV Champion Rob Van Dam bested Too Cold Scorpio; ECW World Champion Taz toppled Chris Candido.

Air Date: 16th April 1999
Jerry Lynn beat Yoshihiro Tajiri; Super Crazy downed Mosco de la Merced; Shane Douglas rolled over Justin Credible. 

Air Date: 23rd April 1999
Axl Rotten and Balls Mahoney beat Little Guido and Sal E Graziano; Taka Michinoku overcame Papi Chulo; D-Von Dudley toppled Rob Van Dam to win the ECW World Tag Title.

Air Date: 30th April 1999
Nova beat Little Guido; Jerry Lynn bested Taka Michinoku; Tommy Dreamer toppled Lance Storm.

May 1999

Air Date: 7th May 1999
ECW TV Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Buh Buh Ray Dudley; Jerry Lynn dropped Justin Credible.

Air Date: 14th May 1999
Little Guido beat El Mosco; Taka Michinoku and Yoshihiro Tajiri bested Super Crazy and Nova; Chris Candido battled Chris Chetti to a no-contest; ECW World Tag Champions Buh The Dudley Boys overturned Balls Mahoney and Axl Rotten

Air Date: 21st May 1999
Balls Mahoney beat New Jack; ECW TV Champion Rob Van Dam pinned Skull Von Krush.

Air Date: 28th May 1999
ECW TV Champion Rob Van Dam defeated Spike Dudley; Lance Storm dropped Chris Chetti; ECW World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys toppled Balls Mahoney and Axl Rotten in a falls-count-anywhere match.

June 1999

Air Date: 4th June 1999
Little Guido defeated Super Nova; Super Crazy beat Taka Michinoku; ECW World Champion Taz rolled over Chris Candido. 

Air Date: 11th June 1999
Taka Michinoku beat Mosco de la Merced; Jerry Lynn defeated Tony DeVito.

Air Date: 18th June 1999
Jerry Lynn beat Kid Kash; Yoshihiro Tajiri bested Little Guido and Super Crazy in a three-way elimination match; ECW World Champion Taz toppled Spike Dudley in a falls-count-anywhere match; ECW TV Champion Rob Van Dam downed D-Von Dudley. 

Air Date: 25th June 1999
Little Guido beat Yoshihiro Tajiri; Jerry Lynn dropped Justin Credible; ECW World Champion Taz toppled Rhino.

July 1999

Air Date: 2nd July 1999
ECW World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys bested Balls Mahoney and Spike Dudley; ECW TV Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Lance Storm.

Air Date: 9th July 1999
ECW TV Champion Rob Van Dam bested Balls Mahoney; Justin Credible and Lance Storm rolled over Sabu and Jerry Lynn.

Air Date: 16th July 1999
Yoshihiro Tajiri overcame Super Crazy and Little Guido in a three-way elimination match; Jerry Lynn beat Lance Storm.

Air Date: 23rd July 1999
Show was a highlight show of ECW Heatwave 1999

Air Date: 30th July 1999
Included the Storm vs. Dreamer match from ECW Hardcore Heaven 1999
ECW World Tag Champions Spike Dudley and Balls Mahoney dropped The Dudley Boys in a falls-count-anywhere match.

Aug 1999

Air Date: 6th August 1999
Yoshihiro Tajiri downed Little Guido; ECW World Tag Champions Balls Mahoney and Spike Dudley overturned New Jack and Axl Rotten.

Air Date: 13th August 1999
Included the Jazz vs. Jason match from ECW Heat Wave 1999
Chris Chetti and Nova toppled Simon Diamond and Super Crazy.

Air Date: 20th August 1999
The Dudley Boys toppled ECW World Tag Champions Spike Dudley and Balls Mahoney to win the titles; Rhino pinned Prodigy; Jazz beat Steve Corino.

Air Date: 27th August 1999
Spike Dudley and Balls Mahoney rolled over ECW World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys to win the titles; Yoshihiro Tajiri downed Super Crazy.

Sep 1999

Air Date: 3rd September 1999
Axl Rotten overcame Balls Mahoney and New Jack in a three-way elimination match; Uganda toppled Prodigy; Super Crazy toppled Yoshihiro Tajiri.

Air Date: 10th September 1999
Also included Tanaka vs. Awesome match from Heatwave 1998
Miss Congeniality, Danny Doring and Roadkill overturned Chris Chetti, Nova and Jazz; ECW World Tag Champions Raven and Tommy Dreamer bested Rhino and Steve Corino.

Air Date: 17th September 1999
Featured the Storm vs. Van Dam, and Lynn vs. Van Dam matches from the ECW on TNN broadcast on 9/17.

Air Date: 24th September 1999
Show was a review of ECW Anarchy Rulz 1999.

Oct 1999

Air Date: 1st October 1999
Featured the Rhino vs. Daniels and Dudley vs. Rhino matches from ECW Hostile City Showdown.
Danny Doring and Roadkill beat Nova and Jazz; Jerry Lynn pinned Super Crazy.

Air Date: 8th October 1999
Featured the Van Dam vs. Balls Mahoney match from ECW Anarchy Rulz 1999.
Featured the Awesome vs. Rhino match from the 10/1 broadcast of ECW on TNN

Air Date: 15th October 1999
Jazz beat Jason Knight; Yoshihiro Tajiri overcame Little Guido and Simon Diamond in a three-way elimination match; ECW TV Champion Rob Van Dam bested Spike Dudley. 

Air Date: 22nd October 1999
Axl Rotten and Balls Mahoney downed Bill Wiles and CW Anderson; Justin Credible and Lance Storm dropped Danny Doring and Roadkill.

Air Date: 29th October 1999
ECW World Champion Mike Awesome toppled Mikey Whipwreck;  Yoshihiro Tajiri defeated Jerry Lynn; ECW World Tag Champions Raven and Tommy Dreamer battled Justin Credible and Lance Storm to a no-contest.

Nov 1999

Air Date: 5th November 1999
ECW TV Champion Rob Van Dam battled Sabu to a draw. 

Air Date: 12th November 1999
ECW TV Champion Rob Van Dam defeated Little Guido; ECW World Champion Mike Awesome dropped Taz; Danny Doring and Roadkill overturned Bill Wiles and CW Anderson, and Axl Rotten and Balls Mahoney in a three-way elimination match. 

Air Date: 19th November 1999
Featured the Sabu/Rob Van Dam match from Wrestlepalooza 1998.

Air Date: 26th November 1999
Danny Doring and Roadkill beat Bo Dupp and Jack Dupp; Chris Candido toppled Simon Diamond; Angel and Tony DeVito overcame Axl Rotten and Balls Mahoney.

Dec 1999

Air Date: 3rd December 1999
Kid Kash and Nova downed Bo and Jack Dupp; ECW TV Champion Rob Van Dam toppled Uganda

Air Date: 10th December 1999
Super Crazy beat Super Calo; ECW TV Champion Rob Van Dam toppled Tracy Smothers.

Air Date: 17th December 1999
Simon Diamond beat Jazz; Mikey Whipwreck overcame CW Anderson; ECW World Champion Mike Awesome rolled over Masato Tanaka.

Air Date: 24th December 1999
Balls Mahoney beat Steve Corino; ECW TV Champion Rob Van Dam toppled Mikey Whipwreck. 

Air Date: 31st December 1999
Also included the Awesome/Tanaka match from the 12/31 edition of ECW on TNN.
Yukihiro Kanemura beat CW Anderson; Little Guido downed Super Calo.
