OSW Update for December!


Yo! Hope you had an amazing Christmas! Or at least ate too much and slept a lot, which is what everyone can hope for. Here’s the scoop on OSW:
• Myself and the boys recorded the 2nd (well technically 3rd) Tattoo review, IWA King of the DeathMatch ’95, last weekend! I used new equipment to record it but I’m running into trouble with syncing issues, so I’m having to sync it manually which is horrific! It should be out next weekend (~2nd Jan).
• Afterwards we’ll do our annual Golden Noggers & Q&A session celebrating 2015 in OSW! And after that we’ll start a new storyline. Since there’s a few vids before that, we haven’t decided on what it’ll be. We’ll be choosing an arc from this thread: OSWreview.com/what-should-we-cover-next!
• The Dirty Lee sign is Big Sammy (with Ben in the what barrrr shart beside him!). So awesome seeing so much love/signage at the event. Sadly I don’t have the time to get an NXT video review up but it was a great show.
• I have another radio slot for a Super OSW 64! Level 7 is happening next week! I’ve no specific air time but the podcast version should be available on Wednesday. I’m currently finalising the track list, you can request a track here: OSWreview.com/super-osw-64-level-7-requests
• Got to watch a couple films over the Xmas break, written up a few new reviews! Latest one is Inside Out!

So what’s in the future for OSW:
VIDEO:
IWA King of the Deathmatch (Saturday)
Q&A
Golden Noggers year end awards (Voting will start soon after IWA KOTDM is released)
Part 1 of next storyline

AUDIO:
Super OSW 64 Level 7 (Wednesday)
Jay interview talking Death in Videogames
Legend of Zelda concert interview + music
Super Rick & Morty music show

So from your boys at OSW, hope you had a Merry Christmas, a Happy Hanukkah, Kwazy Kwanzaa, a tip-top Tet, and a solemn & dignified Ramadan
