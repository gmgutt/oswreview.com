WWE FASTLANE RESULTS

22nd February 2015: “WWE Fast Lane 2015”: Memphis, TN – 10,300
Shown on PPV/WWE Network
Seth Rollins, Kane and The Big Show beat Ryback, Dolph Ziggler and Erick Rowan; Goldust pinned Stardust; Cesaro and Tyson Kidd downed WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso to win the titles; WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella defeated Paige; WWE Intercontinental Champion Bad News Barrett overcame Dean Ambrose by DQ; WWE US Champion Rusev bested John Cena by stoppage; Roman Reigns rolled over Daniel Bryan.

21st February 2016: “WWE Fast Lane 2016”: Cleveland, OH – 14,406
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: WWE US Champion Kaliston defeated Alberto Del Rio in a “best-of-three-falls” match.
PPV Broadcast: Becky Lynch and Sasha Banks overcame Tamina and Naomi; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens bested Dolph Ziggler; The Big Show, Kane and Ryback overturned Luke Harper, Erick Rowan and Braun Strowman; WWE Divas Champion Charlotte downed Brie Bella; AJ Styles toppled Chris Jericho; Curtis Axel pinned R-Truth; Roman Reigns won a three-way over Brock Lesnar and Dean Ambrose.

5th March 2017: “WWE Fastlane 2017”: Milwaukee, WI – 11,000
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Akira Tozawa and Rich Swann overcame Brian Kendrick and Noam Dar.
PPV Broadcast: Samoa Joe downed Sami Zayn; WWE Raw Tag Champions Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows defeated Big Cass and Enzo Amore; Sasha Banks overturned Nia Jax; Cesaro bested Jinder Mahal; The Big Show defeated Rusev; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Neville defeated Jack Gallagher; Roman Reigns overturned Braun Strowman; WWE Raw Women’s Champion Bayley defeated Charlotte Flair; Goldberg defeated WWE Universal Champion Kevin Owens to win the title.
