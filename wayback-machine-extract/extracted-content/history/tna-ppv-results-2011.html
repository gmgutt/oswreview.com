TNA PPV Results 2011

TNA PPV RESULTS 2011

9th January 2011: “TNA Genesis 2011”: Orlando, FL – 1,100 (0 paid)
Kazarian dropped TNA X-Division Champion Jay Lethal to win the title; TNA Knockout’s Champion Madison Rayne overcame Mickie James; Beer Money Inc downed TNA World Tag Champions The Motorcity Machine Guns to win the title; Brother Ray bested Brother Devon by DQ; Abyss defeated TNA TV Champion Douglas Williams to win the title; Matt Hardy toppled Rob Van Dam; Kurt Angle overcame Jeff Jarrett by DQ in a “MMA Exhibition” match; Mr Anderson pinned Matt Morgan; Mr Anderson rolled over TNA World Champion Jeff Hardy to win the title.

 

13th February 2011: “TNA Against All Odds 2011”: Orlando, FL – 1,100 (0 paid)
TNA X-Division Champion Kazarian beat Robbie E; Scott Steiner and TNA World Tag Champions Beer Money Inc dropped Rob Terry, Gunner and Murphy; Samoa Joe bested The Pope; TNA Knockouts Champion Madison Rayne overturned Mickie James in a Last Man Standing encounter; Rob Van Dam pinned Matt Hardy; Bully Ray defeated Brother Devon in a streetfight; Jeff Jarrett toppled Kurt Angle; Jeff Hardy rolled over TNA World Champion Mr Anderson in a Ladder Match to win the title.

 

13th March 2011: “TNA Victory Road 2011”: Orlando, FL – 1,100 (0 paid)
Tommy Dreamer beat Bully Ray in a falls-count-anywhere encounter; Sarita and Rosita overcame TNA Knockouts Tag Champions Angelina Love and Winter to win the titles; Hernandez bested Matt Morgan in a First Blood match; TNA X-Division Champion Kazarian won an Ultimate X match over Robbie E and Generation Me; TNA World Tag Champions Beer Money Inc toppled Shannon Moore and Jesse Neal; AJ Styles pinned Matt Hardy; Mr Anderson battled Rob Van Dam to a no-contest; TNA World Champion Sting rolled over Jeff Hardy.

 

17th April 2011: “TNA Lockdown 2011”: Cincinnati, OH – 4,000 (3,500 paid)
All matches were held inside a steel cage
Dark match: Brother Devon downed Anarquia.
PPV Broadcast: Max Buck overcame Jay Lethal, Jeremy Buck, Amazing Red, Chris Sabin, Robbie E, Brian Kendrick and Suicide in an Eight Way elimination match; Shannon Moore and Jesse Neal overcame Doug Williams and Brutus Magnus, Orlando Jordan and Eric Young, and Scott Steiner and Crimson in a Four Way; Mickie James defeated TNA Knockouts Champion Madison Rayne in a “Title vs. Hair” encounter; Samoa Joe bested The Pope; Matt Morgan pinned Hernandez; Jeff Jarrett overturned Kurt Angle in a best-of-three-falls match; TNA World Champion Sting toppled Mr Anderson and Rob Van Dam in a Three Way; TNA World Tag Champions Beer Money Inc , TNA X-Division Champion Kazarian and Christopher Daniels rolled over Ric Flair, Abyss, Matt Hardy and Bull Ray in a Lethal Lockdown match.

 

15th May 2011: “TNA Sacrifice 2011”: Orlando, FL – 1,100 (0 paid)
Hernandez and Anarquia beat Jesse Neal and Shannon Moore; Brian Kendrick beat Robbie E; TNA Knockouts Champion Mickie James defeated Madison Rayne; TNA X-Division Champion Kazarian downed Max Buck; Crimson dropped Abyss; TNA World Tag Champions Beer Money Inc bested Chris Harris and Matt Hardy; Tommy Dreamer toppled AJ Styles in a no-DQ match; Kurt Angle and Chyna overturned Jeff Jarrett and Karen Jarrett; TNA World Champion Sting rolled over Rob Van Dam.

 

12th June 2011: “TNA Slammiversary IX”: Orlando, FL – 1,100 (0 paid)
TNA World Tag Champions Alex Shelley (a permitted substitute for Robert Roode) and James Storm overcame Brutus Magnus and Douglas Williams; Matt Morgan overturned Scott Steiner; TNA X-Division Champion Abyss dropped Kazarian and Brian Kendrick in a Three Way; Crimson downed Samoa Joe; TNA Knockouts Champion Mickie James toppled Angelina Love; Bully Ray defeated AJ Styles in a Last Man Standing Match; Mr Anderson pinned TNA World Champion Sting to win the title; Kurt Angle rolled over Jeff Jarrett . 

 

10th July 2011: “TNA Destination X 2011”: Orlando, FL – 1,100 (0 paid)
Kazarian beat Samoa Joe; Doug Williams downed Mark Haskins; TNA TV champion Eric Young and Shark Boy bested Generation Me; Alex Shelley dropped Robbie E, Amazing Red and Shannon Moore in a four-way Ultimate X encounter; Rob Van Dam defeated Jerry Lynn; Austin Aries toppled Low-Ki, Zema Ion, and Jack Evans in a four-way to earn a TNA contract; Brian Kendrick overcame TNA X-Division champion Abyss to win the title; AJ Styles rolled over Christopher Daniels.

 

7th August 2011: “TNA Hardcore Justice 2011”: Orlando, FL – 1,100 (0 paid)
TNA X-Division Champion Brian Kendrick overcame Alex Shelley and Austin Aries in a three-way; TNA Knockouts Tag Champions Tara and Miss Tessmacher beat Rosita and Sarita; The Pope toppled Devon (BFG Series); Winter toppled TNA Knockouts Champion Mickie James to win the title; Crimson dropped Rob Van Dam by DQ (BFG Series); AJ Styles, Christopher Daniels and Kazarian overturned Scott Steiner, Abyss and Gunner; Bully Ray downed Mr Anderson; TNA World Tag Champions Beer Money Inc bested Hernandez and Anarquia; Kurt Angle rolled over TNA World Champion Sting to win the title. 

11th September 2011: “TNA No Surrender 2011”: Orlando, FL – 1,100 (0 paid
Jesse Sorensen beat Kid Kash; Bully Ray overcame James Storm by DQ (BFG Series – Semi-Final); Winter downed TNA Knockouts Champion Mickie James to capture the title; TNA World Tag Champions Hernandez and Anarquia defeated The Pope and Devon; Matt Morgan dropped Samoa Joe; Bobby Roode overcame Gunner (BFG Series – Semi-Final); Austin Aries toppled TNA X-Division Champion Brian Kendrick to capture the title; Bobby Roode overturned Bully Ray (BFG Series – Final); TNA World Champion Kurt Angle rolled over Sting and Mr Anderson in a three-way.

16th October 2011: “TNA Bound For Glory 2011”: Philadelphia, PA – 4,000
TNA X-Division Champion Austin Aries downed Brian Kendrick; Rob Van Dam dropped Jerry Lynn in a Full Metal Mayhem match; Crimson defeated Matt Morgan and Samoa Joe in a three-way; Mr Anderson overcame Bully Ray in a streetfight; Velvet Sky overturned TNA Knockouts Champion Winter, Madison Rayne and Mickie James in a four-way to capture the title. Karen Jarrett was guest referee; AJ Styles bested Christopher Daniels in an I Quit encounter; Sting toppled Hulk Hogan in a match where control of TNA was on the line; TNA World Champion Kurt Angle rolled over Bobby Roode.

13th November 2011: “TNA Turning Point 2011”: Orlando, FL – 1,100 (0 paid)
Robbie E beat TNA TV Champion Eric Young to capture the title;  TNA World Tag Champions Hernandez and Anarquia, and Sarita overturned Shannon Moore, Jesse Neal and Toxxin; TNA X-Division Champion Austin Aries bested Kid Kash and Jesse Sorensen in a three-way; Rob Van Dam pinned Christopher Daniels in a no-DQ match;  Matt Morgan fought Crimson to a no-contest; Abyss and Mr Anderson downed Bully Ray and Scott Steiner;  Gail Kim dropped TNA Knockouts Champion Velvet Sky to capture the title;  Jeff Hardy toppled Jeff Jarrett; TNA World Champion Bobby Roode rolled over AJ Styles.

11th December 2011: “TNA Final Resolution 2011”: Orlando, FL – 1,100 (0 paid)
Rob Van Dam bested Christopher Daniels; TNA TV Champion Robbie E toppled Eric Young; TNA World Tag Champions Crimson and Matt Morgan overcame The Pope and Devon; TNA X-Division Champion Austin Aries dropped Kid Kash; TNA Knockouts Champion Gail Kim overcame Mickie James; James Storm pinned Kurt Angle; Jeff Hardy downed Jeff Jarrett in a cage match; TNA World Champion Bobby Roode battled AJ Styles to a 30-minute draw in an Iron Man match (3:3).
