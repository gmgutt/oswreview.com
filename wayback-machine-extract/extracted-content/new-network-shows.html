New WWE Network shows!

Any of these NEW WWE Network Shows sound decent?

CAMP WWE (Late 2015)
Seth Green’s new short animated comedy series. WWE wrestlers as kids at Summer Camp run by Mr. McMahon. Touted as “WWE’s first adult comedy”. We’ll see.

SWERVED (May)
A WWE version of Jackass/Bad Grandpa.

JERRY SPRINGER PRESENTS WWE TOO HOT FOR TV (April)
Here’s the blurb: Hosted by legendary talk show host Jerry Springer, this series will re-introduce the WWE Universe to the most outrageous stunts and cringe-inducing moments in TV history. The series will be chock full of insane situations and racy romps from WWE’s past that will have members of the WWE Universe asking, “Did they really do this?!” Springer will inject his own brand of humour into each episode with themes such as “Love Hurts,” “All in the Family” and “Weddings and Funerals.” This sounds like WWE Countdown but with the only talking head being Jerry Springer.

DIVA SEARCH (This Fall)
New season of Diva Search.

LIVE! WITH CHRIS JERICHO – DEBUTS MONDAY, APRIL 6 w/JOHN CENA
Much like Austin’s TV podcast, Jericho’s got one now, one per month.

UNFILTERED: WITH RENEE YOUNG (May)
Interviews with wrestlers & celebrities about everything except what they are famous for.

CULTURE SHOCK: WITH COREY GRAVES (June)
Blurb: WWE commentator Corey Graves reveals a variety of unique venues, customs, music, food and people as WWE’s tour travels around the world. From Marvel comic headquarters to a tattoo shop on the lower east side of Manhattan, Graves examines the local culture with his unique perspective and style. Short-form (i.e. only a few minutes long)

WWE THE LIST – COMING IN JUNE
WWE Countdown but we can’t call it countdown.

NEW EPISODES:
More episodes of WWE 24, Countdown & Rivalries, as well as airing Hulk Hogan’s Rock N Wrestling.

One off SPECIAL:
FLOYD MAYWEATHER VS. THE BIG SHOW: THE BIGGEST VS. THE BEST (APRIL 27)
To capitalise on the Mayweather fight.

NXT PPVS
Although the press release said monthly, WWE later clarified it’ll be keeping the same schedule (i.e. quarterly)

AN EVENING WITH MICK FOLEY
A taped “comedy” show by Mick with some heavy editing I guarantee! They weren’t filming in Dublin sadly

Note re: New season of Tough Enough
Said to be more like American Idol (with live voting), it’s on TV (USA Network). They did recently put Season 2 of Tough Enough (with OOC’s boy Matt Morgan) on the WWE Network. I did review S1 of TE, you can check it out here!
