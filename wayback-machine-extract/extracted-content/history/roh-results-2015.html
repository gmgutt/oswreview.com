14th November 2015:“ROH Survival of the Fittest 2015 – Night 2”: Hopkins, MN – 850
Shaheem Ali dropped Kevin Lee Davidson; Cedric Alexander rolled over Will Ferrara; Dalton Castle downed Adam Page; Mark Briscoe downed Frankie Kazarian; Adam Cole, and ROH Tag Champions Michael Bennett and Matt Taven toppled Moose, Raymond Rowe and Hanson; Joey Daddiego bested Cheeseburger; The Young Bucks overcame Kenny King and Rhett Titus; AJ Styles overcame Matt Sydal; IWGP Jr Tag Champions Bobby Fish and Kyle O’Reilly overturned ROH Champion Jay Lethal and Donovan Dijak; Michael Elgin won “Survival of the Fittest” over Jay Briscoe, ACH, Silas Young and Christopher Daniels (Survival of the Fittest 2015 – Final).
