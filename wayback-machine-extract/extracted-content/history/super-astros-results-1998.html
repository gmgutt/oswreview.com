Super Astros Results 1998

SUPER ASTROS RESULTS (1998)

Nov 1998

Air Date: 22nd November 1998
Armando Fernandez beat Scott Taylor; El Merenguero defeated Christopher Martinez; El Hijo Del Santo dropped Super Loco.

Air Date: 29th November 1998
Papi Chulo pinned Manuel Gomez; Miguel Perez beat Super Loco; Negro Casas beat Sho Funaki.

Dec 1998

Air Date: 6th December 1998
Miguel Perez downed Rey Ortiz; Apollo Dantes defeated El Merenguero; Negro Casas dropped Dick Togo.

Air Date: 13th December 1998
El Hijo Del Santo beat Jose Estrada Jr; El Torito pinned Mini Nova; Apollo Dantes battled Negro Casas to a double-DQ.

Air Date: 20th December 1998
El Hiji Del Santo and Negro Casas downed Jose Estrada Jr and Apollo Dantes; Armando Fernandez beat Julio Sanchez; Max Mini pinned El Torito.

Air Date: 27th December 1998
Armando Fernandez defeated Sho Funaki; Papi Chulo downed Buddy Wayne; El Merenguero dropped Apollo Dantes by DQ.
