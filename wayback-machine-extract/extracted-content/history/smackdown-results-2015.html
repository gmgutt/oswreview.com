Smackdown Results 2015

WWE SMACKDOWN RESULTS (2015)

Jan 2015

Air Date: 2nd January 2015
Bray Wyatt rolled over Erick Rowan; WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso, and R-Truth beat Goldust, Stardust and Adam Rose; The Big Show beat Ryback by count-out; Tyson Kidd and Cesaro bested Los Matadores; The Ascension overcame Lance Anoai and Rhett Titus; Roman Reigns rolled over WWE US Champion Rusev by DQ.

Air Date: 9th January 2015
Big E beat Adam Rose; The Ascension won a squash; Alicia Fox beat Naomi; WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso won a three-way over Goldust and Stardust, and The Miz and Damien Mizdow; Sin Cara defeated WWE Intercontinental Champion Bad News Barrett in a non-title match; Roman Reigns and Dean Ambrose downed Seth Rollins and The Big Show.

Air Date: 15th January 2015
Daniel Bryan overcame Kane by DQ; The Miz, Damien Mizdow and Alicia Fox beat WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso, and Naomi; Natalya defeated WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella in a non-title match; WWE Intercontinental Champion Bad News Barrett rolled over Sin Cara; Daniel Bryan, Roman Reigns and Dean Ambrose downed The Big Show, Kane and Seth Rollins.

Air Date: 22nd January 2015
Dolph Ziggler toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Bad News Barrett in a non-title match; Ryback overturned WWE US Champion Rusev by count-out; Brie Bella defeated Naomi; Luke Harper toppled Erick Rowan; Daniel Bryan overcame Kane in a no-DQ match.

Air Date: 29th January 2015
Roman Reigns bested The Big Show; Tyson Kidd toppled Jey Uso; The Ascension overcame Goldust and Stardust; Daniel Bryan rolled over Kane in a casket match.

Feb 2015

Air Date: 5th February 2015
Ryback and Dolph Ziggler toppled Goldust and Stardust; Dean Ambrose downed Curtis Axel; Fandango bested Adam Rose; Roman Reigns beat The Miz; WWE US Champion Rusev beat Erick Rowan; Paige downed Alicia Fox; Daniel Bryan overcame Seth Rollins, Joey Mercury and Jamie Noble in a handicap match.

Air Date: 12th February 2015
Bray Wyatt beat R-Truth; Paige downed Summer Rae; Fandango bested Adam Rose.
Tag Team Turmoil: Daniel Bryan and Roman Reigns rolled over The Miz and Damien Mizdow; Daniel Bryan and Roman Reigns rolled over WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso in a non-title match; Daniel Bryan and Roman Reigns rolled over Los Matadores; Daniel Bryan and Roman Reigns rolled over Heath Slater and Titus O’Neil; Daniel Bryan and Roman Reigns rolled over The Ascension by DQ; Daniel Bryan and Roman Reigns rolled over The Big Show and Kane.

Air Date: 19th February 2015
Daniel Bryan rolled over The Miz; Ryback beat Kane; R-Truth beat WWE Intercontinental Champion Bad News Barrett in a non-title match; The Ascension rolled over Big E and Xavier Woods; Roman Reigns overcame Luke Harper; Paige downed Cameron; Jimmy Uso battled to a no-contest with Tyson Kidd; Seth Rollins overcame Dolph Ziggler.

Air Date: 26th February 2015
Dean Ambrose downed The Miz; Natalya defeated Naomi; Daniel Bryan toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Bad News Barrett in a non-title match; Fandango bested Curtis Axel; Goldust beat Adam Rose; Dolph Ziggler, Ryback and Erick Rowan toppled Seth Rollins, The Big Show and Kane.

Mar 2015

Air Date: 5th March 2015
WWE World Tag Champions Tyson Kidd and Cesaro bested Los Matadores; Ryback beat Kane; The Big Show beat Ryback; AJ Lee downed Brie Bella; Kofi Kingston and Big E beat The Miz and Damien Mizdow; WWE US Champion Rusev beat Jack Swagger; Dean Ambrose and Dolph Ziggler overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Bad News Barrett and Luke Harper.

Air Date: 12th March 2015
WWE World Tag Champions Tyson Kidd and Cesaro, and Los Matadores dropped Jimmy Uso, Jey Uso, Kofi Kingston and Xavier Woods; Ryback beat The Miz; AJ Lee and Paige downed Summer Rae and Cameron; Daniel Bryan, Dolph Ziggler and Dean Ambrose downed Luke Harper, Stardust and WWE Intercontinental Champion Bad News Barrett.

Air Date: 19th March 2015
Paige downed Brie Bella; Gauntlet match: Dean Ambrose downed Stardust, Dean Ambrose downed R-Truth, Luke Harper toppled Dean Ambrose, Daniel Bryan overturned Luke Harper, Dolph Ziggler toppled Daniel Bryan. WWE World Tag Champions Cesaro and Tyson Kidd, and Natalya defeated Los Matadores and Torito; Roman Reigns overcame Seth Rollins and Kane in a handicap match.

Air Date: 26th March 2015
Jimmy Uso won a four-way over Fernando, Big E and Cesaro; John Cena, Mark Henry, Daniel Bryan and Roman Reigns overcame Bray Wyatt, Seth Rollins, The Big Show and Kane.

Apr 2015

Air Date: 2nd April 2015
Randy Orton defeated The Big Show by DQ; Naomi downed Natalya; The Miz pinned R-Truth; Dean Ambrose bested Luke Harper by DQ; Sheamus overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion Daniel Bryan by count-out.

Air Date: 9th April 2015
WWE World Tag Champions Tyson Kidd and Cesaro bested Big E and Kofi Kingston; Neville downed Curtis Axel; Natalya defeated Alicia Fox. Cameron was guest referee; Bray Wyatt beat Erick Rowan; Roman Reigns, WWE Intercontinental Champion Daniel Bryan and Dolph Ziggler overturned Bad News Barrett, Sheamus and The Big Show.

Air Date: 16th April 2015
Bray Wyatt overturned R-Truth; Bad News Barrett overturned The Miz; Neville downed Sheamus by DQ; Kofi Kingston and Big e downed Los Matadores; Cameron won a three-way over Alicia Fox and Natalya; Fandango bested Adam Rose; John Cena and WWE Intercontinental Champion Daniel Bryan overcame WWE World Tag Champions Cesaro and Tyson Kidd in a non-title match.

Air Date: 23rd April 2015
WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins battled to a no-contest with Kane; Dolph Ziggler and Neville downed Sheamus and Bad News Barrett; Naomi dropped Natalya; Ryback beat Rusev by DQ; Kofi Kingston toppled Cesaro; Roman Reigns and Dean Ambrose downed Luke Harper and WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins.

Air Date: 30th April 2015
Roman Reigns overcame Kane by count-out; Damien Sandow beat Curtis Axel; Ryback beat Luke Harper; WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella defeated Cameron; Tyson Kidd and Cesaro bested WWE World Tag Champions Big E and Kofi Kingston by DQ; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins overcame Dean Ambrose in a non-title match.

May 2015

Air Date: 7th May 2015
Dolph Ziggler overcame Bad News Barrett by DQ; Dolph Ziggler and Neville downed Bad News Barrett and Sheamus; Luke Harper overcame Fandango; Big E, Kofi Kingston and Xavier Woods overcame Ryback, Cesaro and Tyson Kidd; Naomi dropped Emma; Sin Cara and Kalisto bested Los Matadores.

Air Date: 14th May 2015
Dean Ambrose downed Sheamus; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins beat Ryback; Tyson Kidd beat Kofi Kingston; Neville downed Bo Dallas; Roman Reigns beat Kane in a no-DQ match.

Air Date: 21st May 2015
Sin Cara & Kalisto won a four-way over Tyson Kidd & Cesaro, Los Matadores, and The Ascension; Dolph Ziggler overturned King Barrett; R-Truth bested Stardust; Axel Mania & Macho Mandow beat Heath Slater & Adam Rose; Dean Ambrose downed Bray Wyatt.

Air Date: 28th May 2015
Sin Cara & Kalisto bested Tyson Kidd & Cesaro in a lumberjack match; R-Truth bested King Barrett; Ryback beat Rusev by DQ; Paige downed Naomi; Roman Reigns & Dean Ambrose downed WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins & Kane by DQ.

Jun 2015

Air Date: 4th June 2015
Titus O’Neil & Darren Young won a three-way over The Ascension, and Sin Cara & Kalisto; WWE Intercontinental Champion Ryback rolled over Stardust; WWE NXT Champion Kevin Owens overturned Zack Ryder; Neville downed Kofi Kingston; Roman Reigns overturned Sheamus by DQ.

Air Date: 11th June 2015
Sin Cara & Kalisto toppled The Ascension; WWE Intercontinental Champion  Ryback rolled over The Miz; King Barrett rolled over Jack Swagger; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins overturned Dolph Ziggler; Paige downed Alicia Fox; Roman Reigns, Randy Orton& Neville downed Sheamus, Kane & Kofi Kingston by DQ.

Air Date: 18th June 2015
Kane and Sheamus beat Dean Ambrose by DQ in a handicap match; Brie Bella pinned Paige; Dolph Ziggler dropped Bo Dallas; Neville bested Xavier Woods; WWE NXT Champion Kevin Owens toppled Cesaro; Kane and Sheamus rolled over Dean Ambrose and Roman Reigns.

Air Date: 25th June 2015
Sheamus bested Dolph Ziggler; Alicia Fox toppled Naomi; Ryback rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Kane by DQ; Kalisto, Sin Cara, and WWE World Tag Champions Darren Young & Titus O’Neill defeated Bo Dallas, Big E, Xavier Woods & Kofi Kingston; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins rolled over Dean Ambrose.

Jul 2015

Air Date: 2nd July 2015
Bray Wyatt toppled Dean Ambrose; R-Truth beat Adam Rose; WWE Intercontinental Champion Ryback rolled over Mark Henry; Brie Bella defeated Naomi; Titus O’Neil & Darren Young beat The Ascension; Roman Reigns rolled over WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins by DQ.

Air Date: 9th July 2015
Dean Ambrose downed Bo Dallas; Rusev beat Fandango; Roman Reigns rolled over The Big Show by DQ; Titus O’Neil beat Big E; Brie Bella & Alicia Fox beat Naomi & Tamina Snuka; WWE Intercontinental Champion Ryback rolled over WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins by count-out.

Air Date: 16th July 2015
Big E & Kofi Kingston bested Kalisto & Sin Cara; King Barrett overcame Jack Swagger; Cesaro toppled Rusev;  Neville pinned Stardust; Roman Reigns and Dean Ambrose rolled over Sheamus & The Big  Show by DQ.

Air Date: 23rd  July 2015
Sheamus downed Dean Ambrose; Neville dropped Adam Rose; Rusev bested Kevin Owens by count-out; WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella & Brie Bella overcame Naomi & WWE NXT Women’s Champion Sasha Banks; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins rolled over Cesaro.

Air Date: 30th July 2015
Cesaro battled Seth Rollins to a no-contest; Los Matadores, Kalisto & Sin Cara overcame The Ascension, Big E & Kofi Kingston; Rusev toppled Jack Swagger; Stardust bested R-Truth; Cesaro & Dean Ambrose rolled over Kevin Owens & Seth Rollins.

Aug 2015

Air Date: 6th August 2015
Mark Henry & WWE World Tag Champions Darren Young & Titus O’Neil beat Big E, Kofi Kingston & Xavier Woods; Charlotte battled Naomi to a no-contest; Naomi & WWE NXT Women’s Champion Sasha Banks beat Becky Lynch & Charlotte; Stardust beat Zack Ryder; Roman Reigns overcame Rusev.

Air Date: 13th August 2015
Charlotte bested Naomi; Roman Reigns toppled Luke Harper by DQ; WWE World Tag Champions Darren Young & Titus O’Neil overcame Big E & Xavier Woods; Kevin Owens & WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins overcame Cesaro & Neville.

Air Date: 20th August 2015
WWE Intercontinental Champion Ryback battled The Big Show to a double count-out; Big E, Xavier Woods & Kofi Kingston topped Los Matadores & El Torito; Neville pinned Bo Dallas; Brie Bella won a three-way over Sasha Banks & Becky Lynch; Roman Reigns, Randy Orton, Cesaro & Dean Ambrose rolled over Bray Wyatt, Kevin Owens, Sheamus & Luke Harper.

Air Date: 27th August 2015
The Dudley Boys rolled over The Ascension; Kevin Owens rolled over Neville; Charlotte and Becky Lynch defeated The Bella Twins; Dolph Ziggler and WWE Intercontinental Champion Ryback downed Rusev and The Big Show; Sheamus rolled over Dean Ambrose.

Sep 2015

Air Date: 3rd September 2015
The Dudley Boys rolled over Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; Sheamus rolled over Cesaro; Bo Dallas rolled over R-Truth; Charlotte downed Tamina Snuka; Roman Reigns and Dean Ambrose downed Big E and Kofi Kingston by DQ.

Air Date: 10th September 2015
Cesaro bested The Miz; Paige battled to a no-contest with Sasha Banks; Roman Reigns, Dean Ambrose and Jimmy Uso battled to a no-contest with Big E, Kofi Kingston and Xavier Woods; The Ascension overcame Sin Cara and Kalisto; WWE World Heavyweight Champion and WWE US Champion Seth Rollins rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Ryback in a non-title lumberjack match.

Air Date: 17th September 2015
Naomi and Sasha Banks rolled over Paige and Becky Lynch; Kofi Kingston overcame Devon Dudley; The Big Show beat Cesaro; Kevin Owens rolled over Dolph Ziggler by DQ; Roman Reigns and Dean Ambrose downed WWE World Heavyweight Champion and WWE US Champion Seth Rollins and Sheamus.

Air Date: 24th September 2015
Roman Reigns rolled over Luke Harper; Big E, Kofi Kingston and Xavier Woods rolled over Sin Cara, Kalisto and Neville; Cesaro bested Bo Dallas; Ryback and Dolph Ziggler overturned Rusev and WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens; Dean Ambrose downed WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins in a non-title match.

Oct 2015

Air Date: 1st October 2015
Sasha Banks, Naomi and Tamina defeated The Bella Twins and Alicia Fox; Ryback downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens by count-out; Sin Cara, Kalisto and Neville downed Stardust and The Ascension; The Dudley Boys and Kane downed WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins, Kofi Kingston and Big E.

Air Date: 8th October 2015
Big E downed Dolph Ziggler; WWE Divas Champion Charlotte, Becky Lynch and Natalya defeated The Bella Twins and Alicia Fox; Ryback downed Rusev; King Barrett beat Neville; Randy Orton and Roman Reigns rolled over Bray Wyatt and Braun Strowman by DQ.

Air Date: 15th October 2015
Roman Reigns rolled over Bo Dallas; King Barrett and Sheamus rolled over Cesaro and Neville; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens rolled over Zack Ryder; Dolph Ziggler overturned Rusev. Summer Raw was guest referee; Braun Strowman and Luke Harper overturned Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; WWE Divas Champion Charlotte downed Alicia Fox; Dean Ambrose and The Dudley Boys rolled over Big E, Kofi Kingston and Xavier Woods by DQ.

Air Date: 22nd October 2015
WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins rolled over Cesaro; Nikki Bella defeated Paige; Sheamus and King Barrett beat Sin Cara and Kalisto; Ryback downed Bo Dallas; Roman Reigns and Dean Ambrose downed Big E and Kofi Kingston.

Air Date: 29th October 2015
Roman Reigns rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens by count-out; Sin Cara and Kalisto won a four-way over The Ascension, The Dudley Boys, and King Barrett and Sheamus; Dolph Ziggler overturned The Miz in a “Trick or Treat” streetfight; Paige downed Natalya; WWE US Champion Alberto Del Rio bested R-Truth; Luke Harper, Erick Rowan and Braun Strowman overcame Ryback, Cesaro and Dean Ambrose.

Nov 2015

Air Date: 5th November 2015
WWE US Champion Alberto Del Rio bested Neville; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso overcame The Ascension; Bray Wyatt, Erick Rowan, Luke Harper and Braun Strowman toppled Sin Cara, Kalisto, Titus O’Neil and Darren Young in an elimination match; Ryback beat King Barrett; Natalya defeated Tamina; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens overcame Dean Ambrose by DQ.

Air Date: 12th November 2015
Braun Strowman toppled Fandango; Neville downed King Barrett (WWE World Heavyweight Title Tournament – 1st Round); Kalisto bested Ryback (WWE World Heavyweight Title Tournament – 1st Round); WWE US Champion Alberto Del Rio bested Stardust (WWE World Heavyweight Title Tournament – 1st Round); Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso overcame Luke Harper and Erick Rowan by DQ.

Air Date: 19th November 2015
Cesaro bested The Miz; The Dudley Boys and Neville downed The Ascension and Stardust; Tyler Breeze downed Zack Ryder; WWE Divas Champion Charlotte downed Brie Bella; Big E downed Kalisto; Roman Reigns and Dean Ambrose downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens and WWE US Champion Alberto Del Rio by DQ.

Air Date: 26th November 2015
The Dudley Boys overturned Erick Rowan and Braun Strowman by DQ; Paige downed Becky Lynch; Jack Swagger rolled over WWE US Champion Alberto Del Rio by DQ; Sin Cara and Kalisto bested WWE World Tag Champions Big E and Kofi Kingston in a non-title match; Dean Ambrose won a three-way over Tyler Breeze and Dolph Ziggler.

Dec 2015

Air Date: 3rd December 2015
Tyler Breeze dropped Neville; Brie Bella downed Becky Lynch by DQ; Kofi Kingston, Big E and Xavier Woods rolled over Jimmy Uso, Jey Uso and Dean Ambrose; Bray Wyatt overcame D-Von Dudley; Roman Reigns rolled over WWE World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus, WWE US Champion Alberto del Río, Rusev and King Barrett by count-out in a handicap match.

Air Date: 10th December 2015
Sin Cara and Kalisto bested Kofi Kingston and Big E; Ryback defeated Konnor and Viktor in a handicap match; Dolph Ziggler beat Tyler Breeze; Becky Lynch rolled over Paige; Roman Reigns and Dean Ambrose downed WWE World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus and WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens.

Air Date: 17th December 2015
WWE US Champion Alberto Del Rio bested Ryback; Titus O’Neil beat Tyler Breeze; Sin Cara & Kalisto bested WWE World Tag Champions Kofi Kingston & Xavier Woods in a non-title match; Becky Lynch toppled Brie Bella; Dolph Ziggler overturned Kevin Owens by DQ.

Air Date: 22nd December 2015
WWE World Tag Champions Kofi Kingston & Big E beat Sin Cara & Kalisto; Bray Wyatt, Braun Strowman, Erick Rowan & Luke Harper overturned The Dudley Boys, Kane & Ryback; WWE US Champion Alberto Del Rio & Rusev beat Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso; Neville & Titus O’Neil beat The Ascension; WWE Divas Champion Charlotte downed Brie Bella; WWE Intercontinental Champion Dean Ambrose won a three-way over Kevin Owens and Dolph Ziggler.

Air Date: 31st December 2015
Kalisto and The Dudley Boys beat Xavier Woods, Big E and Kofi Kingston; Tyler Breeze downed Goldust; Luke Harper and Braun Strowman toppled Jey Uso and Jimmy Uso; Brie Bella and Alicia Fox defeated Naomi and Tamina Snuka; Dolph Ziggler rolled over Bo Dallas; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Roman Reigns and WWE Intercontinental Champion Dean Ambrose downed Sheamus and Kevin Owens by DQ.
