WWE Night of Champions Results

WWE NIGHT OF CHAMPIONS RESULTS

24th June 2007: “WWE Vengeance: Night of Champions 2007”: Houston, TX – 15,000 sellout
($800,000 live gate)
Dark match: Super Crazy downed Carlito.
PPV Broadcast: WWE World Tag Champions Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch defeated Matt and Jeff Hardy; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr pinned Jimmy Wang Yang; Johnny Nitro bested CM Punk to win the vacant ECW title; WWE Intercontinental Champion Santino Marella downed Umaga by DQ; WWE US Champion MVP toppled Ric Flair; WWE Tag Champions Deuce and Domino overcame Jimmy Snuka and Sgt. Slaughter; World Heavyweight Champion Edge overturned Batista by count-out; Candice Michelle pinned WWE Women’s Champion Melina to win the title; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over King Booker, Mick Foley, Randy Orton, and Bobby Lashley in a five-way.

29th June 2008: “WWE Night of Champions 2008”: Dallas, TX – 12,766 sellout
($743,280 live gate)
Dark match:  Jeff Hardy beat MVP.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Tag Champions The Miz and John Morrisson defeated Finlay and Hornswoggle; WWE US Champion Matt Hardy downed Chavo Guerrero; Mark Henry dropped ECW Champion Kane and The Big Show in a three-way to win the title; Cody Rhodes and Ted Dibiase Jr won the WWE World Tag Titles from Hardcore Holly in a handicap match; Kofi Kingston pinned WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho to win the title; WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James bested Katie Lea; World Heavyweight Champion Edge toppled Batista; WWE World Champion Triple H rolled over John Cena.

26th July 2009: “WWE Night of Champions 2009”: Philadelphia, PA – 14,003
($892,585 live gate)
Dark match: Cryme Tyme beat DH Smith and Tyson Kidd.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Unified World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and The Big Show defeated Cody Rhodes and Ted Dibiase Jr; Christian downed ECW Champion Tommy Dreamer to win the title; WWE US Champion Kofi Kingston won a six-way over MVP, Jack Swagger, Carlito, Primo, and The Miz; Michelle McCool beat WWE Women’s Champion Melina to win the title; WWE World Champion Randy Orton bested John Cena and Triple H in a three-way; Mickie James dropped WWE Divas Champion Maryse to win the title; WWE Intercontinental Champion Rey Mysterio toppled Dolph Ziggler; Jeff Hardy rolled over World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk to win the title.

19th September 2010: “WWE Night of Champions 2010”: Chicago, IL – 12,500 sellout (11,000 paid)
($782,205 live gate)
Dark match: John Morrison beat Ted Dibiase Jr.
PPV Broadcast WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler defeated Kofi Kingston; The Big Show pinned CM Punk; Daniel Bryan bested WWE US Champion The Miz to win the title; WWE Women’s Champion  Michelle McCool (a permitted substitute for Layla) toppled WWE Diva’s Champion Melina to unify the titles in a lumberjill match; World Heavyweight Champion Kane overturned The Undertaker; Drew McIntyre and Cody Rhodes won a gauntlet match to win the WWE Unified World Tag Titles: Jimmy and Jey Uso beat Tyson Kidd and DH Smith, Jimmy and Jey Uso beat Santino Marella and Vladimir Kozlov, Evan Bourne and Mark Henry beat Jimmy and Jey Uso, Drew McIntyre and Cody Rhodes beat Mark Henry and Evan Bourne; Randy Orton rolled over WWE World Champion Sheamus, John Cena, Wade Barrett, Chris Jericho and Edge in a six-way elimination match to win the title.

18th September 2011: “WWE Night of Champions 2011”: Buffalo, NY – 11,000
($700,000 live gate)
Dark match: Daniel Bryan beat Heath Slater.
PPV Broadcast: WWE World Tag Champions Evan Bourne and Kofi Kingston downed The Miz and R-Truth by DQ; WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes dropped Ted Dibiase Jr; WWE US Champion Dolph Ziggler overturned Alex Riley, John Morrison and Jack Swagger in a four-way; Mark Henry toppled World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton to win the title; WWE Divas Champion Eve Torres overcame Beth Phoenix; John Cena bested WWE World Champion Alberto Del Rio to win the title; Triple H rolled over CM Punk in a no-DQ match.

16th September 2012: “WWE Night of Champions 2012”: Boston, MA – 14,886 (11,500 paid)
YouTube pre-show: Zack Ryder won a 16-man battle royal, last eliminating Tensai.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz overcame Rey Mysterio, Sin Cara and Cody Rhodes in a four-way; Kane and Daniel Bryan dropped WWE World Tag Champions Kofi Kingston and R-Truth to win the titles; WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro overturned Zack Ryder; Randy Orton bested Dolph Ziggler; Eve Torres downed WWE Divas Champion Layla to win the title; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus toppled Alberto Del Rio; WWE World Champion CM Punk battled John Cena to a double-pinfall finish.

15th September 2013: “WWE Night of Champions 2013”: Detroit, MI – 10,500
Pre-show: Titus O’Neill and Darren Young won a “tag team turmoil” encounter over Jack Swagger and Antonio Cesaro, Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso, Brodus Clay and Tensai, and Drew McIntyre and Heath Slater.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion Curtis Axel defeated Kofi Kingston; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee won a four-way over Natalya, Brie Bella, and Naomi; Rob Van Dam dropped World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio by DQ; The Miz downed Fandango; Paul Heyman and WWE Intercontinental Champion Curtis Axel toppled CM Punk in a handicap match; WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose bested Dolph Ziggler; WWE World Tag Champions Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns overturned Titus O’Neill and Darren Young; Daniel Bryan rolled over WWE World Champion Randy Orton to win the title.

21st September 2014: “WWE Night of Champions 2014”: Nashville, TN – 11,000
Shown on PPV / WWE Network
Goldust and Stardust defeated WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso to win the titles; WWE US Champion Sheamus downed Cesaro; The Miz dropped WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler to win the title; Rusev toppled Mark Henry; Randy Orton pinned Chris Jericho; AJ Lee bested WWE Divas Champion Paige and Nikki Bella in a three-way to win the title; John Cena rolled over WWE World Heavyweight Champion Brock Lesnar by DQ.


20th September 2015: “WWE Night of Champions 2015”: Houston, TX – 11,000 sellout
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Dark match: Stardust and The Ascension beat Neville, Kalisto and Sin Cara
PPV Broadcast: Kevin Owens overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion Ryback to win the title; Dolph Ziggler pinned Rusev; The Dudley Boys downed WWE World Tag Champions Big E and Kofi Kingston by DQ; Charlotte dropped WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella to win the title; Bray Wyatt, Luke Harper and Braun Strowman bested Roman Reigns, Dean Ambrose and Chris Jericho; John Cena toppled WWE US Champion Seth Rollins to win the title; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins rolled over Sting.
