WWF PPV Results 1988

WWF PPV RESULTS (1988)

See also: WWF PPV Statistics 1985-1989

27th March 1988: “WWF Wrestlemania IV”: Atlantic City, NJ – 18,165 sellout (17,000 paid)
($1,400,000 live gate)
Closed Circuit: 175,000 ($2,300,000)
PPV Broadcast: Bad News Brown won a 20-man battle royal; Ted Dibiase beat Jim Duggan (WWF World Title Tournament – First Round); Don Muraco defeated Dino Bravo by DQ (WWF World Title Tournament – First Round); Randy Savage pinned Butch Reed (WWF World Title Tournament – First Round); Greg Valentine downed Ricky Steamboat (WWF World Title Tournament – First Round); One Man Gang dropped Bam Bam Bigelow by count-out (WWF World Title Tournament – First Round); Rick Rude battled Jake Roberts to a draw (WWF World Title Tournament – First Round); The Ultimate Warrior overcame Hercules; Hulk Hogan battled Andre the Giant to a double-DQ (WWF World Title Tournament – Second Round); Ted Dibiase overturned Don Muraco (WWF World Title Tournament – Second Round); Randy Savage bested Greg Valentine (WWF World Title Tournament – Second Round); Brutus Beefcake toppled WWF Intercontinental Champion The Honkytonk Man by DQ; Bobby Heenan, Haku, and Tama defeated Koko B Ware and The British Bulldogs; Randy Savage toppled One Man Gang by DQ (WWF World Title Tournament – Semi-Final); Demolition bested WWF World Tag Champions Tito Santana and Rick Martel to win the titles; Randy Savage rolled over Ted Dibiase to win the vacant WWF World Title (WWF World Title Tournament – Final).

29th August 1988: “WWF Summerslam 1988”: New York, NY (MSG) – 20,000 sellout
($355,345 live gate)
PPV Broadcast: Davey Boy Smith and The Dynamite Kid battled Jacques and Raymond Rougeau to a draw; Bad News Brown rolled over Ken Patera; Rick Rude beat The Junkyard Dog by DQ; The Powers of Pain defeated Boris Zhukoff and Nikolai Volkoff; The Ultimate Warrior pinned WWF Intercontinental Champion The Honkytonk Man to win the title; Dino Bravo downed Don Muraco; WWF World Tag Champions Demolition toppled Bret Hart and Jim Neidhart; The Big Bossman rolled over Koko B Ware; Jake Roberts overturned Hercules; Hulk Hogan and WWF World Champion Randy Savage bested Ted Dibiase and Andre the Giant.

24th November 1988: “WWF Survivor Series 1988”: Richfield, OH – 13,500
PPV Broadcast: WWF Intercontinental Champion The Ultimate Warrior, The Blue Blazer, Jim Brunzell, Sam Houston, and Brutus Beefcake bested The Honkytonk Man, Greg Valentine, Ron Bass, Bad News Brown, and Danny Davis in a “Survivor Series” elimination match; The Barbarian and The Warlord, Bret Hart and Jim Neidhart, Davey Boy Smith and The Dynamite Kid, Jim Powers and Paul Roma, and Shawn Michaels and Marty Jannetty overcame WWF World Tag Champions Demolition, The Conquistadors, Arn Anderson and Tully Blanchard, Jacques and Raymond Rougeau, and Nikolai Volkoff and Boris Zhukov in a “Survivor Series” elimination match; Andre The Giant, Dino Bravo, Curt Hennig, Harley Race, and Rick Rude bested Jake Roberts, Ken Patera, Jim Duggan, Tito Santana, and Scott Casey in a “Survivor Series” elimination match; Hulk Hogan, WWF World Champion Randy Savage, Koko B Ware, Hillbilly Jim, and Hercules overturned The Big Bossman, Akeem, Ted Dibiase, King Haku, and The Red Rooster in a “Survivor Series” elimination match.
