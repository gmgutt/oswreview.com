WWF PPV Results 1990

WWF PPV RESULTS (1990)

See also: WWF PPV Statistics 1990-1993

21st January 1990: “WWF Royal Rumble 1990”: Orlando, FL – 16,000 sellout
($170,000 live gate)
Dark match: Paul Roma beat The Brooklyn Brawler.
PPV Broadcast: Jim Duggan defeated The Big Bossman by DQ; The Bushwhackers downed The Rougeaus; Lanny Poffo battled Brutus Beefcake to a double-DQ; Ronnie Garvin toppled Greg Valentine in an “I Quit” encounter; Hulk Hogan won the 30-man Royal Rumble by last eliminating Curt Hennig.

1st April 1990: “WWF Wrestlemania VI”: Toronto, ONT – 64,287 sellout (61,864 paid)
($3,490,857 live gate)
Closed Circuit: 53,000 ($600,000)
Dark match: Paul Roma defeated The Brooklyn Brawler.
PPV Broadcast: Rick Martel downed Koko B Ware; Demolition rolled over WWF World Tag Champions Andre The Giant and Haku to win the titles; Earthquake bested Hercules; Brutus Beefcake bested Curt Hennig; Roddy Piper battled Bad News Brown to a double count-out; Bret Hart and Jim Neidhart dropped Nikolai Volkoff and Boris Zhukov; The Barbarian rolled over Tito Santana; Dusty Rhodes and Sapphire bested Randy Savage and Sensational Sherri; The Orient Express overturned Shawn Michaels and Marty Jannetty by count-out; Jim Duggan rolled over Dino Bravo; Ted Dibiase bested Jake Roberts by count-out; The Big Bossman rolled over Akeem; Rick Rude bested Jimmy Snuka; WWF Intercontinental Champion The Ultimate Warrior toppled WWF World Champion Hulk Hogan to win the title.

27th August 1990: “WWF Summerslam 1990”: Philadelphia, PA – 19,304 sellout (18,703 paid)
($338,452 live gate)
Dark match: Shane Douglas overturned Buddy Rose.
PPV Broadcast:  Paul Roma and Hercules overturned Shawn Michaels and Marty Jannetty; Kerry Von Erich pinned WWF Intercontinental Champion Curt Hennig to win the title; Sensational Sherri defeated Sapphire by forfeit; The Warlord beat Tito Santana; Bret Hart and Jim Neidhart dropped WWF World Tag Champions Demolition (Crush and Smash) in a “best-of-three-falls” encounter to win the titles; Jake Roberts overturned Bad News Brown by DQ; Jim Duggan and Nikolai Volkoff defeated The Orient Express; Randy Savage bested Dusty Rhodes; Hulk Hogan rolled over Earthquake by count-out; WWF World Champion The Ultimate Warrior toppled Rick Rude in a cage match.

22nd November 1990: “WWF Survivor Series 1990”: Hartford, CT – 16,000 (13,000 paid)
($216,000 live gate)
Dark match: Shane Douglas overturned Buddy Rose.
PPV Broadcast: WWF World Champion The Ultimate Warrior, WWF Intercontinental Champion Kerry Von Erich, and The Legion of Doom defeated Curt Hennig, Ax, Smash, and Crush in a “Survivor Series” elimination match; Ted Dibiase, Greg Valentine, The Honkytonk Man, and The Undertaker rolled over Dusty Rhodes, Koko B Ware, and WWF World Tag Champions Bret Hart and Jim Neidhart in a “Survivor Series” elimination match; Rick Martel, The Warlord, Paul Roma, and Hercules overturned Jake Roberts, Jimmy Snuka, and Shawn Michaels and Marty Jannetty in a “Survivor Series” elimination match ; Hulk Hogan, The Big Bossman, Tugboat, and Jim Duggan toppled Earthquake, Dino Bravo, Haku, and The Barbarian in a “Survivor Series” elimination match; Nikolai Volkoff, Tito Santana, and The Bushwhackers overcame Sgt Slaughter, Boris Zhukoff, and The Orient Express in a “Survivor Series” elimination match ; Hulk Hogan, Tito Santana, and WWF World Champion The Ultimate Warrior dropped Ted Dibiase, Rick Martel, The Warlord, Paul Roma, and Hercules  in a  handicap “Survivor Series” elimination match.
