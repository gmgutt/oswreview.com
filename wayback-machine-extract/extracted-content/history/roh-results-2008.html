Jan 2008

26th January 2008: “ROH Without Remorse”: Chicago Ridge, IL
Austin Aries and Bryan Danielson beat FIP Champion Erick Stevens and Jay Briscoe; Shane Hagadorn defeated Pelle Primeau; Chris Hero and Sara Del Ray overturned Alex Payne and Kyle Durden; Chris Hero and Sara Del Ray overcame Jigsaw and Daizee Haze; Claudio Castagnoli battled El Generico to a draw; Mark Briscoe dropped Joey Matthews; Delirious bested Adam Pearce in a “dog collar” match; Davey Richards and Rocky Romero toppled ROH Tag Team Champions Jimmy Jacobs and Tyler Black, Austin Aries and Bryan Danielson, and BJ Whitmer and Brent Albright in a four-way “Ultimate Endurance” match to win the titles; ROH Champion Nigel McGuinness rolled over Roderick Strong.

23rd February 2008: “ROH 6th Anniversary Show”: New York, NY (Manhattan Center)
