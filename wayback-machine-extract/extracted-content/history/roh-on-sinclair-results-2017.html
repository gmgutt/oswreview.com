ROH on Sinclair Results 2017

ROH ON SINCLAIR TV RESULTS 2017

Jan 2017

Air Date: 7th January 2017
Jay Briscoe downed BJ Whitmer (Decade of Excellence Tournament – Quarter-Final); Beer City Bruiser and Silas Young defeated Alex Reynolds and John Silver; Vinny Marseglia, TK O’Ryan and Matt Taven overcame Will Ferrara, Cheeseburger and Joey Daddiego; Chris Sabin overcame Colt Cabana (Decade of Excellence Tournament – Quarter-Final).

Air Date: 14th January 2017
Lio Rush, Jay White and Donovan Dijak defeated Kenny King, Caprice Coleman and Rhett Titus; Cody Rhodes overturned Steve Corino; Jay Lethal defeated Jushin Liger (Decade of Excellence Tournament – Quarter-Final).

Air Date: 21st January 2017
Marty Scurll defeated Jonathan Gresham; Christopher Daniels overturned Chris Sabin (Decade of Excellence Tournament – Semi-Final); Kushida beat Dragon Lee and Will Ospreay in a three-way.

Air Date: 28th January 2017
Sho Tanaka and Yohei Komatsu toppled Cheeseburger and Will Ferrara, and Leon St. Giovanni and Shaheem Ali in a three-way; ROH TV Champion Marty Scurll defeated Juice Robinson; Jay Briscoe downed Jay Lethal (Decade of Excellence Tournament – Semi-Final).

Feb 2017

Air Date: 4th February 2017
Punishment Martinez and BJ Whitmer battled Raymond Rowe and Hanson to a double count-out; Vinny Marseglia, TK O’Ryan and Matt Taven overcame Corey Hollis, John Skyler and Joseph A’Gau; ROH Tag Champions The Young Bucks defeated Sho Tanaka and Yohei Komatsu; Donovan Dijak defeated Alex Shelley, Chris Sabin, Jay White, Jonathan Gresham and Lio Rush in a six-way.

Air Date: 11th February 2017
Hangman Page downed Matt Sells; Jay Lethal and Bobby Fish defeated Adam Cole and Cody; Mark Briscoe downed Sal Rinauro; Caprice Coleman, Kenny King and Rhett Titus overturned Dalton Castle, Brandon Tate and Brent Tate.

Air Date: 18th February 2017
Silas Young defeated Bull James; Colt Cabana beat Brandon Tate and Brent Tate in a handicap match; Christopher Daniels overturned Jay Briscoe (Decade of Excellence Tournament – Final).

Air Date: 25th February 2017
Adam Cole and Hangman Page downed Christopher Daniels and Frankie Kazarian; John Skyler rolled over Sean Carr (Top Prospect Tournament – Quarter-Final); ROH TV Champion Marty Scurll defeated Donovan Dijak.

Mar 2017

Air Date: 4th March 2017
ROH 6-Man Tag Champions Matt Taven, TK O’Ryan and Vinny Marseglia defeated Caprice Coleman, Kenny King and Rhett Titus; Brian Milonas overturned Raphael King (Top Prospect Tournament – Quarter-Final); ROH Champion Adam Cole, Matt Jackson, Nick Jackson and Cody Rhodes toppled Jay Briscoe, Mark Briscoe, Jay Lethal and Bobby Fish.

Air Date: 11th March 2017
The Young Bucks overcame Leon St. Giovanni and Shaheem Ali; Curt Stallion overcame Preston Quinn (Top Prospect Tournament – Quarter-Final); Dalton Castle downed Jonathan Gresham; Punishment Martinez and BJ Whitmer rolled over Raymond Rowe and Hanson in a no-DQ match.

Air Date: 18th March 2017
Silas Young and Beer City Bruiser rolled over Cheeseburger and Will Ferrara; Josh Woods overturned Chris LeRusso (Top Prospect Tournament – Quarter-Final); Kenny King defeated Lio Rush; Jay Briscoe downed Jay White.

Air Date: 25th March 2017
ROH Tag Champions Matt Hardy and Jeff Hardy defeated Mark Briscoe and Jay Briscoe; Dalton Castle, ROH Champion Christopher Daniels and Kazarian overcame Adam Cole, and The Young Bucks.

Apr 2017

Air Date : 1st April 2017
Raymond Rowe downed Davey Boy Smith Jr; Josh Woods overturned Brian Milonas (Top Prospect Tournament – Semi-Final); ROH TV Champion Marty Scurll defeated Kenny King.

Air Date : 8th April 2017
Punishment Martinez defeated Hanson; John Skyler rolled over Curt Stallion (Top Prospect Tournament – Semi-Final); Mark Briscoe, Jay Briscoe and Bully Ray toppled ROH 6-Man Tag Champions Matt Taven, Vinny Marseglia and Silas Young to win the titles.

Air Date : 15th April 2017
Chris Sabin overcame Caprice Coleman; Baretta and Rocky Romero bested Will Ferrara and Cheeseburger; Jay White, Jay Lethal, Adam Page and Silas Young defeated Bobby Fish, Lio Rush, Colt Cabana and Hanson.

Air Date : 22nd April 2017
Shane Taylor rolled over Lio Rush; Josh Woods overturned John Skyler (Top Prospect Tournament – Final); ROH TV Champion Marty Scurll defeated Kazarian.

Air Date : 29th April 2017
Matt Taven and Vinny Marseglia beat Leon St. Giovanni and Shaheem Ali; Matt Sydal rolled over Flip Gordon; Jay Lethal rolled over Hangman Page, Jay White and Silas Young in a four-way.

May 2017

Air Date: 6th May 2017
ROH Tag Champions The Young Bucks overturned Mikey and Kenny; Punishment Martinez defeated Beer City Bruiser; Adam Cole and Cody Rhodes toppled Dalton Castle and ROH Champion Christopher Daniels.

Air Date: 13th May 2017
Chris Sabin and Jonathan Gresham defeated Cheeseburger and Will Ferrara; Silas Young defeated Bobby Fish; ROH 6-Man Tag Champions Bully Ray, Mark Briscoe and Jay Briscoe defeated Caprice Coleman, Shane Taylor and Rhett Titus.
