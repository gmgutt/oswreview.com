Raw Results 2008

WWE RAW RESULTS (2008)

Jan 2008


Air Date: 7th January 2008
Shawn Michaels and Mr Kennedy bested Charlie Haas and Trevor Murdoch; Hardcore Holly defeated Carlito; Ashley won a “lingerie pillow fight” over Maria, Mickie James, Melina, and Jillian Hall; Triple H dropped William Regal in a “first blood” match; Chris Jericho battled John Bradshaw Layfield and Snitsky to a no-contest in a handicap match; Hornswoggle and Mick Foley overturned Rory and Robbie McAllister; WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy rolled over Umaga in a cage match.

Air Date: 14th January 2008
WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix, Melina, and Jillian Hall overcame Ashley, Maria, and Mickie James; Shawn Michaels bested Trevor Murdoch; Snitsky dropped Triple H by DQ; Hornswoggle won a 6-man Royal Rumble match; Ric Flair overturned William Regal; WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy rolled over WWE World Champion Randy Orton by DQ.

Air Date: 21st January 2008
Shawn Michaels bested Mr Kennedy; WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix overcame Mickie James; Finlay and Hornswoggle downed Robbie and Rory McAllister; Umaga toppled Brian Kendrick; Carlito overturned Hardcore Holly; Triple H won an “over-the-top-rope” gauntlet match over Mark Henry, Snitsky, and William Regal.

Air Date: 28th January 2008
WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix and Jillian Hall defeated Mickie James and Ashley; Carlito downed Cody Rhodes; Triple H and Shawn Michaels bested Umaga and Snitsky; Mr Kennedy toppled Brian Kendrick; Maria pinned Melina; WWE World Champion Randy Orton and John Bradshaw Layfield rolled over Chris Jericho and WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy.

Feb 2008

Air Date: 4th February 2008
Mickie James and Kelly Kelly defeated WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix and Victoria; Mr Kennedy downed Super Crazy; Carlito and Santino Marella dropped Brian Kendrick and Paul London; WWE World Tag Champions Hardcore Holly and Cody Rhodes bested Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch; Shawn Michaels, WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy, and Chris Jericho toppled John Bradshaw Layfield, Umaga, and Snitsky.

Air Date: 11th February 2008
Hornswoggle pinned Vince McMahon in a no-DQ match; Chris Jericho bested John Bradshaw Layfield; Paul Burchill overturned Brian Kendrick; John Cena defeated Mark Henry; Melina downed Maria; WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy rolled over Shawn Michaels.

Air Date: 18th February 2008
WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy pinned Snitsky; Paul Birchill overcame Super Crazy; Mr Kennedy overturned Val Venis; Maria bested WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix in a non-title match; John Cena rolled over WWE World Champion Randy Orton in a non-title match. Triple H was guest referee.

Air Date: 25th February 2008
WWE World Tag Champions Hardcore Holly and Cody Rhodes dropped Carlito and Santino Marella; Chris Jericho pinned WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy; Umaga bested DH Smith; Shawn Michaels toppled Lance Cade by DQ; Triple H and John Cena rolled over WWE World Champion Randy Orton and Mr Kennedy.

Mar 2008

Air Date: 3rd March 2008 
The Big Show pinned Brandon Hill in a “MMA” match; Ric Flair and Shawn Michaels dropped Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch; Umaga squashed Super Crazy; John Cena rolled over Mr Kennedy; Carlito pinned Cody Rhodes; Triple H battled WWE World Champion Randy Orton to a no-contest.

Air Date: 10th March 2008
The Undertaker defeated Mark Henry in a casket match; Mr Kennedy dropped Finlay by DQ; Chris Jericho bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy to win the title; Triple H bested Kane; Melina pinned Maria; World Heavyweight Champion Edge rolled over CM Punk; Shawn Michaels battled John Cena to a no-contest.

Air Date: 17th March 2008
CM Punk pinned Carlito; Umaga downed Paul London and Brian Kendrick in a handicap match; John Bradshaw Layfield dropped Colin Delaney; Ric Flair bested Vince McMahon in a streetfight; Maria and Candice Michelle defeated Jillian Hall and Victoria; The Big Show toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho by DQ; WWE World Champion Randy Orton and John Cena battled Umaga, Val Venis, Snitsky, Santino Marella, Lance Cade, Trevor Murdoch, Brian Kendrick, Carlito, WWE World Tag Champions Hardcore Holly and Cody Rhodes, DH Smith, Paul Burchill, Robbie McAllister, Charlie Haas, John Bradshaw Layfield, Jim Duggan, and Super Crazy to a no-contest in a handicap elimination match.

Air Date: 24th March 2008
WWE US Champion MVP and Carlito overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho and CM Punk; The Great Khali beat Hardcore Holly; Santino Marella, Melina, and WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix bested Jerry Lawler, Maria, and Ashley; John Cena, Triple H, Shawn Michaels, and Ric Flair rolled over WWE World Champion Randy Orton, Umaga, John Bradshaw Layfield, and The Big Show in a no-DQ match.

Air Date: 31st March 2008
Cryme Tyme beat Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch; Brian Kendrick and Paul London dropped WWE World Tag Champions Hardcore Holly and Cody Rhodes in a non-title match; WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho bested CM Punk; WWE World Champion Randy Orton rolled over Matt Hardy; Maria beat Santino Marella in a no-DQ match.

Apr 2008

Air Date: 7th April 2008
Mickie James and Ashley overcame Melina and Jillian Hall; Brian Kendrick and Paul London dropped Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch; Triple H bested WWE World Champion Randy Orton and John Bradshaw Layfield in a non-title handicap match; Carlito and Santino Marella downed WWE World Tag Champions Hardcore Holly and Cody Rhodes in a non-title match; WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix beat Maria; John Cena toppled Triple H and John Bradshaw Layfield in a handicap match.

Air Date: 14th April 2008
Carlito and Santino Marella defeated Brian Kendrick and Paul London; Mickie James downed WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix to win the title; JTG dropped Trevor Murdoch; WWE World Champion Randy Orton rolled over William Regal; WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho bested Umaga; Paul Burchill beat Jim Duggan; Triple H rolled over John Bradshaw Layfield by DQ.

Air Date: 21st April 2008
WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho bested WWE US Champion MVP in a non-title match (King of the Ring 2008 – quarter-final); CM Punk overcame Matt Hardy (King of the Ring 2008 – quarter-final); Finlay defeated The Great Khali by DQ (King of the Ring 2008 – quarter-final); William Regal downed Hornswoggle (King of the Ring 2008 – quarter-final); Carlito overturned Hardcore Holly; CM Punk pinned WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho (King of the Ring 2008 – semi-final); William Regal bested Finlay (King of the Ring 2008 – semi-final);  “Barack Obama”  imposter battled “Hillary Clinton” imposter to a no-contest; William Regal toppled CM Punk (King of the Ring 2008 – final); WWE World Champion Randy Orton, Edge, Chavo Guerrero Jr, and John Bradshaw Layfield rolled over World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker, Triple H, John Cena, and ECW Champion Kane.

Air Date: 28th April  2008
WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James, Maria, Ashley, Michelle McCool, Cherry, and Kelly Kelly beat Beth Phoenix, Melina, Jillian Hall, Victoria, Natalya Neidhart, and Layla; John Bradshaw Layfield downed Robbie McAllister; Paul London and Brian Kendrick overcame Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch; Paul Burchill and Katie Lea defeated Super Crazy in a handicap match; Cody Rhodes pinned Santino Marella; WWE World Champion Triple H battled Randy Orton to a no-contest.

May 2008


Air Date: 5th May 2008
WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James bested Beth Phoenix in a no-DQ lumberjack match; Paul Burchill and Katie Lea downed John Cutler in a handicap match; Shawn Michaels and WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho toppled WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz in a non-title match; Randy Orton battled CM Punk to a no-contest; John Bradshaw Layfield pinned DH Smith; ECW Champion Kane, CM Punk, WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz, Kofi Kingston, Mike Knox, Stevie Richards, Tommy Dreamer, Shelton Benjamin, Elijah Burke, Matt Striker, Chavo Guerrero Jr and Bam Neely rolled over WWE World Champion Triple H and Mr Kennedy in a handicap match.

Air Date: 12th May 2008
WWE World Tag Champions Hardcore Holly and Cody Rhodes downed Carlito and Santino Marella; Mr Kennedy dropped Snitsky; WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James and Maria beat Beth Phoenix and Melina; Jeff Hardy toppled Umaga; Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch defeated Cryme Tyme; Randy Orton rolled over John Cena. JBL was guest referee.

Air Date: 19th May 2008
Jeff Hardy battled Umaga to a double count-out; Beth Phoenix beat Maria; Batista pinned WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho in a non-title match; Mr Kennedy dropped William Regal in a no-DQ match; Randy Orton and John Bradshaw Layfield rolled over WWE World Champion Triple H and John Cena.

Air Date: 26th May 2008
Melina beat Jillian Hall; WWE World Tag Champions Bob Holly and Cody Rhodes overcame Paul London and Brian Kendrick; JBL and Umaga bested John Cena and Jeff Hardy; Mr Kennedy downed Carlito; Shawn Michaels battled WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho to a double count-out.

Jun 2008

Air Date: 2nd June 2008
Mr Kennedy downed Umaga by count-out; Carlito and Santino Marella toppled WWE World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Bob Holly in a non-title match; WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho dropped JBL by DQ; Beth Phoenix and Katie Lea overcame Mickie James and Melina; Lance Cade defeated Trevor Murdoch; John Cena bested Jeff Hardy.

Air Date: 9th June 2008
Mr Kennedy downed Paul Burchill; WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James overturned Beth Phoenix; John Cena bested JBL; Umaga and Snitsky defeated WWE World Tag Champions Bob Holly and Cody Rhodes in a non-title match; Cryme Tyme overcame Santino Marella and Carlito; Jeff Hardy toppled WWE World Champion Triple H by count-out.

Air Date: 16th June 2008
John Cena bested Umaga in a streetfight; Maria won a bikini contest over Eve Torres, Jillian Hall, Layla, Lena Yada, Maryse and Melina; Jeff Hardy toppled Carlito; WWE World Tag Champions Bob Holly and Cody Rhodes overcame Cryme Tyme; Paul Burchill and Katie Lea overcame Mr Kennedy and Mickie James; WWE World Champion Triple H rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho by DQ.

Air Date: 23rd June 2008
Triple H rolled over Mark Henry; Finlay and Hornswoggle beat Santino Marella and Carlito; WWE World Tag Champions Bob Holly and Cody Rhodes overcame Chavo Guerrero and Bam Neely; WWE Tag Champions The Miz and John Morrison beat WWE US Champion Matt Hardy and Jeff Hardy; Melina and Mickie James battled Natalya and Victoria to a no-contest; John Cena bested World Heavyweight Champion Edge by count-out; MVP toppled Tommy Dreamer; JBL pinned Kofi Kingston; Edge won an  “All-Star” battle royal.

Air Date: 30th June 2008
CM Punk overturned World Heavyweight Champion Edge to win the title; WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James overturned Jillian Hall; WWE World Tag Champions Ted Dibiase Jr and Cody Rhodes overcame Steve Anthony and Darin Waid; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston dropped Chris Jericho by DQ; World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk overturned JBL.

Jul 2008

Air Date: 7th July 2008
Rey Mysterio pinned Santino Marella; WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James and Kelly Kelly beat Jillian Hall and Layla; World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk overturned Snitsky; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston dropped Charlie Haas; Batista won a four-way over JBL, John Cena and Kane.

Air Date: 14th July 2008
WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James overturned Katie Lee Burchill; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston dropped Paul Burchill; Beth Phoenix downed Santino Marella; World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk overturned Kane by count-out; Chris Jericho dropped Paul London; John Cena and Cryme Tyme overcame JBL and WWE World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Ted Dibiase Jr by DQ.

Air Date: 21st July 2008
Lance Cade downed Paul London; Beth Phoenix dropped Kelly Kelly; John Cena and Cryme Tyme overcame JBL, Cody Rhodes and Ted Dibiase Jr; Paul Burchill and Katie Lea Burchill toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston and WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James; D-Lo Brown overcame Santino Marella; Word Heavyweight Champion CM Punk battled Batista to a no-contest.

Air Date: 28th July 2008
Santino Marella and Beth Phoenix beat D-Lo Brown and Kelly Kelly; WWE World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Ted Dibiase Jr defeated Jerry Lawler and Michael Cole; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston dropped Jamie Noble; World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk overturned William Regal; WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James overturned Jillian Hall; John Cena and Batista toppled JBL and Kane.

Aug 2008

Air Date: 4th August 2008
WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston dropped Paul Burchill; WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James overturned Katie Lea Burchill; JBL and Chris Jericho dropped World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk in a non-title handicap match; Kane pinned Matt Striker; John Cena and Batista toppled WWE World Tag Champions Ted Dibiase Jr and Cody Rhodes to win the titles.

Air Date: 11th August 2008
Beth Phoenix beat Kelly Kelly; Santino Marella defeated WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James in a non-title match; Cryme Tyme overcame The Highlanders; Chris Jericho dropped Kane; William Regal bested Jamie Noble; Ted Dibiase Jr and Cody Rhodes overcame WWE World Tag Champions John Cena and Batista to win the titles.

Air Date: 18th August 2008
Batista toppled Paul Burchill; Mickie James and Kelly Kelly overcame Jillian Hall and Katie Lea Burchill; JBL downed Jamie Noble; John Cena bested WWE World Tag Champions Ted Dibiase Jr and Cody Rhodes in a non-title handicap match; D-Lo Brown toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Santino Marella by DQ; Chris Jericho dropped World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk in a non-title match.

Air Date: 25th August 2008
World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk overturned JBL; Kelly Kelly overturned WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix in a non-title match; Primo beat Charlie Haas; WWE World Tag Champions Ted Dibiase Jr and Cory Rhodes overcame Jerry Lawler and Jim Duggan; WWE Intercontinental Champion Santino Marella overturned Kofi Kingston; Batista toppled Kane by DQ.

Sep 2008

Air Date: 1st September 2008
Kofi Kingston dropped Charlie Haas; Mark Henry won a battle royal; Jamie Noble defeated William Regal; Kelly Kelly, Mickie James and Candice Michelle bested WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix, Jillian Hall and Katie Lea Burchill; WWE Intercontinental Champion Santino Marella toppled D-Lo Brown; Kane won a battle royal.

Air Date: 8th September 2008
WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix beat Mickie James; William Regal defeated Jamie Noble; Charlie Haas downed JBL by count-out; Rey Mysterio and Evan Bourne dropped The Miz and John Morrison; WWE World Tag Champions Ted Dibiase Jr and Cody Rhodes, and Manu overcame Cryme Tyme and Kofi Kingston; WWE Intercontinental Champion Santino Marella toppled Snitsky; Batista battled World Heavyweight Champion Chris Jericho to a no-contest; World Heavyweight Champion Chris Jericho and JBL rolled over Batista in a handicap match.

Air Date: 15th September 2008
World Heavyweight Champion Chris Jericho dropped CM Punk in a cage match; Candice Michelle overturned Jillian Hall; JBL pinned Tommy Dreamer; WWE World Tag Champions Ted Dibiase Jr and Cody Rhodes, and Manu overcame Kofi Kingston, Jerry Lawler and Charlie Haas; Jamie Noble bested Paul Burchill; Rey Mysterio toppled Kane by DQ.

Air Date: 22nd September 2008
CM Punk overturned Cody Rhodes; WWE Intercontinental Champion Santino Marella downed Deuce; Kane dropped Evan Bourne; Kelly Kelly overturned WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix in a non-title match; John Morrison and The Miz overcame Cryme Tyme; World Heavyweight Champion Chris Jericho, Lance Cade and JBL rolled over Shawn Michaels and Batista in a handicap match.

Air Date: 29th September 2008
Batista toppled Santino Marella; The Miz overcame JTG; Jamie Noble and Mickie James overturned Paul Burchill and Katie Lea Burchill; Manu, Kane and WWE World Tag Champions Ted Dibiase Jr and Cody Rhodes overcame Rey Mysterio, Evan Bourne, Kofi Kingston and CM Punk; WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix and Jillian Hall overturned Kelly Kelly and Candice Michelle; Charlie Haas bested Deuce; Shawn Michaels and WWE World Champion Triple H rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Chris Jericho and Lance Cade by DQ.

Oct 2008

Air Date: 6th October 2008
WWE Intercontinental Champion Santino Marella and WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix overturned Mickie James and Jamie Noble; Ted Dibiase Jr defeated Kofi Kingston; Mark Henry and Kane downed Rey Mysterio and ECW Champion Matt Hardy; Shawn Michaels toppled Lance Cade in a no-DQ match; Kelly Kelly overcame Jillian Hall; Batista toppled JBL. Chris Jericho was guest referee.

Air Date: 13th October 2008
Kane and Mark Henry overcame Rey Mysterio and ECW Champion Matt Hardy; JBL pinned Charlie Haas; Batista battled Shawn Michaels to a no-contest in a lumberjack match; Cryme Tyme and Kelly Kelly overcame The Miz, John Morrison and Jillian Hall; CM Punk overturned World Heavyweight Champion Chris Jericho in a non-title match. Batista was guest referee.

Air Date: 20th October 2008
Candice Michelle and Kelly Kelly defeated Jillian Hall and Katie Lea Burchill; JBL downed Charlie Haas; Rey Mysterio toppled Snitsky; The Miz and John Morrison overcame Kofi Kingston and CM Punk; Non-title Gauntlet Series: Batista toppled William Regal; Chris Jericho dropped William Regal; Mark Henry overcame Chris Jericho by DQ; Batista toppled Mark Henry; Kane bested Chris Jericho; Batista toppled Kane.

Air Date 27th October 2008
CM Punk and Kofi Kingston dropped WWE World Tag Champions Ted Dibiase Jr and Cody Rhodes to win the title; WWE Intercontinental Champion Santino Marella battled Charlie Haas to a no-contest; Kelly Kelly, Mickie James and Candice Michelle overturned Katie Lea, Jillian Hall and Layla; Rey Mysterio toppled Evan Bourne; World Heavyweight Champion Batista and Shawn Michaels toppled JBL and Chris Jericho.

Nov 2008

Air Date: 3rd November 2008
Jeff Hardy and ECW Champion Matt Hardy bested MVP and Brian Kendrick; William Regal won a battle royal; Vladimir Kozlov overcame Charlie  Haas; WWE World Champion Triple H and Shawn Michaels downed The Miz and John Morrison; WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix, Jillian Hall, Katie Lea, Layla, Lena Yada, Maryse, Natalya and Victoria defeated Brie Bella, Candice Michelle, Eve Torres, Kelly Kelly, Mae Young, WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool, Mickie James and Tiffany; CM Punk dropped Randy Orton by DQ; The Undertaker bested JBL by count-out; Kane and Mark Henry toppled Kofi Kingston and Rey Mysterio; Chris Jericho rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Batista in a cage match to win the title.

Air Date: 10th November 2008
William Regal defeated WWE Intercontinental Champion Santino Marella to win the title; Mike Knox downed D-Lo Brown; Rey Mysterio toppled Kane in a no-DQ match; Batista toppled Cody Rhodes; WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix dropped Mickie James; World Heavyweight Champion Chris Jericho dropped Shawn Michaels in a “last man standing” match.

Air Date: 17th November 2008
Kelly Kelly beat Victoria; Batista toppled Manu; The Miz and John Morrison defeated Rey Mysterio and Shawn Michaels; JBL and Kane downed Cryme Tyme; WWE Intercontinental Champion William Regal battled ECW Champion Matt Hardy to a double count-out; Randy Orton rolled over CM Punk in a lumberjack match.

Air Date: 24th November 2008
Shawn Michaels and Rey Mysterio toppled The Miz and John Morrison; Kofi Kingston dropped Kane by DQ (WWE Intercontinental Title #1 Contender Tournament – First Round); Chris Jericho won a three-way over Randy Orton and Batista; Goldust bested Santino Marella; CM Punk overturned Snitsky (WWE Intercontinental Title #1 Contender Tournament – First Round); Melina, Candice Michelle and Mickie James overturned WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix, Jillian Hall and Katie Lea.

Dec 2008

Air Date: 1st December 2008
WWE World Tag Champions CM Punk and Kofi Kingston dropped Manu and Cody Rhodes; Melina downed Jillian Hall; Batista toppled Dolph Ziggler; John Morrison bested Finlay (WWE Intercontinental Title #1 Contender Tournament – First Round); Rey Mysterio toppled The Miz (WWE Intercontinental Title #1 Contender Tournament – First Round); World Heavyweight Champion John Cena rolled over Kane.

Air Date: 8th December 2008
CM Punk overturned John Morrison (WWE Intercontinental Title #1 Contender Tournament – Semi-Final); Charlie Haas overcame MVP; Chris Jericho dropped Jeff Hardy; Finlay and Hornswoggle toppled Santino Marella and WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix; Rey Mysterio toppled Kofi Kingston (WWE Intercontinental Title #1 Contender Tournament – Semi-Final); Randy Orton, Manu and Cody Rhodes overcame Batista and Triple H in a handicap match; Dolph Ziggler bested R-Truth by count-out; World Heavyweight Champion John Cena battled WWE World Champion Edge to a no-contest.

Air Date: 15th December 2008
Kelly Kelly and Melina defeated WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix and Jillian Hall; Rey Mysterio downed Sim Snuka by DQ; WWE World Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz overcame Kofi Kingston and CM Punk; Dolph Ziggler overturned Charlie Haas; World Heavyweight Champion John Cena and Batista toppled Randy Orton, Manu and Cody Rhodes by DQ in a handicap match.

Air Date: 22nd December 2008
Shawn Michaels toppled Kane; Kofi Kingston dropped Manu; Melina and Mickie James overturned Jillian Hall and Layla; JBL overcame Rey Mysterio by DQ; Chris Jericho dropped CM Punk; World Heavyweight Champion John Cena and Trish Stratus rolled over Santino Marella and WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix.

Air Date: 29th December 2008
Cody Rhodes defeated CM Punk by count-out; Melina won a women’s battle royal; Sim Snuka downed Charlie Haas; World Heavyweight Champion John Cena, Rey Mysterio, Kofi Kingston, and Cryme Tyme dropped Kane, Dolph Ziggler, Mike Knox, and WWE World Tag Champions The Miz and John Morrison; ECW Champion Matt Hardy bested Manu; JBL won a four-way over Shawn Michaels, Randy Orton, and Chris Jericho in a four-way.
