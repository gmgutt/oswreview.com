WWE PPV Results 2013

WWE PPV RESULTS (2013)

See also: WWE PPV Statistics 2013

27th January 2013: “WWE Royal Rumble 2013”: Phoenix, AZ – 13,000 sellout (12,000 paid)
Pre-show: WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro defeated The Miz.
PPV Broadcast: World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio bested The Big Show in a “Last Man Standing” match; WWE World Tag Champions Kane and Daniel Bryan toppled Damien Sandow and Cody Rhodes; John Cena won the Royal Rumble, last eliminating Ryback; The Rock rolled over WWE World Champion CM Punk to win the title.

17th February 2013: “WWE Elimination Chamber 2013”: New Orleans, LA – 13,000 sellout (12,000 paid)
You-Tube pre-show: Brodus Clay and Tensai beat Cody Rhodes and Damien Sandow.
PPV Broadcast: World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio bested The Big Show; WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro downed The Miz by DQ; Jack Swagger won an “Elimination Chamber” match over Randy Orton, Chris Jericho, Mark Henry, Daniel Bryan and Kane;  Dean Ambrose, Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns dropped John Cena, Ryback and Sheamus;  Dolph Ziggler toppled Kofi Kingston; WWE Divas Champion Kaitlyn defeated Tamina Snuka; WWE World Champion The Rock rolled over CM Punk.

7th April 2013: “WWE Wrestlemania XXIX”: East Rutherford, NJ – 72,000 sellout (66,100 paid)
($11,900,000 live gate)
Pre-show: The Miz beat WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett to win the title.
PPV Broadcast: Seth Rollins, Roman Reigns and Dean Ambrose defeated Randy Orton, Sheamus and The Big Show; Mark Henry defeated Ryback; WWE World Tag Champions Daniel Bryan and Kane downed Dolph Ziggler and Big E Langston; Fandango dropped Chris Jericho; World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio toppled Jack Swagger; The Undertaker pinned CM Punk; Triple H bested Brock Lesnar in a “no-holds-barred” encounter; John Cena rolled over WWE World Champion The Rock to win the title.

19th May 2013: “WWE Extreme Rules 2013”: St Louis, MO – 14,500 sellout
Pre-show: The Miz downed Cody Rhodes.
PPV Broadcast: Chris Jericho defeated Fandango; Dean Ambrose beat WWE US Champion Kofi Kingston to win the title; Sheamus overcame Mark Henry in a strap match; Alberto Del Rio bested Jack Swagger in an “I Quit” match; Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns overturned WWE World Tag Champions Daniel Bryan and Kane to win the titles in a tornado match; Randy Orton pinned The Big Show in an “Extreme Rules” match; WWE World Champion John Cena battled Ryback to a no-contest in a “last man standing” match; Brock Lesnar rolled over Triple H in a cage match.

16th June 2013: “WWE Payback 2013”: Chicago, IL – 12,500 sellout  (11,500 paid)
Pre-show: Sheamus beat Damien Sandow.
PPV Broadcast: Curtis Axel overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett and The Miz in a three-way to win the title; AJ Lee defeated WWE Divas Champion Kaitlyn to win the title; WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose downed Kane by count-out; Alberto Del Rio dropped World Heavyweight Champion Dolph Ziggler to win the title; CM Punk bested Chris Jericho; WWE World Tag Champions Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns toppled Daniel Bryan and Randy Orton; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Ryback in a “three stages of hell” match.

14th July 2013: “WWE Money in the Bank 2013”: Philadelphia, PA – 15,000 sellout (13,000 paid)
Pre-show: WWE World Tag Champions Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns beat Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso.
PPV Broadcast: Damien Sandow won a “Money in the Bank” ladder match over Jack Swagger, Antonio Cesaro, Wade Barrett, Cody Rhodes, Dean Ambrose and Fandango; WWE Intercontinental Champion Curtis Axel pinned The Miz; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee downed Kaitlyn; Ryback dropped Chris Jericho; World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio overturned Dolph Ziggler by DQ; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Mark Henry; Randy Orton won a “Money in the Bank” ladder match over Daniel Bryan, Rob Van Dam, Sheamus, Christian and CM Punk.

18th August 2013: “WWE Summerslam 2013”: Los Angeles, CA – 14,500 sellout
($1,000,000 live gate)
Pre-show: Rob Van Dam beat WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose by DQ.
PPV Broadcast: Bray Wyatt defeated Kane in a “Ring of Fire” match; Cody Rhodes downed Damien Sandow; World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio dropped Christian; Natalya overcame Brie Bella; Brock Lesnar bested CM Punk in a no-DQ match; Dolph Ziggler and Kaitlyn toppled Big E Langston and WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee; Daniel Bryan rolled over WWE World Champion John Cena to win the title; Randy Orton pinned WWE World Champion Daniel Bryan to win the title.

15th September 2013: “WWE Night of Champions 2013”: Detroit, MI – 10,500
Pre-show: Titus O’Neill and Darren Young won a “tag team turmoil” encounter over Jack Swagger and Antonio Cesaro, Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso, Brodus Clay and Tensai, and Drew McIntyre and Heath Slater.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion Curtis Axel defeated Kofi Kingston; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee won a four-way over Natalya, Brie Bella, and Naomi; Rob Van Dam dropped World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio by DQ; The Miz downed Fandango; Paul Heyman and WWE Intercontinental Champion Curtis Axel toppled CM Punk in a handicap match; WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose bested Dolph Ziggler; WWE World Tag Champions Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns overturned Titus O’Neill and Darren Young; Daniel Bryan rolled over WWE World Champion Randy Orton to win the title.

6th October 2013: “WWE Battleground 2013”: Buffalo, NY – 11,700
Pre-show: Dolph Ziggler beat Damien Sandow.
PPV Broadcast: World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio defeated Rob Van Dam in a hardcore match; Jack Swagger and Antonio Cesaro downed The Great Khali and Santino Marella; WWE Intercontinental Champion Curtis Axel toppled R-Truth; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee overturned Brie Bella; Cody Rhodes and Goldust dropped WWE World Tag Champions Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns in a non-title match; Bray Wyatt overcame Kofi Kingston; CM Punk bested Ryback; Randy Orton battled Daniel Bryan to a no-contest.

27th October 2013: “WWE Hell in a Cell 2013”: Miami, FL – 9,000
($500,000 live gate)
Pre-show: Damien Sandow defeated Kofi Kingston.
PPV Broadcast: WWE World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Goldust won a three-way over Jimmy and Jey Uso, and Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns; Fandango and Summer Rae downed The Great Khali and Natalya; Big E Langston overturned Dean Ambrose by count-out; CM Punk pinned Ryback in a “Hell in a Cell” match; Los Matadores dropped Antonio Cesaro and Jack Swagger; World Heavyweight Champion John Cena bested Alberto Del Rio; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee overturned Brie Bella; Randy Orton rolled over Daniel Bryan in a “Hell in a Cell” match to win the vacant WWE World Title. Shawn Michaels was guest referee.

24th November 2013: “WWE Survivor Series 2013”: Boston, MA – 13,500 sellout (12,000 paid)
Pre-show: The Miz pinned Kofi Kingston.
PPV Broadcast: Roman Reigns, Seth Rollins, WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose, Jack Swagger and Antonio Cesaro defeated Rey Mysterio, Jimmy Uso, Jey Uso, and WWE World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Goldust; WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E Langston dropped Curtis Axel; Natalya, Nikki Bella, Brie Bella, Cameron, Naomi, Eva Marie, and Jo Jo overcame WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee, Tamina Snuka, Kaitlyn, Summer Rae, Rosa Mendes, Alicia Fox, and Aksana; Mark Henry overturned Ryback; World Heavyweight Champion John Cena bested Alberto Del Rio; Daniel Bryan and CM Punk toppled Luke Harper and Erick Rowan; WWE World Champion Randy Orton rolled over The Big Show.

15th December 2013: “WWE TLC 2013”: Houston, TX – 12,000 sellout
Pre-show: Fandango beat Dolph Ziggler.
PPV Broadcast: CM Punk bested Seth Rollins, Roman Reigns, and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose in a handicap match; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee downed Natalya; WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E Langston downed Damien Sandow; WWE World Tag Champions Goldust and Cody Rhodes won a four-way over Rey Mysterio and The Big Show, Jack Swagger and Antonio Cesaro, and Ryback and Curtis Axel; R-Truth pinned Brodus Clay; Kofi Kingston overcame The Miz in a no-DQ encounter; Bray Wyatt, Luke Harper, and Erick Rowan toppled Daniel Bryan in a handicap match; WWE World Champion Randy Orton rolled over World Heavyweight Champion John Cena in a TLC match to unify the titles.
