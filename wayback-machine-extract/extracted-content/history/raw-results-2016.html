Raw Results 2016

WWE RAW RESULTS (2016)

Jan 2016

Air Date: 4th January 2016
Kevin Owens overturned Neville; Titus O’Neil defeated Stardust; Becky Lynch defeated WWE Divas Champion Charlotte in a non-title match; The Big Show battled Ryback to a no-contest; WWE US Champion Alberto del Río and Rusev defeated Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso; Heath Slater rolled over Dolph Ziggler; Big E, Kofi Kingston and Xavier Woods overturned The Dudley Boys and Kalisto; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Roman Reigns defeated Sheamus. Vince McMahon was guest referee.

Air Date: 11th January 2016
WWE Intercontinental Champion Dean Ambrose battled Sheamus to a double count-out; Titus O’Neil beat Stardust; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso toppled Big E and Kofi Kingston; Heath Slater, Bo Dallas, Adam Rose and Curtis Axel battled Bray Wyatt, Braun Strowman, Erick Rowan and Luke Harper to a no-contest; Kalisto pinned WWE US Champion Alberto Del Rio to win the title; Stardust, Viktor, Konnor, Big E, Xavier Woods, Kofi Kingston, Erick Rowan, Braun Strowman, Luke Harper, Bray Wyatt, Kevin Owens, Alberto del Río, Sheamus and Tyler Breeze battled WWE World Heavyweight Champion Roman Reigns to a no-contest.

Air Date: 18th January 2016
Roman Reigns beat Rusev. Chris Jericho was guest referee; Natalya downed Brie Bella; Bray Wyatt, Erick Rowan and Luke Harper rolled over The Dudley Boys and Ryback; The Big Show defeated Heath Slater; Becky Lynch rolled over Tamina Snuka; WWE US Champion Alberto del Río and Sheamus beat Kalisto and WWE Intercontinental Champion Dean Ambrose; Big E defeated Jey Uso; Mark Henry, Neville, R-Truth and Titus O’Neil overcame Viktor, Konnor, Tyler Breeze and Stardust.

Air Date: 25th January 2016
Kevin Owens beat Dolph Ziggler; The Dudley Boys defeated Bo Dallas and Curtis Axel; AJ Styles beat Chris Jericho; Becky Lynch battled Sasha Banks to a no-contest; Bray Wyatt beat Kane; Paige and Natalya downed Brie Bella and Alicia Fox; Kalisto bested The Miz; Roman Reigns and WWE Intercontinental Champion Dean Ambrose dropped Sheamus and Rusev.

Feb 2016

Air Date: 1st February 2016
WWE US Champion Kalisto bested Rusev by count-out; Jey Uso and Jimmy Uso bested Curtis Axel and Adam Rose; Brie Bella downed WWE Divas Champion Charlotte in a non-title match; The Big Show defeated Erick Rowan; Titus O’Neil beat Tyler Breeze; Dolph Ziggler rolled over Kevin Owens; Sasha Banks overturned Becky Lynch by DQ; Roman Reigns and WWE Intercontinental Champion Dean Ambrose dropped Big E and Kofi Kingston.

Air Date: 8th February 2016
Dolph Ziggler rolled over Kevin Owens; WWE Divas Champion Charlotte downed Alicia Fox; Bray Wyatt defeated Ryback; Adam Rose downed Titus O’Neil; Alberto del Rio and Rusev defeated Sin Cara and Kalisto; Tamina Snuka beat Becky Lynch; Jimmy Uso, Jey Uso, Bubba Ray Dudley and D-Von Dudley defeated Big E, Kofi Kingston, Xavier Woods and Mark Henry in a tables match.

Air Date: 15th February 2016
Kevin Owens overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Dean Ambrose, Dolph Ziggler, Stardust and Tyler Breeze in a five-way to win the title; Big E defeated Mark Henry; AJ Styles overturned The Miz; Summer Rae downed Paige; Heath Slater rolled over Zack Ryder; Alberto del Río, Sheamus and Rusev defeated Sin Cara, WWE US Champion Kalisto and Neville; Becky Lynch rolled over Naomi; The Big Show defeated Braun Strowman by DQ.

Air Date: 22nd February 2016
Kofi Kingston, Big E and Xavier Woods overturned Neville, Sin Cara and WWE US Champion Kalisto; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso overturned The Ascension; AJ Styles and Chris Jericho bested Heath Slater and Curtis Axel; Bray Wyatt, Erick Rowan and Luke Harper overcame Ryback, Kane and The Big Show; Sasha Banks overturned Naomi; Roman Reigns overturned Sheamus by count-out.

Air Date: 29th February 2016
Becky Lynch battled to a double pinfall draw with Sasha Banks; The Miz beat Dolph Ziggler; Sheamus and Rusev beat Sin Cara and Kalisto; Ryback beat Adam Rose; AJ Styles and Chris Jericho bested Big E and Kofi Kingston; Bubba Ray Dudley pinned Jey Uso; The Big Show beat WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens by count-out; Naomi dropped Brie Bella; Dean Ambrose battled to a no-contest with Alberto Del Rio.

Mar 2016

Air Date: 7th March 2016
WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens overturned Neville; Summer Rae downed Brie Bella; Sheamus, Rusev and King Barrett beat Dolph Ziggler in a handicap elimination match; Sasha Banks and Becky Lynch rolled over Naomi and Tamina; WWE World Tag Champions Kofi Kingston and Big E beat Chris Jericho and AJ Styles; WWE US Champion Kalisto bested Tyler Breeze; Ryback dropped Curtis Axel; Dean Ambrose downed Bray Wyatt by DQ.

Air Date: 14th March 2016
WWE World Tag Champions Kofi Kingston and Xavier Woods overturned Alberto Del Rio and Rusev; Ryback dropped Sin Cara; Sami Zayn toppled The Miz; Naomi and Tamina defeated Brie Bella and Alicia Fox; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso overturned Bo Dallas and Adam Rose; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Triple H beat Dolph Ziggler; Neville downed Chris Jericho by DQ.

Air Date: 21st March 2016
WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens overturned AJ Styles; Big E beat Rusev; Chris Jericho bested Fandango; A three-way between Stardust, Sin Cara and Zack Ryder ended in a no contest; WWE Divas Champion Charlotte downed Natalya; Bubba Ray Dudley beat R-Truth; Braun Strowman toppled Dean Ambrose by DQ.

Air Date: 28th March 2016
Zack Ryder overcame Chris Jericho; WWE Divas Champion Charlotte downed Becky Lynch; The Big Show and Kane downed Curtis Axel and Bo Dallas by DQ; Kofi Kingston toppled Alberto Del Rio; WWE US Champion Kalisto bested Konnor; Emma defeated Paige; Sami Zayn, Dolph Ziggler and Sin Cara defeated WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens, Stardust and The Miz.

Apr 2016

Air Date: 4th April 2016
WWE World Tag Champions Big E and Kofi Kingston toppled King Barrett and Sheamus; Sasha Banks overturned Summer Rae; Apollo Crews overturned Tyler Breeze; Baron Corbin battled to a double count-out with Dolph Ziggler;  The Miz beat WWE Intercontinental Champion Zack Ryder to win the title; The  Dudley Boys overturned Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso in a cage match; AJ Styles won a four-way over Chris Jericho, Cesaro and Kevin Owens.

Air Date: 11th April 2016
Cesaro bested Kevin Owens; The Dudleys overturned Sin Cara and WWE US Champion Kalisto (WWE World Tag Title #1 Contender Tournament – 1st Round); Natalya defeated WWE Women’s Champion Charlotte by DQ; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso overturned Heath Slater and Curtis Axel (WWE World Tag Title #1 Contender Tournament – 1st Round); AJ Styles overturned Sami Zayn; Apollo Crews overturned Adam Rose; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Roman Reigns and Bray Wyatt beat Sheamus and Alberto Del Rio.

Air Date: 18th April 2016
Chris Jericho bested Sami Zayn; Enzo Amore and Big Cass overturned The Dudley Boys (WWE World Tag Title #1 Contender Tournament – Semi-Final); Baron Corbin toppled Fandango; Kofi Kingston, Xavier Woods, Big E and Cesaro bested Sheamus, Alberto Del Rio, Rusev and WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz; Paige, Sasha Banks, Natalya and Becky Lynch rolled over Naomi, Tamina, WWE Women’s Champion Charlotte and Summer Rae; Aiden English and Simon Gotch rolled over Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso (WWE World Tag Title #1 Contender Tournament – Semi-Final); Apollo Crews overturned Heath Slater; Dean Ambrose downed Kevin Owens.

Air Date: 25th April 2016
AJ Styles overturned Sheamus; Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows overturned Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso; Sami Zayn overcame Rusev; Apollo Crews overturned Stardust; Natalya beat Emma; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Roman Reigns overturned Alberto del Río.

May 2016

Air Date: 2nd May 2016
Cesaro bested Kevin Owens by DQ; Tyler Breeze downed Goldust; Big E, Xavier Woods, Kofi Kingston and Big Cass overturned Aiden English, Simon Gotch, D-Von Dudley and Bubba Ray Dudley; Emma beat Becky Lynch; Rusev won a battle royal; AJ Styles, Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows overturned WWE World Heavyweight Champion Roman Reigns, Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso.

Air Date: 9th May 2016
Baron Corbin overcame Dolph Ziggler; R-Truth defeated Fandango; Paige downed WWE Women’s Champion Charlotte in a non-title match; Sami Zayn overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz in a non-title match; Sin Cara beat Rusev; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Roman Reigns, Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso bested AJ Styles, Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows by DQ in an elimination match; Kevin Owens overturned Zack Ryder; Bubba Ray Dudley and D-Von Dudley toppled Big E and Kofi Kingston.

Air Date: 16th May 2016
Cesaro battled Sami Zayn to a no-contest; Sami Zayn and Kevin Owens overturned Cesaro and WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz; Primo and Epico bested Brian Kennedy and Scott Jackson; Dana Brooke downed Becky Lynch; Tyler Breeze and Fandango bested Goldust and R-Truth; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso bested Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows; Big Cass overturned D-Von Dudley; Alberto del Río bested WWE US Champion Kalisto in a non-title match.

Air Date: 23rd May 2016
Sami Zayn overcame Sheamus (MITB Qualifier); Kofi Kingston, Big E and Xavier Woods overturned Heath Slater, Curtis Axel and Bo Dallas; Cesaro bested WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz in a non-title match (MITB Qualifier); Chris Jericho bested Apollo Crews (MITB Qualifier); Big Cass overturned Bubba Ray Dudley; Dean Ambrose downed Dolph Ziggler (MITB Qualifier); Kevin Owens overturned AJ Styles (MITB Qualifier).

Air Date: 30th May 2016
Kofi Kingston and Big E downed Aiden English and Simon Gotch by DQ; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso bested Fandango and Tyler Breeze; WWE US Champion Rusev defeated Zack Ryder; Enzo Amore and Big Cass overturned Bubba Ray Dudley and D-Von Dudley; Dana Brooke downed Natalya; Baron Corbin overcame Dolph Ziggler by DQ; Cesaro, Sami Zayn and Dean Ambrose downed Alberto del Río, Kevin Owens and Chris Jericho.

Jun 2016

Air Date: 13th June 2016

Air Date: 20th June 2016
Sami Zayn overcame Kevin Owens; Big Cass and Enzo Amore downed Aiden English and Simon Gotch; John Cena beat Karl Anderson by DQ; Baron Corbin overcame Zack Ryder; WWE Women’s Champion Charlotte downed Paige; Roman Reigns battled Seth Rollins to a double count-out.

Air Date: 27th June 2016
Paige and Sasha Banks overturned WWE Women’s Champion Charlotte and Dana Brooke; Titus O’Neil defeated WWE US Champion Rusev by count-out; Seth Rollins overturned John Cena; Big Cass and Enzo Amore downed Carlos Hershey and Mitch Walden; Kane downed WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz by count-out; Apollo Crews and Cesaro bested Sheamus and Alberto del Río; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Dean Ambrose downed AJ Styles.

Air Date: 25th July 2016
Finn Balor rolled over Cesaro, Kevin Owens and WWE US Champion Rusev in a four-way; Nia Jax defeated Britt Baker; Roman Reigns overturned Chris Jericho, Sami Zayn and Sheamus in a four-way; Neville downed Curtis Axel; Sasha Banks overturned WWE Women’s Champion Charlotte to win the title; Braun Strowman overcame James Ellsworth; Enzo Amore and Big Cass overturned Primo and Epico; Finn Balor rolled over Roman Reigns.

Air Date: 31st October 2016

Nia Jax defeated Bayley; Sheamus and Cesaro bested Primo and Epico; WWE US Champion Roman Reigns defeated Chris Jericho by DQ.

Nov 2016

Air Date: 14th November 2016

Air Date: 28th November 2016
