WWE PPV Results 2010

WWE PPV RESULTS (2010)

See also: WWE PPV Statistics 2010

31st January 2010: “WWE Royal Rumble 2010”: Atlanta, GA – 16,697 sellout (13,500 paid)
($800,000 live gate)
Dark match: Kelly Kelly, Eve, Gail Kim, Brie and Nikki Bella defeated Maryse, Natalya Neidhart, Jillian Hall, Katie Lea and Alicia Fox.
PPV Broadcast: ECW Champion Christian bested Ezekiel Jackson; WWE US Champion The Miz downed MVP; WWE World Champion Sheamus rolled over Randy Orton by DQ; Mickie James overcame WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool to win the title; World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker pinned Rey Mysterio; Edge won the 30-man Royal Rumble, by last eliminating John Cena.

21st February 2010: “WWE Elimination Chamber 2010”: St Louis, MO – 17,628 sellout (14,000 paid)
($850,000 live gate)
Dark match: Christian bested Ezekiel Jackson
PPV Broadcast: John Cena defeated WWE World Champion Sheamus, Kofi Kingston, Randy Orton, Ted Dibiase Jr and Triple H in an Elimination Chamber match to win the title; Batista pinned WWE World Champion John Cena in an impromptu match to win the title;  WWE Intercontinental Champion Drew McIntyre downed Kane; Michelle McCool and Layla dropped Gail Kim and Maryse; WWE US Champion The Miz toppled MVP; Chris Jericho rolled over World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker, Rey Mysterio, R-Truth, John Morrison and CM Punk in an Elimination Chamber match to win the title.

28th March 2010: “WWE Wrestlemania 26”: Glendale, AZ – 61,093 (55,000 paid)
($5,529,172 live gate)
Dark match: Yoshi Tatsu won a 26-man battle royal.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Unified World Tag Champions The Miz and The Big Show overturned John Morrison and R-Truth; Randy Orton defeated Ted Dibiase Jr and Cody Rhodes in a Triple Threat; Jack Swagger won the Money in the Bank match over WWE Intercontinental Champion Drew McIntyre, Kane, Christian, MVP, Kofi Kingston, Matt Hardy, Evan Bourne, Shelton Benjamin and Dolph Ziggler; Triple H downed Sheamus; Rey Mysterio pinned CM Punk; Bret Hart toppled Vince McMahon in a “No Holds Barred” match; World Heavyweight Champion Chris Jericho downed Edge; WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool, WWE Divas Champion Maryse, Vickie Guerrero, Alicia Fox and Layla defeated Beth Phoenix, Eve, Mickie James, Kelly Kelly and Gail Kim; John Cena bested WWE World Champion Batista to win the title; The Undertaker rolled over Shawn Michaels in a no-DQ, no-countout “Career vs. Streak” main event.

25th April 2010: “WWE Extreme Rules 2010”: Baltimore, MD – 9,000 sellout
($550,000 live gate)
Dark match: Kofi Kingston downed Dolph Ziggler.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Unified World Tag Champions The Miz and The Big Show bested John Morrison and R-Truth by DQ; WWE Unified World Tag Champions The Miz and The Big Show downed MVP and Mark Henry; DH Smith and Tyson Kidd overcame WWE Unified World Tag Champions The Miz and The Big Show in a non-title match; CM Punk dropped Rey Mysterio; JTG beat Shad Gaspard in a strap match; World Heavyweight Champion Jack Swagger toppled Randy Orton in an Extreme Rules match; Sheamus downed Triple H in a streetfight; Beth Phoenix pinned WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool in an extreme makeover match to win the title; Edge bested Chris Jericho in a cage match; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Batista in a last man standing match.

23rd May 2010: “WWE Over The Limit 2010”: Detroit, MI – 11,000
($675,000 live gate)
Dark match: MVP beat Chavo Guerrero Jr.
PPV Broadcast: Kofi Kingston pinned WWE Intercontinental Champion Drew McIntyre to win the title; R-Truth defeated Ted Dibiase Jr; Rey Mysterio downed CM Punk in a “SES membership vs. Hair” encounter; WWE Unified World Tag Champions DH Smith and Tyson Kidd dropped The Miz and Chris Jericho; Randy Orton battled Edge to a double count-out; The Big Show bested World Heavyweight Champion Jack Swagger by DQ; WWE Divas Champion Eve Torres toppled Maryse; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Batista in an “I Quit” match.

20th June 2010: “WWE Fatal Four Way 2010”: Uniondale, NY – 10,000
($650,000 live gate)
Dark match: Zack Ryder beat MVP.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston defeated Drew McIntyre; Alicia Fox downed WWE Divas Champion Eve Torres, Gail Kim, and Maryse in a fatal four-way to win the title; Evan Bourne dropped Chris Jericho; Rey Mysterio bested World Heavyweight Champion Jack Swagger, The Big Show, and MVP in a fatal four-way to win the title; WWE US Champion The Miz toppled R-Truth; WWE Unified World Tag Champions DH Smith and Tyson Kidd, and Natalya overcame Jimmy and Jey Uso, and Tamina Snuka; Sheamus rolled over WWE World Champion John Cena, Randy Orton, and Edge in a four-way to win the title.

18th July 2010: “WWE Money in the Bank 2010”: Kansas City, KS – 8,000
($550,000 live gate)
Dark match: Santino Marella beat William Regal.
PPV Broadcast: Kane won the Smackdown “Money in the Bank” ladder match over The Big Show, Dolph Ziggler, Matt Hardy, Christian, Drew McIntyre, Cody Rhodes, and Kofi Kingston; WWE Divas Champion Alicia Fox pinned Eve Torres; WWE Unified World Tag Champions DH Smith and Tyson Kidd downed Jimmy and Jey Uso; World Heavyweight Champion Rey Mysterio dropped Jack Swagger; Kane pinned World Heavyweight Champion Rey Mysterio to win the title; WWE Women’s Champion Layla overcame Kelly Kelly; WWE US Champion The Miz won the Raw “Money in the Bank” ladder match over Mark Henry, Evan Bourne, Chris Jericho, Edge, Ted Dibiase Jr, John Morrison, and Randy Orton; WWE World Champion Sheamus rolled over John Cena in a cage match.

15th August 2010: “WWE Summerslam 2010”: Los Angeles, CA – 14,178 sellout
($922,600 live gate)
Dark match: Evan Bourne beat Zack Ryder.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler battled Kofi Kingston to a no-contest; Melina defeated WWE Divas Champion Alicia Fox to win the title; The Big Show downed CM Punk, Luke Gallows and Joey Mercury in a handicap match; Randy Orton bested WWE World Champion Sheamus by DQ; World Heavyweight Champion Kane toppled Rey Mysterio; John Cena, Daniel Bryan, Chris Jericho, Edge, R-Truth, John Morrison and Bret Hart rolled over Wade Barrett, David Otunga, Heath Slater, Skip Sheffield, Darren Young, Michael Tarver and Justin Gabriel in an elimination match.

19th September 2010: “WWE Night of Champions 2010”: Chicago, IL – 12,500 sellout (11,000 paid)
($782,205 live gate)
Dark match: John Morrison beat Ted Dibiase Jr.
PPV Broadcast WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler defeated Kofi Kingston; The Big Show pinned CM Punk; Daniel Bryan bested WWE US Champion The Miz to win the title; WWE Women’s Champion  Michelle McCool (a permitted substitute for Layla) toppled WWE Diva’s Champion Melina to unify the titles in a lumberjill match; World Heavyweight Champion Kane overturned The Undertaker; Drew McIntyre and Cody Rhodes won a gauntlet match to win the WWE Unified World Tag Titles: Jimmy and Jey Uso beat Tyson Kidd and DH Smith, Jimmy and Jey Uso beat Santino Marella and Vladimir Kozlov, Evan Bourne and Mark Henry beat Jimmy and Jey Uso, Drew McIntyre and Cody Rhodes beat Mark Henry and Evan Bourne; Randy Orton rolled over WWE World Champion Sheamus, John Cena, Wade Barrett, Chris Jericho and Edge in a six-way elimination match to win the title.

3rd October 2010: “WWE Hell in a Cell 2010”: Dallas, TX – 7,500
Dark match: Goldust, Kofi Kingston and R-Truth beat WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler and WWE Unified World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Drew McIntyre.
PPV Broadcast: WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan won a submissions-only “falls-count-anywhere” three-way over The Miz and John Morrison; WWE World Champion Randy Orton bested Sheamus in a “Hell-in-a-Cell” match;  Edge toppled Jack Swagger; Wade Barrett dropped John Cena; Natalya overcame WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool by DQ; World Heavyweight Kane rolled over The Undertaker in a “Hell-in-a-Cell” encounter

24th October 2010: “WWE Bragging Rights 2010”: Minneapolis, MN – 7,702
($495,766 live gate)
Dark match: MVP beat Chavo Guerrero.
PPV Broadcast: WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler in a non-title match; John Cena and David Otunga bested WWE Unified World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Drew McIntyre to win the titles; Ted Dibiase Jr downed Goldust; WWE Divas Champion Layla (a permitted substitute for Michelle McCool) dropped Natalya; World Heavyweight Champion Kane overturned The Undertaker in a “Buried Alive” match; Alberto Del Rio, Edge, Jack Swagger, Kofi Kingston, Rey Mysterio, The Big Show and Tyler Reks overturned CM Punk, Ezekiel Jackson, John Morrison, R-Truth, Santino Marella, Sheamus and The Miz in an elimination match; Wade Barrett rolled over WWE World Champion Randy Orton by DQ.

21st November 2010: “WWE Survivor Series 2010”: Miami, FL – 8,000
Dark match: R-Truth beat Zack Ryder.
PPV Broadcast: WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan bested Ted Dibiase Jr; John Morrison pinned Sheamus; WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler dropped Kaval; Rey Mysterio Jr, the Big Show, Kofi Kingston, MVP and Chris Masters rolled over Alberto Del Rio, Cody Rhodes, Drew McIntyre, Jack Swagger and Tyler Reks in a Survivors Elimination match; Natalya Neidhart overcame WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool and WWE Divas Champion Layla to win the title in a handicap match; Edge battled World Heavyweight Champion Kane to a draw; WWE Tag Team Champions Heath Slater and Justin Gabriel dropped Santino Marella and Vladimir Kozlov; WWE World Champion Randy Orton toppled Wade Barrett in a No DQ, no count-out match. John Cena was guest referee.

19th December 2010: “WWE TLC 2010”: Houston, TX – 11,500
Dark match: WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan beat Ted Dibiase Jr.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler defeated Kofi Kingston and Jack Swagger in a Triple Threat ladder match; WWE Divas Champion Natalya Neidhart and Beth Phoenix downed Michelle McCool and Layla in a tables match; WWE World Tag Champions Santino Marella and Vladimir Kozlov overcame Justin Gabriel and Heath Slater by DQ; John Morrison downed Sheamus in a ladder match; WWE World Champion The Miz bested Randy Orton in a tables match; Edge toppled World Heavyweight Champion Kane, Rey Mysterio and Alberto Del Rio in a Fatal Four Way TLC match to win the title; John Cena rolled over Wade Barrett in a chairs encounter.
