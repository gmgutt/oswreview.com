WWE PPV Results 2007

WWE PPV RESULTS (2007)

See also: WWE PPV Statistics 2007

7th January 2007: “WWE New Year’s Revolution 2007”: Kansas City, MO – 10,000
($560,000 live gate)
Dark match: Vladimir Kozlov beat Eugene.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Johnny Nitro in a cage match; Cryme Tyme overcame Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas, Robbie and Rory McAllister, and Super Crazy and Jim Duggan in a “tag team turmoil” match; Kenny Dykstra overturned Ric Flair; WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James dropped Victoria; Triple H and Shawn Michaels battled WWE World Tag Champions Edge and Randy Orton to a no-contest; Chris Masters toppled Carlito; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Umaga.

28th January 2007: “WWE Royal Rumble 2007”: San Antonio, TX – 13,500 sellout
($780,000 live gate)
Dark match: JTG beat Lance Cade.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy and Matt Hardy downed Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury; ECW Champion Bobby Lashley toppled Test by count-out; World Heavyweight Champion Batista rolled over Mr Kennedy; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Umaga in a “last man standing” match; The Undertaker won the 30-man Royal Rumble by last eliminating Shawn Michaels.

18th February 2007: “WWE No Way Out 2007”: Los Angeles, CA – 14,000 sellout
($750,000 live gate)
Dark match: Rob Van Dam downed Shelton Benjamin.
PPV Broadcast: WWE US Champion Chris Benoit, WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy, and Matt Hardy downed MVP, Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury; Chavo Guerrero Jr won a 8-man gauntlet match to win the WWE Cruiserweight title: Scotty 2 Hotty pinned Daivari, Gregory Helms downed Scotty 2 Hotty, Gregory Helms pinned Sho Funaki, Gregory Helms pinned Shannon Moore, Jimmy Wang Yang pinned Gregory Helms,  Jimmy Wang Yang pinned Jamie Noble, Chavo Guerrero Jr pinned Jimmy Wang Yang; Finlay and Hornswoggle beat The Boogeyman and Little Boogeyman; Kane bested King Booker; WWE Tag Champions Paul London and Brian Kendrick overcame Deuce and Domino; Mr Kennedy dropped ECW Champion Bobby Lashley by DQ; WWE World Tag Champions John Cena and Shawn Michaels toppled World Heavyweight Champion Batista and The Undertaker.

1st April 2007: “WWE Wrestlemania 23”: Detroit, MI – 74,687 sellout (66,670 paid)
($5,380,000 live gate)
Dark match: Ric Flair and Carlito beat WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr and Gregory Helms in a lumberjack match.
PPV Broadcast: Mr Kennedy won a “Money in the Bank” ladder match over King Booker, CM Punk, Edge, Randy Orton, Finlay, Jeff Hardy and Matt Hardy; The Great Khali toppled Kane; WWE US Champion Chris Benoit dropped MVP; The Undertaker rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Batista to win the title; Rob Van Dam, Sabu, The Sandman, and Tommy Dreamer defeated Elijah Burke, Marcus Cor Von, Matt Striker, and Kevin Thorn; ECW Champion Bobby Lashley toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Umaga in a non-title “Donald Trump’s hair vs. Vince McMahon’s hair” match. Steve Austin was guest referee; WWE Women’s Champion Melina pinned Ashley in a lumberjill match; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Shawn Michaels.

29th April 2007: “WWE Backlash 2007”: Atlanta, GA – 14,000 sellout (13,000 paid)
($650,000 live gate)
Dark match: Carlito beat Johnny Nitro.
PPV Broadcast: WWE World Tag Champions Matt and Jeff Hardy downed Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch; WWE Women’s Champion Melina pinned Mickie James; WWE US Champion Chris Benoit dropped MVP; Umaga, Vince McMahon and Shane McMahon defeated ECW Champion Bobby Lashley in a handicap match. By virtue of a pre-match stipulation, Vince won the ECW Title; World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker battled Batista to a draw in a “last man standing” match; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Randy Orton, Shawn Michaels, and Edge in a four-way.

20th May 2007: “WWE Judgment Day 2007”: St Louis, MO – 10,500 (9,399 paid)
($575,000 live gate)
Dark match: Kane bested William Regal.
PPV Broadcast: Ric Flair bested Carlito; Bobby Lashley toppled ECW Champion Vince McMahon, Shane McMahon, and Umaga in a handicap match; CM Punk overturned Elijah Burke; Randy Orton dropped Shawn Michaels by stoppage; WWE World Tag Champions Matt and Jeff Hardy downed Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch; World Heavyweight Champion Edge pinned Batista; MVP defeated WWE US Champion Chris Benoit in a “best of three falls” match to win the title;  WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over The Great Khali.

3rd June 2007: “WWE One Night Stand 2007”: Jacksonville, FL – 7,000 
($450,000 live gate)
Dark match: WWE Intercontinental Champion Santino Marella beat Chris Masters.
PPV Broadcast: Rob Van Dam toppled Randy Orton in a stretcher match; CM Punk, Tommy Dreamer, and The Sandman dropped Elijah Burke, Marcus Cor Von, and Matt Striker in a tables match; WWE World Tag Champions Matt and Jeff Hardy downed Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas in a ladder match; Mark Henry defeated Kane in a lumberjack match; Bobby Lashley overcame ECW Champion Vince McMahon in a streetfight; Candice Michelle overturned WWE Women’s Champion Melina in a non-title “pudding” match; World Heavyweight Champion Edge bested Batista in a cage match; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over The Great Khali in a “falls-count-anywhere” match.

24th June 2007: “WWE Vengeance: Night of Champions 2007”: Houston, TX – 15,000 sellout
($800,000 live gate)
Dark match: Super Crazy downed Carlito.
PPV Broadcast: WWE World Tag Champions Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch defeated Matt and Jeff Hardy; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr pinned Jimmy Wang Yang; Johnny Nitro bested CM Punk to win the vacant ECW title; WWE Intercontinental Champion Santino Marella downed Umaga by DQ; WWE US Champion MVP toppled Ric Flair; WWE Tag Champions Deuce and Domino overcame Jimmy Snuka and Sgt. Slaughter; World Heavyweight Champion Edge overturned Batista by count-out; Candice Michelle pinned WWE Women’s Champion Melina to win the title; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over King Booker, Mick Foley, Randy Orton, and Bobby Lashley in a five-way.

22nd July 2007: “WWE Great American Bash 2007”: San Jose, CA – 13,034 sellout (11,398 paid)
($761,410 live gate)
Dark match: Chuck Palumbo defeated Chris Masters.
PPV Broadcast: WWE US Champion MVP overturned Matt Hardy; Hornswoggle defeated WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr, Jamie Noble, Shannon Moore, Sho Funaki, and Jimmy Wang Yang inn a six-way to win the title; Carlito beat The Sandman in a “singapore cane-on-a-pole” match; WWE Women’s Champion Candice Michelle downed Melina; WWE Intercontinental Champion Umaga dropped Jeff Hardy; ECW Champion John Morrison bested CM Punk; Randy Orton pinned Dusty Rhodes in a bullrope match; World Heavyweight Champion The Great Khali bested Batista and Kane in a three-way; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Bobby Lashley.

26th August 2007: “WWE Summerslam 2007”: East Rutherford, NJ – 16,000 sellout (14,000 paid)
($1,000,000 live gate)
Dark match: WWE World Tag Champions Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch defeated Paul London and Brian Kendrick.
PPV Broadcast: Kane bested Finlay; WWE Intercontinental Champion Umaga won a three-way over Mr Kennedy and Carlito; Rey Mysterio Jr toppled Chavo Guerrero Jr; Beth Phoenix won a battle royal; ECW Champion John Morrison overturned CM Punk; Triple H bested King Booker; Batista rolled over World Heavyweight Champion The Great Khali by DQ; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Randy Orton.

16th September 2007: “WWE Unforgiven 2007”: Memphis, TN – 12,000
($550,000 live gate)
Dark match: Kane bested Kenny Dykstra.
PPV Broadcast: ECW Champion CM Punk overturned Elijah Burke; WWE Tag Champions MVP and Matt Hardy downed Deuce and Domimo; Triple H pinned Carlito in a “no-DQ for Carlito” match; WWE Women’s Champion Candice Michelle beat Beth Phoenix; Batista rolled over World Heavyweight Champion The Great Khali and Rey Mysterio in a three-way to win the title; WWE World Tag Champions Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch overturned Paul London and Brian Kendrick; Randy Orton bested WWE World Champion John Cena by DQ; The Undertaker rolled over Mark Henry.

7th October 2007: “WWE No Mercy 2007”: Chicago, IL – 12,500 sellout
($700,000 live gate)
Dark match: Hardcore Holly dropped Cody Rhodes.
PPV Broadcast: Triple H pinned WWE World Champion Randy Orton to win the title; Mr Kennedy, and WWE World Tag Champions Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy, Paul London and Brian Kendrick; ECW Champion CM Punk overturned Big Daddy V by DQ; WWE World Champion Triple H bested Umaga; Rey Mysterio battled Finlay to a no-contest; Beth Phoenix pinned WWE Women’s Champion Candice Michelle to win the title; World Heavyweight Champion Batista rolled over The Great Khali in a “Punjabi Prison” match; Randy Orton rolled over WWE World Champion Triple H in a “last man standing” match to win the title.

28th October 2007: “WWE Cyber Sunday 2007”: Washington, DC – 10,097
($594,000 live gate)
Dark match: Jesse and Festus overturned Deuce and Domino.
PPV Broadcast: Rey Mysterio toppled Finlay in a stretcher match; ECW Champion CM Punk overturned The Miz; Mr Kennedy dropped WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy in a non-title match; Kane bested WWE US Champion MVP by count-out; Shawn Michaels toppled WWE World Champion Randy Orton by DQ; Triple H pinned Umaga in a streetfight; World Heavyweight Champion Batista rolled over The Undertaker. Steve Austin was guest referee.

 

18th November 2007: “WWE Survivor Series 2007”: Miami, FL – 12,500
($750,000 live gate)
PPV Broadcast: ECW Champion CM Punk overturned John Morrison and The Miz in a three-way; Maria, Torrie Wilson, Michelle McCool, Kelly Kelly, and Mickie James dropped WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix, Layla, Melina, Jillian Hall, and Victoria; WWE World Tag Champions Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch overturned Hardcore Holly and Cody Rhodes; Triple H, WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy, Kane, and Rey Mysterio toppled Umaga, Mr Kennedy, Big Daddy V, Finlay, and WWE US Champion MVP in a handicap “Survivor Series” elimination match; The Great Khali overturned Hornswoggle by DQ;WWE World Champion Randy Orton bested Shawn Michaels; World Heavyweight Champion Batista rolled over The Undertaker in a “Hell in a Cell” match.

16th December 2007: “WWE Armageddon 2007”: Pittsburgh, PA – 12,500 sellout (11,000 paid)
($675,000 live gate)
Dark match: Jesse and Festus overturned WWE Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz in a non-title match.
PPV Broadcast: Rey Mysterio toppled WWE US Champion MVP by count-out; Big Daddy V and Mark Henry dropped ECW Champion CM Punk and Kane; Shawn Michaels toppled Mr Kennedy; WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Triple H; Finlay overturned The Great Khali; Chris Jericho bested WWE World Champion Randy Orton by DQ; WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix overturned Mickie James; Edge toppled World Heavyweight Champion Batista and The Undertaker to win the title in a no-DQ three-way.
