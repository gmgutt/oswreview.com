NXT Results 2013

NXT RESULTS (2013)

Jan 2013

Air Date: 2nd January 2013
Garrett Dylan battled Denton Blackwell to a no-contest; Bo Dallas defeated Epico; Tamina Snuka downed Sasha Banks; Kassius Ohno and Leo Kruger overcame Tyson Kidd and Justin Gabriel; Corey Graves bested WWE NXT Champion Seth Rollins by DQ.

Air Date: 9th January 2013
Luke Harper and Erick Rowan beat Yoshi Tatsu and Percy Watson; Paige defeated Emma; Primo and Epico downed Bo Dallas and Michael McGillicutty; Big E Langston pinned WWE NXT Champion Seth Rollins to win the title.

Air Date: 16th January 2013
Adrian Neville beat Sakamoto; Trent Barreta toppled Leo Kruger by reverse decision-DQ; WWE NXT Champion Big E Langston toppled Camacho; Tyson Kidd and Justin Gabriel rolled over Damien Sandow and WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro.

Air Date: 23rd January 2013
Luke Harper and Erick Rowan downed Yoshi Tatsu and Percy Watson (NXT Tag Title Tournament – First Round); Sasha Banks dropped Alicia Fox; Corey Graves bested Jake Carter; Adrian Neville and Oliver Grey overcame Heath Slater and Drew McIntyre (NXT Tag Title Tournament – First Round).

Air Date: 30th January 2013
Kassius Ohno and Leo Kruger toppled Alex Riley and Derrick Bateman (NXT Tag Title Tournament – First Round);  Mason Ryan defeated Sakamoto; Paige overturned Aksana; Bo Dallas and Michael McGillicutty bested Primo and Epico (NXT Tag Title Tournament – First Round).

Feb 2013

Air Date: 6th February 2013
Oliver Grey and Adrian Neville downed Kassius Ohno and Leo Kruger (NXT Tag Title Tournament – Semi-Final); Conor O’Brien overcame Mike Dalton; Luke Harper and Erick Rowan rolled over Bo Dallas and Michael McGillicutty (NXT Tag Title Tournament – Semi-Final).

Air Date: 13th February 2013
Corey Graves beat Alex Riley; Summer Rae defeated Paige; Adrian Neville and Oliver Grey downed Luke Harper and Erick Rowan to become the first NXT Tag Champions (WWE NXT Tag Title Tournament – Finals).

Air Date: 20th February 2013
Bray Wyatt bested Yoshi Tatsu; Xavier Woods toppled El Local (Ricardo Rodriguez); Audrey Marie pinned Sasha Banks; WWE NXT Champion Big E Langston battled Conor O’Brien to a no-contest.

Air Date: 27th February 2013
Justin Gabriel beat Leo Kruger; Aksana overcame Emma; Conor O’Brien battled Corey Graves to a no-contest.

Mar 2013

Air Date: 6th March 2013
Adrian Neville defeated Judas Devlin and Scott Dawson in a handicap match; Leo Kruger downed Yoshi Tatsu; Naomi, Cameron and Sasha Banks dropped Aksana, Alicia Fox and Audrey Marie; Conor O’Brien overcame Bo Dallas and Corey Graves in a three-way.

Air Date: 13th March 2013
Mason Ryan bested Brandon Traven; Kassius Ohno pinned Derrick Bateman; Summer Rae beat Emma; Bo Dallas toppled Bray Wyatt

Air Date: 20th March 2013
Leo Kruger defeated Justin Gabriel; Paige downed Baylee; Luke Harper dropped Adrian Neville.

Air Date: 27th March 2013
WWE Divas Champion Kaitlyn overcame Natalya; Brodus Clay bested El Local (Ricardo Rodriguez); Randy Orton rolled over Damien Sandow.

Apr 2013

Air Date: 3rd April 2013
Bo Dallas and Adrian Neville defeated Luke Harper and Erick Rowan; Sasha Banks and Paige downed Summer Rae and Audrey Marie; WWE NXT Champion Big E Langston dropped Conor O’Brien.

Air Date: 10th April 2013
Seth Rollins, Roman Reigns and Dean Ambrose bested Percy Watson, Axl Keegan and Scott Dawson; The Bella Twins overcame Naomi and Cameron; William Regal overturned Kassius Ohno.

Air Date: 17th April 2013
WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro defeated Adrian Neville; WWE Divas Champion Kaitlyn downed AJ Lee; WWE NXT Champion Big E Langston dropped Brad Maddox; WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett bested Bo Dallas.

Air Date: 24th April 2013
Leo Kruger beat Justin Gabriel; Emma defeated Bailey; Bray Wyatt downed Yoshi Tatsu; Seth Rollins overcame Corey Graves in a lumberjack match.

May 2013

Air Date: 1st May 2013
Luke Harper and Erick Rowan won a three-way over Bo Dallas and Adrian Neville, and Primo and Epico; Paige defeated Summer Rae; Chris Jericho rolled over Bray Wyatt.

Air Date: 8th May 2013
Damien Sandow beat Baron Corbin; Paige defeated Bailey; Briley Pierce battled Sakamoto to a no-contest; Kassius Ohno downed Camacho; Erick Rowan and Luke Harper dropped WWE NXT Tag Champions Adrian Neville and Bo Dallas to win the titles.

Air Date: 15th May 2013
Summer Rae beat Natalya; Bray Wyatt overcame Danny Burch; Conor O’Brian downed Briley Pierce and Sakamoto in a handicap match; WWE NXT Champion Big E Langston dropped Damien Sandow.

Air Date: 22nd May 2013
Sami Zayn overturned Curt Hawkins; Antonio Cesaro defeated Yoshi Tatsu; Sami Zayn bested Antonio Cesaro; Mason Ryan downed Enzo Amore; Bray Wyatt toppled Corey Graves.

Air Date: 29th May 2013
Erick Rowan and Luke Harper rolled over Travis Tyler and Sawyer Fulton; Emma beat Audrey Marie; Scott Dawson and Garrett Dylan defeated Jake Carter and Brandon Traven; WWE NXT Champion Big E Langston bested Derrick Bateman; Bo Dallas won a battle royal.

Jun 2013

Air Date: 5th June 2013
Paige beat Tamina Snuka (WWE NXT Women’s Title Tournament – First Round); Mason Ryan defeated Colin Casaday; Conor O’Brian downed Alex Riley; WWE NXT Tag Champions Erick Rowan and Luke Harper dropped Corey Graves and Kassius Ohno.

Air Date: 12th June 2013
Antonio Cesaro bested Sami Zayn; Scott Dawson and Garrett Dylan overcame Travis Tyler and Baron Corbin; Alicia Fox defeated Bayley (WWE NXT Women’s Title Tournament – First Round); Bo Dallas bested WWE NXT Champion Big E Langston to win the title.

Air Date: 19th June 2013
Sami Zayn beat Angelo Dawkins; Adrian Neville defeated Bray Wyatt by DQ; Summer Rae toppled Sasha Banks (WWE NXT Women’s Title Tournament – First Round); Xavier Woods overcame Jake Carter; Bray Wyatt, Luke Harper and Erick Rowan bested Adrian Neville, Corey Graves and Kassius Ohno.

Air Date: 26th June 2013
Big E Langston beat Aiden English; Leo Kurger defeated Dante Dash; Bo Dallas downed Mickie Keegan; Emma dropped Aksana(WWE NXT Women’s Title Tournament – First Round);  Corey Graves and Kassius Ohno rolled over Garrett Dylan and Scott Dawson.

Jul 2013

Air Date: 3rd July 2013
Paige beat Alicia Fox (WWE NXT Divas Title Tournament – Semi-Final); Conor O’Brian defeated Andy Baker; Xavier Woods downed Scott Dawson; Mason Ryan dropped Enzo Amore; Mason Ryan overcame Colin Casaday; WWE NXT Champion Bo Dallas toppled Antonio Cesaro.

Air Date: 10th July 2013
Emma defeated Summer Rae (WWE NXT Divas Title Tournament – Semi-Final);  Leo Kruger downed Sami Zayn; Enzo Amore and Colin Casaday dropped Mason Ryan in a handicap match; Bray Wyatt and WWE NXT Tag Champions Luke Harper and Erick Rowan overturned Adrian Neville, Corey Graves and William Regal.

Air Date: 17th July 2013
Leo Kruger won a three-way over Antonio Cesaro and Sami Zayn; WWE NXT Champion Bo Dallas beat Scott Dawson; Charlotte defeated Bayley; Corey Graves and Adrian Neville bested WWE NXT Tag Champions Luke Harper and Erick Rowan to win the titles.

Air Date: 24th July 2013
Paige downed Emma to win the WWE NXT Divas Title (WWE NXT Divas Title Tournament – Final); Tyler Breeze (Mike Dalton) dropped Angelo Hawkins; Rick Victor and Conor O’Brian bested Mickey Keegan and Aiden English; Sheamus rolled over Luke Harper.

Air Date: 31st July 2013
Charlotte beat Sasha Banks; Luke Harper and Erick Rowan defeated Aiden English and Mickey Keegan; Corey Graves downed Scott Dawson; Leo Kruger and Antonio Cesaro bested WWE NXT Champion Bo Dallas and Sami Zayn.

Aug 2013

Air Date: 7th August 2013
Adrian Neville defeated WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose by DQ; Emma downed Summer Rae in a “dance battle”; Tyler Breeze dropped Danny Burch; WWE NXT Champion Bo Dallas overcame Leo Kruger.

Air Date: 14th August 2013
Big E Langston toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Curtis Axel by DQ; Mason Ryan beat Scott Dawson; WWE NXT Divas Champion Paige overcame Summer Rae; WWE World Tag Champions Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns, and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose dropped Corey Graves, Adrian Neville and Xavier Woods.

Air Date: 21st August 2013
WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee overcame Bayley; Conor O’Brian and Rick Victor defeated Mickael Zaki and Ron Hicks; CJ Parker downed Baron Corbin; Dolph Ziggler overturned Alexander Rusev; Antonio Cesaro bested Sami Zayn in a “best-of-three-falls” encounter.

Air Date: 28th August 2013
Tyler Breeze beat CJ Parker; Emma defeated Summer Rae; Scott Dawson downed Enzo Amore; Adrian Neville dropped Conor O’Brian.

Sep 2013

Air Date: 4th September 2013
Bayley and Charlotte beat Alicia Fox and Aksana; Corey Graves defeated Rick Victor; Alexander Rusev overcame Mason Ryan; Jack Swagger bested Sami Zayn.

Air Date: 11th September 2013
Enzo Amore and Colin Casaday overturned Scott Dawson and Alexander Rusev by DQ; WWE NXT Divas Champion Paige beat Sasha Banks; Xavier Woods toppled Leo Kruger.

Air Date: 18th September 2013
Sami Zayn defeated Curt Hawkins; Aiden English downed QT Marshall; Adrian Neville, Corey Graves, CJ Parker and Xavier Woods rolled over Conor O’Brian, Leo Kruger, Rick Victor and Tyler Breeze.

Air Date: 25th September 2013
Conor O’Brien and Rick Victor won a four-way over Colin Cassidy and Enzo Amore, CJ Parker and Tyler Breeze, and Scott Dawson and Alexander Rusev; Sasha Banks toppled Bayley; Aiden English beat Bull Dempsey; Sami Zayn rolled over El Local (Ricardo Rodriguez).

Oct 2013

Air Date: 2nd October 2013
Santino Marella and Emma defeated Fandango and Summer Rae; Luke Harper overcame Kassius Ohno; Conor O’Brien and Rick Victor overcame WWE NXT Tag Champions Adrian Neville and Corey Graves to win the titles; El Local (Sami Zayn) beat WWE NXT Champion Bo Dallas in a non-title match.

Air Date: 9th October 2013
Antonio Cesaro bested Leo Kruger; Charlotte downed Santana Garrett; Mojo Rawley overturned Danny Burch; Alexander Rusev and Scott Dawson rolled over Colin Cassidy and Enzo Amore; Rob Van Dam pinned Aiden English.

Air Date: 16th October 2013
WWE NXT Tag Champions Conor O’Brien and Rick Victor overcame Adrian Neville and Corey Graves; Tyler Breeze downed CJ Parker; Sasha Banks and Summer Rae downed WWE NXT Women’s Champion Paige and Emma; WWE NXT Champion Bo Dallas toppled Sami Zayn.

Air Date: 23rd October 2013
Sasha Banks toppled Emma; WWE NXT Tag Champions Conor O’Brien and Rick Victor overcame Casey Marin and Tommy Taylor; Alexander Rusev beat CJ Parker; Corey Graves toppled Adrian Neville.

Air Date: 30th October 2013
Alexander Rusev and Sylvester Lefort beat Enzo Amore and Colin Cassidy; Aiden English beat Jason Jordan; WWE NXT Women’s Champion Paige downed Summer Rae; CJ Parker overcame Tyler Breeze.

Nov 2013

Air Date: 6th November 2013
Alexander Rusev beat Sylvester Lefort; Leo Kruger overcame El Local; WWE NXT Tag Champions Conor O’Brian and Rick Victor overcame Travis Tyler and Troy McClain; Mojo Rawley overturned Tye Dillinger; Luke Harper overcame Kassius Ohno.

Air Date: 13th November 2013
Sasha Banks and Summer Rae downed Bayley and Charlotte; Aiden English beat Camacho; Mason Ryan rolled over Danny Burch; Adrian Neville downed Corey Graves in a “best-of-three-falls” encounter.

Air Date: 20th November 2013
Alexander Rusev beat Colin Cassady (Beat the Clock); Kassius Ohno bested Tyler Breeze (Beat the Clock); Hunico and Camacho bested Jonathan Ortagun and Chris Rockwell; Adrian Neville downed Aiden English (Beat the Clock); Sami Zayn rolled over Leo Kruger (Beat the Clock).

Air Date: 27th November 2013
Antonio Cesaro bested Yoshi Tatsu; WWE NXT Champion Bo Dallas toppled CJ Parker; Tyler Breeze downed Kassius Ohno; Hunico and Camacho bested WWE NXT Tag Champions Conor O’Brian and Rick Victor in a non-title match; Adrian Neville downed Sami Zayn.

Dec 2013

Air Date: 4th December 2013
WWE NXT Women’s Champion Paige downed Natalya; Aiden English won a squash; Tyson Kidd beat Leo Kruger; Adrian Neville downed WWE NXT Champion Bo Dallas by count-out.

Air Date: 11th December 2013
Rick Victor and Conor O’Brien rolled over Hunico and Camacho; Alexander Rusev beat Kassius Ohno; Summer Rae and Sasha Banks toppled Bayley and Natalya; Mojo Rawley overturned Scott Dawson; Leo Kruger overcame Sami Zayn.

Air Date: 18th December 2013
Sami Zayn and Tyson Kidd beat Antonio Cesaro and Leo Kruger; WWE NXT Women’s Champion Paige downed Sasha Banks; WWE NXT Tag Champions Konnor O’Brian and Rick Viktor overcame The American Pitbulls (Eddie Edwards and Davey Richards); WWE NXT Champion Bo Dallas toppled Adrian Neville in a lumberjack match.

Air Date: 25th December 2013
Antonio Cesaro bested William Regal.
