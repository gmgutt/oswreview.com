Heat Results 1998

HEAT RESULTS (1998)

Aug 1998

Air Date: 2nd August 1998
Edge pinned Jeff Jarrett; Droz and The Headbangers beat Kaientai; WWF European Champion D-Lo Brown downed Ken Shamrock by DQ; WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock and Owen Hart overturned Kane and Mankind by count-out.

Air Date: 9th August 1998
Vader dropped Mark Henry by DQ; The Headbangers battled Southern Justice to a double-DQ; X-Pac pinned WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku in a non-title match; Droz toppled Jeff Jarrett; The Undertaker battled Mankind to a no-contest.

Air Date: 16th August 1998
WWF World Tag Champions Kane and Mankind overturned The Legion of Doom; Edge pinned Brian Christopher; Vader battled Bart Gunn to a no-contest; Gangrel bested Scott Taylor; Triple H and X-Pac toppled Southern Justice by DQ.

Air Date: 23rd August 1998
WWF European Champion D-Lo Brown battled Road Warrior Animal to a no-contest; Dan Severn and Owen Hart overturned The Headbangers; WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku downed Scorpio; Jeff Jarrett bested Droz by DQ.

Air Date: 30th August 1998
Brian Christopher and Scott Taylor beat The Legion of Doom; Gangrel bested Dustin Runnels; Skull and Eight-Ball downed Bradshaw and Vader.

Sep 1998

Air Date: 6th September 1998
Gangrel pinned Dick Togo; Bradshaw defeated Ken Shamrock by DQ; Road Dogg beat Dennis Knight by DQ; The Undertaker and Kane toppled Road Warrior Animal and Droz; Edge downed WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku in a non-title match; WWF European Champion D-Lo Brown overcame Val Venis.

Air Date: 13th September 1998
Ken Shamrock toppled Vader; Val Venis downed Marc Mero by DQ; Gangrel pinned WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku in a non-title match; Kane toppled WWF European Champion D-Lo Brown by DQ; Jeff Jarrett overcame X-Pac in a lumberjack match.

Air Date: 20th September 1998
Jackie defeated Luna; WWF European Champion D-Lo Brown, Owen Hart and Jeff Jarrett downed WWF Intercontinental Champion Triple H, X-Pac and Billy Gunn; Mankind overturned Dustin Runnels; Edge dropped WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Taka Michinoku in a non-title match; The Undertaker and Kane toppled Skull and Eight-Ball.

Air Date: 27th September 1998
Golga beat Mosh; Matt and Jeff Hardy overcame Mens Teoih and Sho Funaki; Eight-Ball defeated Billy Gunn and Skull in a Triple Threat match.

Oct 1998

Air Date: 4th October 1998
WWF European Champion X-Pac pinned Owen Hart; Sho Funaki beat Matt Hardy; Golga and Kurrgan downed The Headbangers; Skull and Eight-Ball dropped Ken Shamrock and Mankind;  The Rock battled Jeff Jarrett to a no-contest.

Air Date: 11th October 1998
WWF Women’s Champion Jackie bested Starla Sexton; Kaientai overcame Brian Christopher, Scott Taylor, Matt Hardy and Jeff Hardy; Edge bested Vader; Al Snow downed Ken Shamrock by DQ; Jeff Jarrett defeated Road Dogg by DQ; WWF World Champion Steve Austin rolled over WWF European Champion D-Lo Brown in a non-title match.

Air Date: 18th October 1998
Steve Blackman beat Bradshaw; Giant Silva, Kurrgan and Golga downed Jose Estrada, Miguel Perez and Jesus Castillo; The Godfather defeated Faarooq; Scorpio dropped Jeff Jarrett.

Air Date: 25th October 1998
WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Christian pinned Brian Christopher; Jeff Jarrett beat Golga; The Headbangers overcame Skull and Eight-Ball; WWF Intercontinental Champion Ken Shamrock and D-Lo Brown downed WWF European Champion X-Pac and Mankind.

Nov 1998

Air Date: 1st  November 1998
Matt and Jeff Hardy defeated The Legion of Doom; Steven Regal pinned Bradshaw; Edge, Gangrel and WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Christian downed Kurrgan, Golga and Giant Silva; Steve Blackman dropped D-Lo Brown; Jeff Jarrett bested Val Venis; The Undertaker battled The Godfather to a no-contest.

Air Date: 8th November 1998
Scorpio and Bob Holly beat Brian Christopher and Scott Taylor; The Godfather downed Marc Mero. Shane McMahon was guest referee; Droz overcame Jeff Jarrett by DQ.

Air Date: 15th November 1998
Matt and Jeff Hardy battled Brian Christopher and Scott Taylor to a no-contest; Al Snow battled Gangrel to a no-contest; The Big Bossman pinned Marc Mero; Jeff Jarrett bested Kurrgan; WWF Intercontinental Champion Ken Shamrock toppled Val Venis by DQ.

Air Date: 22nd November 1998
Matt and Jeff Hardy battled Brian Christopher and Scott Taylor to a no-contest; Al Snow battled Gangrel to a no-contest; The Big Bossman pinned Marc Mero; Jeff Jarrett bested Kurrgan; WWF Intercontinental Champion Ken Shamrock toppled Val Venis by DQ.

Air Date: 29th November 1998
WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Duane Gill battled Christian to a no-contest; D-Lo Brown downed Steve Blackman by count-out; The Godfather and Val Venis defeated The Headbangers; WWF European Champion X-Pac dropped Mark Henry; Kane toppled Jeff Jarrett by DQ; WWF Intercontinental Champion Ken Shamrock and The Big Bossman bested Al Snow and Mankind.

Dec 1998

Air Date: 6th December 1998
WWF Light Heavyweight Champion Duane Gill beat Taka Michinoku; Animal and Droz overcame Brian Christopher and Scott Taylor; WWF World Champion The Rock, WWF Intercontinental Champion Ken Shamrock and The Big Bossman toppled Edge, Christian and Gangrel by DQ; Jeff Jarrett defeated Mark Henry by DQ; Kurrgan and Luna battled Tiger Ali Singh and Babu to a no-contest; Mankind overturned The Undertaker by DQ.

Air Date: 13th December 1998
WWF Light Heavyweight Champions Gillberg beat Matt Hardy; Kevin Quinn downed Brian Christopher; Triple H bested Droz; WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn overcame The Acolytes by DQ.

Air Date: 20th December 1998
WWF European Champion X-Pac beat Tiger Ali Singh; Kevin Quinn and Brian Christopher defeated Matt and Jeff Hardy; Al Snow pinned Droz; The Headbangers bested Golga and Kurrgan by DQ; Billy Gunn and Road Dogg overcame Skull and Eight-Ball; WWF World Champion The Rock rolled over Mankind, The Big Bossman and Ken Shamrock in a Fatal Four Way match.

Air Date: 27th December 1998
Mosh beat Golga by DQ; Jeff Jarrett and Owen Hart defeated The Godfather and Val Venis; WWF World Tag Champions Ken Shamrock and The Big Bossman downed Matt and Jeff Hardy; Kane bested WWF European Champion X-Pac in a non-title match.
