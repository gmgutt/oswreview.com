NXT Results 2014

NXT RESULTS (2014)

Jan 2014

Air Date: 1st January 2014
Alexander Rusev beat Kofi Kingston; Emma defeated Natalya; Tyler Breeze downed Mason Ryan; Sami Zayn rolled over Leo Kruger in a “best-of-three-falls” encounter.

Air Date: 8th January 2014
Bayley overturned Summer Rae; Aiden English beat Colin Cassaday; Alexander Rusev beat Xavier Woods; Tyson Kidd beat Baron Corbin; Adrian Neville downed Tyler Breeze; WWE NXT Tag Champions Connor O’Brian and Rick Viktor overcame Hunico and Camacho in a tornado match.

Air Date: 15th January 2014
CJ Parker overcame Jason Jordan; WWE NXT Champion Bo Dallas toppled Mojo Rawley; Kofi Kingston rolled over Alexander Rusev.

Air Date: 22nd January 2014
Adrian Neville beat Wesley Blake; Alexander Rusev defeated Xavier Woods; Antonio Cesaro bested CJ Parker; Natalya downed Summer Rae; Adrian Neville rolled over WWE NXT Champion Bo Dallas in a non-title match.

Air Date: 29th January 2014
Connor O Brian and Rick Viktor defeated Mike Cuellari and John Icarino; Corey Graves defeated Camacho; Bayley overcame Sasha Banks; Tyler Breeze defeated Colin Cassady; The Miz pinned CJ Parker; WWE NXT Champion Bo Dallas rolled over Danny Burch.

 

Feb 2014

Air Date: 5th February 2014
Alexander Rusev defeated Sin Cara; Emma downed Alicia Fox; Mason Ryan dropped Sylvester Lefort; Tyson Kidd pinned Aiden English; Adrian Neville overcame Corey Graves.

Air Date: 12th February 2014
Natalya, Emma and Bayley defeated Sasha Banks, Summer Rae and Alicia Fox; Aiden English beat Colin Cassady; CJ Parker downed Tye Dillinger; Erick Rowan and Luke Harper rolled over Jason Jordan and Marcus Louis.

Air Date: 19th February 2014
WWE NXT Tag Champions Konnor O’Brian and Rick Viktor defeated Casey Marion and Mike Lebosca; Emma downed Summer Rae; Adrian Neville pinned Tyler Breeze.

“NXT ArRIVAL”: Air Date: 27th February 2014
Cesaro pinned Sami Zayn; Mojo Rawley defeated CJ Parker; WWE NXT Tag Champions Konnor and Viktor downed Too Cool; WWE NXT Women’s Champion Paige dropped Emma; Tyler Breeze battled Xavier Woods to a no-contest; Adrian Neville rolled over WWE NXT Champion Bo Dallas in a ladder match to win the title.

Mar 2014

Air Date: 6th March 2014
NXT Champion Adrian Neville downed Camacho; Charlotte downed Emma; Corey Graves overturned Yoshi Tatsu by count-out; Adam Rose beat Wesley Blake; Sami Zayn toppled Corey Graves.

Air Date: 13th March 2014
NXT Women’s Champion Paige downed Sasha Banks; NXT Tag Champions Konnor and Viktor bested Travis Tyler and Cal Bishop; Mason Ryan toppled Wesley Blake; Alexander Rusev beat Xavier Woods; Bo Dallas overturned Colin Cassady.

Air Date: 20th March 2014
Mojo Rawley rolled over Bull Dempsey; Adam Rose downed Camacho; Bayley rolled over Sasha Banks; Sheamus overturned Aiden English.

Air Date: 27th March 2014
Mojo Rawley rolled over CJ Parker; Tyler Breeze downed Xavier Woods; Corey Graves overturned Yoshi Tatsu; Natalya defeated Charlotte by DQ; NXT Champion Adrian Neville downed Bo Dallas.

Apr 2014

Air Date: 3rd April 2014
Emma defeated Sasha Banks; Konnor and Viktor bested Jack Hurley and Jonny Vandal; Brodus Clay rolled over Xavier Woods; Tyler Breeze downed Yoshi Tatsu; Corey Graves overturned Sami Zayn.

Air Date: 10th April 2014
Adam Rose downed Danny Burch; Bayley rolled over Sasha Banks; Mojo Rawley rolled over Sylvester Lefort; Bo Dallas overturned Justin Gabriel.

Air Date: 17th April 2014
Aiden English beat Colin Cassady; Camacho overcame Oliver Grey; NXT Tag Champions Konnor and Viktor bested Wesley Blake and Cal Bishop; The Great Khali dropped CJ Parker; Jason Jordan and Tye Dillinger bested Sawyer Fulton and Baron Corbin; NXT Champion Adrian Neville downed Brodus Clay by count-out.

Air Date: 24th April 2014
Alexander Rusev beat Travis Tyler; Charlotte and Sasha Banks overturned NXT Divas Champion Paige and Emma; Tyson Kidd beat Mason Ryan; Tyler Breeze downed Angelo Dawkins; WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso, and Sami Zayn toppled NXT Tag Champions Konnor and Viktor, and Corey Graves.

May 2014

Air Date: 1st May 2014
Tyson Kidd beat Bo Dallas; Sasha Banks overturned Bayley (NXT Women’s Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); Adam Rose downed Danny Burch; Natalya defeated Layla (NXT Women’s Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); Mojo Rawley rolled over Oliver Grey; NXT Champion Adrian Neville downed Brodus Clay.

Air Date: 8th May 2014
Charlotte downed Emma (NXT Women’s Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); Kalisto and El Local beat Marcus Louis and Sylvester Lefort; Camacho overcame Captain Comic; Alexa Bliss overturned Alicia Fox (NXT Women’s Title Tournament – Quarter-Final);Sami Zayn, Tyler Breeze and Tyson Kidd co-won a 20-man battle royal.

Air Date: 15th May 2014
Viktor and Konnor bested Buddy Murphy and Elias Sampson; Charlotte downed Alexa Bliss (NXT Women’s Title Tournament – Semi-Final); Mojo Rawley rolled over Aiden English; Colin Cassady overturned Angelo Dawkins; Tyson Kidd won a three-way over Sami Zayn and Tyler Breeze.

Air Date: 22nd May 2014
WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E toppled Bo Dallas in a “Win or go home” match; WWE Divas Champion Paige downed Tamina Snuka; Adam Rose downed Camacho by count-out; Natalya defeated Sasha Banks (NXT Women’s Title Tournament – Semi-Final); NXT Champion Adrian Neville downed Curt Hawkins.

There was no show on 5/29. It was pre-empted by the live broadcast of NXT Takeover.

Jun 2014

Air Date: 5th June 2014
Mojo Rawley rolled over Aiden English; Charlotte downed Bayley; Jason Jordan and Tye Dillinger bested Phillip Gouljar and Stuart Cumberland; NXT Champion Adrian Neville downed Justin Gabriel.

Air Date: 12th June 2014
Bayley, Emma and WWE Divas Champion Paige downed NXT Women’s Champion Charlotte, Sasha Banks and Summer Rae; Colin Cassady rolled over Sylvester Lefort; Sami Zayn toppled Mr NXT (Bo Dallas); NXT Champion Adrian Neville downed Tyson Kidd.

Air Date: 19th June 2014
Alexa Bliss overturned Sasha Banks; Mojo Rawley rolled over Garrett Dylan; Aiden English and Simon Gotch beat Travis Tyler and Angelo Dawkins; Tyler Breeze downed Kalisto; NXT Tag Champions Konnor and Viktor toppled Sami Zayn and Tyson Kidd.

Air Date: 26th June 2014
Aiden English and Simon Gotch beat Tee Perkins and Matt Sugarman; Bull Dempsey rolled over Xavier Woods; Becky Lynch beat Summer Rae; Colin Cassady rolled over Sawyer Fulton; NXT Champion Adrian Neville downed Rob Van Dam.

Jul 2014

Air Date: 3rd July 2014
Colin Cassady overcame Sylvester Lefort; NXT Women’s Champion Charlotte and Sasha Banks overturned Bayley and Becky Lynch; CJ Parker bested Steve Cutler; Sami Zayn toppled Justin Gabriel.

Air Date: 10th July 2014
Summer Rae defeated Bayley; Sin Cara beat Wesley Blake; Bull Dempsey overcame Angelo Dawkins; Tyson Kidd and Justin Gabriel beat NXT Champion Adrian Neville and Sami Zayn.

Air Date: 17th July 2014
Aiden English and Simon Gotch bested Enzo Amore and Colin Cassady; CJ Parker bested Xavier Woods; Sasha Banks overturned Alexa Bliss; Adam Rose defeated Jason Jordan; Sami Zayn toppled Tyson Kidd.

Air Date: 24th July 2014
NXT Tag Champions Konnor and Viktor toppled Johnny Vandal and Aaron Solo; Tyler Breeze defeated Mojo Rawley; NXT Women’s Champion Charlotte defeated Summer Rae; Sin Cara and Kalisto dropped Aiden English and Simon Gotch; NXT Champion Adrian Neville defeated Rusev by DQ.

Air Date: 31st July 2014
Tyler Breeze defeated Angelo Dawkins; NXT Women’s Champion Charlotte defeated Becky Lynch; NXT Tag Champions Konnor and Viktor toppled Mac Miles and Steve Cutler; Bull Dempsey and Mojo Rawley overcame Scott Dawson and Dash Wilder; Adam Rose defeated Tyson Kidd.

Aug 2014

Air Date: 7th August 2014
Colin Cassady and Enzo Amore defeated Jason Jordan and Tye Dillinger (WWE NXT Tag Title #1 Contender Tournament – 1stRound); CJ Parker bested Xavier Woods; Bayley overcame Eva Marie; Adam Rose and Sami Zayn toppled Tyson Kidd and Justin Gabriel (WWE NXT Tag Title #1 Contender Tournament – 1st Round).

Air Date: 14th August 2014
Simon Gotch and Aiden English bested Bull Dempsey and Mojo Rawley (WWE NXT Tag Title #1 Contender Tournament – 1stRound); Bayley overcame Sasha Banks; Sin Cara and Kalisto dropped Buddy Murphy and Wesley Blake (WWE NXT Tag Title #1 Contender Tournament – 1st Round); NXT Champion Adrian Neville defeated Tyler Breeze by DQ.

Air Date: 21st August 2014
Aiden English and Simon Gotch bested Enzo Amore and Colin Cassady (NXT Tag Title #1 Contender Tournament – Semi-Final); Tyson Kidd toppled Tyler Breeze by count-out; NXT Women’s Champion Charlotte defeated Becky Lynch; Mojo Rawley overcame Steve Cutler; Sin Cara and Kalisto dropped Adam Rose and Sami Zayn (NXT Tag Title #1 Contender Tournament – Semi-Final);

Air Date: 28th August 2014
NXT Tag Champions The Ascension won a squash; Bayley overcame Sasha Banks; Bull Dempsey overcame Angelo Dawkins; Tyson Kidd and Tyler Breeze defeated NXT Champion Adrian Neville and Sami Zayn.

Sep 2014

Air Date: 4th September 2014
NXT Women’s Champion Charlotte defeated Alexa Bliss; Tyler Breeze defeated Tye Dillinger; Tyson Kidd beat Adam Rose; Sami Zayn toppled Marcus Louis; NXT Champion Adrian Neville defeated CJ Parker; Sin Cara and Kalisto dropped Aiden English and Simon Gotch(NXT Tag Title #1 Contender Tournament – Final).

Air Date: 25th September 2014
Bull Dempsey rolled over Mojo Rawley; Tyler Breeze downed Justin Gabriel; Bayley rolled over Alexa Bliss; Enzo Amore downed Marcus Louis; WWE NXT Tag Champions Sin Cara and Kalisto bested The Ascension.
