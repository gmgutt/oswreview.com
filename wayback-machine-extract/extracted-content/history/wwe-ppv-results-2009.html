WWE PPV Results 2009

WWE PPV RESULTS (2009)

See also: WWE PPV Statistics 2009

25th January 2009: “WWE Royal Rumble 2009”: Detroit, MI – 16,685 sellout (14,000 paid)
($800,000 live gate)
Dark match: Jimmy Wang Yang beat Paul Burchill.
PPV Broadcast: ECW Champion Jack Swagger defeated Matt Hardy; Melina downed WWE Diva’s Champion Beth Phoenix to win the title; World Heavyweight Champion John Cena pinned John Bradshaw Layfield; Edge rolled over WWE World Champion Jeff Hardy in a no-DQ match to win the title; Randy Orton won the 30-man Royal Rumble by last eliminating Triple H.

15th February 2009: “WWE No Way Out 2009”: Seattle, WA – 11,362
($539,505 live gate)
Dark match: WWE Women’s Champion Melina beat Beth Phoenix.
PPV Broadcast: Triple H overcame WWE World Champion Edge, Jeff Hardy, The Undertaker, The Big Show and Vladimir Kozlov in an “Elimination Chamber” match to win the title; Randy Orton toppled Shane McMahon in a “no-holds-barred” encounter; ECW Champion Jack Swagger dropped Finlay; Shawn Michaels rolled over John Bradshaw Layfield; Edge bested World Heavyweight Champion John Cena, Rey Mysterio, Kane, Mike Knox and Chris Jericho in an “Elimination Chamber” match to win the title.

5th April 2009: “WWE Wrestlemania 25”: Houston, TX – 61,611
($7,211,673 live gate)
Dark match: WWE Tag Champions Carlito and Primo defeated WWE World Tag Champions John Morrison and The Miz to unify the titles.
PPV Broadcast: CM Punk won the “Money in the Bank” ladder match over Christian, Kane, Kofi Kingston, Finlay, Shelton Benjamin, WWE US Champion MVP and Mark Henry; Santino Marella won a diva’s battle royal; Chris Jericho dropped Jimmy Snuka, Roddy Piper and Ricky Steamboat in a handicap elimination match; Matt Hardy downed Jeff Hardy in an “Extreme Rules” match; Rey Mysterio bested WWE Intercontinental Champion John Bradshaw Layfield to win the title; The Undertaker pinned Shawn Michaels; John Cena toppled World Heavyweight Champion Edge and The Big Show in a three-way to win the title; WWE World Champion Triple H rolled over Randy Orton.

26th April 2009: “WWE Backlash 2009”: Providence, RI – 8,357 sellout
($523,855 live gate)
Dark match: Kofi Kingston beat Dolph Ziggler.
PPV Broadcast: Christian defeated ECW Champion Jack Swagger to win the title; Chris Jericho downed Ricky Steamboat; Kane pinned CM Punk; Jeff Hardy bested Matt Hardy in an I Quit match; Santina Marella beat Beth Phoenix; Randy Orton, Ted Dibiase Jr, and Cody Rhodes overturned WWE World Champion Triple H, Batista, and Shane McMahon. By virtue of a stipulation, Orton won the WWE World Title; Edge rolled over World Heavyweight Champion John Cena in a “last man standing” match to win the title.

17th May 2009 “WWE Judgment Day 2009”: Chicago, IL – 13,433 sellout (11,500 paid)
($852,985 live gate)
Dark match: Mickie James beat Beth Phoenix.
PPV Broadcast: Umaga defeated CM Punk; ECW Champion Christian downed Jack Swagger; John Morrison dropped Shelton Benjamin; WWE Intercontinental Champion Rey Mysterio pinned Chris Jericho; Batista bested WWE World Champion Randy Orton by DQ; John Cena toppled The Big Show; World Heavyweight Champion Edge rolled over Jeff Hardy.

7th June 2009: “WWE Extreme Rules 2009”: New Orleans, LA – 9,214
($448,990 live gate)
Dark match: Mickie James and Kelly Kelly beat Beth Phoenix and Rosa Mendes.
PPV Broadcast: WWE US Champion Kofi Kingston overcame MVP, William Regal, and Matt Hardy in a four-way; Chris Jericho defeated WWE Intercontinental Champion Rey Mysterio in a “no-holds-barred” match to win the title; CM Punk downed Umaga in a strap match; Tommy Dreamer overturned ECW Champion Christian and Jack Swagger in a three-way “extreme rules” match to win the title; Santino Marella won a handicap “Hog Pen” match over Vickie Guerrero and Chavo Guerrero Jr; Batista bested WWE World Champion Randy Orton in a cage match to win the title; John Cena toppled The Big Show in a submission match; Jeff Hardy rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Edge in a ladder match to win the title; CM Punk pinned World Heavyweight Champion Jeff Hardy to win the title.

28th June 2009: “WWE The Bash 2009”: Sacramento, CA – 11,946 (10,412 paid)
($670,360 live gate)
Dark match: R-Truth beat Shelton Benjamin.
PPV Broadcast: ECW Champion Tommy Dreamer won a scramble match over Christian, Jack Swagger, Finlay, and Mark Henry; Rey Mysterio toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho in a “mask vs. title” match to win the title; Dolph Ziggler defeated The Great Khali in a no-DQ encounter; Edge and Chris Jericho overcame WWE Unified World Tag Champions Carlito and Primo Colon, and Cody Rhodes and Ted Dibiase Jr in a three-way to win the titles; Michelle McCool downed WWE Women’s Champion Melina to win the title;  Jeff Hardy bested World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk by DQ; John Cena overtured The Miz; WWE World Champion Randy Orton rolled over Triple H in a “best-of-three-falls (normal, falls-count-anywhere, stretcher) encounter, (2:1).

26th July 2009: “WWE Night of Champions 2009”: Philadelphia, PA – 14,003
($892,585 live gate)
Dark match: Cryme Tyme beat DH Smith and Tyson Kidd.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Unified World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and The Big Show defeated Cody Rhodes and Ted Dibiase Jr; Christian downed ECW Champion Tommy Dreamer to win the title; WWE US Champion Kofi Kingston won a six-way over MVP, Jack Swagger, Carlito, Primo, and The Miz; Michelle McCool beat WWE Women’s Champion Melina to win the title; WWE World Champion Randy Orton bested John Cena and Triple H in a three-way; Mickie James dropped WWE Divas Champion Maryse to win the title; WWE Intercontinental Champion Rey Mysterio toppled Dolph Ziggler; Jeff Hardy rolled over World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk to win the title.

23rd August 2009: “WWE Summerslam 2009”: Los Angeles, CA – 14,116 sellout
($913,880 live gate)
Dark match: Beth Phoenix won a divas battle royal.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion Rey Mysterio dropped Dolph Ziggler; MVP downed Jack Swagger; WWE Unified World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and The Big Show defeated Cryme Tyme; Kane overturned The Great Khali; Triple H and Shawn Michaels bested Ted Dibiase Jr and Cody Rhodes; ECW Champion Christian toppled William Regal; WWE World Champion Randy Orton pinned John Cena; CM Punk rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Jeff Hardy in a TLC encounter to win the title.

13th September 2009: “WWE Breaking Point 2009”: Montreal, QUE – 12,000
($730,000 live gate)
Dark match: Evan Bourne beat Chavo Guerrero Jr.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Unified World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and The Big Show beat MVP and Mark Henry; WWE US Champion Kofi Kingston pinned The Miz; Ted Dibiase and Cody Rhodes bested Triple H and Shawn Michaels in a “falls-count-anywhere” submissions match; Kane overturned The Great  Khali in a “Singapore cane” match; ECW Champion Christian defeated William Regal; John Cena bested WWE Word Champion Randy Orton in an “I Quit” match; World Heavyweight Champion  CM Punk rolled over The Undertaker in a submission match by Montreal finish.

4th October 2009: “WWE Hell in a Cell 2009”: Newark, NJ – 12,356 sellout
($781,970 live gate)
Dark match: Matt Hardy beat Mike Knox.
PPV Broadcast: The Undertaker bested World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk to win the title in a “Hell in a Cell” match; WWE Intercontinental Champion John Morrison defeated Dolph Ziggler; WWE Divas Champion Mickie James downed Alicia Fox; WWE Unified World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and The Big Show dropped Batista and Rey Mysterio; Randy Orton toppled WWE World Champion John Cena to win the title in a “Hell in a Cell” match; Drew McIntyre overcame R-Truth; WWE US Champion Kofi Kingston overturned The Miz and Jack Swagger in a three-way; Triple H and Shawn Michaels rolled over Ted Dibiase Jr and Cody Rhodes in a “Hell in a Cell” match.

25th October 2009: “WWE Bragging Rights 2009”: Pittsburgh, PA – 7,981
($449,465 live gate)
Dark match: ECW Champion Christian beat Paul Burchill.
PPV Broadcast: WWE US Champion The Miz toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion John Morrison in a non-title match; Beth Phoenix, Natalya, and WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool overcame Gail Kim, Melina, and Kelly Kelly; Chris Jericho, Kane, Matt Hardy, R-Truth, Finlay, DH Smith, and Tyson Kidd defeated Triple H, Shawn Michaels, The Big Show, Jack Swagger, Cody Rhodes, Kofi Kingston, and Mark Henry; World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker bested Batista, CM Punk and Rey Mysterio in a four-way; John Cena rolled over WWE World Champion Randy Orton in a 60-minute “falls-count-anywhere, no-DQ” Iron Man match (6:5) to win the title.

22nd November 2009: “WWE Survivor Series 2009”: Washington, DC – 12,500
($650,000 live gate)
WWE US Champion The Miz, Sheamus, Drew McIntyre, Dolph Ziggler, and Jack Swagger defeated WWE Intercontinental Champion John Morrison, Matt Hardy, Evan Bourne, Shelton Benjamin, and Finlay in a Survivors elimination match; Batista toppled Rey Mysterio; Kofi Kingston, ECW Champion Christian, R-Truth, MVP, and Mark Henry downed Randy Orton, Cody Rhodes, Ted Dibiase Jr, CM Punk, and William Regal in a Survivors elimination match; World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker bested Chris Jericho and The Big Show in a three-way; WWE Divas Champion Mickie James, Melina, Gail Kim, Eve Torres, and Kelly Kelly dropped WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool, Beth Phoenix, Alicia Fox, Jillian Hall, and Layla; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Triple H and Shawn Michaels in a three-way.

 

13th December 2009: “WWE TLC 2009”: San Antonio, TX – 13,500 sellout (12,000 paid)
($770,000 live gate)
Dark match: R-Truth beat CM Punk.
PPV Broadcast: ECW Champion Christian defeated Shelton Benjamin in a ladder match; Drew McIntyre downed WWE Intercontinental Champion John Morrison to win the title; WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool dropped Mickie James; Sheamus toppled WWE World Champion John Cena in a tables match to win the title; World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker bested Batista in a “chairs” match; Randy Orton pinned Kofi Kingston; Triple and Shawn Michaels rolled over WWE Unified World Tag Champions in a TLC encounter to win the titles.
