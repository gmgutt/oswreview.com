WWE Great American Bash Results

WWE GREAT AMERICAN BASH RESULTS

27th June 2004: “WWE Great American Bash 2004”: Norfolk, VA – 6,500 (5,500 paid)
($320,000 live gate)
Heat: (06/27): Spike Dudley beat Jamie Noble.
PPV Broadcast: WWE US Champion John Cena bested Booker T, Rene Dupree, and Rob Van Dam in a four-way elimination match; Luther Reigns pinned Charlie Haas; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio dropped Chavo Guerrero Jr; Kenzo Suzuki toppled Billy Gunn; Sable overcame Torrie Wilson; Mordecai pinned Hardcore Holly; John Bradshaw Layfield rolled over WWE World Champion Eddie Guerrero in a bullrope match to win the title; The Undertaker bested WWE Tag Team Champions The Dudley Boys in a non-title handicap match.

24th July 2005: “WWE Great American Bash 2005”: Buffalo, NY – 8,000
($375,000 live gate)
Heat: (07/24): WWE Cruiserweight Champion Paul London beat Nunzio.
PPV Broadcast: John Heidenreich and Road Warrior Animal downed WWE Tag Champions Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury to win the titles; Booker T pinned Christian; WWE US Champion Orlando Jordan dropped Chris Benoit; The Undertaker overturned Muhammad Hassan; Juventud Guerrera, Super Crazy, and Psychosis overcame Stevie Richards, The Blue Meanie, and Nova; Rey Mysterio bested Eddie Guerrero; Melina beat Torrie Wilson in a “bra and panties” match. Candice Michelle was guest referee; John Bradshaw Layfield rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Batista by DQ.

23rd July 2006: “WWE Great American Bash 2006”: Indianapolis, IN – 9,750 (8,750 paid)
($490,400 live gate)
Dark match: Sho Funaki beat Simon Dean.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Tag Champions Brian Kendrick and Paul London beat Kid Kash and Jamie Noble; WWE US Champion Finlay downed William Regal; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Gregory Helms downed Matt Hardy; The Undertaker rolled over ECW World Champion The Big Show in a non-title “Punjabi Prison” match; Ashley downed Kristal, Michelle McCool, and Jillian Hall in a four-way “bra and panties” match; Mr Kennedy toppled Batista by DQ; King Booker rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Rey Mysterio to win the title.

22nd July 2007: “WWE Great American Bash 2007”: San Jose, CA – 13,034 sellout (11,398 paid)
($761,410 live gate)
Dark match: Chuck Palumbo defeated Chris Masters.
PPV Broadcast: WWE US Champion MVP overturned Matt Hardy; Hornswoggle defeated WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr, Jamie Noble, Shannon Moore, Sho Funaki, and Jimmy Wang Yang inn a six-way to win the title; Carlito beat The Sandman in a “singapore cane-on-a-pole” match; WWE Women’s Champion Candice Michelle downed Melina; WWE Intercontinental Champion Umaga dropped Jeff Hardy; ECW Champion John Morrison bested CM Punk; Randy Orton pinned Dusty Rhodes in a bullrope match; World Heavyweight Champion The Great Khali bested Batista and Kane in a three-way; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Bobby Lashley.

20th July 2008: “WWE Great American Bash 2008”: Uniondale, NY – 12,454 sellout
($817,875 live gate)
Dark match: Umaga beat Mr Kennedy.
PPV Broadcast: Curt Hawkins and Zack Ryder won the WWE Tag Titles in a four-way over Finlay and Hornswoggle, The Miz and John Morrison, and Jesse and Festus; Shelton Benjamin toppled WWE US Champion Matt Hardy to win the title; ECW Champion Mark Henry dropped Tommy Dreamer; Chris Jericho bested Shawn Michaels by stoppage; Michelle McCool overcame Natalya to win the WWE Divas Title; World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk battled Batista to a double-DQ; JBL downed John Cena in a “NYC Parking Lot Brawl”; WWE World Champion Triple H rolled over Edge.
