WWE PPV Results 2012

WWE PPV RESULTS (2012)

See also: WWE PPV Statistics 2012

29th January 2012: “WWE Royal Rumble 2012”: St Louis, MO – 18,121 sellout (15,000 paid)
($950,000 live gate)
Dark match: Yoshi Tatsu beat Heath Slater.
PPV Broadcast: World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan downed The Big Show and Mark Henry in a three-way cage match; WWE  Divas Champion Beth Phoenix, Natalya and The Bella Twins overcame Kelly Kelly, Eve Torres, Alicia Fox and Tamina; John Cena battled Kane to a double count-out; Brodus Clay bested Drew McIntyre; WWE World Champion CM Punk pinned Dolph Ziggler. John Laurinaitis was guest referee; Sheamus won the 30-man Royal Rumble.

19th February 2012: “WWE Elimination Chamber 2012”: Milwaukee, WI – 15,306 sellout (12,000 paid)
($750,000 live gate)
Dark match: Hunico beat Alex Riley.
PPV Broadcast: WWE World Champion CM Punk defeated The Miz, Chris Jericho, R-Truth, Kofi Kingston and Dolph Ziggler in an Elimination Chamber match; WWE Divas Champion Beth Phoenix bested Tamina Snuka; World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan toppled Santino Marella, The Big Show, Wade Barrett, Cody Rhodes and The Great Khali in an Elimination Chamber match; WWE US Champion Jack Swagger overturned Justin Gabriel; John Cena rolled over Kane in an ambulance match.

1st April 2012: “WWE Wrestlemania 28”: Miami, FL – 64,900 (62,400 paid)
($8,290,000 live gate)
Pre-show: WWE World Tag Champions Primo and Epico overcame Tyson Kidd and Justin Gabriel, and Jimmy and Jey Uso in a three-way.
PPV Broadcast: Sheamus squashed World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan to win the title; Kane pined Randy Orton; The Big Show downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes to win the title; Maria Menounos and Kelly Kelly dropped WWE Diva’s Champion Beth Phoenix and Eve Torres; The Undertaker overturned Triple H in a “Hell in a Cell” encounter; WWE World Champion CM Punk bested Chris Jericho; The Miz, Dolph Ziggler, Drew McIntyre, Jack Swagger, Mark Henry and David Otunga rolled over Zack Ryder, The Great Khali, Booker T, R-Truth, WWE US Champion Santino Marella and Kofi Kingston; The Rock rolled over John Cena.

29th April 2012: “WWE Extreme Rules 2012”: Chicago, IL – 14,817 sellout (11,500 paid)
($700,000 live gate)
Youtube pre-show: (63,000 views) WWE US Champion Santino Marella beat The Miz.
PPV Broadcast: Randy Orton bested Kane in a falls-count-anywhere match; Brodus Clay defeated Dolph Ziggler; Cody Rhodes overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion The Big Show in a tables match to win the title; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus toppled Daniel Bryan in a “best-of-three-falls” encounter; Ryback squashed Aaron Relic and Jay Hatton in a handicap match; WWE World Champion CM Punk dropped Chris Jericho in a streetfight; Layla beat WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella to win the titles; John Cena rolled over Brock Lesnar in an “Extreme Rules” match

20th May 2012: “WWE Over The Limit 2012”: Raleigh, NC – 8,000
Youtube pre-show: (44,049 views): Kane defeated Zack Ryder.
PPV Broadcast: Christian won a battle royal; WWE World Tag Champions R-Truth and Kofi Kingston downed Jack Swagger and Dolph Ziggler; WWE Divas Champion Layla overturned Beth Phoenix; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus dropped Randy Orton, Chris Jericho and Alberto Del Rio in a four-way; Brodus Clay bested The Miz; Christian overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz to win the title; WWE World Champion CM Punk toppled Daniel Bryan; Ryback squashed Camacho; John Laurinaitis rolled over John Cena in a no-DQ, no-count-out encounter.

17th June 2012: “WWE No Way Out 2012”: East Rutherford, NJ – 10,000
Youtube pre-show: (43,855 views): Brodus Clay beat David Otunga by count-out.
PPV Broadcast: World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus defeated Dolph Ziggler; WWE US Champion Santino Marella overcame Ricardo Rodriguez in a “Tuxedo streetfight”; WWE  Intercontinental Champion Christian bested Cody Rhodes; Titus O’Neil and Darren Young won a four-way over Justin Gabriel and Tyson Kidd, Primo and Epico, and Jimmy and Jey Uso; WWE Divas Champion Layla dropped Beth Phoenix; Sin Cara downed Hunico; WWE World Champion CM Punk toppled Daniel Bryan and Kane in a three-way; Ryback squashed Dan Delaney and Rob Grimes in a handicap match; John Cena rolled over The Big Show in a cage match.

15th July 2012: “WWE Money in the Bank 2012”: Phoenix, AZ – 9,000
Youtube pre-show: (46,834 views): WWE World Tag Champions Kofi Kingston and R-Truth beat Hunico and Camacho.
PPV Broadcast: Dolph Ziggler won the Smackdown “Money in the Bank” ladder match over Cody Rhodes, Sin Cara, WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian, Tyson Kidd, Tensai, Damien Sandow and WWE US Champion Santino Marella; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus pinned Alberto Del Rio; Primo and Epico overcame Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; WWE World Champion CM Punk rolled over Daniel Bryan in a no-DQ match. AJ was guest referee; Ryback squashed Tyler Reks and Curt Hawkins in a handicap match; WWE Divas Champion Layla, Kaitlyn and Tamina Snuka overturned Beth Phoenix, Natalya and Eve Torres; John Cena won the Raw “Money in the Bank” ladder match over The Big Show, Chris Jericho, Kane and The Miz.

19th August 2012: “WWE Summerslam 2012”: Los Angeles, CA – 17,482 sellout (14,205 paid)
($1,135,590 live gate)
Live YouTube pre-show: Antonio Cesaro overcame WWE US Champion Santino Marella to win the title.
PPV Broadcast: Chris Jericho defeated Dolph Ziggler; Daniel Bryan downed Kane; WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz dropped Rey Mysterio; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus pinned Alberto Del Rio; WWE World Tag Champions Kofi Kingson and R-Truth beat Darren Young and Titus O’Neil; WWE World Champion CM Punk toppled John Cena and The Big Show in a three-way; Brock Lesnar rolled over Triple H.

16th September 2012: “WWE Night of Champions 2012”: Boston, MA – 14,886 (11,500 paid)
YouTube pre-show: Zack Ryder won a 16-man battle royal, last eliminating Tensai.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz overcame Rey Mysterio, Sin Cara and Cody Rhodes in a four-way; Kane and Daniel Bryan dropped WWE World Tag Champions Kofi Kingston and R-Truth to win the titles; WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro overturned Zack Ryder; Randy Orton bested Dolph Ziggler; Eve Torres downed WWE Divas Champion Layla to win the title; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus toppled Alberto Del Rio; WWE World Champion CM Punk battled John Cena to a double-pinfall finish.

28th October 2012: “WWE Hell in a Cell 2012”: Atlanta, GA – 10,000
Randy Orton pinned Alberto Del Rio; Cody Rhodes and Damien Sandow defeated WWE World Tag Champions Daniel Bryan and Kane by DQ; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston overturned The Miz; WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro downed Justin Gabriel; Rey Mysterio and Sin Cara dropped Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; The Big Show toppled World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus to win the title; WWE Divas Champion Eve Torres bested Layla and Kaitlyn in a three-way; WWE World Champion CM Punk rolled over Ryback in a “Hell-in-a-Cell” match.

18th November 2012: “WWE Survivor Series 2012”: Indianapolis, IN – 8,500
Youtube pre-show: (3,380 views): Heath Slater and Jinder Mahal beat Santino Marella and Zack Ryder.
PPV Broadcast: Rey Mysterio, Sin Cara, Tyson Kidd, Justin Gabriel and Brodus Clay defeated Tensai, Titus O’Neil, Darren Young, Primo and Epico in a “Survivors” elimination match; WWE Divas Champion Eve Torres downed Kaitlyn; WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro dropped R-Truth; Sheamus toppled World Heavyweight Champion The Big Show by DQ; Dolph Ziggler, Alberto Del Rio, Damien Sandow, Wade Barrett and David Otunga bested Randy Orton, The Miz, WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston and WWE World Tag Champions Kane and Daniel Bryan in a “Survivors” elimination match; WWE World Champion CM Punk rolled over John Cena and Ryback in a three-way.

16th December 2012: “WWE TLC 2012”: Brooklyn, NY – 15,748 sellout (12,500 paid)
Dark match: JTG beat David Otunga.
Pre-show: Eve Torres won a battle royal.
PPV Broadcast: Cody Rhodes and Damien Sandow overturned Rey Mysterio and Sin Cara in a “tables match; WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro downed R-Truth; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston dropped Wade Barrett; Dean Ambrose, Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns bested Ryback and WWE World Tag Champions Kane and Daniel Bryan in a “TLC” match; WWE Divas Champion Eve Torres downed Naomi; World Heavyweight Champion The Big Show defeated Sheamus in a “chairs” match; The Miz, Alberto Del Rio and The Brooklyn Brawler overcame Heath Slater, Jinder Mahal and Drew McIntyre; Dolph Ziggler rolled over John Cena in a ladder match.
