Hellooooooooo and welcome to the 4th Annual Golden Nogger awards! Voting is now open!
If this is your first time, every year we do an ‘awards show’ where we hotdog and grandstand about this last year in OSW! You guys vote in the categories (and so choose the winners), which will remain open until the day we record the show (in about a week), and we’ll have a video where we have a laugh revealing the results!
You can write in your nomination as well!

The Rules (RULES!):
• If a wrestler is nominated one year, he/she cannot be nominated next year.
• Nominations will be taken from episodes released since the last Golden Noggers (The Room, Starrcade ’94, Fall Brawl, World War 3, SummerSlam 2016, Superbrawl and Uncensored!)

So let’s do it to it!
