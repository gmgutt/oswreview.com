Raw Results 2004

WWE RAW RESULTS (2004)

Jan 2004

Air Date: 5th January 2004
Spike Dudley beat Rene Dupree; Chris Jericho and Christian downed The Hurricane and Rosey; Steven Richards beat Test; Trish Stratus bested WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly by DQ; Rob Van Dam rolled over Mark Henry; WWE World Tag Champions Ric Flair and Batista overturned The Dudley Boys.

Air Date: 12th January 2004
WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly and Jazz beat Trish Stratus and Lita; Bill Goldberg pinned Matt Hardy; Batista pinned D-Von Dudley; Booker T overturned Kane by DQ; Mark Henry beat Chris Jericho; WWE Intercontinental Champion Randy Orton rolled over Rob Van Dam.

Air Date: 19th January 2004
Booker T and Rob Van Dam rolled over Matt Hardy and Christian; Chris Jericho bested Rene Dupree; Mark Henry beat Rico; Spike Dudley toppled Kane by DQ; Bill Goldberg defeated Scott Steiner and Test in a three-way; Jazz dropped Lita; WWE Intercontinental Champion Randy Orton rolled over The Hurricane; Bill Goldberg won a battle royal.

Air Date: 26th January 2004
WWE Intercontinental Champion Randy Orton, WWE World Tag Champions Ric Flair and Batista overturned Chris Jeicho and Rob Van Dam in a handicap match; Victoria and Lita beat WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly and Jazz; Bubba Ray Dudley beat Kane by DQ; Rico bested beat Rob Conway; Bill Goldberg rolled over Jonathan Coachman in a no-DQ match.

Feb 2004

Air Date: 2nd February 2004
Chris Benoit toppled Mark Henry; Rico beat Rene Dupree; Kane battled Trish Stratus to a no-contest; Bill Goldberg battled Kane to a no-contest; Booker T overturned Matt Hardy; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H rolled over Spike Dudley; WWE World Tag Champions Ric Flair and Batista overturned Chris Jericho and Christian; Shawn Michaels overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Randy Orton in a non-title match.

Air Date: 9th February 2004
Chris Jericho and Trish Stratus overcame Matt Hardy and WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly; Chris Benoit toppled Ric Flair; Kane toppled The Hurricane; WWE Intercontinental Champion Randy Orton rolled over Rob Van Dam and Booker T in a three-way.

Air Date: 16th February 2004
Booker T and Rob Van Dam rolled over WWE World Tag Champions Ric Flair and Batista to win the titles; Kane bested Chris Jericho by stoppage; Trish Stratus and Victoria beat WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly and Jazz; Shawn Michaels overturned Chris Benoit.

Air Date: 23rd February 2004
Victoria toppled WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly, Jazz, and Lita in a four-way elimination match to win the title; WWE Intercontinental Champion Randy Orton rolled over Val Venis; Chris Benoit toppled Batista; WWE World Tag Champions Rob Van Dam and Booker T overturned Rob Conway and Rene Dupree; Christian downed Trish Stratus; Vince McMahon battled Eric Bischoff to a no-contest. Steve Austin was guest referee.

Mar 2004

Air Date: 1st March 2004
WWE Women’s Champion Victoria and Lita beat Molly Holly and Jazz; Mark Jindrak and Garrison Cade toppled The Hurricane and Rosey; WWE Intercontinental Champion Randy Orton and Batista overturned Chris Benoit and Shawn Michaels; WWE World Tag Champions Rob Van Dam and Booker T overturned Matt Hardy and Test.

Air Date: 8th March 2004
Mark Jindrak, Garrison Cade, Rene Dupree, and Rob Conway beat The Dudley Boys and WWE World Tag Champions Rob Van Dam and Booker T; WWE Intercontinental Champion Randy Orton, Ric Flair, and Batista overturned The Hurricane and Rosey in a handicap match; Chris Benoit toppled Matt Hardy; Molly Holly downed Lita; Steven Richards overturned Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 15th March 2004
WWE Women’s Champion Victoria and Lita downed Molly Holly and Jazz; Matt Hardy beat Chris Jericho by DQ; Kane toppled Val Venis; Stacy Keibler battled Jackie Gayda to a no-contest; WWE World Tag Champions Rob Van Dam and Booker T rolled over The Dudley Boys; World Heavyweight Champion Chris Benoit and Shawn Michaels overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Randy Orton, Ric Flair, and Batista in a handicap match.

Air Date: 22nd March 2004
Chris Jericho bested Rene Dupree; Kane toppled Rico; Christian beat Spike Dudley; Ric Flair and Batista overturned WWE World Tag Champions Rob Van Dam and Booker T to win the titles; World Heavyweight Champion Chris Benoit toppled Rhyno; WWE World Champion Eddie Guerrero overcame Triple H by DQ.

Air Date: 29th March 2004
Nidia pinned Molly Holly; Rhyno overturned Lance Storm; WWE World Tag Champions Ric Flair and Batista overturned World Heavyweight Champion Chris Benoit and Shawn Michaels by DQ. Johnny Nitro was guest referee; Tajiri beat Kane by count-out in a no-DQ match; Shelton Benjamin bested Triple H.

Apr 2004

Air Date: 5th April 2004
World Heavyweight Champion Chris Benoit toppled Rob Conway; Chris Jericho bested Matt Hardy; Shelton Benjamin rolled over Triple H by count-out; Lita won a battle royal; Christian downed Tajiri; Kane toppled Rhyno.

Air Date: 12th April 2004
Kane toppled Brian Christopher; Tajiri beat The Masked Ninja; Lita battled Trish Stratus to a no-contest; The Hurricane dropped Sylvain Grenier; World Heavyweight Champion Chris Benoit, Shawn Michaels, Mick Foley, and Shelton Benjamin bested Triple H, WWE Intercontinental Champion Randy Orton, and WWE World Tag Champions Ric Flair and Batista.

Air Date: 19th April 2004
Christian beat Chris Jericho; WWE Women’s Champion Victoria toppled Molly Holly by DQ; Garrison Cade overcame Tajiri; World Heavyweight Champion Chris Benoit and Edge dropped WWE World Tag Champions Ric Flair and Batista to win the titles.

Air Date: 26th April 2004
WWE Women’s Champion Victoria and Lita beat Molly Holly and Gail Kim; Rhyno overcame Rob Conway; Christian downed Brian Christopher; Triple H rolled over Tajiri; Matt Hardy battled Kane to a no-contest; WWE World Tag Champions Chris Benoit and Edge dropped Ric Flair and Batista.

May 2004

Air Date: 3rd May 2004
Shelton Benjamin, Tajiri, and Edge dropped Triple H, Batista, and WWE Intercontinental Champion Randy Orton; Rob Conway beat The Hurricane; Kane toppled Steven Richards; Gail Kim beat WWE Women’s Champion Victoria in a non-title match; World Heavyweight Champion Chris Benoit toppled Shawn Michaels.

Air Date: 10th May 2004
Shelton Benjamin battled Triple H to a no-contest; Batista overturned Tajiri; WWE Intercontinental Champion Randy Orton rolled over Edge; Molly Holly, Gail Kim, and Jazz defeated WWE Women’s Champion Victoria, Nidia, and Stacy Keibler; Matt Hardy battled Val Venis to a no-contest; Eugene beat Rob Conway; Chris Jericho bested Christian in a cage match.

Air Date: 17th May 2004
Trish Stratus overcame Lita; Kane toppled Val Venis; WWE World Tag Champions Chris Benoit and Edge dropped WWE Intercontinental Champion Randy Orton and Batista; Kane won a battle royal.

Air Date: 24th May 2004
Edge toppled Ric Flair; Rob Conway and Sylvain Grenier dropped The Hurricane and Rosey; Chris Jericho and Shelton Benjamin bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Randy Orton and Batista; WWE Women’s Champion Victoria toppled Molly Holly; World Heavyweight Champion Chris Benoit and Eugene overcame Garrison Cade and Jonathan Coachman.

Air Date: 31st May 2004
Sylvain Grenier and Rob Conway beat WWE World Tag Champions Chris Benoit and Edge to win the titles; WWE Women’s Champion Victoria toppled Jazz; Shelton Benjamin bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Randy Orton in a non-title match; Matt Hardy beat Garrison Cade; Eugene overcame Kane by DQ.

Jun 2004

Air Date: 7th June 2004
Lita beat Gail Kim; Batista pinned Shelton Benjamin; Chris Jericho bested Albert; WWE World Tag Champions Sylvain Grenier and Rob Conway beat World Heavyweight Champion Chris Benoit by DQ in a handicap match; Kane and WWE World Tag Champions Sylvain Grenier and Rob Conway beat World Heavyweight Champion Chris Benoit and Edge in a handicap match; Eugene beat Johnny Nitro; Shawn Michaels overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Randy Orton by DQ.

Air Date: 14th June 2004
Matt Hardy and Lita beat Tyson Tomko and WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus; WWE World Tag Champions Rob Conway and Sylvain Grenier dropped The Hurricane and Rosey in a flag match; Gail Kim and Molly Holly downed Stacy Keibler and Nidia; Tajiri beat Albert; World Heavyweight Champion Chris Benoit, Edge, and Chris Jericho bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Randy Orton, Ric Flair, and Batista in an elimination match.

Air Date: 21st June 2004
WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus beat Victoria; WWE Intercontinental Champion Randy Orton and Batista overturned Edge and Chris Jericho; Eugene battled Triple H to a no-contest.

Air Date: 28th June 2004
Chris Jericho and Edge dropped WWE Intercontinental Champion Randy Orton and Batista; Triple H rolled over William Regal by DQ. Eugene was guest referee; WWE World Tag Champions Rob Conway and Sylvain Grenier dropped Sgt Slaughter and Rhyno; World Heavyweight Champion Chris Benoit toppled Kane.

Jul 2004

Air Date: 5th July 2004
WWE World Tag Champions Sylvain Grenier and Rob Conway beat Rhyno and Val Venis; Kane battled Batista to a no-contest; WWE Intercontinental Champion Randy Orton rolled over Chris Jericho; Nidia defeated Molly Holly and Victoria in a three-way “#1 contender contract-on-a-pole” match; WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus rolled over Nidia; Triple H, Ric Flair, and Eugene overcame World Heavyweight Champion Chris Benoit and Edge in a handicap match.

Air Date: 12th July 2004
WWE Intercontinental Champion Edge toppled Batista; Rhyno beat Rob Conway; Tyson Tomko downed Maven; Chris Jericho bested Kane by DQ; Ric Flair overturned The Hurricane; World Heavyweight Champion Chris Benoit battled Eugene to a no-contest.

Air Date: 19th July 2004
Sylvain Grenier beat Tajiri; Tyson Tomko defeated The Hurricane; World Heavyweight Champion Chris Benoit toppled Batista by DQ; Kane toppled Chris Jericho in a “falls count anywhere” match; WWE Intercontinental Champion Edge toppled Randy Orton.

Air Date: 26th July 2004
Randy Orton won a battle royal; World Heavyweight Champion Chris Benoit toppled Triple H in a 60-minute Ironman match. 4:3.

Aug 2004

Air Date: 2nd August 2004
Rhyno and Tajiri beat WWE World Tag Champions Sylvain Grenier and Rob Conway in a non-title match; Tyson Tomko overcame Rosey; William Regal bested Triple H by DQ; Kane toppled Maven; Randy Orton, Batista, and Ric Flair overturned World Heavyweight Champion Chris Benoit, WWE Intercontinental Champion Edge, and Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 9th August 2004
Victoria, Stacy Keibler, and Nidia defeated Molly Holly, Gail Kim, and Jazz; Chris Jericho bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Edge in a non-title match; Rhyno and Tajiri battled Brent Albright and Chris Cage to a draw; World Heavyweight Champion Chris Benoit toppled Triple H and Randy Orton by DQ in a handicap match.

Air Date: 16th August 2004
Rhyno overturned Sylvain Grenier; Victoria toppled Gail Kim; WWE Intercontinental Champion Edge dropped Kane; Chris Jericho bested Batista by DQ; World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton rolled over Chris Benoit.

Air Date: 23rd August 2004
Chris Jericho bested WWF Intercontinental Champion Edge by DQ; William Regal beat Ric Flair.

Air Date: 30th August 2004
Jonathan Coachman and WWE World Tag Champions Rob Conway and Sylvain Grenier dropped Rhyno and Tajiri in a handicap match; Batista overturned William Regal; Kane downed Fat Hardy (Skulu), Nat Hardy (Lil Nate), Pat Hardy (Shannon Ballard) and Rat Hardy (Shawn Riddick) in a handicap match; Chris Benoit toppled Ric Flair by DQ; Victoria and Nidia defeated WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus and Gail Kim; Eugene overcame Triple H in a no-DQ match.

Sep 2004

Air Date: 6th September 2004
Chris Benoit, Tajiri, Rhyno, and William Regal overcame Ric Flair, Batista, and WWE World Tag Champions Rob Conway and Sylvain Grenier; WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus overcame Nidia; Triple H rolled over Eugene in a cage match; Tyson Tomko bested Chris Jericho; Kane dropped World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton by DQ; World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton rolled over Kane in a cage match.

Air Date: 13th September 2004
Chris Benoit beat Robert Conway; Nidia, Stacy Keibler and Victoria defeated Gail Kim, Molly Holly and WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus; Shawn Michaels and WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho toppled Christian and Tyson Tomko; Kane battled Snitsky to a no-contest; Randy Orton rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Triple H, Batista, and Ric Flair by DQ in a handicap match.

Air Date: 20th September 2004
Victoria and Stacy Keibler dropped WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus and Molly Holly; Tajiri beat The Hurricane; Shawn Michaels battled WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho to a no-contest; Maven pinned Sylvan Grenier; Randy Orton, Chris Benoit, and Shelton Benjamin bested World Heavyweight Champion Triple H, Batista, and Ric Flair.

Air Date: 27th September 2004
Shelton Benjamin bested World Heavyweight Champion Triple H by DQ; The Hurricane and Rosey beat Rhyno and Tajiri; Snitsky overcame Val Venis; Chrisitan and Tyson Tomko downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho and Shawn Michaels; Batista overturned Randy Orton in a no-DQ match.

Oct 2004

Air Date: 4th October 2004
Shawn Michaels toppled Christian; Batista overturned Chris Benoit by DQ; WWE World Tag Champions Rob Conway and Sylvain Grenier defeated The Hurricane and Rosey; Stacy Keibler pinned Molly Holly; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho in a non-title lumberjack match.

Air Date: 11th October 2004
WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus overcame Stacy Keibler; WWE World Tag Champions Rob Conway and Sylvain Grenier defeated William Regal and Eugene; Chris Jericho battled Rhyno to a no-contest; WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho and Rhyno overturned Christian and Tyson Tomko; Chris Benoit, Edge, and Shawn Michaels rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Triple H, Ric Flair, and Batista.

Air Date: 18th October 2004
Snitsky overcame Eugene; Victoria, Stacy Keibler, and Nidia defeated WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus, Molly Holly, and Gail Kim; Ric Flair and Batista overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho and Randy Orton; Shelton Benjamin bested Christian, Rhyno, Tajiri, Jonathan Coachman, and The Hurricane in a six-way elimination match; Edge dropped Chris Benoit and Shawn Michaels in a three-way.

Air Date: 25th October 2004
WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin bested Chris Jericho; Maven pinned Batista; Snitsky overcame William Regal; Ric Flair overturned Randy Orton.

Nov 2004

Air Date: 1st November 2004
Snitsky overcame Eugene in a hardcore match; Christian downed The Hurricane; Rob Conway and Sylvain Grenier defeated WWE World Tag Champions Chris Benoit and Edge to win the titles; WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin bested Viscera; Randy Orton, Chris Jericho, and Maven defeated Ric Flair and Batista in a handicap match.

Air Date: 8th November 2004
Randy Orton rolled over Batista; WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin bested Tyson Tomko; Chris Benoit battled Edge to a no-contest.

Air Date: 15th November 2004
William Regal and Eugene overcame WWE World Tag Champions Rob Conway and Sylvain Grenier, and Rhyno and Tajiri in an elimination match to win the titles; Lita beat Molly Holly; Randy Orton rolled over Jonathan Coachman; Chris Benoit and WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin bested Edge and Christian; Christie Hemme pinned Stacy Keibler in a “lingerie pillow fight”; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H rolled over Maven.

Air Date: 22nd November 2004
Snitsky overcame Maven; Chris Jericho bested Batista by DQ; Rhyno overturned Jonathan Coachman; WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus overcame Lita and Molly Holly in a three-way; WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin, and WWE World Tag Champions Eugene and William Regal overcame Christian, Rob Conway, and Sylvain Grenier; Chris Benoit toppled Edge in a cage match.

Air Date: 29th November 2004
Chris Benoit and Edge co-won a battle royal; Lita and Victoria toppled WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus and Molly Holly; Ric Flair overturned Jerry Lawler; WWE World Tag Champions Eugene and William Regal overcame Rob Conway and Sylvain Grenier; Chris Benoit, Edge, and World Heavyweight Champion Triple H battled to a no-contest in a three-way.

Dec 2004

Air Date: 6th December 2004
Eugene overcame Maven by DQ; Simon Dean pinned The Hurricane; WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin downed Christian; Chris Benoit and Chris Jericho bested Triple H and Batista by DQ; Lita beat WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus to win the title.

Air Date: 13th December 2004
Randy Orton rolled over Edge; Triple H and Batista defeated Chris Benoit and Chris Jericho; Christian, Tyson Tomko, and Maven downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin, and WWE World Tag Champions Eugene and William Regal.

Air Date: 20th December 2004
Year-in-Review highlight show

Air Date: 27th December 2004
Chris Jericho bested Christian; Randy Orton rolled over Maven; WWE Women’s Champion Lita beat Molly Holly; Simon Dean pinned Rosey; Edge dropped Eugene; Chris Benoit toppled Viscera; Batista overturned Rhyno; Triple H rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin in a non-title match.
