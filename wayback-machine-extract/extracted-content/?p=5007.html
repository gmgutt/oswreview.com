OSW Update for January!


Yo! Happy new year! Here’s the scoop on OSW:
• Myself and the boys recorded the 2nd (well technically 3rd) Tattoo review, IWA King of the DeathMatch ’95, before Christmas! I used new equipment to record it (to combine three audio streams) but there’s a minor but noticeable drift with my audio, so I’m having to sync it manually which is horrific! Was hoping to have it out tonight but editing is a lot slower than I’d hoped, still needs a session or two of work. Will update everyone when I’ve finished my first pass.
