Raw Results 1996

WWF RAW RESULTS (1996)

Jan 1996

Air Date: 1st January 1996
WWF World Tag Champions The Smoking Gunns overcame Sid Vicious and The 1-2-3 Kid, WWF Intercontinental Champion Razor Ramon and Savio Vega, and Owen Hart and Yokozuna in a four-way “Raw Bowl” elimination match; Diesel pinned King Mabel.

Air Date: 8th January 1996
Jeff Jarrett beat Hakushi; Ahmed Johnson defeated Jeff Brettler; Goldust dropped Aldo Montoya.

Air Date: 15th January 1996
Owen Hart defeated Marty Jannetty; Steve Austin bested Matt Hardy; WWF World Tag Champions The Smoking Gunns overcame The Spiders; The Undertaker rolled over Issac Yankem.

Air Date: 22nd January 1996
Vader bested Savio Vega; Hunter Hearst Helmsley downed Razor Ramon by count-out; WWF World Champion Bret Hart rolled over WWF Intercontinental Champion Goldust in a non-title match.

Air Date: 29th January 1996
Diesel overcame Davey Boy Smith; The Godwinns toppled Skip and Zip; Shawn Michaels pinned Yokozuna.

Feb 1996

Air Date: 5th February 1996
Shawn Michaels and Diesel toppled Davey Boy Smith and Yokozuna by count-out; The 1-2-3 Kid overcame Hakushi; WWF World Champion Bret Hart battled The Undertaker to a no-contest.

There was no show on 02/12. Raw was pre-empted for the Westminister Dog Show.

Air Date: 19th February 1996
Razor Ramon beat WWF Intercontinental Champion Goldust by count-out; Skip and Zip battled Barry Horowitz and Aldo Montoya to a no-contest; Steve Austin dropped Marty Jannetty; The Undertaker toppled Tatanka.

Air Date: 26th February 1996
Jake Roberts downed Isaac Yankem; Diesel pinned Bob Holly; Ahmed Johnson overturned Shinobu; Yokozuna overturned Owen Hart and Davey Boy Smith by DQ in a handicap match.

Mar 1996

Air Date: 4th March 1996
Shawn Michaels overcame The 1-2-3 Kid; Justin Bradshaw bested Hakushi; WWF World Champion Bret Hart rolled over Hunter Hearst Helmsley.

Air Date: 11th March 1996
Savio Vega battled Steve Austin to a double count-out; The Godwinns beat Jerry Meade and Alex Porteau; Hunter Hearst Helmsley squashed Vin Grier; The Undertaker and Yokozuna bested Owen Hart and Davey Boy Smith by DQ.

Air Date: 18th March 1996
Davey Boy Smith toppled Jake Roberts by count-out; WWF Intercontinental Champion Goldust bested Fatu; Diesel annihilated Barry Horowitz; WWF World Champion Bret Hart toppled Tatanka.

Air Date 25th March 1996
Shawn Michaels downed Leif Cassidy; Hunter Hearst Helmsley overcame Aldo Montoya; Ahmed Johnson bested Owen Hart by DQ.

Apr 1996

Air Date: 1st April 1996
Mankind beat Bob Holly; Marc Mero downed Isaac Yankem; WWF World Tag Champions Skip and Zip overcame Aldo Montoya and Barry Horowitz; The Undertaker bested Justin Bradshaw by DQ.

Air Date: 8th April 1996
Ahmed Johnson beat Davey Boy Smith in an arm wrestling match; Vader toppled Yokozuna via stoppage; Hunter Hearst Helmsley overturned Duke Droese; WWF World Champion Shawn Michaels defeated Jerry Lawler.

Air Date: 15th April 1996
Marc Mero downed Leif Cassidy; Steve Austin dropped Bart Gunn; Savio Vega overcame WWF Intercontinental Champion Goldust to ostensibly win the title. The decision was subsequently overturned and the title declared vacant.

Air Date: 22nd April 1996
Goldust pinned Savio Vega to win the vacant WWF Intercontinental Title; Vader bested Fatu; The Godwinns overcame Yoshihiro Tajiri and Ken Patterson; Mankind rolled over Aldo Montoya.

Air Date: 29th April 1996
Mankind beat Bart Gunn; Steve Austin downed Scott Taylor; The Godwinns battled Marty Jannetty and Leif Cassidy to a double count-out; The Ultimate Warrior bested Isaac Yankem.

May 1996

Air Date: 6th May 1996
Marc Mero dropped The 1-2-3 Kid; Davey Boy Smith downed Fatu; WWF World Tag Champions Skip and Zip overcame Techno Team 2000; The Undertaker pinned Owen Hart.

Air Date: 13th May 1996
Ahmed Johnson defeated Zip; Vader overturned Duke Droese; Justin Bradshaw downed Aldo Montoya; WWF World Champion Shawn Michaels rolled over Hunter Hearst Helmsley.

Air Date: 20th May 1996
Steve Austin toppled Marc Mero by DQ; Savio Vega bested The 1-2-3 Kid; Davey Boy Smith battled Jake Roberts to a no-contest;

Air Date: 27th May 1996
The Ultimate Warrior battled WWF Intercontinental Champion Goldust to a double count-out (KOTR Qualifying match); WWF World Tag Champions The Smoking Gunns overcame Skip and Zip; Vader toppled Ahmed Johnson (KOTR Qualifiying match).

Jun 1996

Air Date: 3rd June 1996
Steve Austin defeated Bob Holly (KOTR Qualifying match); Mankind dropped Barry Horowitz; The Godwinns overcame Techno Team 2000; Jake Roberts toppled Hunter Hearst Helmsley (KOTR Qualifying match).

Air Date: 10th June 1996
Owen Hart pinned Yokozuna (KOTR Qualifying match); Marc Mero downed Skip (KOTR Qualifying match); Davey Boy Smith overcame The Undertaker by count-out.

Air Date: 17th June 1996
Steve Austin bested Savio Vega (KOTR Quarter-Finals); Marc Mero dropped Owen Hart (KOTR Quarter-Finals); Hunter Hearst Helmsley battled Aldo Montoya to a no-contest; Jake Roberts toppled WWF Intercontinental Champion Goldust by reverse decision-DQ.

Air Date: 24th June 1996
WWF Intercontinental Champion Ahmed Johnson dropped Hunter Hearst Helmsley; Skip and Zip defeated The Brooklyn Brawler and Jerry Fox; Vader, Owen Hart, and Davey Boy Smith downed Aldo Montoya, Savio Vega, and Barry Horowitz; The Undertaker toppled Steve Austin by DQ.

Jul 1996

Air Date: 1st July 1996
WWF World Champion Shawn Michaels pinned Marty Jannetty; Mankind bested Duke Droese; Goldust overcame Marc Mero.

Air Date: 8th July 1996
The Ultimate Warrior overcame Owen Hart by DQ; Savio Vega downed Justin Bradshaw; Davey Boy Smith and Vader bested The Godwinns.

Air Date: 15th July 1996
WWF Intercontinental Champion Ahmed Johnson dropped Bart Gunn; Marc Mero defeated TL Hopper; WWF World Champion Shawn Michaels rolled over Billy Gunn.

Air Date: 22nd July 1996
WWF World Champion Shawn Michaels and WWF Intercontinental Champion Ahmed Johnson battled WWF World Tag Champions The Smoking Gunns to a no contest; Marc Mero beat The Goon; Mankind bested Freddie Joe Floyd; Goldust pinned Barry Horowitz; WWF World Champion Shawn Michaels and WWF Intercontinental Champion Ahmed Johnson dropped WWF World Tag Champions The Smoking Gunns by DQ.

Air Date: 29th July 1996
Sid Vicious defeated Justin Bradshaw by DQ; Vader pinned Marc Mero; Davey Boy Smith beat Henry Godwinn; Steve Austin bested The Undertaker by count-out.

Aug 1996

Air Date: 5th August 1996
Jerry Lawler pinned Aldo Montoya; Skip and Zip defeated Marty Jannetty and Leif Cassidy by DQ; WWF Intercontinental Champion Ahmed Johnson won a battle royal.

Air Date: 12th August 1996
Faarooq beat Skip; Crush defeated Savio Vega; The Godwinns downed TL Hopper and Who; WWF World Champion Shawn Michaels pinned Owen Hart.

Air Date: 19th August 1996
Owen Hart defeated Davey Boy Smith by count-out (WWF Intercontinental Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); Vader toppled Freddie Joe Floyd; Goldust won a 4-man battle royal; WWF World Champion Shawn Michaels pinned Yokozuna.

There was no show on 26th August 1996

Sep 1996

Air Date: 6th September 1996
Aired on Friday
Sid Vicious pinned Hunter Hearst Helmsley (WWF Intercontinental Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); Marc Mero downed Steve Austin by DQ (WWF Intercontinental Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); Mankind dropped Alex Porteau; WWF World Champion Shawn Michaels bested Goldust.

Air Date: 9th September 1996
Faarooq toppled Savio Vega (WWF Intercontinental Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); Barry Windham pinned TL Hopper; Crush beat Freddie Joe Floyd; The Undertaker toppled Salvatore Sincere.

Air Date: 16th September 1996
The Sultan overcame Jake Roberts; Bob Holly and Alex Porteau defeated WWF World Tag Champions The Smoking Gunns in a non-title match; Marc Mero pinned Owen Hart (WWF Intercontinental Title Tournament – Semi-Final); Faarooq bested Sid Vicious by DQ (WWF Intercontinental Title Tournament – Semi-Final).

Air Date: 23rd September 1996
Marc Mero beat Faarooq to win the vacant WWF Intercontinental Title. Pat Patterson was guest referee (WWF Intercontinental Title Tournament – Final); Vader pinned Henry Godwinn; WWF World Tag Champions Owen Hart and Davey Boy Smith defeated Skip and Zip; Barry Windham downed Hunter Hearst Helmsley.

Air Date: 30th September 1996
Steve Austin bested Jake Roberts; The Godwinns defeated Jason and Jared Grimm; Savio Vega downed Razor Ramon II (Rick Bogner) by DQ; Vader and Jim Cornette toppled WWF World Champion Shawn Michaels and Jose Lothario.

Oct 1996

Air Date: 7th October 1996
WWF Intercontinental Champion Marc Mero defeated Diesel II (Glen Jacobs) by DQ; The Smoking Gunns overturned Marty Jannetty and Leif Cassidy; The Sultan downed Aldo Montoya; Sid Vicious pinned Goldust.

Air Date: 14th October 1996
Vader pinned Phinneas Godwinn; Jake Roberts overcame Jerry Lawler; Freddie Joe Floyd defeated Hunter Hearst Helmsley by count-out; Faarooq downed Alex Porteau; WWF World Champion Shawn Michaels defeated Steve Austin by DQ.

Air Date: 21st October 1996
Sid Vicious overcame Owen Hart by DQ; The Godwinns defeated The Smoking Gunns; Hunter Hearst Helmsley pinned WWF Intercontinental Champion Marc Mero to win the title.

Air Date: 28th October 1996
Jesse Jammes pinned Salvatore Sincere; Crush beat Aldo Montoya; Billy Gunn downed Freddie Joe Floyd; WWF World Champion Shawn Michaels rolled over Davey Boy Smith by DQ.

Nov 1996

Air Date: 4th November 1996
Goldust battled Barry Windham to a double-DQ; The Sultan dropped Alex Porteau; Razor Ramon II toppled Marc Mero.

Air Date: 11th November 1996
WWF World Tag Champions Owen Hart and Davey Boy Smith defeated WWF World Champion Shawn Michaels and Sid Vicious; Mankind bested Freddie Joe Floyd; Steve Austin toppled Bob Holly.

Air Date: 18th November 1996
Steve Austin bested Mankind by DQ in a “no-holds-barred” match; Faarooq pinned Savio Vega; Doug Furnas and Phil LaFon downed Leif Cassidy and Bob Holly.

Air Date: 25th November 1996
Bret Hart defeated Owen Hart by DQ; The Executioner downed Freddie Joe Floyd; Rocky Maivia overcame Salvatore Sincere; Marc Mero dropped Billy Gunn by DQ.

Dec 1996

Air Date: 2nd December 1996
Flash Funk beat The Goon; Diesel II defeated Phinneas Godwinn; Justin Bradshaw overturned Jesse Jammes; Marc Mero and Jake Roberts dropped WWF Intercontinental Champion Hunter Hearst Helmsley and Billy Gunn.

Air Date: 9th December 1996
WWF World Champion Sid Vicious dropped WWF Intercontinental Champion Hunter Hearst Helmsley by count-out; Goldust pinned Bart Gunn; Jesse Jammes dropped Justin Bradshaw; The Undertaker toppled Mankind in a “no-holds-barred” match.

Air Date: 16th December 1996
Vader battled Steve Austin to a no-contest; Diesel II and Razor Ramon II defeated The Godwinns; Doug Furnas and Phil LaFon downed TL Hopper and Doctor X; Bart Gunn downed Billy Gunn by stoppage.

Air Date: 23rd December 1996
Marc Mero defeated WWF Intercontinental Champion Hunter Hearst Helmsley by count-out; Rocky Maivia pinned Salvatore Sincere; Pierroth and Cibernetico downed Marty Jannetty and Leif Cassidy; Bret Hart dropped Razor Ramon II.

Air Date: 30th December 1996
Bret Hart and Savio Vega downed Steve Austin and Faarooq by DQ; WWF Intercontinental Champion Hunter Hearst Helmsley pinned Flash Funk; Jerry Lawler defeated Goldust by count-out.
