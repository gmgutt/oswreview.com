WWF PPV Results 1985-1987

WWF PPV RESULTS (1985-1987)

See also: WWF PPV Statistics 1985-1989

7th November 1985: “The Wrestling Classic”: Rosemont, IL – 14,000 (12,000 paid)
($148,000 live gate)
PPV Broadcast: Adrian Adonis toppled Corporal Kirschner (Wrestling Classic Tournament – First Round); Dynamite Kid dropped Nikolai Volkoff (Wrestling Classic Tournament – First Round); Randy Savage pinned Ivan Putski (Wrestling Classic Tournament – First Round); Ricky Steamboat bested Davey Boy Smith by stoppage (Wrestling Classic Tournament – First Round); The Junkyard Dog overturned The Iron Sheik (Wrestling Classic Tournament – First Round); Moondog Spot overcame Terry Funk by count-out (Wrestling Classic Tournament – First Round); WWF Intercontinental Champion Tito Santana defeated Don Muraco (Wrestling Classic Tournament – First Round); Paul Orndorff beat Bob Orton Jr by DQ (Wrestling Classic Tournament – First Round); Dynamite Kid downed Adrian Adonis (Wrestling Classic Tournament – Quarter-Final); Randy Savage toppled Ricky Steamboat (Wrestling Classic Tournament – Quarter-Final); The Junkyard Dog dropped Moondog Spot (Wrestling Classic Tournament – Quarter-Final); WWF Intercontinental Champion Tito Santana battled Paul Orndorff to a double count-out (Wrestling Classic Tournament – Quarter-Final); WWF World Champion Hulk Hogan bested Roddy Piper by DQ; Randy Savage pinned Dynamite Kid (Wrestling Classic Tournament – Semi-Final); The Junkyard Dog rolled over Randy Savage by count-out (Wrestling Classic Tournament – Final).

7th April 1986: “WWF Wrestlemania II”
Closed Circuit: 319,000 ($4,000,0000)
Uniondale, NY: (16,585 sellout – $414,615): Don Muraco battled Paul Ordorff to a double count-out; WWF Intercontinental Champion Randy Savage pinned George Steele; Jake Roberts beat George Wells; Mr T defeated Roddy Piper by DQ in a boxing match.
Rosemont, IL: (9,000 – $294,000): WWF Women’s Champion Fabulous Moolah downed Velvet McIntyre; Corporal Kirschner dropped Nikolai Volkoff in a flag match; The British Bulldogs bested WWF World Tag Champions Greg Valentine and Brutus Beefcake to win the titles; Andre the Giant won a battle royal.
Los Angeles, CA: (14,500 – $380,000): Ricky Steamboat overcame Hercules; Adrian Adonis downed Uncle Elmer; Terry Funk and Dory Funk Jr overturned The Junkyard Dog and Tito Santana; WWF World Champion Hulk Hogan rolled over King Kong Bundy in a cage match.

29th March 1987: “WWF Wrestlemania III”: Pontiac, MI – 78,000 sellout (75,700 paid)
($1,599,000 live gate)
Closed Circuit: 441,000 ($5,200,000)
PPV Broadcast:  Rick Martel and Tom Zenk defeated Don Muraco and Bob Orton Jr; Billy Jack Haynes battled Hercules to a double count-out; Hillbilly Jim, The Haiti Kid, and Little Beaver toppled King Kong Bundy, Lord Littlebrook, and Little Tokyo by DQ; Harley Race pinned The Junkyard Dog; Greg Valentine and Brutus Beefcake downed Jacques Rougeau and Raymond Rougeau; Roddy Piper toppled Adrian Adonis in a “Hair vs. Hair” match; Danny Davis and WWF World Tag Champions Bret Hart and Jim Neidhart dropped Tito Santana, Davey Boy Smith, and The Dynamite Kid; Butch Reed beat Koko B Ware; Ricky Steamboat bested WWF Intercontinental Champion Randy Savage to win the title; The Honkytonk Man overturned Jake Roberts; Nikolai Volkoff and The Iron Sheik defeated B Brian Blair and Jim Brunzell by DQ; WWF World Champion Hulk Hogan rolled over Andre the Giant.

26th November 1987: “WWF Survivor Series 1987”: Richfield, OH – 21,300 sellout
PPV Broadcast:  Randy Savage, Jim Duggan, Jake Roberts, Ricky Steamboat, and Brutus Beefcake bested WWF Intercontinental Champion The Honkytonk Man, King Harley Race, Danny Davis, Hercules, and Ron Bass in a “Survivor Series” elimination match; The Fabulous Moolah, Velvet McIntyre, Rockin Robin, and The Jumping Bomb Angels overturned WWF Women’s Champion Sensational Sherri, WWF Women’s Tag Team Champions Lelani Kai and Judy Martin, Dawn Marie, and Donna Christianello in a “Survivor Series” elimination match; WWF World Tag Champions Tito Santana and Rick Martel, Davey Boy Smith and The Dynamite Kid, Jacques and Raymond Rougeau, Jim Powers and Paul Roma, and B Brian Blair and Jim Brunzell defeated Bret Hart and Jim Neidhart, The Islanders, Nikolai Volkoff and Boris Zhukov, Demolition, and Dino Bravo and Greg Valentine in a “Survivor Series” elimination match; Andre The Giant, Rick Rude, King Kong Bundy, Butch Reed, and The One Man Gang rolled over WWF World Champion Hulk Hogan, Bam Bam Bigelow, Paul Orndorff, Ken Patera, and Don Muraco in a “Survivor Series” elimination match.
