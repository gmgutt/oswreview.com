WWE Superstars Results 2015

WWE SUPERSTARS RESULTS (2015)

Jan 2015

Air Date: 1st January 2015
Erick Rowan overcame Tyson Kidd; Fandango bested R-Truth.

Air Date: 8th January 2015
Jack Swagger toppled Titus O’Neil; Goldust and Stardust rolled over Los Matadores.

Air Date: 16th January 2015
Summer Rae downed Cameron; Fandango, Goldust and Stardust rolled over Los Matadores and Justin Gabriel.

Air Date: 23rd January 2015
Naomi dropped Cameron; Fandango bested Sin Cara.

Air Date: 30th January 2015
The scheduled RAW taping on 1/26 in Hartford, CT was cancelled due to a travel ban on all roads from 9pm onwards due to a blizzard. No new footage aired.

Feb 2015

Air Date: 6th February 2015
Summer Rae and Cameron overcame Emma and Naomi; Fandango bested Adam Rose.

Air Date: 13th February 2015
Titus O’Neil beat Jack Swagger; Luke Harper overcame R-Truth.

Air Date: 20th February 2015
Fandango bested Adam Rose; Jack Swagger toppled Curtis Axel.

Air Date: 27th February 2015
Erick Rowan toppled Adam Rose; Luke Harper overturned Kofi Kingston.

Mar 2015

Air Date: 5th March 2015
The Ascension toppled Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; Zack Ryder overturned Adam Rose.

Air Date: 12th March 2015
Sin Cara and Kalisto bested The Ascension; Zack Ryder overturned Adam Rose.

Air Date: 19th March 2015
Jack Swagger toppled Adam Rose; Sin Cara and Kalisto bested The Ascension.

Air Date: 26th March 2015
Summer Rae downed Emma; Xavier Woods and Kofi Kingston overturned The Ascension.

Apr 2015

Air Date: 3rd April 2015
Darren Young and Titus O’Neill beat Adam Rose and Heath Slater; Fandango defeated Jack Swagger; R-Truth pinned Luke Harper.

Air Date: 10th April 2015
Jack Swagger overturned Adam Rose; R-Truth toppled Curtis Axel.

Air Date: 17th April 2015
R-Truth beat Curtis Axel; Zack Ryder toppled Heath Slater.

Air Date: 24th April 2015
Bad News Barrett beat Jack Swagger; WWE World Tag Champions Tyson Kidd and Cesaro bested Los Matadores.

May 2015

Air Date: 1st May 2015
Titus O’Neil and Darren Young beat Los Matadores; Sin Cara and Kalisto bested The Ascension.

Air Date: 8th May 2015
Luke Harper overcame Jack Swagger; Sin Cara and Kalisto bested Los Matadores.

Air Date: 15th May 2015
Kalisto overcame Heath Slater; Stardust toppled R-Truth.

Air Date: 22nd May 2015
Alicia Fox beat Summer Rae; Damien Sandow & Curtis Axel bested Heath Slater & Adam Rose.

Air Date: 29th May 2015
Heath Slater overturned Adam Rose; Luke Harper & Erick Rowan toppled Curtis Axel & Macho Mandow.

Jun 2015

Air Date: 5th June 2015
Sin Cara & Kalisto toppled The Ascension; Luke Harper & Erick Rowan dropped Los Matadores.

Air Date: 12th June 2015
Paige downed Layla; The Ascension dropped Sin Cara & Kalisto.

Air Date: 19th June 2015
Cesaro bested Bo Dallas; Luke Harper & Erick Rowan won three-way over The Ascension and Los Matadores.

Air Date: 26th June 2015
The Lucha Dragons beat Bo Dallas & Heath Slater; Cesaro overcame Luke Harper.

Jul 2015

Air Date: 3rd July 2015
The Ascension overcame Macho Mandow & Curtis Axel; Adam Rose bested Fandango.

Air Date: 10th July 2015
Neville downed Heath Slater; WWE World Tag Champions Titus O’Neil & Darren Young beat The Ascension.

Air Date: 17th July 2015
Jack Swagger beat Adam Rose; Sin Cara, Kalisto & Zack Ryder overcame The Ascension & Bo Dallas.

Air Date: 24th July 2015
R-Truth defeated Heath Slater; Neville downed Bo Dallas.

Air Date: 31st July 2015
Bo Dallas beat R-Truth; King Barrett defeated Jack Swagger.

Aug 2015

Air Date: 7th August 2015
Fandango dropped Heath Slater; Jack Swagger rolled over Bo Dallas.

Air Date: 14th August 2015
The Ascension beat Jack Swagger and Fandango; Bo Dallas defeated R-Truth.

Air Date: 21st August 2015
Curtis Axel beat Adam Rose; The Ascension defeated Fandango & R-Truth.

Air Date: 28th August 2015
Zack Ryder beat Heath Slater; Los Matadores defeated The Ascension.

Sep 2015

Air Date: 4th September 2015
R-Truth defeated Adam Rose; Neville, Sin Cara and Kalisto bested Stardust and The Ascension.

Air Date: 11th September 2015
Neville downed Bo Dallas; Stardust beat Jack Swagger.

Air Date: 18th September 2015
Zack Ryder overturned Adam Rose; Bo Dallas rolled over Jack Swagger.

Air Date: 25th September 2015
R-Truth defeated Heath Slater; Jack Swagger overturned Adam Rose.

Oct 2015

Air Date: 2nd October 2015
Cesaro bested Heath Slater; Sin Cara and Kalisto bested The Ascension.

Air Date: 9th October 2015
Cesaro bested Adam Rose; Titus O’Neil and Darren Young toppled The Ascension.

Air Date: 16th October 2015
Jack Swagger overturned Adam Rose; Stardust beat Fandango.

Air Date: 23rd October 2015
Fandango bested Heath Slater; Stardust beat Zack Ryder.

Air Date: 30th October 2015
Kalisto bested Viktor; Bo Dallas rolled over Fandango.

Nov 2015

Air Date: 6th November 2015
Neville downed Heath Slater; Bo Dallas overcame Zack Ryder.

Air Date: 13th November 2015
Fandango bested Bo Dallas; The Dudley Boys and Ryback beat The Ascension and Stardust.

Air Date: 20th November 2015
Brie Bella defeated Naomi; Titus O’Neil beat Bo Dallas.

Air Date: 27th November 2015
R-Truth toppled Adam Rose; The Miz beat Zack Ryder.

Dec 2015

Air Date: 4th December 2015
Zack Ryder rolled over Heath Slater; Neville dropped Bo Dallas.

Air Date: 11th December 2015
Fandango toppled Adam Rose; Bo Dallas rolled over Darren Young.

Air Date: 18th December 2015
Goldust beat Adam Rose; Fandango bested Heath Slater.

Air Date: 25th December 2015
Best of 2015 Show. No original matches took place.
