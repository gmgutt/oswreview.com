WWE Main Event Results 2015

WWE MAIN EVENT RESULTS (2015)

Jan 2015

Air Date: 6th January 2015
Paige downed WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella in a non-title match; Fandango bested R-Truth; Titus O’Neil beat Jack Swagger.

Air Date: 13th January 2015
Los Matadores rolled over Goldust and Stardust; The Ascension won a squash; WWE US Champion Rusev beat Justin Gabriel; Big E, Kofi Kingston and Xavier Woods rolled over Cesaro, Tyson Kidd and Adam Rose.

Air Date: 20th January 2015
Kofi Kingston overcame Tyson Kidd; The Miz and Damien Mizdow beat Los Matadores; Natalya defeated Summer Rae; Bray Wyatt rolled over Jack Swagger.

Air Date: 30th January 2015
Big E and Kofi Kingston defeated Damien Mizdow and The Miz; Erick Rowan downed Curtis Axel; Dolph Ziggler pinned Luke Harper.

Feb 2015

Air Date: 3rd February 2015
WWE Intercontinental Champion Bad News Barrett beat Sin Cara; Brie Bella defeated Summer Rae; The Ascension overcame Danger Dean and Jason Jaxon; Tyson Kidd and Cesaro bested Kofi Kingston and Big E.

Air Date: 13th February 2015
The Ascension won a squash; Naomi dropped Natalya; Cesaro bested Sin Cara.

Air Date: 21st February 2015
Sin Cara and Kalisto bested Heath Slater and Curtis Axel; Stardust overturned Zack Ryder; Titus O’Neil beat Adam Rose by DQ; Cesaro bested Jack Swagger.

Air Date: 28th February 2015
WWE World Tag Champions Tyson Kidd and Cesaro bested Sin Cara and Kalisto; The Bella Twins dropped Paige and Emma; Stardust beat Kofi Kingston.

Mar 2015

Air Date: 7th March 2015
Fandango bested Curtis Axel; Adam Rose downed Zack Ryder; Paige downed Summer Rae; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso dropped The Ascension.

Air Date: 14th March 2015
Erick Rowan toppled Curtis Axel; Fandango bested Adam Rose; Titus O’Neil and Darren Young beat The Ascension; Naomi dropped WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella in a non-title match.

Air Date: 21st March 2015
Curtis Axel beat Jack Swagger; Naomi dropped Alicia Fox; Zack Ryder toppled Adam Rose; Ryback pinned The Miz.

Air Date: 28th March 2015
Fandango bested Heath Slater; Naomi dropped Alicia Fox.

Apr 2015

Air Date: 4th April 2015
Stardust beat Jack Swagger; Paige and Emma dropped Summer Rae and Cameron; Kalisto and Sin Cara toppled The Ascension; Titus O’Neill and Darren Young overturned Big E and Xavier Woods.

Air Date: 11th April 2015
Fandango bested R-Truth; Summer Rae downed Emma; Mark Henry beat Heath Slater; Jack Swagger overturned The Miz.

Air Date: 18th April 2015
Bo Dallas overturned Zack Ryder; Layla defeated Emma; Jimmy Uso bested Xavier Woods; Rusev beat Dolph Ziggler.

Air Date: 25th April 2015
Fandango bested Curtis Axel; Brie Bella defeated Alicia Fox; Stardust beat R-Truth; Sin Cara and Kalisto bested The Ascension.
