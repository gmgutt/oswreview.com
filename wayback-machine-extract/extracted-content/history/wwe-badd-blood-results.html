WWE Badd Blood Results

WWF/E BADD BLOOD RESULTS

5th October 1997: “WWF IYH: Badd Blood”: St Louis, MO – 21,151 sellout (17,404 paid)
($212,550 live gate)
($101,115 merchandise sales)
Brian Pillman was scheduled for the show in singles matches against Dude Love and Goldust, but had died earlier that day.
The Rock, D-Lo Brown and Kama beat The Legion of Doom in a handicap match; Max Mini and Nova overturned Tarantula and Mosaic; The Godwinns downed WWF World Tag Champions The Headbangers to win the titles; Owen Hart toppled Faarooq to win the vacant WWF Intercontinental Title (WWF Intercontinental Title Tournament – Final); Crush, Chainz, Skull and Eight-Ball dropped Savio Vega, Miguel Perez, Jose Estrada and Jesus Castillo; WWF World Champion Bret Hart and Davey Boy Smith bested The Patriot and Vader in a flag match; WWF European Champion Shawn Michaels rolled over The Undertaker in a “Hell-in-a-Cell” match.

15th June 2003: “WWE Bad Blood 2003”: Houston, TX – 10,000
($500,000 live gate)
Heat: (06/15): Ivory beat Molly Holly.
PPV Broadcast: Christopher Nowinski and Rodney Mack beat The Dudley Boys; Scott Steiner dropped Test; Booker T overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian by DQ; Sylvain Grenier and Rene Dupree downed WWE World Tag Champions Rob Van Dam and Kane to win the titles; Bill Goldberg pinned Chris Jericho; Ric Flair overturned Shawn Michaels; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H rolled over Kevin Nash in a “Hell in a Cell” match. Mick Foley was guest referee.

13th June 2004: “WWE Bad Blood 2004”: Columbus, OH – 9,000 (7,500 paid)
($494,000 live gate)
Heat: (06/13): Batista overturned Maven.
PPV Broadcast: World Heavyweight Champion Chris Benoit and Edge dropped WWE World Tag Champions Rob Conway and Sylvain Grenier by DQ; Chris Jericho bested Tyson Tomko; WWE Intercontinental Champion Randy Orton rolled over Shelton Benjamin; Trish Stratus won a four-way over Lita, Gail Kim, and WWE Women’s Champion Victoria to win the title; Eugene overcame Jonathan Coachman; World Heavyweight Champion Chris Benoit toppled Kane; Triple H rolled over Shawn Michaels in a “Hell in a Cell” match.
