WWE PPV Results 2017

WWE PPV RESULTS (2017)

See also: WWE Network Statistics

29th January 2017: “WWE Royal Rumble 2017”: San Antonio, TX – 42,000
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Kickoff show: Becky Lynch, Nikki Bella and Naomi dropped Alexa Bliss, Mickie James and Natalya; Luke Gallows and Karl Anderson overcame WWE Raw Tag Champions Cesaro and Sheamus to win the titles; Nia Jax defeated Sasha Banks.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Raw Women’s Champion Charlotte Flair defeated Bayley; WWE Universal Champion Kevin Owens defeated Roman Reigns in a no-DQ match; Neville downed WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rich Swann to win the title; John Cena beat WWE World Champion AJ Styles to win the title; Randy Orton won the 30-man Royal Rumble, last eliminating Roman Reigns.

12th February 2017: “WWE Elimination Chamber 2017”: Phoenix, AZ – 11,000
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Mojo Rawley toppled Curt Hawkins.
PPV Broadcast: Becky Lynch defeated Mickie James; Apollo Crews and Kalisto bested Dolph Ziggler in a handicap match; WWE Smackdown Tag Champions Jason Jordan and Chad Gable won a Tag Team Turmoil match over Simon Gotch and Aiden English, Fandango and Tyler Breeze, Heath Slater and Rhyno, Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso, and Konnor and Viktor; Natalya battled Nikki Bella to a double count-out; Randy Orton overcame Luke Harper; Naomi dropped WWE Smackdown Women’s Champion Alexa Bliss to win the title; Bray Wyatt won an Elimination Chamber match over WWE World Champion John Cena, AJ Styles, Baron Corbin, WWE Intercontinental Champion Dean Ambrose and The Miz to win the title.

5th March 2017: “WWE Fastlane 2017”: Milwaukee, WI – 11,000
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Akira Tozawa and Rich Swann overcame Brian Kendrick and Noam Dar.
PPV Broadcast: Samoa Joe downed Sami Zayn; WWE Raw Tag Champions Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows defeated Big Cass and Enzo Amore; Sasha Banks overturned Nia Jax; Cesaro bested Jinder Mahal; The Big Show defeated Rusev; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Neville defeated Jack Gallagher; Roman Reigns overturned Braun Strowman; WWE Raw Women’s Champion Bayley defeated Charlotte Flair; Goldberg defeated WWE Universal Champion Kevin Owens to win the title.

2nd April 2017: “WWE Wrestlemania 33”: Orlando, FL (Camping World Stadium) – 62,000 sellout
($14,500,000 live gate – includes ticket service charge)
($3,700,000 merchandise sales)
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Wrestlemania Kickoff Show: WWE Cruiserweight Champion Neville defeated Austin Aries; Mojo Rawley won the “Andre The Giant Memorial” battle royal; WWE Intercontinental Champion Dean Ambrose defeated Baron Corbin.
PPV Broadcast: AJ Styles overturned Shane McMahon; Kevin Owens overturned WWE US Champion Chris Jericho to win the title; WWE Raw Women’s Champion Bayley defeated Charlotte Flair, Nia Jax and Sasha Banks in a four-way elimination match; Matt Hardy and Jeff Hardy toppled Enzo Amore and Big Cass, Sheamus and Cesaro, and WWE Raw Tag Champions Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows to win the title in a four-way ladder match; John Cena and Nikki Bella beat The Miz and Maryse; Seth Rollins overturned Triple H in an unsanctioned match; Randy Orton overcame WWE World Champion Bray Wyatt to win the title; Brock Lesnar rolled over WWE Universal Champion Goldberg to win the title; Naomi dropped WWE Smackdown Women’s Champion Alexa Bliss, Becky Lynch, Carmella, Mickie James and Natalya in a six-way to win the title; Roman Reigns overturned The Undertaker in a “No Holds Barred” match.

30th April 2017: “WWE Payback 2017”: San Jose, CA – 12,000 sellout
Pre-show: Enzo Amore and Big Cass beat Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows.
PPV Broadcast: Chris Jericho bested WWE US Champion Kevin Owens to win the title; Austin Aries overturned WWE Cruiserweight Champion Neville by DQ; WWE Raw Tag Champions Matt Hardy and Jeff Hardy defeated Sheamus and Cesaro; Alexa Bliss overturned WWE Raw Women’s Champion Bayley to win the title; Bray Wyatt defeated WWE World Champion Randy Orton in a non-title “House of Horrors” match; Seth Rollins overturned Samoa Joe; Braun Strowman overcame Roman Reigns.
