Shotgun Results 1997

SHOTGUN RESULTS (1997)

Jan 1997

Air Date: 4th January 1997
The Flying Nuns (The Headbangers) overcame The Godwinns; Goldust defeated The Sultan by count-out; Ahmed Johnson downed Crush by DQ; Mascarita Sagrada Jr dropped Mini Vader.

Air Date: 11th January 1997
The Fake Diesel (Glen Jacobs) pinned Marc Mero; Farooq downed Savio Vega; Rocky Maivia dropped Razor Ramon II (Rick Bogner); Doug Furnas and Phil LaFon dropped The Headbangers.

Air Date: 18th January 1997
Rocky Maivia defeated WWF Intercontinental Champion Hunter Hearst Helmsley by count-out; Mini Mankind and Histeria downed Mascarita Sagrada Jr and Venum; Farooq overcame Jesse James; Steve Austin bested Goldust by DQ.

Air Date: 25th January 1997
Bret Hart defeated Mankind by DQ; Savio Vega overcame Rocky Maivia by count-out; Jake Roberts pinned Salvatore Sincere; Crush downed Sid Vicious by DQ.

Feb 1997

Air Date: 1st February 1997
Ahmed Johnson beat Vader by DQ; Mankind downed Davey Boy Smith; Savio Vega pinned Jesse James; The Godwinns battled The Headbangers to a no-contest.

Air Date: 8th February 1997
Faarooq and Crush beat The Godwinns; WWF Intercontinental Champion Hunter Hearst Helmsley downed The Undertaker by DQ; Savio Vega, Farooq and Crush toppled The Godwinns and Aldo Montoya.

Air Date: 15th February 1997
Highlight show. No original matches broadcast

Air Date: 22nd February 1997
Goldust beat TL Hopper; The Godwinns overcame The Headbangers by DQ; The Headbangers downed The New Blackjacks by DQ; Crush overturned Davey Boy Smith.

Mar 1997

Air Date: 1st March 1997
Bart Gunn beat TL Hopper; Bradshaw downed Henry Godwinn; The Headbangers defeated Freddie Joe Floyd and Barry Horowitz; Crush and Savio Vega dropped Goldust and Miguel Perez Jr.

Air Date: 8th March 1997
Salvatore Sincere defeated Alex Porteau; Doug Furnas and Phil LaFon downed Aldo Montoya and Bob Holly; Ahmed Johnson dropped Leif Cassidy.

Air Date: 15th March 1997
Doug Furnas and Phil LaFon battled The Headbangers to a double count-out; Hector Garza pinned Heavy Metal; Hunter Hearst Helmsley dropped Bob Holly; Flash Funk downed Burt Centeno.

Air Date: 22nd March 1997
Goldust pinned Tony DeVito; Bradshaw battled Henry Godwinn to a no-contest.

Air Date: 29th March 1997
Mankind overturned Aldo Montoya; Crush pinned Barry Horowitz; Hysteria beat Venum; Jesse Jammes overcame Sony Rogers; The New Blackjacks defeated RT Williams and Rod Bell.

Apr 1997

Air Date: 5th April 1997
Mankind overturned Bob Holly; Sid Vicious overcame Jerry Fox; Ludxor, Super Nova, Discovery, and Venum dropped Alismo Negro, El Mosco, Maniaco, and Histeria; Doug Furnas and Phil LaFon downed RT Williams and Aldo Montoya.

Air Date: 12th April 1997
WWF World Tag Champions Owen Hart and Davey Boy Smith defeated Barry Horowitz and Bob Holly in a non-title match; Bart Gunn battled Leif Cassidy to a no-contest; Billy Gunn toppled Freddie Joe Floyd.

Air Date: 19th April 1997
The New Blackjacks overcame Doug Furnas and Phil LaFon; Flash Funk defeated Salvatore Sincere.

Air Date: 26th April 1997
Flash Funk defeated Nick Barberry; Crush, Savio Vega, and D-Lo Brown downed Aldo Montoya, Steve Corino, and Freddie Joe Floyd; Goldust beat Mike Bell; The Legion of Doom dropped The Godwinns.

May 1997

Air Date: 3rd May 1997
Ahmed Johnson toppled Mike DeCero; Vader pinned Willie Choke; Savio Vega and Crush defeated Derek Stone and Neil Haley; WWF World Tag Champions Davey Boy Smith and Owen Hart defeated The New Blackjacks by DQ.

Air Date: 10th May 1997
Mankind overturned Hunter Hearst Helmsley by DQ; WWF Intercontinental Champion Owen Hart defeated Sonny Rogers; Jesse Jammes overcame Leif Cassidy; The New Blackjacks overcame Savio Vega and Crush by count-out.

Air Date: 17th May 1997
Jim Neidhart pinned Marty Garner; Leif Cassidy bested Matt Hardy; Rocky Maivia beat Rockabilly by DQ; Faarooq pinned Jason Ahrdnt.

Air Date: 24th May 1997
The Godwinns overcame Doug Furnas and Phil LaFon; Jesse Jammes beat David Haskins; The New Blackjacks overturned Karl Fergie and Reginald Walker; Brian Pillman toppled Tony Williams; Ahmed Johnson overcame D-Lo Brown.

Air Date: 31st May 1997
Phinneas Godwinn battled Blackjack Bradshaw to a double count-out; The Headbangers overcame Flash Funk and Rocky Maivia by DQ; Tony Williams overcame Scott Taylor; Faarooq, Savio Vega, and Crush defeated WWF World Champion The Undertaker, Vader, and 
Mankind.

Jun 1997

Air Date: 7th June 1997
WWF Intercontinental Champion Owen Hart and WWF European Champion Davey Boy Smith defeated Jesse Jammes and Bob Holly; Blackjack Bradshaw pinned Phil LaFon; Mankind and Ahmed Johnson downed Hunter Hearst Helmsley and Jerry Lawler.

Air Date: 14th June 1997
Jim Neidhart pinned Jesse Jammes; Henry Godwinn downed Salvatore Sincere; Scott Taylor rolled over Steve Ramsey; Rob Van Dam and Jerry Lawler battled The Headbangers to a no-contest.

Air Date: 21st June 1997
Mankind overturned Rockabilly; The Godwinns overcame Ed Ramos and Salvatore Sincere; Brian Christopher rolled over Tommy Rogers; Ahmed Johnson, Faarooq, and Kama defeated Mike Bell, Tony DeVito, and Jeff Riebolt.

Air Date: 28th June 1997
Jesse Jammes overcame The Brooklyn Brawler; The Truth Commission downed Al Brown, Terry Richards, and Gene Miller; Crush and Chainz defeated The Headbangers; WWF Intercontinental Champion Owen Hart beat Goldust.

Jul 1997

Air Date: 5th July 1997
The Truth Commission downed Adam O’Brien, Kevin Flynn, and Chris Steele; The New Blackjacks overcame Miguel Perez and Jesus Castillo by DQ; Scott Putski defeated Rod Bell; Savio Vega beat Flash Funk.

Air Date: 12th July 1997
The Legion of Doom defeated Steve Rivers and Ken Johnson; Kama downed Dale Hutchinson; Ken Shamrock dropped Salvatore Sincere; WWF Intercontinental Champion Owen Hart bested Flash Funk.

Air Date: 19th July 1997
Flash Funk dropped Leif Cassidy; Scott Putski downed Nick Golden; The Godwinns overcame Bob Holly and Jesse Jammes.

Air Date: 26th July 1997
Kama beat Flash Funk; Jerry Lawler downed Scott Taylor; The Headbangers defeated Scott D’Amore and Tony Roy; Mankind dropped WWF Intercontinental Champion Owen Hart by DQ.

Aug 1997

Air Date: 2nd August 1997
Brian Pillman defeated Lou Marconi; Brian Christopher downed Ace Darling; Triple H pinned Jesse Jammes; WWF Intercontinental Champion Owen Hart and WWF European Champion Davey Boy Smith overturned The New Blackjacks.

Air Date: 9th August 1997
Recon, Sniper and The Interrogator beat Zolta Bigg, George Fouts and Hammond Hagar; Tiger Ali Singh downed Bob Holly; The Patriot won a battle royal.

Air Date: 16th August 1997
Owen Hart downed Goldust; Brian Christopher dropped Tony Williams; D-Lo Brown and Kama overcame Skull and Eight-Ball by count-out; Vader bested Hunter Hearst Helmsley by DQ.

Air Date: 23rd August 1997
The Headbangers overcame Salvatore Sincere and Rockabilly; The Rock bested Flash Funk; Owen Hart and WWF European Champion Davey Boy Smith toppled Dude Love and Goldust.

Air Date: 30th August 1997
The Godwinns beat Flash Funk and Jesse Jammes; Tiger Ali Singh downed Adam O’Brien.

Sep 1997

Air Date: 6th September 1997
Savio Vega dropped D-Lo Brown; Jerry Lynn pinned Steve Casey.

Air Date: 13th September 1997
Sniper, Recon and The Interrogator beat Burt King, Brian Taylor and Matt Duvall; Brian Christopher downed Super Loco; El Torito dropped Little Lucky; Flash Funk beat The Sultan; The Legion of Doom bested Faarooq and Kama by DQ.

Air Date: 20th September 1997
Vader defeated The Sultan; Owen Hart downed Flash Funk; The Godwinns overcame Skull and Eight-Ball; El Pantera dropped Super Loco; Rockabilly and Jesse Jammes overturned Jesus Castillo and Jose Estrada Jr.

Air Date: 27th September 1997
Savio Vega and Miguel Perez defeated The New Blackjacks; Jesse Jammes downed Flash Funk; Crush and Chainz dropped Recon and Sniper by DQ; Vader and The Patriot bested The Godwinns.

Oct 1997

Air Date: 4th October 1997
The Legion of Doom defeated Bert Senteno and Nick Barberry; Thrasher downed Henry Godwinn; Jerry Lawler and Brian Christopher dropped Flash Funk and Scott Taylor; Marc Mero overcame Salvatore Sincere; The Rock bested Crush; Brian Pillman toppled The Patriot by DQ.

Air Date: 11th October 1997

Air Date: 18th October 1997
Road Dogg and Billy Gunn beat The New Blackjacks; Marc Mero downed Marty Garner; The Headbangers overcame Matt and Jeff Hardy; Dude Love toppled The Sultan.

Air Date: 25th October 1997
Brian Christopher beat Scott Taylor; The Godwinns overcame The New Blackjacks; Savio Vega downed The Sultan; Davey Boy Smith and Jim Neidhart toppled The Patriot and Goldust

Air Date: 8th November 1997

Air Date: 15th November 1997

Air Date: 22nd November 1997
Brian Christopher beat Devon Storm; The Interrogator and Jackyl overcame Dan Dalton and Donny Rude; D-Lo Brown overturned Flash Funk; Crush, Chainz, Skull and Eight-Ball dropped Savio Vega, Miguel Perez, Jesus Castillo and Jose Estrada.

Air Date: 29th November 1997
Flash Funk downed Taka Michinoku; The Interrogator beat Matt and Jeff Hardy in a handicap match; Crush, Skull, Eight-Ball and Chainz overcame Marty Garner, Steve Corino, Mike Hollow and an unnamed jobber; Dude Love overturned The Sultan; Kama and D-Lo Brown bested The Headbangers.

Air Date: 13th December 1997

Air Date: 20th December 1997

Air Date: 27th December 1997
