ROH iPPV Results 2011

ROH iPPV RESULTS 2011

26th February 2011: “ROH 9th Anniversary Show”: Chicago Ridge, IL – 1,400
1,500 iPPV buys
Shown on ippv (GoFightLive.tv)
Davey Richards beat Colt Cabana; Mike Bennett overcame Steve Corino, Grizzly Redwood and Kyle O’Reilly in a Four Way match; El Generico dropped Michael Elgin; ROH Champion Roderick Strong toppled Homicide in a streetfight; Sara Del Ray downed MsChif; ROH Tag Champions Chris Hero and Claudio Castagnoli defeated Rhett Titus and Kenny King; ROH TV Champion Christopher Daniels fought Eddie Edwards to a draw in a best-of-three-falls encounter; Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin rolled over Jay and Mark Briscoe.

1st April 2011: “ROH Honor Takes Center Stage – Chptr 1”: Atlanta, GA – 800 sellout
1,725 iPPV buys
Shown on ippv (GoFightLive.tv)
Michael Elgin downed El Generico; Homicide overcame Caleb Konley, Colt Cabana and Tommaso Ciampa in a Four Way encounter; Hiroyo Matsumoto and Ayumi Kurihara toppled Sara Del Ray and Serena Deeb; Jay and Mark Briscoe overcame Adam Cole and Kyle O’Reilly; Davey Richards toppled Roderick Strong; Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin rolled over ROH Tag Champions Chris Hero and Claudio Castagnoli to win the titles; ROH Champion Eddie Edwards bested ROH TV Champion Christopher Daniels. Only the ROH Title was on the line.

2nd April 2011: “ROH Honor Takes Center Stage – Chptr 2”: Atlanta, GA – 800 sellout
1,725 iPPV buys
Shown on ippv (GoFightLive.tv)
Chris Hero and Claudio Castagnoli beat Adam Cole and Kyle O’Reilly; Colt Cabana downed Dave Taylor; Tommaso Ciampa dropped Homicide; ROH TV Champion Christopher Daniels pinned Michael Elgin; Shimmer Tag Champions Daizee Haze and Tomoaka Nakagawa overcame Hiroyo Matsumoto and Ayumi Kurihara; Jay and Mark Briscoe toppled Rhett Titus and Kenny King; El Generico bested Roderick Strong; ROH Tag Champions Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas rolled over ROH Champion Eddie Edwards and Davey Richards.

26th June 2011: “ROH Best in The World 2011”: New York, NY (Hammerstein Ballroom) – 2,500 sellout
2,100 iPPV buys
Shown on ippv (GoFightLive.tv).
Dark match: Generation Me beat Adam Cole and Kyle O’Reilly by DQ
iPPV Broadcast: Tommaso Ciampa downed Colt Cabana; Jay Lethal defeated Mike Bennett; Homicide dropped Rhino in a streetfight; Michael Elgin overcame Steve Corino; El Generico bested ROH TV Champion Christopher Daniels to win the title; ROH Tag Champions Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas overturned Jay and Mark Briscoe, Chris Hero and Claudio Castagnoli, and Rhett Titus and Kenny King in an elimination match; Davey Richards rolled over ROH Champion Eddie Edwards to win the title.

17th September 2011: “ROH Death Before Dishonor IX”: New York, NY (Manhattan Center) – 1,200 sellout
972 iPPV buys
Shown on ippv (GoFightLive.tv).
Dark match: Andy Ridge beat Grizzly Redwood
iPPV Broadcast: Rhino and Tommaso Ciampa downed ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal and Homicide; Shelton Benjamin dropped Mike Bennett; The Young Bucks defeated The Bravado Brothers and Adam Cole and Kyle O’Reilly in a Three Way elimination match; El Generico battled Jimmy Jacobs to a no-contest; Charlie Haas toppled Michael Elgin; Eddie Edwards bested Roderick Strong in a best-of-three falls (pinfall only, submission only, ironman) encounter (2:1); Kenny King and Rhett Titus rolled over Jay and Mark Briscoe in a Ladder match.

23rd December 2011: “ROH Final Battle 2011”: New York, NY (Hammerstein Ballroom) – 1,500
2,000 iPPV buys
Shown on ippv (GoFightLive.tv).
Michael Elgin beat TJ Perkins; Tommasso Ciampa downed Jimmy Rave; ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal overcame El Generico and Mike Bennett in a Three Way elimination match; Kevin Steen bested Steve Corino in a no-DQ encounter. Jimmy Jacobs was guest referee; The Young Bucks defeated Caprice Coleman and Cedric Alexander, Kenny King and Rhett Titus, Adam Cole and Kyle O’Reilly, and Harlem and Lance Bravado in a gauntlet match; Roderick Strong bested Chris Hero; Jay and Mark Briscoe toppled ROH Tag Champions Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin to win the title; ROH Champion Davey Richards rolled over Eddie Edwards.
