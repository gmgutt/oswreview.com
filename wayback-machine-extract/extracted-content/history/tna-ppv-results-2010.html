TNA PPV Results 2010

TNA PPV RESULTS 2010

17th January 2010: “TNA Genesis 2010”: Orlando, FL – 1,100 (0 paid)
TNA X Division Champion Amazing Red beat Brian Kendrick; Sean Morley dropped Christopher Daniels; Tara overcame TNA Knockouts Champion ODB to win the title; Matt Morgan and Hernandez defeated TNA World Tag Champions Doug Williams and Brutus Magnus to win the titles; Desmond Wolfe toppled The Pope; Beer Money Inc downed Kevin Nash and Sean Waltman; Mr Anderson bested Abyss; TNA World Champion AJ Styles rolled over Kurt Angle.

 

14th February 2010: “TNA Against All Odds 2010”: Orlando, FL – 1,100 (0 paid)
The Pope beat Desmond Wolfe (No.1 Contender Tournament – First Round); Matt Morgan defeated Hernadez (No.1 Contender Tournament – First Round); Mr Anderson bested Kurt Angle (No.1 Contender Tournament – First Round); Abyss overcame Mick Foley in a no-DQ match (No.1 Contender Tournament – First Round); The Nasty Boys downed Team 3D; The Pope bested Matt Morgan (No.1 Contender Tournament – Semi Final); Mr Anderson overturned Abyss (No.1 Contender Tournament – Semi Final); TNA World Champion AJ Styles rolled over Samoa Joe. Eric Bischoff was guest referee; The Pope toppled Mr Anderson(No.1 Contender Tournament – Final).

 

21st March 2010: “TNA Destination X 2010”: Orlando, FL – 1,100 (0 paid)
Kazarian beat Daniels, The Amazing Red and Brian Kendrick in a Four Way Ladder match; TNA Knockouts Champion Tara downed Daffney; TNA Global Champion Rob Terry defeated Brutus Magnus; The Motorcity Machine Guns bested Generation Me in an Ultimate X match; Scott Hall and Sean Waltman toppled Kevin Nash and Eric Young; TNA X-Division Champion Doug Williams dropped Shannon Moore; TNA World Tag Champions Matt Morgan and Hernandez overturned Beer Money Inc; Kurt Angle defeated Mr Anderson; TNA World Champion AJ Styles battled Abyss to a no-contest.

 

18th April 2010: “TNA Lockdown 2010”: St Louis, MO – 3,023 (2,955 paid)
($164,585 live gate)
All matches held inside a cage: Rob Van Dam downed James Storm; Homicide overcame Brian Kendrick, Alex Shelley and Chris Sabin in a Four Way, Ultimate Xscape match; Kevin Nash pinned Eric Young; TNA Knockouts Tag Champions Madison Rayne and Velvet Sky toppled TNA Knockouts Champion Angelina Love and Tara. By virtue of the win, Rayne captured the Knockouts Title; Kazarian overcame Homicide and Shannon Moore in a Three Way to capture the vacant TNA X Division Title; Team 3D bested Kevin Nash and Scott Hall in a streetfight; Kurt Angle toppled Mr Anderson; TNA World Champion AJ Styles dropped The Pope; Jeff Hardy, Jeff Jarrett, Rob Van Dam and Abyss rolled over Sting, Desmond Wolfe and Beer Money Inc in a Lethal Lockdown match.

 

16th May 2010: “TNA Sacrifice 2010”: Orlando, FL – 1,100 (0 paid)
Chris Sabin and Alex Shelley downed Robert Roode and James Storm, and Team 3D in a three-way; TNA Global Champion Rob Terry beat Orlando Jordan; Doug Williams overcame TNA X-Division Champion Kazarian to win the title; TNA Knockout’s Champion Madison Ryan downed Tara; TNA World Tag Champions Kevin Nash and Scott Hall dropped Jesse Neal and Shannon Moore; Abyss toppled Desmond Wolfe in a “Chelsea vs. Ring” encounter; Jeff Hardy bested Mr Anderson; Sting pinned Jeff Jarrett; TNA World Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over AJ Styles.

 

13th June 2010: “TNA Slammiversary VIII”: Orlando, FL – 1,100 (0 paid)
Kurt Angle bested Kazarian; TNA X-Division Champion Doug Williams overturned Brian Kendrick; TNA Knockouts Champion Madison Rayne downed Roxxi; Jesse Neal dropped Brother Ray; Matt Morgan toppled Hernandez by DQ; Abyss overturned Desmond Wolfe; Jay Lethal overcame AJ Styles; Jeff Hardy and Mr Anderson bested Robert Roode and James Storm; TNA World Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Sting. 

 

11th July 2010: “TNA Victory Road 2010”: Orlando, FL – 1,100 (0 paid)
Dark match: Jeff Jarrett toppled Desmond Wolfe.
PPV Broadcast: TNA X-Division Champion Doug Williams beat Brian Kendrick in an “Ultimate X” submission match; Brother Ray downed Devon and Jesse Neal in a three-way; Angelina Love toppled TNA Knockouts Champion Madison Rayne by DQ in a “Title vs. Career” match; AJ Styles and Kazarian dropped TNA Global Champion Rob Terry and Samoa Joe; Hernandez defeated Matt Morgan in a cage match; Jay Lethal overcame Ric Flair; Chris Sabin and Alex Shelley bested Robert Roode and James Storm to win the vacant TNA World Tag Titles; Kurt Angle toppled The Pope; TNA World Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Jeff Hardy, Abyss and Mr Anderson in a four-way.

8th August 2010: “TNA Hardcore Justice 2010”: Orlando, FL – 1,100 (0 paid)
Tracy Smothers, Guido Maritato and Tony Luke defeated Kid Kash, Johnny Swinger and Simon Diamond; Too Cold Scorpio bested CW Anderson; Stevie Richards overcame PJ Polaco; Rhino overturned Brother Runt and Al Snow in a three-way; Team 3D bested Axl Rotten and Kahones in a streetfight; Raven pinned Tommy Dreamer. Mick Foley was guest referee; TNA World Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Sabu.

5th September 2010:“TNA No Surrender 2010”: Orlando, FL – 1,100 (0 paid)
TNA World Tag Champions Alex Shelley and Chris Sabin beat Generation Me; TNA X-Division Champion Doug Williams defeated Sabu; Velvet Sky downed Madison Rayne; Abyss dropped Rhino in a “falls-count-anywhere” match; Jeff Jarrett and Samoa Joe bested Kevin Nash and Sting; TNA TV Champion AJ Styles toppled Tommy Dreamer in a “I Quit” match; Kurt Angle battled Jeff Hardy to a no-contest (TNA World Title Tournament – Semi Final); Mr Anderson rolled over The Pope (TNA World Title Tournament – Semi Final). 

10th October 2010: “TNA Bound For Glory 2010”: Daytona Beach – 3,500 (heavily papered)
TN World Tag Champions Chris Sabin and Alex Shelley downed Generation Me; Tara overcame TNA Knockouts Champion Angelina Love, Velvet Sky and Madison Rayne in a four-way to win the title. Mickie James was guest referee; Shannon Moore and Jesse Neal overcame Orlando Jordan and Eric Young; TNA X-Division Champion Jay Lethal pinned Doug Williams; Rob Van Dam toppled Abyss in a “Monster’s Ball” encounter; Sting, Kevin Nash and The Pope bested Jeff Jarrett and Samoa Joe in a handicap match; Raven, Sabu, Rhino, Tommy Dreamer and Stevie Richards overcame TNA TV Champion AJ Styles, Kazarian, Matt Morgan, James Storm and Robert Roode in a “Lethal Lockdown” encounter; Jeff Hardy rolled over Kurt Angle and Mr Anderson in a three-way to win the vacant TNA World Title.

7th November 2010: “TNA Turning Point 2010: Orlando, FL – 1,100 (0 paid)
Robbie E beat TNA X-Division Champion Jay Lethal to win the title; Tara battled Mickie James to a no-contest; TNA World Tag Champions Chris Sabin and Alex Shelley bested Team 3D; Rob Van Dam toppled Tommy Dreamer; TNA TV Champion AJ Styles, Kazarian, Robert Roode, James Storm and Doug Williams overcame Brian Kendrick, Raven, Sabu, Rhino and Stevie Richards; Abyss toppled The Pope in a lumberjack match; Jeff Jarrett bested Samoa Joe; TNA World Champion Jeff Hardy rolled over Matt Morgan.

5th December 2010: “TNA Final Resolution 2010”: Orlando, FL – 1,100 (0 paid)
Beer Money Inc overturned Jesse Neal and Shannon Moore; Tara downed Mickie James in a falls-count-anywhere encounter; TNA X-Division Champion Robbie E beat Jay Lethal by DQ; Rob Van Dam toppled Rhino in a First Blood match; Doug Williams bested TNA TV Champion AJ Styles to win the title; TNA World Tag Champions The Motorcity Machine Guns downed Generation Me in a Full Metal Mayhem match; Abyss dropped The Pope in a casket match; Jeff Jarrett defeated Samoa Joe in a submission match; TNA World Champion Jeff Hardy rolled over Matt Morgan. Mr Anderson was guest referee.
