Smackdown Results 2013

WWE SMACKDOWN RESULTS (2013)

Jan 2013

Air Date:  4th January 2013
The Miz beat Heath Slater; The Great Khali, Hornswoggle and Natalya defeated Primo, Epico and Rosa Mendes; WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett downed Kofi Kingston; Tamina Snuka dropped Layla; Sheamus and Randy Orton bested World Heavyweight Champion The Big Show and WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro.

Air Date: 11th January 2013
Randy Orton beat WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro by DQ; Dolph Ziggler and AJ overcame The Great Khali and Natalya; Sheamus defeated Heath Slater, Drew McIntyre and Jinder Mahal in a handicap match; WWE World Tag Champions Kane and Daniel Bryan toppled Titus O’Neill and Darren Young; Alberto Del Rio rolled over World Heavyweight Champion The Big Show in a “Last Man Standing” match to win the title.

Air Date: 18th January 2013
WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro defeated Kofi Kingston; Randy Orton and WWE World Tag Champions Daniel Bryan and Kane downed Wade Barrett, Damien Sandow and Cody Rhodes; The Miz dropped Primo; WWE Divas Champion Kaitlyn overcame Aksana; Sheamus and World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio rolled over Dolph Ziggler and The Big Show.

Air Date: 25th January 2013
Sheamus pinned WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett in a non-title match; Natalya overcame Rosa Mendes; Randy Orton bested Drew McIntyre by DQ; The Miz overturned Darren Young; World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio and WWE World Tag Champions Kane and Daniel Bryan rolled over The Big Show, Cody Rhodes and Damien Sandow in an elimination match.

Feb 2013

Air Date: 1st February 2013
Rey Mysterio and Sin Cara bested WWE World Tag Champions Kane and Daniel Bryan in a non-title match; The Great Khali beat Jinder Mahal; Sheamus battled Jinder Mahall to a no-contest; Randy Orton pinned WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett in a non-title match; Jack Swagger overcame Kofi Kingston; World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio rolled over Dolph Ziggler.

Air Date: 8th February 2013
Cody Rhodes defeated Kofi Kingston; The Great Khali beat Titus O’Neil; The Big Show downed Kane; Jack Swagger dropped Justin Gabriel; Tensai overcame Drew McIntyre by DQ; WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro bested Sin Cara; Mark Henry rolled over Randy Orton.

Air Date: 15th February 2013
Mark Henry beat Randy Orton by DQ; Tamina Snuka defeated Layla; Brodus Clay and Tensai downed Jinder Mahal and Heath Slater; The Miz dropped Cody Rhodes; Jack Swagger bested Zack Ryder; The Big Show rolled over Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 22nd February 2013
Sheamus beat Damien Sandow; WWE Divas Champion Kaitlyn and Layla defeated Tamina Snuka and Aksana; Jack Swagger downed Randy Orton; The Miz dropped Cody Rhodes; World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett in a non-title match.

Mar 2013

Air Date: 1st March 2013:
World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio defeated Damien Sandow; WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro downed The Miz in a “best-of-three-falls” match; Titus O’Neil and Darren Young overcame WWE World Tag Champions Kane and Daniel Bryan in a non-title match (Daniel Bryan’s hand tied behind his back/Kane blindfolded); Sin Cara dropped Heath Slater; Randy Orton battled The Big Show to a no-contest.

Air Date: 8th March 2013
WWE World Tag Champions Kane and Daniel Bryan defeated Drew McIntyre and Heath Slater; Ryback downed Damien Sandow; World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio dropped Dolph Ziggler; Mark Henry overcame Yoshi Tatsu; Tamina Snuka overturned WW Divas Champion Kaitlyn in a non-title match; Sheamus battled The Big Show to a no-contest.

Air Date: 15th March 2013
Sheamus and Randy Orton defeated Cody Rhodes and Damien Sandow; Layla and Kaitlyn dropped Tamina Snuka and Aksana; Dolph Ziggler overcame Kane; Ryback battled Mark Henry to a no-contest; Jack Swagger bested Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 22nd March 2013
Mark Henry beat Zack Ryder; Dolph Ziggler defeated Kofi Kingston; Jack Swagger downed Chris Jericho; Cody Rhodes and Damien Sandow battled to a no-contest with Brodus Clay and Tensai; The Miz bested WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro in a non-title match; The Big Show, Sheamus and Randy Orton toppled Heath Slater, Drew McIntyre and Jinder Mahal.

Air Date: 29th March 2013
Chris Jericho beat WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett in a non-title match; Ryback defeated Mark Henry by DQ in a weight lifting contest; Daniel Bryan and WWE Divas Champion Kaitlyn bested Dolph Ziggler and AJ Lee; Jack Swagger battled to a double count-out with The Great Khali; Randy Orton, Sheamus and The Big Show toppled Cody Rhodes, Damien Sandow and WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro.

Apr 2013

Air Date: 5th April 2013
Ryback bested Primo and Epico in a handicap match; Jack Swagger toppled Kofi Kingston.

Air Date: 12th April 2013
WWE World Tag Champions Kane and Daniel Bryan defeated Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett downed Santino Marella; The Bella Twins and Tamina Snuka dropped Kaitlyn, Naomi and Cameron; Sheamus and Randy Orton overcame The Big Show by count-out; Kofi Kingston bested WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro in a non-title match; World Heavyweight Champion Dolph Ziggler toppled Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 19th April 2013
Fandango bested Santino Marella; WWE US Champion Kofi Kingston toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett in a non-title match; Alberto Del Rio overturned Jack Swagger; The Great Khali, Hornswoggle and Natalya dropped Primo, Epico and Rosa Mendes; The Big Show and Mark Henry rolled over Randy Orton and Sheamus.

Air Date: 26th April 2013
Jack Swagger defeated Alberto Del Rio in a no-DQ match; Layla downed Aksana; Fandango dropped Justin Gabriel; The Big Show overturned Sheamus; Wade Barrett bested William Regal; Mark Henry toppled Randy Orton by DQ; The Undertaker rolled over Dean Ambrose.

May 2013

Air Date: 3rd May 2013
Ryback dropped Daniel Bryan; Fandango overcame Zack Ryder; Ricardo Rodriguez battled Zeb Colter to a no-contest; Alberto Del Rio and Ricardo Rodriguez battled Zeb Colter and Jack Swagger to a no-contest; Alberto Del Rio and Ricardo Rodriguez won a three-way over Dolph Ziggler and Big E Langton, and Jack Swagger and Zeb Colter; Randy Orton rolled over Damien Sandow; Dean Ambrose bested Kane.

Air Date: 10th May 2013
WWE US Champion Kofi Kingston defeated Cody Rhodes; Dean Ambrose bested Daniel Bryan by DQ; The Big Show toppled Tensai; Chris Jericho dropped Ryback by DQ.

Air Date: 17th May 2013
Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns overcame Jimmy and Jey Uso; Brodus Clay and Tensai toppled Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; Chris Jericho bested Antonio Cesaro; The Miz overcame Damien Sandow; WWE Divas Champion Kaitlyn defeated Aksana; Randy Orton, Sheamus and Alberto Del Rio rolled over Mark Henry, The Big Show and Jack Swagger.

Air Date: 24th May 2013
The Miz overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett by DQ; Daniel Bryan bested Jack Swagger; Chris Jericho toppled The Big Show by count-out; Curtis Axel bested Sin Cara; Kofi Kingston defeated WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose by DQ; WWE World Tag Champions Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns, and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose rolled over Kofi Kingston, Randy Orton and Sheamus.

Air Date: 31st May 2013
Kane bested Seth Rollins; Roman Reigns toppled Daniel Bryan by DQ; Ryback defeated Kofi Kingston; Big E Langston downed Alberto Del Rio; Curtis Axel dropped Sin Cara; Chris Jericho overcame Cody Rhodes; Randy Orton overturned WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose by DQ.

Jun 2013

Air Date: 7th June 2013
Curtis Axel bested Chris Jericho; Kane toppled Ryback by DQ; Fandango overcame Zack Ryder; Randy Orton and Daniel Bryan rolled over WWE World Tag Champions Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns by DQ.

Air Date: 14th June 2013
Sheamus toppled Antonio Cesaro; Heath Slater overcame The Great Khali; Chris Jericho and Alberto Del Rio overturned Dolph Ziggler and Big E Langston; Curtis Axel beat WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett in a non-title match; Daniel Bryan, Kane and Randy Orton rolled over WWE World Tag Champions Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns, and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose.

Air Date: 21st June 2013
Sheamus toppled Cody Rhodes; WWE Intercontinental Champion Curtis Axel downed Wade Barrett; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee overcame Natalya; World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio overturned Chris Jericho by DQ; Christian defeated Drew McIntyre; Daniel Bryan rolled over Randy Orton by count-out.

Air Date: 28th June 2013
Sheamus beat Damien Sandow in a “Dublin” streetfight; Natalya dropped WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee in a non-title match; Randy Orton pinned Kane; Ryback overcame Justin Gabriel; Christian and Jimmy and Jey Uso rolled over WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose and WWE World Tag Champions Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns.

Jul 2013

Air Date: 5th July 2013
Jimmy and Jey Uso beat Damien Sandow and Cody Rhodes; Alicia Fox defeated Kaitlyn; Randy Orton pinned Christian; Dolph Ziggler bested Drew McIntyre; Fandango toppled Justin Gabriel; World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio battled CM Punk to a double count-out.

Air Date: 12th July 2013
Daniel Bryan rolled over Christian; Seth Rollins pinned Jey Uso; Chris Jericho toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Curtis Axel by count-out; Ryback downed The Miz; Fandango overcame Wade Barrett; Sheamus battled Randy Orton to a no-contest.

Air Date: 19th July 2013
Dolph Ziggler bested Jack Swagger; Daniel Bryan rolled over Wade Barrett; WWE Intercontinental Champion Curtis Axel downed Chris Jericho; Rob Van Dam pinned Darren Young; Randy Orton rolled over Alberto Del Rio.

Air Date: 26th July 2013
Randy Orton rolled over Damien Sandow; Mark Henry, Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso defeated Titus O’Neil, Darren Young and Wade Barrett; World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio overturned Rob Van Dam; Luke Harper and Erick Rowan dropped Brodus Clay and Tensai; Christian defeated Jack Swagger.

Aug 2013

Air Date: 2nd August 2013
Cody Rhodes overturned Jack Swagger; Big E Langston defeated Sin Cara; CM Punk rolled over Fandango; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee overcame Kaitlyn; Christian won a three-way over Randy Orton and Rob Van Dam.

Air Date: 9th August 2013
Randy Orton bested Rob Van Dam; Fandango toppled Kofi Kingston; Daniel Bryan overcame Wade Barrett; Kane beat Heath Slater, Drew McIntyre and Jinder Mahal; Christian rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio in a non-title match.

Air Date: 16th August 2013
Christian defeated Damien Sandow; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee and Layla downed Kaitlyn and Natalya; Rob Van Dam, Mark Henry and Big Show bested WWE World Tag Champions Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns, and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose; The Miz dropped Jack Swagger; WWE Intercontinental Champion Curtis Axel toppled Zack Ryder; Daniel Bryan rolled over Wade Barrett in a no-DQ match.

Air Date: 23rd August 2013
WWE Intercontinental Champion Curtis Axel defeated Cody Rhodes; Dolph Ziggler downed Big E Langston; World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio dropped Christian; The Big Show and Mark Henry beat Drew McIntyre, Jinder Mahal and Heath Slater in a handicap match; Darren Young bested Antonio Cesaro; Daniel Bryan toppled Wade Barrett in a cage match.

Air Date: 30th August 2013
WWE World Champion Randy Orton bested The Miz; Rob Van Dam toppled Damien Sandow; WWE World Tag Champions Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns, and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose overturned Dolph Ziggler in a handicap match; Luke Harper and Erick Rowan defeated Brodus Clay and Tensai; Daniel Bryan rolled over Ryback by DQ.

Sep 2013

Air Date: 6th September 2013
WWE World Champion Randy Orton rolled over Rob Van Dam; Ryback overturned Dolph Ziggler; Kofi Kingston rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Curtis Axel in a non-title match; Naomi battled Brie Bella to a no-contest; Jack Swagger and Antonio Cesaro bested Jimmy and Jey Uso; The Big Show beat Heath Slater, Jinder Mahal and Drew McIntyre in a handicap match; Daniel Bryan rolled over Seth Rollins.

Air Date: 13th September 2013
Brie Bella, Natalya and Naomi dropped Alicia Fox, Aksana and Layla by DQ; Santino Marella defeated Damien Sandow; World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio bested Ricardo Rodriguez; Dolph Ziggler defeated WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose by DQ; WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose and WWE World Tag Champions Roman Reigns and Seth Rollins toppled Jimmy and Jey Uso, and Dolph Ziggler.

Air Date: 20th September 2013
WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee downed Naomi; Ryback overturned Nick; WWE World Tag Champions Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns, and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose downed Darren Young in a handicap match; WWE World Tag Champions Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns, and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose downed Titus O’Neil in a handicap match; WWE World Tag Champions Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns, and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose downed Dolph Ziggler in a handicap match; Seth Rollins and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose battled to a no-contest with Rob Van Dam in a handicap match; Luke Harper and Erick Rowan rolled over Justin Gabriel and Zack Ryder; World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio bested R-Truth; Daniel Bryan, and Jimmy and Jey Uso toppled WWE World Tag Champions Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns, and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose.

Air Date: 27th September 2013
World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio bested R-Truth; Jack Swagger and Antonio Cesaro bested Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; Dolph Ziggler overcame WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose by DQ; WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose and WWE World Tag Champions Roman Reigns and Seth Rollins toppled Dolph Ziggler, Rob Van Dam and Kofi Kingston; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee downed Cameron; Santino Marella defeated Heath Slater; The Miz beat Randy Orton by DQ; Randy Orton rolled over The Miz in a no-DQ match.

Oct 2013

Air Date: 4th October 2013
Rob Van Dam beat Fandango by DQ; Ryback overturned R-Truth; World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio bested Dolph Ziggler; Brie Bella defeated Aksana; Los Matadores bested Heath Slater and Jinder Mahal; Kofi Kingston rolled over Big E Langston; The Big Show beat Randy Orton, WWE World Tag Champions Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns, and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose by DQ in a handicap match.

Air Date: 11th October 2013
WWE Intercontinental Champion Curtis Axel beat R-Truth; Los Matadores toppled Los Locales (Ricardo Rodriguez and Tyson Kidd); Naomi, Cameron and Brie Bella defeated Kaitlyn, Eva Marie and Natalya; World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio bested Damien Sandow; The Great Khali, Titus O’Neil and Darren Young beat Heath Slater, Jinder Mahal and Drew McIntyre; Cody Rhodes and Goldust beat Luke Harper and Erick Rowan.

Air Date: 18th October 2013
Erick Rowan and Luke Harper overcame The Miz and Kofi Kingston; Brie Bella defeated WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee in a non-title match; CM Punk overturned Big E Langston; Jimmy and Jey Uso toppled Jack Swagger and Antonio Cesaro; Daniel Bryan and WWE World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Goldust beat Seth Rollins, Roman Reigns and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose.

Air Date: 25th October 2013
Jimmy and Jey Uso toppled Luke Harper and Erick Rowan; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee downed Nikki Bella; Los Matadores toppled Heath Slater and Drew McIntyre; Ryback and Paul Heyman rolled over CM Skunk in a handicap match; The Great Khali battled to a no-contest with Fandango; Daniel Bryan, Big E Langston and WWE World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Goldust beat Randy Orton, Seth Rollins, Roman Reigns and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose.

Nov 2013

Air Date: 1st November 2013
Jimmy and Jey Uso toppled WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose and Seth Rollins; Erick Rowan and Luke Harper overcame Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; The Bella Twins and Natalya defeated WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee, Tamina Snuka and Alicia Fox; World Heavyweight Champion John Cena and WWE World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Goldust beat Damien Sandow, Jack Swagger and Antonio Cesaro.

Air Date: 8th November 2013
CM Punk overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Curtis Axel in a non-title match; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee and Tamina Snuka defeated Naomi and Cameron; Alberto Del Rio bested The Great Khali; Jimmy and Jey Uso, and R-Truth beat Heath Slater, Drew McIntyre and Jinder Mahal; Daniel Bryan bested Luke Harper by DQ; World Heavyweight Champion John Cena defeated Ryback.

Air Date: 15th November 2013
Luke Harper and Erick Rowan rolled over Jimmy and Jey Uso; Natalya defeated Tamina Snuka; Titus O’Neil, Darren Young and R-Truth beat Heath Slater, Drew McIntyre and Jinder Mahal; World Heavyweight Champion John Cena defeated Alberto Del Rio in an “arm-wrestling” match; Naomi and Cameron rolled over The Bella Twins; The Great Khali dropped Hunico and Camacho in a handicap match; CM Punk and Daniel Bryan battled WWE Intercontinental Champion Curtis Axel and Ryback to a no-contest.

Air Date: 22nd November 2013
WWE World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Goldust beat Jack Swagger and Antonio Cesaro; Ryback overturned The Great Khali; Naomi and Cameron rolled over WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee in a handicap match; Rey Mysterio and Jimmy and Jey Uso toppled Seth Rollins, Roman Reigns and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose; Dolph Ziggler and Big E Langston rolled over Curtis Axel and Damien Sandow; Daniel Bryan rolled over Luke Harper.

Air Date: 29th November 2013
Mark Henry overturned Curtis Axel; Los Matadores and Torito bested Heath Slater, Jinder Mahal and Drew McIntyre; Brodus Clay and Tensai dropped R-Truth and Xavier Woods; Antonio Cesaro bested Titus O’Neil by DQ; WWE World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Goldust beat Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns by DQ; CM Punk and WWE World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Goldust beat Seth Rollins, Roman Reigns and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose by DQ; WWE World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Goldust, CM Punk, Rey Mysterio, and Jimmy and Jey Uso toppled Seth Rollins, Roman Reigns, WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose, Bray Wyatt, Luke Harper and Erick Rowan.

Dec 2013

Air Date: 6th December 2013
WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E Langston rolled over Fandango; Ryback and Curtis Axel beat Goldust and Cody Rhodes; CM Punk overturned WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose in a non-title match; Natalya defeated Tamina Snuka; Rey Mysterio and The Big Show beat Jack Swagger and Antonio Cesaro; WWE World Champion Randy Orton rolled over Daniel Bryan.

Air Date: 13th December 2013
Daniel Bryan rolled over Erick Rowan by DQ; Jack Swagger and Antonio Cesaro bested WWE World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Goldust in a non-title match; Mark Henry overturned Damien Sandow by count-out; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee and Tamina Snuka defeated The Bella Twins; Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns toppled Jimmy and Jey Uso; The Big Show beat Ryback.

Air Date: 20th December 2013
WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E Langston defeated Jack Swagger; Brie Bella downed Tamina Snuka; Sin Cara dropped Drew McIntyre; Tensai bested Brodus Clay; Luke Harper and Erick Rowan overcame WWE World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Goldust in a non-title match; Damien Sandow beat The Miz; Fandango toppled Kofi Kingston; John Cena and CM Punk rolled over Seth Rollins, Roman Reigns and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose by DQ.

Air Date: 27th December 2013
Luke Harper and Erick Rowan beat Jimmy and Jey Uso; Antonio Cesaro defeated Cody Rhodes; Titus O’Neil and Darren Young downed Ryback and Curtis Axel; Daniel Bryan dropped Damien Sandow; WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E Langston bested WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose in a non-title match; Roman Reigns pinned Mark Henry; John Cena overturned Seth Rollins.
