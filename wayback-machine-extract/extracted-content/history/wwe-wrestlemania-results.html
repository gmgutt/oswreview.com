WWF/E WRESTLEMANIA RESULTS

31st March 1985: “WWF Wrestlemania”: New York, NY (MSG) – 22,000 sellout
($502,000 live gate)
Closed Circuit: 398,000 ($3,800,000)
Pro-wrestling’s first ever PPV event. PPV aired only in limited markets, did not air nationally.
Tito Santana defeated The Executioner (Buddy Rose); King Kong Bundy overcame SD Jones; Ricky Steamboat dropped Matt Borne; David Sammartino battled Brutus Beefcake to a double-DQ; The Junkyard Dog downed WWF Intercontinental Champion Greg Valentine by count-out; Nikolai Volkoff and The Iron Sheik overcame WWF World Tag Champions Mike Rotundo and Barry Windham to win the titles; Andre the Giant bested Big John Studd in a “$15,000 bodyslam” match; Wendi Richter pinned WWF Women’s Champion Leilani Kai to win the title; WWF World Champion Hulk Hogan and Mr T rolled over Roddy Piper and Paul Orndorff. Muhammad Ali was the special outside referee.

Los Angeles, CA: (14,500 – $380,000): Ricky Steamboat overcame Hercules; Adrian Adonis downed Uncle Elmer; Terry Funk and Dory Funk Jr overturned The Junkyard Dog and Tito Santana; WWF World Champion Hulk Hogan rolled over King Kong Bundy in a cage match.
