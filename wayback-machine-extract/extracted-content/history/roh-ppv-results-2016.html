ROH PPV Results 2016

ROH PPV RESULTS 2016

 

26th February 2016: “ROH 14th Anniversary Show”: Las Vegas, NV – 800 sellout
Shown on PPV
Dark match: Silas Young rolled over Cheeseburger, Gedo and Will Ferrara in a four-way.
PPV Broadcast: ROH TV Champion Tomohiro Ishii defeated Bobby Fish and Roderick Strong in a three-way; BJ Whitmer overcame Adam Page; Hirooki Goto bested Dalton Castle; Alex Shelley defeated Christopher Daniels; Hiroshi Tanahashi and Michael Elgin toppled Jay Briscoe and Mark Briscoe; IWGP World Champion Kazuchika Okada beat Moose; Never Openweight 6-Man Tag Champions Kenny Omega and The Young Bucks defeated ACH, IWGP Jr Champion Kushida and Matt Sydal; ROH Tag Champions Hanson and Raymond Rowe defeated Kenny King and Rhett Titus in a no-DQ match; ROH Champion Jay Lethal defeated Adam Cole and Kyle O’Reilly in a three-way.

8th May 2016: “NJPW/ROH Global Wars 2016”: Chicago Ridge, IL – 1,500 sellout
Shown live on PPV
Dark matches: Kelly Klein overcame Mary Dobson; Juice Robinson and Kamaitachi dropped Silas Young and Beer City Bruiser, and Rhett Titus and Kenny King in a three-way.
PPV Broadcast: Dalton Castle downed ACH, Hangman Page and Roderick Strong in a four-way; Cheeseburger and Jushin Liger rolled over Christopher Daniels and Kazarian; ROH Tag Champions Raymond Rowe and Hanson defeated Mark Briscoe and Jay Briscoe; IWGP World Champion Tetsuya Naito bested Kyle O’Reilly; Moose and Kazuchika Okada beat Hiroshi Tanahashi and Michael Elgin; Bobby Fish toppled ROH TV Champion Tomohiro Ishii to win the title; Matt Jackson, Nick Jackson, Tama Tonga and Tanga Roa beat Chris Sabin, Alex Shelley, IWGP Jr Champion Kushida and Matt Sydal; Colt Cabana battled ROH Champion Jay Lethal to a no-contest.

24th June 2016: “ROH Best in the World 2016”: Concord, NC – 800
Shown live on PPV
Kyle O’Reilly toppled Kamaitachi; ACH defeated Silas Young; Mark Briscoe downed Roderick Strong; Adam Cole and The Young Bucks overturned Moose, Raymond Rowe and Hanson in a tornado match; ROH Tag Champions Christopher Daniels and Kazarian defeated Alex Shelley and Chris Sabin; BJ Whitmer rolled over Steve Corino in a “Fight Without Honor”; ROH TV Champion Bobby Fish defeated Dalton Castle; ROH Champion Jay Lethal defeated Jay Briscoe.

19th August 2016: “ROH Death Before Dishonor XIV”: Las Vegas, NV – 800 sellout
Shown live on PPV
Dark match: Candice LeRae beat Veda Scott.
PPV Broadcast: Donovan Dijak defeated Jay White, Kamaitachi and Lio Rush in a four-way; Katsuyori Shibata beat Silas Young; Rocky Romero, Baretta and Toru Yano bested Tama Tonga, Tanga Roa and Yujiro Takahashi; Hangman Page downed Jay Briscoe in a no-DQ match; IWGP World Champion Kazuchika Okada beat Dalton Castle; ROH TV Champion Bobby Fish defeated Mark Briscoe; ROH Tag Champions Christopher Daniels and Kazarian defeated Tetsuya Naito and Evil, and Hiroshi Tanahashi and IWGP Intercontinental Champion Michael Elgin in a three-way; Adam Cole downed ROH Champion Jay Lethal to win the title.

30th September 2016: “ROH All Star Extravaganza VIII”: Lowell, MA – 1,200
Shown live on PPV
Dark match: Silas Young defeated Evil and Will Ferrara in a three-way.
PPV Broadcast: ROH TV Champion Bobby Fish defeated Donovan Dijak; Colt Cabana and Dalton Castle downed Hanson and Raymond Rowe, Keith Lee and Shane Taylor, and Kenny King and Rhett Titus in a four-way; Dragon Lee downed Kamaitachi; Kyle O’Reilly toppled Hangman Page; ACH, Jay White and Kushida beat Jay Briscoe, Mark Briscoe and Toru Yano (ROH 6-Man Tag Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); Jay Lethal rolled over Tetsuya Naito; ROH Champion Adam Cole defeated Michael Elgin; The Young Bucks overturned Alex Shelley and Chris Sabin, and ROH Tag Champions Christopher Daniels and Kazarian in a three-way  ladder match to win the titles.

2nd December 2016: “ROH Final Battle 2016”: New York, NY – 1,800 sellout
Shown live on PPV
Dark match: Cheeseburger and Will Ferrara beat Sho Tanaka and Yohei Komatsu.
PPV Broadcast: Rhett Titus, Kenny King and Caprice Coleman overcame Alex Shelley, Chris Sabin and Donovan Dijak; Silas Young defeated Jushin Liger; Dalton Castle downed Colt Cabana; Cody Rhodes overturned Jay Lethal; Vinny Marseglia, TK O’Ryan and Matt Taven overcame Lio Rush, Jay White and Kushida to become the first ROH 6-Man Tag Champions (ROH 6-Man Tag Title Tournament – Final); ROH TV Champion Marty Scurll defeated Dragon Lee and Will Ospreay in a three-way; ROH Tag Champions The Young Bucks defeated Jay Briscoe and Mark Briscoe; Kyle O’Reilly toppled ROH Champion Adam Cole in a no-DQ match to win the title.
