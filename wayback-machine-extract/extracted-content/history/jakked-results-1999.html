Jakked Results 1999

JAKKED RESULTS (1999)

Sep 1999

Air Date: 4th September 1999
Steve Blackman downed Mike Hollow; Kane bested Shawn Stasiak; Stevie Richards and The Blue Meanie defeated Papi Chulo and Sho Funaki; The Dudley Boyz dropped Tim McNeeny and Knuckles Nelson; Val Venis defeated Mideon.

Air Date: 11th September 1999
X-Pac downed Droz; Viscera and Prince Albert battled to a double count-out; The Big Boss Man toppled Knuckles Nelson.

Air Date: 18th September 1999
Edge bested Mideon; Shawn Stasiak downed Prince Albert.

Air Date: 25th September 1999
The Godfather downed Joey Abs; Gangrel defeated The Blue Meanie; Droz and Prince Albert dropped Chris Germany and Kevin Northcutt.

Oct 1999

Air Date: 2nd October 1999
The Godfather defeated Mark Henry; Test downed Gangrel; Shawn Stasiak beat Murray Hopper.

Air Date: 9th October 1999
The Godfather downed The Big Boss Man; The Big Show dropped Chaz; WWF Hardcore Champion Al Snow bested Steve Blackman; Gangrel toppled The Inferno Kid.

Air Date: 16th October 1999
Gangrel downed Shawn Stasiak; Prince Albert dropped Stevie Richards; The Dudley Boys beat Mideon and Viscera; Papi Chulo overcame Taka Michinoku.

Air Date: 23rd October 1999
Stevie Richards toppled Steve Blackman; Prince Albert downed Shawn Stasiak; Luna Vachon beat Jacqueline; WWF European Champion D-Lo Brown overcame Rob Conway.

Air Date: 30th October 1999
The Blue Meanie downed Tom O’Sullivan; The Headbangers beat Knuckles Nelson and Dukes Dalton; Val Venis dropped Shawn Stasiak; Gangrel and Al Snow battled to a double count-out.

Nov 1999

Air Date: 6th November 1999
WWF European Champion Davey Boy Smith downed The Godfather; The Acolytes dropped Mideon and Viscera; D-Lo Brown beat Sho Funaki; Steve Blackman overturned Shawn Stasiak.

Air Date: 13th November 1999
Crash Holly defeated Mark Henry, Prince Albert, and Viscera in a four-way match; Matt Hardy bested D-Von Dudley; D-Lo Brown overcame Steve Blackman; Rikishi pinned Julio Fantastico. 

Air Date: 20th November 1999
D-Lo Brown dropped Mark Henry; Brian Christopher downed Edge; The Headbangers defeated The Dudley Boys, The Hardy Boys and Kaientai in a four-way match; Rikishi pinned Crash Holly.

Air Date: 27th November 1999
D-Lo Brown defeated Shawn Stasiak; Mideon downed Lou Marconi.

Dec 1999

Air Date: 4th December 1999
D-Lo Brown dropped Joey Abs; The Headbangers overcame Kaientai; Mideon downed Shawn Stasiak.

Air Date: 11th December 1999
Davey Boy Smith downed Gangrel; Christian dropped D-Von Dudley; Kaientai defeated Mark Henry in a handicap match; The Headbangers overcame Dukes Dalton and Knuckles Nelson.

Air Date: 18th December 1999
WWF European Champion Val Venis toppled Gangrel by DQ; Kurt Angle downed Mideon; Rikishi Phatu pinned Davey Boy Smith; D-Lo Brown beat Steve Blackman; Crash Holly and Hardcore Holly overturned The Headbangers.

Air Date: 25th December 1999
Prince Albert downed D-Lo Brown by DQ; Gangrel bested Mideon; The Headbangers overcame Kaientai.
