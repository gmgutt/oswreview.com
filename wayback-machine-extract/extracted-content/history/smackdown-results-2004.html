Smackdown Results 2004

WWE SMACKDOWN RESULTS (2004)

Jan 2004

Air Date: 1st January 2004
Rey Mysterio dropped WWE Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri to win the title; Bradshaw pinned Rhyno; John Cena and Chris Benoit beat Nunzio, Chuck Palumbo, and Johnny Stamboli in a handicap match; WWE US Champion The Big Show toppled Hardcore Holly by DQ; Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas downed Eddie and Chavo Guerrero Jr.

Air Date: 8th January 2004
Chris Benoit toppled Johnny Stamboli, Chuck Palumbo, and Nunzio in a “Royal Rumble” match; Rikishi and Scotty 2 Hotty battled WWE Tag Team Champions Doug and Danny Basham to a no-contest; WWE Tag Team Champions Doug and Danny Basham beat Eddie Guerrero and Chavo Guerrero Jr; John Cena bested Albert; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio dropped Akio; Tajiri beat Nidia; Hardcore Holly downed WWE US Champion The Big Show in a non-title streetfight.

Air Date: 15th January 2004
Jamie Noble beat Tajiri; Rikishi pinned Scotty 2 Hotty; Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas downed WWE Tag Team Champions Doug and Danny Basham in a non-title match; Nunzio beat Chuck Palumbo and Johnny Stamboli in a “Royal Rumble” match; WWE US Champion The Big Show toppled Sho Funaki; John Cena bested Rhyno in a “loser gets mouth washed with soap”.

Air Date: 22nd January 2004
WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio dropped Chuck Palumbo; Tajiri beat Billy Kidman; Albert overcame Shannon Moore;  Chris Benoit and John Cena bested WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar, Matt Morgan, and Rhyno in a handicap match; Bradshaw defeated Akio and Sakoda in a three-way; Chavo Guerrero Jr and Chavo Guerrero Sr defeated Eddie Guerrero and Kurt Angle.

Air Date: 29th January 2004
WWE Tag Team Champions Doug and Danny Basham beat Billy Kidman and Paul London; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio dropped Jamie Noble; WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar dropped Orlando Jordan; Eddie Guerrero won a 15-man “Royal Rumble” match by last eliminating Kurt Angle.

Feb 2004

Air Date: 5th February 2004
Rikishi and Scotty 2 Hotty beat WWE Tag Team Champions Doug and Danny Basham to win the titles; WWE US Champion The Big Show toppled Billy Gunn; Billy Kidman bested Jamie Noble; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio dropped Chavo Guerrero Sr; Kurt Angle toppled Hardcore Holly.

Air Date: 12th February 2004
WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio beat Tajiri; Danny Basham downed Scotty 2 Hotty; Hardcore Holly dropped Rhyno by DQ; Bradshaw overcame Shelton Benjamin; Eddie Guerrero and John Cena rolled over WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar and WWE US Champion The Big Show.

Air Date 19th February 2004
Kurt Angle and John Cena bested Doug and Danny Basham; WWE US Champion The Big Show toppled Hardcore Holly; WWE World Champion Eddie Guerrero battled WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr to a no-contest. Kurt Angle was guest referee.

Air Date: 26th February 2004
Rey Mysterio, Nunzio, Billy Kidman, and Jamie Noble battled to a no-contest in a four-way; The Acolytes overturned WWE Tag Team Champions Rikishi and Scotty 2 Hotty in a non-title match; Brock Lesnar dropped Billy Gunn; John Cena and Rey Mysterio rolled over WWE US Champion The Big Show and WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr.

Mar 2004

Air Date: 4th March 2004
Brock Lesnar dropped Hardcore Holly; Danny Basham dropped Scotty 2 Hotty; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr beat Sho Funaki; Rey Mysterio, Ultimo Dragon, and Billy Kidman bested Tajiri, Akio, and Sakoda; John Cena bested Albert; WWE World Champion Eddie Guerrero battled Paul Heyman to a no-contest.

Air Date: 11th March 2004
John Cena bested Rhyno; Rey Mysterio battled Jamie Noble to a no-contest; Rey Mysterio, Ultimo Dragon, Billy Kidman, and Sho Funaki overturned Jamie Noble, Tajiri, Akio, and Sakoda; WWE World Champion Eddie Guerrero overcame Shelton Benjamin; WWE US Champion The Big Show toppled John Walters and Arch Kincaid in a handicap match; WWE Tag Team Champions Rikishi and Scotty 2 Hotty beat Doug and Danny Basham in a non-title match.

Air Date: 18th March 2004
WWE US Champion John Cena bested Rhyno; WWE Tag Team Champions Rikishi and Scotty 2 Hotty beat The Acolytes; Rey Mysterio won a gauntlet match over The Big Show, Shelton Benjamin, Billy Gunn, and Charlie Haas; WWE World Champion Eddie Guerrero overcame Rey Mysterio.

Air Date: 25th March 2004
Rob Van Dam rolled over Charlie Haas; Rene Dupree toppled Billy Kidman; The Dudley Boys overturned Doug and Danny Basham; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr  beat Spike Dudley; WWE World Champion Eddie Guerrero overcame Booker T by DQ.

Apr 2004

Air Date: 1st April 2004
The Big Show toppled Rikishi; Rey Mysterio and Spike Dudley beat WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr and Chavo Guerrero Sr; Charlie Haas battled Rob Van Dam to a double count-out; Booker T overturned Hardcore Holly; The Dudley Boys bested Akio and Sakoda; WWE US Champion John Cena rolled over Nunzio and Johnny Stamboli in a non-title handicap match.

Air Date: 8th April 2004
The Dudley Boys overturned WWE Tag Team Champions Rikishi and Scotty 2 Hotty in a non-title match; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr beat Jamie Noble; WWE US Champion John Cena bested Danny Basham; The Big Show and Charlie Haas downed Booker T and Rob Van Dam; Rene Dupree overcame Orlando Jordan; Spike Dudley beat Johnny Stamboli.

Air Date: 15th April 2004
WWE US Champion John Cena bested WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr in a non-title match; Rico beat Charlie Haas by count-out; WWE World Champion Eddie Guerrero overcame The Big Show; Mark Jindrak toppled Spike Dudley; Rob Van Dam rolled over Booker T by DQ.

Air Date: 22nd April 2004
Booker T overturned Rob Van Dam; Charile Haas and Rico beat WWE Tag Team Champions Rikishi and Scotty 2 Hotty to win the titles; John Bradshaw Layfield rolled over El Gran Luchadore (a masked Paul London); WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr and Chavo Guerrero Sr. downed WWE US Champion John Cena in a handicap match; D-Von Dudley overturned WWE World Champion Eddie Guerrero in a non-title match.

Air Date: 29th April 2004
Torrie Wilson battled Rene Dupree to a no-contest; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr defeated Nunzio; Booker T overturned Billy Gunn; WWE Tag Team Champions Charlie Haas and Rico downed Doug and Danny Basham; WWE World Champion Eddie Guerrero overcame Bubba Ray Dudley.

May 2004

Air Date: 6th May 2004
WWE US Champion John Cena bested Doug Basham; Jacquelyn toppled WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr to win the title; The Undertaker beat Nunzio and Johnny Stamboli in a handicap match; Torrie Wilson battled Dawn Marie to a no-contest; Rey Mysterio and Rob Van Dam rolled over The Dudley Boys.

Air Date: 13th May 2004
D-Von Dudley dropped Rob Van Dam; Chavo Guerrero Sr overturned WWE Cruiserweight Champion Jacquelyn in a non-title match; Booker T overturned Nunzio and Johnny Stamboli in a handicap match; Hardcore Holly beat Rico; WWE US Champion John Cena bested Sakoda; John Bradshaw Layfield rolled over Rey Mysterio.

Air Date: 20th May 2004
WWE Tag Team Champions Charlie Haas and Rico overcame Nunzio and Johnny Stamboli; Akio battled Billy Kidman to a no-contest; Rene Dupree downed WWE US Champion John Cena by count-out; Chavo Guerrero Sr won a three-way over WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Jr and Spike Dudley to win the title; Booker T overturned Sho Funaki; John Bradshaw Layfield and The Dudley Boys overturned WWE World Champion Eddie Guerrero, Rob Van Dam, and Rey Mysterio.

Air Date: 27th May 2004
The Dudley Boys overturned Rey Mysterio and Rob Van Dam; Booker T overturned Scotty 2 Hotty; WWE World Champion Eddie Guerrero battled Danny Basham to a no-contest; Torrie Wilson and Spike Dudley beat Dawn Marie and Jamie Noble; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Sr beat Akio; WWE US Champion John Cena bested Rene Dupree in a lumberjack match.

Jun 2004

Air Date: 3rd June 2004
WWE World Champion Eddie Guerrero overcame Danny Basham; Jackie Gayda defeated Sable, Torrie Wilson, and Dawn Marie in a bikini contest; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Sr pinned Sho Funaki; Rey Mysterio dropped Rene Dupree; The Undertaker battled Booker T to a no-contest.

Air Date: 10th June 2004
Kenzo Suzuki pinned Scotty 2 Hotty; Rey Mysterio dropped Chavo Guerrero Jr; Gauntlet match: WWE US Champion John Cena battled Booker T to a draw; WWE US Champion John Cena battled Rob Van Dam to a draw; WWE US Champion John Cena battled Rene Dupree to a draw; WWE US Champion John Cena bested Rene Dupree.

Air Date: 17th June 2004
The Dudley Boys overturned WWE Tag Team Champions Charlie Haas and Rico to win the titles; WWE World Champion Eddie Guerrero overcame Doug Basham; Kenzo Suzuki pinned Spike Dudley; Rey Mysterio dropped WWE Cruiserweight Champion Chavo Guerrero Sr to win the title; Luther Reigns overturned Sho Funaki; WWE US Champion John Cena and Rob Van Dam battled Booker T and Rene Dupree to a no-contest.

Air Date: 24th June 2004
Chavo Guerrero Jr won a battle royal; Billy Gunn bested Kenzo Suzuki by DQ; WWE World Champion Eddie Guerrero overcame Luther Reigns by DQ; Rene Dupree toppled Rob Van Dam; The Undertaker bested WWE US Champion John Cena in a non-title match.

Jul 2004

Air Date: 1st July 2004
Booker T overturned Rob Van Dam and Rene Dupree in a three-way; Rey Mysterio dropped Mordecai; Torrie Wilson bested Sable; WWE World Champion John Bradshaw Layfield rolled over Spike Dudley.

Air Date: 8th July 2004
Rob Van Dam rolled over Mark Jindrak; Billy Kidman and Paul London overcame WWE Tag Team Champions The Dudley Boys to win the titles; El Gran Luchadore (a masked Shannon Moore) toppled WWE World Champion John Bradshaw Layfield by count-out; Chavo Guerrero Jr, Jamie Noble, and Akio defeated WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio, Spike Dudley, and Scotty 2 Hotty; WWE US Champion John Cena bested Booker T by DQ.

Air Date: 15th July 2004
WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio and Spike Dudley beat Chavo Guerrero Jr and Jamie Noble; Booker T, Rene Dupree, and Kenzo Suzuki downed John Cena in a handicap elimination match; Paul London beat D-Von Dudley; WWE World Champion John Bradshaw Layfield rolled over Eddie Guerrero in a cage match.

Air Date: 22nd July 2004
Spike Dudley beat Jamie Noble and Chavo Guerrero Jr in a three-way; Bubba Ray Dudley dropped Billy Kidman; WWE World Champion John Bradshaw Layfield rolled over Mike Kruel; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio dropped D-Von Dudley; John Cena bested Luther Reigns.

Air Date: 29th July 2004
Spike Dudley beat WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio to win the title; WWE Tag Team Champions Paul London and Billy Kidman bested The Dudley Boys; Booker T won an eight-way elimination match to win the vacant WWE US Title over Rob Van Dam, John Cena, Luther Reigns, Billy Gunn, Kenzo Suzuki, Rene Dupree, and Charlie Haas.

Aug 2004

Air Date: 5th August 2004
John Cena bested Rob Van Dam; Orlando Jordan bested Rene Dupree; Kurt Angle toppled Charlie Haas; WWE Tag Team Champions Billy Kidman and Paul London overcame Doug and Danny Basham; The Dudley Boys overturned Rey Mysterio and WWE Cruiserweight Champion Spike Dudley.

Air Date: 12th August 2004
WWE Cruiserweight Champion Spike Dudley beat Paul London; Nunzio beat Chavo Guerrero Jr; The Undertaker bested Orlando Jordan by DQ; John Cena, Rob Van Dam, and Charlie Haas rolled over WWE US Champion Booker T, Luther Reigns, and Rene Dupree in a “Summer Games Relay” match.

Air Date: 19th August 2004
WWE Cruiserweight Champion Spike Dudley beat Scotty 2 Hotty; Chavo Guerrero Jr and Jamie Noble toppled WWE Tag Team Champions Paul London and Billy Kidman; John Cena bested D-Von Dudley; Rob Van Dam rolled over Rene Dupree; Eddie Guerrero and Rey Mysterio dropped Kurt Angle and Luther Reigns.

Air Date: 26th August 2004
Rob Van Dam rolled over Kenzo Suzuki; Billy Kidman bested Chavo Guerrero Jr; WWE US Champion Booker T overturned John Cena (Best of Five Series – match #2); Kurt Angle toppled Rey Mysterio; The Undertaker bested Orlando Jordan by DQ.

Sep 2004

Air Date: 2nd September 2004
John Cena, Rob Van Dam, and Rey Mysterio dropped WWE US Champion Booker T, Kenzo Suzuki, and Rene Dupree; Nunzio and Johnny Stamboli defeated WWE Tag Team Champions Billy Kidman and Paul London in a non-title match; Orlando Jordan bested Charlie Haas; The Dudley Boys overturned Billy Gunn and Hardcore Holly; Kurt Angle toppled Eddie Guerrero in a “best-of-three-falls” encounter.

Air Date: 9th September 2004
WWE World Champion John Bradshaw Layfield rolled over Charlie Haas; Rene Dupree and Kenzo Suzuki downed WWE Tag Team Champions Paul London and Billy Kidman to win the titles; Rey Mysterio, Rob Van Dam, and Hardcore Holly downed The Dudley Boys and WWE Cruiserweight Champion Spike Dudley; Eddie Guerrero battled Kurt Angle to a no-contest in a lumberjack match.

Air Date: 16th September 2004
John Cena bested WWE US Champion Booker T (Best of 5 series); Rob Van Dam and Rey Mysterio dropped The Dudley Boys; WWE Tag Team Champions Rene Dupree and Kenzo Suzuki downed Nunzio and Johnny Stamboli; Charlie Haas downed WWE Cruiserweight Champion Spike Dudley by DQ.

Air Date: 23rd September 2004
WWE US Champion Booker T overturned Paul London; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Spike Dudley beat Rey Mysterio; The Undertaker bested Gangrel and Viscera in a handicap match; The Fabulous Moolah and Mae Young defeated Torrie Wilson and Dawn Marie in a “school girl” match; Eddie Guerrero and The Big Show battled Kurt Angle and Luther Reigns to a no-contest.

Air Date: 30th September 2004
The Dudley Boys overturned Rico and Charlie Haas; WWE US Champion Booker T overturned Paul London; Hardcore Holly downed WWE World Champion John Bradshaw Layfield by DQ; Nunzio beat WWE Cruiserweight Champion Spike Dudley in a non-title match; Luther Reigns and Mark Jindrak defeated Eddie Guerrero and Rob Van Dam.

Oct 2004

Air Date: 7th October 2004
Rey Mysterio dropped Kenzo Suzuki; Hardcore Holly downed WWE World Champion John Bradshaw Layfield by count-out; The Big Show toppled Mark Jindrak; Billy Kidman battled Charlie Haas to a no contest; Carlito rolled over WWE US Champion John Cena to win the title.

Air Date: 14th October 2004
Rob Van Dam rolled over Rene Dupree; Billy Kidman pinned Charlie Haas; Rey Mysterio won a battle royal; WWE World Champion John Bradshaw Layfield rolled over Hardcore Holly in a hardcore match; WWE US Champion Carlito bested Rey Mysterio; Eddie Guerrero overcame Luther Reigns by DQ.

Air Date: 21st October 2004
WWE US Champion Carlito beat Rico; John Heidenreich downed Shannon Moore; Mark Jindrak toppled Eddie Guerrero; Booker T, Rob Van Dam, and Rey Mysterio dropped WWE World Champion John Bradshaw Layfield and WWE Tag Team Champions Kenzo Suzuki and Rene Dupree.

Air Date: 28th October 2004
Chavo Guerrero Jr pinned Nunzio; Rey Mysterio dropped Kurt Angle; Booker T overturned Orlando Jordan.

Nov 2004

Air Date: 4th November 2004
Kurt Angle toppled Chris Nawrocki; Kurt Angle toppled Daniel Puder; Booker T overturned Orlando Jordan by DQ; Rob Van Dam and Rey Mysterio dropped Mark Jindrak and Luther Reigns; Booker T and Josh Matthews bested WWE World Champion John Bradshaw Layfield and Orlando Jordan.

Air Date: 11th November 2004
Chavo Guerrero Jr and Rey Mysterio dropped WWE Cruiserweight Champion Spike Dudley and Billy Kidman; Josh Matthews overturned Orlando Jordan; Eddie Guerrero and Rob Van Dam rolled over Mark Jindrak and Luther Reigns; The Big Show toppled Kurt Angle by DQ.

Air Date: 18th November 2004
Charlie Haas downed John Heidenreich by count-out; Rob Van Dam and Rey Mysterio dropped WWE Tag Team Champions Rene Dupree and Kenzo Suzuki in a non-title match; Kurt Angle toppled Dean Visk; John Cena bested WWE US Champion Carlito to win the title.

Air Date: 25th November 2004
Rey Mysterio dropped Rene Dupree; Rob Van Dam rolled over Kenzo Suzuki by DQ; Kurt Angle toppled Cody Steele; Dawn Marie battled Jackie Gayda to a no-contest; WWE World Champion John Bradshaw Layfield and Orlando Jordan dropped Eddie Guerrero and Booker T.

Dec 2004

Air Date: 2nd December 2004
Rey Mysterio, Rob Van Dam, and Torrie Wilson defeated Hiroku, and WWE Tag Team Champions Kenzo Suzuki and Rene Dupree; Doug and Danny Basham beat Eddie Guerrero and Booker T; Kurt Angle toppled Sebastian McCauley; The Undertaker bested WWE World Champion John Bradshaw Layfield and Orlando Jordan in a handicap match by DQ.

Air Date: 9th December 2004
Sho Funaki won a battle royal; Rob Van Dam and Rey Mysterio dropped WWE Tag Team Champions Rene Dupree and Kenzo Suzuki to win the titles; The Big Show toppled Mark Jindrak by DQ; Eddie Guerrero, Booker T, and The Undertaker bested WWE World Champion John Bradshaw Layfield, Orlando Jordan, Doug Basham and Danny Basham in a handicap match.

Air Date: 16th December 2004
WWE Tag Team Champions Rob Van Dam and Rey Mysterio dropped Billy Kidman and Akio; Eddie Guerrero and Booker T overturned Rene Dupree and Kenzo Suzuki; Kurt Angle toppled WWE World Champion John Bradshaw Layfield by DQ.

Air Date: 23rd December 2004
Booker T overturned Rene Dupree; The Undertaker bested John Heidenreich by count-out; Hardcore Holly downed Kenzo Suzuki; Eddie Guerrero and Rey Mysterio dropped Kurt Angle and Luther Reigns.

Air Date: 30th December 2004
WWE US Champion John Cena bested Rene Dupree; Kurt Angle toppled Matt Stryker in a “Kurt Angle Invitational”; WWE World Champion John Bradshaw Layfield rolled over Shannon Moore; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Sho Funaki overturned Spike Dudley; WWE Tag Team Champions Rob Van Dam and Rey Mysterio dropped Eddie Guerrero and Booker T.
