WWE No Mercy Results

WWF/E NO MERCY RESULTS

17th October 1999: “WWF No Mercy 1999”: Cleveland, OH – 18,742 sellout (17,430 paid)
($478,156 live gate)
($130,303 merchandise sales)
The Godfather beat Mideon; The Fabulous Moolah upset WWF Women’s Champion Ivory to win the title; Hardcore and Crash Holly downed Billy Gunn and Road Dogg by DQ; Chyna pinned WWF Intercontinental Champion Jeff Jarrett to win the title in a “Good Housekeeping” match; The Rock bested Davey Boy Smith; Matt and Jeff Hardy overturned Edge and Christian in a ladder match; Val Venis toppled Mankind; X-Pac defeated Kane, Farooq and Bradshaw in a Fatal Four Way elimination match; WWF World Champion Triple H bested Steve Austin in a “no-holds-barred” match.

22nd October 2000: “WWF No Mercy 2000”: Albany, NY – 14,342 sellout (13,872 paid)
($693,225 live gate)
($98,671 merchandise sales)
PPV Broadcast: The Dudley Boys overturned Bull Buchanon and The Goodfather, Too Cool, D-Lo Brown and Chaz, and Raven and Tazz in a “tag team table invitational” gauntlet match; The Acolytes and WWF Women’s Champion Lita battled Test, Albert, and Trish Stratus to a no-contest; Chris Jericho dropped X-Pac in a cage match; Val Venis and Stevie Richards overturned Billy Gunn and Chyna; Steve Austin battled Rikishi to a no-contest in a “No Holds Barred” match; WWF European Champion William Regal bested Mideon; Los Conquistadors (Edge and Christian under masks) defeated WWF World Tag Champions The Hardy Boys to win the titles; Triple H pinned Chris Benoit; Kurt Angle toppled WWF World Champion The Rock in a no-DQ match to win the title.

21st October 2001: “WWF No Mercy 2001”: St Louis, MO – 15,647 (14,486 paid)
($762,255 live gate)
($123,486 merchandise sales)
Heat: (10/21): The Acolytes overturned Hugh Morrus and Kanyon; WCW Cruiserweight Champion Billy Kidman bested Scotty 2 Hotty.
PPV Broadcast: WCW World Tag Champions The Hardy Boys beat WWF European Champion The Hurricane and Lance Storm; Test overcame Kane; Torrie Wilson bested Stacy Keibler; Edge dropped WWF Intercontinental Champion Christian in a ladder match to win the title; WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys overturned The Big Show and Tajiri; The Undertaker bested Booker T; Chris Jericho bested WCW World Champion The Rock to win the title; WWF World Champion Steve Austin bested Kurt Angle and WWF Hardcore Champion Rob Van Dam in a no-DQ, three-way match.

20th October 2002: “WWE No Mercy 2002”: Little Rock, AR – 9,074
($333,050 live gate)
Heat: (10/20): The Hurricane dropped Steven Richards.
PPV Broadcast: WWE World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and Christian downed Booker T and Goldust; Torrie Wilson bested Dawn Marie; Rob Van Dam rolled over Ric Flair; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Jamie Noble beat Tajiri; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Kane to unify the titles; Kurt Angle and Chris Benoit toppled Edge and Rey Mysterio (WWE Tag Title Tournament – Final); WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus overcame Victoria; WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar rolled over The Undertaker in a “Hell in a Cell” match.

19th October 2003: “WWE No Mercy 2003”: Baltimore, MD – 8,500
($500,000 live gate)
Heat: (10/19): Billy Kidman bested Shannon Moore.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Cruiserweight Champion Tajiri beat Rey Mysterio; Chris Benoit toppled Albert; Zach Gowen bested Matt Hardy; Doug and Danny Basham beat The Acolytes; Vince McMahon dropped Stephanie McMahon in an “I Quit” match; Kurt Angle toppled John Cena; The Big Show toppled WWE US Champion Eddie Guerrero to win the title; WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar dropped The Undertaker in a “Biker Chain” match.

3rd October 2004: “WWE No Mercy 2004”: East Rutherford, NJ – 10,000
($700,000 live gate)
Heat: (10/03): Mark Jindrak beat Scotty 2 Hotty.
PPV Broadcast: Eddie Guerrero overcame Luther Reigns; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Spike Dudley beat Nunzio; Billy Kidman toppled Paul London; WWE Tag Team Champions Kenzo Suzuki and Rene Dupree downed Rey Mysterio and Rob Van Dam; The Big Show toppled Kurt Angle; WWE US Championship Best of 5 Series: John Cena bested WWE US Champion Booker T to win the title (Best of 5 series); Charlie Haas, Rico, and Jackie Gayda defeated The Dudley Boys and Dawn Marie; WWE World Champion John Bradshaw Layfield rolled over The Undertaker in a “Last Ride” match.

9th October 2005: “WWE No Mercy 2005”: Houston, TX – 7,000 (5,000 paid)
($440,000 live gate)
Heat: (10/09): William Regal and Paul Burchill overcame Paul London and Brian Kendrick.
PPV Broadcast:  Christy Hemme and WWE Tag Champions Road Warrior Animal and John Heidenreich overturned Johnny Nitro, Joey Mercury, and Melina; Bobby Lashley pinned Simon Dean; WWE US Champion Chris Benoit won a four-way over Booker T, Christian, and Orlando Jordan; Mr Kennedy toppled Hardcore Holly; John Bradshaw Layfield beat Rey Mysterio; Randy and Bob Orton Jr bested The Undertaker in a handicap casket match; Juventud Guerrera beat WWE Cruiserweight Champion Nunzio to win the title; World Heavyweight Champion Batista downed Eddie Guerrero.

8th October 2006: “WWE No Mercy 2006”: Raleigh, NC – 9,000
($400,000 live gate)
Dark match: Jimmy Wang Yang beat Sylvan.
PPV Broadcast: Matt Hardy downed WWE Cruiserwight Champion Gregory Helms in a non-title match; WWE Tag Champions Paul London and Brian Kendrick defeated KC James and Idol Stevens; MVP pinned Marty Garner; WWE US Champion Mr Kennedy downed The Undertaker by DQ; Rey Mysterio bested Chavo Guerrero Jr in a “falls count anywhere” match; Chris Benoit toppled William Regal; World Heavyweight Champion King Booker rolled over Batista, Bobby Lashley, and Finlay in a four-way.

7th October 2007: “WWE No Mercy 2007”: Chicago, IL – 12,500 sellout
($700,000 live gate)
Dark match: Hardcore Holly dropped Cody Rhodes.
PPV Broadcast: Triple H pinned WWE World Champion Randy Orton to win the title; Mr Kennedy, and WWE World Tag Champions Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy, Paul London and Brian Kendrick; ECW Champion CM Punk overturned Big Daddy V by DQ; WWE World Champion Triple H bested Umaga; Rey Mysterio battled Finlay to a no-contest; Beth Phoenix pinned WWE Women’s Champion Candice Michelle to win the title; World Heavyweight Champion Batista rolled over The Great Khali in a “Punjabi Prison” match; Randy Orton rolled over WWE World Champion Triple H in a “last man standing” match to win the title.

5th October 2008: “WWE No Mercy 2008”: Portland, OR – 9,527 (73% full)
($464,725 live gate)
Dark match: WWE Tag Champions Carlito and Primo beat The Miz and John Morrison.
PPV Broadcast: ECW Champion Matt Hardy downed Mark Henry; WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix dropped Candice Michelle; Rey Mysterio overturned Kane by DQ; Batista pinned JBL; The Big Show toppled The Undertaker by stoppage; WWE World Champion Triple H bested Jeff Hardy; World Heavyweight Champion Chris Jericho rolled over Shawn Michaels in a ladder match.

9th October 2016: “WWE No Mercy 2016”: Sacramento, CA – 12,000 sellout
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Chad Gable, Jason Jordan, Mojo Rawley and Zack Ryder rolled over Simon Gotch, Aiden English, Konnor and Viktor.
PPV Broadcast: WWE World Champion AJ Styles defeated Dean Ambrose and John Cena in a three-way; Nikki Bella beat Carmella; WWE Smackdown Tag Champions Heath Slater and Rhyno defeated Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso; Baron Corbin overcame Jack Swagger; Dolph Ziggler rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz to win the title; Naomi dropped Alexa Bliss; Bray Wyatt defeated Randy Orton.
