WWE Payback Results

WWE PAYBACK RESULTS

16th June 2013: “WWE Payback 2013”: Chicago, IL – 12,500 sellout  (11,500 paid)
Pre-show: Sheamus beat Damien Sandow.
PPV Broadcast: Curtis Axel overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett and The Miz in a three-way to win the title; AJ Lee defeated WWE Divas Champion Kaitlyn to win the title; WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose downed Kane by count-out; Alberto Del Rio dropped World Heavyweight Champion Dolph Ziggler to win the title; CM Punk bested Chris Jericho; WWE World Tag Champions Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns toppled Daniel Bryan and Randy Orton; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Ryback in a “three stages of hell” match.

1st June 2014: “WWE Payback 2014”: Chicago, IL – 11,000 sellout
Shown on PPV / WWE Network
Pre-show: Torito beat Hornswoggle in a “mask vs hair” match.
PPV Broadcast: WWE US Champion Sheamus defeated Cesaro;  Ryback and Curtis Axel downed Goldust and Cody Rhodes; Rusev dropped Big E; Bo Dallas battled to a no-contest with Kofi Kingston; WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett overcame Rob Van Dam; John Cena bested Bray Wyatt in a “last man standing” match; WWE Divas Champion Paige overturned Alicia Fox; Roman Reigns, Dean Ambrose and Seth Rollins rolled over Triple H, Randy Orton and Batista in a “No Holds Barred” elimination match.


17th May 2015: “WWE Payback 2015”: Baltimore, MD – 10,000 sellout
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Kick-off show: R-Truth beat Stardust; The Ascension overcame Curtis Axel & Damien Sandow.
PPV Broadcast: Sheamus pinned Dolph Ziggler; WWE World Tag Champions Big E & Kofi Kingston defeated Tyson Kidd & Cesaro in a “best-of-three-falls” match; Bray Wyatt downed Ryback; WWE US Champion John Cena dropped Rusev in a “I Quit” match; Naomi & Tamina Snuka bested The Bella Twins; Neville toppled King Barrett by count-out; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins won a four-way over Dean Ambrose, Roman Reigns & Randy Orton.

1st May 2016: “WWE Payback 2016”: Chicago, IL – 12,540 sellout
($1,081,970 live gate)
Shown on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Dolph Ziggler rolled over Baron Corbin; WWE US Champion Kalisto defeated Ryback.
PPV Broadcast: Aiden English and Simon Gotch battled Enzo Amore and Colin Cassady to a no-contest (WWE Tag Title #1 Contender Tournament – Final); Kevin Owens overturned Sami Zayn; WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz defeated Cesaro; Dean Ambrose downed Chris Jericho; WWE Women’s Champion Charlotte defeated Natalya; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Roman Reigns defeated AJ Styles.

30th April 2017: “WWE Payback 2017”: San Jose, CA – 12,000 sellout
Pre-show: Enzo Amore and Big Cass beat Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows.
PPV Broadcast: Chris Jericho bested WWE US Champion Kevin Owens to win the title; Austin Aries overturned WWE Cruiserweight Champion Neville by DQ; WWE Raw Tag Champions Matt Hardy and Jeff Hardy defeated Sheamus and Cesaro; Alexa Bliss overturned WWE Raw Women’s Champion Bayley to win the title; Bray Wyatt defeated WWE World Champion Randy Orton in a non-title “House of Horrors” match; Seth Rollins overturned Samoa Joe; Braun Strowman overcame Roman Reigns.
