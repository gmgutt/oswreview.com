Smackdown Results 2010

WWE SMACKDOWN RESULTS (2010)

Jan 2010

Air Date: 1st January 2010
CM Punk dropped Matt Hardy (Beat The Clock); Kane battled Dolph Ziggler to a draw (Beat The Clock); The Great Khali overturned The Carolina Panther; WWE Intercontinental Champion Drew McIntyre overcame John Morrison by DQ; Mickie James toppled Beth Phoenix; Rey Mysterio bested Chris Jericho (Beat The Clock); Batista battled R-Truth to a draw (Beat The Clock).

Air Date: 8th January 2010
Dolph Ziggler downed Kane by count-out; Chris Jericho, DH Smith and Tyson Kidd overturned R-Truth and Cryme Tyme; Luke Gallows pinned Matt Hardy; Beth Phoenix dropped Layla; Rey Mysterio battled Batista to a no-contest.

Air Date: 15th January 2010
Chris Jericho toppled John Morrison; CM Punk and Luke Gallows overcame Cryme Tyme, Matt Hardy and The Great Khali, and DH Smith and Tyson Kidd in a Fatal Four Way; Mickie James downed Beth Phoenix by DQ; Kane pinned Dolph Ziggler; Rey Mysterio rolled over Batista in a cage match via cage escape.

Air Date: 22nd January 2010
Finlay dropped Batista by DQ; John Morrison and R-Truth overturned Chris Jericho and WWE Intercontinental Champion Drew McIntyre; Cryme Tyme battled Mike Knox and Charlie Haas to a no-contest; The Great Khali and Matt Hardy bested DH Smith and Tyson Kidd.

Air Date: 29th January 2010
Triple H bested CM Punk by DQ; R-Truth upset Chris Jericho; WWE Intercontinental Champion Drew McIntyre toppled John Morrison in a no-DQ, no count-out match; WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool defeated Layla (disguised as Mickie James); Shawn Michaels battled Rey Mysterio to a no-contest.

Feb 2010

Air Date: 5th February 2010
John Morrison overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion Drew McIntyre and Kane in a non-title Triple Threat (Elimination Chamber qualifier); R-Truth beat Mike Knox (Elimination Chamber qualifier); CM Punk bested Batista by count-out (Elimination Chamber qualifier);Chris Jericho overturned Matt Hardy (Elimination Chamber qualifier); WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James and Beth Phoenix beat Michelle McCool and Layla; Rey Mysterio overcame Dolph Ziggler (Elimination Chamber qualifier).

Air Date: 12th February 2010
Rey Mysterio pinned CM Punk; Kane battled WWE Intercontinental Champion Drew McIntyre to a double count-out; Michelle McCool and Layla defeated WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James in a non-title handicap match; The Great Khali and Matt Hardy toppled DH Smith and Tyson Kidd; R-Truth overturned John Morrison; Chris Jericho rolled over World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker in a non-title no-DQ encounter.

Air Date: 19th February 2010
Kane downed Dolph Ziggler; Cryme Tyme beat Caylen Croft and Trent Barretta; CM Punk and Luke Gallows dropped John Morrison and R-Truth; DH Smith, Tyson Kidd and Natalya Neidhart overturned Matt Hardy, The Great Khali and Maria; Edge battled Batista to a no-contest.

Air Date: 26th February 2010
Dolph Ziggler beat John Morrison and R-Truth in a Triple Threat (Money in the Bank Qualifier); Michelle McCool pinned WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James to win the title. Vickie Guerrero was guest referee; Kane bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Drew McIntyre in a non-title match (Money in the Bank Qualifier); Shelton Benjamin upset CM Punk (Money in the Bank Qualifier); Edge pinned WWE US Champion The Miz in a non-title match.

Mar 2010

Air Date: 5th March 2010
Matt Hardy bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Drew McIntyre in a non-title match (Money in the Bank Qualifier); Shelton Benjamin downed Dolph Ziggler; Ezekiel Jackson toppled Jimmy Wang Yang; Rey Mysterio overturned Luke Gallows; John Morrison and R-Truth overcame DH Smith and Tyson Kidd, and Cryme Tyme in a Triple Threat match; Edge rolled over The Big Show.

Air Date: 12th March 2010
R-Truth overturned WWE US Champion The Miz in a non-title match; WWE IC Champion Drew McIntyre bested Aaron Bolo (Money in the Bank Qualifier; The Big Show dropped John Morrison; Cryme Tyme battled DH Smith and Tyson Kidd to a no-contest; Tiffany overcame WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool by DQ; Kane beat Luke Gallows by DQ.

Air Date: 19th March 2010
Dolph Ziggler and Jack Swagger defeated MVP and Matt Hardy; Beth Phoenix and Tiffany downed WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool and Layla; John Morrison and R-Truth downed DH Smith and Tyson Kidd; Luke Gallows dropped Rey Mysterio; The Undertaker pinned WWE Intercontinental Champion Drew McIntyre in a non-title match.

Air Date: 26th March 2010
WWE Intercontinental Champion Drew McIntyre, Dolph Ziggler, Kane, Shelton Benjamin and Matt Hardy toppled Christian, Evan Bourne, Kofi Kingston, MVP and Jack Swagger; WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool, WWE Divas Champion Maryse, Layla, Vickie Guerrero and Alicia Fox overturned Beth Phoenix in a handicap match; The Big Show bested R-Truth by DQ.

Apr 2010

Air Date: 2nd April 2010
Jack Swagger pinned World Heavyweight Champion Chris Jericho to win the title; John Morrison and R-Truth dropped Cryme Tyme; WWE Intercontinental Champion Drew McIntyre battled Matt Hardy to a no-contest; Dolph Ziggler defeated The Great Khali; Beth Phoenix and Tiffany overcame WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool and Layla; Kane toppled Daniel Bryan, Heath Slater, Michael Tarver, Skip Sheffield, Wade Barrett, Darren Young, David Otunga and Justin Gabriel in a handicap elimination match.

Air Date: 9th April 2010
World Heavyweight Champion Jack Swagger pinned John Morrison; WWE Intercontinental Champion Drew McIntyre downed Matt Hardy; DH Smith and Tyson Kidd overcame Trent Barretta and Caylen Croft; Edge battled Chris Jericho to a double count-out.

Air Date: 16th April 2010
WWE Intercontinental Champion Drew McIntyre defeated Kane by DQ; Dolph Ziggler beat R-Truth; WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool downed Mickie James; Rey Mysterio, DH Smith and Tyson Kidd overcame CM Punk, Luke Gallows and Darren Young; JTG downed Caylen Croft; World Heavyweight Champion Jack Swagger bested Edge and Chris Jericho in a Triple Threat match.

Air Date: 23rd April 2010
John Morrison pinned World Heavyweight Champion Jack Swagger in a non-title match; WWE Intercontinental Champion Drew McIntyre and Dolph Ziggler defeated R-Truth and Matt Hardy by stoppage; JTG dropped Mike Knox; WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool and Layla bested Beth Phoenix and Mickie James; CM Punk and Luke Gallows rolled over Rey Mysterio Jr and Kane.

Air Date: 30th April 2010
Kofi Kingston pinned Chris Jericho; Kane battled Chavo Guerrero to a no-contest; Cody Rhodes dropped John Morrison; WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix and Kelly Kelly overcame Michelle McCool and Layla; Rey Mysterio Jr and MVP toppled CM Punk and Luke Gallows.

May 2010

Air Date: 7th May 2010
MVP bested Luke Gallows; Michelle McCool and Layla toppled Kelly Kelly and Tiffany; Kofi Kingston beat Dolph Ziggler (WWE Intercontinental Title Tournament – Semi-Final); Christian overturned Cody Rhodes (WWE Intercontinental Title Tournament – Semi-Final); Kane dropped World Heavyweight Champion Jack Swagger by DQ.

Air Date: 14th May 2010
Kofi Kingston beat Christian to ostensibly win the WWE Intercontinental Title (WWE Intercontinental Title Tournament – Final); Shad Gaspard defeated Jesse Guyver; MVP and JTG downed Caylen Croft and Trent Barretta; Michelle McCool and Layla dropped WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix in a handicap match. By virtue of the win Layla won the title; Kane pinned Chavo Guerrero; Rey Mysterio bested CM Punk by DQ.

Air Date: 21st May 2010
Michelle McCool and WWE Divas Champion Layla beat Tiffany and Kelly Kelly; JTG defeated Trent Barretta; Curt Hawkins and Vance Archer downed Jeremy Barner and Brent Roberts; Kofi Kingston pinned World Heavyweight Champion Jack Swagger in a non-title match; Rey Mysterio and MVP bested CM Punk and Luke Gallows; The Big Show rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Drew McIntyre by DQ.

Air Date: 28th May 2010
The Big Show and WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston bested World Heavyweight Champion Jack Swagger and Drew McIntyre; CM Punk toppled Kane; Christian and Hornswoggle defeated Dolph Ziggler and Chavo Guerrero; Luke Gallows defeated MVP; The Undertaker rolled over Rey Mysterio.

Jun 2010

Air Date: 4th June 2010
World Heavyweight Champion Jack Swagger bested MVP; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston dropped Drew McIntyre; Kelly Kelly overcame Rosa Mendes; Rey Mysterio won a battle royal.

Air Date: 11th June 2010
Rey Mysterio bested World Heavyweight Champion Jack Swagger in a non-title match; WWE Divas Champion Layla overcame Tiffany; Christian defeated Dolph Ziggler; The Big Show downed CM Punk by DQ.

Air Date: 18th June 2010
Vance Archer and Curt Hawkins defeated MVP and Christian; JTG downed Chavo Guerrero; Drew McIntyre dropped Teddy Long; Kelly Kelly beat WWE Women’s Champion  Layla in a non-title match; Dolph Ziggler bested Chris Masters; The Big Show and Rey Mysterio rolled over CM Punk and World Heavyweight Champion Jack Swagger.

Air Date: 25th June 2010
The Big Show defeated Jack Swagger by DQ; MVP, Christian, and WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston downed Dolph Ziggler, Vance Archer, and Curt Hawkins; Matt Hardy bested Drew McIntyre; Kelly Kelly toppled Rosa Mendes; Kane battled CM Punk to a no-contest in a no-DQ match.

Jul 2010

Air Date: 2nd July 2010
WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston downed Dolph Ziggler; Kane dropped Luke Gallows by DQ; MVP overcame Chavo Guerrero; Kelly Kelly beat Michelle McCool; World Heavyweight Champion Rey Mysterio and The Big Show bested Jack Swagger and Cody Rhodes by DQ.

Air Date: 9th July 2010
Dolph Ziggler won a three-way over Chavo Guerrero and MVP; Curt Hawkins and Vance Archer defeated Matt Hardy and Christian; Drew McIntyre downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston in a non-title match; Cody Rhodes dropped JTG; The Big Show battled Jack Swagger to a double count-out.

Air Date: 16th July 2010
WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston battled Cody Rhodes to a no-contest; Drew McIntyre defeated Christian; Chris Masters and Kelly Kelly downed WWE Women’s Champion Layla and Trent Barretta; Dolph Ziggler bested Matt Hardy.

Air Date: 23rd July 2010
Drew McIntyre and Cody Rhodes defeated Matt Hardy and Christian; Dolph Ziggler downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston in a non-title match; The Big Show dropped Masked Mystery Man (Joey Mercury); Rey Mysterio rolled over Jack Swagger in a “best-of-three-falls” encounter.

Air Date: 30th July 2010
Christian bested Drew McIntyre; The Big Show toppled Luke Gallows by DQ; Dolph Ziggler overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston by DQ; WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool (a permitted substitute for Layla) overcame Tiffany; Rey Mysterio battled Jack Swagger to a no-contest in a no-DQ encounter.

Aug 2010

Air Date: 6th August 2010
Cody Rhodes defeated Matt Hardy; Dolph Ziggler overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston to win title; CM Punk, Luke Gallows and Joey Mercury bested JTG, MVP and Chris Masters; Rey Mysterio toppled Drew McIntyre.

Air Date: 13th August 2010
World Heavyweight Champion Kane rolled over Kofi Kingston; Cody Rhodes defeated Christian; The Big Show squashed Derek Werley, Fernando Vega, and Jody Reese; Drew McIntyre bested Matt Hardy; MVP defeated Jack Swagger; Rey Mysterio rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler in a non-title match.

Air Date: 20th August 2010
Kofi Kingston defeated WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler by DQ; Luke Gallows and Serena toppled The Big Show and Kelly Kelly; Christian beat Drew McIntyre; Alberto Del Rio downed Rey Mysterio.

Air Date: 27th August 2010
World Heavyweight Champion Kane rolled over Rey Mysterio in a no-DQ match; CM Punk toppled JTG; Kofi Kingston defeated WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler by count-out; Jack Swagger downed MVP by DQ in a “takedown challenge”; Alberto Del Rio bested Carlos Sanchez; The Big Show overturned Luke Gallows.

Sep 2010

Air Date: 3rd September 2010
Christian beat Drew McIntyre; Michelle McCool defeated Kelly Kelly; Dolph Ziggler overturned Chris Masters; The Big Show defeated Luke Gallows and CM Punk in a handicap match; Cody Rhodes rolled over Matt Hardy.

Air Date: 10th September 2010
Jack Swagger defeated MVP; Alberto Del Rio overcame Matt Hardy; Drew McIntyre toppled Kaval; Kofi Kingston and Chris Masters defeated Dolph Ziggler and Chavo Guerrero; The Undertaker rolled over CM Punk.

Air Date: 17th September 2010
Cody Rhodes and Drew McIntyre toppled WWE Unified World Tag Champions DH Smith and Tyson Kidd in a non-title match; Kofi Kingston downed Jack Swagger; WWE Women’s Champion Layla and Michelle McCool defeated Kelly Kelly and Rosa Mendes; CM Punk dropped Christian.

Air Date: 24th September 2010
World Heavyweight Champion Kane rolled over Chris Masters; WWE Unified World Tag Champion Drew McIntyre and Cody Rhodes dropped DH Smith and Tyson Kidd; Chavo Guerrero defeated Kaval; The Big Show overturned Curt Hawkins and Vance Archer in a handicap match; CM Punk dropped Luke Gallows;MVP overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler by count-out.

Oct 2010

Air Date: 1st October 2010
WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler battled MVP to a no-contest; Natalya, Kelly Kelly and The Bella Twins defeated Layla, WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool, Alicia Fox and Maryse; David Otunga, Wade Barrett, Heath Slater, Justin Gabriel and Michael Tarver downed The Big Show in a handicap match; The Undertaker rolled over CM Punk; WWE World Champion Randy Orton rolled over Cody Rhodes; John Cena battled World Heavyweight Champion Kane to a no-contest in a lumberjack match.

Air Date: 8th October 2010
Edge downed Jack Swagger; Layla bested Kelly Kelly; WWE Unified World Tag Champions Drew McIntyre and Cody Rhodes overcame Kaval and Kofi Kingston; WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler toppled MVP; Rey Mysterio overturned Alberto Del Rio.

Air Date: 15th October 2010
Rey Mysterio overturned Cody Rhodes; Jack Swagger dropped MVP; Alberto Del Rio dropped Chris Masters; Edge downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler in a non-title match; The Big Show battled Kaval to a draw; Tyler Reks beat Kaval; Kofi Kingston downed Drew McIntyre.

Air Date: 22nd October 2010
The Big Show, Rey Mysterio and Kofi Kingston downed The Miz, Sheamus and Ezekiel Jackson; Jack Swagger dropped Santino Marella; Alberto Del Rio dropped John Morrison; Edge battled CM Punk to a no-contest; Natalya and Kelly Kelly overcame Layla and WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool; WWE World Champion Randy Orton rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Kane in a non-title match.

Air Date: 29th October 2010
WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler; Jack Swagger dropped Kaval; Kelly Kelly, The Bella Twins and Melina beat Alicia Fox, Rosa Mendes, Layla and WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool; The Big Show and Kofi Kingston downed Drew McIntyre and Cody Rhodes; Edge won a three-way over Alberto Del Rio and Rey Mysterio.

Nov 2010

Air Date: 5th November 2010
Edge battled Alberto Del Rio to a no-contest; WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler downed JTG in a non-title match; MVP dropped Drew McIntyre and Cody Rhodes in a Triple Threat encounter; Vickie Guerrero pinned Kaitlyn; Tyler Reks overturned Chris Masters; World Heavyweight Champion Kane, Edge, The Big Show, Kofi Kingston and Alberto Del Rio rolled over David Otunga, Michael McGillicutty, Husky Harris and WWE Tag Team Champions Heath Slater & Justin Gabriel.

Air Date: 12th November 2010
Albert Del Rio toppled Kofi Kingston; Natalya Neidhart overcame WWE Divas Champion Layla in a non-title match; David Otunga pinned Edge in a lumberjack match; WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler bested MVP; The Big Show rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Kane in a non-title match.

Air Date: 19th November 2010
MVP dropped Drew McIntyre; Jack Swagger overcame Chris Masters; Kaval bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler in a non-title match; Cody Rhodes overturned Kofi Kingston; WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool and WWE Divas Champion Layla defeated Natalya Neidhart and Kelly Kelly; The Big Show pinned Tyler Reks; Rey Mysterio Jr defeated Alberto Del Rio by DQ; The Big Show and Rey Mysterio Jr co-won a 10-man battle royal.

Air Date: 26th November 2010
Kofi Kingston pinned Jack Swagger (KOTR Tournament – Qualifier); Alberto Del Rio overturned The Big Show by count-out (KOTR Tournament – Qualifier); Drew McIntyre bested MVP (KOTR Tournament – Qualifier); Kelly Kelly toppled Michelle McCool; Cody Rhodes dropped Rey Mysterio (KOTR Tournament – Qualifier).

Dec 2010

Air Date: 3rd December 2010
WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler and Drew McIntyre defeated MVP and Kaval; Kofi Kingston dropped Jack Swagger; Cody Rhodes downed Chris Masters; Layla bested Rosa Mendes; Edge pinned World Heavyweight Champion Kane in a non-title match.

Air Date: 10th December 2010
Jack Swagger beat Kaval; Hornswoggle downed The Soaring Eagle; Kofi Kingston toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler by DQ; Chris Masters defeated Cody Rhodes in a “Masterlock Challenge”; WWE World Tag Champions Santino Marella and Vladimir Kozlov overcame Drew McIntyre and Chavo Guerrero Jr ; WWE Divas Champion Natalya Neidhart dropped Layla; Edge and Rey Mysterio rolled over Alberto Del Rio in a handicap match.

Air Date: 17th December 2010
Kofi Kingston and Kaval toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler and Jack Swagger; Cody Rhodes downed Chris Masters; WWE World Champion The Miz dropped Rey Mysterio; WWE World Tag Champions Santino Marella and Vladimir Kozlov overcame Chavo Guerrero Jr and Tyler Reks; Edge and Rey Mysterio battled WWE World Champion The Miz and Alberto Del Rio to a no-contest.

Air Date: 21st December 2010
Randy Orton toppled WWE World Champion The Miz by DQ; Santino Marella overcame Chavo Guerrero Jr; Drew McIntyre bested Kaval; Rey Misterio Jr and Kofi Kingston dropped Alberto Del Rio and Jack Swagger; John Cena rolled over WWE Intercontinental Dolph Ziggler and Vickie Guerrero in a handicap match.

Air Date: 31st December 2010
The Big Show defeated Cody Rhodes by count-out; Drew McIntyre beat Trent Barretta; World Heavyweight Champion Edge and Rey Mysterio Jr downed Kane and Alberto Del Rio; WWE Divas Champion Natalya Neidhart and Beth Phoenix overcame Michelle McCool and Layla; WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler dropped Kofi Kingston and Jack Swagger in a Triple Threat encounter.
