Raw Results 2006

WWE RAW RESULTS (2006)

Jan 2006

Air Date: 2nd January 2006
Kane toppled Shawn Michaels; Maria beat Victoria; Chris Masters defeated Shelton Benjamin; Kurt Angle bested WWE World Champion John Cena in a non-title “first blood” match.

Air Date: 9th January 2006
Ashley Massaro bested WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus by DQ; Chavo Guerrero defeated Rob Conway; Chris Masters and Carlito downed Shawn Michaels and Kurt Angle; Shelton  Benjamin bested Val Venis; Kane rolled over Gene Snitsky.

Air Date: 16th January 2006
Shawn Michaels defeated World Heavyweight Champion Kurt Angle in a non-title match; Victoria, Candice Michelle and Torrie Wilson overcame WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus, Mickie James and Ashley Massaro; Shelton Benjamin downed The Big Show by count-out; WWE World Champion Edge rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Ric Flair in a TLC match.

Air Date: 23rd January 2006
Kane beat Carlito by DQ; Ashley Massaro and WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus defeated Victoria and Candice Michelle; Shawn Michaels downed Shelton Benjamin; Jonathan Coachman dropped Jerry Lawler; The Big Show overcame Lance Cade, Rob Conway and Gregory Helms in a four-way; John Cena and WWE Intercontinental Champion Ric Flair rolled over WWE World Champion Edge and Chris Masters.

Air Date: 30th January 2006
Rob Van Dam beat Gene Snitsky; Triple H defeated Chavo Guerrero Jr; WWE World Tag Champions Kane and The Big Show downed Carlito and Chris Masters; Shelton Benjamin dropped Goldust; Edge bested WWE World Champion John Cena by DQ.

Feb 2006

Air Date: 6th February 2006
Chris Masters dropped Kane (Road to Wrestlemania Tournament – Quarter-Final); Ashley overcame Mickie James; Triple H bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Ric Flair in a non-title match (Road to Wrestlemania Tournament – Quarter-Final); The Big Show toppled Shelton Benjamin (Road to Wrestlemania Tournament – Quarter-Final); Rob Van Dam pinned Carlito (Road to Wrestlemania Tournament – Quarter-Final); WWE World Champion John Cena and Maria overturned Edge and Lita.

Air Date: 16th February 2006
Triple H battled The Big Show to a double count-out (Road to Wrestlemania – Semi-Final); Shelton Benjamin defeated Eugene; Ashley downed Torrie Wilson; Rob Van Dam toppled Chris Masters (Road to Wrestlemania – Semi-Final); WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Edge.

Air Date: 20th February 2006
Candice Michelle won a battle royal; Edge pinned Jim Duggan; Kane downed Chris Masters; Shelton Benjamin overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion  Ric Flair to win the title; Shawn Michaels dropped Nicky by DQ; Carlito downed Nick Burke; Triple H rolled over the The Big Show and Rob Van Dam in a three-way (Road to Wrestlemania Tournament – Final).

Air Date: 27th February 2006
WWE World Tag Champions The Big Show and Kane defeated Val Venis and Viscera; Rob Van Dam pinned Trevor Murdoch (MITB Qualifier); WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus downed Candice Michelle; WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin dropped Chavo Guerrero Jr (MITB Qualifier); Ric Flair rolled over Carlito (MITB Qualifier).

Mar 2006

Air Date: 6th March 2006
Ric Flair bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin by DQ; Triple H toppled Kane; Shane McMahon beat Shawn Michaels; Vince McMahon beat Shawn Micheals; Kenny downed Eugene; Lita dropped Maria; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over The Big Show.

Air Date: 13th March 2006
WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus toppled Victoria; Edge overcame Goldust; Kenny, Johnny, Nicky, and Mikey defeated Shawn Michaels in a cage match; WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin beat Rob Van Dam; WWE World Champion John Cena, and WWE World Tag Champions Kane and The Big Show beat Triple H, Carlito, and Chris Masters by DQ.

Air Date: 20th March 2006
Kane overturned Carlito; WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin dropped Ric Flair and Rob Van Dam in a three-way; Victoria dropped Torrie Wilson; The Big Show beat Chris Masters by DQ; Triple H and Shane McMahon beat WWE World Champion John Cena and Shawn Michaels by DQ. Shawn Michaels battled Triple H and Shane McMahon to a no-contest in a handicap match.

Air Date: 27th March 2006
Kane overturned Carlito by DQ; Shawn Michaels battled Triple H to a no-contest; Mikey, Johnny, and Kenny defeated Eugene, Viscera, and Val Venis; WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus and Torrie Wilson beat Victoria and Candice Michelle; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Vince McMahon by DQ.

Apr 2006

Air Date: 3rd April 2006
Kenny and Mikey defeated WWE World Tag Champions Kane and The Big Show to win the titles; Chris Masters toppled Eric Priest; WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James and Candice Michelle overcame Trish Stratus and Torrie Wilson; WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin beat Chavo Guerrero Jr; Triple H and Edge overcame WWE World Champion John Cena in a non-title handicap match.

Air Date: 10th April 2006
Rob Van Dam downed Rob Conway; WWE World Tag Champions Johnny and Mikey defeated Kane and The Big Show by DQ; Umaga toppled Chris Guy (Colt Cabana); WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James toppled Maria; WWE World Champion John Cena and Triple H toppled Edge in a handicap match.

Air Date: 17th April 2006
Kane overturned Rob Conway; Charlie Haas toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin in a non-title match; Shawn Michaels toppled Umaga by DQ; WWE World Tag Champions Kenny, Johnny, Mikey, Nicky, and Mitch defeated Rob Van Dam in a handicap match; WWE World Champion John Cena and Edge overcame Triple H in a handicap match.

Air Date: 24th April 2006
Rob Van Dam, Carlito, and Charlie Haas toppled Chris Masters, Matt Striker, and WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin; Candice Michelle overcame Maria, Torrie Wilson, and Victoria in a bikini contest; Shawn Michaels battled Shane McMahon to a no-contest; Umaga toppled Steve Lewington; WWE World Champion John Cena, Edge, and Triple H battled WWE World Tag Champions The Spirit Squad to a no-contest in a handicap match.

May 2006

Air Date: 1st May 2006
Torrie Wilson and Maria dropped WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James and Victoria; WWE World Tag Champions Mikey, Nicky, and Johnny downed Eugene, Goldust and Gene Snitsky; Kane overturned Rob Conway. Shawn Michaels was guest referee; Umaga toppled Rory Fox; WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam and Carlito overcame Shelton Benjamin and Chris Masters; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Kenny. Triple H was guest referee.

Air Date: 8th May 2006
WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam downed Charlie Haas, Chris Masters, and Shelton Benjamin in a four-way; Matt Striker overcame Carlito by DQ; Mick Foley downed Edge and Tommy Dreamer in a three-way hardcore match; WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James toppled Maria; Umaga toppled Kevin Martenson and Tommy Wilson in a handicap match; The Big Show bested Kane by DQ; WWE World Tag Champions The Spirit Squad bested WWE World Champion John Cena and Shawn Michaels in a handicap match.

Air Date: 15th May 2006
Triple H, Shelton Benjamin, and Chris Masters toppled WWE World Champion John Cena and WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam in a “Texas Tornado” handicap match. By virtue of a pre-match stipulation, Benjamin won the WWE Intercontinental Title; WWE World Tag Champions Johnny and Nicky overturned Goldust and Gene Snitsky; Matt Striker overcame Eugene; Umaga toppled Chris Wellman; Shawn Michaels battled Kenny to a no-contest. Shane McMahon was guest referee.

Air Date: 22nd May 2006
WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin dropped Rob Van Dam by DQ; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Chris Masters; Kane overturned Trevor Murdoch; WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James toppled Torrie Wilson; Shawn Michaels battled WWE World Tag Champions Kenny, Mikey, Nickey, Mitch, and Johnny to a no-contest in a handicap match.

Air Date: 29th May 2006
Kane battled WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin to a no-contest; Umaga toppled Viscera; Torrie Wilson and Beth Phoenix beat Victoria and Candice Michelle; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Johnny Nitro; Edge overcame The Big Show; Triple H toppled  Kenny.

Jun 2006

Air Date: 5th June 2006
Carlito pinned WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin in a non-title match; The Big Show beat Triple H by DQ; Eugene toppled Matt Striker; Lance Cade overturned Kane by count-out; Johnny Nitro overcame Charlie Haas; Beth Phoenix defeated Victoria

Air Date: 12th June 2006
Kane battled Randy Orton to a double count-out; Torrie Wilson beat Candice Michelle in a “Wet and Wild Water” contest; Triple H battled WWE World Tag Champions Mikey, Kenny, Johnny, Nicky, and Mitch to a no-contest in a gauntlet match; Umaga toppled Jim Duggan; Johnny Nitro pinned Carlito; John Cena battled Edge to a no-contest.

Air Date: 19th June 2006
WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James and Candice Michelle overcame Torrie Wilson and Maria in a “bra and panties” match; Charlie Haas battled Viscera to a no-contest; Umaga toppled John McChestney; John Cena rolled over Balls Mahoney; Randy Orton beat Snitsky; Johnny Nitro and WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin toppled Carlito in a handicap match; Edge overcame Ric Flair.

Air Date: 26th June 2006
WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James toppled Trish Stratus; Umaga toppled Kamala; Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch overcame Val Venis and Viscera; Randy Orton beat Kane; John Cena battled WWE World Champion and ECW Champion Rob Van Dam to a no-contest.

Jul 2006

Air Date: 3rd July 2006
Lita dropped Torrie Wilson; WWE World Tag Champions Johnny, Kenny, Mikey, Nicky, and Mitch defeated Eugene, Val Venis, Viscera, Gene Snitsky, and Jim Duggan in a non-title match; Rory and Robbie McAllister defeated Rob Conway and Matt Striker; Carlito overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion Johnny Nitro by DQ; Umaga toppled Scott Wright; Edge overcame WWE World Champion and ECW Champion Rob Van Dam and John Cena in a no-DQ three-way match to win the WWE World Title.

Air Date: 10th July 2006
John Cena rolled over Shelton Benjamin; Melina pinned Trish Stratus; Robbie and Rory McAllister defeated Rob Conway and Matt Striker; Viscera battled Charlie Haas to a no-contest; Randy Orton beat Val Venis; Eugene overcame Vince and Shane McMahon in a no-DQ handicap match.

Air Date: 17th July 2006
Carlito pinned Shelton Benjamin; Trish Stratus and Torrie Wilson beat WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James and Victoria. Candice Michelle was guest referee; Robbie and Rory McAllister defeated Rod Steele and Russell Simpson; Umaga toppled John Cena; Randy Orton beat Eugene; Shawn Michaels rolled over Shane McMahon by DQ.

Air Date: 24th July 2006
WWE World Tag Champions Mikey and Kenny defeated Robbie and Rory McAllister; Shelton Benjamin beat Carlito; WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James toppled Candice Michelle; Shawn Michaels toppled Jonathan Coachman by DQ; John Cena and Ric Flair bested WWE World Champion Edge and WWE Intercontinental Champion Johnny Nitro.

Air Date: 31st July 2006
WWE Intercontinental Champion Johnny Nitro defeated Shelton Benjamin and Carlito in a three-way; Trish Stratus and Candice Michelle overcame WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James and Candice Michelle; John Cena rolled over Matt Striker; Rory McAllister downed Johnny; Umaga toppled Shawn Michaels.

Aug 2006

Air Date: 7th August 2006
Trish Stratus battled WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James to a no-contest; Kane overturned Shelton Benjamin; John Cena rolled over Viscera; WWE World Champion Edge and Lita dropped Carlito and Trish Stratus; Randy Orton beat Jerry Lawler; Umaga bested Triple H.

Air Date: 14th August 2006
Lita dropped WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James to win the title; Umaga toppled Alex Sage; Ric Flair bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Johnny Nitro by DQ; Eugene and Jim Duggan beat WWE World Tag Champions Mikey and Kenny in a non-title match; WWE World Champion Edge rolled over Carlito by DQ.

Air Date: 21st August 2006
Jim Duggan, Eugene, Robbie and Rory McAllister defeated WWE World Tag Champions Mickey, Nicky, Kenny, and Johnny; Trish Stratus toppled Victoria; Jeff Hardy downed WWE World Champion Edge by DQ; Kane overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Johnny Nitro by DQ; Ric Flair bested Randy Orton by reverse decision-DQ.

Air Date: 28th August 2006
John Cena rolled over Chris Masters by DQ; Candice Michelle overcame Torrie Wilson in a “paddle-on-a-pole” match; Jeff Hardy downed Randy Orton; WWE World Tag Champions Mikey and Kenny dropped Jim Duggan and Eugene; WWE Women’s Champion Lita dropped Mickie James; Shawn Michaels and Triple H toppled Mr Kennedy, WWE US Champion Finlay, and William Regal in a handicap match.

Sep 2006

Air Date: 4th September 2006
Jeff Hardy downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Johnny Nitro by DQ; Super Crazy downed Chris Masters; Robbie and Rory McAllister defeated Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch, and Viscera and Charlie Haas in a three-way; WWE World Champion Edge, WWE Women’s Champion Lita, and Randy Orton beat John Cena, Trish Stratus, and Carlito.

Air Date: 11th September 2006
Umaga toppled Ric Flair by DQ; Roddy Piper, Robbie and Rory McAllister defeated Johnny, Kenny, and Mikey; John Cena, Jeff Hardy, and Carlito overcame WWE World Champion Edge, WWE Intercontinental Champion Johnny Nitro, and Randy Orton; Super Crazy downed Chris Masters; Trish Stratus toppled Mickie James; Vince McMahon beat Triple H in a “no holds barred” match.

Air Date: 18th September 2006
Umaga toppled Kane by DQ; Ric Flair bested Johnny; WWE Intercontinental Champion Johnny Nitro defeated Randy Orton, Carlito, Super Crazy, Chris Masters, and Jeff Hardy in a six-way; Candice Michelle overcame Lita; Edge, Lance Cade, and Trevor Murdoch overcame WWE World Champion John Cena, Triple H, and Shawn Michaels by DQ.

Air Date: 25th September 2006
Lita dropped Candice Michelle (WWE Women’s Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); Kane overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Johnny Nitro by DQ; Ric Flair bested Mikey; Shawn Michaels and Triple H toppled Robbie and Rory McAllister, Charlie Haas and Viscera, and Trevor Murdoch and Lance Cade in a gauntlet match;  Jeff Hardy downed Shelton Benjamin; Carlito and Super Crazy downed Randy Orton and Chris Masters; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Lita in a “no holds barred” encounter.

Oct 2006

Air Date: 2nd October 2006
Shawn Michaels and Triple H toppled Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch, Robbie and Rory McAllister, and Charlie Haas and Viscera in a handicap “Texas Tornado” match; Jeff Hardy downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Johnny Nitro to win the title; Umaga toppled Gene Snitsky; Randy Orton beat Carlito; Sgt. Slaughter pinned Nicky; Mickie James toppled Victoria (WWE Women’s Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Edge in a cage match.

Air Date: 9th October 2006
Umaga toppled Kane in a “Loser leaves Raw” encounter; ECW World Champion The Big Show beat WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy in a non-title match; Triple H and Shawn Michaels toppled Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch in a streetfight; Chris Benoit toppled Shelton Benjamin; Batista, Bobby Lashley, and Rey Mysterio bested Finlay, William Regal, and Chavo Guerrero Jr; Melina beat Torrie Wilson in a lumberjack match (WWE Women’s Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); World Heavyweight Champion King Booker pinned Rob Van Dam; Ric Flair overturned  Mitch; WWE World Champion John Cena battled The Undertaker to a no-contest.

Air Date: 16th October 2006
Cryme Tyme overcame WWE World Tag Champions Mikey and Johnny in a non-title match; WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Chris Masters, Shelton Benjamin, and Super Crazy in a four-way; Carlito pinned Rob Conway; Maria dropped Torrie Wilson, Candice Michelle, and Victoria in a “bra and panties” match (WWE Women’s Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); Triple H bested Randy Orton.

Air Date: 23rd October 2006
Mickie James toppled Melina (WWE Women’s Title Tournament – Semi-Final); Shelton Benjamin and Chris Masters toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy and Carlito; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Johnny Nitro; Cryme Tyme overcame WWE World Tag Champions Mikey and Nicky in a non-title match; Kenny pinned Ric Flair; Randy Orton rolled over Triple H.

Air Date: 30th October 2006
Carlito overcame Shelton Benjamin and Johnny Nitro in a three-way; Lita dropped Maria (WWE Women’s Title Tournament – Semi-Final); Triple H battled Randy Orton to a no-contest; Umaga toppled Jim Duggan and Eugene in a handicap match; Cryme Tyme overcame Viscera and Charlie Haas; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Jonathan Coachman.

Nov 2006

Air Date: 6th November 2006
Johnny Nitro pinned WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy to win the title in a no-DQ match; Kenny and Johnny dropped Jim Duggan and Eugene in a “loser team must disband” encounter; Umaga battled Maria to a no-contest; Chris Masters toppled Jerry Lawler; Carlito pinned Shelton Benjamin; WWE Women’s Champion Lita dropped Mickie James; WWE World Tag Champions Ric Flair and Roddy Piper rolled over Randy Orton and Edge in a no-DQ match. Eric Bischoff was guest referee.

Air Date: 13th November 2006
Triple H and Shawn Michaels toppled Lance Cade, Trevor Murdoch, Viscera, and Charlie Haas in a handicap match; Kenny and Johnny downed Robbie and Rory McAllister; Chris Masters toppled Jerry Lawler in a “Masterlock Challenge”; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Umaga by DQ; Jeff Hardy downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Johnny Nitro to win the title; Mickie James toppled WWE Women’s Champion Lita in a non-title match; Edge and Randy Orton beat WWE World Tag Champions Ric Flair and Roddy Piper to win the titles.

Air Date: 20th November 2006
Umaga bested Sabu; WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed Johnny Nitro in a ladder match; Dusty Rhodes toppled Nicky; WWE Women’s Champion Lita dropped Mickie James; WWE World Champion John Cena, Shawn Michaels, Triple H, and Ric Flair bested ECW World Champion The Big Show, WWE World Tag Champions Edge and Randy Orton, and Kenny.

Air Date: 27th November 2006
Triple H, Shawn Michaels, and Ric Flair bested Kenny, Mikey, Nicky, Johnny, and Mitch in a handicap match; Victoria won a battle royal; Eugene overcame Jim Duggan; Jerry Lawler pinned Chris Masters; Matt Hardy and WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy downed WWE World Tag Champions Edge and Randy Orton by DQ.

Dec 2006

Air Date: 4th December 2006
Robbie and Rory McAllister defeated Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch; Victoria dropped Maria; Shelton Benjamin beat Super Crazy; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Chris Masters in a “Masterlock Challenge”; Carlito downed Viscera; WWE World Tag Champions Edge and Randy Orton, Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury overturned Shawn Michaels, Triple H, Matt Hardy, and WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy.

Air Date: 11th December 2006
WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Armando Allejandro Estrada; Carlito and Jerry Lawler defeated Chris Masters and Viscera; Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas toppled Robbie and Rory McAllister; Triple H bested Edge by DQ; Triple H, Shawn Michaels, and Ric Flair bested Kenny, and WWE World Tag Champions Edge and Randy Orton; Victoria dropped Torrie Wilson; Umaga toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy by stoppage in a non-title match; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Johnny Nitro.

Air Date: 18th December 2006
Edge won a battle royal; Johnny Nitro, Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin dropped Matt Hardy and WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy in a handicap match; Kenny Dykstra dropped Ric Flair; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Edge; Victoria dropped WWE Women’s Champion Mickie James in a non-title match; WWE World Champion John Cena, Triple H, and Shawn Michaels toppled Umaga and WWE World Tag Champions Edge and Randy Orton by DQ.

Air Date: 25th December 2006
WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Edge; CM Punk pinned Shelton Benjamin; The Undertaker rolled over Johnny Nitro; ECW World Champion Bobby Lashley dropped Hardcore Holly; Umaga dropped WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy in a non-title match; Carlito bested Randy Orton.
