WWE Royal Rumble Results

WWF/E ROYAL RUMBLE RESULTS

24th January 1988: “WWF Royal Rumble 1988”: Hamilton, ONT – 18,000 sellout
Card was not broadcast on PPV. Card was broadcast on USA Network (8.2 TV Rating)
Ricky Steamboat dropped Rick Rude by DQ; The Jumping Bomb Angels overturned WWF Women’s Tag Team Champions Lelani Kai and Judy Martin in a “best-of-three-falls” encounter;  Jim Duggan won the 20-man Royal Rumble match by last eliminating The One Man Gang; The Islanders rolled over Jim Powers and Paul Roma in a “best-of-three-falls” encounter.

15th January 1989: “WWF Royal Rumble 1989”: Houston, TX – 19,000 sellout
Dark matches: Jim Powers beat Barry Horowitz; Sam Houston defeated Steve Lombardi.
PPV Broadcast: Bret Hart, Jim Duggan, and Jim Neidhart downed Dino Bravo and The Rougeau Brothers; WWF Women’s Champion Rockin Robin bested Judy Martin;  Haku toppled Harley Race; Big John Studd won the 30-man Royal Rumble by last eliminating Ted Dibiase.

21st January 1990: “WWF Royal Rumble 1990”: Orlando, FL – 16,000 sellout
($170,000 live gate)
Dark match: Paul Roma beat The Brooklyn Brawler.
PPV Broadcast: Jim Duggan defeated The Big Bossman by DQ; The Bushwhackers downed The Rougeaus; Lanny Poffo battled Brutus Beefcake to a double-DQ; Ronnie Garvin toppled Greg Valentine in an “I Quit” encounter; Hulk Hogan won the 30-man Royal Rumble by last eliminating Curt Hennig.

19th January 1991: “WWF Royal Rumble 1991”: Miami, FL – 16,000 sellout
($210,000 live gate)
Dark match: Jerry Saggs defeated Sam Houston.
PPV Broadcast: The Rockers toppled The Orient Express; The Big Bossman overcame The Barbarian; Sgt Slaughter pinned WWF World Champion The Ultimate Warrior to win the title; The Mountie dropped Koko B Ware; Ted Dibiase and Virgil bested Dusty Rhodes and Dustin Rhodes; Hulk Hogan won the 30-man Royal Rumble by last eliminating Earthquake.

19th January 1992: “WWF Royal Rumble 1992”: Albany, NY – 17,000 sellout
($210,000 live gate)
Dark match: Chris Walker beat The Brooklyn Brawler by reverse decision DQ.
PPV Broadcast: Owen Hart and Jim Neidhart toppled The Orient Express ; Roddy Piper defeated WWF Intercontinental Champion The Mountie to win the title; The Beverly Brothers dropped The Bushwhackers; The Natural Disasters overcame WWF World Tag Champions The Legion of Doom by count-out; Ric Flair captured the vacant WWF World Title by winning the 30-man Royal Rumble match by last eliminating Sid Justice.

24th January 1993: “WWF Royal Rumble 1993”: Sacramento, CA – 16,000 sellout (15,000 paid)
($187,000 live gate)
Dark match: Doink beat Jim Powers.
PPV Broadcast: Rick and Scott Steiner defeated The Beverly Brothers; WWF Intercontinental Champion Shawn Michaels pinned Marty Jannetty; Bam Bam Bigelow dropped The Big Bossman; WWF World Champion Bret Hart rolled over Razor Ramon; Yokozuna won the 30-man Royal Rumble match, last eliminating Randy Savage.

22nd January 1994: “WWF Royal Rumble 1994”: Providence, RI – 14,500 sellout
($160,000 live gate)
Dark match: The Brooklyn Brawler defeated Jim Powers.
PPV Broadcast: Tatanka bested Bam Bam Bigelow; WWF World Tag Champions The Quebecers beat Bret Hart and Owen Hart by stoppage; WWF Intercontinental Champion Razor Ramon pinned IRS; WWF World Champion Yokozuna rolled over The Undertaker in a casket match; Bret Hart and Lex Luger co-won the Royal Rumble.

22nd January 1995: “WWF Royal Rumble 1995”: Tampa, FL – 10,000
($140,000 live gate)
Dark match: Buck Quartermaine downed The Brooklyn Brawler.
PPV Broadcast: Jeff Jarrett pinned WWF Intercontinental Champion Razor Ramon to win the title following a restart; The Undertaker overturned IRS; WWF World Champion Diesel battled Bret Hart to a draw; Bob Holly and The 1-2-3 Kid dropped Bam Bam Bigelow and Tatanka to win the vacant WWF World Tag Titles (WWF World Tag Title Tournament – Final); Shawn Michaels won the 30-man Royal Rumble, last eliminating Davey Boy Smith.

21st January 1996: “WWF Royal Rumble 1996”: Fresno, CA – 9,600 sellout (8,000 paid)
($130,000 live gate)
Free for All: Duke Droese overturned Hunter Hearst Helmsley by reverse decision DQ.
PPV Broadcast: Ahmed Johnson toppled Jeff Jarrett by DQ; WWF World Tag Champions The Smoking Gunns overcame Skip and Zip; Goldust bested WWF Intercontinental Champion Razor Ramon to win the title; Shawn Michaels won the 30-man Royal Rumble; The Undertaker rolled over WWF World Champion Bret Hart by DQ.

19th January 1997: “WWF Royal Rumble 1997”: San Antonio, TX – 60,525 (48,014 paid)
($480,013 live gate)
Dark matches: Perro Aguayo Jr and Venum defeated Maniaco and Mosco de la Merced; Octagon, Blue Demon Jr and Tinieblas Jr beat Heavy Metal, Abismo Negro and Histeria.
Free For All: La Parkita and Mascarita Sagrada Jr downed Mini Vader and Mini Mankind.
PPV Broadcast: WWF Intercontinental Champion Hunter Hearst Helmsley bested Goldust; Ahmed Johnson downed Farooq by DQ; Vader toppled The Undertaker; Perro Aguayo, Canek and Hector Garza overcame Fuerza Guerrera, Heavy Metal and Jerry Estrada; Steve Austin won the 30-man Royal Rumble by last eliminating Bret Hart; Shawn Michaels rolled over WWF World Champion Sid Vicious to win the title.

18th January 1998: “WWF Royal Rumble 1998”: San Jose, CA – 18,542 sellout (16,661 paid)
($414,373 live gate)
($159,023 merchandise sales)
Vader pinned TAFKA Goldust; Max Mini, Nova, and Mosaic overcame El Torito, Tarantula, and Battallion; WWF Intercontinental Champion The Rock downed Ken Shamrock by reverse decision-DQ; The Legion of Doom dropped WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg by DQ; Steve Austin won the 30-man Royal Rumble match by last eliminating The Rock; WWF World Champion Shawn Michaels rolled over The Undertaker in a casket match.

24th January 1999: “WWF Royal Rumble 1999”: Anaheim, CA – 14,816 sellout (13,597 paid)
($630,050 live gate)
($137,813 merchandise sales)
The Big Bossman  defeated WWF Hardcore Champion Road Dogg in a non-title match; WWF Intercontinental Champion Ken Shamrock bested Billy Gunn; WWF European Champion X-Pac pinned Gangrel; WWF Women’s Champion Sable toppled Luna in a strap match; The Rock rolled over WWF World Champion Mankind in an I Quit match to win the title; Vince McMahon won the 30-man Royal Rumble, last eliminating Steve Austin.

23rd January 2000: “WWF Royal Rumble 2000”: New York, NY (MSG) – 19,231 sellout (16,629 paid)
($1,142,540 live gate)
($157,375 merchandise sales)
Set record for the largest MSG gate in history at that point in time.
Tazz bested Kurt Angle; Matt and Jeff Hardy toppled The Dudley Boys in a tables match; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho defeated Chyna and Hardcore Holly in a Triple Threat to become the sole holder of the title; WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg downed The Acolytes; WWF World Champion Triple H rolled over Cactus Jack in a streetfight; The Rock won the 30-man Royal Rumble by last eliminating The Big Show.

21st January 2001: “WWF Royal Rumble 2001”: New Orleans, LA – 16,056 sellout  (13,481 paid)
($666,700 live gate)
($93,253 merchandise sales)
Heat: (01/21): D-Lo Brown and Chaz defeated Taka Michinoku and Sho Funaki.
PPV Broadcast: The Dudley Boys dropped WWF World Tag Champions Edge and Christian to win the titles; Chris Jericho bested WWF Intercontinental Champion Chris Benoit in a ladder match to win the title; WWF Women’s Champion Ivory toppled Chyna; WWF World Champion Kurt Angle rolled over Triple H; Steve Austin won the 30-man Royal Rumble, last eliminating Kane.

20th January 2002: “WWF Royal Rumble 2002”: Atlanta, GA – 12,915 sellout
($859,690 live gate)
($142,615 merchandise sales)
PPV Broadcast:  WWF World Tag Champions Tazz and Spike Dudley beat The Dudley Boys; William Regal pinned WWF Intercontinental Champion Edge to win the title; WWF Women’s Champion Trish Stratus overcame Jazz; Ric Flair overturned Vince McMahon in a streetfight; WWF Undisputed World Champion Chris Jericho bested The Rock; Triple H won the 30-man Royal Rumble match.

19th January 2003: “WWE Royal Rumble 2003”: Boston, MA – 14,712
($850,000 live gate)
Heat: (01/19): Spike Dudley beat Steven Richards.
PPV Broadcast: Brock Lesnar dropped The Big Show; The Dudley Boys overturned WWE World Tag Champions Lance Storm and William Regal to win the titles; Torrie Wilson bested Dawn Marie; Scott Steiner toppled World Heavyweight Champion Triple H by DQ; WWE World Champion Kurt Angle rolled over Chris Benoit; Brock Lesnar won the Royal Rumble match by last eliminating The Undertaker.

25th January 2004: “WWE Royal Rumble 2004”: Philadelphia, PA – 17,289 sellout
($1,085,000 live gate)
Heat: (01/25): Victoria beat WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly in a non-title match.
PPV Broadcast: WWE World Tag Champions Ric Flair and Batista overturned The Dudley Boys in a tables match; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio dropped Jamie Noble; Eddie Guerrero overcame Chavo Guerrero Jr; WWE World Champion Brock Lesnar dropped Hardcore Holly; Shawn Michaels battled World Heavyweight Champion Triple H to a draw in a “last man standing” match; Chris Benoit won the 30-man Royal Rumble match.

30th January 2005: “WWE Royal Rumble 2005”: Fresno, CA – 12,000 (11,000 paid)
($750,000 live gate)
Heat: (01/30): Maven beat Rhyno.
PPV Broadcast: Edge dropped Shawn Michaels; The Undertaker bested John Heidenreich in a casket match; WWE World Champion John Bradshaw Layfield won a three-way over  Kurt Angle and The Big Show; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H pinned Randy Orton;  Batista won the 30-man Royal Rumble match by last eliminating John Cena.

29th January 2006: “WWE Royal Rumble 2006”: Miami, FL – 14,500 sellout
($800,000 live gate)
Heat: (01/29): Finlay beat Brian Kendrick.
PPV Broadcast: Gregory Helms defeated WWE Cruiserweight Champion Kid Kash, Sho Funaki, Jamie Noble, Paul London, and Nunzio to win the title in a six-way match; Mickie James downed Ashley. Trish Stratus was guest referee; The Boogeyman dropped John Bradshaw Layfield; Rey Mysterio won the 30-man Royal Rumble by last eliminating Randy Orton; John Cena toppled WWE World Champion Edge to win the title; World Heavyweight Champion Kurt Angle rolled over Mark Henry.

28th January 2007: “WWE Royal Rumble 2007”: San Antonio, TX – 13,500 sellout
($780,000 live gate)
Dark match: JTG beat Lance Cade.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy and Matt Hardy downed Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury; ECW Champion Bobby Lashley toppled Test by count-out; World Heavyweight Champion Batista rolled over Mr Kennedy; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Umaga in a “last man standing” match; The Undertaker won the 30-man Royal Rumble by last eliminating Shawn Michaels.

27th January 2008: “WWE Royal Rumble 2008”: New York, NY – 17,382 sellout
($1,383,980 live gate)
Dark match: Jimmy Wang Yang and Shannon Moore beat Deuce and Domino.
PPV Broadcast: Ric Flair bested WWE US Champion MVP in a non-title match; John Bradshaw Layfield toppled Chris Jericho by DQ; World Heavyweight Champion Edge pinned Rey Mysterio; WWE World Champion Randy Orton rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Jeff Hardy; John Cena won the 30-man Royal Rumble by last eliminating Triple H.

25th January 2009: “WWE Royal Rumble 2009”: Detroit, MI – 16,685 sellout (14,000 paid)
($800,000 live gate)
Dark match: Jimmy Wang Yang beat Paul Burchill.
PPV Broadcast: ECW Champion Jack Swagger defeated Matt Hardy; Melina downed WWE Diva’s Champion Beth Phoenix to win the title; World Heavyweight Champion John Cena pinned John Bradshaw Layfield; Edge rolled over WWE World Champion Jeff Hardy in a no-DQ match to win the title; Randy Orton won the 30-man Royal Rumble by last eliminating Triple H.

31st January 2010: “WWE Royal Rumble 2010”: Atlanta, GA – 16,697 sellout (13,500 paid)
($800,000 live gate)
Dark match: Kelly Kelly, Eve, Gail Kim, Brie and Nikki Bella defeated Maryse, Natalya Neidhart, Jillian Hall, Katie Lea and Alicia Fox.
PPV Broadcast: ECW Champion Christian bested Ezekiel Jackson; WWE US Champion The Miz downed MVP; WWE World Champion Sheamus rolled over Randy Orton by DQ; Mickie James overcame WWE Women’s Champion Michelle McCool to win the title; World Heavyweight Champion The Undertaker pinned Rey Mysterio; Edge won the 30-man Royal Rumble, by last eliminating John Cena.

30th January 2011: “WWE Royal Rumble 2011”: Boston, MA – 15,113 sellout
($800,000 live gate)
Dark match: R-Truth beat Curt Hawkins
PPV Broadcast: World Heavyweight Champion Edge pinned Dolph Ziggler; WWE World Champion The Miz rolled over Randy Orton;  Eve defeated WWE Divas Champion Natalya Neidhart, Michelle McCool and Layla to win the title; Alberto Del Rio won the 40-man Royal Rumble by last eliminating Santino Marella.

29th January 2012: “WWE Royal Rumble 2012”: St Louis, MO – 18,121 sellout (15,000 paid)
($950,000 live gate)
Dark match: Yoshi Tatsu beat Heath Slater.
PPV Broadcast: World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan downed The Big Show and Mark Henry in a three-way cage match; WWE  Divas Champion Beth Phoenix, Natalya and The Bella Twins overcame Kelly Kelly, Eve Torres, Alicia Fox and Tamina; John Cena battled Kane to a double count-out; Brodus Clay bested Drew McIntyre; WWE World Champion CM Punk pinned Dolph Ziggler. John Laurinaitis was guest referee; Sheamus won the 30-man Royal Rumble.

27th January 2013: “WWE Royal Rumble 2013”: Phoenix, AZ – 13,000 sellout (12,000 paid)
Pre-show: WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro defeated The Miz.
PPV Broadcast: World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio bested The Big Show in a “Last Man Standing” match; WWE World Tag Champions Kane and Daniel Bryan toppled Damien Sandow and Cody Rhodes; John Cena won the Royal Rumble, last eliminating Ryback; The Rock rolled over WWE World Champion CM Punk to win the title.

26th January 2014: “WWE Royal Rumble 2014”: Pittsburgh, PA – 12,000 sellout
Pre-show: Billy Gunn and Road Dogg toppled WWE World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Goldust to win the titles.
PPV Broadcast: Bray Wyatt bested Daniel Bryan; Brock Lesnar dropped The Big Show; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton pinned John Cena; Batista won the 30-man Royal Rumble match by last eliminating Roman Reigns.

25th January 2015: “WWE Royal Rumble 2015”: Philadelphia, PA – 14,000 sellout
Shown on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Tyson Kidd and Cesaro toppled Kofi Kingston and Big E.
PPV Broadcast: The Ascension bested Road Dogg and Billy Gunn; WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso downed The Miz and Damien Mizdow; The Bella Twins dropped Natalya and Paige; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Brock Lesnar won a three-way over John Cena and Seth Rollins; Roman Reigns won the 30-man Royal Rumble, last eliminating Rusev.

24th January 2016: “WWE Royal Rumble 2016”: Orlando, FL – 12,000 sellout
Shown on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Jack Swagger and Mark Henry won a four-way over The Dudley Boys, The Ascension, and Darren Young and Damien Sandow.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion Dean Ambrose defeated Kevin Owens in a “last man standing” match; WWE World Tag Champions Big E and Kofi Kingston downed Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso; Kalisto bested WWE US Champion Alberto Del Rio to win the title; WWE Divas Champion Charlotte toppled Becky Lynch; Triple H won the 30-man Royal Rumble, last eliminating Dean Ambrose. By virtue of his victory, Triple H was awarded the WWE World Heavyweight Title.

29th January 2017: “WWE Royal Rumble 2017”: San Antonio, TX – 42,000
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Kickoff show: Becky Lynch, Nikki Bella and Naomi dropped Alexa Bliss, Mickie James and Natalya; Luke Gallows and Karl Anderson overcame WWE Raw Tag Champions Cesaro and Sheamus to win the titles; Nia Jax defeated Sasha Banks.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Raw Women’s Champion Charlotte Flair defeated Bayley; WWE Universal Champion Kevin Owens defeated Roman Reigns in a no-DQ match; Neville downed WWE Cruiserweight Champion Rich Swann to win the title; John Cena beat WWE World Champion AJ Styles to win the title; Randy Orton won the 30-man Royal Rumble, last eliminating Roman Reigns.
