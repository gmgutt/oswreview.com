WWF PPV Results 1994

WWF PPV RESULTS (1994)

See also: WWF PPV Statistics 1994-1995

22nd January 1994: “WWF Royal Rumble 1994”: Providence, RI – 14,500 sellout
($160,000 live gate)
Dark match: The Brooklyn Brawler defeated Jim Powers.
PPV Broadcast: Tatanka bested Bam Bam Bigelow; WWF World Tag Champions The Quebecers beat Bret Hart and Owen Hart by stoppage; WWF Intercontinental Champion Razor Ramon pinned IRS; WWF World Champion Yokozuna rolled over The Undertaker in a casket match; Bret Hart and Lex Luger co-won the Royal Rumble.

20th March 1994: “WWF Wrestlemania X”: New York, NY (MSG) – 18,065 sellout
($960,000 live gate)
($237,000 merchandise sales)
An additional 4,200 watched the event on closed-circuit in the The Paramount. There were an additional 7 Fan-Fests with 1,700 each (1,000 paid).
Dark match: The Heavenly Bodies beat The Bushwhackers.
PPV Broadcast: Owen Hart pinned Bret Hart; Bam Bam Bigelow and Luna Vachon defeated Doink and Dink; Randy Savage dropped Crush in a “falls-count-anywhere (Texas Death)” match; WWF Women’s Champion Alundra Blayze overturned Leilani Kai; Men on a Mission overcame WWF World Tag Champions The Quebecers by count-out; WWF World Champion Yokozuna bested Lex Luger by DQ. Curt Hennig was guest referee; Earthquake downed Adam Bomb; WWF Intercontinental Champion Razor Ramon toppled Shawn Michaels in a ladder match; Bret Hart rolled over WWF World Champion Yokozuna to win the title. Roddy Piper was guest referee.

19th June 1994: “WWF King of the Ring 1994”: Baltimore, MD – 12,000 sellout
($170,000 live gate)
Dark match: Sparky Plugg beat Bob Holly.
PPV Broadcast: Razor Ramon pinned Bam Bam Bigelow (KOTR Quarter-Final); IRS downed Mabel (KOTR Quarter-Final); Owen Hart toppled Tatanka (KOTR Quarter-Final); The 1-2-3 Kid overturned Jeff Jarrett (KOTR Quarter-Final); WWF Intercontinental Champion Diesel rolled over WWF World Champion Bret Hart by DQ; Razor Ramon downed IRS (KOTR Semi-Final); Owen Hart toppled The 1-2-3 Kid (KOTR Semi-Final); WWF World Tag Champions The Headshrinkers overturned Yokozuna and Crush; Owen Hart bested Razor Ramon (KOTR Final); Roddy Piper pinned Jerry Lawler.

29th August 1994: “WWF Summerslam 1994”: Chicago, IL – 23,000 sellout (19,500 paid)
($386,435 live gate)
Dark match: Adam Bomb beat Kwang
PPV Broadcast: Bam Bam Bigelow and IRS downed The Headshrinkers by DQ; WWF Women’s Champion Alundra Blayze dropped Bull Nakano; Razor Ramon overturned WWF Intercontinental Champion Diesel to win the title; Tatanka downed Lex Luger; Jeff Jarrett bested Mabel; WWF World Champion Bret Hart rolled over Owen Hart in a cage match; The Undertaker (Mark Callaway) pinned The Undertaker (Brian Lee).

23rd November 1994: “WWF Survivor Series 1994”: San Antonio, TX –  10,000 (8,000 paid)
($140,000 live gate)
Dark match: Bob Holly beat Kwang.
PPV Broadcast: WWF Intercontinental Champion Razor Ramon, The 1-2-3 Kid, Davey Boy Smith and TheNew Headshrinkers overturned Owen Hart, Jim Neidhart, Jeff Jarrett and WWF World Tag Champions Shawn Michaels and Diesel in a Survivors elimination match; Jerry Lawler, Queasy, Sleazy and Cheesy bested Doink, Dink, Wink and Pink; Bob Backlund rolled over WWF World Champion Bret Hart to win the title via stoppage in a match that could only end when the combatants seconds (Owen Hart or Davey Boy Smith respectively) threw in the towel; Tatanka, Bam Bam Bigelow, King Kong Bundy and The Heavenly Bodies toppled Lex Luger, Adam Bomb, Mabel and The Smoking Gunns; The Undertaker dropped Yokozuna in a casket match. Chuck Norris was guest ringside enforcer.
