WWE NXT Live Specials 2017

WWE NXT LIVE SPECIALS (2017)

28th January 2017: “WWE NXT Takeover: San Antonio”: San Antonio, TX – 7,000 sellout
Shown live on WWE Network
Dark match: Wolfgang defeated Aaron Solow.
NXT: (02/01): Ember Moon overcame Aliyah; Tyler Bate downed Oney Lorcan; No Way Jose downed Elias Samson
NXT Takeover: San Antonio Live Broadcast: Eric Young defeated Tye Dillinger; Roderick Strong defeated Andrade Almas; Akam and Rezar rolled over WWE NXT Tag Champions Johnny Gargano and Tommaso Ciampa to win the titles; WWE NXT Women’s Champion Asuka defeated Billie Kay, Nikki Cross and Peyton Royce in a four-way; Bobby Roode downed WWE NXT Champion Shinsuke Nakamura to win the title.

1st April 2017: “WWE NXT Takeover: Orlando”: Orlando, FL – 11,975 sellout
Shown live on WWE Network
NXT: (04/05): Tucker Knight and Otis Dozovic defeated Harv Sihra and Gurv Sihra; Peyton Royce downed Aliyah; Oney Lorcan overcame El Vagabundo.
WWE NXT Takeover Orlando Live Broadcast: Killian Dain, Eric Young, Nikki Cross and Alexander Wolfe downed Tye Dillinger, Roderick Strong, Kassius Ohno and Ruby Riot; Aleister Black defeated Andrade Almas; WWE NXT Tag Champions Akam and Rezar defeated Scott Dawson and Dash Wilder, and Johnny Gargano and Tommaso Ciampa in a three-way elimination match; WWE NXT Women’s Champion Asuka defeated Ember Moon; WWE NXT Champion Bobby Roode defeated Shinsuke Nakamura.
