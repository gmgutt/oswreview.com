WWE PPV Results 2014

WWE PPV RESULTS (2014)

See also: WWE Network Statistics

See also: WWE PPV Statistics 2014

26th January 2014: “WWE Royal Rumble 2014”: Pittsburgh, PA – 12,000 sellout
Pre-show: Billy Gunn and Road Dogg toppled WWE World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Goldust to win the titles.
PPV Broadcast: Bray Wyatt bested Daniel Bryan; Brock Lesnar dropped The Big Show; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton pinned John Cena; Batista won the 30-man Royal Rumble match by last eliminating Roman Reigns.

23rd February 2014: “WWE Elimination Chamber 2014”: Minneapolis, MN – 11,000 sellout
($700,000 live gate)
Pre-show: Cody Rhodes and Goldust beat Ryback and Curtis Axel.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E Langston overcame Jack Swagger; WWE World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn overturned Jimmy and Jey Uso; Titus O’Neill toppled Darren Young; Bray Wyatt, Luke Harper and Erick Rowan rolled over Roman Reigns, Seth Rollins and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose; Cameron bested WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee by DQ; Batista pinned Alberto Del Rio; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton won an Elimination Chamber match over Daniel Bryan, John Cena, Cesaro, Christian and Sheamus.

6th April 2014: “WWE Wrestlemania XXX”: New Orleans, LA  – 65,000 (60,000 paid)
($9,800,000 live gate)
Shown on PPV / WWE Network
Pre-show: WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso won a four-way elimination match over Cesaro and Jack Swagger, Los Matadores, and Ryback and Curtis Axel.
PPV Broadcast: Daniel Bryan bested Triple H; Roman Reigns, WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose, and Seth Rollins defeated Kane, Billy Gunn, and Road Dogg; Cesaro won the 31-man “Andre the Giant memorial” battle royal by last eliminating The Big Show; John Cena downed Bray Wyatt; Brock Lesnar pinned The Undertaker; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee won a “Divas Invitational” match over Naomi, Cameron, Natalya, Brie Bella, Nikki Bella, Eva Marie, Summer Rae, Alicia Fox, Aksana, Emma, Rosa Mendes, Layla, and Tamina Snuka; Daniel Bryan won a three-way over WWE World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton and Batista to win the title.

4th May 2014: “WWE Extreme Rules 2014”: East Rutherford, NJ – 14,500 sellout (12,900 paid)
Shown on PPV / WWE Network
Pre-show: El Torito beat Hornswoggle in a “WeeLC” encounter.
PPV Broadcast: Cesaro won a three-way elimination match over Jack Swagger and Rob Van Dam; Rusev defeated R-Truth and Xavier Woods in a handicap match; Wade Barrett downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E to win the title; Roman Reigns, WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose and Seth Rollins bested Triple H, Batista and Randy Orton; Bray Wyatt dropped John Cena in a cage match; WWE Divas Champion Paige toppled Tamina Snuka; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan rolled over Kane in an “Extreme Rules” match.

1st June 2014: “WWE Payback 2014”: Chicago, IL – 11,000 sellout
Shown on PPV / WWE Network
Pre-show: Torito beat Hornswoggle in a “mask vs hair” match.
PPV Broadcast: WWE US Champion Sheamus defeated Cesaro;  Ryback and Curtis Axel downed Goldust and Cody Rhodes; Rusev dropped Big E; Bo Dallas battled to a no-contest with Kofi Kingston; WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett overcame Rob Van Dam; John Cena bested Bray Wyatt in a “last man standing” match; WWE Divas Champion Paige overturned Alicia Fox; Roman Reigns, Dean Ambrose and Seth Rollins rolled over Triple H, Randy Orton and Batista in a “No Holds Barred” elimination match.

29th June 2014: “WWE Money in the Bank 2014”: Boston, MA – 12,000 sellout
Shown on PPV/WWE Network
PPV Broadcast: WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso beat Luke Harper and Erick Rowan; WWE Divas Champion Paige defeated Naomi; Adam Rose downed Damien Sandow; Seth Rollins won a “Money in the Bank” ladder match over Dean Ambrose, Dolph Ziggler, Kofi Kingston, Jack Swagger, and Rob Van Dam; Goldust and Stardust dropped Ryback and Curtis Axel; Rusev overcame Big E; Layla pinned Summer Rae. Fandango was guest referee; John Cena won an eight-way ladder match over Roman Reigns, Randy Orton, Sheamus, Kane, Bray Wyatt, Alberto Del Rio, and Cesaro to win the vacant WWE World Heavyweight Title.

20th July 2014: “WWE Battleground 2014”: Tampa, FL – 11,000
Shown on WWE Network/PPV
Pre-show: Adam Rose beat Fandango; Cameron defeated Naomi.
PPV Broadcast: WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso downed Luke Harper and Erick Rowan in a “best-of-three-falls” encounter; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee defeated Paige; Rusev beat Jack Swagger by count out; Chris Jericho bested Bray Wyatt; The Miz won a battle royal to win the vacant WWE Intercontinental Title, last eliminating Dolph Ziggler; WWE World Heavyweight Champion John Cena rolled over Kane, Randy Orton, and Roman Reigns in a four-way.

17th August 2014: “WWE Summerslam 2014”: Los Angeles, CA – 14,000 sellout
Shown on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Rob Van Dam pinned Cesaro.
PPV Broadcast: Dolph Ziggler beat WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz to win the title; Paige downed WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee to win the title; Rusev overturned Jack Swagger by stoppage in a flag match; Seth Rollins dropped Dean Ambrose in a lumberjack match; Bray Wyatt toppled Chris Jericho; Stephanie McMahon bested Brie Bella; Roman Reigns pinned Randy Orton; Brock Lesnar rolled over WWE World Heavyweight Champion John Cena to win the title.

21st September 2014: “WWE Night of Champions 2014”: Nashville, TN – 11,000
Shown on PPV / WWE Network
Goldust and Stardust defeated WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso to win the titles; WWE US Champion Sheamus downed Cesaro; The Miz dropped WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler to win the title; Rusev toppled Mark Henry; Randy Orton pinned Chris Jericho; AJ Lee bested WWE Divas Champion Paige and Nikki Bella in a three-way to win the title; John Cena rolled over WWE World Heavyweight Champion Brock Lesnar by DQ.

26th October 2014: “WWE Hell in a Cell 2014”: Dallas, TX – 13,000 sellout (12,100 paid)
Shown on PPV / WWE Network
Pre-show: Mark Henry pinned Bo Dallas.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler overcame Cesaro in a “best-of-three-falls” match; Nikki Bella beat Brie Bella in a “loser-becomes-winner’s-assistant-for-30-days” match; WWE World Tag Champions Goldust and Stardust toppled Jimmy and Jey Uso;  John Cena bested Randy Orton in a “Hell-in-a-Cell” match; WWE US Champion Sheamus downed The Miz; Rusev overturned The Big Show; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee beat Paige; Seth Rollins rolled over Dean Ambrose in a “Hell-in-a-Cell” match.

23rd November 2014: “WWE Survivor Series 2014”: St Louis, MO – 12,000 (11,000 paid)
Shown on PPV / WWE Network
Pre-show: Fandango beat Justin Gabriel; Jack Swagger defeated Cesaro.
PPV Broadcast: The Miz and Damien Mizdow won a four-way over WWE World Tag Champions Goldust and Stardust, Jimmy and Jey Uso, and Los Matadores to win the titles; Natalya, Naomi, Emma and Alicia Fox downed Paige, Summer Rae, Cameron and Layla in a “Survivor Series” elimination match; Bray Wyatt bested Dean Ambrose by DQ; Adam Rose and The Bunny dropped Titus O’Neil and Heath Slater; Nikki Bella overcame WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee to win the title; John Cena, Dolph Ziggler, Ryback, The Big Show and Erick Rowan rolled over Seth Rollins, Kane, WWE Intercontinental Champion Luke Harper, WWE US Champion Rusev and Mark Henry in a “Survivor Series” elimination match.

14th December 2014: “WWE TLC 2014”: Cleveland, OH – 14,000
Shown on PPV / WWE Network
Pre-show: Kofi Kingston and Big E defeated Goldust and Stardust.
PPV Broadcast: Dolph Ziggler dropped WWE Intercontinental Champion Luke Harper in a ladder match to win the title; Jimmy and Jey Uso downed WWE World Tag Champions The Miz and Damien Mizdow by DQ; The Big Show toppled Erick Rowan in a stairs match; John Cena bested Seth Rollins in a tables match; WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella overturned AJ Lee; Ryback pinned Kane in a chairs match; WWE US Champion Rusev overcame Jack Swagger; Bray Wyatt rolled over Dean Ambrose in a TLC match.
