WWF PPV Results 1999

WWF PPV RESULTS (1999)

See also: WWF PPV Statistics 1999

24th January 1999: “WWF Royal Rumble 1999”: Anaheim, CA – 14,816 sellout (13,597 paid)
($630,050 live gate)
($137,813 merchandise sales)
The Big Bossman  defeated WWF Hardcore Champion Road Dogg in a non-title match; WWF Intercontinental Champion Ken Shamrock bested Billy Gunn; WWF European Champion X-Pac pinned Gangrel; WWF Women’s Champion Sable toppled Luna in a strap match; The Rock rolled over WWF World Champion Mankind in an I Quit match to win the title; Vince McMahon won the 30-man Royal Rumble, last eliminating Steve Austin.

14th February 1999: “WWF St Valentine’s Day Massacre”: Memphis, TN – 19,028 sellout (17,977 paid)
($316,618 live gate)
($143,215 merchandise sales)
Goldust pinned The Blue Meanie; Hardcore Holly defeated Al Snow in a Hardcore match to capture the vacant WWF Hardcore Title; The Big Bossman beat Mideon; WWF World Tag Champions Jeff Jarrett and Owen Hart dropped Mark Henry and D-Lo Brown; Val Venis toppled WWF Intercontinental Champion Ken Shamrock to win the title. Billy Gunn was guest referee; Kane and Chyna bested Triple H and WWF European Champion X-Pac; The Rock battled WWF World Champion Mankind to a double knock-out in a Last Man Standing encounter; Steve Austin rolled over Vince McMahon in a cage match.

28th March 1999: “WWF Wrestlemania XV”: Philadelphia, PA – 18,274 sellout (16,756 paid)
($1,438,050 live gate)
($274,384 merchandise sales)
Hardcore Holly overcame WWF Hardcore Champion Billy Gunn and Al Snow in a Triple Threat match to win the title; WWF World Tag Champions Jeff Jarrett and Owen Hart defeated Test and D-Lo Brown; Butterbean beat Bart Gunn in a boxing match; Mankind downed The Big Show by DQ; WWF Intercontinental Champion Road Dogg overturned Goldust, Val Venis and Ken Shamrock in a Fatal Four Way elimination match; Kane toppled Triple H by DQ; WWF Women’s Champion Sable bested Tori; WWF European Champion Shane McMahon downed X-Pac; The Undertaker dropped The Big Bossman in a Hell-in-a-Cell match; Steve Austin rolled over WWF World Champion The Rock to win the title. Mankind was guest referee.

25th April 1999: “WWF Backlash 1999”: Providence, RI – 10,939 sellout
($293,873 live gate)
($83,282 merchandise sales)
The Acolytes and Mideon defeated Edge, Christian and Gangrel; Al Snow downed WWF Hardcore Champion Hardcore Holly to win the title; WWF Intercontinental Champion The Godfather bested Goldust; Road Dogg and Billy Gunn toppled Owen Hart and Jeff Jarrett; Mankind dropped The Big Show in a Boiler Room Brawl; Triple H pinned X-Pac; The Undertaker overturned Ken Shamrock; WWF World Champion Steve Austin rolled over The Rock in a “No Holds Barred” match. Shane McMahon was guest referee.

23rd May 1999: “WWF Over The Edge 1999”: Kansas City, MO – 16,472 sellout
($453,893 live gate)
($122,994 merchandise sales)
WWF Intercontinental Champion The Godfather vs. Blue Blazer (Owen Hart) was scheduled for the show. However the bout did not take place. Hart died whilst performing a pre-match stunt during his entrance.
WWF World Tag Champions X-Pac and Kane dropped D-Lo Brown and Mark Henry; WWF Hardcore Champion Al Snow downed Hardcore Holly; Val Venis and Nicole Bass bested Jeff Jarrett and Debra; Billy Gunn toppled Road Dogg; Mankind, The Big Show, Test and Ken Shamrock dropped The Acolytes, Viscera and The Big Bossman in an elimination match; The Rock toppled Triple H by DQ; The Undertaker rolled over WWF World Champion Steve Austin to win the title. Vince McMahon was guest referee.

27th June 1999: “WWF King of the Ring 1999”: Greensboro, NC – 19,761 sellout (18,574 paid)
($574,200 live gate)
($110,692 merchandise sales)
X-Pac beat Hardcore Holly by DQ (KOTR 1999 – Quarter-Final); Kane downed The Big Show (KOTR 1999 – Quarter-Final); Billy Gunn bested Ken Shamrock by stoppage (KOTR 1999 – Quarter-Final); Road Dogg pinned Chyna (KOTR 1999 – Quarter-Final); Matt and Jeff Hardy dropped Edge and Christian; Billy Gunn toppled Kane (KOTR 1999 – Semi-Final); X-Pac downed Road Dogg (KOTR 1999 – Semi-Final); WWF World Champion The Undertaker dropped The Rock; Billy Gunn toppled X-Pac (KOTR 1999 – Final); Vince McMahon and Shane McMahon rolled over Steve Austin in a handicap “ 100% Control of the Company” ladder match.

25th July 1999: “WWF Fully Loaded 1999”: Buffalo, NY – 16,605 (15,194 paid)
($547,380 live gate)
($102,007 merchandise sales)
Jeff Jarrett defeated WWF Intercontinental Champion Edge to win the title; The Acolytes beat Michael Hayes and WWF World Tag Champions Matt and Jeff Hardy in a no-DQ, handicap match to win the titles; D-Lo Brown pinned WWF European Champion Mideon to win the title; The Big Bossman bested WWF Hardcore Champion Al Snow to win the title; The Big Show downed Kane. Hardcore Holly was guest referee; Ken Shamrock dropped Steve Blackman in an Iron Circle match; X-Pac and Road Dogg toppled Billy Gunn and Chyna; Triple H bested The Rock in a no-DQ, falls-count-anywhere, strap match; WWF World Champion Steve Austin rolled over The Undertaker in a First Blood match.

22nd August 1999: “WWF Summerslam 1999”: Minneapolis, MN –  17,370 sellout (15,973 paid)
($557,129 live gate)
($168,456 merchandise sales)
Jeff Jarrett defeated WWF Intercontinental and European Champion D-Lo Brown to win the titles; The Acolytes beat Matt and Jeff Hardy, Edge and Christian, Mideon and Viscera, Albert and Droz, and Hardcore and Crash Holly in a tag team turmoil match; Al Snow pinned WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Bossman to win the title; WWF Women’s Champion Ivory bested Tori; Ken Shamrock dropped Steve Blackman in a “Lion’s Den” match; Test pinned Shane McMahon in a “Greenwich” streetfight; The Undertaker and The Big Show toppled WWF World Tag Champions Kane and X-Pac to win the titles; The Rock rolled over Billy Gunn in a “Kiss My Ass” match; Mankind downed WWF World Champion Steve Austin and Triple H in a Triple Threat match to win the title. Jesse Ventura was the guest referee.

26th September 1999: “WWF Unforgiven 1999”: Charlotte, NC – 15,779
($505,477 live gate)
($93,006 merchandise sales)
Val Venis beat Steve Blackman; D-Lo Brown pinned WWF European Champion Mark Henry to win the title; WWF Intercontinental Champion Jeff Jarrett downed Chyna by reverse decision-DQ; The Acolytes bested The Dudley Boys; WWF Women’s Champion Ivory defeated Luna in a hardcore match; WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg toppled Edge and Christian; WWF Hardcore Champion Al Snow overcame The Big Bossman in a “Kennel from Hell” match; X-Pac toppled Chris  Jericho by DQ;  Triple H bested The Rock, Mankind, The Big Show, Davey Boy Smith and Kane in a ”six-pack” match to win the vacant WWF World Title.

17th October 1999: “WWF No Mercy 1999”: Cleveland, OH – 18,752 sellout (17,430 paid)
($478,156 live gate)
($130,303 merchandise sales)
The Godfather beat Mideon; The Fabulous Moolah upset WWF Women’s Champion Ivory to win the title; Hardcore and Crash Holly downed Billy Gunn and Road Dogg by DQ; Chyna pinned WWF Intercontinental Champion Jeff Jarrett to win the title in a “Good Housekeeping” match; The Rock bested Davey Boy Smith; Matt and Jeff Hardy overturned Edge and Christian in a ladder match; Val Venis toppled Mankind; X-Pac defeated Kane, Farooq and Bradshaw in a Fatal Four Way elimination match; WWF World Champion Triple H bested Steve Austin in a “no-holds-barred” match.

14th November 1999: “WWF Survivor Series 1999”: Detroit, MI – 18,735 sellout (17,294 paid)
($841,095 live gate)
($153,536 merchandise sales)
The Godfather, D-Lo Brown and The Headbangers overcame The Acolytes and The Dudley Boys in a Survivor Series elimination match;Kurt Angle bested Shawn Stasiak; Val Venis, Mark Henry, Gangrel and Steve Blackman downed WWF European Champion Davey Boy Smith, Pete Gas, Rodney and Joey Abs in a Survivor Series elimination match; The Fabulous Moolah, Mae Young, Tori and Debra dropped WWF Women’s Champion Ivory, Jackie, Luna Vachon and Terri in a Survivor Series elimination match; Kane toppled X-Pac by DQ; The Big Show overturned WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Bossman, Albert, Mideon and Viscera in a handicap elimination match; WWF Intercontinental Champion Chyna overcame Chris Jericho; Too Cool, Hardcore Holly and Crash Holly defeated Edge, Christian, Matt Hardy and Jeff Hardy in a Survivor Series elimination match; WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn toppled Mankind and Al Snow; The Big Show rolled over WWF World Champion Triple H and The Rock in a Triple Threat match to win the title.

12th December 1999: “WWF Armageddon 1999”: Fort Lauderdale, FL – 15,749 sellout (13,981 paid)
($402,413 live gate)
($102,044 merchandise sales)
The Acolytes won an 8-team battle royal; Kurt Angle pinned Steve Blackman; Miss Kitty beat Jackie, BB and WWF Women’s Champion Ivory in a swimming pool evening gown match to win the title; Hardcore and Crash Holly dropped Viscera and Rikishi; Val Venis downed WWF European Champion Davey Boy Smith and D-Lo Brown in a Triple Threat match to win the title; Kane toppled X-Pac in a cage match; Chris Jericho rolled over WWF Intercontinental Champion Chyna to win the title; The Rock and Mankind downed WWF World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn by DQ; WWF World Champion The Big Show pinned WWF Hardcore Champion The Big Bossman; Triple H bested Vince McMahon in a “no-holds-barred” match.
