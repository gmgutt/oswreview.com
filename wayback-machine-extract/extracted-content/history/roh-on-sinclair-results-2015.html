ROH on Sinclair Results 2015

ROH ON SINCLAIR TV RESULTS 2015

Jan 2015

Air Date: 3rd January 2015
Best of 2014 Show. Featured no original matches.
Featured the AJ Styles vs. Roderick Strong match (taped 01/04/2014), Kevin Steen vs. Steve Corino match (taped 07/19/2014), and the Redragon vs. The Young Bucks match (taped 05/17/2014).

Air Date: 10th January 2015
Roderick Strong beat Mark Brisco; Will Ferrara defeated J Diesel (Top Prospect 2015 – 1st Round); Michael Elgin, Tommaso Ciampa and Hanson battled to a no-contest in a three-way.

Air Date: 17th January 2015
Frankie Kazarian overcame Cedric Alexander; Donovan Dijak overturned Jake Dirden (Top Prospect 2015 – 1st Round); ROH Champion Jay Briscoe downed Matt Taven.

Air Date: 24th January 2015
Mark and Jay Briscoe downed Michael Bennett and Matt Taven by DQ; Beer City Bruiser toppled Mikey Webb (Top Prospect 2015 – 1st Round); AAA World Champion Alberto El Patron overcame Christopher Daniels.

Air Date: 31st January 2015
Moose downed TheRomantic Touch; Jimmy Jacobs, Adam Page and Chris LaRusso bested Kris Chambers, Leon St Giovanni and Shaheem Ali; Ashley Sixx beat Dalton Castle(Top Prospect 2015 – 1st Round); ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal won a three-way over ACH and Matt Sydal.

 

Feb 2015

Air Date: 7th February 2015
Donovan Dijak overturned Ashley Sixx (Top Prospect 2015 – Semi-Final); Will Ferrara defeated Beer City Bruiser (Top Prospect 2015 – Semi-Final); Roderick Strong beat QT Marshall; Michael Bennett, Matt Taven and Maria Kanellis rolled over Mark Briscoe, ROH Champion Jay Briscoe and ODB.

Air Date: 14th February 2015
Tommaso Ciampa defeated Jimmy Jacobs; Hanson overcame The Romantic Touch; Michael Elgin battled to a no-contest with Moose; AJ Styles and Young Bucks rolled over ACH, Matt Sydal and Cedric Alexander.

Air Date: 21st February 2015
Featured the Alberto El Patron vs. ACH, Michael Elgin vs. Mark Briscoe, and Jay Lethal vs. Roderick Strong matches from ROH Winter Warriors card in Dearborn, MI on 01/30.

Air Date: 28th February 2015
ROH 13th Anniversary preview show. Featured no original matches.

Mar 2015

Air Date: 7th March 2015
ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal and J Diesel beat Christopher Daniels and Frankie Kazarian; Donovan Dijak overturned Will Ferrara (Top Prospect 2015 – Final); ROH Tag Champions & IWGP Jr Tag Champions Bobby Fish and Kyle O’Reilly beat BJ Whitmer and Adam Page.

Air Date: 14th March 2015
Christopher Daniels and Frankie Kazarian toppled ACH and Matt Sydal; Matt Taven and Michael Bennett beat IWGP Jr Tag Champions The Young Bucks in a non-title match.

Air Date: 21st March 2015
Roderick Strong beat Bobby Fish; ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal beat Dalton Castle; ROH Champion Jay Briscoe downed Jimmy Jacobs.

Air Date: 28th March 2015
Moose downed Caprice Coleman; BJ Whitmer and Adam Page downed Leon St. Giovanni and Shaheem Ali; Donovan Dijak and J Diesel beat Brutal Bob Evans and Cheeseburger; Samoa Joe downed Kyle O’Reilly.

Apr 2015

Air Date: 4th April 2015
Michael Elgin toppled Will Ferrara; Hanson and Rowe downed Antony Greene and Cam Zagami; Hanson and Rowe downed Michael Elgin by DQ in a handicap match; ACH beat QT Marshall; IWGP World Champion AJ Styles dropped Mark Briscoe.

Air Date: 11th April 2015
No Original Matches. Featured the Jushin Liger vs. Jay Lethal and Samoa Joe vs. Jay Briscoe matches from ROH Supercard of Honor IX on 3/27 in Redwood City, CA.

Air Date: 18th April 2015
Donovan Dijak toppled Dalton Castle; Hanson and Raymond Rowe downed Andy Dalton and Ken Phoenix; Michael Elgin overcame Cedric Alexander.

Air Date: 25th April 2015
BJ Whitmer won a four-way over Moose, Caprice Coleman and Will Ferrara; Adam Page downed ACH; Christopher Daniels and Frankie Kazarian overcame ROH Tag Champions Bobby Fish and Kyle O’Reilly to win the titles.

May 2015

Air Date: 2nd May 2015
GHC World Tag Champions and NWA Tag Champions Lance Archer and Davey Boy Smith Jr battled to a no-contest with Raymond Rowe and Hanson;  ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal beat Tommaso Ciampa.

Air Date: 9th May 2015
Chris Sabin overcame Kyle O’Reilly; AAA World Champion Alberto El Patron, ACH and Matt Sydal beat Mark  Briscoe, ROH Champion Jay Briscoe and PWG Champion Roderick Strong.

Air Date: 16th May 2015
Featured the Page vs. ACH & Briscoes vs. War Machine matches from 4/25 in Hopkins, MN

Air Date: 23rd May 2015
Featured the Elgin vs. Coleman, Lethal vs. O’Reilly, and Briscoe vs. O’Reilly matches from 4/25 in Hopkins, MN

Air Date: 30th May 2015
Kushida defeated Will Ferrara; Silas Young beat Takaaki Watanabe; Moose downed Colby Corino; Mark Briscoe & ROH Champion Jay Briscoe downed J Diesel & Donovan Dijak.

June 2015

Air Date: 6th June 2015
Kyle O’Reilly beat ROH Tag Champions Christopher Daniels & Frankie Kazarian by DQ; Jushin Liger overturned Dalton Castle; Brutal Bob Evans battled to a no-contest with Cheeseburger; IWGP World Tag Champions Michael Bennett & Matt Taven battled to a double-DQ with Karl Anderson & Doc Gallows.

Air Date: 13th June 2015
Raymond Rowe & Hanson toppled BJ Whitmer & Adam Page; Cedric Alexander overturned Moose; Hiroshi Tanahashi & Tetsuya Naito bested ACH & Matt Sydal.

Air Date: 20th June 2015
Shinsuke Nakamura defeated Roderick Strong; Michael Elgin toppled Gedo; IWGP World Champion AJ Styles & IWGP Jr Tag Champions The Young Bucks overcame Kazuchika Okada, Trent Baretta & Rocky Romero.

Air Date: 27th June 2015
Featured the Daniels/Kazarian vs. Okada/Gedo &  Page vs. Watanabe matches from War of the Worlds Night 2 on 5/13 in Philadelphia.
Featured the AJ Styles/Adam Cole match from War of the Worlds Night 1 on 5/12 in Philadelphia.

July 2015

Air Date: 4th July 2015
Dalton Castle downed Takaaki Watanabe; Michael Elgin, Bobby Fish & Kyle O’Reilly beat Adam Cole, and IWGP World Tag Champions Matt Taven & Michael Bennett.

Air Date: 11th July 2015
Matt Sydal beat Adam Page; Will Ferrara defeated Silas Young; ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal bested Mark Briscoe.

Air Date: 18th July 2015
ODB, Roderick Strong, Jay Briscoe & Mark Briscoe rolled over Donovan Dijak, J Diesel, ROH & ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal & Truth Martini.

Air Date: 25th July 2015
Matt Taven & Michael Bennett overturned Corey Hollis & Jonathan Gresham; Moose won a six-way over Bob Evans, Caprice Coleman, Cheeseburger, Dalton Castle and Silas Young; Bobby Fish beat ACH.

Aug 2015

Air Date: 1st August 2015
The Young Bucks toppled War Machine; Cedric Alexander overcame The Romantic Touch; Adam Cole battled Kyle O’Reilly to a no-contest; Adam Cole & Kyle O’Reilly rolled over ROH Tag Champions Christopher Daniels & Frankie Kazarian in a non-title match. 

Air Date: 8th August 2015
ACH beat Takaaki Watanabe; War Machine beat Donovan Dijak & J. Diesel; Adam Page overturned Tim Hughes; Matt Taven & Michael Bennett overcame Bobby Fish & Kyle O’Reilly.

Air Date: 15th August 2015
Dalton Castle downed Silas Young; Moose beat Will Ferrara; The Young Bucks rolled over Beretta & Rocky Romero. 

Air Date: 22nd August 2015
Jay Briscoe & Mark Briscoe toppled Grey Wolf & Red Scorpion; Roderick Strong toppled Donovan Dijak; ROH Tag Champions Christopher Daniels & Kazarian rolled over Adam Cole & Kyle O’Reilly.

Air Date: 29th August 2015
Jay Briscoe downed Adam Page by DQ; Caprice Coleman overcame Cedric Alexander; Cheeseburger overturned Bob Evans by count-out; ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal pinned Hanson.

Sep 2015

Air Date: 5th September 2015
ROH Tag Champions Christopher Daniels and Frankie Kazarian overcame The Young Bucks; ROH Champion Jay Lethal beat Roderick Strong.

Air Date: 12th September 2015
IWGP Jr Tag Champions Bobby Fish and Kyle O’Reilly dropped Donovan Dijak and J Diesel; Cedric Alexander overturned Dalton Castle; Hanson and Ray Rowe downed Matt Taven and Michael Bennett.

Air Date: 19th September 2015
Michael Elgin overcame Silas Young; ACH beat Caprice Coleman; Mark Briscoe, Jay Briscoe and IWGP Intercontinental Champion Hirooki Goto bested IWGP World Champion Kazuchika Okada, Rocky Romero and Trent Baretta.

Air Date: 26th September 2015
Matt Sydal beat IWGP Jr Champion Kushida in a non-title match; Takaaki Watanabe won four-way over Adam Page, Will Ferrara and Moose; Shinsuke Nakamura defeated Adam Cole.

Oct 2015

Air Date: 3rd October 2015
Featured the The Briscoes vs. Kushida/Shelly, and Okada vs. Strong matches from ROH Field of Honor 2015 on 8/22 in Brooklyn, NY.

Air Date: 10th October 2015
Matt Sydal beat ACH (Best of 5 Series – Match #4); Silas Young and Beer City Bruiser overturned The Tate Twins; ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal beat Takaaki Watanabe.

Air Date: 17th October 2015
Kenny King and Rhett Titus rolled over Christopher Daniels and Frankie Kazarian; Jay Briscoe downed Adam Page in a “no holds barred” match.

Air Date: 24th October 2015
Moose downed Donovan Dijak; Dalton Castle downed Cedric Alexander; IWGP Jr Tag Champions Bobby Fish and Kyle O’Reilly, and Michael Elgin overcame ROH Tag Champions Michael Bennett and Matt Taven, and Adam Cole.

Air Date: 31st October 2015
The Young Bucks battled to a no-contest with Silas Young and Beer City Bruiser; The Young Bucks rolled over The Tate Twins; Roderick Strong toppled Will Ferrara; Adam Cole won a three-way over AJ Styles and Kyle O’Reilly.

Nov 2015

Air Date: 7th November 2015
ACH beat Cedric Alexander; Caprice Coleman overcame Will Ferrara; Mark and Jay Briscoe downed Rhett Titus and Kenny King.

Air Date: 14th November 2015
Raymond Rowe and Hanson overcame Silas Young and Beer City Bruiser; Michael Elgin overcame Kevin Lee Davidson; ROH Tag Champions Michael Bennett and Matt Taven overcame Christopher Daniels and Frankie Kazarian.

Air Date: 21st November 2015
Moose downed Dominic Carter; Moose and Michael Elgin overcame J Diesel and Donovan Dijak; Roderick Strong toppled ROH Champion and ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal to win the ROH TV Title.

Air Date: 28th November 2015
Will Ferrara defeated Adam Page; Cheeseburger overturned Tim Hughes; Dalton Castle battled Adam Cole to a no-contest; ROH Tag Champions Michael Bennett and Matt Taven, and Adam Cole downed Dalton Castle, Raymond Rowe and Hanson.

Dec 2015

Air Date: 12th December 2015
Adam Cole downed Corey Hollis; Raymond Rowe and Hanson overcame Jon and Trey Williams; Dalton Castle downed Mike Posey; ACH and Matt Sydal beat Rocky Romero and Trent Baretta.

Air Date: 19th December 2015
Will Ferrara battled to a no-contest with Caprice Coleman; Kenny King and Rhett Titus overturned Donovan Dijak and Ken Phoenix; ROH TV Champion Roderick Strong, Cedric Alexander, Moose, Mark Briscoe and Matt Jackson overcame ROH Champion Jay Lethal, Cheeseburger, Jay Briscoe, Adam Page and Cliff Compton.
