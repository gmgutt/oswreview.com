Raw Results 2015

WWE RAW RESULTS (2015)

Jan 2015

Air Date: 5th January 2015
Bad News Barrett rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler to win the title in a “best-of-three-falls” match; The Ascension won a squash; Roman Reigns rolled over The Big Show by DQ; Natalya defeated WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella in a non-title match; Luke Harper toppled Erick Rowan. Joey Mercury and Jamie Noble were guest referees ; Bray Wyatt rolled over Dean Ambrose in an ambulance match; The Miz, Damien Mizdow and Alicia Fox beat WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso, and Naomi; Kane and Seth Rollins rolled over Ryback in a handicap match; Big E beat Adam Rose by DQ.

Air Date: 12th January 2015
John Cena defeated Seth Rollins in a lumberjack match; Kofi Kingston and Xavier Woods rolled over Tyson Kidd and Cesaro; Roman Reigns rolled over Luke Harper; Alicia Fox beat Naomi. Naomi wrestled with one hand tied behind her back; The Miz beat Jey Uso; Brie Bella defeated Paige; The Ascension overcame Bu Ku Dao and Socoro; WWE US Champion Rusev beat Dean Ambrose by stoppage.

Air Date: 19th January 2015
Bray Wyatt rolled over Daniel Bryan; Dean Ambrose downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Bad News Barrett in a non-title match; Kofi Kingston and Big E beat Tyson Kidd and Cesaro; Paige and Natalya defeated Summer Rae and Alicia Fox; WWE US Champion Rusev beat R-Truth; Jey Uso bested The Miz; John Cena defeated The Big Show, Kane and Seth Rollins in a handicap match.

Air Date: 26th January 2015
The scheduled RAW taping on 1/26 in Hartford, CT was cancelled due to a travel ban on all roads from 9pm onwards due to a blizzard. A Studio show with Royal Rumble footage aired instead.

Feb 2015

Air Date: 2nd February 2015
The Big Show beat Roman Reigns; The Ascension overcame Goldust and Stardust; Ryback beat Luke Harper; Cesaro bested Jimmy Uso; Bray Wyatt beat Dolph Ziggler; Paige downed Alicia Fox; Sin Cara defeated The Miz; Daniel Bryan overcame Seth Rollins.

Air Date: 9th February 2015
Daniel Bryan and Roman Reigns rolled over The Big Show and Kane by DQ; Ryback beat Seth Rollins by DQ; Paige downed Brie Bella; Bray Wyatt beat Dolph Ziggler; Kofi Kingston and Big E beat Goldust and Stardust; Tyson Kidd and Cesaro bested WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso in a non-title match; Sin Cara defeated Damien Mizdow; Dean Ambrose overturned Curtis Axel; Daniel Bryan and Roman Reigns rolled over The Big Show, Kane, Seth Rollins, Jamie Noble and Joey Mercury in a handicap match.

Air Date: 16th February 2015
Dean Ambrose downed Luke Harper; Xavier Woods and Kofi Kingston rolled over Goldust and Stardust; Roman Reigns overcame Kane by count-out; Paige downed Summer Rae; Dolph Ziggler toppled Seth Rollins by DQ; WWE Intercontinental Champion Bad News Barrett overturned Damien Mizdow; Jimmy Uso and Naomi dropped Tyson Kidd and Natalya; The Big Show beat Daniel Bryan by DQ.

Air Date: 23rd February 2015
Dolph Ziggler overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Bad News Barrett in a non-title match; Titus O’Neil and Darren Young beat The Ascension; WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso dropped Tyson Kidd and Cesaro by DQ; Jack Swagger overturned Stardust; The Bella Twins dropped Paige and Emma; Ryback beat Curtis Axel; Roman Reigns and Daniel Bryan toppled Seth Rollins and Randy Orton.

Mar 2015

Air Date: 2nd March 2015
Dean Ambrose downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Bad News Barrett in a non-title match; Jimmy Uso, Jey Uso, and Naomi dropped WWE World Tag Champions Tyson Kidd and Cesaro, and Natalya; John Cena defeated Curtis Axel; Paige downed WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella by DQ; Daniel Bryan toppled Luke Harper; Seth Rollins dropped Roman Reigns.

Air Date: 9th March 2015
Daniel Bryan toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Bad News Barrett in a non-title match; Dean Ambrose downed Stardust; Ryback and Erick Rowan toppled Kane and The Big Show; AJ Lee downed Summer Rae; WWE US Champion Rusev beat Curtis Axel; Big E and Xavier Woods dropped WWE World Tag Champions Tyson Kidd and Cesaro in a non-title match; Naomi dropped Natalya; Los Matadores dropped Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso; Roman Reigns dropped Randy Orton and Seth Rollins in a handicap match.

Air Date: 16th March 2015
WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella defeated AJ Lee; Ryback beat The Miz; WWE World Tag Champions Cesaro and Tyson Kidd beat Big E and Kofi Kingston; Daniel Bryan, Dolph Ziggler and Dean Ambrose downed Luke Harper, WWE Intercontinental Champion Bad News Barrett and Stardust.

Air Date: 23rd March 2015
Dean Ambrose and R-Truth beat Stardust and Luke Harper; Ryback, Titus O’Neil, Darren Young, Zack Ryder and Erick Rowan overturned The Miz, Damien Mizdow, Adam Rose and The Ascension; Randy Orton overturned Seth Rollins, Jamie Noble and Joey Mercury in a handicap match; WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella defeated Paige; Los Matadores and Torito bested WWE World Tag Champions Tyson Kidd and Cesaro, and Natalya; WWE US Champion Rusev beat Jack Swagger; Dolph Ziggler toppled Daniel Bryan. Dean Ambrose was guest referee.

Air Date: 30th March 2015
WWE Intercontinental Champion Daniel Bryan defeated Dolph Ziggler; Kalisto, Sin Cara, Kofi Kingston and Big E overcame WWE World Tag Champions Cesaro and Tyson Kidd, and The Ascension; Damien Mizdow overturned Stardust; Adrian Neville pinned Curtis Axel; WWE US champion John Cena downed Dean Ambrose; Paige, AJ Lee and Naomi toppled The Bella Twins and Natalya; Rusev bested Goldust; Roman Reigns, Randy Orton and Ryback rolled over WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins, Kane and The Big Show.

Apr 2015

Air Date: 6th April 2015
Randy Orton overcame Kane by DQ; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins rolled over Neville; WWE US Champion John Cena defeated Stardust; Naomi and Paige downed The Bella Twins; Ryback toppled Luke Harper; Sin Cara and Kalisto bested Xavier Woods and Big E; Roman Reigns rolled over The Big Show; Sheamus rolled over Mark Henry; The Miz beat Damien Mizdow; Randy Orton won a three-way over Ryback and Roman Reigns.

Air Date: 13th April 2015
WWE US Champion John Cena defeated Bad News Barrett; Paige won a battle royal; Sin Cara and Kalisto bested The Ascension; Randy Orton overcame Cesaro by DQ; Randy Orton overcame Cesaro and Tyson Kidd in a handicap match; Dean Ambrose downed Adam Rose; Stardust overturned Fandango; Seth Rollins overturned Kane; Damien Mizdow beat The Miz; Ryback beat Luke Harper by DQ; Dolph Ziggler toppled Neville.

Air Date: 20th April 2015
Dean Ambrose battled to a double count-out with Luke Harper; Kofi Kingston and Big E downed Lucha Dragons by count-out; Fandango bested Curtis Axel; Naomi dropped Brie Bella; Sheamus beat Zack Ryder by DQ; WWE US Champion John Cena defeated Kane; The Miz beat Damien Mizdow; Ryback beat Adam Rose; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins beat Dolph Ziggler.

Air Date: 27th April 2015
Bad News Barrett beat Dolph Ziggler (King of the Ring – Quarter-Final); Big E downed Tyson Kidd; Ryback beat Bo Dallas; R-Truth beat Stardust (King of the Ring – Quarter-Final); Adam Rose downed Fandango; Naomi dropped Brie Bella; Sheamus overcame Dean Ambrose by DQ (King of the Ring – Quarter-Final); Neville downed Luke Harper (King of the Ring – Quarter-Final); Randy Orton and Roman Reigns overcame WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins and Kane.

May 2015

Air Date: 4th May 2015
Big E, Kofi Kingston and Xavier Woods overcame Randy Orton and Roman Reigns in a handicap match; Tyson Kidd and Cesaro bested The Ascension; Dean Ambrose downed WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins in a non-title match; Rusev beat Fandango; Stardust beat R-Truth; WWE US Champion John Cena defeated Sami Zayn; Bad News Barrett and Sheamus overcame Neville and Dolph Ziggler; Roman Reigns battled to a no-contest with Randy Orton.

Air Date: 11th May 2015
Dean Ambrose downed Joey Mercury and Jamie Noble in a handicap match; King Barrett toppled Dolph Ziggler; Erick Rowan defeated Fandango; WWE US Champion John Cena battled to a no-contest with Neville; Tamina Snuka bested Brie Bella; Curtis Axel battled to a no-contest with Macho Mandow (Damien Sandow); Cesaro overcame Big E; Randy Orton defeated WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins by DQ.

Air Date: 18th May 2015
Sheamus overcame Ryback; King Barrett beat Neville; Bray Wyatt beat Dean Ambrose; Tyson Kidd & Cesaro bested WWE World Tag Champions Big E & Kofi Kingston by DQ; Dolph Ziggler overturned Stardust; Luke Harper & Erick Rowan toppled Fandango & Zack Ryder; WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella defeated Naomi by DQ.

Air Date: 25th May 2015
Dean Ambrose & Roman Reigns overcame WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins & Kane; Rusev beat R-Truth; Ryback beat King Barrett; Neville downed Stardust; Sheamus overcame Dolph Ziggler; WWE US Champion John Cena defeated Zack Ryder; Tamina Snuka defeated Paige; Big E, Xavier Woods & Kofi Kingston toppled Tyson Kidd, Cesaro, Los Matadores, The Ascension, Titus O’Neil, Darren Young, Sin Cara & Kalisto by DQ in a handicap match.

Jun 2015

Air Date: 1st June 2015
Roman Reigns overturned King Barrett; Dolph Ziggler overcame Kofi Kingston; Dolph Ziggler, Titus O’Neil & Darren Young rolled over Big E,  Kofi Kingston & Xavier Woods; Roman Reigns overturned Mark Henry by count-out; WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella defeated Paige; Randy Orton dropped Sheamus by DQ; Neville downed Bo Dallas; Roman Reigns overturned Bray Wyatt.

Air Date: 8th June 2015
WWE NXT Champion Kevin Owens overturned Neville; WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella defeated Summer Rae; Sheamus overturned Randy Orton by DQ; Kane downed Dolph Ziggler; Luke Harper & Erick Rowan dropped Los Matadores; Big E beat Titus O’Neill; Roman Reigns overturned Kofi Kingston; Joey Mercury & Jamie Noble downed WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins in a non-title handicap match.

Air Date: 15th June 2015
Dean Ambrose downed Sheamus; R-Truth beat King Barrett; WWE NXT Champion Kevin Owens rolled over Dolph Ziggler; Kane downed Randy Orton in a no-DQ match; The Miz beat Big Show by count-out; The Bella Twins rolled over Paige in a handicap match; Neville & WWE World Tag Champions Titus O’Neil & Darren Young beat Kofi Kingston, Big E & Xavier Woods.

Air Date: 22nd June 2015
Kane downed Dean Ambrose; WWE World Tag Champions Darren Young & Titus O’Neill dropped The Ascension; Sheamus bested Roman Reigns by count-out; Neville toppled Kofi Kingston; King Barrett overcame Zack Ryder; The Bella Twins defeated Naomi & Tamina Snuka; WWE Intercontinental Champion Ryback overcame Mark Henry; Dolph Ziggler pinned Adam Rose.

Air Date: 29th June 2015
The Big Show beat Mark Henry; WWE Intercontinental Champion Ryback rolled over The Miz by count-out; Paige downed Alicia Fox; Cesaro bested WWE US Champion John Cena by DQ; Sin Cara, Kalisto, and WWE World Tag Champions Titus O’Neil & Darren Young beat Kofi Kingston, Big E, Xavier Woods & Bo Dallas; Sheamus rolled over Neville; King Barrett toppled Jack Swagger; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins & Kane downed Dean Ambrose & Roman Reigns in a no-DQ match.

Jul 2015

Air Date: 6th July 2015
WWE Intercontinental Champion Ryback battled to a no-contest with The Big Show; Brie Bella defeated Paige; Sheamus rolled over Roman Reigns by count-out; Dean Ambrose downed Bo Dallas; King Barrett toppled R-Truth; Big E & Kofi Kingston overcame Sin Cara & Kalisto; WWE US Champion John Cena defeated Cesaro.

Air Date: 13th July 2015
Randy Orton & Ryback beat Sheamus & The Big Show; Mark Henry & WWE World Tag Champions Darren Young & Titus O’Neil overcame Big E, Kofi Kingston & Xavier Woods; R-Truth beat King Barrett; Rusev won a three-way over Cesaro and Kevin Owens; Rusev bested WWE US Champion John Cena by DQ; Stardust pinned Neville.

Air Date: 20th July 2015

The Big Show defeated Dean Ambrose by count-out; Neville downed Fandango; WWE NXT Women’s Champion Sasha Banks dropped Paige; Kalisto & Sin Cara bested Los Matadores; Becky Lynch & Charlotte toppled Alicia Fox & WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella; Randy Orton overcame Kevin Owens by DQ; WWE US Champion John Cena rolled over WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins. Only the US Title was on the line.

Aug 2015

WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins overcame Neville; The Ascension, Big E & Kofi Kingston beat Los Matadores, Kalisto & Sin Cara; Becky Lynch & Charlotte overturned The Bella Twins; Rusev dropped Mark Henry; King Barrett downed Zack Ryder; Paige defeated Naomi; Dean Ambrose, Randy Orton & Roman Reigns overcame Bray Wyatt, Luke Harper & Sheamus.

Air Date: 10th August 2015

Air Date: 17th August 2015

Air Date: 24th August 2015

Air Date: 31st August 2015

Air Date: 14th September 2015

Air Date: 21st September 2015
Sin Cara, Kalisto and Neville downed Stardust and The Ascension; Ryback downed Bo Dallas; WWE Divas Champion Charlotte downed Brie Bella; Sheamus rolled over Mark Henry; Rusev, Big E and Kofi Kingston overcame Dolph Ziggler and The Dudley Boys; Naomi dropped Natalya; The Big Show beat Cesaro; WWE US Champion John Cena defeated WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins.

Air Date: 28th September 2015
WWE US Champion John Cena defeated Xavier Woods by DQ; Big E, Kofi Kingston and Xavier Woods rolled over WWE US Champion John Cena and The Dudley Boys; The Big Show beat Mark Henry; The Bella Twins and Alicia Fox beat WWE Divas Champion Charlotte, Becky Lynch and Paige; Luke Harper and Braun Stowman overcame Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; Neville downed Stardust by DQ; Randy Orton overcame Bo Dallas; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens battled to a no-contest with Rusev; Roman Reigns battled to a double count-out with Bray Wyatt.

Air Date: 9th November 2015
Roman Reign toppled The Big Show (WWE World Heavyweight Title Tournament – 1st Round); WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens overcame Titus O’Neil (WWE World Heavyweight Title Tournament – 1st Round); Becky Lynch overturned Paige; Dolph Ziggler rolled over The Miz (WWE World Heavyweight Title Tournament – 1st Round); Natalya defeated Naomi; Cesaro bested Sheamus (WWE World Heavyweight Title Tournament – 1st Round); Dean Ambrose downed Tyler Breeze (WWE World Heavyweight Title Tournament – 1st Round); Big E, Kofi Kingston and Xavier Woods overcame Neville, Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso.

Air Date: 16th November 2015

Air Date: 30th November 2015
Dolph Ziggler rolled over Tyler Breeze; Ryback bested Rusev by count-out; Luke Harper, Braun Strowman and Erick Rowan battled Bubba Ray Dudley, D-Von Dudley and Tommy Dreamer to a no-contest; WWE US Champion Alberto del Río toppled Goldust; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso battled Kalisto and Sin Cara to a double-DQ; Sasha Banks rolled over Brie Bella; Roman Reigns rolled over WWE World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus by DQ; WWE Divas Champion Charlotte dropped Becky Lynch;  WWE World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus, Xavier Woods, Big E, Kofi Kingston, King Barrett, Rusev and WWE US Champion Alberto del Río toppled Roman Reigns, Dean Ambrose, Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso in a handicap match.

Dec 2015

Air Date: 7th December 2015
Roman Reigns, Jimmy Uso, Jey Uso and Dean Ambrose downed WWE World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus, King Barrett, Alberto del Río and Rusev, Bray Wyatt, Luke Harper, Erick Rowan and Braun Strowman, and Bubba Ray Dudley, D-Von Dudley, Tommy Dreamer and Rhyno in a four-way elimination match; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens rolled over Dolph Ziggler; Sasha Banks and Naomi bested Brie Bella and Alicia Fox; Kalisto and Sin Cara dropped Big E and Kofi Kingston; Rusev battled Ryback to a double count-out; Jack Swagger beat Stardust; Braun Strowman overcame Tommy Dreamer.

Air Date: 21st December 2015

Air Date: 28th December 2015
Neville downed Kevin Owens; Sasha Banks overcame Becky Lynch; Kalisto bested Kofi Kingston; Big E defeated Sin Cara; Ryback defeated The Big Show by count-out; Sheamus, King Barrett and Rusev defeated WWE Intercontinental Champion Dean Ambrose, Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso; John Cena downed WWE US Champion Alberto del Río by DQ.
