WWF PPV Results 1989

WWF PPV RESULTS (1989)

See also: WWF PPV Statistics 1985-1989

15th January 1989: “WWF Royal Rumble 1989”: Houston, TX – 19,000 sellout
Dark matches: Jim Powers beat Barry Horowitz; Sam Houston defeated Steve Lombardi.
PPV Broadcast: Bret Hart, Jim Duggan, and Jim Neidhart downed Dino Bravo and The Rougeau Brothers; WWF Women’s Champion Rockin Robin bested Judy Martin;  Haku toppled Harley Race; Big John Studd won the 30-man Royal Rumble by last eliminating Ted Dibiase.

2nd April 1989: “WWF Wrestlemania V”: Atlantic City, NJ – 18,946 sellout (17,500 paid)
($1,628,000 live gate)
Closed Circuit: 175,000 ($2,300,000)
PPV Broadcast: Hercules overturned King Haku; The Big Bossman and Akeem defeated Shawn Michaels and Marty Jannetty; Brutus Beefcake battled Ted Dibiase to a double count-out; The Bushwhackers overturned Jacques and Raymond Rougeau; Curt Hennig pinned The Blue Blazer (Owen Hart); WWF World Tag Champions Demolition rolled over The Powers of Pain and Mr Fuji in a handicap match; Dino Bravo downed Ron Garvin; Arn Anderson and Tully Blanchard defeated Tito Santana and Rick Martel; Jake Roberts overturned Andre The Giant by DQ; Bret Hart and Jim Neidhart dropped The Honkytonk Man and Greg Valentine; Rick Rude bested WWF Intercontinental Champion The Ultimate Warrior to win the title; Jim Duggan battled Bad News Brown to a double-DQ; The Red Rooster toppled Bobby Heenan; Hulk Hogan rolled over WWF World Champion Randy Savage to win the title.

28th August 1989: “WWF Summerslam 1989”: East Rutherford, NJ – 20,000 sellout
($350,000 live gate)
Dark match: Dino Bravo downed Koko B Ware.
PPV Broadcast: WWF World Tag Champions Arn Anderson and Tully Blanchard defeated Bret Hart and Jim Neidhart in a non-title match; Dusty Rhodes overturned The Honkytonk Man; Curt Hennig pinned The Red Rooster; Rick Martel, Jacques Rougeau, and Raymond Rougeau downed Tito Santana, Shawn Michaels and Marty Jannetty;  The Ultimate Warrior toppled WWF Intercontinental Champion Rick Rude to win the title; Jim Duggan and Demolition rolled over Andre The Giant, The Big Bossman, and Akeem; Hercules bested Greg Valentine by DQ; Ted Dibiase bested Jimmy Snuka by count-out; WWF World Champion Hulk Hogan and Brutus Beefcake bested Randy Savage and Zeus.

23rd November 1989: “WWF Survivor Series 1989”: Chicago, IL – 15,294
($239,917 live gate)
Dark match: Boris Zhukov pinned Paul Roma.
PPV Broadcast: Dusty Rhodes, Brutus Beefcake, Tito Santana, and The Red Rooster toppled The Big Bossman, The Honkytonk Man, Bad News Brown, and Rick Martel in a “Survivor Series” elimination match; Randy Savage, Dino Bravo, Greg Valentine, and Earthquake defeated Jim Duggan, Bret Hart, Ron Garvin, and Hercules in a “Survivor Series” elimination match; WWF World Champion Hulk Hogan, Jake Roberts, and WWF World Tag Champions Demolition rolled over Ted Dibiase, Zeus, and The Powers of Pain in a “Survivor Series” elimination match; Rick Rude, Curt Hennig, Jacques Rougeau,  and Raymond Rougeau bested Roddy Piper, Jimmy Snuka, and The Bushwhackers in a “Survivor Series” elimination match; WWF Intercontinental Champion The Ultimate Warrior, Jim Neidhart, and Shawn Michaels and Marty Jannetty overcame Andre The Giant, Haku, Arn Anderson, and Bobby Heenan in a “Survivor Series” elimination match.

27th December 1989: “WWF No Holds Barred: The Match/The Movie”
Broadcast of No Holds Barred movie
(Taped on 12/12/1989 in Nashville, TN): WWF World Champion Hulk Hogan and Brutus Beefcake rolled over Randy Savage and Zeus in a cage match.
