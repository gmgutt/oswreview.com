NWA US TAG TITLE (North East Version) 
07/1958 Mark Lewin and Don Curtis 65 Kansas City, MO
Defeated Hans Schmidt and Dick the Bruiserin a tournament final to become the first champions.
09/04/1958 Jerry Graham and Eddie Graham 98 Washington, D.C.
12/11/1958 Mark Lewin and Don Curtis (2) 167 Washington, D.C.
05/27/1959 Jerry Graham and Eddie Graham (2) 66 Bridgeport, CT
08/1959 VACANT
The titles are declared vacant when Eddie Graham leaves the promotion
11/14/1959 Jerry Graham and Johnny Valentine 108 West Hempstead, NY
Defeated Mark Lewin and Don Curtis to win the vacant titles.
03/1960 Jerry Graham and Eddie Graham (*) 32 New Haven, CT
Eddie Graham returns to the promotion and replaces Johnny Valentine.
04/02/1960 Red and Lou Bastien 14 New Haven, CT
04/16/1960 Jerry Graham and Eddie Graham (3) 7 New Haven, CT
04/23/1960 Red and Lou Bastien (2) 89 Chicago, IL

11/28/1960 Al Costello and Roy Heffernan (3) 409 Washington, D.C.
Defeated Johnny Valentine and Chief Big Heart (a permitted substitute for Buddy Rogers to win the titles.)

03/07/1963 Buddy Austin and Great Scott 70 Washington, D.C.

Parisi leaves the WWWF and Skaaland is selected as Arion’s new partner.
