GAMEPLAY NOTES

 

First WWE Licensed Video Game for the NES. Limited Move Set (punch, kick, headbutt, a back attack, a running attack). All wrestlers can perform body slams (except Bam Bam Bigelow), but only Hulk Hogan can slam Andre. All wrestlers, except Bam Bam Bigelow and Andre can perform a top turnbuckle move off the bottom two turnbuckles. Bam Bam Bigelow can perform two running attacks. Wrestlers cannot leave the ring. Icons, unique to each wrestler, randomly appear in the ring. These icons can be collected to gain health. A Wrestler can turn “red” when on the defensive, indicating anger. When a wrestler is in an angered state, he can inflict greater the usual damage on his opponent.

Players can either play a single exhibition match (one player versus the computer or two players head-to-head) or a tournament. In a single player tournament, the player chooses one wrestler and must defeat the other five in a series of matches to win the championship. In a tournament with two or more player-controlled wrestlers (up to six can play), each wrestler faces every other wrestler once (fifteen total matches). At the end of the tournament, the wrestler with the best record wins the championship. In the event of a tie, the wrestler in the tie who achieved the single fastest victory during the tournament will be declared the winner.
