ROH Results 2007

Jan 2007

26th January 2007: “ROH Dedicated”: Braintree, MA – 600
Rocky Romero beat Davey Richards; Jack Evans defeated Eddie Edwards; Delirious and Pelle Primeau downed Adam Pearce and Shane Hagadorn; Brent Albright, Jimmy Jacobs and Lacey overcame BJ Whitmer, Colt Cabana and Daizee Haze; Samoa Joe dropped Nigel McGuinness; Jimmy Rave overcame Christopher Daniels, Jason Blade and Shingo Takagi in a four-way; ROH Champion Homicide rolled over Chris Hero; Jay and Mark Briscoe toppled Austin Aries and Roderick Strong in a best-of-three falls match.

27th January 2007: “ROH Battle of the Icons”: Edison, NJ – 500
Shane Hagadorn beat Michael Elgin; Roderick Strong defeated Alex Payne and Mitch Franklin in a three-way; Jimmy Rave downed Nigel McGuinness in an I Quit match; Adam Pearce dropped Pelle Primeau; Chris Hero bested Christopher Daniels and Claudio Castagnoli in a three-way; Jimmy Jacobs toppled BJ Whitmer in a Last Man Standing match; Jay and Mark Briscoe overcame Ricky Reyes and Rocky Romero; Colt Cabana and Sara Del Ray overturned Brent Albright and Lacey; Austin Aries, Roderick Strong and Jack Evans defeated Davey Richards, Delirious and Shingo Takagi; ROH Champion Homicide rolled over Samoa Joe.

Feb 2007

16th February 2007: “ROH The Fifth Year Festival: New York”: Manhattan, NY – 1,200
Takeshi Morishima beat Pelle Primeau; Delirious downed Adam Pearce; Allison Danger and Sara Del Rey defeated Alexa Thatcher and Daizee Haze; Jack Evans dropped Jimmy Jacobs, Shingo Takagi and Xavier in a four-way elimination match; BJ Whitmer overcame Brent Albright in a “Tables are Legal” match; ROH Tag Champions Christopher Daniels and Matt Sydal overturned Austin Aries and Roderick Strong; Nigel McGuinness and Colt Cabana bested Jay and Mark Briscoe; Samoa Joe toppled Takeshi Morishima; ROH Champion Homicide rolled over Jimmy Rave.

17th February 2007: “ROH The Fifth Year Festival: Philadelphia”: Philadelphia, PA – 800
Pelle Primeau and Rhett Titus beat Alex Payne and Bobby Dempsey; Shane Hagadorn defeated Alex Payne; Sara Del Rey downed Mitch Franklin; Nigel McGuinness dropped TJ Perkins; Austin Aries overcame Claudio Castagnoli; Colt Cabana, BJ Whitmer and Daizee Haze overcame Adam Pearce, Jimmy Jacobs and Lacey; Jay and Mark Briscoe toppled El Generico and Kevin Steen; Samoa Joe bested Jimmy Rave; FIP Champion Roderick Strong beat Delirious by count-out; ROH Tag Champions Christopher Daniels and Matt Sydal overturned Jack Evans and Shingo Takagi; Takeshi Morishima rolled over ROH Champion Homicide to win the title.

23rd February 2007: “ROH The Fifth Year Festival: Dayton”: Dayton, OH – 700
Colt Cabana and Nigel McGuinness beat Jimmy Jacobs and Brent Albright; Jimmy Rave defeated Sterling Keenan;  Lacey downed Daizee Haze; Homicide overcame Christopher Daniels and Jay Briscoe in a three-way; OTBG Champion Matt Sydal pinned Austin Aries; Tank Toland overcame Mitch Franklin; Claudio Castagnoli overturned Mark Briscoe, Matt Cross, Pelle Primeau, Roderick Strong and Shingo Takagi in a six-way; Samoa Joe bested Davey Richards; ROH Champion Takeshi Morishima rolled over BJ Whitmer.

24th February 2007: “ROH The Fifth Year Festival: Chicago”: Chicago, IL
Brent Albright beat Claudio Castagnoli; Sara Del Rey and Allison Danger defeated MsChif and Serena Deeb; Matt Cross overcame Shingo Takagi, Trik Davis and CJ Otis in a four-way; Jimmy Rave dropped BJ Whitmer; Jay and Mark Briscoe captured the ROH Tag Titles from Christopher Daniels and Matt Sydal; Austin Aries and Matt Cross dropped Davey Richards and Roderick Strong; Colt Cabana bested Jimmy Jacobs in a Death match; ROH Champion Takeshi Morishima and Nigel McGuinness rolled over Homicide and Samoa Joe.

Mar 2007

3rd March 2007: “ROH Fifth Year Festival – Liverpool”: Liverpool, England – 1,000
Homicide overturned Davey Richards; Sara Del Ray beat Allison Danger; Jimmy Rave and Jimmy Jacobs overcame BJ Whitmer and Colt Cabana; Matt Sydal beat Delirious in a “best-of-three-falls” encounter; FIP Champion Roderick Strong dropped Pac; Naruki Doi and Shingo bested ROH Tag Champions Jay and Mark Briscoe to win the titles; Samoa Joe rolled over Nigel McGuinness.

4th March 2007: “ROH Fifth Year Festival Finale – Liverpool”: Liverpool, England
Delirious downed Colt Cabana; Sara Del Ray and Allison Danger beat Jetta and Eden Black; BJ Whitmer bested Jimmy Jacobs in a “falls-count-anywhere” match; Matt Sydal dropped Pac; Jay Briscoe battled Mark Briscoe to a double knockout; ROH Tag Champions Naruki Doi and Shingo defeated Davey Richards and Roderick Strong; Nigel McGuinness downed Jimmy Rave in a “fight without honor”; Samoa Joe rolled over Homicide. 

30th March 2007: “ROH All Star Extravaganza III”: Detroit, MI – 1,000
Adam Pearce won a four-way over Colt Cabana, Matt Sydal, and Chris Hero; Erick Stevens overcame Alex Payne; Masaaki Mochizuki beat Davey Richards; Jimmy Jacobs and Lacey defeated BJ Whitmer and Daizee Haze in an “anything goes” match; Jay and Mark Briscoe toppled ROH Tag Champions Shingo and Naruki Doi to win the titles; Yamato bested Pelle Primeau; Brent Albright downed Nigel McGuinness; Homicide defeated Christopher Daniels; Roderick Strong overturned Jack Evans; CIMA, Susumu Yokosuka, Dragon Kid and Ryo Saito rolled over Austin Aries, Delirious, Claudio Castagnoli and Rocky Romero.

31st March 2007: “ROH Supercard of Honor II”: Detroit, MI
Jay Briscoe and Delirious downed Christopher Daniels and Matt Sydal; Claudio Castagnoli defeated Yamato; Erick Stevens beat Mitch Franklin; Jack Evans and Naruki Doi dropped Rocky Romero and Davey Richards; Homicide and Colt Cabana bested Brent Albright and Adam Pearce; Nigel McGuinness downed Chris Hero; Jimmy Jacobs overcame BJ Whitmer in a cage match; ROH Champion Roderick Strong toppled Austin Aries; CIMA, Susumu Yokosuka and Shingo Takagi defeated Dragon Kid, Ryo Saito and Masaaki Mochizuki.

Apr 2007

13th April 2007: “ROH This Means War II”: Lake Grove, NY
Bobby Dempsey and Rhett Titus beat Alex Payne and Ernie Osiris; Pepper Parks overturned Mitch Franklin; Kevin Steen defeated Pelle Primeau; Daizee Haze downed Sara Del Rey; Erick Stevens won a four-way over Jason Blade, Eddie Edwards and El Generico; Delirious dropped Colt Cabana; Claudio Castagnoli bested Jay Briscoe ; Brent Albright dropped Homicide ; Jack Evans defeated Shingo Takagi; Roderick Strong, Davey Richards and Rocky Romero overturned Austin Aries, Matt Cross and Erick Stevens;  Nigel McGuinness and Doug Williams rolled over ROH Champion Takeshi Morishima and Chris Hero.

14th April 2007: “ROH Fighting Spirit”: Edison, NJ – 700
Jason Blade and Eddie Edwards defeated Chris Hero and Tank Toland; Pelle Primeau downed Shane Hagadorn to win the “Top of the Class” Trophy; Rocky Romero dropped Claudio Castagnoli; Kevin Steen and El Generico bested ROH World Tag Team Champions Jay and Mark Briscoe in a non-title match; Doug Williams beat Colt Cabana; Sara Del Rey overcame Ernie Osiris; Shingo Takagi overturned Matt Cross; Roderick Strong and Davey Richards toppled Jack Evans and Delirious; ROH Champion Takeshi Morishima rolled over Nigel McGuinness.

27th April 2007:“ROH The Battle of St Paul”: St Paul, MN
Alex Payne, Bobby Dempsey, and Ernie Osiris beat Ash, Billy Roc, and Dingo; Lacey and Rain defeated Sara Del Rey and Allison Danger; Michael Elgin battled Rhett Titus to a no-contest; Shingo Takagi downed BJ Whitmer; ROH Tag Champions Jay and Mark Briscoe won a “Ultimate Endurance” match over Pelle Primeau and Mitch Franklin, Hallowicked and Gran Akuma, and Mike Quackenbush and Jigsaw; Brent Albright and Adam Pearce bested Colt Cabana and Homicide in an “anything goes” match; Rocky Romero won a four-way over Jack Evans, Delirious, and Erick Stevens; FIP Champion Roderick Strong downed Christopher Daniels by count-out; ROH Champion Takeshi Morishima rolled over Austin Aries.

28th April 2007: “ROH Good Times, Great Memories”: Chicago Ridge, IL
Delirious won a six-way over Gran Akuma, Mike Quackenbush, Pelle Primeau, Hallowicked and Jigsaw; Erick Stevens battled Christopher Daniels to a draw; Brent Albright won a four-way over BJ Whitmer, Homicide and Jimmy Rave; Austin Aries defeated Rocky Romero; ROH Champion Takeshi Morishima rolled over Shingo Takagi; Tank Toland defeated Alex Payne; Roderick Strong downed Jack Evans; ROH Tag Champions Jay Briscoe and Mark Briscoe overcame Alex Shelley and Chris Sabin; Colt Cabana rolled over Adam Pearce.
