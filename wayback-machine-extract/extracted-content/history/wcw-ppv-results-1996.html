WCW PPV Results 1996

WCW PPV RESULTS 1996

11th February 1996: “WCW Superbrawl VI”: St Petersburg, FL – 7,200 sellout (6,000 paid)
($90,000 live gate)
Main Event: The Road Warriors beat Bunkhouse Buck and Dick Slater; Hugh Morrus defeated Konnan; Big Bubba Rogers and VK Wallstreet downed Craig Pittman and Joey Maggs; Jim Duggan beat Lochness by DQ.
PPV Broadcast: The Nasty Boys beat Public Enemy in a “falls-count-anywhere” match; WCW TV Champion Johnny B Badd defeated Diamond Dallas Page; WCW World Tag Champions Sting and Lex Luger downed Harlem Heat; WCW US Champion Konnan dropped The One Man Gang; Kevin Sullivan beat Brian Pillman in a strap match; Arn Anderson battled Kevin Sullivan to a no-contest; WCW World Tag Champions Sting and Lex Luger battled The Road Warriors to a double-DQ; Ric Flair bested WCW World Champion Randy Savage to win the title in a cage match; Hulk Hogan rolled over The Giant in a cage match.

24th March 1996: “WCW Uncensored 1996”: Tupelo, MS – 9,000 (7,300 paid)
($104,000 live gate)
Main Event: (03/24): Mr JL beat Dean Malenko (WCW Cruiserweight Title Tournament – 1st Round); Jim Duggan defeated Big Bubba Rogers; Dick Slater downed Alex Wright; The Steiners battled The Nasty Boys to a no-contest.
PPV Broadcast: WCW US Champion Konnan defeated Eddie Guerrero; Finlay downed Lord Steven Regal by DQ; Col Rob Parker pinned Madusa; The Booty Man dropped Diamond Dallas Page; The Giant bested Loch Ness (Luke McMasters); Booker T and Sting bested The Road Warriors in a streetfight; Hulk Hogan and Randy Savage rolled over WCW World Champion Ric Flair, Arn Anderson, Meng, The Barbarian, Kevin Sullivan, WCW TV Champion Lex Luger, Z Gangsta (Tom Lister), and The Ultimate Solution (Jeep Swenson)in a handicap “Doomsday” triple tier cage match.

 

19th May 1996: “WCW Slamboree 1996”: Baton Rouge, LA – 7,791 (6,308 paid)
($104,760 live gate)
Main Event: (05/19): Marcus Bagwell and Scotty Riggs bested Shark and Maxx.
PPV Broadcast: Road Warrior Animal and Booker T battled Road Warrior Hawk and WCW TV Champion Lex Luger to a double count-out (Lethal Lottery – First Round); Public Enemy defeated Chris Benoit and Kevin Sullivan (Lethal Lottery – First Round); Rick Steiner and The Booty Man downed Scott Steiner and Sgt Craig Pittman (Lethal Lottery – First Round); VK Wallstreet and Jim Duggan dropped Lord Steven Regal and Dave Taylor (Lethal Lottery – First Round); Dick Slater and Bobby Eaton toppled Alex Wright and Disco Inferno(Lethal Lottery – First Round); Diamond Dallas Page and The Barbarian overturned Meng and Hugh Morrus(Lethal Lottery – First Round); Scott Norton and Ice Train dropped Big Bubba and Stevie Ray (Lethal Lottery – First Round); Ric Flair and Randy Savage rolled over Arn Anderson and Eddie Guerrero (Lethal Lottery – First Round); WCW Cruiserweight Champion Dean Malenko beat Brad Armstrong; Dick Slater and Bobby Eaton dropped VK Wallstreet and Jim Duggan (Lethal Lottery – Second Round); Public Enemy defeated Ric Flair and Randy Savage by forfeit (Lethal Lottery – Second Round); Diamond Dallas Page and The Barbarian dropped The Booty Man and Rick Steiner (Lethal Lottery – Second Round); WCW US Champion Konnan overturned Jushin Liger; Diamond Dallas Page won an 8-man battle royal; WCW World Champion The Giant rolled over Sting.

16th June 1996: “WCW Great American Bash 1996”: Baltimore, MD – 9,000 (7,323 paid)
($123,406 live gate)
Main Event: (06/16): Rocco Rock pinned Jerry Saggs; VK Wallstreet beat Jim Powers; Jim Duggan overturned Disco Inferno.
PPV Broadcast: The Steiners bested Ice Train and Scott Norton; WCW US Champion Konnan overturned El Gato; Diamond Dallas Page rolled over Marcus Bagwell; WCW Cruiserweight Champion Dean Malenko beat Rey Mysterio Jr; John Tenta pinned Big Bubba; Chris Benoit overcame Kevin Sullivan in a “falls count anywhere” match; Sting toppled Lord Steven Regal; Ric Flair and Arn Anderson defeated Steve McMichael and Kevin Greene; WCW World Champion The Giant pinned WCW TV Champion Lex Luger.

 

7th July 1996: “WCW Bash at the Beach 1996”: Daytona Beach, FL – 8,300 sellout (6,400 paid)
($72,000 live gate)
Dark match: Jim Powers pinned Hugh Morrus.
The Main Event: (07/07): The Steiners bested WCW World Tag Champions Harlem Heat by DQ; Bobby Walker overcame Billy Kidman; Ricky Morton and Robert Gibson dropped Scott Norton and Ice Train; Eddie Guerrero beat Lord Steven Regal.
PPV Broadcast: Rey Misterio Jr pinned Psicosis; John Tenta beat Big Bubba in a “silver-dollars-on-a-pole” match; Diamond Dallas Page rolled over Jim Duggan in a “taped fist” match; The Nasty Boys bested Public Enemy in a double dog collar match; WCW Cruiserweight Champion Dean Malenko beat Disco Inferno; Steve McMichael overturned Joe Gomez; Ric Flair bested WCW US Champion Konnan to win the title; WCW World Champion The Giant and Kevin Sullivan dropped Arn Anderson and Chris Benoit; Sting, WCW TV Champion Lex Luger, and Randy Savage battled Kevin Nash and Scott Hall to a no-contest.

10th August 1996: “WCW Hog Wild 1996”: Sturgis, SD – 5,000 (0 paid)
Saturday Night: (08/10): The Public Enemy defeated Dick Slater and Mike Enos; Konnan overturned Chavo Guerrero Jr; The Nastys Boys bested High Voltage; Alex Wright pinned Bobby Eaton; Kevin Sullivan, Meng, and The Barbarian dropped Jim Powers, Mark Starr, and Joe Gomez; David Taylor overcame JL; Diamond Dallas Page rolled over The Renegade; Arn Anderson overturned Hugh Morrus.
PPV Broadcast: WCW Cruiserweight Champion Rey Misterio Jr pinned Ultimo Dragon; Scott Norton dropped Ice Train; Madusa pinned Bull Nakano; Chris Benoit overcame Dean Malenko; WCW World Tag Champions Harlem Heat defeated The Steiners; WCW US Champion Ric Flair bested Eddie Guerrero; Kevin Nash and Scott Hall rolled over Sting and WCW TV Champion Lex Luger; Hulk Hogan overturned WCW World Champion The Giant to win the title.

15th September 1996: “WCW Fall Brawl 1996”: Winston-Salem, NC – 11,300 (10,714 paid)
($153,914 live gate)
($52,000 merchandise sales)
Diamond Dallas Page pinned Chavo Guerrero Jr; Ice Train defeated Scott Norton in a submission match; Konnan downed Juventud Guerrera; Chris Benoit dropped Chris Jericho; WCW Cruiserweight Champion Rey Mysterio bested Super Calo; WCW World Tag Champions Harlem Heat overcame The Nasty Boys; The Giant bested Randy Savage; WCW World Champion Hulk Hogan, Kevin Nash, Scott Hall and Imposter Sting (Jeff Farmer) rolled over WCW US Champion Ric Flair, Lex Luger, Sting and Arn Anderson in a “War Games” encounter.

27th October 1996: “WCW Halloween Havoc 1996”: Las Vegas, NV – 10,000 (8,390 paid) (70% full)
($224,660 live gate)
($69,000 merchandise sales – $35,000 of which was NWO merchandise)
Set a new record live gate and one day merchandise sales for WCW
The event also grossed $250,000 sponsorship revenue from Slim Jim
Dark matches: Jim Powers beat Pat Tanaka; Psicosis and Juventus Guerrera defeated Damian and Halloween.
PPV Broadcast: Dean Malenko captured the WCW Cruiserweight title from Rey Misterio Jr; Diamond Dallas Page pinned Eddie Guerrero; The Giant downed Jeff Jarrett by DQ; Syxx toppled Chris Jericho; Lex Luger overturned Arn Anderson; Chris Benoit and Steve McMichael overcame Meng and The Barbarian; Kevin Nash and Scott Hall bested WCW World Tag Champions Harlem Heat to win the titles; WCW World Champion Hulk Hogan rolled over Randy Savage.

24th November 1996: “WCW World War III 1996”: Norfolk, VA – 10,314 sellout (7,018 paid)
($118,000 live gate)
J-Crown Champion Ultimo Dragon overturned Rey Mysterio Jr; Chris Jericho overcame Nick Patrick; The Giant pinned Jeff Jarrett; Harlem Heat downed Jacques Rougeau and Carl Oulette; WCW Cruiserweight Champion Dean Malenko beat Psicosis; WCW World Tag Champions Kevin Nash and Scott Hall defeated The Nasty Boys, and The Barbarian and Meng in a three-way; The Giant won the 3-ring, 60-man battle royal by last eliminating Kevin Nash and Lex Luger.

29th December 1996: “WCW Starrcade 1996”: Nashville, TN – 9,030 sellout (8,327 paid)
($113,040 live gate)
($60,000 merchandise sales)
Dark match: Mascarita Sagrada and Octagoncito beat Jerrito Estrada and Piratita Morgan.
PPV Broadcast:  J Crown Champion Ultimo Dragon overturned WCW Cruiserweight Champion Dean Malenko to win the title; Akira Hokuto toppled Madusa to win the WCW Women’s title (WCW Women’s Title Tournament – Final); Jushin Liger overcame Rey Misterio Jr; Jeff Jarrett pinned Chris Benoit in a no-DQ match; WCW World Tag Champions Kevin Nash and Scott Hall dropped Meng and The Barbarian; Eddie Guerrero beat Diamond Dallas Page to win the vacant WCW US Title (WCW US Title Tournament – Final);Lex Luger overcame The Giant; Roddy Piper defeated WCW World Champion Hulk Hogan in a non-title match.
