WWE Superstars Results 2013

WWE SUPERSTARS RESULTS (2013)

Jan 2013

Air Date: 3rd January 2013
Jimmy and Jey Usos defeated Darren Young and Titus O’Neil; Brodus Clay downed Primo.

Air Date: 10th January 2013
The Miz bested Tensai; Brodus Clay and Zack Ryder defeated Titus O’Neil and Darren Young.

Air Date: 17th January 2013
Kofi Kingston beat Michael McGillicutty; Zack Ryder defeated JTG.

Air Date: 24th January 2013
The Great Khali defeated Epico; The Great Khali beat Primo; Kofi Kingston defeated JTG.

Air Date: 31st January 2013
Kofi Kingson beat Michael McGillicutty; Jimmy and Jey Uso overcame Primo and Epico.

Feb 2013

Air Date: 7th February 2013
Zack Ryder beat Heath Slater; Damien Sandow defeated Justin Gabriel.

Air Date: 15th February 2013
Justin Gabriel beat Titus O’Neil; WWE Divas Champion Kaitlyn overcame Natalya.

Air Date: 22nd February 2013
Sin Cara defeated Drew McIntyre; Naomi and Cameron downed Natalya and Alicia Fox.

Mar 2013

Air Date: 1st March 2013
Brodus Clay and Tensai beat Primo and Epico; Kofi Kingston defeated David Otunga.

Air Date: 8th March 2013
Cody Rhodes and Damien Sandow beat Jimmy and Jey Uso; Layla defeated Tamina Snuka.

Air Date: 15th March 2013
R-Truth beat Michael McGillicutty; Zack Ryder defeated JTG.

Air Date: 22nd March 2013
WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro defeated Zack Ryder; Brodus Clay and Tensai downed JTG and Michael McGillicutty.

Air Date: 29th March 2013
Kofi Kingston beat Primo; Natalya defeated Aksana.

Apr 2013

Air Date: 5th April 2013
The Great Khali defeated JTG.

Air Date: 12th April 2013
Jimmy and Jey Uso beat Primo and Epico; The Great Khali defeated Darren Young.

Air Date: 19th April 2013
Natalya beat Tamina Snuka; Brodus Clay and Tensai defeated Primo and Epico.

Air Date: 26th April 2013
Jimmy Uso defeated Michael McGillicutty; Layla and WWE Divas Champion Kaitlyn downed Tamina Snuka and Aksana.

May 2013

Air Date: 3rd May 2013
R-Truth downed Damien Sandow; WWE Divas Champion Kaitlyn beat Tamina Snuka.

Air Date: 10th May 2013
Ted Dibiase Jr beat Michael McGillicutty; Cody Rhodes defeated Justin Gabriel.

Air Date: 17th May 2013
Sin Cara beat Michael McGillicutty; Cody Rhodes defeated Alex Riley.

Air Date: 24th May 2013
Brodus Clay, Tensai, Naomi and Cameron beat Jimmy Uso, Jey Uso and The Bella Twins; Damien Sandow defeated Alex Riley.

Air Date: 31st May 2013
R-Truth beat Antonio Cesaro; Justin Gabriel and Sin Cara defeated Titus O’Neil and Darren Young.

Jun 2013

Air Date: 7th June 2013
Brodus Clay and Tensai defeated Primo and Epico; Natalya beat Tamina Snuka.

Air Date: 14th June 2013
Titus O’Neil beat Tensai; Heath Slater defeated Zack Ryder.

Air Date: 21st June 2013
R-Truth downed Darren Young; Layla and Natalya overcame Naomi and Cameron.

Air Date: 28th June 2013
The Miz beat Wade Barrett; Antonio Cesaro defeated Zack Ryder.

Jul 2013

Air Date: 5th July 2013
Wade Barrett defeated Zack Ryder; Natalya downed Naomi.

Air Date: 12th July 2013
Justin Gabriel dropped Darren Young; Antonio Cesaro bested Zack Ryder.

Air Date: 19th July 2013
Brodus Clay and Tensai defeated Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; Ryback downed Brooklyn Brawler; The Miz overcame Heath Slater.

Air Date: 26th July 2013
Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso defeated Heath Slater and Drew McIntyre; Layla beat Alicia Fox.

Aug 2013

Air Date: 2nd August 2013
R-Truth downed Drew McIntyre; Sin Cara defeated Curt Hawkins.

Air Date: 9th August 2013
The Great Khali beat Jinder Mahal; Titus O’Neil and Darren Young defeated Zack Ryder and Justin Gabriel.

Air Date: 16th August 2013
Kaitlyn beat Tamina Snuka; Zack Ryder defeated Heath Slater; Natalya defeated Naomi; Dolph Ziggler and Kofi Kingston downed Titus O’Neil and Darren Young.

Air Date: 23rd August 2013
Christian beat Big E Langston; Kofi Kingston defeated JTG.

Air Date: 30th August 2013
Jimmy Uso, Jey Uso and Justin Gabriel overcame Drew McIntyre, Heath Slater and Jinder Mahal; Antonio Cesaro defeated Dolph Ziggler.

Sep 2013

Air Date: 6th September 2013
R-Truth beat Drew McIntyre; WWE Intercontinental Champion Curtis Axel beat Kofi Kingston.

Air Date: 13th September 2013
Jimmy and Jey Uso toppled Heath Slater and Jinder Mahal; Big E Langston rolled over Zack Ryder.

Air Date: 20th September 2013

Air Date: 27th September 2013
Big E Langston and Damien Sandow beat Zack Ryder and Justin Gabriel; Jack Swagger and Antonio Cesaro bested Brodus Clay and Tensai.

Air Date: 18th October 2013
Damien Sandow beat Kofi Kingston; Dolph Ziggler overcame Big E Langston.

Air Date: 25th October 2013
Fandango bested Justin Gabriel; Damien Sandow beat R-Truth.

Air Date: 22nd November 2013

Air Date: 29th November 2013

Air Date: 13th December 2013

Air Date: 20th December 2013

Air Date: 27th December 2013
