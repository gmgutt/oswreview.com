WWE Hell in a Cell Results

WWE HELL IN A CELL RESULTS

4th October 2009: “WWE Hell in a Cell 2009”: Newark, NJ – 12,356 sellout
($781,970 live gate)
Dark match: Matt Hardy beat Mike Knox.
PPV Broadcast: The Undertaker bested World Heavyweight Champion CM Punk to win the title in a “Hell in a Cell” match; WWE Intercontinental Champion John Morrison defeated Dolph Ziggler; WWE Divas Champion Mickie James downed Alicia Fox; WWE Unified World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and The Big Show dropped Batista and Rey Mysterio; Randy Orton toppled WWE World Champion John Cena to win the title in a “Hell in a Cell” match; Drew McIntyre overcame R-Truth; WWE US Champion Kofi Kingston overturned The Miz and Jack Swagger in a three-way; Triple H and Shawn Michaels rolled over Ted Dibiase Jr and Cody Rhodes in a “Hell in a Cell” match.

3rd October 2010: “WWE Hell in a Cell 2010”: Dallas, TX – 7,500
Dark match: Goldust, Kofi Kingston and R-Truth beat WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler and WWE Unified World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Drew McIntyre.
PPV Broadcast: WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan won a submissions-only “falls-count-anywhere” three-way over The Miz and John Morrison; WWE World Champion Randy Orton bested Sheamus in a “Hell-in-a-Cell” match;  Edge toppled Jack Swagger; Wade Barrett dropped John Cena; Natalya overcame WWE Divas Champion Michelle McCool by DQ; World Heavyweight Kane rolled over The Undertaker in a “Hell-in-a-Cell” encounter.

2nd October 2011: “WWE Hell in a Cell 2011”: New Orleans, LA – 9,400 (7,500 paid)
($475,000 live gate)
Dark match: Daniel Bryan beat JTG.
PPV Broadcast: Sheamus overcame Christian; Sin Cara (Azul) downed Imposter Sin Cara (Negro); WWE World Tag Champions Kofi Kingston and Evan Bourne dropped WWE US Champion Dolph Ziggler and Jack Swagger; World Heavyweight Champion Mark Henry toppled Randy Orton in a “Hell in a Cell” match; WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes bested John Morrison; Beth Phoenix defeated WWE Divas Champion Kelly Kelly to win the title; Alberto Del Rio bested WWE World Champion John Cena and CM Punk in a three-way “Hell in a Cell” match.

28th October 2012: “WWE Hell in a Cell 2012”: Atlanta, GA – 10,000
Randy Orton pinned Alberto Del Rio; Cody Rhodes and Damien Sandow defeated WWE World Tag Champions Daniel Bryan and Kane by DQ; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston overturned The Miz; WWE US Champion Antonio Cesaro downed Justin Gabriel; Rey Mysterio and Sin Cara dropped Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; The Big Show toppled World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus to win the title; WWE Divas Champion Eve Torres bested Layla and Kaitlyn in a three-way; WWE World Champion CM Punk rolled over Ryback in a “Hell-in-a-Cell” match.

27th October 2013: “WWE Hell in a Cell 2013”: Miami, FL – 9,000
($500,000 live gate)
Pre-show: Damien Sandow defeated Kofi Kingston.
PPV Broadcast: WWE World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Goldust won a three-way over Jimmy and Jey Uso, and Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns; Fandango and Summer Rae downed The Great Khali and Natalya; Big E Langston overturned Dean Ambrose by count-out; CM Punk pinned Ryback in a “Hell in a Cell” match; Los Matadores dropped Antonio Cesaro and Jack Swagger; World Heavyweight Champion John Cena bested Alberto Del Rio; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee overturned Brie Bella; Randy Orton rolled over Daniel Bryan in a “Hell in a Cell” match to win the vacant WWE World Title. Shawn Michaels was guest referee.

26th October 2014: “WWE Hell in a Cell 2014”: Dallas, TX – 13,000 sellout (12,100 paid)
Shown on PPV / WWE Network
Pre-show: Mark Henry pinned Bo Dallas.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler overcame Cesaro in a “best-of-three-falls” match; Nikki Bella beat Brie Bella in a “loser-becomes-winner’s-assistant-for-30-days” match; WWE World Tag Champions Goldust and Stardust toppled Jimmy and Jey Uso;  John Cena bested Randy Orton in a “Hell-in-a-Cell” match; WWE US Champion Sheamus downed The Miz; Rusev overturned The Big Show; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee beat Paige; Seth Rollins rolled over Dean Ambrose in a “Hell-in-a-Cell” match.


25th October 2015: “WWE Hell in a Cell 2015”: Los Angeles, CA – 14,184 sellout
($1,324,625 live gate)
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Dolph Ziggler, Cesaro and Neville beat Rusev, King Barrett and Sheamus.
PPV Broadcast: Alberto Del Rio defeated WWE US Champion John Cena to win the title; Roman Reigns pinned Bray Wyatt in a “Hell in a Cell” match; WWE World Tag Champions Kofi Kingston and Big E bested The Dudley Boys; WWE Divas Champion Charlotte beat Nikki Bella; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins toppled Kane; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Owens overcame Ryback; Brock Lesnar rolled over The Undertaker in a “Hell in a Cell” match.

30th October 2016: “WWE Hell in a Cell 2016”: Boston, MA – 13,000 sellout
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Sin Cara, Lince Dorado and Cedric Alexander rolled over Tony Nese, Drew Gulak and Ariya Daivari.
PPV Broadcast: WWE US Champion Roman Reigns defeated Rusev in a “Hell in a Cell” match;
Bayley toppled Dana Brooke; Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows overturned Enzo Amore and Big Cass; WWE Universal Champion Kevin Owens defeated Seth Rollins in a “Hell in a Cell” match; Brian Kendrick defeated WWE Cruiserweight Champion TJ Perkins to win the title; Sheamus and Cesaro bested WWE Raw Tag Champions Big E and Xavier Woods by DQ; Charlotte Flair rolled over WWE Raw Women’s Champion Sasha Banks to win the title in a “Hell in a Cell” match.
