Raw Results 2014

WWE RAW RESULTS (2014)

Jan 2014

Air Date: 6th January 2014
Jimmy and Jey Uso, and Rey Mysterio bested Daniel Bryan, Luke Harper and Erick Rowan; WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E Langston rolled over Curtis Axel; Alberto Del Rio bested Sin Cara; WWE World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Goldust beat Jack Swagger and Antonio Cesaro; The Great Khali dropped Damien Sandow. Sgt Slaughter was guest referee; Alicia Fox and Aksana defeated The Bella Twins; Rikishi, Scotty 2 Hotty and Brian Christopher beat Jinder Mahal, Heath Slater and Drew McIntyre; Roman Reigns overturned CM Punk.

Air Date: 13th January 2014
Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso bested Daniel Bryan and Bray Wyatt by DQ; John Cena downed Damien Sandow; The Big Show pinned Jack Swagger; Seth Rollins, Roman Reigns and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose bested Billy Gunn, Road Dogg and CM Punk; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee and Tamina Snuka downed Naomi and Cameron; Kofi Kingston dropped WWE World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton in a non-title match; WWE World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Goldust toppled Ryback and Curtis Axel; Alberto Del Rio bested Rey Mysterio; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso bested Daniel Bryan and Bray Wyatt in a cage match.

Air Date: 20th January 2014
Seth Rollins, Roman Reigns and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E Langston and WWE World Tag Champions Cody Rhodes and Goldust; Fandango overcame Xavier Woods; CM Punk pinned Billy Gunn; Alberto Del Rio toppled Rey Mysterio; Naomi and Cameron overturned WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee and Tamina Snuka; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso bested Erick Rowan and Luke Harper; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton rolled over Kofi Kingston by DQ.

Air Date: 27th January 2014
Jack Swagger and Antonio Cesaro defeated Rey Mysterio and Sin Cara; R-Truth dropped Fandango; Dolph Ziggler pinned The Miz; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso bested Ryback and Curtis Axel; Alberto Del Rio toppled Kofi Kingston; Cody Rhodes and Goldust overturned WWE World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn by DQ; The Bella Twins, Naomi and Cameon defeated WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee, Tamina Snuka, Alicia Fox and Aksana; John Cena, Daniel Bryan and Sheamus rolled over Roman Reigns, Seth Rollins and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose by DQ.

 

Feb 2014

Air Date: 3rd February 2014
WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose, Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns beat WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E Langston, Rey Mysterio and Kofi Kingston; Christian bested Jack Swagger; WWE World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Road Dogg downed Goldust and Cody Rhodes in a cage match; Titus O’Neill defeated Zack Ryder; Sheamus dropped Curtis Axel; Bray Wyatt, Luke Harper and Erick Rowan overturned R-Truth, Dolph Ziggler and Xavier Woods; Naomi overcame Aksana; Daniel Bryan rolled over WWE World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton in a non-title match.
Air Date: 10th February 2014
Bray Wyatt, Erick Rowan and Luke Harper defeated Rey Mysterio, Cody Rhodes and Goldust; Fandango pinned Santino Marella; Christian and Sheamus downed Jack Swagger and Antonio Cesaro; Alberto Del Rio dropped Dolph Ziggler; Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso beat Curtis Axel and Ryback; Mark Henry bested WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose by DQ; The Bella Twins and Cameron overcame WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee, Aksana and Alicia Fox; John Cena rolled over WWE World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton in a non-title match.
Air Date: 17th February 2014
Daniel Bryan rolled over Christian; Daniel Bryan rolled over Kane by DQ; Fandango dropped Santino Marella; Roman Reigns toppled Mark Henry; Jack Swagger overcame Kofi Kingston; WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E defeated Drew McIntyre and Jinder Mahal in a handicap match; John Cena beat Cesaro; Bray Wyatt, Erick Rowan and Luke Harper overcame Los Matadores and Sin Cara; Jey Uso dropped Billy Gunn; Sheamus toppled WWE World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton by DQ.
Air Date: 24th February 2014
Alberto Del Rio dropped Batista; WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E defeated Cesaro by DQ; Sheamus toppled Christian; Daniel Bryan rolled over Kane; Emma beat Summer Rae; Jimmy and Jey Uso toppled Road Dogg and Billy Gunn; Bray Wyatt overturned Roman Reigns by DQ.

Mar 2014


Air Date: 3rd March 2014
Jimmy and Jey Uso overturned WWE World Tag Champions Road Dogg and Billy Gunn to win the titles; WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E downed Cesaro by DQ; Bray Wyatt, Erick Rowan and Luke Harper bested Roman Reigns, WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose and Seth Rollins; Santino Marella and Emma defeated Fandango and Summer Rae; Sheamus overturned Christian; The Bella Twins overturned Aksana and Alicia Fox; Dolph Ziggler bested Alberto Del Rio; WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E downed Jack Swagger by DQ; Daniel Bryan toppled Batista by DQ.

Air Date: 10th March 2014
John Cena defeated Erick Rowan; WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso overturned Ryback and Curtis Axel; WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E downed Jack Swagger; Roman Reigns and Seth Rollins overturned Goldust and Cody Rhodes; The Bella Twins overturned AJ Lee and Tamina Snuka; Sheamus overturned Christian in a streetfight; Daniel Bryan and The Big Show beat Batista and WWE World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton.

Air Date: 17th March 2014
Jack Swagger and Cesaro overcame WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso in a non-title match; Sheamus overturned Titus O’Neil; Daniel Bryan toppled WWE World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton in a non-title, no-DQ match; Goldust rolled over Fandango; Naomi and Cameron toppled WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee and Tamina Snuka; The Big Show, WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E, Mark Henry and Dolph Ziggler bested Alberto Del Rio, Damien Sandow, Ryback and Curtis Axel; Bray Wyatt rolled over Kofi Kingston.

Air Date: 24th March 2014
Christian won a four-way over Alberto Del Rio, Sheamus and Dolph Ziggler; Sin Cara pinned Damien Sandow; Los Matadores overturned Curtis Axel and Ryback; Cody Rhodes overturned Fandango; The Big Show beat Titus O’Neil; John Cena battled Luke Harper to a no-contest; Naomi dropped WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee by count-out; Seth Rollins and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose downed Cesaro and Jack Swagger.

Air Date: 31st March 2014
Alberto Del Rio overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion Big E in a non-title match; Summer Rae downed Natalya; Los Matadores and WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso overturned Jack Swagger, Cesaro, Curtis Axel and Ryback; Cody Rhodes and Goldust rolled over Fandango and Damien Sandow; Bray Wyatt rolled over R-Truth; Naomi dropped WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee in a non-title lumberjill match; Kane downed Roman Reigns by DQ; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton battled  Batista to a no-contest in a no-DQ match.

Apr 2014


Air Date: 7th April 2014
Bray Wyatt, Erick Rowan and Luke Harper bested John Cena, Sheamus and Big E; Santino Marella and Emma defeated Fandango and Summer Rae; WWE World Tag Champions  Jimmy and Jey Uso battled Randy Orton and Batista to a double count-out; Rob Van Dam beat Damien Sandow; Wade Barrett rolled over Rey Mysterio; Alexander Rusev beat Zack Ryder; Paige downed WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee to win the title; Cesaro overcame Jack Swagger by count-out; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan battled Triple H to a no-contest.

Air Date: 14th April 2014
Rob Van Dam beat Alberto Del Rio (WWE Intercontinental Title #1 Contender Tournament – 1st Round); Ryback and Curtis Axel beat Goldust and Cody Rhodes; WWE Divas Champion Paige downed Alicia Fox; Batista and Randy Orton toppled WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso by DQ; Cesaro overcame Mark Henry (WWE Intercontinental Title #1 Contender Tournament – 1st Round); Alexander Rusev beat Xavier Woods; Sheamus overturned Jack Swagger (WWE Intercontinental Title #1 Contender Tournament – 1st Round); Fandango and Layla defeated Santino Marella and Emma; Wade Barrett rolled over Dolph Ziggler (WWE Intercontinental Title #1 Contender Tournament – 1stRound); Seth Rollins, Roman Reigns and WWE US Champion Dean Ambrose downed Alberto Del Rio, Jack Swagger, Fandango, Heath Slater, Drew McIntyre, Jinder Mahal, Titus O’Neil, Ryback, Curtis Axel , Alexander Rusev and Wade Barrett by DQ.

Air Date: 21st April 2014
Wade Barrett rolled over Sheamus (WWE Intercontinental Title #1 Contender Tournament – Semi-Final); Los Matadores and El Torito overcame Drew McIntyre, Jinder Mahal and Hornswoggle; WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso overturned Cody Rhodes and Goldust; Emma defeated Layla; Rob Van Dam beat Cesaro by count-out (WWE Intercontinental Title #1 Contender Tournament – Semi-Final); WWE Divas Champion Paige downed Aksana; Alexander Rusev beat Sin Cara; John Cena defeated Bray Wyatt, Luke Harper and Erick Rowan by DQ in a handicap match.

Air Date: 28th April 2014
WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso overturned Ryback and Curtis Axel; Sheamus overturned Titus O’Neil; Cesaro overcame Jack Swagger; Alberto Del Rio overcame Cody Rhodes; Alexander Rusev beat Xavier Woods by DQ; Heath Slater and Drew McIntyre downed Los Matadores; WWE Divas Champion Paige battled Brie Bella to a no-contest; Wade Barrett rolled over Rob Van Dam (WWE Intercontinental Title #1 Contender Tournament – Final); Roman Reigns battled to a no-contest with Randy Orton.

May 2014


Air Date: 5th May 2014
Sheamus won a battle royal to win the WWE US Title, last eliminating Dean Ambrose; Rob Van Dam beat Cesaro by DQ; Ryback beat Cody Rhodes; Alexander Rusev beat Kofi Kingston; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Daniel Bryan toppled Alberto Del Rio; WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett rolled over Big E; Bray Wyatt, Erick Rowan and Luke Harper bested Roman Reigns, Dean Ambrose and Seth Rollins.

Air Date: 12th May 2014
Rob Van Dam beat Jack Swagger; WWE Divas Champion Paige downed Alicia Fox; John Cena and WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso overturned Luke Harper, Erick Rowan and Bray Wyatt; Nikki Bella defeated Natalya; Sheamus overturned Curtis Axel; Sheamus overturned Ryback; Dolph Ziggler bested Fandango; Cody Rhodes overturned Damien Sandow; Batista battled to a no-contest with Roman Reigns.

Air Date: 19th May 2014
Cesaro overcame WWE US Champion Sheamus in a non-title match; Big E downed Ryback (Beat The Clock); Rusev beat Heath Slater; Rob Van Dam beat Alberto Del Rio (Beat The Clock); Seth Rollins overturned Batista by DQ; Alicia Fox beat WWE Divas Champion Paige in a non-title match; Dolph Ziggler battled to a draw with Mark Henry (Beat The Clock); John Cena defeated Luke Harper by DQ.

Air Date: 26th May 2014
Cesaro overcame Rob Van Dam; Eva Marie downed Summer Rae; El Torito overcame Drew McIntyre; Rusev beat Zack Ryder; Randy Orton and Batista defeated Goldust and Cody Rhodes; Randy Orton and Batista defeated Goldust in a handicap match; Bo Dallas overturned Sin Cara; Emma defeated Alicia Fox; Adam Rose downed Damien Sandow; WWE US Champion Sheamus overturned Alberto Del Rio.

Jun 2014


Air Date: 2nd June 2014
Rob Van Dam and Sheamus overturned Cesaro and Wade Barrett; Bo Dallas overturned Kofi Kingston; John Cena defeated Kane by DQ; Los Matadores overturned Drew McIntyre and Heath Slater; Alicia Fox and Aksana defeated Nikki Bella in a handicap match; Adam Rose downed Jack Swagger; Luke Harper and Erick Rowan toppled WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso in a non-title match; Alberto Del Rio overcame Dolph Ziggler (MITB Qualifier); Ryback and Curtis Axel beat Goldust and Sin Cara.

Air Date: 9th June 2014
Sheamus overturned Wade Barrett (MITB Qualifier); Rusev beat Zack Ryder; Ryback and Curtis Axel beat Goldust and R-Truth; WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso overturned Fandango and Damien Sandow; Bo Dallas overturned Xavier Woods; Cesaro overcame Rob Van Dam (MITB Qualifier); WWE Divas Champion Paige downed Alicia Fox; Jack Swagger bested Santino Marella; John Cena, Roman Reigns and Dean Ambrose downed Bray Wyatt, Luke Harper and Erick Rowan.

Air Date: 16th June 2014
Seth Rollins overturned Dolph Ziggler by DQ; WWE Intercontinental Champion Bad News Barrett beat Dean Ambrose by count-out; WWE US Champion Sheamus overturned Bray Wyatt by DQ; Rusev beat Heath Slater; Adam Rose and Summer Rae defeated Fandango and Layla; Roman Reigns won a battle royal (MITB Qualifier); WWE Divas Champion Paige defeated Cameron; Goldust and Stardust (Cody Rhodes) beat Ryback and Curtis Axel; John Cena beat Kane in a stretcher match (MITB Qualifier).

Air Date: 23rd June 2014
Luke Harper bested Jimmy Uso; Jey Uso dropped Erick Rowan; Naomi downed Alicia Fox; Bo Dallas overturned Titus O’Neil; Seth Rollins overturned Rob Van Dam by DQ; WWE Intercontinental Champion Bad News Barrett beat Dolph Ziggler; Jack Swagger bested Kofi Kingston; Big E defeated Damien Sandow; John Cena, Roman Reigns and WWE US Champion Sheamus overturned Alberto Del Rio, Bray Wyatt, Randy Orton and Cesaro.

Air Date: 30th June 2014
Seth Rollins overturned Rob Van Dam; Bray Wyatt, Luke Harper and Erick Rowan toppled Sheamus and WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso; Naomi and Cameron toppled Nikki Bella; Kofi Kingston toppled Cesaro; The Great Khali downed Damien Sandow; Dolph Ziggler bested Fandango; AJ Lee defeated WWE Divas Champion Paige to win the title; John Cena and Roman Reigns overturned Kane and Randy Orton by DQ.

Jul 2014

Air Date: 7th July 2014
Luke Harper and Erick Rowan toppled WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso in a non-title match; Rusev beat Rob Van Dam; Randy Orton toppled Dean Ambrose; Alberto Del Rio dropped Dolph Ziggler; WWE US Champion Sheamus overturned Damien Sandow; Chris Jericho dropped The Miz; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee and Paige defeated Naomi and Cameron; Kofi Kingston toppled Cesaro; Bo Dallas overturned El Torito; WWE World Heavyweight Champion John Cena beat Seth Rollins by DQ.

Air Date: 14th July 2014

Air Date: 21st July 2014
Roman Reigns overturned Randy Orton and Kane in a handicap match; Rosa Mendes, Eva Marie, Cameron and Alicia Fox beat Nikki Bella in a handicap match; Bo Dallas overturned Damien Sandow; Dolph Ziggler bested WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz in a non-title match; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee and Paige defeated Emma and Natalya; Zack Ryder bested Fandango; Ryback and Curtis Axel beat Big E and Kofi Kingston; Rusev beat The Great Khali; Cesaro dropped Dean Ambrose by DQ.

Air Date: 28th July 2014
WWE World Heavyweight Champion John Cena beat Cesaro; Dolph Ziggler and WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso bested Ryback, Curtis Axel and WWE Intercontinental Champion The Miz; R-Truth bested Bo Dallas; Adam Rose defeated Damien Sandow; Diego dropped Fandango; Natalya and Naomi downed Cameron and Alicia Fox; Chris Jericho battled to a no-contest with Seth Rollins.

Aug 2014

Roman Reigns overturned Kane in a last man standing match; Mark Henry overcame Damien Sandow; Dean Ambrose defeated Alberto Del Rio (Beat the Clock); Rusev beat Sin Cara; Dolph Ziggler bested Cesaro; Goldust and Stardust beat Ryback and Curtis Axel; Chris Jericho dropped Luke Harper by DQ; Diego dropped Fandango; Bo Dallas overturned R-Truth; Heath Slater bested Seth Rollins (Beat The Clock).

Air Date: 11th August 2014

Air Date: 18th August 2014

Air Date: 25th August 2014
Rusev beat Jack Swagger; Cesaro bested Rob Van Dam; WWE Divas Champion Paige downed Natalya; Goldust and Stardust rolled over WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso by count-out; WWE Intercontinental Champion Dolph Ziggler overturned Damien Sandow; Roman Reigns toppled Seth Rollins and Kane by DQ in a handicap match; Los Matadores toppled Heath Slater and Titus O’Neil; Bo Dallas toppled Kofi Kingston; John Cena defeated Bray Wyatt by DQ; John Cena, The Big Show and Mark Henry rolled over Bray Wyatt, Luke Harper and Erick Rowan.

Air Date: 8th September 2014

Air Date: 29th September 2014

Oct 2014

Air Date: 13th October 2014

Air Date: 20th October 2014

Air Date: 27th October 2014
