ROH Results 2004

Jan 2004

9th January 2004: “ROH/JAPW Collision Course”: Woodbridge, NJ – 400
Joint show with JAPW
EC Negro and KC Blade beat Dunn and Marcos; Ruckus downed Derek Wylde; DeVito and Trent Acid dropped Johnny Kashmere and Loc; Azrieal, Jay Lethal and The Insane Dragon toppled Deranged, Dixie and Lit; Shane Douglas bested Justin Credible; April Hunter overcame Sumie Sakai; ROH Champion Samoa Joe overturned Balls Mahoney; JAPW Champion Dan Maff downed Slyk Wagner Brown by DQ; Christopher Daniels rolled over AJ Styles, CM Punk and John Walters in a four-way.

10th January 2004: “ROH The Battle Lines are Drawn”: Wilmington, OH – 500
Matt Stryker beat Alex Shelley in a “pure-wrestling match; HWA Champion Nigel McGuinness defeated Cody Hawk; Chris Sabin downed Jimmy Jacobs; Jimmy Rave overcame Rainman, Todd Sexton and Caprice Coleman in a four-way; AJ Styles bested Homicide; Mike Mondo dropped Seth Skyfire; Dixie, Hydro, Joey Matthews and Slim J overturned DeVito, Loc, Dun and Marcos; ROH Tag Champions Mark and Jay Briscoe toppled ROH Champion Samoa Joe and Bryan Danielson; Christopher Daniels, BJ Whitmer  and Dan Maff battled CM Punk, Colt Cabana and Ace Steel to a no-contest.

29th January 2004: “ROH The Last Stand”: Glen Burnie, MD – 500
John Walters beat Chad Collyer in a “pure-wrestling” match; Chris Sabin overcame Caprice Coleman, Hydro, Jack Evans, Slyk Wagner Brown and Sonjay Dutt in a six-way; Trent Acid and Johnny Kashmere bested Dixie and Izzy, and Justin Credible and Loc in a no-DQ, three-way; BJ Whitmer and Dan Maff defeated Matt Stryker and Xavier in a four-way via double pinfall; Alex Shelley toppled Jimmy Jacobs; Homicide overturned CM Punk; ROH Tag Champions Jay and Mark Briscoe rolled over ROH Champion Samoa Joe and Jerry Lynn.

Feb 2004

14th February 2004: “ROH Second Anniversary Show”: Braintree, MA – 500
CM Punk beat John Walters (ROH Pure Wrestling Title Tournament – First Round); Doug Williams defeated Chris Sabin (ROH Pure Wrestling Title Tournament – First Round); Matt Stryker dropped Josh Daniels (ROH Pure Wrestling Title Tournament – First Round); AJ Styles overturned Jimmy Rave (ROH Pure Wrestling Title Tournament – First Round); Dixie, Hydro and Izzy overcame DeVito, Justin Credible and Loc; CM Punk bested Doug Williams (ROH Pure Wrestling Title Tournament – Semi-Final); AJ Styles toppled Matt Stryker (ROH Pure Wrestling Title Tournament – Semi-Final); ROH Tag Champions Jay and Mark Briscoe overturned Trent Acid and Johnny Kashmere; ROH Champion Samoa Joe dropped BJ Whitmer, Dan Maff and Low Ki in a four-way; AJ Styles rolled over CM Punk to capture the ROH Pure Title (ROH Pure Wrestling Title Tournament – Final).

Mar 2004

13th March 2004: “ROH At Our Best”: Elizabeth, NJ – 1,800
The Amazing Red beat Jack Evans, Jimmy Rave, Mark Briscoe, Sonjay Dutt and Teddy Hart in a six-way; Xavier pinned Slyk Wagner Brown; Jerry Lynn defeated Nigel McGuinness; Matt Stryker dropped John Walters; BJ Whitmer and Dan Maff battled Colt Cabana and Ace Steel to a no-contest; ROH Pure Wrestling Champion AJ Styles bested CM Punk. Ricky Steamboat was guest referee; ROH Champion Samoa Joe toppled Jay Briscoe in a cage match; Justin Credible, Loc, Mascada and DeVito rolled over Angel Dust, Deranged, Dixie and Izzy in a cage match.

13th March 2004: “Do or Die II”: Elizabeth, NJ – 600
Mellow beat Jerrelle Clark; Havok and Papadon defeated Jared Steele and Todd Sexton; Caprice Coleman downed Rainman; Allison Danger dropped April Hunter; Jimmy Jacobs bested Brad Bradley; Hydro toppled Roderick Strong; Austin Aries overturned Danny Daniels; Super Dragon overcame Excalibur; Don Juan and Fast Eddie defeated Caprice Coleman and Rainman, Diablo Santiago and Oman Tortuga, and Dunn and Marcos in a tag scramble match.

Apr 2004

23rd April 2004: “ROH Reborn: Stage One”: St Paul, MN – 450
Matt Stryker beat Nigel McGuinness; DeVito and Loc overcame Dunn and Marcos; John Walters toppled Justin Credible; Alex Shelley bested Danny Daniels, Jimmy Jacobs, Jimmy Rave, Jack Evans and Masada in a six-way; BJ Whitmer downed Colt Cabana; Matt Sydal dropped Delirious; ROH Tag Champions Jay and Mark Briscoe bested Ricky Reyes and Rocky Romeo; Bryan Danielson toppled CM Punk. Ricky Steamboat was guest referee; ROH Champion Samoa Joe rolled over Homicide by DQ.

24th April 2004: “ROH Reborn: Stage Two”: Chicago Ridge, IL – 650
BJ Whitmer beat Ace Steel; Justin Credible and Masada defeated Delirious and Shawn Daivari; Chad Collyer downed John Walters; Nigel McGuinness dropped Austin Aries, Jimmy Rave and Rocky Romero in a four-way; Homicide overturned Bryan Danielson; Ricky Reyes bested Danny Daniels; Jack Evans and Matt Sydal overcame Alex Shelley and Jimmy Jacobs, DeVito and Loc, and Dunn and Marcos in a tag team scramble match; ROH Champion Samoa Joe toppled Matt Stryker; CM Punk and Colt Cabana rolled over ROH Tag Champions Jay and Mark Briscoe to win the title.

May 2004

15th May 2004: “ROH Round Robin Challenge III”: Lexington, MA – 500
BJ Whitmer and Dann Maff toppled ROH Tag Champions CM Punk and Colt Cabana to win the titles (ROH Round Robin Challenge – Match 1);Alex Shelley bested Matt Stryker; DeVito, Loc, Dunn and Marcos beat Diablo Santiago, Josh Daniels, Oman Tortuga and Prince Nana; ROH Champion Samoa Joe bested Ricky Reyes; Jay and Mark Briscoe bested ROH Tag Champions BJ Whitmer and Dan Maff to win the titles (ROH Round Robin Challenge – Match 2); Josh Daniels downed Dixie; John Walters beat Hydro, Izzy, Masada, Roderick Strong and Trent Acid in a six-way; Homicide dropped Spanky; CM Punk and Colt Cabana rolled over ROH Tag Champions Jay and Mark Briscoe to win the titles (ROH Round Robin Challenge – Match 3).

22nd May 2004: “ROH Generation Next”: Philadelphia, PA – 350
Austin Aries, Jack Evans and Roderick Strong beat Angel Dust, Dixie and Izzy; Alex Shelley bested Hydro; Danny Daniels, Masada and Trent Acid downed DeVito, Justin Credible and Loc; Nigel McGuinness dropped Jimmy Jacobs; ROH Tag Champions CM Punk and Colt Cabana toppled BJ Whitmer and Dan Maff in a no-DQ match; Jay and Mark Briscoe bested Diablo Santiago and Oman Tortuga; Alex Shelley, Austin Aries, Jack Evans and Roderick Strong overturned Jimmy Rave, John Walters, and Jay and Mark Briscoe; ROH Champion Samoa Joe rolled over Homicide in a “Relaxed Rules” match.

June 2004

12th June 2004: “ROH World Title Classic”: Dayton, OH – 500
Danny Daniels and Masada beat Dunn and Marcos; Armand Hussein and Shawn Osbourne battled JC Starr and TJ Dogg to a no-contest; Jimmy Jacobs downed Caprice Coleman, Delirious, Loc, Matt Sydal and Rainman in a six-way; Austin Aries, Roderick Strong and Alex Shelley bested Matt Stryker, John Walters and Jimmy Rave; Hydro overcame Chad Collyer, Ray Gordy and Superstar Steve in a four-way; ROH Champion Samoa Joe battled CM Punk to a hour draw; BJ Whitmer and Dan Maff rolled over Austin Aries and Jack Evans, Jay and Mark Briscoe, and Colt Cabana and Ace Steel in a four-way “Ultimate Endurance” match.

24th June 2004: “ROH Survival of the Fittest”: Essington, PA – 300
Mark Briscoe beat Alex Shelley (Survival of the Fittest Qualifier); Colt Cabana bested Trent Acid (Survival of the Fittest Qualifier); Austin Aries battled John Walters to a no-contest (Survival of the Fittest Qualifier); Austin Aries and Roderick Strong defeated John Walters and Josh Daniels(Survival of the Fittest Qualifier); Homicide dropped Jay Briscoe (Survival of the Fittest Qualifier); Bryan Danielson overturned Jack Evans(Survival of the Fittest Qualifier); ROH Champion Samoa Joe overcame Matt Stryker (Survival of the Fittest Qualifier); CM Punk defeated Dixie and Hydro, Dunn and Marcos, and Oman Tortuga and Diablo Santiago in a tag team scramble match; CM Punk toppled Hydro; Bryan Danielson rolled over Austin Aries, Homicide, Mark Briscoe, Colt Cabana and ROH Champion Samoa Joe in a six-way non-title elimination match.

24th July 2004: “ROH Death before Dishonor II – Stage II”: Chicago Ridge, IL – 500

Dec 2004

4th December 2004: “ROH All Star Extravaganza II”: Elizabeth, NJ – 800
Davey Andrews, Anthony Franco, Shane Hagadorn and Matt Turner overcame Angel Dust, Deranged, Dixie and Izzy; Jay Lethal dropped  The Weapon of Mask Destruction; Diablo Santiago and Oman Tortuga beat BJ Whitmer and Dan Maff, DeVito and Loc, and Dunn and Marcos in a tag team scramble match; ROH Pure Champion John Walters bested Jimmy Rave by count-out; Austin Aries battled Low Ki to a draw; ROH Tag Champions Rocky Romero and Ricky Reyes downed Chad Collyer and Nigel McGuinness; Trent Acid defeated Jerk Jackson; Colt Cabana and Jimmy Jacobs dropped Roderick Strong and Jack Evans; Bryan Danielson overturned Homicide; ROH Champion Samoa Joe rolled over CM Punk.
