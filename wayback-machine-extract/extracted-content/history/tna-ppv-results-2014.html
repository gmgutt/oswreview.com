TNA PPV Results 2014

TNA PPV RESULTS 2014

3rd January 2014: “TNA One Night Only: Tag Team Tournament”: Orlando, FL
Taped on 03/18/2013
Generation Me beat Sonjay Dutt and Petey Williams (Greatest Tag Team Tournament – Qualifier); Garett Bischoff and Wes Brisco overcame Cassidy Riley and Chase Stevens (Greatest Tag Team Tournament – Qualifier); Austin Aries and Bobby Roode toppled Doug Williams and Rob Terry (Greatest Tag Team Tournament -Quarter-Final); Chavo Guerrero Jr and Hernandez defeated Christopher Daniels and Kazarian (Greatest Tag Team Tournament – Quarter-Final); Samoa Joe and Magnus downed Wes Brisco and Garett Bischoff (Greatest Tag Team Tournament – Quarter-Final); Team 3D dropped Generation Me (Greatest Tag Team Tournament – Quarter-Final); Bobby Roode and Austin Aries toppled Samoa Joe and Magnus (Greatest Tag Team Tournament – Semi-Final); Team 3D bested Chavo Guerrero Jr and Hernandez (Greatest Tag Team Tournament – Semi-Final); Team 3D rolled over Bobby Roode and Austin Aries (Greatest Tag Team Tournament – Final).

 

10th January 2014: “TNA One Night Only: Hardcore Justice 3”: Lowell, MA – 1,000
Taped on 12/29/2013
Ethan Carter III beat Tommy Dreamer in a tables match; Bobby Roode defeated Samoa Joe by DQ; Lei’D Tapa downed Velvet Sky; Eric Young and Joseph Park dropped Christopher Daniels and Kazarian in a “Full Metal Mayhem” match; Bully Ray bested Mr Anderson in a “last man standing” match; Austin Aries won a non-title “Escape cage” match over TNA X-Division Champion Chris Sabin; Kurt Angle, Samoa Joe, James Storm, and Abyss rolled over TNA World Champion Magnus, Bobby Roode, and TNA World Tag Champions Robbie E and Jessie Godderz in a “Lethal Lockdown” match. 

 

7th February 2014: “TNA One Night Only: Old School”: Poughkeepsie, NY – 1,500
Taped on 12/30/2013.
Austin Aries won a non-title three-way over Sonjay Dutt and TNA X-Division Champion Chris Sabin; Ethan Carter III pinned Dewey Barnes; Bully Ray overcame Tommy Dreamer in a “falls-count-anywhere” match; Christopher Daniels, Kazarian, and Velvet Sky overturned Lei’D Tapa and TNA World Tag Champions Jessie Godderz and Robbie E. Eric Young was guest referee; TNA World Champion Magnus bested Samoa Joe; Kurt Angle pinned Mr Anderson; Bobby Roode toppled James Storm in a “last man standing” match; Jeff Hardy rolled over Abyss in a “Monster’s Ball” match.

 

9th March 2014: “TNA Lockdown 2014”: Miami, FL – 1,500
All matches were held inside a steel cage.
The Great Muta, TNA X-Division Champion Seiya Sanada and Yasufumi Nakanoueno beat Chris Sabin, Christopher Daniels and Kazarian;  Samuel Shaw defeated Mr Anderson in an “Escape the Cage” encounter; Tigre Uno downed Manik; Gunner dropped James Storm in a “Last Man Standing” match; TNA Knockouts Champion Madison Rayne bested Gail Kim; TNA World Champion Magnus toppled Samoa Joe in a “Submissions or Knockout match”; Team MVP (MVP, Willow (Jeff Hardy), Davey Richards and Eddie Edwards) rolled over Team Dixie (Bobby Roode, Austin Aries, Robbie E, and Jessie Godderz) in a Lethal Lockdown match for “control of TNA Wrestling Operations”. Bully Ray was guest referee.

27th April 2014: “TNA Sacrifice 2014”: Orlando, FL – 800 sellout
Davey Richards and Eddie Edwards toppled TNA World Tag Champions Robbie E and Jessie Godderz, and DJ Zema to win the titles in a handicap match; Mr Anderson bested Samuel Shaw in a “loser gets committed” match; Kurt Angle and Willow beat Ethan Carter III and Rockstar Spud; Seiya Sanada defeated Tigre Uno; (Best of 3 series – Match #3); Gunner downed James Storm in an ”I Quit” match; Angelina Love dropped TNA Knockouts Champion Madison Rayne to win the title; Bobby Roode overturned Bully Ray in a tables match; TNA World Champion Eric Young rolled over Magnus.

9th May 2014: “TNA One Night Only: Joker’s Wild 2”: Birmingham, England – 3,000
Taped on 02/02/2014
Dark match: Gail Kim beat Shanna.
PPV Broadcast: Doug Williams and Magnus beat Chris Sabin and Gunner; Samoa Joe and Bad Bones defeated Christopher Daniels and Robbie E; Bully Ray and Rockstar Spud downed Ken Anderson and Austin Aries; Frankie Kazarian and Ethan Carter III dropped Curry Man and Eric Young; Eddie Edwards and Davey Richards bested Bobby Roode and James Storm; Samuel Shaw and Abyss overcame Zema Ion and Jessie Godderz; Velvet Sky, TNA Knockouts Champion Madison Rayne and ODB beat Gail Kim, Le’D Tape and Alpha Female in an elimination match; Ethan Carter III won a battle royal.

15th June 2014: “TNA Slammiversary XII”: Arlington, TX – 3,500
TNA X-Division Champion Seiya Sanada won a six-way ladder match over Davey Richards, Eddie Edwards, Manik, Crazy Steve, and Tigre Uno; Bobby Lashley bested Samoa Joe; Magnus overcame Willow; Austin Aries downed Kenny King; Ross Von Erich and Marshall Von Erich dropped DJ Zema and Jessie Godderz by DQ; TNA Knockouts Champion Angelina Love defeated Gail Kim; Ethan Carter III won a Texas Death match over Bully Ray; Mr Anderson toppled James Storm; TNA World Champion Eric Young won a three-way cage match over Austin Aries and Bobby Lashley.

4th July 2014: “TNA One Night Only: Global Impact Japan”: Tokyo, Japan (Sumo Hall) – 4,000
Taped on 03/02/2014.
Dark matches: Kazushi Miyamoto, Nosawa and Mazada beat Hiroshi Yamato, Andy Wu and Daiki Inaba; Ryota Hama pinned Kikutaro; Manabu Soya and Seiki Yoshioka defeated Ryoji Sai and Kazma Sakamoto; Satoshi Kojima downed Yasufumi Nakanoueno.
PPV Broadcast: Christopher Daniels and Kazarian defeated Minoru Tanaka and Koji Kanemoto; Gail Kim pinned TNA Knockouts Champion Madison Rayne in a non-title match; Yoshihiro Takayama battled Abyss to a double count-out; Masakatsu Funaki bested Bobby Roode; Keiji Muto, Taiyo Kea and Rob Terry toppled Samoa Joe, Masayuki Kono and Rene Dupree; Robbie E and Jessie Godderz won  a three-way over TNA World Tag Champions Davey Richards and Eddie Edwards, and Kaz Hayashi and Shuji Kondo to win the titles; Seiya Sanada bested TNA X-Division Champion Austin Aries to win the title; TNA World Champion Magnus rolled over Kai.

1st August 2014: “TNA One Night Only: X-Travaganza 2”: Orlando, FL
Taped on 04/12/2014.
Low Ki dropped Chris Sabin; Rashad Cameron toppled Zema Ion; Kenny King beat Rubix; Ace Vedder (Trent Baretta) beat Manik; Sonjay Dutt rolled over Rockstar Spud; Tigre Uno overcame Petey Williams; Eddie Edwards and Davey Richards overturned Christopher Daniels and Kazarian in a ladder match; TNA X-Division Champion Seiya Sanada defeated Austin Aries in a “best-of-three-falls” outing; Low Ki won a four-way Ultimate X over Rashad Cameron,  Ace Vedder and Sonjay Dutt.

5th September 2014: “TNA One Night Only: World Cup of Wrestling 2”: Orlando, FL
Taped on 04/12/2014.
Eddie Edwards overcame James Storm; TNA World Tag Champions Robbie E and Jessie Godderz beat Mr Anderson and TNA X-Division Champion Seiya Sanada; Gail Kim beat TNA X-Division Champion Madison Rayne in a non-title match; TNA World Champion Eric Young beat Bobby Roode; Kurt Angle downed Magnus; Angelina Love downed ODB; Ethan Carter III dropped Davey Richards; Bully Ray and Gunner bested Samuel Shaw and Kenny King; Eric Young, Bully Ray, Eddie Edwards, Gunner and ODB beat Ethan Carter III, Jessie Godderz, Robbie E, Magnus and Gail Kim.

12th October 2014: “TNA Bound For Glory 2014”: Tokyo, Japan (Korakuen Hall) – 1,500 sellout
Shown on PPV tape delayed.                                                                                           
Samoa Joe was announced as X-Division Champion despite having vacated the title on 9/19. Low Ki won the title on the same day but the title change did not air until 11/19.
Havok was announced as TNA Knockouts Champion despite having lost the title on 9/19 to Taryn Terrell but the title change did not air until 11/19.
Minoru Tanaka defeated Manik; Ethan Carter III downed Ryota Hama; MVP dropped Kazma Sakamoto; Samoa Joe won a three-way over Low Ki and Kaz Hayashi; Jiro Kuroshio and Yusuke Kodama beat Andy Wu and El Hijo del Pantera; Team 3D overcame Tommy Dreamer and Abyss in a hardcore match; Havok pinned Velvet Sky; The Great Muta and Tajiri rolled over James Storm and Seiya Sanada.

7th November 2014: “TNA One Night Only: Knockouts Knockdown”: Orlando, FL
Taped on 05/10/2014.
Gail Kim beat Veda Scott; Angelina Love downed Scarlett Bordeaux; Madison Rayne downed Havoc; Reby rolled over Velvet Sky; Taryn Tarrell beat Karlee Perez; Mia Yim beat Brittany; Brooke Tessmacher bested Deonna; Marti Belle downed ODB; ODB beat Rockstar Spud; Madison Rayne won a “Queen of the Knockouts” gauntlet match.

5th December 2014: “TNA One Night Only: Victory Road”: Orlando, FL
Taped on 05/10/2014.
Bram beat Davey Richards; Austin Aries overturned Kenny King; James Storm and Kazarian toppled Knux and The Freak (Rob Terry); Bobby Lashley rolled over Samoa Joe; Abyss overturned Mr Anderson; Robbie E and Jessie Godderz beat Bully Ray and Rockstar Spud; Gunner bested Magnus; Ethan Carter III dropped TNA X-Division Champion Seiya Sanada in a non-title match; Samuel Shaw beat Crazy Steve; Eddie Edwards won a three-way over DJ Zema and Tigre Uno; Gunner won a battle royal.
