ROH on Sinclair Results 2016

ROH ON SINCLAIR TV RESULTS 2016

Jan 2016

Air Date: 2nd January 2016
ROH TV Champion Roderick Strong rolled over Stevie Richards; Caprice Coleman overcame Will Ferrera in a no-DQ match; Jonathan Gresham beat Cedric Alexander; Michael Elgin overcame Donovan Dijak.

Air Date: 9th January 2016
Rhett Titus & Kenny King rolled over Rocky Romero & Trent Baretta; ROH Tag Champions Ray Rowe & Hanson overcame Donovan Dijak & Joey Daddiego; Dalton Castle won a four-way over Jay Briscoe, Matt Sydal and Moose.

Air Date: 16th January 2016
Christopher Daniels & Frankie Kazarian overcame ACH & Alex Shelley; Mark Briscoe downed Adam Page; The Young Bucks won a three-way over Adam Cole & Michael Bennett, and IWGP Jr Tag Champions Bobby Fish & Kyle O’Reilly.

Air Date: 23rd January 2016
Brian Fury defeated Shaheem Ali (Top Prospect Tournament – Quarter-Final); Cedric Alexander rolled over Cheeseburger; Jonathan Gresham defeated Cedric Alexander by DQ; Jay Briscoe dropped Michael Elgin and Moose in a three-way.

Air Date: 30th January 2016
ROH TV Champion Roderick Strong defeated Masada; Luis Martinez defeated Colby Corino (Top Prospect Tournament – Quarter-Final); Bobby Fish and Kyle O’Reilly defeated Adam Cole and Jay Lethal.

Feb 2016

Air Date: 6th February 2016:
Alex Shelley defeated Frankie Kazarian; Dalton Castle dropped J. Diesel; Action Ortiz defeated Leon St. Giovanni (Top Prospect Tournament – Quarter-Final); Kenny King and Rhett Titus overturned ROH Tag Champions Hanson and Raymond Rowe by DQ.

Air Date: 13th February 2016:
Lio Rush beat Jason Kincaid (Top Prospect Tournament – Quarter-Final); Caprice Coleman and Will Ferrara downed Silas Young and Beer City Bruiser; Mark Briscoe dropped Tim Hughes; ACH and Matt Sydal beat Matt Jackson and Nick Jackson.

Air Date: 20th February 2016:
ROH TV Champion Roderick Strong defeated Jonathan Gresham; ROH Tag Champions Hanson and Raymond Rowe downed Bob Evans and Tim Hughes; Jay Briscoe and Mark Briscoe downed ACH and Matt Sydal.

Air Date: 27th February 2016
Silas Young and Beer City Bruiser rolled over Brandon Tate and Brent Tate; Brian Fury defeated Action Ortiz (Top Prospect 2006 Tournament – Semi-Final); Lio Rush defeated Luis Martinez (Top Prospect 2006 Tournament – Semi-Final); BJ Whitmer rolled over Chase Brown; Matt Sydal defeated Adam Cole.

March 2016

Air Date: 5th March 2016
Lio Rush defeated Brian Fury (Top Prospect 2006 Tournament – Final); Moose downed Kongo; Cheeseburger rolled over Mike Posey; Christopher Daniels overturned Dalton Castle.

Air Date: 12th March 2016
Berretta and Rocky Romero won a 7 team gauntlet match over Bob Evans and Tim Hughes, Bobby Fish and Kyle O’Reilly, Caprice Coleman and Will Ferrara, Chris LeRusso and J. Diesel, Frankie Kazarian and Christopher Daniels, and The Young Bucks.

Air Date: 19th March 2016
ROH TV Champion Tomohiro Ishii dropped Cedric Alexander; Mark and Jay Briscoe downed Adam Thornstowe and Luster the Legend; Hiroshi Tanahashi and Michael Elgin overcame The Young Bucks.

Air Date: 26th March 2016
Roderick Strong won a four-way over Adam Page, Moose and Matt Sydal; Cheeseburger battled to a no-contest with Foxx Vinyer; ROH Champion Jay Lethal beat Hirooki Goto.

April 2016

Air Date: 2nd April 2016
Bobby Fish and Kyle O’Reilly beat IWGP World Champion Kazuchika Okada and Gedo; Adam Cole downed IWGP Jr Champion Kushida in a non-title match; Alex Shelley and Chris Sabin overcame Frankie Kazarian and Christopher Daniels.

Air Date: 9th April 2016
ROH Tag Champions Ray Rowe and Hanson overcame Rocky Romero and Trent Baretta; IWGP Intercontinental Champion Kenny Omega defeated ACH; Dalton Castle downed Silas Young in a “Fight Without Honor”.

Air Date: 16th April 2016
Matt Sydal and Jushin Liger rolled over Dalton Castle and Ryusuke Taguchi; Delirious overturned Gedo; Tomohiro Ishii dropped ROH TV Champion Roderick Strong to win the title.

Air Date: 23rd April 2016
ROH Champion Jay Lethal and Tetsuya Naito bested IWGP World Champion Kazuchika Okada and Yoshi-Hashi; IWGP Jr Champion Kushida beat Kazarian; IWGP Intercontinental Champion Kenny Omega, and The Young Bucks overturned NEVER Champion Katsuyori Shibata, Bobby Fish and Kyle O’Reilly.

Air Date: 30th April 2016
Hiroshi Tanahashi, Tomoaki Honma, Moose and Michael Elgin overcame Bad Luck Fale, Tama Tonga, Yujiro Takahashi and Cody Hall; Jay Briscoe and Mark Briscoe downed Karl Anderson and Doc Gallows.
