ROH on Sinclair Results 2012

ROH ON SINCLAIR TV RESULTS 2012

Jan 2012

Air Date: 7th January 2012
Featured the Lethal/Perkins and Haas & Benjamin vs. Elgin & Strong matches  from ROH Southern Defiance on 12/03/2011. There were no original matches broadcast

Air Date: 14th January 2012
Featured the eight man elimination tag from ROH Northern Aggression on 12/04/2011. There were no original matches broadcast

Air Date: 21st January 2012
Eddie Edwards toppled Mike Mondo; Roderick Strong downed Ricky Reyes; Matt Jackson toppled Kenny King.

Air Date: 28th January 2012
Tommaso Ciampa dropped TJ Perkins; Michael Elgin overcame Grizzly Redwood; ROH Champion Davey Richards and Kyle O’Reilly bested Caprice Coleman and Cedric Alexander.

Feb 2012

Air Date: 4th February 2012
ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal beat Mike Bennett; ROH Tag Champions Mark and Jay Briscoe overcame Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas by DQ

Air Date: 11th February 2012
Eddie Edwards toppled Kyle O’Reilly; Mike Mondo downed Matt Taven by DQ; Michael Elgin and Roderick Strong rolled over ROH Tag Champions Mark and Jay Briscoe by DQ.

Air Date: 18th February 2012
Kevin Steen defeated Andy Ridge; Eddie Edwards and Adam Cole downed Matt Taven and Mike Mondo; Mark Briscoe dropped Charlie Haas.

Air Date: 25th February 2012
The Young Bucks overturned Caprice Coleman and Cedric Alexander; ROH Champion Davey Richards bested ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal.

Mar 2012

Air Date: 3rd March 2012
Mike Bennett toppled TJ Perkins; Tommaso Ciampa overcame Guy Alexander; Eddie Edwards battled Roderick Strong to a no-contest.

Air Date: 10th March 2012
ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal bested Kenny King; Kevin Steen toppled Adam Cole; Michael Elgin defeated Kyle O’Reilly.

Air Date: 17th March 2012
Adam Cole beat Chris Silvio; ROH TV Champion Jay Lethal toppled Roderick Strong (March Mayhem Qualifying match).

Air Date: 24th March 2012
Caprice Coleman and Cedric Alexander defeated Shelton Benjamin and Charlie Haas; Adam Cole downed Michael Elgin (March Madness Qualifying match).

Air Date: 31st March 2012
Tommaso Ciampa dropped Kyle O’Reilly (March Madness Qualifying match); ROH Champion Davey Richards bested Ryan McBride; Mike Bennett overturned Eddie Edwards (March Madness Qualifying match).

Apr 2012

Air Date: 7th April 2012
The Young Bucks beat TJ Perkins and Shiloh Jonze; Mike Mondo pinned Matt Taven; Tommaso Ciampa won a four-way over Adam Cole, Jay Lethal and Mike Bennett (March Madness Tournament final).

Air Date: 14th April 2012
Featured the Briscoes vs. Nicolls/Haste, Bennett/Storm and Steen/Generico matches from ROH Showdown in the Sun –  Night 1. There were no original matches broadcast.

Air Date: 21st April 2012
Featured the Briscoes vs. Haas/Benjamin, Perkins/Ant and Strong/Lethal matches from ROH Showdown in the Sun –  Night 2. There were no original matches broadcast.

Air Date: 28th April 2012
Caprice Coleman and Cedric Alexander beat ROH Tag Champions  Mark and Jay Briscoe in a non-title match; Rhino pinned Jimmy Marsalia; ROH TV Champion Roderick Strong bested Adam Cole.

May 2012

Air Date: 5th May 2012
Tommaso Ciampa beat Matt Taven; Mike Mondo downed TJ Perkins; Kevin Steen overturned Kyle O’Reilly.

Air Date: 12th May 2012
Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin overturned Body Snatcher and Black Ice; Fire Ant, Soldier Ant and Green Ant dropped Los Ice Creams and Ophidian; Jay Lethal pinned Ricky Reyes; ROH Champion Davey Richards rolled over Rhino.

Air Date: 19th May 2012
Eddie Edwards downed Mike Bennett; Grizzly Redwood and Jamal Dickinson overturned Michael Elgin by DQ; Kenny King and Rhett Titus bested The Young Bucks in an elimination match. 

Air Date: 26th May 2012
Featured the Richards/Steen match from ROH Border Wars. There were no original matches broadcast.

June 2012

Air Date: 2nd June 2012
Adam Cole beat Mike Mondo; Tommaso Ciampa defeated Nick Westgate; Kenny King and Rhett Titus downed Cedric Alexander and Caprice Coleman.

Air Date: 9th June 2012
Michael Elgin bested Matt Taven; Eddie Edwards toppled Davey Richards.

Air Date: 16th June 2012
Mike Bennett defeated Adam Page; Mark and Jay Briscoe overturned QT Marshall and Sam Shaw; ROH TV Champion Roderick Strong dropped Tommaso Ciampa by DQ.

Air Date: 23rd June 2012
Eddie Edwards beat Brutal Bob Evans; Jay Lethal bested Chris Silvio; Davey Richards and Kyle O’Reilly battled ROH Champion Kevin Steen and Jimmy Jacobs to a no-contest.

Air Date: 30th June 2012
Davey Richards battled Mike Mondo to a draw; Adam Cole overcame BJ Whitmer, Kyle O’Reilly and Roderick Strong in a four-way; Jay Briscoe toppled Charlie Haas in a Texas Death match.

July 2012

Air Date: 7th July 2012
Jay Lethal bested Michael Elgin; ROH Champion Kevin Steen rolled over Eddie Edwards.

Air Date: 14th July 2012
Featured the Elgin/Finlay, and Strong/Lethal/Ciampa matches from ROH Hostage Crisis. There were no original matches broadcast.

Air Date: 21st July 2012
Adam Cole and Eddie Edwards beat Mike Bennett and Brutal Bob Evans; Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin defeated Damien Dragon and Matt Saigon; ROH Champion Kevin Steen bested Mike Mondo.

Air Date: 28th July 2012
Rhino beat Rocco Abruzzi; Tomasso Ciampa downed Mike Sydal; Adam Cole toppled ROH TV Champion Roderick Strong to win the title.

Aug 2012

Air Date: 4th August 2012

Air Date: 11th August 2012
Kyle O’Reilly beat Matt Taven; Rhino defeated Todd Sople; Harlem and Lance Bravado toppled Michael Elgin and Roderick Strong; Jay Lethal and ROH Tag Champions Kenny King and Rhett Titus rolled over ROH Champion Kevin Steen, Steve Corino and Jimmy Jacobs.

Air Date: 18th August 2012
Sara Del Rey beat Mia Yim; Rhino defeated Eddie Edwards in an “Anything Goes” encounter; ROH Champion Kevin Steen bested Homicide.

Air Date: 25th August 2012
Cedric Alexander and Caprice Coleman downed The Young Bucks (ROH Tag Title Tournament – First Round); QT Marshall bested Tadarius Thomas; Mike Mondo overcame Tomasso Ciampa, Roderick Strong, Jay Lethal, Mike Bennett and TJ Perkins in a six-way.
