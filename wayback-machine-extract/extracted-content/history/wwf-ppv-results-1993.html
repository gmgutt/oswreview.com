WWF PPV Results 1993

WWF PPV RESULTS (1993)

See also: WWF PPV Statistics 1990-1993

24th January 1993: “WWF Royal Rumble 1993”: Sacramento, CA – 16,000 sellout (15,000 paid)
($187,000 live gate)
Dark match: Doink beat Jim Powers.
PPV Broadcast: Rick and Scott Steiner defeated The Beverly Brothers; WWF Intercontinental Champion Shawn Michaels pinned Marty Jannetty; Bam Bam Bigelow dropped The Big Bossman; WWF World Champion Bret Hart rolled over Razor Ramon; Yokozuna won the 30-man Royal Rumble match, last eliminating Randy Savage.

4th April 1993: “WWF Wrestlemania IX”: Las Vegas, NV – 15,045 (14,000 paid)
($1,100,000 live gate)
Dark match: Tito Santana beat Papa Shango.
PPV Broadcast: Tatanka dropped WWF Intercontinental Champion Shawn Michaels by count-out; The Steiners defeated The Headshrinkers; Doink downed Crush; Razor Ramon overcame Bob Backlund; WWF World Tag Champions Ted Dibiase and IRS bested Hulk Hogan and Brutus Beefcake by DQ; Lex Luger pinned Curt Hennig; The Undertaker toppled Giant Gonzalez by DQ; Yokozuna rolled over WWF World Champion Bret Hart to win the title; Hulk Hogan pinned WWF World Champion Yokozuna to win the title.

13th June 1993: “WWF King of the Ring 1993”: Dayton, OH – 8,000 sellout (7,000 paid)
($78,000 live gate)
Dark match: Papa Shango beat Owen Hart.
PPV Broadcast: Bret Hart dropped Razor Ramon (KOTR Tournament- Quarter-Final); Curt Hennig downed Mr Hughes by DQ (KOTR Tournament- Quarter-Final); Bam Bam Bigelow bested Jim Duggan (KOTR Tournament- Quarter-Final); Tatanka battled Lex Luger to a draw (KOTR Tournament- Quarter-Final); Bret Hart dropped Curt Hennig (KOTR Tournament – Semi-Final); Yokozuna pinned WWF World Champion Hulk Hogan to win the title; The Smoking Gunns and The Steiners overcame The Headshrinkers and WWF World Tag Champions Ted Dibiase and IRS; WWF Intercontinental Champion Shawn Michaels toppled Crush; Bret Hart rolled over Bam Bam Bigelow (KOTR Tournament – Final).

30th August 1993: “WWF Summerslam 1993”: Auburn Hills, MI – 23,594 sellout
($215,000 live gate)
Dark match: Owen Hart defeated Barry Horowitz.
PPV Broadcast: Razor Ramon overcame Ted Dibiase; WWF World Tag Champions The Steiners defeated The Heavenly Bodies; WWF Intercontinental Champion Shawn Michaels downed Curt Hennig by count-out; IRS pinned The 1-2-3 Kid; Bret Hart dropped Doink by DQ; Jerry Lawler toppled Bret Hart by reverse decision-DQ; Ludvig Borga toppled Marty Jannetty; The Undertaker bested The Giant Gonzalez in a “Rest in Peace” match; Tatanka and The Smoking Gunns defeated Bam Bam Bigelow and The Headshrinkers; Lex Luger rolled over WWF World Champion Yokozuna by count-out.

24th November 1993: “WWF Survivor Series 1993”: Boston, MA – 15,509 sellout
($180,000 live gate)
Dark match: Billy Gunn pinned The Brooklyn Brawler.
PPV Broadcast: WWF Intercontinental Champion Razor Ramon, Randy Savage, The 1-2-3 Kid, and Marty Jannetty defeated IRS, Rick Martel, Adam Bomb and Diesel in a Survivors elimination match; Bret Hart, Owen Hart, Bruce Hart, and Keith Hart downed Shawn Michaels, The Black Knight (Jeff Gaylord), The Blue Knight (Greg Valentine), and The Red Knight (Barry Horowitz) in a Survivors elimination match; The Heavenly Bodies dropped SMW Tag Champions The Rock and Roll Express to win the titles; Men on a Mission and The Bushwhackers bested Bam Bam Bigelow, Bastian Booger, and The Headshrinkers in a Survivors elimination match; Lex Luger, The Undertaker and The Steiners rolled over WWF World Champion Yokozuna, Ludvig Borga, Crush, and Jacques Rougeau in a Survivors elimination match.
