WWE Superstars Results 2010

WWE SUPERSTARS RESULTS (2010)

Jan 2010

Air Date: 7th January 2010
Gail Jim defeated Jillian Hall (WWE Divas Title Tournament – Quarter Final); Ted Dibiase Jr toppled Chris Masters; Yoshi Tatsu bested Caylen Croft; Finlay defeated Mike Knox.

Air Date: 14th January 2010
Primo and Gail Kim bested Chavo Guerrero Jr and Jillian Hall; Chris Masters dropped Carlito; Slam Master J and Jimmy Wang Yang dropped Mike Knox and Charlie Haas; ECW Champion Christian bested William Regal.

Air Date: 21st January 2010
Kelly Kelly downed Jillian Hall; Shelton Benjamin overcame Vance Archer; Kane toppled Chris Jericho by count-out.

Air Date: 28th January 2010
The Bella Twins overcame Jillian Hall and Katie Lea; Chris Masters bested Chavo Guerrero Jr and Primo Colon in a Triple Threat match; DH Smith dropped Matt Hardy.

Feb 2010

Air Date: 4th February 2010
Alicia Fox and Katie Lea overcame Kelly Kelly and Eve; Evan Bourne bested Carlito; John Morrison, Matt Hardy and The Great Khali defeated WWE Intercontinental Champion Drew McIntyre, DH Smith and Tyson Kidd.

Air Date: 11th February 2010
Jack Swagger bested Santino Marella; ECW Champion Christian bested Zack Ryder; Dolph Ziggler overcame JTG.

Air Date: 18th February 2010
Evan Bourne and Primo downed Carlito and Chavo Guerrero; Gail Kim toppled Katie Lea; Rey Mysterio overturned Mike  Knox.

Air Date: 25th February 2010
William Regal and Jack Swagger downed Santino Marella and Chris Masters; Zack Ryder beat Primo; Luke Gallows downed Silas Young; Tyson Kidd, DH Smith and Natalya Neidhart bested Matt Hardy, The Great Khali and Maria.

Mar 2010

Air Date: 4th March 2010
Kofi Kingston beat Chavo Guerrero; Evan Bourne pinned William Regal; Kane toppled Mike Knox; World Heavyweight Champion Chris Jericho downed Goldust.

Air Date: 11th March 2010
Zack Ryder beat Santino Marella; Christian and MVP overturned Carlito and Chavo Guerrero; Dolph Ziggler downed Shelton Benjamin; Goldust overcame Mike Knox.

Air Date: 18th March 2010
The Bella Twins overcame Jillian Hall and Katie Lea; Christian, Mark Henry and Yoshi Tatsu defeated Jack Swagger, William Regal and Zack Ryder; Kane toppled Shelton Benjamin.

Air Date: 25th March 2010
CM Punk pinned JTG; Rey Mysterio overcame Tyson Kidd.

Apr 2010

Air Date: 1st April 2010
MVP and Kofi Kingston overturned Carlito and Zack Ryder; Yoshi Tatsu beat William Regal; DH Smith and Tyson Kidd downed Caylen Croft and Trent Barretta.

Air Date: 8th April 2010
Evan Bourne and Yoshi Tatsu dropped Chavo Guerrero and Zack Ryder; MVP downed Carlito; Beth Phoenix dropped Layla.

Air Date: 15th April 2010
Christian downed Chavo Guerrero; Ted Dibiase Jr bested MVP; Matt Hardy overcame Carlito.

Air Date: 22nd April 2010
Yoshi Tatsu bested Zack Ryder; John Morrison downed Carlito; Shad Gaspard downed Goldust.

Air Date: 29th April 2010
Gail Kim dropped Jillian Hall; Zack Ryder pinned Primo; WWE Unified World Tag Champions DH Smith and Tyson Kidd downed Caylen Croft and Trent Barretta.

May 2010

Air Date: 6th May 2010
Carlito battled Primo to a no-contest; Chris Masters dropped Chavo Guerrero; WWE Women’s Champion Beth Phoenix bested Rosa Mendes; Shad Gaspard overcame JTG.

Air Date: 13th May 2010
Jillian Hall and Maryse beat The Bella Twins; Vladimir Kozlov defeated Ryan Mitchell; Curt Hawkins and Vance Archer defeated James White and Matthew Busch; Dolph Ziggler beat Chris Masters.

Air Date: 20th May 2010
Vladimir Kozlov defeated Santino Marella; Goldust downed William Regal; Cody Rhodes beat Chris Masters; Christian defeated Dolph Ziggler.

Air Date: 27th May 2010
Cody Rhodes defeated JTG; Yoshi Tatsu overcame Primo; Zack Ryder toppled Evan Bourne.

Jun 2010

Air Date: 3rd June 2010
Mark Henry defeated Primo; Gail Kim downed Jillian Hall; Michelle McCool defeated Tiffany; Chavo Guerrero and Dolph Ziggler downed Christian in a handicap match.

Air Date: 10th June 2010
Alicia Fox beat Gail Kim; Primo and Zack Ryder defeated Goldust and Yoshi Tatsu; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston dropped Luke Gallows.

Air Date: 17th June 2010
Primo beat Yoshi Tatsu; Jimmy and Jey Uso defeated Mark Henry and Goldust; WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston bested Trent Barretta.

Air Date: 24th June 2010
JTG beat Caylen Croft; Luke Gallows defeated Chris Masters; Brie Bella defeated Jillian Hall; R-Truth downed William Regal.

Jul 2010

Air Date: 1st July 2010
Zack Ryder and Primo beat Goldust and Yoshi Tatsu; Nikki Bella defeated Jillian Hall; Christian defeated Curt Hawkins.

Air Date: 8th July 2010
Yoshi Tatsu and Gail Kim defeated Primo and Jillian Hall. The Bella Twins were guest referees; Zack Ryder downed Goldust; WWE Women’s Champion Layla and Michelle McCool defeated Kelly Kelly and Tiffany.

Air Date: 15th July 2010
Chavo Guerrero beat JTG; Vance Archer and Curt Hawkins defeated Matt Cross and Jamin Canseco; The Bella Twins beat Maryse and Jillian Hall; Chris Jericho defeated Yoshi Tatsu.

Air Date: 22nd July 2010
MVP and Chris Masters beat Trent Baretta and Caylen Croft; Chavo Guerrero dropped JTG; Primo defeated Yoshi Tatsu; WWE Unified World Tag Champions DH Smith and Tyson Kidd downed Jimmy and Jey Uso.

Air Date: 29th July 2010
Santino Marella and Vladimir Kozlov beat William Regal and Primo; Caylen Croft defeated Chris Masters; MVP downed Chavo Guerrero.

Aug 2010

Air Date: 5th August 2010
WWE Women’s Champion Layla beat Tiffany; Curt Hawkins and Vance Archer defeated Caylen Croft and Trent Barreta; Evan Bourne downed  Zack Ryder.

Air Date: 12th August 2010
Jimmy and Jey Uso beat Yoshi Tatsu and Goldust; Chavo Guerrero defeated Chris Masters; Michelle McCool downed Kelly Kelly.

Air Date: 19th August 2010
Yoshi Tatsu beat Primo; MVP battled Vance Archer to a no-contest; Curt Hawkins and Vance Archer defeated JTG and MVP; Jack Swagger bested Chris Masters.

Air Date: 26th August 2010
William Regal defeated Goldust; Trent Baretta and Caylen Croft downed Vance Archer and Curt Hawkins; Drew McIntyre beat Chris Masters.

Sep 2010

Air Date: 2nd September 2010
Ted Dibiase Jr defeated Goldust; Santino Marella beat Jimmy Uso; Kofi Kingston defeated Chavo Guerrero.

Air Date: 9th September 2010
R-Truth defeated William Regal; Goldust and Gail Kim defeated Primo and Jillian Hall; Trent Baretta and Caylen Croft defeated Vance Archer and Curt Hawkins; The Big Show overturned Luke Gallows.

Air Date: 16th September 2010
Vladimir Kozlov and Santino Marella defeated Jimmy and Jey Uso; The Great Khali overcame Primo; MVP overcame Luke Gallows.

Air Date: 23rd September 2010
Primo beat JTG; Goldust downed William Regal.

Air Date: 30th September 2010
The Bella Twins beat Alicia Fox and Jillian Hall; Kofi Kingston downed Jack Swagger by DQ.

Oct 2010

Air Date: 7th October 2010
Chris Masters dropped Vance Archer; William Regal beat Darren Young; Jimmy and Jey Uso defeated DH Smith and Tyson Kidd.

Air Date: 14th October 2010
Kelly Kelly overcame Layla; Curt Hawkins overcame Trent Barreta; Darren Young beat Primo; Melina defeated Alicia Fox.

Air Date: 21st October 2010
Vladimir Kozlov and Santino Marella beat Jimmy and Jey Uso; William Regal defeated Darren Young; WWE Unified World Tag Champions Drew McIntyre and Cody Rhodes overcame MVP and Chris Masters.

Air Date: 28th October 2010
Mark Henry beat Jey Uso; Zack Ryder defeated Tyson Kidd; MVP downed Curt Hawkins; Tyler Reks dropped Chris Masters.

Nov 2010


Air Date: 4th November 2010
Gail Kim toppled Alicia Fox; Jimmy and Jey Uso overturned Mark Henry in a handicap match; Jack Swagger overcame Kaval; Luke Gallows defeated Vance Archer.

Air Date: 11th November 2010
Yoshi Tatsu bested Zack Ryder; Ezekiel Jackson toppled Primo Colon; Drew McIntyre downed Kaval.

Air Date: 18th November 2010
Yoshi Tatsu dropped Primo Colon; Melina and Gail Kim overcame Maryse and Alicia Fox; Chavo Guerrero downed JTG; Trent Barretta overcame Curt Hawkins.

Air Date: 25th November 2010
Santino Marella and Vladimir Kozlov beat Zack Ryder and Primo Colon; Mark Henry defeated Jimmy and Jey Uso in a handicap match; Curt Hawkins overcame Trent Barretta.

Dec 2010

Air Date: 2nd December 2010
William Regal defeated Darren Young; DH Smith dropped Tyson Kidd; Tyler Reks beat Trent Barretta.

Air Date: 9th December 2010
Ezekiel Jackson beat Zack Ryder; Eve bested Alicia Fox; Tyler Reks downed JTG; Trent Barretta overturned Curt Hawkins.

Air Date: 16th December 2010
Zack Ryder beat Darren Young; Drew McIntyre toppled Yoshi Tatsu; JTG bested Curt Hawkins.

Air Date: 23rd December 2010
DH Smith pinned Zack Ryder; Jimmy and Jey Uso overcame Yoshi Tatsu and Darren Young; Beth Phoenix and Kelly Kelly overturned Michelle McCool and Layla.

Air Date: 30th December 2010
Jimmy and Jey Uso beat Yoshi Tatsu and Darren Young; William Regal downed DH Smith; Tyler Reks bested Chris Masters.
