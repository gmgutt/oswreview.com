WWF Superstars Results 1993

WWF SUPERSTARS RESULTS (1993)

Jan 1993

Air Date: 2nd January 1993
Curt Hennig bested The Berzerker; Yokozuna toppled George Anderson; Marty Jannetty pinned Tom Stone; Damien Demento downed Mitch Bishop; Crush defeated Dave Sigfrieds.

Air Date: 9th January 1993
Kamala beat Todd Becker; The Steiner dropped Rock Werner and Butch Banks; Lance Cassidy defeated George Petrovsky; The Undertaker pinned Scott Zappa; Bam Bam Bigelow overcame Red Tyler.

Air Date: 16th January 1993
Razor Ramon pinned Buck Zumhoff; The Nasty Boys defeated Mickey Jay and Rock Warner; Crush downed WT Jones; Yokozuna dropped Carl Almont; Tatanka rolled over Tom Bennett.

Air Date: 23rd January 1993
The Undertaker pinned Dave Silguero; Marty Jannetty defeated Ken Johnson; Papa Shango downed Rudy Gonzalez; Bam Bam Bigelow bested Gary Jackson; Kamala beat Dale Wolfe.

Air Date: 30th January 1993
The Headshrinkers defeated Reno Riggins and Bobby Perez; Damien Demento pinned Tommy Knight; Curt Hennig downed Louie Spicolli; WWF Intercontinental Champion Shawn Michaels rolled over Gary Jackson; Owen Hart and Koko B Ware beat The Brooklyn Brawler and Chuck West; Bob Backlund overturned Iron Mike Sharpe; Razor Ramon overcame Pete Christie.

Feb 1993

Air Date: 6th February 1993
The Nasty Boys defeated Dave Silguero and Robert Taylor; Bam Bam Bigelow bested Rudi Flores; Doink overcame Reno Riggins; Jim Duggan dropped Yokozuna in a challenge where Duggan had to knock Yokozuna off his feet; The Beverly Brothers beat Pete Christie and Bob East.

Air Date: 13th February 1993
Tatanka downed WWF Intercontinental Champion Shawn Michaels in a non-title match; Rick and Scott Steiner defeated Alan Burke and Larry Sampson; Lex Luger pinned Larry Ludden; Crush beat The White Shadow; Giant Gonzalez crushed Louie Spicolli, Scott Bazo and Dan Farren by count-out in a handicap match.

Air Date: 20th February 1993
Razor Ramon overcame Buddy Wayne; Curt Hennig downed Bobby Young; Bam Bam Bigelow bested Earthquake by count-out; Kamala beat Mike Starr; The Undertaker squashed Raven Clark.

Air Date: 27th February 1993
Yokozuna crushed Brian West; The Nasty Boys defeated Mark Ming and Jim Gorman; Lex Luger pinned Jim Powers; The Big Bossman bested Doink by reverse decision-DQ.

Mar 1993

Air Date: 6th March 1993
Razor Ramon overcame Brian Boyer; Crush beat Louie Spicolli; The Headshrinkers defeated Gary Key and Davey Starr; Bam Bam Bigelow bested Brian West; The Steiners overcame Bill Marcetti and Dan Farren (a masked Beau Beverly).

Air Date: 13th March 1993
Papa Shango beat Mark Ming; The Nasty Boys defeated Ed Arce and The White Shadow; Yokozuna squashed Casey Cleric; Tatanka downed Al Burke; Lex Luger pinned Joey Maggs.

Air Date: 20th March 1993
WWF World Tag Champions Ted Dibiase and IRS beat Alan Reid and Robert Thompson; Kamala defeated Jim Gorman; WWF Intercontinental Champion Shawn Michaels rolled over Joey Maggs; Bob Backlund overturned Larry Ludden; Doink overcame Larry Sampson.

Air Date: 27th March 1993
Crush beat Lee Armstrong; The Headshrinkers won a squash; The Nasty Boys defeated Duane Gill and Barry Hardy; Lex Luger won a squash.

Apr 1993

Air Date: 3rd April 1993
Curt Hennig downed Duane Gill; Bam Bam Bigelow bested Glen Ruth; Tatanka downed Barry Hardy; Doink overcame Dale Wolfe; Giant Gonzalez defeated Virgil; The Steiners won a squash; Giant Gonzalez toppled Virgil.

Air Date: 10th April 1993
Jim Duggan pinned Barry Horowitz; Papa Shango beat David Clements; Tito Santana defeated The Brooklyn Brawler; Lex Luger downed Dale Wolfe; Kamala dropped Duane Gill; Bob Backlund overturned Anthony Howard.

Air Date: 17th April 1993
Crush beat Glen Ruth; The Headshrinkers defeated Ron Preston and JD Stryker; Curt Hennig downed Barry Hardy; Bam Bam Bigelow bested Owen Hart; Yokozuna pinned Chris Katowski.

Air Date: 24th April 1993
WWF Intercontinental Champion Shawn Michaels rolled over Dale Wolfe; The Steiners defeated Reno Riggins and Billy Jones; Tito Santana pinned Louie Spicolli; Damien Demento beat KC Cleric; Jim Duggan overcame Tim Patterson; Mr Hughes pinned Raven Clark.

May 1993

Air Date: 1st May 1993
The Headshrinkers defeated Scott Bazo and Bob Young; Virgil toppled The White Shadow; Curt Hennig battled Doink to a draw (KOTR Qualifier); Typhoon pinned Larry Ludden.

Air Date: 8th May 1993
Mr Hughes, and WWF World Tag Champions Ted Dibiase and IRS defeated Kamala and The Nasty Boys; Yokozuna downed Sean Dakota; Crush beat Steve Vega; Razor Ramon overcame Tito Santana (KOTR Qualifier).

Air Date: 15th May 1993
The Steiners defeated Mark Ming and Jim Armstrong; Mr Hughes pinned Bobby Young; Bob Backlund overturned Terry Zeller; Virgil toppled Jose Martinez; WWF Intercontinental Champion Shawn Michaels rolled over Billy Jones; Tatanka downed Giant Gonzalez by DQ (KOTR Qualifier).

Air Date: 22nd May 1993
The Smoking Gunns defeated Duane Gill and Chris Duffy; Bam Bam Bigelow bested Jerry Seavey; Crush battled WWF Intercontinental Champion Shawn Michaels to a double count-out (KOTR Qualifier); Adam Bomb beat Burt Centeno; Jim Duggan pinned The Brooklyn Brawler.

Air Date: 29th May 1993
Tatanka downed Ricky Rich; Doink overcame Kevin Armstrong; Yokozuna pinned Tony Roy; The Steiners defeated Mike Bell and Tony Devito; Curt Hennig downed Rich Myers.

Jun 1993

Air Date: 5th June 1993
Bret Hart dropped Papa Shango; The Headshrinkers defeated Tim McNeany and Scott Taylor; Kamala pinned Phil Apollo; Lex Luger bested Jason Knight; Mr Hughes beat Russ Greenberg; The Smoking Gunns defeated Gus Kantarakis and Dale Wolfe.

Air Date: 12th June 1993
The Steiners defeated Iron Mike Sharpe and Ralph LaRue; Razor Ramon overcame Burt Centeno; Jim Duggan pinned Mike Bell; The Undertaker toppled PJ Walker; WWF World Tag Champions Ted Dibiase and IRS overcame Tony Ray and Buddy Lane.

Air Date: 19th June 1993
The Smoking Gunns beat Reno Riggins and Scott Taylor; Virgil toppled Bastion Booger; Tatanka downed Mike Davis; Lex Luger pinned Phil Apollo; Crush beat Joey Tempest; Adam Bomb overturned Jerry Seavey.

Air Date: 26th June 1993
Mr Hughes pinned Tito Santana; Doink overcame Scott Dane; The Headshrinkers downed Buddy Lenny and Mike Davis; Bastion Booger bested Virgil; Curt Hennig downed Burt Satano.

Jul 1993

Air Date: 3rd July 1993
Marty Jannetty beat Tom Stone; Lex Luger pinned Rich Myers; WWF World Tag Champions The Steiners defeated Barry Horowitz and Brian Costello; Bam Bam Bigelow toppled Rudi Gonzalez; Adam Bomb overcame Jay Sledge; The 1-2-3 Kid toppled Reno Riggins.

Air Date: 10th July 1993
The Smoking Gunns dropped WT Jones and Rock Werner; Crush toppled Doink by count-out; WWF Intercontinental Champion Shawn Michaels beat Kevin Kruger; Men on a Mission defeated Mitch Bishop and Chad Miller.

Air Date: 17th July 1993
Tatanka downed Brian Costello; Adam Bomb beat Rich Myers; WWF World Tag Champions The Steiners downed Ted Dibiase and IRS by reverse decision-DQ; Mr Hughes pinned Sonny Rogers.

Air Date: 24th July 1993
Jim Duggan pinned Duane Gill; Ludvig Borga beat Tony Dimauro; WWF World Tag Champions The Steiners defeated Ted Dibiase and IRS by count-out; The 1-2-3 Kid downed Barry Horowitz; The Quebecars dropped Tony Webb and Rich Myers.

Air Date: 31st July 1993
Men on a Mission defeated The Brooklyn Brawler and Brian Costello; Adam Bomb downed Tony DeVito; Mr Hughes pinned Jason Headings; The Headshrinkers beat Dave Thornberg and Bret Tyler; Curt Hennig downed Glen Ruth; Sensational Sherri battled Luna Vachon to a no-contest.

Aug 1993

Air Date: 7th August 1993
The Smoking Gunns defeated Well Dunn; Adam Bomb downed Chaz Ware; Doink overcame Joey Maggs; Tito Santana pinned Barry Hardy; Bastion Booger bested Mike Bell.

Air Date: 14th August 1993
Razor Ramon overcame Duane Gill; Ludvig Borga dropped Mike Khoury; WWF World Tag Champions The Steiners defeated Glen Ruth and Mike Bell; WWF Intercontinental Champion Shawn Michaels bested Darren Trujillo; Tatanka downed Fred Williams; The Heavenly Bodies dropped Bert Centeno and Rich Meyers.

Air Date: 21st August 1993
Marty Jannetty pinned Barry Horowitz; The Quebecers defeated Dan Dubiel and Scott Despres; Adam Bomb beat Mike Moraldo; Mr Hughes overcame Mike Bucci; Bam Bam Bigelow downed Bobby Who; Men on a Mission dropped Iron Mike Sharpe and Jeff Libolt.

Air Date: 28th August 1993
The 1-2-3 Kid pinned The Brooklyn Brawler; The Heavenly Bodies defeated Tony Roy and Jason Headings; Ludvig Borga downed Virgil; Curt Hennig dropped Duane Gill; The Smoking Gunns defeated Reno Riggins and Barry Hardy.

Sep 1993

Air Date: 4th September 1993
The Quebecers defeated Mike Khoury and Aaron Ferguson; Doink overcame Mike Moraldo; Men on a Mission beat Russ Greenburg and Mark Thomas; Bastion Booger pinned Ralph Mosca; Razor Ramon overcame Reno Riggins; WWF World Tag Champions The Steiners toppled Fred Williams and The Brooklyn Brawler.

Air Date: 11th September 1993
The Headshrinkers defeated Marty Jannetty and Virgil; IRS pinned Jeff LiBolt; Tatanka downed Damien Demento; Adam Bomb bested Ron Neal.

Air Date: 18th September 1993
Bret Hart dropped Blake Beverly; Well Dunn defeated Mark Thomas and Russ Greenberg; Razor Ramon overcame Max Shane; Ludvig Borga bested PJ Walker; Curt Hennig downed Barry Horowitz; WWF Intercontinental Champion Shawn Michaels overturned Dan Dubiel; The Smoking Gunns defeated Mike Bell and Tony DeVito.

Air Date: 25th September 1993
Adam Bomb pinned Kevin Kruger; Razor Ramon overcame Brian Costello; Bastion Booger beat Todd Becker; The 1-2-3 Kid downed Jack Reno; Tatanka downed Barry Horowitz; The Smoking Gunns defeated Tom Stone and Dan Rubins.

Oct 1993

Air Date: 2nd October 1993
The Steiners defeated Chad Miller and Tom Bennett; Ludvig Borga dropped Sonny Rogers; IRS pinned Gary Jackson; Bam Bam Bigelow beat Rod Bell; Marty Jannetty overturned Steve Moore; The Heavenly Bodies downed Reno Riggins and John Paul.

Air Date: 9th October 1993
Men on a Mission defeated Damien Demento and an unknown; Adam Bomb pinned Mitch Bishop; Curt Hennig downed Red Tyler; Well Dunn dropped Virgil and Jerry Fox; The Undertaker toppled Rod Bell; The Headshrinkers overcame Steve Nixon and Tony Bithera.

Air Date: 16th October 1993
Tatanka downed Mike Bell; Ludvig Borga defeated Tony Roy; The 1-2-3 Kid pinned Pat Tanaka; Owen Hart beat Scott Taylor; Mike Davis and Jerry Seavey dropped WWF World Tag Champions The Quebecers by DQ.

Air Date: 23rd October 1993
Doink overcame Barry Horowitz; Bastion Booger pinned Raymond Roy; IRS beat Ken Garrison; SMW Tag Champions Ricky Morton and Robert Gibson dropped Flex Armstrong and Richie Rich; Diesel squashed Ralph Mosca; WWF Intercontinental Champion Razor Ramon overcame Tony Devito.

Air Date: 30th October 1993
The Smoking Gunns defeated The Executioners; Bam Bam Bigelow beat Tim McNeany; The Steiners downed Bert Centeno and Chris Duffy; The Heavenly Bodies bested PJ Walker and Mark Thomas; Ludvig Borga pinned Tatanka.

Nov 1993

Air Date: 6th November 1993
Rick Steiner beat Jacques Rougeau; Adam Bomb bested Rich Myers; Crush toppled Gary Jackson; The 1-2-3 Kid pinned Tony DeVito; The Headshrinkers overturned Greg Hatfield and Cohito; Adam Bomb defeated Marty Jannetty by count-out.

Air Date: 13th November 1993
Men on a Mission downed Iron Mike Sharpe and Chuck Greenman; Ludvig Borga defeated Dan Dubiel; Bastion Booger pinned Joe Brenan; WWF Intercontinental Champion Razor Ramon overcame Rick Martel by count-out; Diesel dropped Chaz Ware.

Air Date: 20th November 1993
The Smoking Gunns defeated Phil Apollo and Mike Bell; Bam Bam Bigelow beat Scott Taylor; Owen Hart pinned Iron Mike Sharpe; Well Dunn downed PJ Walker and Dennis Diamond; Bret Hart dropped IRS by DQ.

Air Date: 27th November 1993
The Steiners downed Steve Smith and Lavern Gill; WWF Intercontinental Champion Razor Ramon dropped Mike Khoury; Adam Bomb defeated Mike Bucci; Shawn Michaels overturned Dan Dubiel; Doink rolled over Sid Garrison; Crush overcame Paul Jones.

Dec 1993

Air Date: 4th December 1993
Ludvig Borga beat John Paul; Bob Backlund overcame Iron Mike Sharpe; The Headshrinkers dropped Greg Hatfield and Todd Mata; The 1-2-3 Kid downed Glen Ruth; Diesel pinned Frank Kanyak; Men on a Misson overcame Steve Smith and Gus Kantarakis; Marty Jannetty bested Reno Riggins.

Air Date: 11th December 1993
WWF World Tag Champions The Quebecers downed Paul Jones and Chaz Ware; Bastion Booger pinned Tony Webb; IRS beat Mike Moraldo; Tatanka bested The Brooklyn Brawler; The Smoking Gunns defeated Corey Student and Joe Brennon; Rick Martel toppled JS Storm.

Air Date: 18th December 1993
Bam Bam Bigelow bested PJ Walker; The 1-2-3 Kid dropped Duane Gill; Jeff Jarrett downed Chris Duffy; Crush beat Ross Greenberg; The Steiners defeated The Brooklyn Brawler and Tony DeVito.

Air Date: 25th December 1993
Shawn Michaels rolled over Phil Apollo; Men on a Mission defeated Iron Mike Sharpe and Mike Bell; Adam Bomb beat Brian Walsh; Doink overcame Reno Riggins; IRS defeated Ralph Mosca.
