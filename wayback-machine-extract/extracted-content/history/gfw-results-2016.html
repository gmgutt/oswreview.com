GFW Results 2016

Jan 2016

22nd January 2016: Poughkeepsie, NY
Cody Deaner defeated Cliff Compton and Henry Maxwell in a three-way; Desirable Dustin and Gigolo Justin bested Alex Reynolds and Kyle Reynolds; GFW Women’s Champion Christina Von Eerie won a three-way over Kimber Lee and Mickie James; Colt Cabana pinned Jeff Jarrett; GFW Nex*Gen Champion Sonjay Dutt overcame Jigsaw; Andrew Everett, Brian Myers and GFW Global Champion Nick Aldis defeat Kevin Matthews, Pat Buck and The Amazing Red.

30th January 2016: “PWS vs. GFW”: Rahway, NJ
Joint show with PWS
Habib From The Car Wash defeated Sonjay Dutt; Pepper Parks defeated Cliff Compton; PWS TV Champion Brian Myers defeated Chris Payne in a “falls-count-anywhere” match; PWS Tag Champions Craig Steele and Sandy Mann won a three-way over Beefcake Charlie and Damian Gibbs, and Alex Reynolds and Kyle Reynolds; Anthony Bowens defeated Bobby Wayward, CPA, Maxwell J. Feinstein, Nikos Rikos and The Drunken Swashbuckler in a six-way; Matt Macintosh defeated Dan Maff; Team PWS (Fallah Bahh, Kevin Matthews, Mario Bokara and Pat Buck) beat Team GFW (Desirable Dustin, Gigolo Justin, Jeff Jarrett and Nick Aldis).
