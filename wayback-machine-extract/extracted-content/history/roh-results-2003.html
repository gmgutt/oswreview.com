ROH Results 2003

Jan 2003

11th January 2003: “ROH Revenge on the Prophecy”: West Mifflin, PA – 300
Chad Collyer defeated Matt Stryker; CM Punk pinned Michael Shane; Konnan, Chris Devine and Quiet Storm downed Ghost Shadow, Joel and Jose Maximo; Mafia and Monsta Mack dropped HC Loc and Tony DeVito in a weapons match; Jay Lethal, Yeyo, Angel Dust and Deranged overcame Diablo Santiago, Oman Turtoga, Marcos and Dunn in a scramble match; Paul London beat BJ Whitmer, Homicide and Colt Cabana in a four-way; Samoa Joe toppled Bryan Danielson; Low Ki and AJ Styles rolled over ROH Champion Xavier and Christopher Daniels in a no holds barred match.

Feb 2003

8th February 2003: “ROH 1st Anniversary Show”: Queens, NY – 700
EZ Money defeated Chad Collyer, Michael Shane and Colt Cabana in a four-way; Fast Eddie, Don Juan and Hotstuff Hernandez downed HC Loc, Tony DeVito and Masada by DQ; Jay Briscoe pinned Mark Briscoe; Steve Corino defeated Homicide; CM Punk beat CW Anderson; Bryan Danielson bested Samoa Joe; Paul London downed AJ Styles and Low Ki in a three-way; ROH Champion Xavier pinned Paul London; Mikey Whipwreck, Mafia and Monster Mack, and Jose and Joel Maximo, Chris Devine and Quiet Storm overturned Special K in a scramble match.

Mar 2003

15th March 2003: “ROH Expect the Unexpected”: Cambridge, MA – 600
Chad Collyer defeated Matt Stryker; The Amazing Red beat Slim J; AJ Styles downed Jose and Joel Maximo, HC Loc and Tony Devito, and Johnny Kashmere and Trent Acid in a scramble match; Low Ki, Mafia and Monster Mack beat Dixie, Deranged and Angeldust by DQ; Low Ki, Mafia and Monster Mack overcame Dixie, Deranged and Angeldust; Quiet Storm and Ghost Shadow defeated Marcos and Dunn; CM Punk toppled Raven in a “Raven Rules” outing;  Samoa Joe bested Homicide, BJ Whitmer and EZ Money in a four-way; AJ Styles and The Amazing Red rolled over ROH Tag Champions Christopher Daniels and ROH Champion Xavier to win the titles.

22nd March 2003: “ROH Night of Champions”: Philadelphia, PA
BJ Whitmer defeated Dixie, Matt Stryker, and Alex Arion in a four-way; Special K beat Quiet Storm, Jose and Joel Maximo in a scramble match; Johnny Kashmere and Trent Acid downed Marcos andd Dunn; Douglas Williams pinned FWA British Heavyweight Champion Christopher Daniels to win the title; Homicide, Mafia and Monster Mack downed CW Anderson, Samoa Joe and Jack Victory by DQ; Tony DeVito and HC Loc dropped Mace and Hotstuff Hernandez; ROH Tag Champions AJ Styles and Amazing Red bested Mark and Jay Briscoe; Low Ki pinned Jody Fleisch; Raven and Colt Cabana toppled CM Punk and Ace Steele in a “Ravens Rules” match; Samoa Joe rolled over ROH Champion Xavier to win the title.

Apr 2003

12th April 2003: “ROH Epic Encounter”: Philadelphia, PA 
Johnny Kashmere and Trent Acid defeated Mafia and Monster Mack; Jay Lethal and Dixie beat Marcus and Dunn, and HC Loc and Tony DeVito in a scramble match; ROH Tag Champions AJ Styles and The Amazing Red dropped Mark and Jay Briscoe; BJ Whitmer battled CM Punk to a no-contest; Homicide overturned Christopher Daniels; Alexis Laree pinned Ariel; ROH Champion Samoa Joe bested Hernandez; ROH Champion Samoa Joe toppled Colt Cabana, Matt Stryker and Tom Carter in a four-way; Paul London rolled over Bryan Danielson in a best-of-three-falls match.

26th April 2003: “ROH Retribution: Round Robin Challenge II”: Pittsburgh, PA – 400
Christopher Daniels (1-0) beat The Amazing Red (0-1); Colt Cabana and Ace Steel defeated Dan Maff and Monster Mack; Joel and Jose Maximo downed Dunn and Marcos, EZ Money and Sterling James Keenan, and Special K in a four-way; Homicide bested CM Punk; HC Loc, Tony DeVito and Masada downed Don Juan, Fast Eddie and Hernandez in a no-DQ match; Paul London (1-0) pinned The Amazing Red; Alexis Laree overcame Persephonie; Matt Stryker defeated Chad Collyer, Michael Shane, BJ Whitmer, and Donovan Morgan in a five-way; Christopher Daniels (2-0) toppled Paul London (1-1); ROH Champion Samoa Joe rolled over Douglas Williams.

May 2003

17th May 2003: “ROH/FWA Frontiers of Honor”: London, England – 1,200
James Tighe beat Paul London; Jack Xavier pinned Mikey Whipwreck; Paul Burchill defeated The Double Dragons in a handicap match; AJ Styles bested Jonny Storm; ROH Champion Samoa Joe toppled The Zebra Kid; Low Ki battled Flash Barker to a draw; Christopher Daniels rolled over Jody Fleisch.

31st May 2003: “ROH Do or Die”: Philadelphia, PA
Dan Maff beat BJ Whitmer; Tony Mamaluke defeated Jason Cross; Iceberg pinned Oman Tortuga; Matt Stryker downed Tom Carter; CM Punk and Colt Cabana dropped Mark and Jay Briscoe, Special K, and HC Loc and Tony DeVito in a four-way; Andy Anderson downed John Walters; Christopher Daniels toppled Frankie Kazarian, CM Punk and Jimmy Rave in a four-way; Trent Acid and Johnny Kashmere defeated Slim J and Jody Fleisch; ROH Champion Samoa Joe rolled over Homicide.

June 2003

14th June 2003: “ROH Night of the Grudges”: Cambridge, MA
Dark matches: Jimmy Cash beat Lit; Angeldust, Slugger and Lit overcame Marcos, Dunn and Slyk Wagner Brown.
HC Loc and Tony DeVito defeated Buff E and Mace; Matt Stryker downed Chance Beckett in a “pure rules” match; Homicide overturned Justin Credible, John Walters and Chris Sabin in a four-way; CM Punk and Colt Cabana dropped Raven and BJ Whitmer in a “Ravens Rules” match; Prince Nana pinned Diablo Santiago; Joel and Jose Maximo bested Mikey Whipwreck and Dixie; Paul London battled NWA World Champion AJ Styles to a draw; Christopher Daniels, Donovan Morgan and Dan Maff rolled over ROH World Champion Samoa Joe, Michael Shane and CW Anderson in a “loser faction must disband”.

28th June 2003: “ROH Wrestlerave 2003”: Philadephia, PA
Dark matches: Benny Blanco beat Lit; Hydro overcame Angel Dust; Buff E and Mace downed Dialo Santiago and Oman Tortuga.
Dunn and Marcos defeated Prince Nana and Jimmy Cash; Alexis Laree pinned Sumie Sakai; Chad Collyer dropped Matt Stryker in a submission match; HC Loc, Tony DeVito and Justin Credible overturned Izzy, Dixie and Deranged; ROH Champion Samoa Joe toppled Dan Maff; BJ Whitmer overturned Jimmy Jacobs, Tony Mamaluke and Alex Shelley in a four-way; CM Punk and Colt Cabana bested Raven and Christopher Daniels; NWA World Champion AJ Styles toppled Chris Sabin; Trent Acid rolled over Homicide in a “Fight Without Honor”. 

July 2003

19th July 2003: “ROH Death before Dishonor”: Elizabeth, NJ – 1,200
Low Ki defeated Deranged; Matt Stryker pinned Jimmy Rave; Fast Eddie, Don Juan, Rudy Boy Gonzalez and Hernandez beat HC Loc, Tony DeVito, Justin Credible and Masada in a hardcore match; Tony Mamaluke and John Walters downed Oman Tortuga and Diablo Santiago; Tom Carter dropped Douglas Williams; BJ Whitmer overcame Colt Cabana, Homicide and Dan Maff in a four-way; Mikey Whipwreck, Jay Lethal, Angeldust and Brian XL overturned Jose and Joel Maximo, and Trent Acid and Johnny Kashmere in a scramble match; Jeff Hardy bested Krazy K and Joey Matthews in a three-way; CM Punk toppled Raven in a dog collar match; ROH Tag Champions AJ Styles and The Amazing Red bested Mark and Jay Briscoe; ROH Champion Samoa Joe rolled over Paul London.

Aug 2003

9th August 2003: “ROH Wrath of the Racket”: Dayton, OH – 625
Deranged and Jay Lethal defeated Joel and Jose Maximo, HC Loc and Masada, and Fast Eddie and Don Juan in a scramble match; Nigel McGuinness pinned Chet Jablonski; Homicide beat Chris Sabin; Ace Steel and Colt Cabana downed Alex Shelley and Jimmy Jacobs; Matt Stryker overcame Justin Credible; Michael Shane overturned Hernandez, Slyk Wagner Brown and Scoot Andrews in a four-way; Jimmy Rave defeated Slim J; ROH Champion Samoa Joe toppled BJ Whitmer; ROH Tag Team Champions AJ Styles and Homicide (a permitted substitute for Amazing Red) rolled over Christopher Daniels and Dan Maff.

16th August 2003: “ROH Bitter Friends, Stiffer Enemies”: Fairfield, CT – 400
Homicide beat Prince Nana; Tony Mamaluke and John Walters downed Dunn and Marcos; Alexis Laree defeated Becky Bayless; Matt Stryker battled BJ Whitmer to a draw; Dixie and Izzy beat Trent Acid and Johnny Kashmere, Jose and Joel Maximo, and HC Loc and Tony DeVito in a scramble match; Christopher Daniels pinned Xavier; Deranged dropped Jay Lethal, Jonny Storm and Sky Wagner Brown in a four-way; ROH Champion Samoa Joe rolled over CM Punk.

Sep 2003

6th September 2003: “ROH Beating the Odds: Wakefield, MA – 750
Colt Cabana pinned Jimmy Rave (Field of Honor – Block B); Slyk Wagner Brown beat Diablo Santiago; Trent Acid and Johnny Kashmere defeated Jose and Joel Maximo, Marcos and Dunn, and Izzy and Dixie in a scramble match; Matt Stryker beat John Walters (Field of Honor – Block A); HC Loc, Tony DeVito and Justin Credible downed Jay Lethal, Deranged and Angeldust; Mark Briscoe bested BJ Whitmer; Homicide toppled Trent Acid; Jay Briscoe dropped Chris Sabin, AJ Styles and ROH Champion Samoa Joe in a four-way; Raven rolled over CM Punk in a Clockwork Orange House of Fun steel cage match.

20th September 2003: “ROH Glory by Honor II”: Philadelphia, PA – 550
Diablo Santiago and Oman Tortuga beat April Hunter and Slyk Wagner Brown; Angel Dust downed Joey Matthews; Jimmy Jacobs overcame Alex Shelley, Roderick Strong and Josh Daniels in a four-way; BJ Whitmer beat Jimmy Rave (Field of Honor – Block B); Xavier downed John Walters (Field of Honor – Block A); Teddy Hart pinned TJ Wilson; Steve Corino dropped Raven; Colt Cabana bested AJ Styles, Chris Sabin and Matt Stryker in a four-way; Trent Acid and Johnny Kashmere won the vacant ROH Tag Titles in a gauntlet match over Dixie and Izzy, Mark and Jay Briscoe, Dunn and Marcos, Deranged and Hydro, and DeVito and Loc; Alexis Laree downed Hijinx. Julius Smokes was guest referee; Terry Funk toppled CM Punk by DQ; ROH Champion Samoa Joe rolled over Christopher Daniels.

Oct 2003

16th October 2003: “ROH Tradition Continues”: Glen Burnie, MD – 500
Grim Reefer and Slugger beat Danny Doring and Josh Daniels, Slyk Wagner Brown and Sonjay Dutt, and Dunn and Marcos in a tag scramble match; Matt Stryker downed Chris Sabin (Field of Honor – Block A); Colt Cabana defeated BJ  Whitmer (Field of Honor – Block B); Dixie and Izzy bested ROH Tag Champions Trent Acid and Johnny Kashmere to win the titles; AJ Styles downed CM Punk; Dan Maff dropped Jimmy Rave (Field of Honor – Block B); Raven pinned Justin Credible; Xavier overcame Homicide, John Walters and Mark Briscoe in a four-way; ROH Champion Samoa Joe rolled over Jay Briscoe.

25th October 2003: “ROH Empire State Showdown”: Spencerport, NY – 300
BJ Whitmer beat Dan Maff (Field of Honor – Block B); Dunn and Marcos downed Alex Shelley and Jimmy Jacobs, Don Juan and Fast Eddie, and Diablo Santiago and Oman Tortuga in a tag team scramble match; Christopher Daniels defeated John Walters; ROH Tag Champions Dixie and Izzy overcame DeVito and Loc; Xavier bested Chris Sabin (Field of Honor – Block A); CM Punk battled Steve Corino to a draw; John Walters toppled Colt Cabana, Jimmy Rave and Justin Credible  in a four-way; Homicide rolled over ROH Champion Samoa Joe in a non-title “No Holds Barred” match. 

Nov 2003

1st November 2003: “ROH Main Event Spectacles”: Elizabeth, NJ – 800
Dark matches: (shown at Final Battle Convention on 12/27) Diablo Santiago and Oman Tortuga beat Cloudy and Lit; Luis Ortiz defeated Jose Perez; Grim Reefer and Slyk Wagner Brown downed Dunn and Marcos.
Dan Maff beat Colt Cabana (Field of Honor – Block B); Nigel McGuinness and Xavier bested John Walters and Tony Mamaluke; Matt Stryker dropped Justin Credible; Mark and Jay Briscoe toppled ROH Tag Champions Dixie and Izzy to win the titles; Homicide bested BJ Whitmer in a “Fighting Spirit Challenge”; Trent Acid and Johnny Kashmere overcame Angel Dust and Hydro, DeVito and Loc, Jack Evans and Teddy Hart, and Joel Maximo and Jose Maximo in a tag team scramble match; CM Punk and Steve Corino bested Christopher Daniels and ROH Champion Samoa Joe in a non-title four-way ending via double pinfall; AJ Styles rolled over Bryan Danielson.

28th November 2003: “ROH The Conclusion”: Fairfield, CT – 700
Dark matches: (shown at Final Battle Convention on 12/27): Bison and Slugga overcame Havok and Papadon; Slyk Wagner Brown downed Jimmy Cash; Allison Danger beat Sarah Stock; Angel Dust and Hydro defeated Dunn and Marcos.
Trent Acid and Johnny Kashmere downed Diablo Santiago and Oman Tortuga; Homicide dropped John Walters; Joel and Jose Maximo overturned Fast Eddie and Hernandez, Dixie and Izzy, and DeVito and Loc in a tag team scramble match; Christopher Daniels bested Jimmy Rave; Josh Daniels downed Steve Corino; Matt Stryker defeated Xavier (Field of Honor – Block A Final); BJ Whitmer overcame Colt Cabana and Dan Maff in a three-way (Field of Honor – Block B Final); ROH Tag Champions Jay and Mark Briscoe toppled AJ Styles and ROH Champion Samoa Joe; CM Punk rolled over Raven in a “Lottery from Hell Old School” cage match.

29th November 2003: “ROH War of the Wire”: Framingham, MA – 600
Trent Acid and Johnny Kashmere beat Fast Eddie and Hernandez; Special K won a tag team scramble match over Slugger, The Carnage Crew and The SAT; BJ Whitmer overcame Matt Stryker, Colt Cabana, CM Punk and Jimmy Rave in a gauntlet match; Diablo Santiago and Oman Tortuga dropped April Hunter and Slyk Wagner Brown; John Walters defeated Xavier; ROH Tag Champions Jay and Mark Briscoe toppled Christopher Daniels and Dan Maff; ROH Champion Samoa Joe bested AJ Styles; Steve Corino rolled over Homicide in a “no-rope, barbed-wire” match.

Dec 2003

27th December 2003: “ROH Final Battle 2003”: Philadelphia, PA
Convention: (drew 400): Sumie Sakai beat Allison Danger; Josh Daniels defeated Puma; Buff E and Mace battled Dunn and Marcos to a no-contest; CM Punk rolled over Mace; Angel Dust, Dixie and Izzy battled DeVito, Loc and Justin Credible to a no-contest; Hydro won a four-way over Nick Gage, Oman Tortuga and Slyk Wagner Brown; Trent Acid downed Sonjay Dutt; ROH Tag Champions Jay and Mark Briscoe rolled over Joel and Jose Maximo. 
Event:(drew 1,500): Bryan Danielson downed Jay Briscoe; John Walters defeated Xavier in a “Fight without Honor”; Matt Stryker toppled BJ Whitmer (Field of Honor – Final); ROH Champion Samoa Joe bested Mark Briscoe; CM Punk and Colt Cabana overturned Kazushi Miyamato and Tomoaki Honma; AJ Styles pinned Kaz Hayashi; Satoshi Kojima bested Homicide; AJPW World Double Tag Champions The Great Muta and Arashi rolled over Christopher Daniels and Dan Maff.
