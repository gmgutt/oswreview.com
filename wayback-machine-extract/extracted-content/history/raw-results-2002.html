Raw Results 2002

WWF/E RAW RESULTS (2002)

Jan 2002

Air Date: 7th January 2002
Rob Van Dam downed Test; Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo downed Albert and Scotty 2 Hotty; WWF Intercontinental Champion Edge bested Lance Storm; Steve Austin and The Rock toppled Booker T and The Big Bossman; WWF World Champion Chris Jericho dropped Rikishi; Tazz and Spike Dudley defeated WWF World Tag Champions The Dudley Boys in a hardcore match to win the titles.

Air Date: 14th January 2002
Spike Dudley beat Bubba Ray Dudley; Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo defeated Tajiri and The Hurricane; Jazz pinned Jacquelyn; Kurt Angle toppled Kane; William Regal and Test overcame WWF Intercontinental Champion Edge and Rob Van Dam; Booker T overturned The Big Show; WWF World Champion Chris Jericho, Lance Storm, and Christian downed Rikishi and The Acolytes.

Air Date: 21st January 2002
Rob Van Dam, and WWF World Tag Champions Tazz and Spike Dudley beat Booker T and The Dudley Boys; WWF Intercontinental Champion William Regal overcame Edge by DQ; Val Venis battled Curt Hennig to a no-contest; Chuck Palumbo and Billy Gunn bested Kane and The Big Show; The Rock and Triple H rolled over WWF World Champion Chris Jericho and Kurt Angle.

Air Date: 28th January 2002
Kane toppled The Big Show; Rob Van Dam rolled over WWF Intercontinental Champion William Regal by DQ; Diamond Dallas Page and The Godfather dropped Lance Storm and Christian; WWF Undisputed World Champion Chris Jericho bested Maven; Billy Gunn, Chuck Palumbo, and Jazz defeated The Acolytes and WWF Women’s Champion Trish Stratus; Booker T overturned Triple H; Steve Austin bested Kurt Angle (WWF Undisputed Title #1 Contender – Final).

Feb 2002

Air Date: 4th February 2002
Triple H rolled over Booker T by DQ; Rob Van Dam rolled over Bubba Ray Dudley; WWF World Tag Champions Tazz and Spike Dudley beat The Acolytes, and Chuck Palumbo and Billy Gunn in a three-way elimination match; WWF Intercontinental Champion William Regal overcame Rikishi by DQ; Jazz pinned WWF Women’s Champion Trish Stratus to win the title; WWF Undisputed World Champion Chris Jericho and The Undertaker bested Steve Austin and The Rock.

Air Date: 11th February 2002
WWF Undisputed World Champion Chris Jericho bested Edge in a non-title match; Goldust overcame Rikishi by DQ; Kurt Angle toppled The Godfather; Steve Austin bested The Undertaker by DQ; Booker T overturned Tazz; Stacy Keibler dropped Torrie Wilson in a bikini match; Rob Van Dam rolled over Christian.

Air Date: 18th February 2002
Matt and Jeff Hardy beat Lance Storm and Christian; Kane toppled Curt Hennig; Booker T overturned The Godfather; Rob Van Dam and Edge dropped Goldust and WWF Intercontinental Champion William Regal; Triple H rolled over Kurt Angle.

Air Date: 25th February 2002
WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo defeated The Hardy Boys; Rob Van Dam rolled over The Big Show and Lance Storm in a three-way; Booker T overturned Rikishi; WWF Women’s Champion Jazz pinned Molly Holly; WWF Undisputed World Champion Chris Jericho beat Kurt Angle; Steve Austin bested Curt Hennig.

Mar 2002


Air Date: 4th March 2002
Steve Austin battled Booker T to a no-contest; Curt Hennig and Test overcame Scotty 2 Hotty and Albert; WWF Hardcore Champion Goldust overcame Tazz. Jacquelyn was guest referee; WWF Women’s Champion Jazz defeated Trish Stratus by DQ; Rob Van Dam rolled over Lance Storm; Scott Hall pinned Spike Dudley; Kurt Angle toppled Triple H in a cage match.

Air Date: 11th March 2002
Rob Van Dam and The Hardy Boys beat William Regal and The Dudley Boys; Test overcame Rikishi; Al Snow pinned WWF Hardcore Champion Goldust to win the title; Kurt Angle and Booker T overturned Edge and The Big Show; Christian downed Billy Gunn; WWF Women’s Champion Jazz and Stacy Keibler dropped Lita and Trish Stratus. Hulk Hogan, Scott Hall, and Kevin Nash defeated The Rock and Steve Austin in a handicap match.

Air Date: 18th March 2002
WWF Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Christian; Lita beat Trish Stratus; Edge and Kane toppled Kurt Angle and Booker T; WWF Hardcore Champion Maven battled Al Snow to a no-contest; WWF World Tag Champions Billy Gunn and Chuck Palumbo defeated The Dudley Boys; Hulk Hogan and The Rock defeated Kevin Nash and Scott Hall by count-out.

Air Date: 25th March 2002
Tazz defeated Curt Hennig; Booker T and Christian downed Edge and Diamond Dallas Page; Trish Stratus overcame Ivory; Kevin Nash, Scott Hall, and X-Pac defeated Hulk Hogan and The Rock by DQ in a handicap match; Jeff Hardy beat Billy Gunn; WWF Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Kurt Angle by DQ; WWF World Champion Triple H rolled over Chris Jericho and Stephanie McMahon in a three-way.

Apr 2002

Air Date: 1st April 2002
WWF Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Booker T; Spike Dudley beat WWF European Champion William Regal in a non-title match; Bubba Ray Dudley beat WWF Hardcore Champion Raven to win the title; The Hardy Boys beat Curt Hennig and The Big Bossman; Trish Stratus overcame Terri in a “paddle-on-a-pole” match; Kane toppled X-Pac by DQ.

Air Date: 8th April 2002
WWF Hardcore Champion Bubba Ray Dudley beat Goldust; X-Pac pinned Kane in a “falls-count-anywhere” match; Spike Dudley beat WWF European Champion William Regal to win the title; The Undertaker bested WWF Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam in a non-title match; Molly Holly downed Trish Stratus; The Big Show toppled Curt Hennig; Steve Austin rolled over Scott Hall.

Air Date: 15th April 2002
Raven bested WWF Hardcore Champion Bubba Ray Dudley to win the title; Tommy Dreamer dropped WWF Hardcore Champion Raven to win the title; Steven Richards overturned WWF Hardcore Champion Tommy Dreamer to win the title; Bubba Ray Dudley beat WWF Hardcore Champion Steven Richards to win the title; The Big Show toppled Shawn Stasiak;  Jacquelyn toppled Crash Holly; Eddie Gurrero and William Regal overcame WWF Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam and WWF European Champion Spike Dudley; Trish Stratus overcame Molly Holly; Booker T and Goldust overcame The Hardy Boys; Steve Austin and Bradshaw defeated The Undertaker, Scott Hall, and X-Pac in a handicap match.

Air Date: 22nd April 2002
Brock Lesnar beat Matt Hardy; Rob Van Dam rolled over Curt Hennig; WWF Women’s Champion Jazz and Molly Holly downed Trish Stratus and Jacquelyn; Goldust and Booker T overturned WWF Hardcore Champion Bubba Ray Dudley and WWF European Champion Spike Dudley; William Regal pinned Shawn Stasiak; Steve Austin and The Big Show battled Scott Hall and X-Pac to a no-contest.

Air Date: 29th April 2002
WWF Intercontinental Champion Eddie Guerrero overcame Jeff Hardy; Rob Van Dam rolled over Booker T; Brock Lesnar dropped Shawn Stasiak; Steve Austin and Bradshaw defeated Scott Hall and X-Pac. Ric Flair was guest referee; WWF Hardcore Champion Bubba Ray Dudley battled Jazz to a no-contest (Steven Richards pinned WWF Hardcore Champion Bubba Ray Dudley to win the title); WWF European Champion Spike Dudley beat Goldust; WWF World Champion Hulk Hogan battled William Regal to a no-contest.

May 2002

Air Date: 6th May 2002
WWE Women’s Champion Jazz beat Trish Stratus in a no-DQ match; Bubba Ray Dudley beat WWE Hardcore Champion Steven Richards to win the title; Raven bested WWE Hardcore Champion Bubba Ray Dudley to win the title; Justin Credible toppled WWE Hardcore Champion Raven to win the title; Crash Holly downed WWE Hardcore Champion Justin Credible to win the title; Trish Stratus overcame WWE Hardcore Champion Crash Holly to win the title; Steven Richards overturned WWE Hardcore Champion Trish Stratus to win the title; William Regal overcame WWE European Champion Spike Dudley to win the title; Brock Lesnar beat Shawn Stasiak; Rob Van Dam and Jeff Hardy beat Booker T and WWE Intercontinental Champion Eddie Guerrero; Terri defeated Molly Holly in a swimsuit contest; Steve Austin, Bradshaw, and Ric Flair battled The Big Show, Scott Hall, and X-Pac to a no-contest.

Air Date: 13th May 2002
The Hardy Boys beat Brock Lesnar by DQ in a handicap match; WWE Intercontinental Champion Eddie Guerrero overcame Shawn Stasiak; Molly Holly downed Terri; WWE World Champion Hulk Hogan bested Ric Flair in a no-DQ match; Trish Stratus and Bubba Ray Dudley beat WWE Women’s Champion Jazz and Steven Richards. By virtue of a pre-match stipulation Trish Stratus was awarded the WWE Women’s Title; X-Pac and The Big Show toppled Bradshaw in a handicap match; Steve Austin bested Booker T in a lumberjack match.

Air Date: 20th May 2002
The Hardy Boys beat The Big Show and X-Pac; WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus overcame Jacquelyn; Bubba Ray Dudley beat WWE European Champion William Regal by DQ; The Hardy Boys beat Booker T and Goldust; Jerry Lawler dropped Raven by count-out; WWE World Champion The Undertaker bested Rob Van Dam.

Air Date: 27th May 2002
Spike Dudley and WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus overcame WWE European Champion William Regal and Molly Holly; Terri pinned WWE Hardcore Champion Steven Richards to win the title; Steven Richards overturned WWE Hardcore Champion Terri to win the title; Booker T and X-Pac defeated The Hardy Boys; Brock Lesnar beat Bubba Ray Dudley; The Big Show toppled Bradshaw; Goldust overcame Crash Holly; Rob Van Dam rolled over WWE Intercontinental Champion Eddie Guerrero in a ladder match to win the title.

Jun 2002

Air Date: 3rd June 2002
Bradshaw pinned WWE Hardcore Champion Steven Richards to win the title; X-Pac pinned Goldust (KOTR Qualifier); WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus overcame Terri in a lingerie match; Eddie Guerrero and Brock Lesnar beat WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam and Bubba Ray Dudley; Booker T overturned WWE European Champion William Regal in a non-title match (KOTR Qualifier); WWE World Champion The Undertaker bested Tommy Dreamer in a non-title match; Steve Austin bested Ric Flair in an “pure wrestling (no punches allowed)” match.

Air Date: 10th June 2002
Booker T, The Big Show, and X-Pac defeated Tommy Dreamer, Spike Dudley, and Shawn Stasiak; WWE European Champion William Regal pinned WWE Hardcore Champion Bradshaw; Molly Holly downed WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus in a non-title match; WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Eddie Guerrero (KOTR Qualifier); Brock Lesnar beat Bubba Ray Dudley (KOTR Qualifier); Vince McMahon bested Ric Flair in a “no holds barred” match to gain 100% ownership of WWE.

Air Date: 17th June 2002
WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over X-Pac (KOTR 2002 – Quarter-Final); Jeff Hardy beat Raven; Christopher Nowinski defeated Spike Dudley; D-Lo Brown and WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus overcame Crash Holly and Molly Holly; WWE World Champion The Undertaker bested Matt Hardy; Brock Lesnar beat Booker T (KOTR 2002 – Quarter-Final).

Air Date: 24th June 2002
WWE European Champion William Regal and Christopher Nowinski defeated WWE Hardcore Champion Bradshaw and Spike Dudley; Eddie Guerrero overcame Bubba Ray Dudley; Tommy Dreamer dropped Raven in a “loser leaves RAW” encounter; WWE World Champion The Undertaker bested Jeff Hardy; Trish Stratus and Linda Miles overturned WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly and Jackie Gayda; X-Pac and The Big Show toppled Booker T and Goldust when Show toppled Goldust; WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over Brock Lesnar by DQ.

Jul 2002

Air Date: 1st July 2002
Brock Lesnar beat Ric Flair; Christopher Nowinski pinned WWE Hardcore Champion Bradshaw in a non-title match; WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over WWE European Champion William Regal in a non-title match;  Bubba Ray Dudley and Spike Dudley beat Eddie Guerrero and Chris Benoit; Booker T overturned The Big Show by count-out; WWE World Champion The Undertaker bested Jeff Hardy in a ladder match.

Air Date: 8th July 2002
Booker T overturned Eddie Guerrero; Chris Benoit beat Bubba Ray Dudley; WWE Hardcore Champion Bradshaw and Trish Stratus overcame Chris Nowinski and Jackie Gayda; Ric Flair overturned Steven Richards; Jeff Hardy beat WWE European Champion William Regal to win the title; Kevin Nash, X-Pac, The Big Show, Chris Benoit, and Eddie Guerrero overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam, Booker T, Goldust, Bubba Ray Dudley, and Spike Dudley.

Air Date: 15th July 2002
William Regal, Chris Benoit, and Eddie Guerrero overcame WWF European Champion Jeff Hardy, Bubba Ray Dudley, and Spike Dudley in an elimination match;  Tommy Dreamer dropped Steven Richards in a “Singapore Cane” match; WWF Women’s Champion Molly Holly downed Trish Stratus; Booker T overturned The Big Show by DQ; WWE Hardcore Champion Bradshaw battled Christopher Nowinski to a no-contest (Johnny Stamboli pinned WWE Hardcore Champion Bradshaw to win the title; Bradshaw pinned WWE Hardcore Champion Johnny Stamboli); WWE World Champion The Undertaker and Brock Lesnar beat Ric Flair and WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam.

Air Date: 22nd July 2002
WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over WWE European Champion Jeff Hardy in a ladder match to unify the titles; William Regal and WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly downed Bubba Ray Dudley and Trish Stratus; Brock Lesnar beat Tommy Dreamer in a “Singapore Cane” match; D-Lo Brown battled Shawn Stasiak to a no-contest; Chris Benoit toppled Booker T; The Big Show toppled Spike Dudley; WWE World Champion The Rock pinned Eddie Guerrero.

Air Date: 29th July 2002
Booker T overturned Eddie Guerrero; Bubba Ray Dudley and Trish Stratus overcame The Big Show and WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly; Jeff Hardy beat WWE Hardcore Champion Bradshaw to win the title (Johnny Stamboli pinned WWE Hardcore Champion Jeff Hardy to win the title; Tommy Dreamer dropped WWE Hardcore Champion Johnny Stamboli to win the title); Chris Benoit toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam to win the title; The Undertaker bested Christopher Nowinski; WWE World Champion The Rock pinned Ric Flair.

Aug 2002

Air Date: 5th August 2002
Bubba Ray Dudley beat The Big Show in a tables match; WWE Hardcore Champion Tommy Dreamer dropped Bradshaw; Test battled Sgt Slaughter to a no-contest; Booker T and Goldust overcame William Regal and Christopher Nowinski; Victoria toppled Trish Stratus; The Hardy Boys beat WWE World Tag Champions Lance Storm and Christian by DQ; Rob Van Dam rolled over Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 12th August 2002
Christopher Nowinski and WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly downed Spike Dudley and Trish Stratus; Booker T defeated Lance Storm; Chris Jericho and The Big Show toppled Ric Flair and Bubba Ray Dudley; WWE Hardcore Champion Tommy Dreamer dropped Steven Richards; Rob Van Dam rolled over Jeff Hardy; Triple H, Test, and WWE World Tag Champions Lance Storm and Christian downed WWE World Champion The Rock, Booker T, Goldust, and The Undertaker.

Air Date: 19th August 2002
WWE Hardcore Champion Tommy Dreamer won a “Hardcore Challenge scramble” over Crash Holly, Bradshaw, Bubba Ray Dudley, Jeff Hardy, Steven Richards, Johnny Stamboli, Terri Runnels, and Tommy Dreamer; Trish Stratus overcame Stacy Keibler in a “bra and panties mud wrestling” match; Test and WWE World Tag Champions Christian and Lance Storm defeated The Undertaker, Booker T, and Goldust; Rob Van Dam battled The Big Show to a no-contest; WWE World Champion The Rock battled Triple H to a no-contest.

Air Date: 26th August 2002
Booker T downed Christian;  Bubba Ray Dudley and Spike Dudley beat William Regal and Christopher Nowinski; Jeff Hardy bested Chris Jericho by DQ; WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over WWE Hardcore Champion Tommy Dreamer in a hardcore match to unify the titles; Lillian Garcia overcame Howard Finkel in an “Evening Gown/Tuxedo” match; Triple H rolled over The Undertaker.

Sep 2002

Air Date: 2nd September 2002
Bubba Ray Dudley and Trish Stratus overcame Christopher Nowinski and WWE Women’s Champion Molly Holly in a tables match; Booker T overturned William Regal; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H rolled over Ric Flair; Kane toppled WWE World Tag Champions Lance Storm and Christian in a non-title handicap match; The Big Show toppled Tommy Dreamer by DQ; Jeff Hardy battled Crash Holly to a no-contest; Terri pinned Stacy Keibler in a “Lingerie Pillow Fight”. Jerry Lawler was guest referee; WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam and Ric Flair overturned World Heavyweight Champion Triple H and Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 9th September 2002
WWE World Tag Champions Lance Storm and Christian downed Bradshaw and Kane; Bubba Ray Dudley beat Steven Richards; Trish Stratus and Terri defeated Stacy Keibler and Victoria; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H rolled over Spike Dudley; Test and William Regal battled Booker T and Goldust to a double-DQ; WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam rolled over The Big Show, Jeff Hardy, and Chris Jericho in a four-way elimination match.

Air Date: 16th September 2002
Rico pinned Ric Flair; Booker T overturned Test; WWE World Tag Champions Lance Storm and Christian downed Bubba Ray Dudley and Spike Dudley; Chris Jericho bested WWE Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam to win the title; Kane toppled William Regal by DQ; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H rolled over Jeff Hardy.

Air Date: 23rd September 2002
WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus overcame Molly Holly and Victoria in a three-way; The Big Show toppled Jeff Hardy; Randy Orton rolled over Steven Richards; WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho bested Goldust; Kane and The Hurricane beat WWE World Tag Champions Lance Storm and Christian to win the titles; Booker T overturned Rico; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H and Ric Flair overturned Rob Van Dam and Bubba Ray Dudley.

Air Date: 30th September 2002
Rosey and Jamal defeated Booker T and Goldust; Randy Orton rolled over Lance Storm; Randy Orton rolled over Christian; Jeff Hardy beat The Big Show by count-out; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H rolled over Bubba Ray Dudley; WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus overcame Victoria by DQ; Rob Van Dam and Tommy Dreamer dropped William Regal and Test; Kane toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Chris Jericho to win the title.

Oct 2002

Air Date: 7th October 2002
Booker T overturned The Big Show in a cage match; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H rolled over D-Lo Brown in a blindfold match; William Regal pinned Goldust in a “Showgirl” match; WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus overcame Stacy Keibler in a “bra and panties” match; Al Snow pinned Test in a streetfight; Jerry Lawler dropped Steven Richards in an ‘It’s Legal in Nevada” match; WWE World Tag Champion Kane toppled Chris Jericho and Christian, Bubba Ray and Spike Dudley, and Rob Van Dam and Jeff Hardy in a handicap four-way TLC match.

Air Date: 14th October 2002
Chris Jericho and Christian downed WWE World Tag Champions The Hurricane and WWE Intercontinental Champion Kane to win the titles; Lance Storm dropped Al Snow; Jeff Hardy beat Rico; WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus and Jacquelyn toppled Molly Holly and Victoria. Stacy Keibler was guest referee; Christopher Nowinski defeated Tommy Dreamer; The Big Show overcame Booker T in a “falls-count-anywhere” match; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H rolled over Rob Van Dam in a lumberjack strap match.

Air Date: 21st October 2002
Christopher Nowinski pinned Jeff Hardy; Bubba Ray Dudley and Spike Dudley beat Lance Storm and William Regal; Test overcame D-Lo Brown. Stacy Kiebler was guest referee; Victoria, and WWE World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and Christian downed Booker T, Goldust, and WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus; Tommy Dreamer dropped Al Snow in a “Singapore Cane” match; The Big Show toppled Rico, Rosey, and Jamal in a handicap match; Rob Van Dam and Kane toppled World Heavyweight Champion Triple H and Ric Flair.

Air Date: 28th October 2002
Lance Storm and William Regal overcame Rob Van Dam and Tommy Dreamer; Test beat Goldust; WWE World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and Christian downed Bubba Ray Dudley and Jeff Hardy; Christopher Nowinski defeated Booker T by DQ; WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus overcame Jacquelyn and Molly Holly in a three-way;  Kane toppled World Heavyweight Champion Triple H in a non-title casket match.

Nov 2002

Air Date: 4th November 2002
Rosey and Jamal beat Bubba Ray Dudley and Jeff Hardy; WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus overcame Ivory; William Regal and Lance Storm defeated Tommy Dreamer and Al Snow; Batista overturned Justin Credible; Test beat The Hurricane; Rob Van Dam rolled over Christian; Kane and Booker T overturned World Heavyweight Champion Triple H and Chris Jericho.

Air Date: 11th November 2002
Rob Van Dam and Kane toppled WWE World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and Christian by DQ; Jeff Hardy beat Lance Storm; Batista overturned D-Lo Brown; Rosey and Jamal defeated Bubba Ray Dudley and Spike Dudley; Christopher Nowinski pinned Al Snow; Test and Steven Richards overturned The Hurricane and Goldust; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H rolled over Booker T.

Air Date: 18th November 2002
Bubba Ray Dudley, D-Von Dudley, and Spike Dudley beat Rosey, Jamal, and Rico; Lance Storm and William Regal overcame Tommy Dreamer and Jeff Hardy; WWE Women’s Champion Victoria toppled Stacy Keibler; Christopher Nowinski pinned Al Snow; Kane battled Ric Flair to a no-contest; Rob Van Dam rolled over Booker T and Chris Jericho in a no-DQ three-way match.

Air Date: 25th November 2002
WWE World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and Christian downed The Dudley Boys;  Test beat Steven Richards; Maven battled Chris Nowinski to a no-contest; WWE Women’s Champion Victoria toppled Trish Stratus; Lance Storm and William Regal overcame Jeff Hardy and The Hurricane; Batista pinned Kane; Goldust overcame Rico; Rob Van Dam rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Shawn Michaels by DQ.

Dec 2002

Air Date: 2nd December 2002
The Dudley Boys overturned Rosey and Jamal; Trish Stratus overcame Ivory; Lance Storm and William Regal overcame Tommy Dreamer and Jeff Hardy; Batista pinned The Hurricane; Booker T battled Chris Jericho to a no-contest; WWE World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and Christian downed Booker T and Goldust; Jacquelyn toppled WWE Women’s Champion Victoria in a non-title match; Triple H rolled over Rob Van Dam. Shawn Michaels was guest referee.

Air Date: 9th December 2002
Lance Storm and William Regal overcame Goldust and Booker T; Steven Richards overturned Jacquelyn; Batista defeated Rob Van Dam by DQ; D-Lo Brown and Christopher Nowinski defeated Al Snow and Maven; Triple H rolled over Jeff Hardy; Kane toppled Rosey and Jamal in a handicap match; The Dudley Boys and Trish Stratus overcame WWE Women’s Champion Victoria, WWE World Tag Champions Chris Jericho and Christian in a tables match.

Air Date: 16th December 2002
Test and Trish Stratus overcame Victoria and Steven Richards; Christopher Nowinski pinned Maven; Christian downed Goldust; Kane and Rob Van Dam toppled Batista, Rosey, and Jamal in a handicap match; William Regal and Lance Storm defeated The Dudley Boys; Jeff Hardy beat D-Lo Brown; Booker T overturned Chris Jericho by DQ.

Air Date: 23rd December 2002
Rob Van Dam and Kane toppled Rosey and Jamal; Test beat D-Lo Brown by DQ; The Hurricane dropped Christopher Nowinski; Trish Stratus, Jacquelyn, and Stacy Keibler dropped WWE Women’s Champion Victoria, Molly Holly, and Ivory; Batista pinned Spike Dudley; WWE World Tag Champions Booker T and Goldust overcame Chris Jericho and Christian; Jim Ross and Jerry Lawler dropped William Regal and Lance Storm.

Air Date: 30th December 2002
Year-in-Review highlight show.
