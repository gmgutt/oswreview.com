WWE PPV Results 2005

WWE PPV RESULTS (2005)

See also: WWE PPV Statistics 2005

9th January 2005: “WWE New Year’s Revolution 2005”: San Juan, PR – 15,764 sellout
($825,000 live gate)
Dark match: The Hurricane and Rosey beat Rob Conway and Sylvan Grenier.
PPV Broadcast: WWE World Tag Champions Eugene and William Regal defeated Christian and Tyson Tomko; Trish Stratus downed WWE Women’s Champion Lita to win the title; WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin dropped Maven; Muhammad Hassan overcame Jerry Lawler; Kane bested Gene Snitsky; Triple H won an Elimination Chamber match over Randy Orton, Chris Jericho, Chris Benoit, Batista, and Edge to win the vacant World Heavyweight Title. Shawn Michaels was guest referee.

30th January 2005: “WWE Royal Rumble 2005”: Fresno, CA – 12,000 (11,000 paid)
($750,000 live gate)
Heat: (01/30): Maven beat Rhyno.
PPV Broadcast: Edge dropped Shawn Michaels; The Undertaker bested John Heidenreich in a casket match; WWE World Champion John Bradshaw Layfield won a three-way over  Kurt Angle and The Big Show; World Heavyweight Champion Triple H pinned Randy Orton;  Batista won the 30-man Royal Rumble match by last eliminating John Cena.

20th February 2005: “WWE No Way Out 2005”: Pittsburgh, PA – 9,500 (8,000 paid)
($492,000 live gate)
Heat: (02/20): Hardcore Holly and Charlie Haas beat Kenzo Suzuki and Rene Dupree.
PPV Broadcast: Eddie Guerrero and Rey Mysterio downed WWE Tag Champions Doug Basham and Danny Basham to win the titles; Booker T dropped John Heidenreich by DQ; Chavo Guerrero Jr won a gauntlet match over WWE Cruiserweight Champion Sho Funaki, Spike Dudley, Paul London, Shannon Moore, and Akio to win the title; The Undertaker toppled Luther Reigns; WWE US Champion John Cena bested Kurt Angle (WWE World Title #1 Contender Tournament – Final); WWE World Champion John Bradshaw Layfield rolled over The Big Show in a “barbed wire cage” match.

3rd April 2005: “WWE Wrestlemania 21”: Los Angeles, CA – 20,193 sellout
($2,100,000 live gate)
Velocity: (04/23): Booker T won a battle royal.
PPV Broadcast: Rey Mysterio dropped Eddie Guerrero; Edge won a “Money in the Bank” ladder match over Chris Benoit, Chris Jericho, Christian, WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin, and Kane; The Undertaker rolled over Randy Orton; WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus pinned Christy Hemme; Kurt Angle bested Shawn Michaels; Akebono defeated The Big Show in a sumo match; John Cena toppled WWE World Champion John Bradshaw Layfield to win the title; Batista downed World Heavyweight Champion Triple H to win the title.

1st May 2005: “WWE Backlash 2005”: Manchester, NH – 14,000 sellout (12,000 paid)
($700,000 live gate)
Heat: (05/01): Tyson Tomko beat Val Venis.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion Shelton Benjamin defeated Chris Jericho; The Hurricane and Rosey downed WWE World Tag Champions William Regal and Tajiri, The Heart Throbs, Rob Conway and Sylvan Grenier, and Maven and Simon Dean in a “tag team turmoil” match to win the titles; Edge dropped Chris Benoit in a “last man standing” match; Kane pinned Viscera; Shawn Michaels and Hulk Hogan bested Muhammad Hassan and Daivari; World Heavyweight Champion Batista downed Triple H.

22nd May 2005: “WWE Judgment Day 2005”: Minneapolis, MN – 9,500
($500,000 live gate)
Heat: (05/22): Nunzio beat Akio.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Tag Champions Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury defeated Hardcore Holly and Charlie Haas; Carlito downed The Big Show; WWE Cruiserweight Champion Paul London dropped Chavo Guerrero Jr; Booker T bested Kurt Angle; WWE US Champion Orlando Jordan toppled John Heidenreich; Rey Mysterio overturned Eddie Guerrero by DQ; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over John Bradshaw Layfield in an “I Quit” match.

12th June 2005: “ECW One Night Stand 2005”: New York, NY (Hammerstein Ballroom) – 2,500
($484,000 live gate)
PPV Broadcast: Lance Storm bested Chris Jericho; Super Crazy downed Yoshihiro Tajiri and Little Guido in a three-way; Rey Mysterio toppled Psicosis; Sabu bested Rhyno; Chris Benoit overturned Eddie Guerrero; Mike Awesome defeated Masato Tanaka; The Dudley Boys rolled over Tommy Dreamer and The Sandman.

26th June 2005: “WWE Vengeance 2005”: Las Vegas, NV – 9,850 (8,218 paid)
($455,600 live gate)
Heat: (06/26): WWE World Tag Champions The Hurricane and Rosey beat The Heart Throbs.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Intercontinental Champion Carlito bested Shelton Benjamin; Victoria beat Christy Hemme; Kane pinned Edge; Shawn Michaels toppled Kurt Angle; WWE World Champion John Cena won a three-way over Chris Jericho and Christian; World Heavyweight Champion Batista downed Triple H in a “Hell in a Cell” match.

24th July 2005: “WWE Great American Bash 2005”: Buffalo, NY – 8,000
($375,000 live gate)
Heat: (07/24): WWE Cruiserweight Champion Paul London beat Nunzio.
PPV Broadcast: John Heidenreich and Road Warrior Animal downed WWE Tag Champions Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury to win the titles; Booker T pinned Christian; WWE US Champion Orlando Jordan dropped Chris Benoit; The Undertaker overturned Muhammad Hassan; Juventud Guerrera, Super Crazy, and Psychosis overcame Stevie Richards, The Blue Meanie, and Nova; Rey Mysterio bested Eddie Guerrero; Melina beat Torrie Wilson in a “bra and panties” match. Candice Michelle was guest referee; John Bradshaw Layfield rolled over World Heavyweight Champion Batista by DQ.

21st August 2005: “WWE Summerslam 2005”: Washington, DC – 15,700 sellout (14,200 paid)
($834,000 live gate)
Heat: (08/21): Chris Masters beat The Hurricane.
PPV Broadcast:  Chris Benoit defeated WWE US Champion Orlando Jordan to win the title; Edge downed Matt Hardy by stoppage; Rey Mysterio dropped Eddie Guerrero in a ladder match; Kurt Angle bested Eugene; Randy Orton overturned The Undertaker; WWE World Champion John Cena beat Chris Jericho; World Heavyweight Champion Batista downed John Bradshaw Layfield in a “no holds barred” match; Hulk Hogan rolled over Shawn Michaels.

18th September 2005: “WWE Unforgiven 2005”: Oklahoma City, OK – 8,000
($485,000 live gate)
Heat: Rob Conway beat Tajiri.
PPV Broadcast: Ric Flair defeated WWE Intercontinental Champion Carlito to win the title; WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus and Ashley dropped Victoria and Torrie Wilson; The Big Show pinned Gene Snitsky; Shelton Benjamin downed Kerwin White; Matt Hardy toppled Edge in a cage match; Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch overturned WWE World Tag Champions The Hurricane and Rosey to win the titles; Shawn Michaels bested Chris Masters; Kurt Angle rolled over WWE World Champion John Cena by DQ.

9th October 2005: “WWE No Mercy 2005”: Houston, TX – 7,000 (5,000 paid)
($440,000 live gate)
Heat: (10/09): William Regal and Paul Burchill overcame Paul London and Brian Kendrick.
PPV Broadcast:  Christy Hemme and WWE Tag Champions Road Warrior Animal and John Heidenreich overturned Johnny Nitro, Joey Mercury, and Melina; Bobby Lashley pinned Simon Dean; WWE US Champion Chris Benoit won a four-way over Booker T, Christian, and Orlando Jordan; Mr Kennedy toppled Hardcore Holly; John Bradshaw Layfield beat Rey Mysterio; Randy and Bob Orton Jr bested The Undertaker in a handicap casket match; Juventud Guerrera beat WWE Cruiserweight Champion Nunzio to win the title; World Heavyweight Champion Batista downed Eddie Guerrero.

1st November 2005: “WWE Taboo Tuesday 2005”: San Diego, CA – 6,000
Heat: (11/01): Kerwin White and Matt Striker beat Shelton Benjamin and Val Venis.
PPV Broadcast: Matt Hardy and Rey Mysterio toppled Chris Masters and Gene Snitsky; Euguene and Jimmy Snuka beat Rob Conway and Tyson Tomko; Mankind dropped Carlito; The Big Show and Kane bested WWE World Tag Champions Trevor Murdoch and Lance Cade to win the titles; World Heavyweight Champion Batista downed Jonathan Coachman in a streetfight; WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus defeated Ashley, Victoria, Mickie James, Maria, and Candice in a “lingerie” battle royal; WWE Intercontinental Champion Ric Flair toppled Triple H in a cage match; WWE World Champion John Cena won a three-way over Kurt Angle and Shawn Michaels.

27th November 2005: “WWE Survivor Series 2005”: Detroit, MI – 15,000
($700,000 live gate)
Heat: (11/27): WWE Cruiserweight Champion Juventud Guerrera beat Simon Dean.
PPV Broadcast: Booker T rolled over Chris Benoit (Best of Seven Series – match #1); WWE Women’s Champion Trish Stratus downed Melina; Triple H overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Ric Flair in a non-title “last man standing” match; WWE World Champion John Cena beat Kurt Angle; Teddy Long bested Eric Bischoff; World Heavyweight Champion Batista, Randy Orton, John Bradshaw Layfield, Bobby Lashley, and Rey Mysterio toppled Shawn Michaels, Chris Masters, Carlito and WWE World Tag Champions Kane and The Big Show in a “Survivor Series” elimination match.

18th December 2005: “WWE Armageddon 2005”: Providence, RI – 8,000
($450,000 live gate)
Heat: (12/18): Jamie Noble bested Sho Funaki.
PPV Broadcast: John Bradshaw Layfield beat Matt Hardy; Johnny Nitro and Joey Mercury downed Super Crazy and Psychosis; Chris Benoit bested Booker T (Best of Seven Series – match #4); Bobby Lashley dropped William Regal and Paul Burchill in a handicap match; Kid Kash overturned WWE Cruiserweight Champion Juventud Guerrera to win the title; WWE World Tag Champions Kane and The Big Show downed WWE Tag Champions Batista and Rey Mysterio in a non-title match; The Undertaker rolled over Randy Orton in a “Hell in a Cell” encounter.
