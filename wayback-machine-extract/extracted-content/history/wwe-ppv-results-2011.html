WWE PPV Results 2011

WWE PPV RESULTS (2011)

See also: WWE PPV Statistics 2011

30th January 2011: “WWE Royal Rumble 2011”: Boston, MA – 15,113 sellout
($800,000 live gate)
Dark match: R-Truth beat Curt Hawkins
PPV Broadcast: World Heavyweight Champion Edge pinned Dolph Ziggler; WWE World Champion The Miz rolled over Randy Orton;  Eve defeated WWE Divas Champion Natalya Neidhart, Michelle McCool and Layla to win the title; Alberto Del Rio won the 40-man Royal Rumble by last eliminating Santino Marella.

20th February 2011: “WWE Elimination Chamber 2011”: Oakland, CA – 11,500
($700,000 live gate)
Dark match: WWE US Champion Daniel Bryan bested Ted Dibiase Jr.
PPV Broadcast: Alberto Del Rio toppled WWE Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston in a non-title match; World Heavyweight Champion Edge defeated Kane, Drew McIntyre, Wade Barrett, Rey Mysterio and The Big Show in an Elimination Chamber match; Heath Slater and Justin Gabriel overcame WWE World Tag Champions Santino Marella and Vladimir Kozlov to win the titles; WWE World Champion The Miz pinned Jerry Lawler; John Cena rolled over CM Punk, John Morrison, Sheamus, R-Truth and Randy Orton in an Elimination Chamber match.

3rd April 2011: “WWE Wrestlemania 27”: Atlanta, GA – 61,617 (59,000 paid)
($6,268,391 live gate)
Dark matches: WWE US Champion Sheamus battled Daniel Bryan to a no-contest; The Great Khali won a battle royal.
PPV Broadcast: World Heavyweight Champion Edge pinned Alberto Del Rio; Cody Rhodes bested Rey Mysterio; Randy Orton toppled CM Punk; Michael Cole overturned Jerry Lawler by reverse decision-DQ. Steve Austin was special referee;  Kofi Kingston, Kane, The Big Show, and Santino Marella downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett, Ezekiel Jackson and WWE World Tag Champions Heath Slater and Justin Gabriel; The Undertaker dropped Triple H; John Morrison, Trish Stratus and Snooki defeated Dolph Ziggler, Michelle McCool and Layla; WWE World Champion The Miz rolled over John Cena following a restart.

1st May 2011: “WWE Extreme Rules 2011”: Tampa, FL – 10,000
($630,000 live gate)
Dark match: Sin Cara bested Tyson Kidd.
PPV Broadcast: Randy Orton downed CM Punk in a Last Man Standing match; Kofi Kingston defeated WWE US Champion Sheamus in a tables match to win the title; Michael Cole and Jack Swagger overturned  Jerry Lawler and Jim Ross in a “Country Whipping” match; Rey Mysterio toppled Cody Rhodes in a falls-count-anywhere encounter; Layla overcame Michelle  McCool in a no-DQ, no count-out, “Loser leaves WWE” match;  Christian defeated Alberto Del Rio in a ladder match to win the vacant World Heavyweight Title; WWE World Tag Champions Kane and The Big Show downed WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett and Ezekiel Jackson in a lumberjack match; John Cena rolled over WWE World Champion The Miz and John Morrison in a Three Way Steel Cage Match to win the title.

22nd May 2011: “WWE Over The Limit 2011”: Seattle, WA – 6,500
($410,000 live gate)
Dark match: Daniel Bryan beat Drew McIntyre.
PPV Broadcast: R-Truth downed Rey Mysterio; Ezekiel Jackson overturned WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett by DQ; Sin Cara pinned Chavo Guerrero; WWE Divas Champion Brie Bella defeated Kelly Kelly; WWE World Tag Champions The Big Show and Kane overturned CM Punk and Mason Ryan; World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton toppled Christian; Jerry Lawler bested Michael Cole in a “Kiss my Foot” encounter; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over The Miz in an I Quit match.

19th June 2011: “WWE Capitol Punishment 2011”: Washington, DC – 9,850
($684,115 live gate)
Dolph Ziggler bested WWE US Champion Kofi Kingston by stoppage to win the title; Alex Riley toppled The Miz; Alberto Del Rio overturned The Big Show by stoppage; Ezekiel Jackson overcame WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett to win the title; CM Punk dropped Rey Mysterio; World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton pinned Christian; Evan Bourne downed Jack Swagger; WWE Champion John Cena rolled over R-Truth.

17th July 2011: “WWE Money in the Bank 2011”: Chicago, IL – 12,000 sellout
($775,000 live gate)
Dark match: Santino Marella and Vladimir Kozlov overturned WWE World Tag Champions Michael McGillicutty and David Otunga
PPV Broadcast: Daniel Bryan overcame Sin Cara, Cody Rhodes, Sheamus, Kane, Wade Barrett, Justin Gabriel and Heath Slater in a Money in the Bank ladder match; WWE Divas Champion Kelly Kelly defeated Brie Bella; Mark Henry downed The Big Show; Alberto Del Rio overcame Evan Bourne, Rey Mysterio, The Miz, Kofi Kingston, Alex Riley, Jack Swagger and R-Truth in a Money in the Bank ladder match; Christian toppled World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton by DQ. By virtue of a pre-match stipulation, Christian captured the title; CM Punk rolled over WWE World Champion John Cena to win the title.

14th August 2011: “WWE Summerslam 2011”: Los Angeles, CA – 17,404 sellout (14,105 paid)
($1,008,150 live gate)
Dark match: WWE US Champion Dolph Ziggler beat Alex Riley.
PPV Broadcast: Rey Mysterio, Kofi Kingston and John Morrison bested R-Truth, The Miz and Alberto Del Rio; Mark Henry overcame Sheamus by count-out; WWE Divas Champion Kelly Kelly beat Beth Phoenix; Wade Barrett pinned Daniel Bryan; Randy Orton toppled World Heavyweight Champion Christian to win the title in a “No Holds Barred” match; WWE World Champion CM Punk rolled over WWE Interim World Champion John Cena to unify the titles. Triple H was guest referee; Alberto Del Rio pinned WWE World Champion CM Punk to win the title.

18th September 2011: “WWE Night of Champions 2011”: Buffalo, NY – 11,000
($700,000 live gate)
Dark match: Daniel Bryan beat Heath Slater.
PPV Broadcast: WWE World Tag Champions Evan Bourne and Kofi Kingston downed The Miz and R-Truth by DQ; WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes dropped Ted Dibiase Jr; WWE US Champion Dolph Ziggler overturned Alex Riley, John Morrison and Jack Swagger in a four-way; Mark Henry toppled World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton to win the title; WWE Divas Champion Eve Torres overcame Beth Phoenix; John Cena bested WWE World Champion Alberto Del Rio to win the title; Triple H rolled over CM Punk in a no-DQ match.

2nd October 2011: “WWE Hell in a Cell 2011”: New Orleans, LA – 9,400 (7,500 paid)
($475,000 live gate)
Dark match: Daniel Bryan beat JTG.
PPV Broadcast: Sheamus overcame Christian; Sin Cara (Azul) downed Imposter Sin Cara (Negro); WWE World Tag Champions Kofi Kingston and Evan Bourne dropped WWE US Champion Dolph Ziggler and Jack Swagger; World Heavyweight Champion Mark Henry toppled Randy Orton in a “Hell in a Cell” match; WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes bested John Morrison; Beth Phoenix defeated WWE Divas Champion Kelly Kelly to win the title; Alberto Del Rio bested WWE World Champion John Cena and CM Punk in a three-way “Hell in a Cell” match.

23rd October 2011: “WWE Vengeance 2011”: San Antonio, TX – 8,000
($450,000 live gate)
Dark match: Wade Barrett beat Daniel Bryan; WWE World Tag Champions Evan Bourne and Kofi Kingston dropped Dolph Ziggler and Jack Swagger; WWE US Champion Dolph Ziggler bested Zack Ryder; WWE Divas Champion Beth Phoenix overcame Eve Torres; Sheamus bested Christian; R-Truth and The Miz toppled CM Punk and Triple H; Randy Orton pinned WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes in a non-title match; World Heavyweight Champion Mark Henry battled The Big Show to a no-contest; WWE World Champion Alberto Del Rio rolled over John Cena in a “Last Man Standing” match.

20th November 2011: “WWE Survivor Series 2011”: New York, NY (MSG) – 16,749 sellout (13,500 paid)
($1,000,000 live gate)
Dark match: Santino Marella beat Jinder Mahal.
PPV Broadcast: WWE US Champion Dolph Ziggler defeated John Morrison; WWE Divas Champion Beth Phoenix downed Eve Torres in a lumberjack match; Wade Barrett, Cody Rhodes, Hunico, Jack Swagger and WWE US Champion Dolph Ziggler overturned Randy Orton, Sheamus, Kofi Kingston, Mason Ryan and Sin Cara in a “Survivors” elimination match; The Big Show toppled World Heavyweight Champion Mark Henry by DQ; CM  Punk bested WWE World Champion Alberto Del Rio to win the title; The Rock and John Cena rolled over The Miz and R-Truth.

18th December 2011: “WWE TLC 2011”: Baltimore, MD – 8,000
($500,000 live gate)
Dark match: Drew McIntyre beat Alex Riley.
PPV Broadcast: Zack Ryder defeated WWE US Champion Dolph Ziggler to win the title; WWE World Tag Champions Kofi Kingston and Evan Bourne downed Primo and Epico; Randy Orton dropped Wade Barrett in a tales match; WWE Divas Champion Beth Phoenix overcame Kelly Kelly; Triple H toppled Kevin Nash in a ladder match; Sheamus overturned Jack Swagger; The Big Show pinned World Heavyweight Champion Mark Henry in a chairs match to win the title; Daniel Bryan beat World Heavyweight Champion The Big Show to win the title; WWE Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes bested  Booker T; WWE World Champion  CM Punk rolled over The Miz and Alberto Del Rio in a three-way TLC match.
