WWE Money in the Bank Results

WWE MONEY IN THE BANK RESULTS

18th July 2010: “WWE Money in the Bank 2010”: Kansas City, KS – 8,000
($550,000 live gate)
Dark match: Santino Marella beat William Regal.
PPV Broadcast: Kane won the Smackdown “Money in the Bank” ladder match over The Big Show, Dolph Ziggler, Matt Hardy, Christian, Drew McIntyre, Cody Rhodes, and Kofi Kingston; WWE Divas Champion Alicia Fox pinned Eve Torres; WWE Unified World Tag Champions DH Smith and Tyson Kidd downed Jimmy and Jey Uso; World Heavyweight Champion Rey Mysterio dropped Jack Swagger; Kane pinned World Heavyweight Champion Rey Mysterio to win the title; WWE Women’s Champion Layla overcame Kelly Kelly; WWE US Champion The Miz won the Raw “Money in the Bank” ladder match over Mark Henry, Evan Bourne, Chris Jericho, Edge, Ted Dibiase Jr, John Morrison, and Randy Orton; WWE World Champion Sheamus rolled over John Cena in a cage match.

17th July 2011: “WWE Money in the Bank 2011”: Chicago, IL – 12,000 sellout
($775,000 live gate)
Dark match: Santino Marella and Vladimir Kozlov overturned WWE World Tag Champions Michael McGillicutty and David Otunga
PPV Broadcast: Daniel Bryan overcame Sin Cara, Cody Rhodes, Sheamus, Kane, Wade Barrett, Justin Gabriel and Heath Slater in a Money in the Bank ladder match; WWE Divas Champion Kelly Kelly defeated Brie Bella; Mark Henry downed The Big Show; Alberto Del Rio overcame Evan Bourne, Rey Mysterio, The Miz, Kofi Kingston, Alex Riley, Jack Swagger and R-Truth in a Money in the Bank ladder match; Christian toppled World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton by DQ. By virtue of a pre-match stipulation, Christian captured the title; CM Punk rolled over WWE World Champion John Cena to win the title.

15th July 2012: “WWE Money in the Bank 2012”: Phoenix, AZ – 9,000
Youtube pre-show: (46,834 views): WWE World Tag Champions Kofi Kingston and R-Truth beat Hunico and Camacho.
PPV Broadcast: Dolph Ziggler won the Smackdown “Money in the Bank” ladder match over Cody Rhodes, Sin Cara, WWE Intercontinental Champion Christian, Tyson Kidd, Tensai, Damien Sandow and WWE US Champion Santino Marella; World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus pinned Alberto Del Rio; Primo and Epico overcame Titus O’Neil and Darren Young; WWE World Champion CM Punk rolled over Daniel Bryan in a no-DQ match. AJ was guest referee; Ryback squashed Tyler Reks and Curt Hawkins in a handicap match; WWE Divas Champion Layla, Kaitlyn and Tamina Snuka overturned Beth Phoenix, Natalya and Eve Torres; John Cena won the Raw “Money in the Bank” ladder match over The Big Show, Chris Jericho, Kane and The Miz.

14th July 2013: “WWE Money in the Bank 2013”: Philadelphia, PA – 15,000 sellout (13,000 paid)
Pre-show: WWE World Tag Champions Seth Rollins and Roman Reigns beat Jimmy Uso and Jey Uso.
PPV Broadcast: Damien Sandow won a “Money in the Bank” ladder match over Jack Swagger, Antonio Cesaro, Wade Barrett, Cody Rhodes, Dean Ambrose and Fandango; WWE Intercontinental Champion Curtis Axel pinned The Miz; WWE Divas Champion AJ Lee downed Kaitlyn; Ryback dropped Chris Jericho; World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio overturned Dolph Ziggler by DQ; WWE World Champion John Cena rolled over Mark Henry; Randy Orton won a “Money in the Bank” ladder match over Daniel Bryan, Rob Van Dam, Sheamus, Christian and CM Punk.

29th June 2014: “WWE Money in the Bank 2014”: Boston, MA – 12,000 sellout
Shown on PPV/WWE Network
PPV Broadcast: WWE World Tag Champions Jimmy and Jey Uso beat Luke Harper and Erick Rowan; WWE Divas Champion Paige defeated Naomi; Adam Rose downed Damien Sandow; Seth Rollins won a “Money in the Bank” ladder match over Dean Ambrose, Dolph Ziggler, Kofi Kingston, Jack Swagger, and Rob Van Dam; Goldust and Stardust dropped Ryback and Curtis Axel; Rusev overcame Big E; Layla pinned Summer Rae. Fandango was guest referee; John Cena won an eight-way ladder match over Roman Reigns, Randy Orton, Sheamus, Kane, Bray Wyatt, Alberto Del Rio, and Cesaro to win the vacant WWE World Heavyweight Title.


14th June 2015: “WWE Money in the Bank 2015”: Columbus, OH – 13,000 sellout (12,000 paid)
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: R-Truth beat King Barrett.
PPV Broadcast: Sheamus won the “Money in the Bank” ladder match over Dolph Ziggler, Kane, Kofi Kingston, Neville, Randy Orton and Roman Reigns; WWE Divas Champion Nikki Bella downed Paige; The Big Show dropped WWE Intercontinental Champion Ryback by DQ; WWE US Champion John Cena bested WWE NXT Champion Kevin Owens; Darren Young & Titus O’Neill overturned WWE World Tag Champions Big E & Xavier Woods to win the titles; WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins rolled over Dean Ambrose in a ladder match.

19th June 2016: “WWE Money in the Bank 2016”: Las Vegas, NV – 12,000
Shown live on PPV/WWE Network
Pre-show: Goldust and R-Truth defeated Tyler Breeze and Fandango; Kalisto and Sin Cara beat Bubba Ray Dudley and D-Von Dudley.
PPV Broadcast: WWE Tag Champions Kofi Kingston and Big E defeated Simon Gotch and Aiden English, Big Cass and Enzo Amore, and Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows in a four-way; Baron Corbin overcame Dolph Ziggler; Dana Brooke and WWE Women’s Champion Charlotte downed Natalya and Becky Lynch; Apollo Crews overturned Sheamus; AJ Styles overturned John Cena; Dean Ambrose won the “Money in the Bank” ladder match over Alberto del Río, Cesaro, Chris Jericho, Kevin Owens and Sami Zayn; WWE US Champion Rusev defeated Titus O’Neil; Seth Rollins overturned WWE World Heavyweight Champion Roman Reigns to win the title; Dean Ambrose downed WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins to win the title.
