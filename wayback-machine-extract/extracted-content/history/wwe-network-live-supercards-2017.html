WWE Network Live Supercards 2017

WWE NETWORK LIVE SUPERCARDS (2017)

 

14th January 2017: “WWE United Kingdom Title Tournament – Day 1”: Blackpool, England – 2,000 sellout
Dark match: Chris Tyler and Jack Star rolled over Ringo Ryan and Tiger Ali.
WWE Network Live Broadcast: Trent Seven overcame HC Dyer (WWE United Kingdom Title Tournament – 1st Round); Jordan Devlin overcame Danny Burch (WWE United Kingdom Title Tournament – 1st Round); Sam Gradwell defeated Saxon Huxley (WWE United Kingdom Title Tournament – 1st Round); Pete Dunne downed Roy Johnson (WWE United Kingdom Title Tournament – 1st Round); Wolfgang defeated Tyson T-Bone (WWE United Kingdom Title Tournament – 1st Round); Joseph Conners overturned James Drake (WWE United Kingdom Title Tournament – 1st Round); Mark Andrews overturned Dan Moloney (WWE United Kingdom Title Tournament – 1st Round); Tyler Bate downed Tucker (WWE United Kingdom Title Tournament – 1st Round).

15th January 2017: “WWE United Kingdom Title Tournament – Day 2”: Blackpool, England – 2,000 sellout
Dark match: Tucker and Saxon Huxley toppled Nathan Cruz and Dan Moloney.
WWE Network Live Broadcast: Pete Dunne downed Sam Gradwell (WWE United Kingdom Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); Mark Andrews overturned Joseph Conners (WWE United Kingdom Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); Wolfgang defeated Trent Seven (WWE United Kingdom Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); Tyler Bate downed Jordan Devlin (WWE United Kingdom Title Tournament – Quarter-Final); Pete Dunne downed Mark Andrews (WWE United Kingdom Title Tournament – Semi-Final); Tyler Bate downed Wolfgang (WWE United Kingdom Title Tournament – Semi-Final); Neville downed Tommy End; Tyler Bate downed Pete Dunne to become the first WWE United Kingdom Title (WWE United Kingdom Title Tournament – Final).
