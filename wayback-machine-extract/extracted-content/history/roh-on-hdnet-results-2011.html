ROH on HDNet Results 2011

ROH ON HDNET TV RESULTS 2011

Jan 2011

Air Date: 3rd January 2011
ROH TV Champion Eddie Edwards beat Mark Briscoe; Mike Mondo downed Grizzly Redwood; Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin dropped Harlem Bravado and Lance Bravado; Homicide overturned Claudio Castagnoli.

Air Date: 10th January 2011
Mike Bennett downed Nick Dinsmore; Adam Cole and Kyle O’Reilly bested Corey Hollis and Mike Posey; ROH Champion Roderick Strong toppled Colt Cabana.

Air Date: 17th January 2011
Jay and Mark Briscoe defeated Adam Revolver and Ted McNaler; MsChif downed Daizee Haze; Christopher Daniels overcame Chris Hero.

Air Date: 24th January 2011
Harlem and Lance Bravado downed Adam Cole and Kyle O’Reilly; Homicide toppled Orion Bishop; Charlie Haas and Shelton Benjamin bested Kenny King and Rhett Titus.

Air Date: 31st January 2011
Colt Cabana beat Mike Mondo; ROH Champion Roderick Strong and Michael Elgin downed Alex Silva and Chrisjen Hayme; Sara Del Rey dropped Taeler Hendrix; Christopher Daniels rolled over ROH TV Champion Eddie Edwards to win the title.

Feb 2011

Air Date: 7th February 2011
Mike Bennett defeated Grizzly Redwood; Charlie Haas, Shelton Benjamin, Jay Briscoe and Mark Briscoe battled Kenny King, Rhett Titus, ROH Tag Champions Chris Hero and Claudio Castagnoli to a no-contest. 

Air Date: 14th February 2011
Homicide dropped Mark Briscoe; Kyle O’Reilly overturned Jonathan Gresham (Top Prospect 2011 Tournament – First Round).

Air Date: 21st February 2011
ROH TV Champion Christopher Daniels bested Devon Storm; Claudio Castagnoli downed Rhett Titus; Kenny King toppled Chris Hero.

Air Date: 28th February 2011
Andy Ridge beat Grizzly Redwood (Top Prospect 2011 Tournament – First Round); Mike Bennett downed Adam Cole (Top Prospect 2011 Tournament – First Round);Michael Elgin toppled Bobby Dempsey (Top Prospect 2011 Tournament – First Round); ROH TV Champion Christopher Daniels dropped Kenny King.

Mar 2011

Air Date: 7th March 2011
Kyle O’Reilly defeated Michael Elgin (Top Prospect 2011 Tournament – Semi-Final); Mike Bennett beat Andy Ridge (Top Prospect 2011 Tournament – Semi-Final); ROH Champion Roderick Strong rolled over El Generico, Homicide and Jay Briscoe in a Four Way.

Air Date: 14th March 2011
Harlem and Lance Bravado overcame Bobby Dempsey and Grizzly Redwood; Davey Richards bested Tony Kozina; Tommaso Ciampa downed Mike Sydal; Mike Bennett toppled Kyle O‘Reilly (Top Prospect 2011 Tournament – Final). 

Air Date: 21st March 2011
Show was pre-empted

Air Date: 28th March 2011
Show was pre-empted

Apr 2011

Air Date: 4th April 2011
Final episode of ROH on HDNet
Davey Richards and Jay and Mark Briscoe rolled over ROH Champion Roderick Strong and ROH Tag Champions Chris Hero and Claudio Castagnoli.
