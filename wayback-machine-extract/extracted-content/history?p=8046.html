WCW Monday Nitro Results 1999

WCW MONDAY NITRO RESULTS 1999

Jan 1999

Air Date: 4th January 1999
Hugh Morrus beat Glacier; Booker T defeated Emory Hale; Chavo Guerrero Jr downed Norman Smiley; Chris Benoit dropped Horace Hogan; Chris Jericho bested Perry Saturn by DQ; Psicosis and Juventud Guerrera overturned WCW Cruiserweight Champion Billy Kidman and Rey Misterio Jr; Konnan toppled WCW TV Champion Scott Steiner by DQ; Bam Bam Bigelow battled Wrath to a double count-out; Diamond Dallas Page pinned Brian Adams; Hulk Hogan rolled over WCW World Champion Kevin Nash to win the title.

Air Date: 11th January 1999
Ernest Miller beat Perry Saturn; Rey Misterio Jr battled Kaz Hayashi to a no-contest; Booker T pinned Lenny Lane; WCW TV Champion Scott Steiner defeated Diamond Dallas Page; Scott Hall bested Bam Bam Bigelow; Ric Flair toppled Curt Hennig; Kevin Nash rolled over The Giant. 

Air Date: 18th January 1999
Booker T beat Chris Jericho; David Flair defeated Eric Bischoff; The Barbarian and Meng downed Mike Enos and Bobby Duncum Jr; Disco Inferno dropped Wrath; WCW TV Champion Scott Steiner bested Perry Saturn; Psicosis toppled Juventud Guerrera; Rey Misterio Jr rolled over Lex Luger by DQ; Bill Goldberg, Scott Hall and Bam Bam Bigelow battled to a no-contest in a three-way.

Air Date: 25th January 1999
Disco Inferno beat Al Greene; Bam Bam Bigelow battled Scott Hall to a no-contest in a ladder match; The Barbarian and Meng downed Dave Taylor and Finlay(WCW World Tag Title Tournament – 1st Round); Perry Saturn bested Norman Smiley; WCW US Champion Bret Hart dropped Booker T; Bill Goldberg toppled Scott Norton; Ric Flair, Chris Benoit and Steve McMichael rolled over WCW World Champion Hulk Hogan, Kevin Nash and WCW TV Champion Scott Steiner by DQ.

Air Date: 15th February 1999

Air Date: 22nd February 1999
